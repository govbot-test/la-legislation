information available to the public.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To urge and request the state Department of Education to provide contact information for
3 the accrediting entity for each nonpublic school with a prekindergarten program that
4 is not subject to monitoring by the department on the School and Center Finder
5 website.
6 WHEREAS, the state Department of Education's School and Center Finder is a
7 public website that helps families search for public schools, charter schools, early learning
8 centers, and school systems and districts; and
9 WHEREAS, the website provides an interactive reporting system to make school
10 quality and performance data easier for Louisiana families to understand; and
11 WHEREAS, the website provides information on school performance scores, early
12 childhood ratings, academic performance data, contact information, principal or director
13 names, grades served, and extracurricular activities and course offerings; and
14 WHEREAS, the website allows families to compare schools and early learning
15 centers in their area, view accountability ratings, and check school demographics; and
16 WHEREAS, the state Department of Education does not accredit or license K-12
17 nonpublic schools; and
18 WHEREAS, there are several organizations with independently structured quality

1 standards and program requirements that accredit nonpublic schools; and
2 WHEREAS, accreditation information helps parents compare schools and make more
3 informed decisions based on objective standards rather than relying on marketing materials
4 or reputation alone; and
5 WHEREAS, access to this information gives parents confidence that the school is
6 being held accountable to external child welfare and safety standards, which is especially
7 important for nonpublic schools with prekindergarten programs that are not subject to
8 monitoring by the state Department of Education.
9 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
10 does hereby urge and request the state Department of Education to provide contact
11 information for the accrediting entity for each nonpublic school with a prekindergarten
12 program that is not subject to monitoring by the department on the School and Center Finder
13 website.
14 THEREFORE, BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be
15 transmitted to the state Department of Education.
