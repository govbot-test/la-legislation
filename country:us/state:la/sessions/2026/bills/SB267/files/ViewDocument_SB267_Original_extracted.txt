SLS 26RS-558 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE BILL NO. 267
BY SENATOR DUPLESSIS
INSURANCE PREMIUMS. Prohibits the use of a consumer's credit information and zip
code in the underwriting or rating of auto insurance premiums. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 22:1508(C), relative to the use of a consumer's credit information and zip code
3 in underwriting or rating for automobile insurance; to provide that an insurer shall
4 not use a consumer's credit information and zip code in underwriting or rating a
5 consumer for automobile insurance; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 22:1508(C) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §1508. Initial notification
9 * * *
10 C.(1) No insurer shall use a consumer's credit information in
11 underwriting or rating a consumer for automobile insurance.
12 (2) No insurer shall use a single United States Postal Service zip code or
13 a combination of zip codes in underwriting or rating a consumer for automobile
14 insurance.

DIGEST
SB 267 Original 2026 Regular Session Duplessis
Present law allows the use of a consumer's credit information and zip code by an insurer in
underwriting or rating the consumer for personal insurance.
Proposed law prohibits an insurer from using a consumer's credit information and zip code
in underwriting or rating the consumer for automobile insurance.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Adds R.S. 22:1508(C))
