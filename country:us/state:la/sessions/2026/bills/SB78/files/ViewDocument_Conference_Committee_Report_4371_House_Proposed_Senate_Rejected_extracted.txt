CONFERENCE COMMITTEE REPORT DIGEST
SB 78 2026 Regular Session Jenkins
Keyword and summary of the bill as proposed by the Conference Committee
SPECIAL DISTRICTS. Provides for the governing authority of the Shreveport Downtown
Development District. (8/1/26)
Report adopts House amendments to:
1. Make technical amendments.
Report rejects House amendments which would have:
2. Authorized the member of the La. House of Representatives who represents
District 2 to submit a list of nominees to the mayor for the appointment to the
Shreveport Downtown Development Authority.
Report amends the bill to:
1. Authorize the member of the La. House of Representatives and the member
of the La. Senate whose district encompasses all or the greater portion of the
area of the district to submit a list of nominees to the mayor for appointment
to the Shreveport Downtown Development Authority.
Digest of the bill as proposed by the Conference Committee
Present law (R.S. 33:2740.38) provides for the composition of the board of commissioners
of the Shreveport Downtown Development District as follows:
1. One member from a list of three nominees submitted to the mayor by Downtown
Shreveport Unlimited.
2. One member from a list of three nominees submitted to the mayor by the Shreveport
Bossier African American Chamber of Commerce or its successor.
3. One member from a list of three nominees submitted to the mayor by the Greater
Shreveport Chamber of Commerce.
4. One member from a list of three nominees submitted to the mayor by the Downtown
Shreveport Development Corporation.
5. One member from a list of three nominees submitted to the mayor by the state
senators whose senatorial districts is District 37 and District 39 and by the state
representative whose representative district is District 4.
6. Two members from a list of seven nominees submitted to the mayor by the
Shreveport city council members.
Proposed law retains present law but provides that the member of the La. House of
Representatives and the member of the La. Senate whose district encompasses all or the
greater portion of the member of the area of the district to submit a list of nominees to the
mayor for appointment to the Shreveport Downtown Development Authority. Further
provides that one member of authority shall have a background in urban planning, land use

planning, commercial real estate development, commercial real estate construction,
commercial district management architecture or engineering.
Present law provides that all members of the authority, other than elected officials shall
reside in the district or have their personal principal place of business or office in the district
or own property in the district.
Proposed law retains present law but provides that the member with the background in urban
planning, land use planning, commercial real estate development, commercial real estate
construction, commercial district management architecture or engineering shall not have to
reside in the district or have his personal principal place of business or office in the district
or own property in the district.
Proposed law will have prospective application only, and will only apply to vacancies that
occur, for any cause, after the effective date of proposed law.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 33:2740.38(C)(1)(e), (2), (3), and (4); adds R.S. 33:2740.38(C)(5))
