ENROLLED
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE BILL NO. 207
BY REPRESENTATIVE CHANCE HENRY
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 37:3111(B) and (C), relative to the Louisiana Auctioneers
3 Licensing Board; to provide for board membership; to provide for board member
4 qualification; to provide for the domicile of the board; to provide for applicability;
5 and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 37:3111(B) and (C) are hereby amended and reenacted to read as
8 follows:
9 §3111. Board creation; qualification; domicile; term of office; confirmation; oath
10 of office; compensation
11 * * *
12 B. The board shall be composed of seven persons, five of whom shall be
13 auctioneers,; one selected from each public service commission district, and two of
14 whom shall be consumers from the public at large, and all appointed by the governor.
15 The auctioneer members shall be appointed from a list, provided by the board, of
16 current, licensed auctioneers in good standing and shall have been a licensee for no
17 less than five consecutive years. Each appointee shall be a citizen of the United
18 States of America, a resident of Louisiana this state, and at least thirty years of age.
19 The governor shall designate one member of the board to serve as chairman. The
20 board shall elect a vice chairman annually by majority vote of the total membership
21 of the board.

HB NO. 207 ENROLLED
1 C. The board shall be domiciled in East Baton Rouge, but shall be authorized
2 to meet elsewhere in the state Parish where the board shall be sued.
3 * * *
4 Section 2. The provisions of R.S. 37:3111(B) as enacted by this Act shall apply only
5 to board members appointed on or after the effective date of this Act.
