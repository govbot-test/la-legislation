HLS 26RS-193 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE HENRY
LICENSING: Provides relative to the Louisiana Auctioneers Licensing Board
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 37:3111(B) and (C), relative to the Louisiana Auctioneers
3 Licensing Board; to provide for board membership; to provide for board member
4 qualification; to provide for the domicile of the board; and to provide for related
5 matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 37:3111(B) and (C) are hereby amended and reenacted to read as
8 follows:
9 §3111. Board creation; qualification; domicile; term of office; confirmation; oath
10 of office; compensation
11 * * *
12 B. The board shall be composed of seven persons, five of whom shall be
13 auctioneers; one selected from each public service commission district, and two of
14 whom shall be consumers from the public at large, and all appointed by the governor.
15 The auctioneer members shall be appointed from a list of current, licensed
16 auctioneers in good standing provided by the board and shall have been a licensee
17 for no less than five consecutive years. Each appointee shall be a citizen of the
18 United States of America, a resident of Louisiana this state, and at least thirty years
19 of age. The governor shall designate one member of the board to serve as chairman.

HB 207 Original 2026 Regular Session Henry
Abstract: Provides for the board of the La. Auctioneers Licensing Board.
Present law provides for the La. Auctioneers Licensing Board.
Present law provides for the membership of the auctioneer board to be composed of seven
members, five of whom shall be auctioneers; one selected from each public service
commission district, and two of whom shall be consumers from the public at large, all
appointed by the governor.
Proposed law amends present law to remove the requirement that there be one member from
each public service commission district, and two of whom shall be consumers from the
public at large.
Proposed law adds that the auctioneer members shall be appointed from a list of current,
licensed auctioneers in good standing provided by the board and shall have been a licensee
for no less than five consecutive years.
Present law provides that each appointee shall be a citizen of the United States of America,
a resident of this state, and at least 30 years of age. The governor shall designate one
member of the board to serve as chairman. The board shall elect a vice chairman annually
by majority vote of the total membership of the board.
Proposed law retains present law.
Present law provides that the board shall be domiciled in Baton Rouge, but shall be
authorized to meet elsewhere in the state.
Proposed law amends present law to provide that the board shall be domiciled in East Baton
Rouge Parish, where the board may be sued. The board may establish its office in the
metropolitan Baton Rouge area.
(Amends R.S. 37:3111(B) and (C))
