2026 Regular Session ACT No. 220 ENROLLED
SENATE BILL NO. 1
BY SENATOR JENKINS AND REPRESENTATIVES BACALA, CARRIER, FISHER,
JACKSON, LAFLEUR, LARVADAIN, LYONS, MARCELLE,
NEWELL AND WALTERS
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 42:14(C), relative to the Open Meetings Law; to provide for viva
3 voce voting; to provide for electronic voting; and to provide for related matters.
4 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
5 Section 1. R.S. 42:14(C) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
6 §14. Meetings of public bodies to be open to the public
7 * * *
8 C. All votes made by members of a public body shall be viva voce and shall
9 be recorded in the minutes, journal, or other official, written proceedings of the body,
10 which shall be a public document. Any vote by an individual member of a public
11 body that is displayed and recorded using electronic machines shall satisfy the
12 concept of viva voce voting requirements of the Open Meetings Law.
13 * * *
14 Section 2. The provisions of this Act shall be applied retroactively.
PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
GOVERNOR OF THE STATE OF LOUISIANA
