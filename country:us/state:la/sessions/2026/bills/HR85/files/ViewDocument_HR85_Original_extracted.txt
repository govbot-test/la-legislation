HLS 26RS-2778 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 85
BY REPRESENTATIVE ILLG
SPECIAL DAY/WEEK/MONTH: Designates April 1, 2026, as Physical Therapy Day at
the state capitol
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate Wednesday, April 1, 2026, as Physical Therapy Day at the state capitol and to
3 commend the physical therapists, physical therapist assistants, and students across
4 Louisiana for their dedication to improving the health and well-being of the citizens
5 of this state.
6 WHEREAS, physical therapists and physical therapist assistants are highly educated,
7 licensed healthcare professionals who play a vital role in the prevention, diagnosis, and
8 treatment of movement dysfunction and pain; and
9 WHEREAS, physical therapy services enhance the overall quality of life for
10 individuals of all ages and help them recover from injury, manage chronic conditions,
11 improve mobility, and maintain independence; and
12 WHEREAS, physical therapists are integral members of the healthcare team and
13 work collaboratively with physicians and other providers to deliver patient-centered care that
14 reduces the need for more invasive and costly treatments; and
15 WHEREAS, physical therapy contributes significantly to reducing healthcare costs
16 by preventing injuries and minimizes the need for surgery, decreases reliance on prescription
17 medications, and facilitates faster recovery times; and
18 WHEREAS, the physical therapy profession is committed to evidence-based
19 practice, continuing education, and innovation to ensure the highest standard of care for
20 patients across Louisiana; and
