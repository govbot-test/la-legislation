HLS 26RS-381 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE MCMAKIN
LEGISLATIVE AUDITOR: Provides for monthly financial reports to the Louisiana
Legislative Auditor from certain East Baton Rouge Parish agencies
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 33:4570.3(D)(4) and R.S. 48:1461(B) and to enact R.S.
3 46:1608.2, relative to East Baton Rouge Parish; to provide relative to certain
4 agencies within the parish, including the Capital Area Transit System, Recreation
5 and Park Commission for the Parish of East Baton Rouge, and East Baton Rouge
6 Council on Aging; to require the submission of certain financial information to the
7 legislative auditor; and to provide for related matters.
8 Notice of intention to introduce this Act has been published
9 as provided by Article III, Section 13 of the Constitution of
10 Louisiana.
11 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
12 Section 1. R.S. 33:4570.3(D)(4) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
13 §4570.3. Powers and duties
14 * * *
15 D.
16 * * *
17 (4)(a) The commission shall timely submit all reports required by the
18 legislative auditor.
Page 1 of 3
CODING: Words in struck through type are deletions from existing law; words underscored
are additions.

HB NO. 204
1 (b) The commission shall submit bank statements to the legislative auditor
2 on a monthly basis. The deadline to submit bank statements shall be on the last day
3 of each month.
4 Section 2. R.S. 48:1461(B) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
5 §1461. Tax and regulatory exemptions; audits; laws relating to public bodies
6 * * *
7 B.(1) Except as provided in this Chapter and by laws generally applicable
8 to political subdivisions, the system shall not be subject in any respect to the
9 authority, control, or supervision of any regulatory body of the state, including but
10 not limited to the Public Service Commission or by any political subdivision of the
11 state. The system shall not be deemed a common carrier by any court of this state
12 in a suit for personal injury or property damage. It shall cause its financial records
13 to be audited annually, shall be subject to inspection and audit by the legislative
14 auditor, and shall be subject to state laws relating to open meetings, public records,
15 and public contracts.
16 (2) The system shall submit bank statements to the legislative auditor on a
17 monthly basis. The deadline to submit bank statements shall be on the last day of
18 each month.
19 Section 3. R.S. 46:1608.2 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
20 §1608.2. East Baton Rouge Council on Aging; duties
21 The East Baton Rouge Council on Aging shall submit bank statements to the
22 legislative auditor on a monthly basis. The deadline to submit bank statements shall
23 be on the last day of each month.

HLS 26RS-381 ORIGINAL

Present law (R.S. 33:4570.3) provides for the powers and duties of the Recreation and Park
Commission for the Parish of East Baton Rouge (BREC). Provides that BREC shall timely
submit all reports required by the legislative auditor.
Proposed law retains present law.
Proposed law requires BREC to submit bank statements to the legislative auditor on a
monthly basis. Further provides the deadline to submit bank statements is the last day of
each month.
Present law (R.S. 48:1461) provides for the tax and regulatory exemptions for the Capital
Area Transit System (CATS). Further provides that CATS shall have its financial records
audited annually and is subject to inspection and audit by the legislative auditor.
Proposed law retains present law.
Proposed law requires CATS to submit bank statements to the legislative auditor on a
monthly basis. Further provides the deadline to submit bank statements is the last day of
each month.
Present law provides for the organization of voluntary councils on aging within parishes.
Proposed law retains present law.
Proposed law (R.S. 46:1608.2) requires the East Baton Rouge Council on Aging to submit
bank statements to the legislative auditor on a monthly basis. Further provides the deadline
to submit bank statements is the last day of each month.
(Amends R.S. 33:4570.3(D)(4) and R.S. 48:1461(B); Adds R.S. 46:1608.2)
