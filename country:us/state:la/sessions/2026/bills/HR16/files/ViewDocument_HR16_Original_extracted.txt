HLS 26RS-1541 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 16
BY REPRESENTATIVE CARPENTER
SPECIAL DAY/WEEK/MONTH: Designates April 14, 2026, as Sigma Gamma Rho
Sorority, Incorporated, Day at the capitol
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate Tuesday, April 14, 2026, as Sigma Gamma Rho Sorority, Incorporated, Day
3 at the capitol.
4 WHEREAS, Sigma Gamma Rho Sorority, Incorporated, was founded on
5 November 12, 1922, on the campus of Butler University in Indianapolis, Indiana, by seven
6 visionary educators who established an organization grounded in the principles of sisterhood,
7 scholarship, and service; and
8 WHEREAS, Sigma Gamma Rho Sorority is an international, nonprofit organization
9 with more than one hundred thousand members and over five hundred chapters globally that
10 serve communities across the United States and internationally; and
11 WHEREAS, Sigma Gamma Rho Sorority chapters and members are actively
12 involved in their local communities through participation in national sorority programs,
13 including Operation Big Book Bag, Youth Symposium, Women's Wellness, Swim 1922, and
14 Project Cradle Care, which address critical issues impacting youth, families, and
15 communities; and
16 WHEREAS, in addition to these national initiatives, chapters across the state of
17 Louisiana participate in and lead local programs and community partnerships that provide
18 mentorship, scholarships, health education, service opportunities, and resources that support
19 individuals and families within their communities; and
20 WHEREAS, Sigma Gamma Rho Sorority encourages its members to activate their
21 leadership and voices through civic engagement and legislative advocacy and promotes

12 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby designate Tuesday, April 14, 2026, as Sigma Gamma
13 Rho Sorority, Incorporated, Day at the capitol and does hereby commend the members of
14 Sigma Gamma Rho Sorority, Incorporated, for their historic legacy of service, leadership,
15 and advocacy on behalf of the citizens of this state.
