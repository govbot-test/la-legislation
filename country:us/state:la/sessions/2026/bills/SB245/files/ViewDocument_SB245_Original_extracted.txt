SLS 26RS-466 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY SENATOR MYERS
MENTAL HEALTH. Provides relative to treatments for veterans. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 28:810, relative to treatments for veterans; to require the Louisiana
3 Department of Health to develop a veteran suicide prevention plan; and to provide
4 for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 28:810 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
7 §810. Veteran suicide prevention
8 A. The legislature finds that a public health approach to preventing
9 suicide among veterans is of the utmost importance. This approach to
10 preventing veteran suicide encompasses a wide range of collaborative efforts.
11 B. The Louisiana Department of Health, office of behavioral health shall
12 develop a comprehensive plan to prevent veteran suicides, including connecting
13 veterans to ongoing, barrier-free care and utilizing non-traditional, high-impact
14 interventions that can rapidly stabilize post-traumatic stress disorder
15 symptoms.
16 C. The department shall submit the plan to the legislature no later than
17 January 1, 2027.

SB 245 Original 2026 Regular Session Myers
Proposed law requires the La. Dept. of Health, office of behavioral health to develop a
comprehensive plan to prevent veteran suicides, including connecting veterans to ongoing,
barrier-free care and utilizing non-traditional, high-impact interventions that can rapidly
stabilize post-traumatic stress disorder symptoms.
Proposed law requires the department to submit the plan to the legislature no later than
January 1, 2027.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Adds R.S. 28:810)
