1 "(3) However, in a regularly scheduled general election where the only
2 candidate's election appearing on any ballot in the parish is a special primary
3 election, then in such instance, the a change made prior to the close of registration
4 for the general election shall become effective prior to the special primary election.
5 In a regularly scheduled or special general election, where the change of registration
6 was made prior to the close of registration for the general election and does not
7 change any issues or candidate offices upon which the voter was entitled to vote
8 prior to the change, the change shall become effective prior to the regularly
9 scheduled or special general election."
