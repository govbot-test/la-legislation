CHILDREN/CARE: Provides relative to fire safety provisions for day care centers
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 40:1563(C)(1) and (4), relative to powers and duties of the fire
3 marshal; to provide for fire prevention and safety inspections of early learning
4 centers and child day care centers; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 40:1563(C)(1) and (4) are hereby amended and reenacted to read as
7 follows:
8 §1563. Powers and duties generally; use of deputies; responsibilities of local
9 governing authorities with fire prevention bureaus; open structures and
10 process structures; fees
11 * * *
12 C.(1) The fire marshal shall not conduct or supervise inspections pursuant
13 to the provisions of Paragraphs (B)(1), (2), (3), and (4) of this Section within the
14 jurisdiction of any local governing authority in which a fire prevention bureau has
15 been properly established by special ordinance and accompanying resolution as
16 provided in this Section, except as provided in Paragraph Paragraphs (4) and (5) of
17 this Subsection.
18 * * *
19 (4) The fire marshal shall remain responsible for all institutional occupancies
20 requiring a state or federal license, detention, colleges, universities, state-owned and

HB 565 Original 2026 Regular Session McMakin
Abstract: Provides relative to fire safety provisions for day care centers
Present law provides that the fire marshal is responsible for the conduct and supervision of
fire safety inspections of all institutional occupancies requiring state or federal license,
including early learning centers and child day care centers as defined in present law.
Proposed law amends present law to clarify that the fire marshal is responsible for the
conduct and supervision of fire safety inspections of early learning centers and child day care
centers.
(Amends R.S. 40:1563(C)(1) and (4))
