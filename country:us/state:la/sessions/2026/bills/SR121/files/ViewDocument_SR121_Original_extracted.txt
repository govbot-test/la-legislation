SLS 26RS-3645 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 121
BY SENATOR DUPLESSIS
SPECIAL DAY/WEEK/MONTH. Designates May 2026 as National Community Action
Month in Louisiana and commends the Association of Community Action Partnerships of
Louisiana.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate May 2026 as National Community Action Month in Louisiana and to
3 commend the Association of Community Action Partnerships of Louisiana and the
4 forty-two community action agencies serving the state of Louisiana for their
5 steadfast commitment to reducing poverty, strengthening communities, and
6 empowering individuals and families to achieve economic self-sufficiency.
7 WHEREAS, the Association of Community Action Partnerships of Louisiana
8 (ACAP-LA) serves as the statewide association representing the forty-two community action
9 agencies (CAAs) that provide services throughout the state; and
10 WHEREAS, ACAP-LA provides valuable training, technical assistance, and
11 advocacy on behalf of its member agencies, helping to ensure effective delivery of services
12 and resources to citizens in need; and
13 WHEREAS, CAAs are local private and public nonprofit organizations that carry out
14 the Community Action Program which was founded by the 1964 Economic Opportunity Act
15 to fight poverty by empowering the poor as part of the War on Poverty; and
16 WHEREAS, these nonprofit organizations work diligently to improve the quality of
17 life for countless Louisiana residents through programs and services designed to promote
18 economic stability, education, workforce development, housing assistance, nutrition, energy

1 assistance, early childhood education, and family support services; and
2 WHEREAS, CAAs receive funding from a variety of sources, including federal,
3 state, and local governments, foundations, corporations, and private donations, enabling
4 CAAs to address the unique needs of communities across Louisiana; and
5 WHEREAS, the mission of the ACAP-LA and its member agencies is to combat
6 poverty by empowering individuals and families to move toward self-sufficiency, thereby
7 fostering stronger families, healthier communities, and a more prosperous Louisiana; and
8 WHEREAS, the dedicated leadership, staff, volunteers, and community partners
9 associated with these agencies provide invaluable service to the residents of the state and
10 deserve recognition for their tireless efforts and lasting contributions.
11 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
12 does hereby designate May 2026 as National Community Action Month in Louisiana and
13 does hereby commend the Association of Community Action Partnerships of Louisiana and
14 its forty-two community action agencies for their unwavering dedication to serving the
15 people of Louisiana and for their significant contributions toward alleviating poverty and
16 promoting economic opportunity throughout the state.
17 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
18 Association of Community Action Partnerships of Louisiana.
