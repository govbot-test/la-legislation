2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 121
BY SENATOR DUPLESSIS
A RESOLUTION
commend the Association of Community Action Partnerships of Louisiana and the
forty-two community action agencies serving the state of Louisiana for their
steadfast commitment to reducing poverty, strengthening communities, and
empowering individuals and families to achieve economic self-sufficiency.
WHEREAS, the Association of Community Action Partnerships of Louisiana
(ACAP-LA) serves as the statewide association representing the forty-two community action
agencies (CAAs) that provide services throughout the state; and
WHEREAS, ACAP-LA provides valuable training, technical assistance, and
advocacy on behalf of its member agencies, helping to ensure effective delivery of services
and resources to citizens in need; and
WHEREAS, CAAs are local private and public nonprofit organizations that carry out
the Community Action Program which was founded by the 1964 Economic Opportunity Act
to fight poverty by empowering the poor as part of the War on Poverty; and
WHEREAS, these nonprofit organizations work diligently to improve the quality of
life for countless Louisiana residents through programs and services designed to promote
economic stability, education, workforce development, housing assistance, nutrition, energy
assistance, early childhood education, and family support services; and
WHEREAS, CAAs receive funding from a variety of sources, including federal,
state, and local governments, foundations, corporations, and private donations, enabling
CAAs to address the unique needs of communities across Louisiana; and
WHEREAS, the mission of the ACAP-LA and its member agencies is to combat
poverty by empowering individuals and families to move toward self-sufficiency, thereby
fostering stronger families, healthier communities, and a more prosperous Louisiana; and
WHEREAS, the dedicated leadership, staff, volunteers, and community partners
associated with these agencies provide invaluable service to the residents of the state and
deserve recognition for their tireless efforts and lasting contributions.
Page 1 of 2
SR NO. 121 ENROLLED
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
does hereby designate May 2026 as National Community Action Month in Louisiana and
does hereby commend the Association of Community Action Partnerships of Louisiana and
its forty-two community action agencies for their unwavering dedication to serving the
people of Louisiana and for their significant contributions toward alleviating poverty and
promoting economic opportunity throughout the state.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
Association of Community Action Partnerships of Louisiana.
PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE
