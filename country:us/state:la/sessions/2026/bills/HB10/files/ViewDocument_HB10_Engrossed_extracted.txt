HLS 26RS-1 ENGROSSED
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE HENRY
FAIRS/FESTIVALS: Provides for the annual closure of the Acadia Parish Courthouse in
recognition of the International Rice Festival
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 1:55(E)(1)(j), relative to legal holidays; to establish the Friday of the
3 International Rice Festival as a legal holiday in the parish of Acadia; to authorize the
4 clerk of court in the parish of Acadia to close the office in observance of the legal
5 holiday; to provide for exceptions; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 1:55(E)(1)(j) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §55. Days of public rest, legal holidays, and half-holidays
9 * * *
10 E.(1)
11 * * *
12 (j) In addition, in the parish of Acadia, the Friday of the International Rice
13 Festival shall be a legal holiday for the purpose of authorizing the clerk of court in
14 the parish of Acadia to close offices in observance of that day, unless there are
15 functions and duties related to an election that require the office to remain open.
16 * * *
