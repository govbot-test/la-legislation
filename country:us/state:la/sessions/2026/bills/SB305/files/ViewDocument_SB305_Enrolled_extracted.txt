1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 17:3138.2 and 3138.13(C), relative to the Board of Regents; to provide for
3 Louisiana Works to collaborate with the Board of Regents to create a statewide
4 dashboard for career alignment; to provide for information on educational and career
5 opportunities; to provide for duties of the Board of Regents; to provide relative to the
6 collection and analysis of data on work-based learning; and to provide for related
7 matters.
8 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
9 Section 1. R.S. 17:3138.2 and 3138.13(C) are hereby enacted to read as follows:
10 §3138.2. Degree program and career alignment transparency; postsecondary
11 workforce outcomes dashboard
12 A. The Board of Regents, in collaboration with postsecondary education
13 management boards, Louisiana Works and other relevant state agencies, shall
14 develop, maintain, and make publicly available a statewide dashboard or series
15 of dashboards that provide information on the alignment between
16 postsecondary degree programs and workforce outcomes in Louisiana.
17 B. The dashboard or dashboards shall be designed to assist in the
18 following:
19 (1) Prospective and current students and their families in understanding
20 career outcomes associated with degree programs.
21 (2) Policymakers and state agencies in evaluating postsecondary
22 program alignment with workforce needs.
23 (3) Postsecondary institutions in strengthening program design and labor
24 market relevance.
25 C. To the extent permitted by state and federal law, the dashboard or
