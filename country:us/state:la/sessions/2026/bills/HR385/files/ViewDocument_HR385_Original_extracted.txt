HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 385
BY REPRESENTATIVE LAFLEUR
CONDOLENCES: Expresses the condolences of the House of Representatives on the death
of Dr. Erma Waddy Hines
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To express the condolences of the House of Representatives on the death of Dr. Erma
3 Waddy Hines.
4 WHEREAS, it is with immense sadness and profound regret that the House of
5 Representatives of the Legislature of Louisiana has learned of the passing, on May 19, 2026,
6 of Dr. Erma Waddy Hines; and
7 WHEREAS, Dr. Erma Waddy Hines was a distinguished educator, administrator,
8 champion of higher education, and professor emeritus of English and speech at Southern
9 University and A&M College; and
10 WHEREAS, Dr. Hines dedicated forty-five years of service to Southern University
11 and left an indelible mark on generations of students, faculty, and staff at the university; as
12 a professor of English and speech, she was committed to academic excellence and developed
13 and refined curricula, while shaping the intellectual and professional growth of her students;
14 and
15 WHEREAS, Dr. Hines held several leadership roles at Southern University; she
16 served as chairman of the Speech and Theater Program, dean of the College of Arts and
17 Humanities, and chief executive officer of the Office of Institutional Advancement; she also
18 served as director of the Southern University System Foundation, where she advanced the
19 university's mission through fundraising and philanthropic engagement; and
20 WHEREAS, Dr. Hines was an Associate/Life Member of the Baton Rouge Chapter
21 in the South Central Region of Jack and Jill of America, Incorporated; she joined the

13 colleagues and students, and loved ones find comfort in their shared memories of her
14 beautiful life.
