HCAHB466 552 3916
HOUSE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
to Original House Bill No. 466 by Representative LaCombe
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, line 4, after "program;" and before "to provide" insert "to make implementation
3 of the rebate subject to approval by certain tax recipient bodies;"
4 AMENDMENT NO. 2
5 On page 1, at the beginning of line 5, insert "other"
6 AMENDMENT NO. 3
7 On page 1, line 13, after "contrary," delete the remainder of the line and delete line 14 in its
8 entirety and insert the following:
9 "the West Feliciana Parish Government, West Feliciana Parish School Board, and
10 West Feliciana Parish Law Enforcement District may implement"
11 AMENDMENT NO. 4
12 On page 1, line 15, after "through which" and before "certain" delete "the body may refund"
13 and insert "taxpayers may be awarded a rebate of"
14 AMENDMENT NO. 5
15 On page 1, delete lines 18 and 19 in their entirety and on page 2, delete lines 1 through 15
16 in their entirety and insert the following:
17 "B. The rebate provided for in this Section may only be granted in the form
18 of a credit against a subsequent year's ad valorem tax liability, expressed as a
19 percentage of the tax liability, but only against taxes levied by the West Feliciana
20 Parish Government, West Feliciana Parish School Board, and West Feliciana Parish
21 Law Enforcement District.
22 C.(1) The president of West Feliciana Parish Government, the president of
23 the West Feliciana Parish School Board, and the sheriff of West Feliciana Parish
24 shall meet to determine the rebate, if any, to be issued.
25 (2) The meeting shall be called by the parish president, held at the parish
26 council chambers on or before June thirtieth of each year, and conducted in
27 accordance with the Open Meetings Law, with the parish president serving as the
28 presiding officer.
29 (3) The percentage rebate shall only become effective upon the unanimous
30 vote of the president of West Feliciana Parish Government, the president of the West
31 Feliciana Parish School Board, and the sheriff of West Feliciana Parish at the
32 meeting.
33 (4) The sheriff of West Feliciana Parish shall apply the percentage rebate
34 approved, if any, on the final ad valorem tax bill for each property prior to issuing
35 those bills."
