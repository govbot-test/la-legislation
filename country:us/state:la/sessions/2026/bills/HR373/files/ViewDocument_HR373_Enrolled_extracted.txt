ENROLLED
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 373
BY REPRESENTATIVE WALTERS
A RESOLUTION
Sanders, Sr.
WHEREAS, it is with immense sadness and profound regret that the House of
Representatives of the Legislature of Louisiana has learned of the passing, on May 25, 2026,
of Marlon Geron Sanders, Sr.; and
WHEREAS, Marlon Sanders was a beloved music teacher at Lucian Adams
Elementary School in Port Arthur, Texas; and
WHEREAS, in addition to his role as a music educator, Mr. Sanders served as a
football and basketball coach at the school, and he performed as a singer, DJ, and musician;
and
WHEREAS, Mr. Sanders was known throughout the Port Arthur community for his
warm personality and infectious energy; and
WHEREAS, Marlon Sanders will be deeply missed, but the legacy of his loving spirit
shall live on in the hearts and minds of those who were privileged to have known and loved
him.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby express heartfelt condolences on the death of Marlon
Geron Sanders, Sr., and does hereby extend sincere wishes that his family, friends,
colleagues, students, and loved ones find comfort in their shared memories of his beautiful
life.
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
