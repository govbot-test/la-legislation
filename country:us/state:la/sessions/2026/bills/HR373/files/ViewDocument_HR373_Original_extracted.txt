2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 373
BY REPRESENTATIVE WALTERS
CONDOLENCES: Expresses the condolences of the House of Representatives on the death
of Marlon Geron Sanders, Sr.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To express the condolences of the House of Representatives upon the death of Marlon Geron
3 Sanders, Sr.
4 WHEREAS, it is with immense sadness and profound regret that the House of
5 Representatives of the Legislature of Louisiana has learned of the passing, on May 25, 2026,
6 of Marlon Geron Sanders, Sr.; and
7 WHEREAS, Marlon Sanders was a beloved music teacher at Lucian Adams
8 Elementary School in Port Arthur, Texas; and
9 WHEREAS, in addition to his role as a music educator, Mr. Sanders served as a
10 football and basketball coach at the school, and he performed as a singer, DJ, and musician;
11 and
12 WHEREAS, Mr. Sanders was known throughout the Port Arthur community for his
13 warm personality and infectious energy; and
14 WHEREAS, Marlon Sanders will be deeply missed, but the legacy of his loving spirit
15 shall live on in the hearts and minds of those who were privileged to have known and loved
16 him.
17 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
18 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby express heartfelt condolences on the death of Marlon
19 Geron Sanders, Sr., and does hereby extend sincere wishes that his family, friends,
20 colleagues, students, and loved ones find comfort in their shared memories of his beautiful
21 life.
