17 AMENDMENT NO. 12
18 On page 4, between lines 12 and 13, insert the following:
19 "Section 2. The Louisiana Department of Health shall convene a stakeholder advisory
20 workgroup, including representatives of the Louisiana Assisted Living Association and
21 LeadingAge Southeast, to discuss implementation, compliance progress, potential barriers,
22 and rulemaking of the provisions provided for in this Act. The stakeholder advisory
23 workgroup shall be convened for its first meeting no later than October 1, 2026, by the

26 On page 4, line 13, change "Section 2." to "Section 3."
