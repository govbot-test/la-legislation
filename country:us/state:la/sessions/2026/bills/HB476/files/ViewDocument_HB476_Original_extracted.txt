Section 1:
HLS 26RS-997 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE DICKERSON
SCHOOLS: Requires each public secondary school to hang flyers about the Safe Haven
Law in restrooms
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 17:17.9 and 3996(B)(92), relative to public health information; to provide
3 relative to information posted in public middle and high schools; to provide for
4 requirements of the Louisiana Department of Children and Family Services and the
5 state Department of Education; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 17.9 and 3996(B)(92) are hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §17.9. Safe Haven Law information; posting in schools
9 A. Each public school with students in any grade six through twelve shall
10 display flyers designed by the Louisiana Department of Children and Family
11 Services, hereafter referred to as the "department", in the prescribed locations and
12 in the manner provided for in this Section.
13 B. The content of each flyer, at a minimum, shall be as follows:
14 (1) A brief, age-appropriate heading that quickly communicates the nature
15 of the flyer.
16 (2) A summary of the Save Haven Law (Children's Code Article 1149 et
17 seq.), including but not limited to a summary of the ways a child can be legally
18 surrendered.
19 (3) The URL to the section on the department's website about the Safe Haven
20 Law and a QR code that goes to such section.

1 (4) Contact information to receive assistance relative to the Safe Haven Law.
2 (5) The Safe Haven Law logo.
3 C. School employees shall hang the flyer on the inside of the door to each
4 bathroom stall in each bathroom in the school building.
5 D. The department shall make the flyer available for download on its
6 website.
7 E. The state Department of Education shall inform public school governing
8 authorities of how to obtain the flyer.
9 * * *
10 §3996. Charter schools; exemptions; requirements
11 * * *
12 B. Notwithstanding any state law, rule, or regulation to the contrary and
13 except as may be otherwise specifically provided for in an approved charter, a
14 charter school established and operated in accordance with the provisions of this
15 Chapter and its approved charter and the school's officers and employees shall be
16 exempt from all statutory mandates or other statutory requirements that are
17 applicable to public schools and to public school officers and employees except for
18 the following laws otherwise applicable to public schools with the same grades:
19 * * *
20 (92) Safe Haven Law flyers, R.S. 17:17.9.
21 * * *

HB 476 Original 2026 Regular Session Dickerson
Abstract: Requires each public middle and high school to hang a flyer about the Safe
Haven Law in every bathroom stall in the school.
Present law (Ch. C. Art. 1149 et seq.) provides for the Safe Haven Law, provisions
authorizing the relinquishment of infants up to 60 days old to certain facilities.

HLS 26RS-997 ORIGINAL
HB NO. 476
Proposed law requires each public middle school and high school, including charter schools,
to post a flyer on the inside of every bathroom stall door with information about the Safe
Haven Law.
Proposed law requires the La. Dept. of Children and Family Services to design the flyer and
make it available to download on its website.
Proposed law requires the state Dept. of Education to provide information to public school
governing authorities about how to obtain the flyer.
(Adds R.S. 17:17.9 and 3996(B)(92))
