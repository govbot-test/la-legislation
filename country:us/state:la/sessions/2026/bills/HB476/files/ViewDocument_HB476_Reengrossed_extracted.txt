HB 476 Reengrossed 2026 Regular Session Dickerson
Abstract: Requires each public middle and high school to display a flyer about the Safe
Haven Law in every bathroom stall in the school.
Present law (Ch. C. Art. 1149 et seq.) provides for the Safe Haven Law, provisions
authorizing the relinquishment of infants up to 60 days old to certain facilities.
Proposed law requires each public middle school and high school, including charter schools,
to display a flyer on the inside of every bathroom stall door with information about the Safe
Haven Law. Provides an exception that, if students in grade six and below use a bathroom
in the normal course of a school day, a school is not required to display the flyer in such a
bathroom, but is authorized to inform the older students of the content via other approaches.
Proposed law requires the La. Dept. of Children and Family Services to design the flyer and
make it available to download on its website.
Proposed law requires the state Dept. of Education to provide information to public school
governing authorities about how to obtain the flyer.
(Adds R.S. 17:17.9 and 3996(B)(92))
Summary of Amendments Adopted by House
The Committee Amendments Proposed by House Committee on Education to the
original bill:
