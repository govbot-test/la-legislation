ENROLLED
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 328
BY REPRESENTATIVE OWEN
A RESOLUTION
WHEREAS, it is most fitting to commend and celebrate the accomplishments of Kay
Frederickson of Scouting America Troop 1103 on the memorable and historic occasion of
her attainment of the prestigious rank of Eagle Scout; and
WHEREAS, the mission of Scouting America, founded on February 8, 1910, and
chartered by the United States Congress in 1916, is to prepare young people to make ethical
and moral choices over their lifetimes by instilling in them the values of the Scout Oath and
Law; and
WHEREAS, to earn the rank of Eagle Scout, the highest rank in Scouting America,
a Scout must fulfill all requirements of leadership, service, and outdoor skills and must earn
twenty-one badges which signify the mastery of Scouting skills including Citizenship in the
Community, Citizenship in the Nation, and Citizenship in the World; and
WHEREAS, Kay faithfully embodies the twelve points of the Scout Law: she is
trustworthy, loyal, helpful, friendly, courteous, kind, obedient, cheerful, thrifty, brave, clean,
and reverent; and
WHEREAS, Kay is a founding member of Scouting America Troop 1103; and
WHEREAS, presented with her Eagle Scout badge, Kay joins the ranks of such
esteemed and respected Eagle Scouts as the Honorable Gerald R. Ford, thirty-eighth
president of the United States; Captain James Lovell, commander of the Apollo 13 space
flight; Neil Armstrong, first man to walk on the moon; Stephen Spielberg, three-time
Academy Award winning director; and Bill Gates, founder of Microsoft; and

WHEREAS, the legacy of Scouting America is indeed a rich one, for it not only
includes a history of service and proud citizenship which spans more than a century, but also
includes the history of service and singular accomplishments of Kay Frederickson, a young
woman to be recognized and commended by future Eagle Scouts and her community for the
deep human values and genuine spirit of good fellowship that she inspires.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Kay Frederickson on achieving the rank of
Eagle Scout; does hereby express utmost gratitude and respect for the exemplary strides she
has taken as a Scout and citizen; does hereby congratulate Kay and the members and leaders
of Scouting America Troop 1103 as they commemorate the significant event of
Ms. Frederickson's pledge of eternal loyalty to the code of the Eagle Scout; and does hereby
extend sincerest wishes to Kay Frederickson for full measures of happiness and success in
all of her endeavors.
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
