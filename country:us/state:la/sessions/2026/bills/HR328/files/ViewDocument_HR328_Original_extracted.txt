HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 328
BY REPRESENTATIVE OWEN
COMMENDATIONS: Commends Kay Frederickson on achieving the rank of Eagle Scout
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Kay Frederickson on achieving the rank of Eagle Scout.
3 WHEREAS, it is most fitting to commend and celebrate the accomplishments of Kay
4 Frederickson of Scouting America Troop 1103 on the memorable and historic occasion of
5 her attainment of the prestigious rank of Eagle Scout; and
6 WHEREAS, the mission of Scouting America, founded on February 8, 1910, and
7 chartered by the United States Congress in 1916, is to prepare young people to make ethical
8 and moral choices over their lifetimes by instilling in them the values of the Scout Oath and
9 Law; and
10 WHEREAS, to earn the rank of Eagle Scout, the highest rank in Scouting America,
11 a Scout must fulfill all requirements of leadership, service, and outdoor skills and must earn
12 twenty-one badges which signify the mastery of Scouting skills including Citizenship in the
13 Community, Citizenship in the Nation, and Citizenship in the World; and
14 WHEREAS, Kay faithfully embodies the twelve points of the Scout Law: she is
15 trustworthy, loyal, helpful, friendly, courteous, kind, obedient, cheerful, thrifty, brave, clean,
16 and reverent; and
17 WHEREAS, Kay is a founding member of Scouting America Troop 1103; and
18 WHEREAS, presented with her Eagle Scout badge, Kay joins the ranks of such
19 esteemed and respected Eagle Scouts as the Honorable Gerald R. Ford, thirty-eighth
20 president of the United States; Captain James Lovell, commander of the Apollo 13 space

13 Ms. Frederickson's pledge of eternal loyalty to the code of the Eagle Scout; and does hereby

15 all of her endeavors.
