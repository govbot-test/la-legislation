SLS 26RS-671 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 81
BY SENATOR FOIL
SPECIAL DAY/WEEK/MONTH. Designates May 16 through 23, 2026, as Safe Boating
Week in Louisiana.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate May 16 through 23, 2026, as Safe Boating Week in Louisiana.
3 WHEREAS, the Federal Boat Safety Act of 1971 created a partnership between the
4 federal government and the states to improve the safety of recreational boating; and
5 WHEREAS, in recognition of the importance of safe boating practices, the United
6 States Congress by joint resolution approved on June 4, 1958, and enacted 36 U.S.C. 131,
7 authorizing and requesting the president of the United States to annually proclaim the
8 seven-day period before Memorial Day weekend as "National Safe Boating Week"; and
9 WHEREAS, the United States has nearly twelve million registered recreational boats
10 and over three hundred thousand of those boats are on Louisiana waters; and
11 WHEREAS, while fishing, sailing, or otherwise enjoying Louisiana's beautiful
12 waterways with family or friends, it is essential to follow safe boating practices for
13 everyone's protection; and
14 WHEREAS, Louisiana encourages all citizens to institute safe boating practices and
15 to enjoy Louisiana's waters responsibly by taking the necessary steps to reduce injuries and
16 fatalities; and
17 WHEREAS, the United States Coast Guard considers operator use of engine cut-off
18 switches to be important because when operators are ejected or fall overboard, the boat will

1 typically continue until it runs out of fuel, runs aground, or collides with another boat or
2 object; and
3 WHEREAS, the vast majority of boating incidents are caused by human error or poor
4 judgment and not by the boat, equipment, or environmental factors; and
5 WHEREAS, United States Coast Guard statistics show that drowning was the
6 reported cause of death in seventy-six percent of recreational boating fatalities in 2024, and
7 that eighty-seven percent of those who drowned were not wearing life jackets; and
8 WHEREAS, while boats are a marvelous source of recreation, unknowledgeable boat
9 operators are a risk to other boaters when they do not obey the navigation rules of the road,
10 drink alcohol or take drugs while operating a boat, or choose not to wear a life jacket; and
11 WHEREAS, boating knowledge and skills are essential in reducing human error and
12 improving judgment; and
13 WHEREAS, boaters who recognize the risks are more likely to take precautionary
14 measures to protect themselves, their friends, family, and others, underscoring the
15 importance of promoting boating safety education in our state.
16 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of Louisiana does hereby
17 designate May 16 through 23, 2026, as Safe Boating Week in Louisiana.
