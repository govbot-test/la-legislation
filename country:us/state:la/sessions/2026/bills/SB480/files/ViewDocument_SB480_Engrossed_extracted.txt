FISH/FISHING. Provides for archorage in certain waterways with fishing gear restriction.
(8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 56:499.3(D), relative to butterfly and bottom nets; to provide for the use of
3 butterfly and bottom nets in certain water bodies while the vessel is underway; to
4 provide for anchorage; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 56:499.3(D) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
7 §499.3. Butterfly and bottom nets; Oyster Bayou
8 * * *
9 D. Notwithstanding any provision of this Section to the contrary, a boat
10 may anchor in the waters described in Subsection A of this Section if the boat
11 is not anchored within the boundaries of an oyster lease and a person remains
12 aboard while the boat is anchored.

SB NO. 480
The original instrument and the following digest, which constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument, were prepared by Senate Legislative Services.
The keyword, summary, and digest do not constitute part of the law or proof
or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
DIGEST
SB 480 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Allain
Present law authorizes the use of butterfly and bottom nets in Oyster Bayou to take shrimp
only when suspended from a fishing boat which is motor propelled and underway. Further,
prohibits using any anchorage or device to connect to the shoreline on such nets.
Proposed law retains present law and allows anchorage provided the boat is not anchored in
an oyster lease and at least one person remains aboard while anchored.
(Adds R.S. 56:499.3(D))
Summary of Amendments Adopted by Senate
Committee Amendments Proposed by Senate Committee on Natural Resources to
the original bill
