HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 172
BY REPRESENTATIVES WALTERS, JACKSON, AND PHELPS
A RESOLUTION
Grandparent Volunteer Program for forty-six years of exceptional service and to
designate Friday, May 1, 2026, as AmeriCorps Seniors Foster Grandparent
Volunteer Day in Louisiana.
WHEREAS, service in AmeriCorps promotes bridge-building, collaboration, and
leadership skills, and AmeriCorps programs bring together people from different
backgrounds, generations, and geographic locations; and
WHEREAS, for more than five decades, millions of AmeriCorps Seniors volunteers
in the Foster Grandparent Program have played an important role in strengthening
communities by sharing their experience, knowledge, and accomplishments with the
individuals they serve; and
WHEREAS, this nationwide program offers seniors, who are fifty-five years of age
or older, the opportunity to serve as one-on-one mentors, tutors, and caregivers to
economically disadvantaged youth; the program serves to improve behavioral and
educational outcomes for students with special and exceptional needs, helps children who
suffer from abuse or neglect, and provides support to children of veterans or who have
deployed family members; it also serves more than two hundred eighty thousand special
needs children through a network of various organizations and helps guide students to higher
academic achievement; and
WHEREAS, the AmeriCorps Seniors Southern University at Shreveport Foster
Grandparent Volunteer Program matches the willingness and capacity of the Caddo/Bossier
Foster Grandparent Volunteer Program's participants with the needs of children to learn and
prepare for the life ahead of them; and

WHEREAS, this program continues to fulfill its mission of providing guidance and
support to disadvantaged children and enriches the lives of the volunteers who serve them;
these volunteers provide comfort and love, which sets children on a path to a successful
future.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend the AmeriCorps Seniors Southern University
at Shreveport Caddo/Bossier Foster Grandparent Volunteer Program for forty-six years of
exceptional service to children; does hereby extend best wishes for continued success in the
future; and does hereby designate Friday, May 1, 2026, as AmeriCorps Seniors Foster
Grandparent Volunteer Day in Louisiana.
