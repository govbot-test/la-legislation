ETHICS/NEPOTISM: Provides an exception for hiring the immediate family member of
a school board member or superintendent for certain positions
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 42:1119(B)(2)(a)(vii) and (viii), relative to nepotism; to provide an exception
3 for a local school board to hire an immediate family member of a board member or
4 the superintendent as a custodian or paraprofessional under certain circumstances;
5 and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 42:1119(B)(2)(a)(vii) and (viii) are hereby enacted to read as
8 follows:
9 §1119. Nepotism
10 * * *
11 B.
12 * * *
13 (2) Notwithstanding the provisions of Paragraph (1) of this Subsection:
14 (a)
15 * * *
16 (vii) Any local school board may employ an immediate family member of
17 any board member or of the superintendent as a paraprofessional provided the family
18 member meets the required criteria of a paraprofessional. Any school board member
19 or superintendent whose immediate family member is employed by the school board
20 shall recuse himself from any decision involving the promotion or assignment of
21 such employee.

HB 661 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Fontenot
Abstract: Provides an exception to the nepotism law for a local school board to hire an
immediate family member of a board member or the superintendent as a custodian
or paraprofessional under certain circumstances.
Present law generally provides that no member of the immediate family of a member of a
governing authority or the chief executive of a governmental entity shall be employed by the
governmental entity. Present law provides for exceptions.
Proposed law retains present law and provides additional exceptions for a school board to
hire an immediate family member of a school board member or superintendent as either a
paraprofessional or custodian provided the family member meets the criteria for such
position and the school board member or superintendent recuse himself from any decision
involving the promotion or assignment of such employee.
(Adds R.S. 42:1119(B)(2)(a)(vii) and (viii))
