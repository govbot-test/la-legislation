HLS 26RS-611 ENGROSSED
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE THOMPSON
PROPERTY: Provides relative to an exchange of property in Tensas Parish
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 41:909, relative to the disposition of school lands; to provide for the sale or
3 exchange of certain state property in Tensas Parish; to provide for the authority of
4 the Tensas Parish School Board; to require appraisal; to provide for the reservation
5 of mineral rights; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 41:909 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §909. Tensas Parish School Board; sale or exchange of school lands
9 A. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, if the Tensas
10 Parish School Board, on behalf of the state of Louisiana, determines that any school
11 land, including but not limited to sixteenth section lands, school indemnity lands, or
12 other immovable property located in Tensas Parish and as more specifically
13 described in any agreements entered into and documents executed by the Tensas
14 Parish School Board, is no longer needed for school purposes and that the best
15 interest of the Tensas Parish school system would be served by the sale or exchange
16 of such land, the school board may sell such lands or exchange it for other lands in
17 Tensas Parish in accordance with this Section.
18 B. The Tensas Parish School Board shall obtain an appraisal of the land to
19 be sold or exchanged from a certified, licensed appraiser and such appraisal shall be
20 available for public inspection.

HB 677 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Thompson
Abstract: Authorizes the Tensas Parish School Board to sell or exchange 16th section and
indemnity lands where it is in the best interest of the Tensas Parish school system to
do so.
Present law authorizes school boards to sell unused 16th section lands through a township
election process.
Present law authorizes the state to sell indemnity lands and provides for sale through a public
auction process.
Present law further authorizes and provides a public auction process for the sale of other
unused school lands.
Proposed law authorizes the Tensas Parish School Board to sell or exchange 16th section
lands, indemnity lands, or other immovable property in Tensas Parish if the school board
determines that the lands are no longer needed for school purposes and the disposition of the
lands would be in the best interest of the school system.
Proposed law requires an appraisal of the land and making the appraisal available to the
public.
Proposed law requires that if land is exchanged, the school board must receive consideration
that is equal to or greater than the value of the land the school board is disposing of in the
exchange. Consideration received would include the value of land the school board receives
in the exchange.
Proposed law provides for the reservation of mineral rights to the state.
(Adds R.S. 41:909)
