HLS 26RS-5724 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 313
BY REPRESENTATIVE MILLER
COMMENDATIONS: Commends the Lemelle family of St. Landry Parish on the occasion
of their pilgrimage to Rome and audience with His Holiness Pope Leo XIV
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend the Lemelle family of St. Landry Parish on the occasion of their pilgrimage
3 to Rome and audience with His Holiness Pope Leo XIV.
4 WHEREAS, the House of Representatives of the Legislature of Louisiana takes great
5 pride in recognizing individuals and families whose heritage and accomplishments bring
6 honor and distinction to the state; and
7 WHEREAS, His Holiness Pope Leo XIV shares ancestral ties with the esteemed
8 Lemelle family of St. Landry Parish, Louisiana, which is a family that has deep roots in the
9 rich cultural and spiritual heritage of the state; and
10 WHEREAS, the Lemelle family has preserved a proud legacy of faith, perseverance,
11 family unity, and service that reflects the enduring values of the people of Louisiana; and
12 WHEREAS, the historic and meaningful connection between Pope Leo XIV and the
13 Lemelle family serves as a source of pride and inspiration for the citizens of St. Landry
14 Parish and the state of Louisiana; and
15 WHEREAS, the Lemelle family is traveling to Rome, Italy, on a sacred pilgrimage
16 that will culminate with the distinguished honor of being granted an audience at the Vatican
17 with the Holy Father on June 17, 2026; and
18 WHEREAS, the Lemelle family is deserving of heartfelt recognition as they
19 undertake this memorable pilgrimage and celebrate an extraordinary moment in their family
20 history, and the House of Representatives of the Legislature of Louisiana proudly commends
