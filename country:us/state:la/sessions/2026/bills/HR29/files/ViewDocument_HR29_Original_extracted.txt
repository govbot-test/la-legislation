HLS 26RS-1362 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 29
BY REPRESENTATIVE DICKERSON
COMMENDATIONS: Commends the Live Oak High School cheerleading team on
winning its tenth consecutive Universal Cheerleaders Association National High
School Cheerleading Championship
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend the Live Oak High School cheerleading team on winning its tenth consecutive
3 Universal Cheerleaders Association National High School Cheerleading
4 Championship.
5 WHEREAS, the Universal Cheerleaders Association (UCA) was founded in 1974
6 to provide the highest quality educational training for cheerleaders to incorporate high-level
7 skills with traditional crowd leading; and
8 WHEREAS, today, the UCA is the largest cheerleading camp company in the world,
9 training over one hundred eighty thousand cheerleaders each summer at over three thousand
10 two hundred sessions nationwide; and
11 WHEREAS, the Universal Cheerleaders Association celebrates cheerleaders at over
12 fifty regional events across the United States and the UCA Championships hosted at Walt
13 Disney World Resort in Orlando, Florida; these events are televised on ESPN, ESPN2,
14 ESPNU, and CBS Sports; and
15 WHEREAS, the Live Oak High School cheerleading team is led by Coach Maddie
16 Guidry and has won ten consecutive UCA National High School Cheerleading
17 Championships; and
18 WHEREAS, the Live Oak cheerleaders and Coach Guidry are most deserving of the
19 highest recognition for their incredible dominance in UCA competitions over the past
20 decade.
