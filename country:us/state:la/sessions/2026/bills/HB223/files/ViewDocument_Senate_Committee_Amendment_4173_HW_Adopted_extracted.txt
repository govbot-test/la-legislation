25 Art. 610. Reporting procedure; reports to the legislature and the United States
26 Department of Defense Family Advocacy Program
27 A. A reporter shall immediately report suspected child abuse or neglect or that child
28 abuse or neglect was a contributing factor in a child's death in the following ways:
29 * * *
30 (4) Reports to the department shall be made as follows:
31 (a) A mandatory reporter, with the exception of law enforcement as specified in
32 Subsubparagraph (d) of this Subparagraph, shall make a report of suspected abuse or neglect
33 requiring immediate assistance via the designated state child protection reporting hotline
34 telephone number. A report of suspected abuse or neglect which is of a nonemergency nature
35 may be reported via the Louisiana Department of Children and Family Services Mandated
36 Reporter Portal online. Reports may also be made in person at any child welfare office.
37 * * *
38 (d) A mandatory reporter who is a police officer or law enforcement official may
39 submit reports directly to the Department of Children and Family Services through a secure
40 web based platform. The secure web based platform may utilize prompts or other methods
41 to assist the person completing the report to provide information pursuant to Paragraph (B)
42 of this Article as well as information deemed relevant or important. The Department of
43 Children and Family Services shall not refuse to receive, log, or process a report submitted
44 via the secure web based platform.

7 law.
8 (bb) The agency is satisfied that the secure web based platform complies with the

10 Criminal Justice Information Services Security Policy, or Family Educational Rights and
11 Privacy Act, as applicable.
12 (cc) The secure web based platform provider demonstrates to the agency that it has
13 been deemed compliant with the relevant security standard by a federal, state, or local
14 government agency or by a third party that has been approved by a federal, state, or local
15 government agency for security and compliance attestation.
16 (ii) A law enforcement agency that utilizes a secure web based platform shall notify
17 the Department of Children and Family Services not less than ten days before implementing
