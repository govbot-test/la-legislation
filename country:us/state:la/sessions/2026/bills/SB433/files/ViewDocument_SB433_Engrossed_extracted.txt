SLS 26RS-1370 ENGROSSED
2026 Regular Session
BY SENATOR BOUDREAUX
MEDICAID. Provides for Medicaid coverage of certain medications. (1/1/27)
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 46:460.38, relative to Medicaid coverage of certain medications; to require
3 Medicaid coverage of FDA-approved weight loss medications; to provide for
4 qualifying patients; to provide for coverage restrictions; to provide for an effective
5 date; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 46:460.38 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §460.38. Weight loss medications; required coverage
9 A. The Louisiana Department of Health shall require each Medicaid
10 managed care organization to provide coverage for FDA-approved weight loss
11 medications prescribed by a licensed healthcare provider for a Medicaid
12 recipient over the age of eighteen who has a body mass index of thirty-five to
13 thirty-nine and at least one associated comorbidity, including but not limited to
14 type 2 diabetes, prediabetes, hypertension, or cardiovascular disease.
15 B. Coverage shall be subject to prior authorization, which shall be
16 limited to verification of the patient eligibility criteria provided for in this
17 Section.

1 C. Initial authorization for coverage shall not exceed six months.
2 Continued coverage shall require clinical improvements documented by the
3 patient's healthcare provider.
4 D. The Louisiana Department of Health or any Medicaid managed care
5 organization shall not require step therapy for coverage of weight loss
6 medication pursuant to this Section.
7 E. The Louisiana Department of Health shall implement weight loss
8 coverage in a manner that ensures fiscal sustainability consistent with federal
9 requirements. However, the department shall not impose additional eligibility
10 restrictions beyond the restrictions provided for in this Section.
11 F. Implementation of this Section is subject to the appropriation of funds
12 by the legislature for this purpose.
13 Section 2. This Act shall become effective on January 1, 2027.
The original instrument and the following digest, which constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument, were prepared by Senate Legislative Services.
The keyword, summary, and digest do not constitute part of the law or proof
or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
DIGEST
SB 433 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Boudreaux
Proposed law requires Medicaid managed care coverage for FDA-approved weight loss
medications prescribed by a licensed healthcare provider for a Medicaid recipient over the
age of 18 that meets certain criteria.
Proposed law requires prior authorization for coverage but limits prior authorization to
verification of patient eligibility.
Proposed law limits initial coverage to six months and provides for certain documentation
for continued coverage.
Proposed law prohibits step therapy requirements for coverage of the weight loss
medications.
Proposed law prohibits the La. Dept. of Health or the Medicaid managed care organizations
from imposing additional restrictions on coverage.
Proposed law provides that implementation of proposed law is subject to appropriation.
Effective January 1, 2027.
(Adds R.S. 46:460.38)
