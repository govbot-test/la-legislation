2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
BY SENATORS BOUDREAUX, BARROW, BARTHELEMY, DUPLESSIS, JENKINS,
OWEN AND SELDERS AND REPRESENTATIVES BRYANT,
CHASSION, FISHER, FREEMAN, GREEN, DANA HENRY,
JACKSON, KERNER, KNOX, LAFLEUR, MANDIE LANDRY,
TERRY LANDRY, LYONS, MARCELLE, MARTINEZ,
MCFARLAND, MILLER, MOORE, MURRAY, MUSCARELLO,
NEWELL, PHELPS, STAGNI, WALTERS AND YOUNG
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 46:460.38, relative to Medicaid coverage of certain medications; to require
3 Medicaid coverage of FDA-approved weight loss medications; to provide for
4 qualifying patients; to provide for coverage restrictions; to provide for an effective
5 date; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 46:460.38 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §460.38. Weight loss medications; required coverage
9 A. The Louisiana Department of Health shall provide Medicaid coverage
10 for FDA-approved weight loss medications that are medically necessary and are
11 prescribed by a licensed healthcare provider for a Medicaid recipient. The
12 coverage criteria for FDA-approved weight loss medications shall be established
13 by the department.
14 B. Coverage may be subject to prior authorization as determined by the
15 department.
16 C. The Louisiana Department of Health or any Medicaid managed care
17 organization shall not require step therapy for coverage of weight loss
18 medication pursuant to this Section.
19 D. The Louisiana Department of Health shall implement weight loss
20 coverage in a manner that ensures fiscal sustainability consistent with federal
21 requirements.
22 Section 2.(A) The provisions of Section 1 of this Act shall become effective when
23 an Act of the Louisiana Legislature containing a specific appropriation of monies for the
24 implementation of the provisions of this Act becomes effective.
