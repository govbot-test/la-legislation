Section 1:
SLS 26RS-1432 REENGROSSED
2026 Regular Session
BY SENATORS PRESSLY, BASS, JENKINS AND SEABAUGH
ROADS/HIGHWAYS. Designates a portion of Louisiana Highway 526 as the "Hayden Lane
Mangum Memorial Highway". (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To provide relative to state highways; to designate a portion of Louisiana Highway 526 in
3 Caddo Parish as "Hayden Lane Mangum Memorial Highway"; to provide for
4 location and placement of signage; to provide for restrictions on costs of materials
5 to the department; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, Louisiana
8 Highway 526, also known as East Bert Kouns Industrial Drive, beginning at Wrenwood
9 Boulevard and ending on the western side of the Interstate 49 Interchange at Premier
10 Court, all of which is situated in Shreveport, Louisiana in Caddo Parish, is hereby
11 designated as "Hayden Lane Mangum Memorial Highway".
12 Section 2. The Department of Transportation and Development is hereby
13 directed to erect and maintain appropriate signs of this designation provided local or
14 private monies are received by the department equal to the department's actual costs
15 for material, fabrication, mounting posts, and installation of each sign, not to exceed
16 the sum of seven hundred fifty dollars per sign.
