SLS 26RS-154 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 189
BY SENATOR REESE
superintendent of the Vernon Parish School System.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend James Williams for his forty-four years of distinguished service to the
3 students, educators, and families of Vernon Parish, to recognize his extraordinary
4 leadership as superintendent of the Vernon Parish School Board, and to congratulate
5 him on the occasion of his retirement.
6 WHEREAS, James Williams graduated from Leesville High School in 1977 and
7 continued his academic journey at Northwestern State University in Natchitoches, Louisiana,
8 where he earned a Bachelor of Science in Education in 1982; and
9 WHEREAS, in 1994, Mr. Williams further advanced his professional preparation by
10 earning a Master's Degree in Education Administration from McNeese State University in
11 Lake Charles, Louisiana; and
12 WHEREAS, Mr. Williams began his career in education in 1982 as a social studies
13 teacher at Leesville High School, where he served until 1986; and
14 WHEREAS, in August 1986, he joined the faculty of Crowley High School as a
15 social studies and physical education teacher, serving the students of Acadia Parish until
16 August 1990; and
17 WHEREAS, Mr. Williams returned to Leesville High School in 1990, where he
18 taught social studies and physical education and coached multiple sports, demonstrating a

1 deep commitment to student development both inside and outside the classroom; and
2 WHEREAS, in 1995, Mr. Williams was promoted to assistant principal of Leesville
3 High School, and in 1999 he became principal, a position he held with distinction for a
4 decade; and
5 WHEREAS, in 2009, Mr. Williams accepted the role of transportation and food
6 service director for the Vernon Parish School Board, ensuring safe, efficient, and
7 student-centered operations across the district; and
8 WHEREAS, in 2014, he was appointed Superintendent of the Vernon Parish School
9 Board, a role in which he has served for over twelve years, guiding the district with integrity,
10 vision, and unwavering dedication; and
11 WHEREAS, throughout his forty-four year career, Mr. Williams has been a steadfast
12 leader, a mentor to educators, and an inspiration to countless students, families, and
13 colleagues; and
14 WHEREAS, Superintendent Williams will now enter a well-earned retirement,
15 leaving behind a legacy of excellence that will continue to shape the Vernon Parish School
16 System for generations.
17 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
18 does hereby commend James Williams for his forty-four years of distinguished service to
19 the students, educators, and families of Vernon Parish, recognizes his extraordinary
20 leadership as superintendent of the Vernon Parish School Board, and congratulates him on
21 the occasion of his retirement.
22 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to
23 James Williams.
