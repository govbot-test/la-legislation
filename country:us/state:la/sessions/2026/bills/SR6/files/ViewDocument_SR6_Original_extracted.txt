SLS 26RS-215 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 6
BY SENATOR HODGES
COMMENDATIONS. Commends Collin Phillip Sprouse on being selected "Student of the
Year" at Walker High School in Livingston Parish.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend and congratulate Collin Phillip Sprouse on being selected the 2025-2026
3 "Student of the Year" at Walker High School in Livingston Parish.
4 WHEREAS, Collin Phillip Sprouse has been selected 2025-2026 "Student of the
5 Year" at Walker High School, honoring his outstanding academic achievement, leadership,
6 and service to his school and community; and
7 WHEREAS, Collin Phillip Sprouse has distinguished himself academically as a
8 National Merit Semifinalist, earning an exceptional ACT composite score of 35, and has
9 demonstrated sustained excellence through rigorous advanced placement and dual
10 Enrollment course work, resulting in recognition as an AP Scholar with Distinction, National
11 Merit Finalist, and consistent high achievement across all core academic subjects; and
12 WHEREAS, Collin has demonstrated exemplary leadership among his peers, serving
13 as captain of the Walker High School Boys Soccer Team, team leader of the Walker Soccer
14 Fellowship of Christian Athletes, school representative to the Livingston Parish School
15 Board and as a member of the Student Government Association, earning the respect of
16 students, faculty, and administrators alike; and
17 WHEREAS, known for his integrity, inclusiveness, and initiative, Collin took it upon
18 himself to learn Spanish to better communicate with and support his teammates, thereby

2 WHEREAS, beyond the classroom, Collin has excelled as a Louisiana Soccer

4 while mentoring younger officials and consistently upholding the highest standards of
5 fairness, professionalism, and sportsmanship; and
6 WHEREAS, Collin's commitment to service is evidenced by more than a decade of
7 volunteer work with Mighty Moms; participation in disaster relief and flood recovery
8 efforts; and the co-founding of KidCreations, a service initiative dedicated to bringing
9 encouragement and joy to children receiving hospital care; and
10 WHEREAS, guided by faith, family, compassion, and a desire to serve others, Collin
11 Phillip Sprouse aspires to pursue a career in healthcare as a Certified Registered Nurse
12 Anesthetist, uniting academic rigor with service to improve the lives of others; and
13 WHEREAS, through his character, leadership, academic excellence, and devotion
14 to service, Collin Phillip Sprouse stands as a role model for students throughout Livingston
15 Parish and the state of Louisiana.
16 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
17 does hereby commend and congratulate Collin Phillip Sprouse upon being selected the 2025-
18 2026 "Student of the Year" at Walker High School.
19 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to
20 Collin Phillip Sprouse.
