Section 1:
ENROLLED
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE LAFLEUR
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 13:842.4(D), relative to the establishment of the Complex
3 Litigation Section Program in the Nineteenth Judicial District Court; to extend the
4 termination date of the program; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 13:842.4(D) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
7 §842.4. Complex Litigation Section Pilot Program; filing fees; Nineteenth Judicial
8 District Court
9 * * *
10 D. The pilot program authorized by this Section shall be effective for a period
11 of two years, unless extended by the legislature terminate on July 31, 2028.
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE
GOVERNOR OF THE STATE OF LOUISIANA
