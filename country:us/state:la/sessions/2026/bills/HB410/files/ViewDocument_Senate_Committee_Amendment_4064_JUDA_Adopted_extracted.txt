Section 1:
SCAHB410 4335 4064
SENATE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
410 by Representative Schlegel
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 3, delete lines 16 and 17, and insert the following:
3 "(4) Recordings or transcriptions of a public official made in any of the
4 following manners:
5 (a) Of a public official performing an official duty in a public place where
6 there is no reasonable expectation of privacy.
7 (b) For the purpose of preserving evidence related to a public corruption
8 crime as defined in R.S. 11:293.
9 (c) For the purpose of preserving evidence related to malfeasance in office
10 as provided in R.S. 14:134."
11 AMENDMENT NO. 2
12 On page 4, between lines 6 and 7, insert the following:
13 "D. This Chapter shall not apply to incidental, unintended, or background capture
14 of individuals or conversations occurring in public places where the recording is not
15 specifically directed at the individuals."
16 AMENDMENT NO. 3
17 On page 4, at the beginning of line 8, insert "A."
18 AMENDMENT NO. 4
19 On page 4, after line 10, insert the following:
20 "B. A claimant shall demonstrate actual damages resulting from intentional unlawful
21 recording or dissemination."
