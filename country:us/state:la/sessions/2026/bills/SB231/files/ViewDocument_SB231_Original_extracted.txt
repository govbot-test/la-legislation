DIGEST
SB 231 Original 2026 Regular Session Barrow
Present law (R.S. 40:600.35) provides that each local governmental subdivision maintaining
a blighted housing property list shall submit the list to the state housing agency no later than
December 31st of each year. Further provides that the information submitted to the state
housing agency shall be updated quarterly.
Proposed law retains present law.
Present law provides that the state housing agency has the duty of maintaining the statewide
blighted housing property list. The state housing agency shall publish, no later than March
15th of each year, a statewide blighted housing property list based on information received
from local governmental subdivisions in accordance with the provisions of present law.
Further provides that the list shall also be maintained on the state housing agency's website
and shall be updated quarterly.
Present law provides that the state housing agency shall utilize a statewide housing locator
system for the purpose of maintaining the blighted housing property list. The system shall
be updated as needed.
Proposed law retains present law but requires the statewide housing locator system to be
made available to the general public online through the state housing agency's website.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 40:600.35(C))
