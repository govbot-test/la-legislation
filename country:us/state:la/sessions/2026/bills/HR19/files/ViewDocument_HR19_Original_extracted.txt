HLS 26RS-1543 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 19
BY REPRESENTATIVE BUTLER
SPECIAL DAY/WEEK/MONTH: Designates Tuesday, March 17, 2026, as Louisiana
Nursery and Landscape Association Day at the state capitol and commends the
members of the association
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate Tuesday, March 17, 2026, as Louisiana Nursery and Landscape Association
3 Day at the state capitol and commends the members of the association for their
4 dedication to advancing horticulture, promoting environmental stewardship, and
5 supporting the beautification and sustainability of the state of Louisiana.
6 WHEREAS, the Louisiana Nursery and Landscape Association (LNLA) is a
7 nonprofit professional trade association that provides services and support to wholesale
8 growers, brokers, retailers, landscape horticulturists, landscape architects, grounds
9 maintenance contractors, interiorscapers, and allied suppliers within Louisiana’s nursery and
10 landscape industry; and
11 WHEREAS, the LNLA strives to provide its members with educational, marketing,
12 and networking opportunities essential to the continued growth and sustainability of the
13 horticultural industry; and
14 WHEREAS, the LNLA works cooperatively with numerous state, regional, and
15 national green industry organizations to advance horticulture, strengthen professional
16 collaboration, and support the nursery and landscape industry; and
17 WHEREAS, the nursery and landscape professionals of Louisiana make significant
18 contributions to the state by diligently growing, cultivating, and promoting the plants, trees,
19 and landscapes that enrich our communities and strengthen our agricultural economy; and

1 WHEREAS, the members of the LNLA are dedicated to advancing the horticulture
2 industry and supporting the continued growth and success of Louisiana’s nursery and
3 landscape sector; and
4 WHEREAS, through their commitment to excellence, education, and responsible
5 industry practices, the members of the LNLA help ensure high professional standards and
6 the continued development of skilled professionals within the industry; and
7 WHEREAS, the nursery and landscape industry plays a vital role in promoting
8 environmental stewardship, conserving natural resources, and enhancing the ecological
9 health of communities throughout Louisiana; and
10 WHEREAS, the dedication and leadership demonstrated by the members of the
11 LNLA continues to enhance Louisiana’s natural beauty and quality of life for residents and
12 visitors alike.
13 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
14 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby designate Tuesday, March 17, 2026, as Louisiana
15 Nursery and Landscape Association Day at the state capitol and does hereby commend the
16 members of the Louisiana Nursery and Landscape Association for their dedication to
17 advancing horticulture, promoting environmental stewardship, and supporting the
18 beautification and sustainability of our great state.
19 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a suitable copy of this Resolution be transmitted
20 to the president of the board of directors of the Louisiana Nursery and Landscape
21 Association.
