SLS 26RS-352 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY SENATOR EDMONDS
WEAPONS. Provides for certain state officials to carry a concealed weapon. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 14:95(H)(1) and (2)(a), relative to the illegal carrying of
3 weapons; to provide for an exception for certain state officials to carry concealed
4 weapons; to provide for an exemption for certain state officials to carry concealed
5 weapons into the state capitol building; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 14:95(H)(1) and (2)(a) are hereby amended and reenacted to read as
8 follows:
9 §95. Illegal carrying of weapons
10 * * *
11 H.(1) Except as provided in Paragraph (A)(4) of this Section and in
12 Paragraph (2) of this Subsection, the provisions of this Section shall not prohibit
13 active justices or judges of the supreme court, courts of appeal, district courts, parish
14 courts, juvenile courts, family courts, city courts, federal courts domiciled in the state
15 of Louisiana, and traffic courts; members of either house of the legislature; officers
16 of either house of the legislature; the legislative auditor; designated investigative
17 auditors; constables; coroners; designated coroner investigators; district attorneys

SB NO. 277
The original instrument and the following digest, which constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument, were prepared by Senate Legislative Services.
The keyword, summary, and digest do not constitute part of the law or proof
or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
DIGEST
SB 277 Original 2026 Regular Session Edmonds
Present law provides for the crime of illegal carrying of a weapon.
Present law provides for an exception to the crime for several state officials enumerated in
present law, if they are qualified annually in the use of firearms by the P.O.S.T. Council.
Proposed law retains present law and adds designated assistants of the governor, lieutenant
governor, secretary of state, treasurer, commissioner of agriculture and forestry, and
commissioner of insurance to the list.
Present law provides an exception to the prohibition against the carrying of a weapon in the
state capitol building for members of the legislature and the attorney general and members
of his La. Bureau of Investigation security detail.
Proposed law retains present law and adds the following state officials to the list:
(1) The governor and designated members of his staff.
(2) The lieutenant governor and designated members of his staff.
(3) The secretary of state and designated members of his staff.
(4) The treasurer and designated members of his staff.
(5) The commissioner of agriculture and forestry and designated members of his staff.
(6) The commissioner of insurance and designated members of his staff.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 14:95(H)(1) and (2)(a))
