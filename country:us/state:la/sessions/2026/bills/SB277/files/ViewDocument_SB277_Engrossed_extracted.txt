DIGEST
SB 277 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Edmonds
Present law provides for the crime of illegal carrying of a weapon.
Present law provides for an exception to the crime for several state officials enumerated in
present law, if they are qualified annually in the use of firearms by the P.O.S.T. Council.
Proposed law retains present law and adds a designated assistant of the governor, lieutenant
governor, secretary of state, treasurer, commissioner of agriculture and forestry, and
commissioner of insurance to the list.
Present law provides an exception to the prohibition against the carrying of a weapon in the
state capitol building for members of the legislature and the attorney general and members
of his La. Bureau of Investigation security detail.
Proposed law retains present law and adds that the following state officials may carry in the
state capitol building but not in the chamber of the Senate, chamber of the House of
Representatives, or legislative committee rooms:
(1) The governor and a designated member of his staff.
(2) The lieutenant governor and a designated member of his staff.
(3) The secretary of state and a designated member of his staff.
(4) The treasurer and a designated member of his staff.
(5) The commissioner of agriculture and forestry and a designated member of his staff.
(6) The commissioner of insurance and a designated member of his staff.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 14:95(H))
