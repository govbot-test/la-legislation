HLS 26RS-62 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE BILL NO. 148
BY REPRESENTATIVE BAGLEY
HIGHWAYS: Designates a portion of Louisiana Highway 5 in Logansport as the "Eugene
Washington Memorial Highway"
1 AN ACT
2 To designate a portion of Louisiana Highway 5 in Logansport, Louisiana, DeSoto Parish as
3 the "Eugene Washington Memorial Highway"; to provide for implementation; and
4 to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. The portion of Louisiana Highway 5 between United States Highway 84
7 and Louisiana Highway 3015 in Logansport, Louisiana, DeSoto Parish shall be known and
8 is hereby designated as the "Eugene Washington Memorial Highway".
9 Section 2. The Department of Transportation and Development is hereby directed
10 to erect and maintain appropriate signage reflecting this designation provided local or private
11 monies are received by the department equal to the department's actual costs for material,
12 fabrication, mounting posts, and installation of the signage, not to exceed the sum of seven
13 hundred fifty dollars per sign.

HB 148 Original 2026 Regular Session Bagley
Designates a portion of La. Hwy. 5 between U.S. Hwy. 84 and La. Hwy. 3015 in Logansport,
La., DeSoto Parish as the "Eugene Washington Memorial Highway".
Directs the Dept. of Transportation and Development (DOTD) to erect and maintain
appropriate signage reflecting this designation provided local or private monies are received
by the DOTD equal to its actual costs for material, fabrication, mounting posts, and
installation of the signage, not to exceed the sum of $750 per sign.
