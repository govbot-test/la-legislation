HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 409
BY REPRESENTATIVE MARCELLE
A RESOLUTION
To express the condolences of the House of Representatives on the death of Pastor Steven
"Troy" Carter.
WHEREAS, it is with immense sadness and profound regret that the House of
Representatives of the Legislature of Louisiana has learned of the passing, on May 24, 2026,
of Pastor Steven "Troy" Carter; and
WHEREAS, Steven "Troy" Carter was born on July 2, 1964, to Donna Jean Carter
and Robert Carter, Sr.; and
WHEREAS, Pastor Carter was passionate about sharing the word of God with all
who would listen and served with dedication as an assistant pastor at Life Cathedral Worship
Center in Gonzales, Louisiana; he offered comforting words accompanied by scripture to
others and was known as "the man with the message"; and
WHEREAS, Pastor Carter believed in providing for those in need and spreading love
to everyone around him; he was a pillar of the community who was always willing to lend
a helping hand; and
WHEREAS, Troy also worked as a manager at Chicken Shack, where he worked for
forty-three years; he loved his job and the people he worked with; he considered Mr. Joe
Delpit and his wife as second parents and their children as his extended siblings; and
WHEREAS, Troy was married to his wife for twenty-four years and cherished every
moment he spent with her; and
WHEREAS, Pastor Steven "Troy" Carter is survived by his wife, Betty J. Thomas-
Carter; stepchildren, Jeffery and Chantel Thomas; fourteen grandchildren; one great-
grandson; brother, Robert Carter, Jr.; sisters, Patrice Jones Carter and Trenna Carter;
godchild and niece, Andrea Stewart Carter; cousin, Johnnie Jones; and a host of nieces and
nephews; he will be deeply missed, but the legacy of his loving spirit shall live on in the
hearts and minds of those who were privileged to have known and loved him.

THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
