HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 409
BY REPRESENTATIVE MARCELLE
CONDOLENCES: Expresses the condolences of the House of Representatives on the death
of Pastor Steven "Troy" Carter
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To express the condolences of the House of Representatives on the death of Pastor Steven
3 "Troy" Carter.
4 WHEREAS, it is with immense sadness and profound regret that the House of
5 Representatives of the Legislature of Louisiana has learned of the passing, on May 24, 2026,
6 of Pastor Steven "Troy" Carter; and
7 WHEREAS, Steven "Troy" Carter was born on July 2, 1964, to Donna Jean Carter
8 and Robert Carter, Sr.; and
9 WHEREAS, Pastor Carter was passionate about sharing the word of God with all
10 who would listen and served with dedication as an assistant pastor at Life Cathedral Worship
11 Center in Gonzales, Louisiana; he offered comforting words accompanied by scripture to
12 others and was known as "the man with the message"; and
13 WHEREAS, Pastor Carter believed in providing for those in need and spreading love
14 to everyone around him; he was a pillar of the community who was always willing to lend
15 a helping hand; and
16 WHEREAS, Troy also worked as a manager at Chicken Shack, where he worked for
17 forty-three years; he loved his job and the people he worked with; he considered Mr. Joe
18 Delpit and his wife as second parents and their children as his extended siblings; and
19 WHEREAS, Troy was married to his wife for twenty-four years and cherished every
20 moment he spent with her; and

10 loved ones find comfort in their shared memories of his beautiful life.
