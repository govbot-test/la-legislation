SLS 26RS-492 ORIGINAL
SB NO. 211
The original instrument and the following digest, which constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument, were prepared by Senate Legislative Services.
The keyword, summary, and digest do not constitute part of the law or proof
or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
DIGEST
SB 211 Original 2026 Regular Session Kleinpeter
Present law authorizes the Property Insurance Association of Louisiana to promulgate fire
insurance rates. Proposed law retains present law.
Present law specifies the membership of the board of directors of the Property Insurance
Association of Louisiana.
Present law requires one member of the board be a representative designated by the
Louisiana Fire Chiefs Association. Proposed law would change this to two members
designated by the Louisiana Fire Chiefs Association.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 22:1460(B)(2)(i))
