SLS 26RS-3971 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 131
BY SENATOR BARROW
SPECIAL DAY/WEEK/MONTH. Commends and congratulates the 2025-2026 Teacher of
the Year from schools in Senate District 15 for exemplary and dedicated service on Teacher
of the Year Day at the Louisiana State Capitol.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend each 2025-2026 Teacher of the Year from schools in Senate District 15 for
3 their exemplary and dedicated service on Teacher of the Year Day at the Louisiana
4 State Capitol.
5 WHEREAS, excellence in teaching is the result of commitment, dedication,
6 inspiration, patience, creativity, hard work, and the highest order of professional integrity;
7 and
8 WHEREAS, research clearly demonstrates that teachers are paramount to student
9 achievement and it has been estimated that a teacher has two or three times the impact of any
10 other school factor; and
11 WHEREAS, Teacher of the Year programs were created to publicly honor,
12 recognize, and emphasize the critical role teacher's play in ensuring student success in school
13 and in life; and
14 WHEREAS, the outstanding educators named 2025-2026 Teacher of the Year for
15 schools located in Senate District 15 are as follows:
16 Brandi Theriot, Brownsfields Elementary School
17 Tyler Smith, Cedarcrest Southmoor Elementary School
18 Tiffany Life, Claiborne Elementary School
