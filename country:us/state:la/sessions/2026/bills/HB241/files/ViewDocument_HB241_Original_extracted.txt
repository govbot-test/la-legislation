HB 241 Original 2026 Regular Session Thomas
Abstract: Amends the disclosure of financial records law to allow disclosures to the
Consumer Financial Protections Bureau and the methods by which service of process
may be made.
Present law provides for disclosures of financial records to supervisory agencies, including
the federal Office of Thrift Supervision, a defunct agency.
Proposed law amends present law to allow disclosures to the federal Consumer Financial
Protection Bureau, a successor agency.
Present law provides for methods of service of demands for financial records to various
entities.
Proposed law amends present law to provide for service of demand to limited liability
companies.
(Amends R.S. 6:333(A)(12)(g) and (D))
