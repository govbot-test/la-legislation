15 technicians, licensed advanced emergency medical technicians, and licensed
16 paramedics to administer patient-carried, time-critical prescription medication
17 to patients with rare diseases, chronic illnesses, and special medical needs in
18 accordance with R.S. 40:1133.19.

1 EMS practitioner's EMS medical director.
2 B. Medications administered pursuant to this Section shall be limited to
3 intranasal, intravenous, and intramuscular medications.
4 C. A licensed emergency medical technician, licensed advanced
5 emergency medical technician, or licensed paramedic that administers a
6 patient-carried, time-critical prescription medication at the request of the
7 patient or patient's family member as provided for in this Section shall be
8 immune from civil liability, criminal prosecution, or disciplinary or other
9 adverse action under any professional licensing statute for any outcomes
10 resulting from the administration of the medication, unless personal injury
11 results from the gross negligence or willful or wanton misconduct of the
12 practitioner administering the medication.
13 Section 2. This Act shall be known and may be cited as "The Danny's Dose EMS
14 Treatment Act".
