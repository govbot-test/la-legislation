1 A. A licensed emergency medical technician, licensed advanced

DIGEST
SB 195 Original 2026 Regular Session Myers
Present law provides for the powers and duties of the La. Emergency Medical Services
Commission, including making recommendations to the La. Dept. of Health, bureau of
emergency medical services regarding requirements and standards of practice for licensed
EMS practitioners.
Proposed law adds a requirement for the commission to establish protocols to authorize
certain licensed EMS practitioners to administer patient-carried, time-critical prescription
medication to patients with rare diseases, chronic illnesses, and special medical needs.
Proposed law authorizes the EMS practitioners to administer the medication at the request
of the patient or patient's family and in accordance with protocols established by the
