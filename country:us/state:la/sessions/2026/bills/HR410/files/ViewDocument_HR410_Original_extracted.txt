HLS 26RS-6418 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 410
BY REPRESENTATIVE DEVILLIER
COMMENDATIONS: Commends Representative Candace N. Newell on being named
Humanitarian of the Year by Winners of the World Magazine and the WOW Women
of Worth Collective
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Representative Candace N. Newell on being named the Humanitarian of the
3 Year by Winners of the World Magazine and the WOW Women of Worth Collective.
4 WHEREAS, a native of New Orleans, Representative Newell attended Southern
5 University, where she earned a bachelor's degree in psychology, a master's degree in
6 criminology, and a law degree; and
7 WHEREAS, Representative Newell represents House District 99 and was sworn into
8 office on January 13, 2020; and
9 WHEREAS, in July of 2026, Representative Newell is being named Humanitarian
10 of the Year by Winners of the World Magazine and the WOW Women of Worth Collective;
11 and
12 WHEREAS, this award is being given to Representative Newell in New Orleans at
13 the WOW Leadership Brunch and Community Recognition Event in recognition of her
14 leadership and service to the citizens and communities of the Crescent City.
15 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
16 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Representative Candace N. Newell on being
17 named the Humanitarian of the Year by Winners of the World Magazine and the WOW
18 Women of Worth Collective and does hereby extend to her best wishes for continued success
19 in her future endeavors.
