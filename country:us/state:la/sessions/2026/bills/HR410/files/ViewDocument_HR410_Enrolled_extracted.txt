HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 410
BY REPRESENTATIVE DEVILLIER
A RESOLUTION
To commend Representative Candace N. Newell on being named the Humanitarian of the
Year by Winners of the World Magazine and the WOW Women of Worth Collective.
WHEREAS, a native of New Orleans, Representative Newell attended Southern
University, where she earned a bachelor's degree in psychology, a master's degree in
criminology, and a law degree; and
WHEREAS, Representative Newell represents House District 99 and was sworn into
office on January 13, 2020; and
WHEREAS, in July of 2026, Representative Newell is being named Humanitarian
of the Year by Winners of the World Magazine and the WOW Women of Worth Collective;
and
WHEREAS, this award is being given to Representative Newell in New Orleans at
the WOW Leadership Brunch and Community Recognition Event in recognition of her
leadership and service to the citizens and communities of the Crescent City.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Representative Candace N. Newell on being
named the Humanitarian of the Year by Winners of the World Magazine and the WOW
Women of Worth Collective and does hereby extend to her best wishes for continued success
in her future endeavors.
