DIGEST
SB 9 Original 2026 Regular Session Talbot
Present law provides for membership of the criers of several divisions of the Civil District
Court for the parish of Orleans in the Sheriffs' Pension and Relief Fund.
Proposed law retains present law but adds the criers of the supreme court to the fund.
Effective June 30, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 11:2174(B)(1)(b)(intro para))
