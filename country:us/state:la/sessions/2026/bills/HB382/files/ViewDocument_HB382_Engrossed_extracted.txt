HB 382 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session McFarland
Abstract: Provides for the authority of the Joint Legislative Committee on the Budget
Present law requires the Joint Legislative Committee on the Budget (JLCB) to review and
approve any new plan of benefits or the annual plan of benefits for the life, health, and other
benefit programs offered through the Office of Group Benefits. Also requires JLCB review
and approval for any professional, personal, and social services contracts negotiated through
the Office of Group Benefits.
Present law requires the oversight committees to review and approve any adjustments to
those same contracts when the adjustment is one million dollars or greater. Proposed law
changes the review and approval from the oversight committees to JLCB.
(Amends R.S. 42:802(D)(2))
