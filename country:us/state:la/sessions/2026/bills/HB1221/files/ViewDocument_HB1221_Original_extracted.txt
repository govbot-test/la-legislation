DIGEST
The digest printed below was prepared by House Legislative Services. It constitutes no part

HB 1221 Original 2026 Regular Session Amedee
Abstract: Prohibits the collection of certain policy data information.
Proposed law retains proposed law that authorizes the commissioner to enter into
cooperative endeavor agreements or other agreements for the receipt of policy information
on surplus lines, directly placed, and other unauthorized insurance subject to the tax imposed
by present law and proposed law.
Proposed law retains proposed law that permits the commissioner to enter into agreements
that may provide for any of the following:
(1) The use of a clearinghouse to perform functions required pursuant to the agreement.
(2) The collection of policy data in addition to premium.
(3) The computation of the tax due on the portion of premium attributable to each risk
classification.
(4) The assessment of a clearinghouse transaction fee.
(5) The reporting of insurance transactions or policyholders that are exempt from the tax
imposed by present law and proposed law.
(6) Any other provisions that facilitate the administration or collection of the tax
imposed by present law and proposed law.
Proposed law retains the provision of proposed law that permits the collection of policy data
in addition to premium but prohibits the name of the insured, the address of the insured, or
the coverage limits of the insurance policy from being included in the collection of policy
data for the Louisiana Fortified Homes Program Fund.
Proposed law provides that (R.S. 22:439(E)(2)(b) of this Act shall supersede the provision
of Act No. 79 of the 2025 Regular Session.
Effective July 1, 2026.
(Adds R.S. 22:439(E)(2)(b))
