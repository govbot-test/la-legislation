FISH/FISHING. Provides for issuance of certain commercial fishing gear licenses. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact the heading of R.S. 56:305 and to repeal R.S. 56:305(C)(2), relative
3 to commercial fishing gear; to provide for nonresident commercial gear licenses; to
4 provide for prohibitions; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. The heading of R.S. 56:305 is hereby amended and reenacted to read as
7 follows:
8 §305. Commercial gear license; issuance to certain nonresidents prohibited;
9 moratorium on certain new gear licenses; renewals
10 * * *
11 Section 2. R.S. 56:305(C)(2) is hereby repealed.
