8 caused damage to underground utilities or facilities and was at fault for such
9 damage, including failure to comply with the Louisiana Underground Utilities
10 and Facilities Damage Prevention Law, R.S. 40:1749.11 et seq.
11 (b) The office may require documentation of repairs, reimbursement of
12 damages, corrective action plans, utility coordination records, locate requests,
13 or other compliance measures prior to the release of withheld funds."
