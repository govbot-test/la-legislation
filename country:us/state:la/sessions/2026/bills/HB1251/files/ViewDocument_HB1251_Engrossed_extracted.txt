HB 1251 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session LaCombe
Abstract: Provides relative to permits for in-state manufacturing distillers producing less
than 100 gallons.
Present law authorizes the commissioner of the office of alcohol and tobacco control to issue
a permit for a fee to entities in the business of manufacturing, supplying, or dealing in
alcoholic beverages.
Proposed law retains present law and provides that an in-state manufacturing distiller that
distills less than 100 gallons per year may receive a permit for a fee of $100.
(Adds R.S. 26:71(A)(8))
