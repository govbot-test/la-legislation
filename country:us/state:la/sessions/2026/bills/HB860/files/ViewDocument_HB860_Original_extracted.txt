HB 860 Original 2026 Regular Session Braud
Abstract: Authorizes public entities to use a fillable electronic bid form for submitting
bids.
Present law specifies that no public entity may waive the rules in present law or its bidding
documents. Requires public entities to use the La. Uniform Bid Form for public work,
which only requires basic bidder information, bid security, acknowledgment of addenda,
base bid, alternates, signatures, and license numbers. Authorizes the use of unit prices only
as specified, and any extra requirements added by the public entity (unless legally required)

Proposed law retains present law and authorizes public entities to use a fillable electronic
bid form for submitting bids, as long as it contains all text and fields from the La. Uniform
Bid Form. Prohibits additional bidder requirements or input fields from being added.
Clarifies that deviations in formatting from the standard form are not violations.
