BY REPRESENTATIVE MURRAY
MTR VEHICLE/LICEN PLATES: Creates the "Louisiana Democratic Party" special
prestige license plate
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 47:463.243, relative to motor vehicle prestige license plates; to establish the
3 "Louisiana Democratic Party" special prestige license plate; to provide for the
4 creation, issuance, design, implementation, fees, distribution, and rule promulgation
5 applicable to such license plates; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 47.463.243 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §463.243. Special prestige license plate; "Louisiana Democratic Party"
9 A.(1) The secretary of the Department of Public Safety and Corrections shall
10 establish a special prestige motor vehicle license plate to be known as the "Louisiana
11 Democratic Party" plate, provided there is a minimum of one thousand applicants for
12 such plate. The plates shall be restricted to use on passenger cars, pickup trucks,
13 recreational vehicles, and vans.
14 (2) The Department of Public Safety and Corrections, office of motor
15 vehicles, shall create this special prestige license plate when the applicable statutory
16 provisions are met and the department's electronic vehicle and registration system
17 is updated to accommodate the creation of new plates.
18 B. The secretary shall work in conjunction with the chair of the Democratic
19 Party of Louisiana to select the color and design of the plate, provided it is in

3 C. The special prestige license plate shall be issued, upon application, to any

5 D. The department shall collect an annual royalty fee of twenty-five dollars
6 that shall be disbursed in accordance with Subsection E of this Section. This fee
7 shall be in addition to the standard motor vehicle license tax imposed by Article VII,
8 Section 5 of the Constitution of Louisiana, and a handling fee of three dollars and
9 fifty cents for each plate to be retained by the department to offset a portion of
10 administrative costs.
11 E. The annual royalty fee shall be collected by the department and forwarded
12 to the Democratic Party of Louisiana. The monies received from the royalty fees
13 shall be used solely for the activities of the Democratic Party of Louisiana.
14 F. The secretary shall adopt rules and regulations as are necessary to
15 implement the provisions of this Section.

HB 1024 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Murray
Abstract: Creates the "Louisiana Democratic Party" special prestige license plate and
provides for the creation, issuance, design, implementation, fees, distribution, and
promulgation of rules applicable to these license plates.
Proposed law requires that the Dept. of Public Safety and Corrections (DPS&C), office of
motor vehicles (OMV), issue a special prestige license plate to be known as the "Louisiana
Democratic Party" license plate, provided that there is a minimum of 1,000 applicants for
such plates.
Proposed law specifies that the plate is restricted to use on passenger cars, pickup trucks,
recreational vehicles, motorcycles, and vans. Further directs the DPS&C, OMV, to create
the special prestige license plate when the applicable statutory provisions are met and the
dept.'s electronic vehicle and registration system is updated to accommodate the creation of
new plates.
Proposed law requires the secretary work in conjunction with the Democratic Party of La.
to select the color and design of the plate. Requires the design include the words "Louisiana
Democratic Party".

Proposed law specifies that the plate will be issued, upon application, to any citizen of the

Proposed law requires that the dept. collect an annual fee of $25 in addition to the standard
