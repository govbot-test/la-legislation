commitment to expanding educational opportunities for students in Louisiana and
designates March 23, 2026, as Thrive Day at the capitol
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Thrive Academy and Thrive Foundation for their commitment to expanding
3 educational opportunity for students in Louisiana and to designate Monday,
4 March 23, 2026, as Thrive Day at the capitol.
5 WHEREAS, Thrive Academy is a public boarding school located in Baton Rouge
6 that serves students from communities across the state of Louisiana and provides an
7 educational environment designed to prepare young people for academic success, leadership,
8 and lifelong opportunity; and
9 WHEREAS, the students of Thrive Academy represent multiple parishes and more
10 than twenty-nine legislative districts across Louisiana and embody the promise, talent, and
11 potential of this state's young people, demonstrating the importance of expanding access to
12 high-quality educational opportunities; and
13 WHEREAS, Thrive Foundation partners with Thrive Academy to help remove
14 barriers and expand opportunities for students through initiatives such as Thrive Essentials,
15 Thrive Within, Thrive Together, Thrive Beyond, and Thrive Tomorrow, which provide
16 resources that support student well-being, family stability, experiential learning, workforce
17 exposure, and college and career readiness; and
18 WHEREAS, the collaborative efforts of Thrive Academy and Thrive Foundation
19 demonstrate a shared commitment to investing in Louisiana's students and strengthening the
20 future of this state through education, opportunity, and community support; and

1 WHEREAS, the Class of 2026, the largest graduating class in Thrive Academy's
2 history, is present at the Louisiana State Capitol in recognition of Thrive Day at the capitol
3 and serves as a testament to the school's continued growth and impact on students across
4 Louisiana.
5 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
6 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Thrive Academy and Thrive Foundation for
7 their dedication to supporting students and expanding educational opportunity in Louisiana;
8 does hereby commend the students of Thrive Academy, including the members of the Class
9 of 2026 present at the state capitol; does hereby extend best wishes for continued success in
10 their academic pursuits and future endeavors; and does hereby designate Monday, March 23,
11 2026, as Thrive Day at the capitol.
