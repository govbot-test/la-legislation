HB 631 Original 2026 Regular Session Riser
Abstract: Provides for a nonresident license for bear hunting and establishes the fee.
Present law provides for recreational hunting licenses for both residents and nonresidents
and provides for the fees.
Proposed law adds a nonresident bear hunting license and establishes the fee of $100.
(Adds R.S. 56:3002(B)(9))
