SLS 26RS-380 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 3
BY SENATOR MYERS
HEALTH CARE. Repeals rules relative to criminal history background checks on licensed
ambulance personnel and nonlicensed persons.
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 To repeal the Department of Public Safety and Corrections rule LAC 55:I.207(D) which
3 provides relative to criminal history background checks on licensed ambulance
4 personnel and nonlicensed persons; to direct the office of the state register to
5 incorporate the repeal into the Louisiana Administrative Code; and to provide for
6 related matters.
7 WHEREAS, LAC 55:I.207(D) requires licensed ambulance personnel and
8 nonlicensed persons to resubmit to the Bureau of Criminal Identification and Information
9 for a fingerprint background check when the result of a name-based background check
10 contains possible arrest information; and
11 WHEREAS, the requirement to have potential job applicants submit to a fingerprint
12 background check rather than a name-based background check will have a substantive
13 negative impact on the current hiring practices which are required to be performed in
14 accordance with La. R.S. 40:1203.2 et seq.; and
15 WHEREAS, R.S. 49:969 provides that the legislature, by concurrent resolution, may
16 suspend, amend, or repeal any rule adopted by a state department, agency, board, or
17 commission.
18 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of Louisiana hereby repeals

DIGEST
SCR 3 Original 2026 Regular Session Myers
Repeals the Dept. of Public Safety and Corrections rule to require licensed ambulance
personnel and nonlicensed persons to resubmit to the Bureau of Criminal Identification and
Information for a fingerprint background check when the result of a name-based background
check contains possible arrest information.
Directs the La. Register to have the repeal of the rule incorporated into the La.
Administrative Code.
