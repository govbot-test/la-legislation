19 "THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of Louisiana hereby amends
20 LAC 55:I.207 to read as follows:
21 A. A request for the bureau to conduct a criminal history check authorized by R.S.
22 40:1203.2 shall be made on a form provided by the bureau and submitted to it by an
23 employer or authorized agency.
24 B. Each request for a criminal history check authorized by R.S. 40:1203.2 submitted
25 to the bureau shall be accompanied by the fee of twenty-six dollars as established by R.S.
26 40:1203.2(B)(2) and LAC 55:I.101.A and a five dollar technology fee as established by R.S.
27 15:587(D).
28 C. Each request for a criminal history check submitted to the bureau by an authorized
29 agency shall be accompanied by a letter of engagement or contract with the employer as
30 proof that the authorized agency may request and receive criminal history information on
31 behalf of the employer. The results of each criminal history check submitted by an
32 authorized agency on behalf of an employer will be reported to the authorized agency.
33 D. Any Effective beginning October 1, 2026, any criminal history check that
34 contains possible arrest information, shall be resubmitted to the bureau for a fingerprint
35 based background check for positive verification.
36 E. Effective until October 1, 2026, the bureau shall allow parish and local law
37 enforcement agencies to perform criminal history checks and return the applicable
38 criminal history information to an authorized agency."
