1 A RESOLUTION

5 WHEREAS, R.S. 31:149 generally addresses the circumstances under which a

11 projects in which a cooperative endeavor agreement is executed and the project is certified

13 to the prescription of nonuse for a period of twenty years; and
14 WHEREAS, significant issues have arisen concerning the circumstances proposed
15 in Senate Bill No. 492 of the 2026 Regular Session of the Legislature as they pertain to the
16 established regime of mineral servitudes.
