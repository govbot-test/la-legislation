HLS 26RS-655 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE BILL NO. 358
BY REPRESENTATIVE BERAULT
HIGHWAYS: Designates a portion of Louisiana Highway 433 as the "Charles R. Bordelon,
Sr. Memorial Highway"
1 AN ACT
2 To designate a portion of Louisiana Highway 433 in Slidell, Louisiana, as the "Charles R.
3 Bordelon Sr. Memorial Highway"; to provide for implementation; and to provide for
4 related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. The portion of Louisiana Highway 433 in Slidell, Louisiana, beginning
7 at the intersection of Louisiana Highway 433 and Vaultz Road and ending at the intersection
8 of Louisiana Highway 433 and Pinehurst Boulevard, shall be known and is hereby
9 designated as the "Charles R. Bordelon Sr. Memorial Highway".
10 Section 2. The Department of Transportation and Development or its contractors are
11 hereby directed to erect and maintain appropriate signage reflecting this designation
12 approximately eight hundred fifty feet west of Marilyn Drive and facing traffic traveling
13 southeast, provided local or private monies are received by the department equal to the
14 department's actual costs for material, fabrication, mounting posts, and installation of the
15 signage, not to exceed the sum of seven hundred fifty dollars per sign.

HB 358 Original 2026 Regular Session Berault
Designates a portion of La. Hwy. 433 in Slidell, La., beginning at the intersection with
Vaultz Rd. and ending at the intersection with Pinehurst Blvd., as the "Charles R. Bordelon
Sr. Memorial Highway".
Directs the Dept. of Transportation and Development (DOTD) or its contractors to erect and
maintain appropriate signage reflecting this designation approximately 850 feet west of
Marilyn Dr., facing southeast-bound traffic, provided local or private monies are received
by the DOTD equal to its actual costs for material, fabrication, mounting posts, and
installation of the signage, not to exceed the sum of $750 per sign.
