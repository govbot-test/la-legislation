DIGEST
SB 248 Original 2026 Regular Session Miller
Present law provides for circumstances when a precinct may be closed during an election.
Proposed law retains present law but adds a situation where if there are less than twenty
eligible voters in the voting district in the precinct for any office or proposition on the ballot
and there is a polling place where the remaining eligible voters may vote pursuant to present
law within the same location as the polling place that will not be opened.
Effective on February 1, 2027.
(Amends R.S. 18:531.1(A)(intro para) and 531.1(A)(2); adds R.S. 18:531.1(A)(3))
