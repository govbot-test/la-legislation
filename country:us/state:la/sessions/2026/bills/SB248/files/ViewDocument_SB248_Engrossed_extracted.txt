BY SENATOR MILLER
VOTERS/VOTING. Provides for consolidation of precincts with fewer than 20 voters.
(8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact the introductory paragraph of R.S. 18:531.1(A) and 531.1(A)(2) and
3 to enact R.S. 18:531.1(A)(3), relative to polling places; to provide that a polling
4 place for a precinct is not required to be opened under certain circumstances; to
5 provide relative to the powers, duties, and authority of registrars of voters relative
6 to polling places; and to provide for related matters.
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
8 Section 1. The introductory paragraph of R.S. 18:531.1(A) and 531.1(A)(2) are
9 hereby amended and reenacted and R.S. 18:531.1(A)(3) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
10 §531.1. ExceptionExceptions to opening polls; alternative voting locations
11 A. Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, the polling place for
12 a particular precinct shall not be required to be opened in either of the following
13 circumstances:
14 * * *
15 (2) When it is determined by the registrar of voters that there are fewer
16 than twenty voters eligible to vote in the voting district in the precinct, for any
17 office or proposition on the ballot, and there is a polling place where the

DIGEST
SB 248 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Miller
Present law provides for circumstances when a precinct may be closed during an election.
Proposed law retains present law but adds a situation where if there are less than twenty
eligible voters in the voting district in the precinct for any office or proposition on the ballot
and there is a polling place where the remaining eligible voters may vote pursuant to present
law within the same location as the polling place that will not be opened.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 18:531.1(A)(intro para) and 531.1(A)(2); adds R.S. 18:531.1(A)(3))
