1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend and congratulate Dr. Jasper Roper Jr. for fifty years of faithful and
3 distinguished service to Christian Bible College and to recognize the golden jubilee
4 of Christian Bible College and its longstanding commitment to biblical education,
5 spiritual leadership, and service to the community.
6 WHEREAS, Dr. Jasper Roper Jr. has served Christian Bible College with
7 unwavering commitment, dedication, and distinction for fifty years, demonstrating steadfast
8 devotion to the institution's vision and ministry; and
9 WHEREAS, Christian Bible College was founded in 1976 with the mission of
10 "Rightly Dividing the Word of Truth", providing biblical instruction, spiritual formation, and
11 Christian leadership grounded in faith and service; and
12 WHEREAS, the impact of Christian Bible College is felt throughout the community
13 through fulfillment of its mission: "Reaching the world with the gospel of Jesus Christ"; and
14 WHEREAS, Christian Bible College has reached the historic milestone of its golden
15 jubilee, marking fifty years of continuous service as a pillar of faith, education, and spiritual
16 enrichment within the community; and
17 WHEREAS, throughout his tenure as president of Christian Bible College, Dr. Jasper
18 Roper Jr. has exemplified exceptional leadership, wisdom, integrity, and dedication and has
