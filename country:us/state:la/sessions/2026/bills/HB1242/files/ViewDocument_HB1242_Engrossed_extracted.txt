HB 1242 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Terry Landry
Abstract: Authorizes the state Dept. of Education (DOE) to issue a Type III license to an
early learning center under certain circumstances.
Present law (R.S. 17:407.36) provides for types of early learning centers, centers that
provide care and education for children ages birth through five.
Proposed law authorizes DOE to issue a Type III license to more than one early learning
center that applies for licensure at the same location as a school.
(Adds R.S. 17:407.39(I))
