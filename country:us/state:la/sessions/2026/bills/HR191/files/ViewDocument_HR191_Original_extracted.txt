HLS 26RS-2638 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 191
BY REPRESENTATIVE WILDER
HIGHWAYS: Urges and requests the Department of Transportation and Development to
study the need for and safety of mountable median curbs on Range Avenue in
Denham Springs, Louisiana
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To urge and request the Department of Transportation and Development to study the
3 installation of and considerations for safety data supporting mountable median curbs,
4 including alternative options, on Range Avenue in Denham Springs, Louisiana.
5 WHEREAS, Range Avenue is a major commercial and transportation corridor in
6 Denham Springs, Louisiana; and
7 WHEREAS, concerns have been raised regarding whether sufficient right-of-way
8 was left available for proper installation of the mountable median curbs; and
9 WHEREAS, the local business community has questions regarding what
10 consideration was given to businesses along the corridor; and
11 WHEREAS, there is a need to ascertain whether safety data supports the installation
12 of the mountable median curbs; and
13 WHEREAS, concerns have been raised regarding possible alternative options that
14 could have been implemented with similar safety outcomes.
15 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
16 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby urge and request the Department of Transportation and
17 Development to study the installation of and considerations for safety data supporting
18 mountable median curbs, including alternative options, on Range Avenue in Denham
19 Springs, Louisiana.

installation of and considerations for safety data supporting mountable median curbs,
