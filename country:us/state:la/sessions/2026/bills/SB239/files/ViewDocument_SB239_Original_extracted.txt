SLS 26RS-599 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE BILL NO. 239
BY SENATOR MILLER
LIABILITY. Provides relative to burden of proof for recovery in tort claims of mental
anguish and emotional distress. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To enact Civil Code Art. 2315(C), relative to recovery in tort claims of mental anguish and
3 emotional distress absent physical injury; to provide relative to an injured person; to
4 provide relative to certain types of mental anguish and emotional distress; to provide
5 relative to the burden of proof; to provide relative to a defendant's conduct; and to
6 provide for related matters.
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
8 Section 1. Civil Code Art. 2315(C) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
9 Art. 2315. Liability for acts causing damages
10 * * *
11 C. To recover for mental anguish or emotional distress, absent physical
12 injury under this Article, the injured person shall prove the especial likelihood
13 of genuine and serious mental distress arising from the circumstances of the
14 defendant's conduct.

SB 239 Original 2026 Regular Session Miller
Proposed law provides that to recover for mental anguish or emotional distress, absent
physical injury under present law, the injured person shall prove the especial likelihood of
genuine and serious mental distress arising from the circumstances of the defendant's
conduct.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Adds C.C. Art. 2315(C))
