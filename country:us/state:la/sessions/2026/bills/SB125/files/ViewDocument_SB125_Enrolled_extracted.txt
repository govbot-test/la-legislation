1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 15:572.8(H)(2) and (Q), relative to reprieve and pardons of
3 convicted persons; to provide for compensation for wrongful conviction and
4 imprisonment; to provide for deadline to file for supplemental compensation; to
5 provide for an effective date; to provide relative to implementation; and to provide
6 for related matters.
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
8 Section 1. R.S. 15:572.8(H)(2) and (Q) are hereby amended and reenacted to read
9 as follows:
10 §572.8. Compensation for wrongful conviction and imprisonment; petition process;
11 compensation; proof; assignment of powers and duties
12 * * *
13 H.(1) * * *
14 (2)(a) Compensation for the physical harm and injury suffered by the
15 petitioner shall be calculated at a rate of twenty-five thousand dollars per year
16 incarcerated, not to exceed a maximum total amount of two hundred fifty thousand
17 dollars, to be paid at a rate of twenty-five thousand dollars annually.
18 (b) After July 1, 2022, through July thirty-first of the calendar year in
19 which this Act becomes effective, compensation for the physical harm and injury

15 Q. Beginning July 1, 2022, August first of the calendar year in which this
16 Act becomes effective, any petitioner who has been awarded compensation by the
17 court pursuant to the provisions of this Section, on or after September 1, 2005, and
18 prior to July 1, 2022, August first of the calendar year in which this Act becomes
19 effective, may file a petition seeking supplemental compensation in the amount
20 authorized by the provisions of Subparagraph (H)(2)(b)(c) of this Section. The
21 petitioner shall file a petition seeking supplemental compensation on or before July
22 1, 2023, August first of the calendar year following the calendar year in which
23 this Act becomes effective, or be forever barred from filing a supplemental petition.
24 Any compensation awarded pursuant to this Subsection shall be awarded at a rate of
25 forty thousand dollars annually.
26 * * *
27 Section 2.(A) The provisions of Section 1 of this Act shall become effective when
28 an Act of the Louisiana Legislature containing a specific appropriation of monies for the
29 implementation of the provisions of this Act becomes effective.
