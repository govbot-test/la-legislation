1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 22:1037.1, relative to insurance coverage of the healthcare services provided
3 by certified registered nurse anesthetists; to provide for reimbursement of services
4 provided by a CRNA; to provide for applicability; to provide for an effective date;
5 and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 22:1037.1 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §1037.1. Health insurance coverage for healthcare services performed by
9 certified registered nurse anesthetists
10 A. For any healthcare service personally performed by a certified
11 registered nurse anesthetist (CRNA) without medical direction from a physician
12 or dentist, as billed per the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services
13 Medicare Part B regulations, and within the CRNA's lawful scope of practice,
14 the allowed amount of reimbursement shall be no less than the allowed amount
15 the insurer would reimburse for the same service when personally performed
16 by a physician, regardless of billing modifier or coding designation used to
17 identify the provider of the service.
18 B. An insurer shall not deny participation or network inclusion to a
19 CRNA solely on the basis of provider type or licensure.
20 C. Nothing in this Section shall be construed to expand or limit the scope
21 of practice of any licensed healthcare professional.
