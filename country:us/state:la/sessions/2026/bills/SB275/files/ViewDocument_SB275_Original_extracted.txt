SLS 26RS-203 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE BILL NO. 275
BY SENATOR MYERS
HEALTH/ACC INSURANCE. Provides for reimbursement rates of services provided by
CRNAs within their lawful scope of practice. (1/1/27)
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 22:1037.1, relative to insurance coverage of the healthcare services provided
3 by certified registered nurse anesthetists; to provide for reimbursement of services
4 provided by a CRNA; to provide for applicability; to provide for an effective date;
5 and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 22:1037.1 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §1037.1. Health insurance coverage for healthcare services performed by
9 certified registered nurse anesthetists
10 A. For any healthcare service personally performed by a certified
11 registered nurse anesthetist (CRNA) within the CRNA's lawful scope of
12 practice, the allowed amount of reimbursement shall be no less than the allowed
13 amount the insurer would reimburse for the same service when personally
14 performed by a physician, regardless of billing modifier or coding designation
15 used to identify the provider of the service.
16 B. An insurer shall not deny participation or network inclusion to a
17 CRNA solely on the basis of provider type or licensure.

DIGEST
SB 275 Original 2026 Regular Session Myers
Proposed law requires health insurers to reimburse certified registered nurse anesthestists
(CRNA) for services within the lawful scope of a CRNA's practice at no less than the same
amount the insurer reimburses a physician for the same service.
Effective January 1, 2027.
(Adds R.S. 22:1037.1)
