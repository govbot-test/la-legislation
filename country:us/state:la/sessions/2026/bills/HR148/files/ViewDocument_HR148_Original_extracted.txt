HLS 26RS-3519 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 148
BY REPRESENTATIVE LACOMBE
SPECIAL DAY/WEEK/MONTH: Designates April 15, 2026, as Iberville Parish Day at the
state capitol
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate Wednesday, April 15, 2026, as Iberville Parish Day at the state capitol.
3 WHEREAS, Iberville Parish was established on March 31, 1807; it is one of
4 Louisiana's original nineteen parishes and stands as a foundational contributor to the state's
5 rich history and development; and
6 WHEREAS, Iberville Parish is named in honor of the French explorer Pierre Le
7 Moyne d'Iberville and has a legacy of leadership, resilience, and cultural pride that continues
8 to define its citizens; and
9 WHEREAS, Iberville Parish serves as a gateway to the Atchafalaya Basin, the largest
10 river swamp in the United States, which offers significant ecological, recreational, and
11 cultural value to residents and visitors; and
12 WHEREAS, the parish is home to a robust industrial corridor along the Mississippi
13 River that serves as the site of major chemical and manufacturing facilities that support
14 thousands of jobs and contribute substantially to Louisiana's economy and tax base; and
15 WHEREAS, the leaders and citizens of Iberville Parish demonstrate a strong
16 commitment to growth, infrastructure development, and regional collaboration that enhances
17 economic opportunity and quality of life; and
18 WHEREAS, critical infrastructure investments, including the development of a new
19 Mississippi River bridge in the Baton Rouge region, remain essential to improving
20 connectivity, supporting industry, and advancing long-term economic development for
21 Iberville Parish and the state of Louisiana; and
