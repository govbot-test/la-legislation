A RESOLUTION

WHEREAS, Chris Hansen is the former host of NBC Dateline's To Catch a Predator
series and current host of Take Down with Chris Hansen; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Hansen's new show recently filmed episodes at a sting house in
Livingston Parish; with his help, the Livingston Parish Sheriff's Office arrested thirteen men
in a child predator sting; and
WHEREAS, Livingston Parish Sheriff Jason Ard described the process as a learning
experience; suspects readily opened up to Mr. Hansen, which gave deputies a measure of
insider access that typically is not available to them; and
WHEREAS, the Livingston Parish Sheriff's Office, Sheriff Ard, Mr. Hansen, and the
TruBlu crew are most deserving of the highest recognition for their efforts to protect
children.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Chris Hansen and TruBlu Crew for helping
the Livingston Parish Sheriff's Office fight crime in Louisiana and does hereby extend
enduring appreciation for their efforts to remove predators from the state's streets.
