SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 40
BY SENATOR PRICE
Boards Association and the 2026 board of directors.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To recognize and commend the members and board of directors of the Louisiana School
3 Boards Association.
4 WHEREAS, the Louisiana School Boards Association (LSBA) represents the sixty-
5 eight locally elected public school systems in the state of Louisiana and has a membership
6 comprised of six hundred thirty elected school board members; and
7 WHEREAS, the members of the LSBA work daily to improve the quality of
8 education provided to each student enrolled in their respective school systems; and
9 WHEREAS, the LSBA is governed by a board of directors, the members of which
10 are elected by their peers from among the association's membership; and
11 WHEREAS, the members of the LSBA board of directors not only serve their local
12 communities and schools, but all of Louisiana's public school systems and students by
13 volunteering their time to promote, support, and advocate on behalf of all of Louisiana's
14 public school students.
15 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
16 does hereby commend and recognize the members of the Louisiana School Boards
17 Association and the LSBA 2026 board of directors: President Taft Kleinpeter of Ascension
18 Parish; President-elect Debra Schum of St. John the Baptist Parish; Vice President Brandon
