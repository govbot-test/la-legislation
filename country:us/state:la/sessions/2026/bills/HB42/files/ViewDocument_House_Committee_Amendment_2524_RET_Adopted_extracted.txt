13 "Section 2. R.S. 17:3357 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
14 §3357. Phased retirement program
15 A. Each public postsecondary education management board may develop
16 and implement a phased retirement program, referred to in this Section as a
17 "program", for institutions under its management and supervision for the purpose of
18 supporting faculty and staff recruitment and retention, facilitating institutional
19 knowledge transfer, and promoting strategic succession planning.
20 B. If a management board opts to exercise its authority, it shall adopt a
21 policy for this purpose which shall address the specific operational and institutional
22 needs of the applicable system and institutions and provide for, at a minimum, all of
23 the following:
24 (1) A provision that participation in the program is voluntary on the part of
25 an employee.
26 (2) A definition of eligible employee with respect to program participation,
27 which shall include, at a minimum, all of the following:
28 (a) He is eligible for retirement as provided in R.S. 11:761 at the time that
29 he begins participating in the program.
30 (b) He has a minimum of ten years of collective service at a public
31 postsecondary education institution in Louisiana, whether consecutive or
32 nonconsecutive, without a refund of retirement contributions.
33 (c) He has reached the age of fifty-nine and one-half years or older.
34 (3) An employee participation period of not less than twelve months and not
35 more than thirty-six months.
36 (4)(a) A requirement that a written memorandum of understanding be
37 executed between the public postsecondary education system or institution and the
38 employee that specifies workload, schedule, and program expectations.
39 (b) A requirement that the memorandum of understanding be approved by
40 the institution's human resources officer, chief academic officer, and chief fiscal
41 officer, as applicable.
42 (5) A requirement that participant workload be no less than fifty percent
43 effort during program participation.
