CRIME/PUNISHMENT. Prohibits using a child's image to train artificial intelligence to
produce child sexual abuse materials. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact the introductory paragraph of R.S. 14:81.1(G) and to enact R.S.
3 14:81.1(A)(3) and (4), relative to offenses affecting the public morals; to provide
4 relative to child sexual abuse materials; to provide relative to the elements of the
5 crime; to prohibit using a child's image to train artificial intelligence to produce child
6 sexual abuse materials; to provide relative to prosecutions; and to provide for related
7 matters.
8 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
9 Section 1. The introductory paragraph of R.S. 14:81.1(G) is hereby amended and
10 reenacted and R.S. 14:81.1(A)(3) and (4) are hereby enacted to read as follows:
11 §81.1. Child sexual abuse materials
12 A.(1) * * *
13 (3) It shall also be a violation of this Section for a person to use an image
14 of a child under the age of seventeen years at the time the image was created
15 with the intent to train an artificial intelligence model to produce child sexual
16 abuse materials.
17 (4) It is not a required element of an offense under this Section that the

SLS 26RS-433 REENGROSSED
SB NO. 110
1 child depicted in the child sexual abuse materials be a natural person.
2 * * *
3 G. In prosecutions for violations of this Section, the trier of fact may
4 determine, utilizing the following factors, whether or not the a natural person
5 displayed or depicted in any photograph, videotape, film, or other video reproduction
6 introduced in evidence was under the age of seventeen years at the time of filming
7 or recording, or whether or not the image used to train an artificial intelligence
8 model depicts a person who appears to be under the age of seventeen years:
9 (1) The general body growth, bone structure, and bone development of the
10 person.
11 (2) The development of pubic or body hair on the person.
12 (3) The development of the person's sexual organs.
13 (4) The context in which the person is placed or the age attributed to the
14 person in any accompanying video, printed, or text material.
15 (5) Available expert testimony and opinion as to the chronological age or
16 degree of physical or mental maturity or development of the person.
17 (6) Such other information, factors, and evidence available to the trier of fact
18 which the court determines is probative and reasonably reliable.
19 * * *
The original instrument and the following digest, which constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument, were prepared by Senate Legislative Services.
The keyword, summary, and digest do not constitute part of the law or proof
or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
DIGEST
SB 110 Reengrossed 2026 Regular Session Cloud
Present law provides that it is unlawful for a person to produce, promote, advertise,
distribute, possess, or possess with the intent to distribute child sexual abuse materials.
Proposed law retains present law and provides that it also will be unlawful for a person to
use an image of a natural person who was under 17 years of age at the time the image was
created or an image that depicts a person who appears to be under the age of seventeen years
with the intent to train an artificial intelligence model to produce child sexual abuse
materials.
Proposed law otherwise retains present law.
