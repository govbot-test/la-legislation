DIGEST
SB 423 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Foil
Present law establishes the East Baton Rouge Parish Riverside Centroplex Fund and
provides for the use of the monies in the fund including capital improvements at the
Riverside Centroplex, the Louisiana Arts and Science Center, Riverfront Promenade, and
related projects in the Riverfront Development Plan, all such projects to be in the city of
Baton Rouge.
Proposed law adds operations to capital improvements as an acceptable use of the monies
by the recipients.
Proposed law changes the appropriation from the Louisiana Arts and Science Center to the
Louisiana Art and Science Museum. Retains all other provisions of present law.
Effective July 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 47:332.2(B))
