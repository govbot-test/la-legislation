4 On page 2, delete lines 7 through 11 in their entirety and insert the following:
5 "A. Louisiana Economic Development may support the recruitment,
6 retention, and expansion of brick manufacturing facilities in this state within existing
7 statutory authority and subject to the availability of funds.
8 B. In accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act, Louisiana
9 Economic Development may develop policies, guidance, or program criteria
10 consistent with existing economic development programs, when appropriate and
11 within existing authority, including but not limited to the following"
12 AMENDMENT NO. 3
13 On page 2, delete lines 20 through 23 in their entirety and insert the following:
14 "(5) Coordinating with ports, rail providers, and local governments regarding
15 infrastructure needs associated with brick manufacturing projects.
16 (6) Other lawful economic development activities consistent with this Part
17 and existing programs."
