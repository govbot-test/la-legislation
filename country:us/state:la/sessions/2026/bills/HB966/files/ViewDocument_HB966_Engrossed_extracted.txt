HIGHWAYS: Designates a portion of Louisiana Highway 15 as the "President Barack
Obama Road"
1 AN ACT
2 To designate a portion of Louisiana Highway 15 in Monroe, Louisiana, as the "President
3 Barack Obama Road"; to provide for implementation; and to provide for related
4 matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. The portion of Louisiana Highway 15 in Monroe, Louisiana, between
7 South Second Street and United States Highway 165, otherwise known as Winnsboro Road,
8 shall be known and is hereby designated as the "President Barack Obama Road".
9 Section 2. The Department of Transportation and Development or its contractors are
10 hereby directed to erect and maintain appropriate signage reflecting this designation
11 provided local or private monies are received by the department equal to the department's
12 actual costs for material, fabrication, mounting posts, and installation of the signage, not to
13 exceed the sum of seven hundred fifty dollars per sign.

HB 966 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Moore
Designates a portion of La. Hwy. 15 in Monroe, La., between S. 2nd St. and U.S. Hwy. 165,
as the "President Barack Obama Road".
Directs the Dept. of Transportation and Development (DOTD) to erect and maintain
appropriate signage reflecting this designation provided local or private monies are received
by the DOTD equal to its actual costs for material, fabrication, mounting posts, and
installation of the signage, not to exceed the sum of $750 per sign.
