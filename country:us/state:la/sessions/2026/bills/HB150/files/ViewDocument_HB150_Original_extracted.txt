HB 150 Original 2026 Regular Session Bourriaque
Abstract: Authorizes the creation of a local insurance program and local insurance fund in
Cameron Parish.
Proposed law authorizes the governing authority of Cameron Parish to establish, by
ordinance, a local insurance program, to assist homeowners who reside within the parish
with offsetting homeowner and flood insurance costs. Requires that the ordinance
establishing the program provide for the administration and management of the program.
Further requires the ordinance to provide for the adoption of rules and regulations to
establish eligibility criteria to determine which homeowners qualify for program assistance.
Proposed law authorizes the parish governing authority to establish, by ordinance, a local
insurance fund to provide funding for the program. Additionally authorizes the parish
governing authority to appropriate local parish funds to the local insurance fund.
Proposed law provides that the program shall be modified or repealed as provided in the
ordinance establishing the program. Provides that program funds must be transferred to the
parish general fund if the program ceases to exist.
(Adds R.S. 33:7151)
