SLS 26RS-402 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE BILL NO. 147
BY SENATOR TALBOT
GOVERNMENTAL REGULATIONS. Provides for standards when constructing a
residential roof. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 40:1730.28.2(A)(4), relative to the State Uniform Construction Code; to
3 provide for fortified roofs on residential property; to provide for requirements and
4 standards; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 40:1730.28.2(A)(4) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
7 §1730.28.2. State Uniform Construction Code; requirements and prohibitions
8 A. The State Uniform Construction Code shall:
9 * * *
10 (4) Require that the construction of a new roof or the replacement of a
11 roof on residential property meet or exceed the fortified roof standards of the
12 Insurance Institute for Business and Home Safety.

DIGEST
SB 147 Original 2026 Regular Session Talbot
Present law provides requirements and prohibitions for the State Uniform Construction
Code.
Proposed law retains the requirements and prohibitions in present law and adds the
requirement that all new roofs and replacement roofs constructed on residential property
meet or exceed the standards of a fortified roof.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Adds R.S. 40:1730.28.2(A)(4))
