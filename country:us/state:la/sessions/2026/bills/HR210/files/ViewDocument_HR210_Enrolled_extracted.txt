HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 210
BY REPRESENTATIVE ORGERON
A RESOLUTION
To designate Wednesday, April 29, 2026, as Gulf States Renewable Energy Industries
Association Day at the state capitol and to commend the association and Project
Vanguard.
WHEREAS, the Gulf States Renewable Energy Industries Association (GSREIA)
is the trade association of member companies leading in all parts of the renewable energy
supply chain; and
WHEREAS, GSREIA members provide good Louisiana jobs, local property tax
revenue, and economic benefits to the state's local communities; and
WHEREAS, Project Vanguard is the nation's premier veteran-led network that
connects transitioning service members and veterans to meaningful careers across the entire
American energy sector from oil and gas to renewables, nuclear, and beyond; and
WHEREAS, GSREIA and Project Vanguard work with a diverse array of companies
throughout the supply chain, including manufacturers, suppliers, distributors, developers of
energy systems and components, and providers of installation, maintenance, and grid
integration services; and
WHEREAS, GSREIA and Project Vanguard support the continued deployment of
renewable energy resources, strengthening grid reliability, and contributing to long-term
economic investment and job creation in the state; and
WHEREAS, GSREIA and Project Vanguard have fostered expansion of the sector
across utility-scale, commercial, and residential energy generation and storage, which creates
jobs and supports economic development; and

WHEREAS, GSREIA and Project Vanguard promote the expansion of new energy
generation and strengthening of the region's renewable energy infrastructure; these efforts
contribute to Louisiana's energy security and resilience, particularly in the face of natural
disasters; and
WHEREAS, GSREIA includes companies across the nation and the Gulf Coast
region, from small businesses to Fortune 500 companies, and stimulates sustainable
economic growth by supporting workforce development, removing market barriers, and
promoting innovation.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby designate Wednesday, April 29, 2026, as Gulf States
Renewable Energy Industries Association Day at the state capitol; does hereby commend
Gulf States Renewable Energy Industries Association and Project Vanguard for their efforts
to promote the expansion of Louisiana's energy sector; and does hereby extend sincerest
wishes that the association continue to cultivate a prosperous future, create jobs, and bolster
innovation in the energy sector in the state and across the country.
