HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 210
BY REPRESENTATIVE ORGERON
Energy Industries Association Day at the state capitol and commends the association
and Project Vanguard
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate Wednesday, April 29, 2026, as Gulf States Renewable Energy Industries
3 Association Day at the state capitol and to commend the association and Project
4 Vanguard.
5 WHEREAS, the Gulf States Renewable Energy Industries Association (GSREIA)
6 is the trade association of member companies leading in all parts of the renewable energy
7 supply chain; and
8 WHEREAS, GSREIA members provide good Louisiana jobs, local property tax
9 revenue, and economic benefits to the state's local communities; and
10 WHEREAS, Project Vanguard is the nation's premier veteran-led network that
11 connects transitioning service members and veterans to meaningful careers across the entire
12 American energy sector from oil and gas to renewables, nuclear, and beyond; and
13 WHEREAS, GSREIA and Project Vanguard work with a diverse array of companies
14 throughout the supply chain, including manufacturers, suppliers, distributors, developers of
15 energy systems and components, and providers of installation, maintenance, and grid
16 integration services; and
17 WHEREAS, GSREIA and Project Vanguard support the continued deployment of
18 renewable energy resources, strengthening grid reliability, and contributing to long-term
19 economic investment and job creation in the state; and
Page 1 of 2

1 WHEREAS, GSREIA and Project Vanguard have fostered expansion of the sector
2 across utility-scale, commercial, and residential energy generation and storage, which creates
3 jobs and supports economic development; and
4 WHEREAS, GSREIA and Project Vanguard promote the expansion of new energy
5 generation and strengthening of the region's renewable energy infrastructure; these efforts
6 contribute to Louisiana's energy security and resilience, particularly in the face of natural
7 disasters; and
8 WHEREAS, GSREIA includes companies across the nation and the Gulf Coast
9 region, from small businesses to Fortune 500 companies, and stimulates sustainable
10 economic growth by supporting workforce development, removing market barriers, and
11 promoting innovation.
12 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
13 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby designate Wednesday, April 29, 2026, as Gulf States
14 Renewable Energy Industries Association Day at the state capitol; does hereby commend
15 Gulf States Renewable Energy Industries Association and Project Vanguard for their efforts
16 to promote the expansion of Louisiana's energy sector; and does hereby extend sincerest
17 wishes that the association continue to cultivate a prosperous future, create jobs, and bolster
18 innovation in the energy sector in the state and across the country.
