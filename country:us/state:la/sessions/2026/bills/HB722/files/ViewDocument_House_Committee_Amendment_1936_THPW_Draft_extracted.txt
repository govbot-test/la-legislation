5 415(B)(1) are"
6 AMENDMENT NO. 3
7 On page 1, after line 20, insert the following:
8 "* * *
9 §415. Operating vehicle while license is suspended; offenses in other states; record
10 of offenses given other states
11 * * *
12 B.(1)(a) Any period of suspension or revocation shall automatically be
13 extended for a period of one year from the date the licensee would otherwise have
14 been entitled to apply for a new license upon his conviction for any offense involving
15 the operation of a motor vehicle committed during such period. No driver shall use
16 a license issued to him in another state or the privilege of a nonresident to drive a
17 motor vehicle in this state, upon receiving notice of his conviction, or of the entry of
18 a plea of guilty and sentence thereupon, or of the forfeiture of bail in another state
19 of federal jurisdiction for any offense, which if committed in this state, would be
20 grounds for suspension or revocation of the license.
21 (b) Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, a person's
22 driver's license shall be automatically reinstated upon full payment of all fees
23 assessed by the Department of Public Safety and Corrections, office of motor
24 vehicles, and proof of payment of any fines, as contemplated by R.S. 32:393, or any
25 other provision under this Title, that resulted in the suspension, cancellation, or
26 revocation of a driver's license.
27 * * *"
