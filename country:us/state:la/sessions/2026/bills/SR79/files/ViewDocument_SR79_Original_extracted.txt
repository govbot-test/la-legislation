SLS 26RS-2085 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 79
BY SENATOR BARROW
SPECIAL DAY/WEEK/MONTH. Designates Wednesday, April 15, 2026, as Child Care
Association of Louisiana Day at the Louisiana State Capitol.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate Wednesday, April 15, 2026, as Child Care Association of Louisiana Day at the
3 Louisiana State Capitol and to commend childcare business owners, early childhood
4 educators, and support staff for their outstanding work.
5 WHEREAS, the Child Care Association of Louisiana is the longest-standing,
6 statewide nonprofit organization and represents the interests of licensed childcare providers
7 and the children they serve; and
8 WHEREAS, the mission of the Child Care Association of Louisiana is to educate,
9 advocate, and collaborate to build a premier and proactive childcare industry for Louisiana
10 families; and
11 WHEREAS, the Child Care Association of Louisiana strives to improve the quality
12 and availability of childcare services by calling attention to the importance of high quality
13 childcare services for all children and families within the state; and
14 WHEREAS, Louisiana's future depends on the quality of the early childhood
15 experiences provided to our children; and
16 WHEREAS, providing high quality early childcare services represents a worthy
17 commitment to the future of Louisiana children; and
18 WHEREAS, Tara Emery, president of the Child Care Association of Louisiana, has

1 stated that "Every child should have access to a high quality early childhood educational
2 program and be prepared to enter kindergarten ready to learn", and it is most fitting to
3 recognize both her and the association for their outstanding efforts to achieve this mission.
4 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
5 does hereby designate Wednesday, April 15, 2026, as Child Care Association of Louisiana
6 Day at the Louisiana State Capitol; does hereby commend childcare business owners, early
7 childhood educators, and support staff for their incredible efforts; and does hereby urge all
8 citizens of our great state to recognize licensed childcare providers for their important work.
