Section 1:
SLS 26RS-251 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY SENATOR ALLAIN
COURTS. Provides for the territorial jurisdiction of the Jeanerette City Court. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 13:1952.2, relative to the city court of Jeanerette; to provide relative to the
3 territorial jurisdiction of the court; to provide relative to concurrent jurisdiction with
4 the justice of the peace in certain circumstances; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 13:1952.2 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
7 §1952.2 City court of Jeanerette; territorial jurisdiction; effect on justice of
8 peace
9 The territorial jurisdiction of the city court of Jeanerette shall extend
10 throughout the portions of Iberia Parish designated as Precincts 10-1, 10-4, 11-
11 6, 12-1, 12-2, 12-3, 12-4 and the portion of Precinct 11-1 west of the shoreline of
12 Lake Fausse Point. In such portions of those precincts that are outside of the
13 city of Jeanerette, the court shall have jurisdiction concurrent with the justice
14 of the peace in those cases in which the justice of the peace would have
15 jurisdiction.
