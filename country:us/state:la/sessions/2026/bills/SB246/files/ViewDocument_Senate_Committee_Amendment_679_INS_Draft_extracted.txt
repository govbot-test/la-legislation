SENATE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
by Senator Luneau
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 2, at the beginning of line 3, change "A." to "A.(1)"
3 AMENDMENT NO. 2
4 On page 2, between lines 7 and 8, insert the following:
5 "(2) "Automated decision system" as used in this Section means any
6 computational process derived from machine learning, statistical modeling, data
7 analytics, or artificial intelligence that issues simplified output, including but
8 not limited to a score, classification, or recommendation, that is used to assist
9 or replace human discretionary decision making and materially impacts a
10 natural person."
11 AMENDMENT NO. 3
12 On page 3, between lines 1 and 2, insert "(b) Any adverse determination shall be signed
13 by a licensed physician who personally reviewed the medical record and is responsible
14 for the clinical judgment."
15 AMENDMENT NO. 4
16 On page 2, at the beginning of line 29, change "D.(1)" to "D.(1)(a)"
17 AMENDMENT NO. 5
18 On page 3, delete lines 15 through 17
19 AMENDMENT NO. 6
20 On page 3, line 18, change "(e)" to "(d)"
21 AMENDMENT NO. 7
22 On page 3, between lines 20 and 21, insert the following:
23 "(3)(a) A health insurance issuer shall disclose to the enrollee and the
24 department when an artificial intelligence or automated decision system was
25 used in any part of a coverage determination or utilization review.
26 (b) The health insurance issuer shall document the extent to which any
27 artificial intelligence or automated decision system influenced the
28 determination."
29 AMENDMENT NO. 8
30 On page 3, between lines 22 and 23, insert "(2) Any adverse determination in which an
31 artificial intelligence or automated decision system materially contributed to the
32 determination shall be presumed invalid unless the health insurance issuer
33 demonstrates that the determination was independently reached through documented
34 clinical judgment without reliance upon algorithmic output."
35 AMENDMENT NO. 9
36 On page 3, at the beginning of line 23, change "(2)" to "(3)"
37 AMENDMENT NO. 10
38 On page 3, between lines 25 and 26, insert the following:
Page 1 of 2
SCASB246 3275 679
1 "F.(1) An entity subject to this Section shall allow the commissioner to
2 inspect and audit the artificial intelligence or automated decision system for
3 compliance with this Section and review policies and procedures for how the
4 artificial intelligence or automated decision system is used in the determination
5 process.
6 (2) The commissioner may require submission and independent review
7 of any artificial intelligence or automated decision system used in utilization
8 review.
9 (3) Upon request of the commissioner, a health insurance issuer shall
10 disclose the data sources, training parameters, and validation methods used to
11 develop any artificial intelligence or automated decision system used in coverage
12 determinations.
13 (4) The insurer shall pay for any independent review that the
14 commissioner deems necessary."
