2 On page 3, delete line 24 and insert in lieu thereof "artificial intelligence or automated
3 decision system, the insurer shall not use an artificial intelligence or an automated
4 decision system in any"
5 AMENDMENT NO. 23
6 On page 3, between lines 25 and 26, insert the following:
7 "F.(1) An entity subject to this Section shall allow the commissioner to
8 inspect and audit the artificial intelligence or automated decision system for
9 compliance with this Section and review policies and procedures for how the
10 artificial intelligence or automated decision system is used in the determination
11 process.
12 (2) The commissioner may require submission and independent review
13 of any artificial intelligence or automated decision system used in utilization
14 review.
15 (3) Upon request of the commissioner, a health insurance issuer shall
16 disclose the data sources, training parameters, and validation methods used to
17 develop any artificial intelligence or automated decision system used in coverage
18 determinations.
19 (4) The health insurance issuer shall pay for any independent review that
20 the commissioner deems necessary."
21 AMENDMENT NO. 24
22 On page 4, line 4, after "request," and before "review" insert "to"

24 On page 4, line 5, after "intelligence" and before "as" insert "or an automated decision
25 system"
26 AMENDMENT NO. 26
27 On page 4, after line 6, insert the following:
28 "Section 2. The provisions of this Act shall apply to any new policy, contract,
29 program, or health coverage plan issued on or after January 1, 2027. Any policy, contract,
30 or health coverage plan in effect prior to January 1, 2027, shall convert to conform to the
31 provisions of this Act on or before the renewal date, but no later than January 1, 2028."
