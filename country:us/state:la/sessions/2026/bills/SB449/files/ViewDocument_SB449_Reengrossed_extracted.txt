3 and goods by public entities; to provide for the purchase of certain non-new rail
4 equipment; to provide for the purchase of related parts by a railroad operated or
5 owned by a political subdivision; to provide for exemptions; and to provide for
6 related matters.
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
8 Section 1. R.S. 38:2212.1(O) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
9 §2212.1. Advertisement and letting to lowest responsible bidder; materials and
10 supplies; exemptions
11 * * *

13 (1) The purchase of animals trained to perform special tasks, including but
14 not limited to narcotics detection, bomb detection, arson investigation, and rescue
15 techniques by the following public entities:
16 (1)(a) Any local law enforcement agency for the principal purpose of aiding
17 in the detection of criminal activity, enforcement of laws, or apprehension of

DIGEST
SB 449 Reengrossed 2026 Regular Session Duplessis
Present law provides for requirements for advertisement and letting to lowest responsible
bidder for materials and supplies and exemptions of purchases paid out of public funds.
Present law further provides for an exemption for animals trained to perform special tasks,
including narcotics and bomb detection, arson investigation, and rescue techniques by law
enforcement or any local public safety agency for specific services.
Proposed law provides for exemptions for a political subdivision that owns or operates a
railroad for the purchase of the following, whether non-new or used, and its component
parts: locomotives, on-track equipment, and other rolling stock, and track maintenance
equipment and its attachments.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 38:2212.1(O))
