HLS 26RS-3999 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 181
BY REPRESENTATIVE MELERINE
COMMENDATIONS: Commends Louisiana State University at Shreveport on the success
of its athletic program
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Louisiana State University at Shreveport for the success of its athletic
3 program.
4 WHEREAS, the Louisiana State University at Shreveport (LSUS) baseball team
5 completed its 2025 season by winning the NAIA World Series and completing the only
6 perfect season in college baseball history; and
7 WHEREAS, the team won its first seven games in 2026 and stretched its consecutive
8 game win streak to sixty-six straight games, which is the longest win streak in college
9 baseball history; and
10 WHEREAS, the LSUS men's and women's basketball teams each won Red River
11 Athletic Conference titles, and the men advanced to the Sweet 16 of the NAIA Men's
12 Basketball National Championship tournament; and
13 WHEREAS, the LSUS men's and women's soccer teams each won Red River
14 Athletic Conference titles; and
15 WHEREAS the LSUS men's soccer team advanced to the NAIA Men's Soccer
16 National Championship tournament; and
17 WHEREAS the LSUS weightlifting team finished its season ranked second
18 nationally; and
19 WHEREAS, Louisiana State University at Shreveport is most deserving of
20 recognition for the incredible achievements of its athletic program.
