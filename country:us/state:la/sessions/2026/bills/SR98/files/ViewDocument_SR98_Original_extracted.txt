SLS 26RS-1862 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 98
BY SENATOR HENSGENS
Renewable Energy Day at the Louisiana State Capitol.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To recognize Tuesday, April 21, 2026, as Louisiana Renewable Energy Day at the Louisiana
3 State Capitol.
4 WHEREAS, Louisiana boasts impressive supplies of onshore and offshore energy
5 resources; and
6 WHEREAS, Louisiana's natural resources continue to provide thousands of jobs and
7 billions of dollars in economic development benefits; and
8 WHEREAS, according to the University of Louisiana at Lafayette, Louisiana's rich
9 history and established expertise in energy innovation and development will pave the way
10 for new emerging industries; and
11 WHEREAS, renewable energy has become increasingly affordable and efficient in
12 recent years, and can provide significant economic and environmental benefits to our state;
13 and
14 WHEREAS, according to the Solar Energy Industries Association, Louisiana's solar
15 energy resources are expected to grow by more than ten-fold in the next few years; and
16 WHEREAS, solar growth in Louisiana could bring over sixteen billion dollars in
17 economic development and over four hundred thirteen thousand jobs over the next decade;
18 and
Page 1 of 2

1 WHEREAS, wind energy is an abundant and growing source of renewable energy
2 that can create jobs and economic opportunities in Louisiana; and
3 WHEREAS, Louisiana's maritime and oil and gas industries were instrumental in
4 building the United States' first offshore wind farm; and
5 WHEREAS, battery storage technology is improving rapidly and can play a critical
6 role in integrating renewable energy sources into our electric grid and creating new jobs in
7 the energy sector; and
8 WHEREAS, hydrogen is a versatile energy carrier that can be produced from
9 renewable sources and used in a variety of applications, including transportation and
10 industrial processes, creating new economic opportunities in Louisiana; and
11 WHEREAS, the vast majority of new power plant facilities proposed in Louisiana
12 are renewable energy projects, spanning thirty parishes; and
13 WHEREAS, the development of renewable energy and clean energy can create
14 good-paying jobs and support local economies in Louisiana, particularly in rural and coastal
15 areas that have weathered significant economic challenges; and
16 WHEREAS, supporting renewable energy and clean energy jobs can help diversify
17 our state's economy and position Louisiana as a leader in the transition to a clean energy
18 future; and
19 WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper for the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana to
20 recognize Louisiana Renewable Energy Day.
21 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
22 does hereby recognize Tuesday, April 21, 2026, as Louisiana Renewable Energy Day at the
23 Louisiana State Capitol.
The original instrument and the following digest, which constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument, were prepared by Senate Legislative Services.
The keyword, summary, and digest do not constitute part of the law or proof
or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
