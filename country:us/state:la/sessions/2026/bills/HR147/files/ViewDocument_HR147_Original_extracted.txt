2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 147
BY REPRESENTATIVE BOUDREAUX
of J.L. "Joseph" Richard
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To express the condolences of the House of Representatives upon the death of J.L. "Joseph"
3 Richard.
4 WHEREAS, it is with immense sadness and profound regret that the House of
5 Representatives of the Legislature of Louisiana has learned of the passing, on April 7, 2026,
6 of J.L. "Joseph" Richard; and
7 WHEREAS, Mr. Richard was a native and lifelong resident of Carencro, Louisiana,
8 a community he loved deeply and served with unwavering dedication throughout his life;
9 he was a devout Catholic and faithful parishioner of St. Peter Roman Catholic Church, where
10 he served as an usher for many years; and
11 WHEREAS, Mr. Richard was devoted to public service and honorably served for
12 thirty-seven years as a town councilman for the city of Carencro, where he left a lasting
13 impact on the community he loved; his commitment to civic duty and passion for helping
14 others established him as a respected and admired figure in Carencro; and
15 WHEREAS, Mr. Richard was a co-founder of the Louisiana Independent Dealers
16 Association and served on the state board under Governor Edwin Edwards; he also served
17 as police commissioner and was a member of numerous organizations, and he always strived
18 to make a difference in the lives of those around him; and
19 WHEREAS, Mr. Richard found the greatest joy in the time he spent with his family;
20 he loved hosting barbecues and treasured every moment spent with his beloved
21 grandchildren; and
Page 1 of 2
HLS 26RS-3420 ORIGINAL
HR NO. 147
1 WHEREAS, J.L. "Joseph" Richard is survived by his wife of seventy-two years,
2 Rosa Boudreaux Richard; children, Judy Johnfore (Joseph "TJ"), Kenneth Richard, Larry
3 Richard, and Jimmie Richard (Brandy); five grandchildren; and fifteen great-grandchildren;
4 he will be deeply missed, but the legacy of his strong faith, servant's heart, and lifelong
5 dedication to his community and family shall live on in perpetuity in the hearts and minds
6 of those who were privileged to have known and loved him.
7 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
8 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby express heartfelt condolences on the death of
9 J.L. "Joseph" Richard and does hereby extend sincere wishes that his family, friends, and
10 loved ones find comfort in their shared memories of his beautiful life.
