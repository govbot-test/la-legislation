HLS 26RS-1383 ORIGINAL
HB NO. 957
Present law requires the sheriff who is proposing the transfer of the prisoner to notify the
court that set bail for the prisoner at least 72 hours before the transfer occurs if the prisoner
is not under sentence to the Dept. of Public Safety and Corrections (DPS&C).
Proposed law amends present law to remove the requirement that notification be provided
to the court at least 72 hours before the transfer.
(Amends R.S. 15:706(A)(2))
