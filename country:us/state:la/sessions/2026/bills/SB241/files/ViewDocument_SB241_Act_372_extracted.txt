2026 Regular Session ACT No. 372 ENROLLED
SENATE BILL NO. 241
BY SENATORS HODGES, BARROW, DUPLESSIS, EDMONDS, MYERS AND
WHEAT
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 22:1674.1(A)(22), 1706(H)(12), and 1807.16(D)(5), relative to requiring the
3 inclusion of certain licensing numbers in written communications; to regulate
4 insurance adjusters, public adjusters, and insurance appraisers; and to provide for
5 related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 22:1674.1(A)(22), 1706(H)(12), and 1807.16(D)(5) are hereby
8 enacted to read as follows:
9 §1674.1. Standards of conduct; acknowledgment required
10 A. The following standards of conduct shall be binding on all claims
11 adjusters:
12 * * *
13 (22) An adjuster shall include his Louisiana license number in all
14 electronic communications to the insured other than text messages composed
15 and sent as part of his business or employment.
16 * * *
17 §1706. Standards of conduct of public adjuster
18 * * *
19 H. Public adjusters shall also adhere to all of the following general
20 requirements:
21 * * *
22 (12) A public adjuster shall include his Louisiana license number in all
23 electronic communications to the insured other than text messages composed
24 and sent as part of his business or employment.
25 * * *
