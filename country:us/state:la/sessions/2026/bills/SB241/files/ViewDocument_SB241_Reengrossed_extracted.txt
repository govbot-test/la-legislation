DIGEST
SB 241 Reengrossed 2026 Regular Session Hodges
Proposed law provides that any person required to be licensed as an insurance producer,
claims adjuster, public adjuster, or insurance consultant must include his La. license number
or National Producer Number and his contact information in all marketing and other
communications, other than text messages, involving any activity for which his license is
required. Proposed law further provides for the minimum type size and required location of
the license number.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Adds R.S. 22:41.2(D), 1674.1(A)(22), 1706(H)(12), and 1807.16(D)(5) and R.S.)
Summary of Amendments Adopted by Senate
Committee Amendments Proposed by Senate Committee on Insurance to the original
bill
1. Requires certain licensees to include their Louisiana license number or
National Producer Number and contact information in all marketing or
written communications other than text messages.
2. Exempts text messages from requirements.
Senate Floor Amendments to engrossed bill
1. Removes requirement that a contractor include his Louisiana license number
in all written communications sent as part of his business or employment.
