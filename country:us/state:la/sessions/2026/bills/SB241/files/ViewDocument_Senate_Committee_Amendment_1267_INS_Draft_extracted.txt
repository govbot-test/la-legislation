SCASB241 3275 1267
SENATE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
by Senator Hodges
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, at the beginning of line 2, after "To enact" delete "R.S. 22:1674.1(A)(22)," and
3 insert "R.S. 22:41.2(D), 1674.1(A)(22),"
4 AMENDMENT NO. 2
5 On page 1, at the beginning of line 7, after "Section 1." delete "R.S. 22:1674.1(A)(22)," and
6 insert "R.S. 22:41.2(D), 1674.1(A)(22),
7 AMENDMENT NO. 3
8 On page 1, between line 8 and line 9, insert the following:
9 "§41.2. Submission of contact information
10 * * *
11 D. A person required to be licensed pursuant to R.S. 22:1543, 1663,
12 1693, or 1808.1 shall include his Louisiana license number or National Producer
13 Number and his contact information including name, mailing address, phone
14 number, and electronic mailing address in all marketing and other
15 communications, other than text messages, involving any activity for which his
16 license is required. The license number shall be in a type size that is no smaller
17 than the largest of any telephone number, street address, or email address of the
18 person or entity included in marketing material or other communications. The
19 license number of an individual licensee shall appear adjacent to or on the line
20 immediately below the individual's name or title.
21 * * *"
