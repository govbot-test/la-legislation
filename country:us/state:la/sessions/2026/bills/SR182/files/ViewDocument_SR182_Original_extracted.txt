SLS 26RS-4584 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 182
BY SENATOR CLOUD
Lady Trojans track and field team on an outstanding 2026 season by winning the LHSAA
Class 5A State Track and Field Championship.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend the Alexandria Senior High School Lady Trojans track and field team on an
3 outstanding 2026 season by winning the Louisiana High School Athletic Association
4 Class 5A State Track and Field Championship and to recognize the outstanding
5 accomplishments of its players, coaches, and staff.
6 WHEREAS, the Alexandria Senior High School Lady Trojans track and field team
7 completed an outstanding 2026 season by capturing the Louisiana High School Athletic
8 Association Class 5A State Track and Field Championship at LSU's Bernie Moore Stadium
9 in Baton Rouge, Louisiana; and
10 WHEREAS, the Lady Trojans earned the Class 5A State Championship with an
11 impressive team score of 85 points, securing the school's first girls state track and field
12 championship since 2007; and
13 WHEREAS, the championship season was highlighted by exceptional individual and
14 relay performances, including state championships won by Brielle Texada in the javelin
15 throw, Nyla Stewart in the discus throw, and the Lady Trojans relay teams in both the
16 4x100-meter relay and 4x200-meter relay events; and
17 WHEREAS, additional standout performances from members of the Lady Trojans
18 included Noelle Williams earning runner-up honors in the triple jump and third place in the
Page 1 of 3
SLS 26RS-4584 ORIGINAL
SR NO. 182
1 long jump, Alyssa Skipper placing third in shot put, Ja'Nya Gray and Amari Dupar placing
2 among the top finishers in sprint events, and numerous relay teams finishing among the best
3 in the state competition; and
4 WHEREAS, the Alexandria Senior High boys team also celebrated state
5 championship success as Joseph Carter Rivet captured the Class 5A boys discus state title
6 with a dominant throw of 186 feet, 7 inches; and
7 WHEREAS, these outstanding accomplishments were achieved under the leadership
8 and guidance of the Alexandria Senior High School coaching staff, whose commitment,
9 discipline, mentorship, and dedication helped shape these student-athletes into champions
10 both on and off the field; and
11 WHEREAS, special recognition should be given to the coaches, athletic department
12 leadership, trainers, school administration, parents, and supporters whose countless hours
13 of hard work and encouragement contributed to this historic season; and
14 WHEREAS, the Alexandria Senior High School track and field teams have brought
15 tremendous pride and recognition to their school, the city of Alexandria, Rapides Parish, and
16 the state of Louisiana through their athletic excellence, perseverance, and sportsmanship.
17 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
18 does hereby commend and congratulate the Alexandria Senior High School Lady Trojans
19 track and field team upon winning the 2026 LHSAA Class 5A Girls State Track and Field
20 Championship and does further recognize the achievements of the Alexandria Senior High
21 boys track and field athletes for their outstanding performances at the state championship
22 meet.
23 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
24 expresses sincere appreciation to the players, coaching staff, athletic department, school
25 administration, and families whose support and leadership made this remarkable
26 accomplishment possible and for the pride they have brought to the state of Louisiana.
27 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to
28 Alexandria Senior High School Principal Jody Goodman, Athletic Director Thomas
29 Bachman, the coaching staff, and the student-athletes of the Alexandria Senior High School
30 track and field program.
