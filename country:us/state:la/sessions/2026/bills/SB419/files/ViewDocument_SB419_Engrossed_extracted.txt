SLS 26RS-660 ENGROSSED
2026 Regular Session
BY SENATOR KLEINPETER
HUNTERS/HUNTING. Provides for use of pre-charged pneumatic devices to take deer
during primitive firearm season. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 56:116(C), relative to pre-charged pneumatic devices; to provide
3 for taking of deer; to provide for special deer seasons; to provide for commission
4 authority; to provide for primitive firearms; to provide for minimum projectile size,
5 weight, and energy; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 56:116(C) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
8 §116. Wild birds and wild quadrupeds; special hunting seasons
9 * * *
10 C.(1) The commission may establish a special deer season for primitive
11 firearms. The taking of deer in such season shall be limited to persons who are
12 licensed in accordance with the provisions of R.S. 56:3002. The commission shall
13 adopt and promulgate rules and regulations governing such season, including the
14 type and size of weapons used, licenses required, sex of deer to be hunted, and the
15 areas included in the season.
16 (2) In addition to any other weapon restriction the commission may adopt,
17 such rules and regulations shall provide that breech for the use of the following

1 during primitive firearm season:
2 (a) Breech loading rifles with a caliber of .35 or larger may be used during
3 primitive firearms season.
4 (b) Pre-charged pneumatic devices that fire a projectile with a caliber of
5 .30 or larger, a weight of one hundred fifty grains or larger, and a minimum
6 muzzle velocity of eight hundred feet per second, or with any combination of
7 bullet weight and muzzle velocity that produces muzzle energy of at least two
8 hundred fifteen foot pounds of energy.
9 (3) The taking of deer during this season primitive firearm season shall be
10 limited to still hunting and may be allowed in all or some areas of the state where
11 deer hunting is now or shall in the future be allowed.
12 (2)(4) Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, during
13 primitive firearms season any licensee may use a magnified scope on any weapon
14 authorized for use during the primitive firearms season.
15 * * *
The original instrument and the following digest, which constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument, were prepared by Senate Legislative Services.
The keyword, summary, and digest do not constitute part of the law or proof
or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
DIGEST
SB 419 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Kleinpeter
Present law provides for commission authority to establish special deer hunting seasons,
including primitive firearm season, and to limit the type of weapons used to take deer.
Proposed law retains present law and requires that commission rules allow for taking deer
with pre-charged pneumatic devices that fire a projectile of at least .30 caliber diameter, 150
grains weight, and 800 feet per second muzzle velocity, or any combination of weight and
velocity that produces 215 ft-lb minimum energy.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 56:116(C))
