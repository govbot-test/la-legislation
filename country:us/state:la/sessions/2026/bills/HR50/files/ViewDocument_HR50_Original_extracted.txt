1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate Tuesday, March 24, 2026, as Geaux Far Day at the capitol and to commend
3 Geaux Far Louisiana for its contributions to early childhood education.
4 WHEREAS, Geaux Far Louisiana was established in 2021 as a cross-sector coalition
5 to advocate for the needs of Louisiana's children from prenatal through age four, with
6 intentional centering of parents and early childhood providers; and
7 WHEREAS, Geaux Far believes in a unified early childhood system and has
8 identified five goals to ensure families have access to seamless and high-quality early
9 childhood development, health, and educational services; and
10 WHEREAS, Geaux Far Louisiana collaborates with Louisiana-based partner
11 organizations dedicated to carrying out specific objectives within each goal area that align
12 with the work they are already doing; and
13 WHEREAS, Louisiana families want their children to be healthy and happy and to
14 thrive; healthy families and young children serve as the backbone of thriving communities
15 and foundation of economic vitality for our state; and
16 WHEREAS, Geaux Far Louisiana aims to shape policies, advocate for change, and
17 improve outcomes for young learners statewide by engaging parents, educators,
18 policymakers, and community leaders; and
