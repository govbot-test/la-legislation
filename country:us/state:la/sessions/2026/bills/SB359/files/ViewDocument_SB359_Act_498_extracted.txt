2026 Regular Session ACT No. 498 ENROLLED
SENATE BILL NO. 359
BY SENATOR JACKSON-ANDREWS
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 46:1053(GG), relative to hospital service districts; to provide for terms of
3 office for certain hospital service district commissioners; and to provide for related
4 matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 46:1053(GG) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
7 §1053. Commission; qualification of members; appointment; vacancies;
8 compensation; removal of commissioners; certain powers
9 * * *
10 GG. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, at the
11 expiration of the terms of office of the commissioners serving on August 1, 2026,
12 of a hospital service district in a parish having a population in excess of twenty-
13 five thousand but not more than twenty-six thousand persons based on the latest
14 federal decennial census, the successors shall be appointed for a term of four
15 years in the same manner as the predecessor appointees were selected, and no
16 commissioner shall serve for more than two consecutive four year terms.
PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
GOVERNOR OF THE STATE OF LOUISIANA
