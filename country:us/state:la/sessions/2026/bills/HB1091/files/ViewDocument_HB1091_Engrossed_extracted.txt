HB 1091 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Boudreaux
Abstract: Provides that fire departments shall obtain identification numbers from the
National Emergency Response System and submit reports of all incidents.
Present law provides that fire departments shall obtain fire department identification
numbers from the state fire marshal.
Proposed law changes present law to provide that a fire department shall instead obtain that
number from the National Emergency Response Information System.
Present law provides that fire departments shall submit structural fire reports electronically
to the fire marshal.
Proposed law changes this requirement to submit reports of all incidents to the fire marshal.
(Amends R.S. 40:1567(A)(1)(a) and (B))
