INSURANCE: Prohibits financial incentives or penalties to encourage healthcare providers

1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 22:1008.1, relative to prohibited health insurance incentives; to prohibit the
3 payment of a financial reward to a healthcare provider to encourage vaccination; to
4 prohibit the denial of a claim or other penalties to encourage vaccination; to provide
5 for definitions; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 22:1008.1 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §1008.1. Health coverage plans; vaccination; prohibited provider incentives
9 A. A health coverage plan shall not pay or offer to pay to any healthcare
10 provider an incentive, bonus, or other amount beyond the contracted reimbursement
11 rate for a covered healthcare service with the intent to, directly or indirectly,
12 influence, persuade, or encourage the healthcare provider to administer any
13 vaccination.
14 B. A health coverage plan shall not deny or otherwise refuse to reimburse
15 any claim for a covered healthcare service submitted by a healthcare provider or
16 impose any other penalty on a healthcare provider with the intent to, directly or
17 indirectly, influence, persuade, or encourage the healthcare provider to administer
18 any vaccination.
19 C. For purposes of this Section, "health coverage plan" means any hospital,
20 health, or medical expense insurance policy, hospital or medical service contract,

HB 452 Original 2026 Regular Session Amedee
Abstract: Prohibits health insurers from offering financial incentives or imposing penalties
to encourage healthcare providers to administer vaccinations.
Proposed law prohibits a health coverage plan from paying or offering to pay to any
healthcare provider an incentive, bonus, or other amount beyond the contracted
reimbursement rate for a covered healthcare service with the intent to, directly or indirectly,
influence, persuade, or encourage the healthcare provider to administer any vaccination.
Proposed law prohibits a health coverage plan from denying or otherwise refusing to
reimburse any claim for a covered healthcare service submitted by a healthcare provider or
imposing any other penalty on a healthcare provider with the intent to, directly or indirectly,
influence, persuade, or encourage the healthcare provider to administer any vaccination.
Proposed law defines "health coverage plan".
(Adds R.S. 22:1008.1)
