ACT No. 163
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE BILL NO. 547
BY REPRESENTATIVES THOMAS AND AMEDEE
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 18:1461.3(C)(5), relative to election offenses; to provide that no person shall
3 knowingly, willfully, or intentionally photograph, record, or otherwise reproduce
4 voter registration information in precinct registers; to provide for penalties; and to
5 provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 18:1461.3(C)(5) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §1461.3. Election offenses affecting election officials or watchers; penalties
9 * * *
10 C. No person shall knowingly, willfully, or intentionally:
11 * * *
12 (5) Photograph, record, or otherwise reproduce voter registration information
13 in precinct registers, except while performing his duties as a clerk of court or an
14 employee of a clerk, an election day or early voting commissioner, a registrar of
15 voters or an employee of a registrar, or the secretary of state or an employee of the
16 secretary of state.
17 * * *
