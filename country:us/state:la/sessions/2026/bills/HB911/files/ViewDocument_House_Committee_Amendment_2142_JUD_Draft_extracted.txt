2 On page 9, between lines 26 and 27, insert the following:
3 "§621.43. Abolition of Orleans Parish Juvenile Court; extension of terms of office;
4 jurisdiction; conferred on Forty-First Judicial District Court; transfer of cases
5 A. The Orleans Parish Juvenile Court is abolished effective December 31,
6 2026. The terms of office of the judges of the juvenile court now in office shall be
7 extended until December 31, 2026. After the effective date of this Section, no judge
8 shall be elected to the Orleans Parish Juvenile Court except to fill a vacancy and in
9 such case the term of office shall expire on December 31, 2026.
10 B. It is the express intent of this Section that the jurisdiction conferred by
11 law, particularly the Louisiana Children's Code, upon the Orleans Parish Juvenile
12 Court, shall be vested in the Forty-First Judicial District Court and shall be the same
13 as it exists on the effective date of this Section. All of its powers, its duties, and its
14 various departments or its personnel, except as provided in this Section, shall be
15 vested in the Forty-First Judicial District Court as provided in this Section and R.S.
16 13:621.45 and 621.46.
17 C. Effective January 1, 2027, all pending cases filed in the Orleans Parish
18 Juvenile Court are hereby transferred to the Forty-First Judicial District Court for the
19 parish of Orleans. All records and files of the Orleans Parish Juvenile Court shall be
20 transferred to the Forty-First Judicial District Court, which shall hear and dispose of
21 each such case with the same legal effect as if it had been instituted in that court in
22 the first instance.
23 §621.44. Forty-First Judicial District Court; initial judges; term of office; election;
24 juvenile section
25 The judges of the Forty-First Judicial District Court in office on January 1,
26 2027, shall en banc create the Juvenile Section of that court. The rule shall designate
27 not less than two judges who shall be assigned all cases involving juvenile matters
28 over which the district court has jurisdiction.
29 §621.45. Implementing legislation
30 The legislature shall enact any such other legislation necessary to effectuate
31 the provisions transferring jurisdiction over juvenile matters to the Forty-First
32 Judicial District Court."
