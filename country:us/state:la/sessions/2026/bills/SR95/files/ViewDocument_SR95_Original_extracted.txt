2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 95
BY SENATOR CATHEY
Day at the Louisiana State Capitol and to commend the state officers of Louisiana 4-H and
the Louisiana FFA Association.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate April 28, 2026, as Louisiana 4-H and FFA Day at the Louisiana State Capitol
3 and to commend the state officers of Louisiana 4-H and the Louisiana FFA
4 Association.
5 WHEREAS, Louisiana 4-H and the Louisiana FFA Association are two of the largest
6 youth leadership organizations in the state, empowering young people through hands-on
7 learning, service, and career development; and
8 WHEREAS, Louisiana 4-H, the youth development program through the LSU
9 AgCenter, engages young people in meaningful learning experiences that build leadership,
10 citizenship, and life skills across all sixty-four parishes; and
11 WHEREAS, the National FFA organization prepares and trains students for
12 leadership and careers in the science, business, and technology of agriculture, and its motto
13 "Learning to Do, Doing to Learn, Earning to Live, Living to Serve" provides members with
14 values to guide their development; and
15 WHEREAS, Louisiana 4-H and FFA members exemplify dedication, leadership,
16 integrity, and service to their schools, communities, and the state of Louisiana.
17 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
18 does hereby designate April 28, 2026, as Louisiana 4-H and FFA Day at the Louisiana State
