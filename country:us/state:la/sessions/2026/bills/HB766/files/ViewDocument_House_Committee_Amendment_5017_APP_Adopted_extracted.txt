766 by Representative Freeman
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 2, delete lines 25 through 29 in their entirety and on page 3 delete line 1 in its
3 entirety and insert the following:
4 "(4) (5) A health insurance issuer that limits the total amount paid by a
5 covered person through all cost-sharing requirements to no more than one hundred
6 dollars per filled prescription for any orally administered anti-cancer medication
7 shall be considered in compliance with this Section. For purposes of this Paragraph,
8 "cost-sharing requirements" shall include copayments, coinsurance, deductibles, and
9 any other amounts paid by the covered person for that prescription.
10 (6) A health"
11 AMENDMENT NO. 2
12 On page 3, at the end of line 8, delete "medications." and insert the following "medications
13 for high-deductible health plan policies that are qualified to be used in conjunction with a
14 health savings account, a medical savings account, or other similar programs authorized
15 pursuant to 26 U.S.C. 220 et seq."
