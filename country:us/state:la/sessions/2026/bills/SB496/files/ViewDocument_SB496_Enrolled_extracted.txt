12 §1962. Definitions
13 As used in this Chapter, the following words shall have the meaning ascribed
14 to them in this Section unless the context clearly indicates otherwise:

16 cable service over a cable system and directly, or through one or more affiliates,
17 owns a significant interest in that cable system, or that otherwise controls or is
18 responsible for, through any arrangement, the management and operation of
19 that system.
20 (2) "Carrier" means any person who transports in a transport vehicle
21 hazardous materials, freight, or passengers and includes a common, contract,
22 or private carrier.
23 (3) "Common carrier" includes all persons engaged in the transportation

5 §1973. Payment by loadable payment card or check for copper or brass materials,
6 aluminum-copper air conditioning coils, or other metals required;
7 check cashing prohibited
8 A.(1) An operator shall not enter into any cash transactions in payment for
9 the purchase of copper or brass materials, or aluminum-copper air conditioning
10 coils. After five business days from the date of the transaction, payment for copper
11 or brass materials, or aluminum-copper air conditioning coils shall be made in the
12 form of a check made payable to the name and address of the seller of the copper or
13 brass materials, or aluminum-copper air conditioning coils, or a loadable payment
14 card and shall be tendered to the seller in any of the following manners:
15 * * *
16 (2) An operator shall not enter into any cash transactions in excess of three
17 hundred dollars in payment for the purchase of metal property other than copper or
18 brass materials, or aluminum-copper air conditioning coils. Payments in excess of
19 three hundred dollars for metals other than copper or brass materials, or aluminum-
20 copper air conditioning coils shall be made in the form of a check made payable to
21 the name and address of the seller or a loadable payment card and may be tendered
22 to the seller at the time of the transaction. The operator, at his discretion, may make
23 payment by either cash or other method for transactions of three hundred dollars or
24 less for all metals other than copper or brass materials, or aluminum-copper air
25 conditioning coils.
26 * * *
27 C. Pursuant to the provisions of this Section, if an operator makes a payment
28 to the seller in the form of a check or loadable payment card, the operator shall
29 require verification of the seller's identification by a valid driver's license or similar
30 means and shall require verification of the seller's address by a current utility bill.
