2026 Regular Session
HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 83
COMMENDATIONS: Commends J. Reed Walters on the occasion of his retirement as
district attorney of the Twenty-Eighth Judicial District
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 To commend J. Reed Walters on the occasion of his retirement as district attorney of the
3 Twenty-Eighth Judicial District.
4 WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize J. Reed Walters for his dedicated service
5 to LaSalle Parish and the state of Louisiana and to commend him on his retirement as district
6 attorney of the Twenty-Eighth Judicial District; and
7 WHEREAS, Mr. Walters is the son of Charles Walters, a forty-year employee of
8 International Paper Company, and Betty Walters, who dedicated more than thirty years of
9 service as a first grade teacher at Olla Elementary School; he was the youngest of four
10 children who were raised in the Standard community near Olla, Louisiana; and
11 WHEREAS, Mr. Walters graduated from LaSalle High School, Northeast Louisiana
12 University, and the LSU Paul M. Hebert Law Center; after earning his law degree, he set up
13 a law practice in his home community; and
14 WHEREAS, Mr. Walters took his first oath of office as the newly elected district
15 attorney of the Twenty-Eighth Judicial District in January of 1991; he went on to serve in
16 this capacity for thirty-six years and holds the distinction of being the second-longest serving
17 district attorney in Louisiana history; and
18 WHEREAS, throughout his tenure as district attorney, Mr. Walters prioritized the
19 protection and service of the citizens of LaSalle Parish and the fulfillment of his oath to the
20 best of his ability; among his many achievements, he delivered on his promise to be present
21 during trials and to complete most of the trial work during prosecutions; and
Page 1 of 2
HLS 26RS-4092 ORIGINAL
HCR NO. 83
1 WHEREAS, Mr. Walters served on the District Attorneys' Retirement System board
2 of trustees, Red River Law Enforcement Planning Agency board of directors, Louisiana
3 Commission on Law Enforcement and Administration of Criminal Justice, and Louisiana
4 District Attorneys Association board of directors; and
5 WHEREAS, Mr. Walters centers his life around his faith and devotion to Christ and
6 is a longtime member of Midway Baptist Church; he credits his faith as the driving force
7 behind his success as district attorney and has expressed deep gratitude for the support and
8 prayers of the members of Midway Baptist and the Christian citizens of the LaSalle Parish;
9 and
10 WHEREAS, Mr. Walters is most proud of the work he has done to help citizens in
11 need, which he considers the greatest source of satisfaction in his role as district attorney;
12 he is most deserving of recognition for his unwavering respect and guardianship of the
13 citizens of LaSalle Parish and the state of Louisiana.
14 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of Louisiana does hereby
15 commend J. Reed Walters on the occasion of his retirement as district attorney of the
16 Twenty-Eighth Judicial District; does hereby express enduring appreciation for his
17 contributions and service to the citizens of the parish and the state of Louisiana; and does
18 hereby extend sincere wishes that he remain prosperous as he begins a new chapter in life.
