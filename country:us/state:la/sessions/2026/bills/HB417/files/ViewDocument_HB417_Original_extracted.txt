HB 417 Original 2026 Regular Session Zeringue
Abstract: Increases the maximum balance of the Hazardous Waste Site Cleanup Fund from
$6M to $8M and requires annual adjustments of the maximum balance by the
percentage increases in the Consumer Price Index (CPI-U).
Present law establishes the Hazardous Waste Site Cleanup Fund (fund), which receives sums
from the hazardous waste tax and sums recovered from judgements, settlements, and
assessed civil and criminal penalties from parties responsible or potentially responsible for
active or abandoned site remediation or cleanup. Further requires monies in the fund to be
used for purposes which includes funding the operating expenses of inactive and abandoned
sites activities, defraying costs associated with hazardous waste sites, such as investigation,
testing, containment, quarantining, control, and cleanup, and matching funds for federal
grants.

Present law limits the balance of the fund from exceeding $6M. Further requires the
treasurer to deposit all excess funds into the Environmental Trust Dedicated Fund Account.
Proposed law retains present law, but increases the maximum balance of the fund from $6M
to $8M and, beginning Jan. 1, 2027, and thereafter, requires the state treasurer to annually
adjust the maximum balance by the percentage increase in the CPI-U for the previous
calendar year.
Effective July 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 30:2205(A)(1))
