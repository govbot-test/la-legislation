HB 1033 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Bacala
Abstract: Adds certain other facilities, installations, platforms, and equipment to the
definition of "critical infrastructure".
Present law (R.S. 14:61 and R.S. 51:3052) defines the term "critical infrastructure".
Proposed law retains present law and adds certain facilities, areas, equipment, and structures
to the definition of "critical infrastructure".
Effective upon signature of governor or lapse of time for gubernatorial action.
(Amends R.S. 14:61(B)(intro. para.) and (1)(a)(v) and R.S. 51:3052(intro. para.) and (2);
Adds R.S. 14:61(B)(1)(a)(viii)-(x))
