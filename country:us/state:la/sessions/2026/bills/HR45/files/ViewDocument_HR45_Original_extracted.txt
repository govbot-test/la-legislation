HLS 26RS-1786 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 45
BY REPRESENTATIVE MIKE JOHNSON
pastor of the Church of Pentecost in Ball, Louisiana
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Pastor Aaron "AJ" Holloway upon his installation as lead pastor of the Church
3 of Pentecost in Ball, Louisiana.
4 WHEREAS, it is most fitting and proper to recognize individuals whose faith,
5 leadership, and service strengthen their communities; and
6 WHEREAS, Pastor Aaron "AJ" Holloway has been installed as lead pastor of the
7 Church of Pentecost in Ball, Louisiana, in 2026; he assumes leadership of a congregation
8 that has long served the spiritual and community life of Rapides Parish and Central
9 Louisiana; and
10 WHEREAS, Pastor Holloway and his wife, Amanda Holloway, are committed to
11 strengthening families, building meaningful relationships within the church and community,
12 and encouraging others through faith and service; and
13 WHEREAS, Pastor Holloway succeeds Bishop Lonnie Weidner, whose faithful
14 leadership helped guide and grow the Church of Pentecost and left a lasting legacy within
15 the congregation and the community; and
16 WHEREAS, Pastor Holloway is most deserving of the highest recognition as he
17 begins a new chapter of service and leadership, and the citizens of Rapides Parish are blessed
18 by the continued ministry of the Church of Pentecost.
19 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
20 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Pastor Aaron "AJ" Holloway upon his
21 installation as lead pastor of the Church of Pentecost in Ball, Louisiana, and does hereby
22 extend best wishes for continued success in his ministry.
