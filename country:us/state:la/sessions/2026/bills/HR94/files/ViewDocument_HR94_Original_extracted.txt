HLS 26RS-710 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 94
BY REPRESENTATIVE COATES
SPECIAL DAY/WEEK/MONTH: Designates November 16, 2026, as Sanfilippo Syndrome
Awareness Day in Louisiana
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate November 16, 2026, as Sanfilippo Syndrome Awareness Day in Louisiana.
3 WHEREAS, Sanfilippo Syndrome, also known as MPS III, is a rare and devastating
4 genetic disorder that primarily affects children and causes progressive neurological decline,
5 severe cognitive impairment, and reduced life expectancy; and
6 WHEREAS, World Sanfilippo Syndrome Awareness Day is observed annually on
7 November sixteenth and aims to raise awareness about this rare disease, support affected
8 families, and encourage research into treatments and a cure; and
9 WHEREAS, educating the community about Sanfilippo Syndrome is an integral
10 aspect of the efforts to foster understanding, compassion, and support for those living with
11 the condition and their families; and
12 WHEREAS, efforts to advance research and increase public awareness play a critical
13 role in the search for effective treatments and are essential to improving the quality of life
14 for individuals with Sanfilippo Syndrome; and
15 WHEREAS, on Sanfilippo Syndrome Awareness Day, Louisiana honors the strength
16 and resilience of individuals and families affected by this genetic disorder and recognizes
17 the tireless efforts of the researchers, healthcare professionals, and advocates who have
18 dedicated themselves to making a difference.
19 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
20 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby designate November 16, 2026, as Sanfilippo Syndrome
