9 Section 1. R.S. 40:1381.1 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
10 §1381.1. Public awareness; discharge of firearm
11 A. The Department of Public Safety and Corrections, office of state police,
12 shall conduct public awareness activities designed to promote the prevention of
13 discharging firearms into the air in public areas where discharging a firearm is
14 prohibited. The public awareness activities shall include but not be limited to public
15 service announcements via radio, television, and social media, information kits and
16 brochures, promotion of relevant telephone hotlines, and provision of information,
17 regarding the danger of, and criminal penalties for, discharging a firearm into the air
18 in public areas where prohibited.

HB 364 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Phelps
Abstract: Directs the Dept. of Public Safety and Corrections (DPS&C), office of state
police, to conduct public safety messaging regarding discharging firearms into the
air.
Proposed law requires DPS&C to conduct public safety messaging regarding the dangers of
and criminal penalties for discharging firearms into the air.
Proposed law requires DPS&C to provide public service materials to municipal police
departments for further dissemination to the public.
Proposed law allows DPS&C to collaborate with the La. Dept. of Health and municipal
police departments.
Proposed law requires DPS&C and municipal police to use funding that comes from a source
already allocated for dissemination of public awareness activities.
Proposed law provides for the time of year for required messaging to the public.
(Adds R.S. 40:1381.1)
