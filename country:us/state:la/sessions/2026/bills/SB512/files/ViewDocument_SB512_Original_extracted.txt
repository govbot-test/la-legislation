Section 1:
SLS 26RS-1507 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY SENATOR CATHEY
AUTOMOBILE INSURANCE. Creates a mandatory right of appraisal for automobile
repairs. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 22:1289.2, relative to a mandatory right of appraisal for automobile repairs;
3 to provide for a mandatory right of appraisal; to provide for the appraisal process;
4 and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 22:1289.2 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
7 §1289.2. Automobile insurance policies; mandatory right to appraisal
8 A. All automobile insurance polices issued in this state shall include a
9 right to appraisal clause, allowing policyholders to resolve disputes over claim
10 valuations through an independent appraisal process.
11 B. The right to appraisal process shall consist of all the following:
12 (1) One appraiser chosen by the insurer.
13 (2) One appraiser chosen by the policyholder.
14 (3) One neutral umpire.
15 C. The right to appraisal process shall follow the processes provided in
16 R.S. 22:1807.13 and 1807.19.
