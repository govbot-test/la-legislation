DIGEST
SB 435 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Myers
Proposed law creates the Louisiana Commission of Fatherhood Engagement within the
office of the governor as a continuation of the Task Force on Fatherhood Engagement
initially created by SCR No. 9 of the 2025 R.S.
Proposed law provides for the membership of the commission, terms of the commission
members, and duties of the commission.
Proposed law requires the commission to submit an annual report to the legislature.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Adds R.S. 46:261)
