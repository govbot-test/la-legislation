HB 474 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Young
Abstract: Creates an individual income tax checkoff for donations to the Grambling
University National Alumni Association.
Proposed law creates an income tax checkoff with which an individual can donate all or a
portion of his state income tax refund to the Grambling University National Alumni
Association.
Proposed law requires donated monies to be administered by the secretary of the Dept. of
Revenue and disbursed to the Grambling University National Alumni Association in
accordance with present law (R.S. 47:120.37).
Proposed law applies to taxable periods beginning on or after Jan. 1, 2027.
(Adds R.S. 47:120.401)
