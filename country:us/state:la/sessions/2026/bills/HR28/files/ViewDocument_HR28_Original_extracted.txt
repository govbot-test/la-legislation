HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 28
BY REPRESENTATIVE DICKERSON
winning the 132-pound weight class 2026 state championship at the Louisiana High
School Athletic Association Wrestling State Tournament
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Live Oak High School wrestler Laken Griffin on winning the 132-pound
3 weight class 2026 state championship at the Louisiana High School Athletic
4 Association Wrestling State Tournament.
5 WHEREAS, Laken Griffin captured the 2026 state title in the 132-pound weight
6 class at the LHSAA Wrestling State Tournament held at the Brookshire Grocery Arena in
7 Bossier City, Louisiana; and
8 WHEREAS, Laken finished the tournament with five wins and zero losses and won
9 all of her matches by pin; she finished the season with an incredible record of forty-eight
10 wins and zero losses; and
11 WHEREAS, in addition to winning the state title, Ms. Griffin was victorious in her
12 weight class at the Louisiana Classic, which was held at the Lamar Dixon Expo Center in
13 Gonzales, Louisiana; and
14 WHEREAS, Laken Griffin is most deserving of the highest recognition for her
15 dominance in competition this year.
16 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
17 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Live Oak High School wrestler Laken
18 Griffin on winning the 132-pound weight class 2026 state championship at the Louisiana
19 High School Athletic Association Wrestling State Tournament and does hereby extend
