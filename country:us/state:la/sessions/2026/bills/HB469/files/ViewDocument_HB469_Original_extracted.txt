PHARMACISTS: Provides relative to the pharmacy education support fee collected by the

1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 37:1207(A)(2)(a)(i) and (b) and 1230(A)(2)(a)(i) and (b),
3 relative to the pharmacy education support fee; to provide relative to licenses and
4 permits issued by the Louisiana Board of Pharmacy; to provide for the allocation of
5 the fee; to remove requirements to receive the fee; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 37:1207(A)(2)(a)(i) and (b) and 1230(A)(2)(a)(i) and (b) are hereby
8 amended and reenacted to read as follows:
9 §1207. Renewal of license, registration, and certification; expiration; reinstatement
10 A.
11 * * *
12 (2)(a)(i) The board shall assess on each pharmacist license renewal as
13 required by this Section an additional fee of one hundred dollars per year to be
14 designated as the "pharmacy education support fee". This fee shall be dedicated and
15 allocated as specified in this Paragraph to an accredited school of pharmacy of a
16 public university in this state. The board shall include on each license renewal form
17 issued to a pharmacist an optional election whereby the person may elect not to remit
18 the one hundred dollar pharmacy education support fee. The board shall also include

HB 469 Original 2026 Regular Session Jordan
Abstract: Provides for the allocation of the pharmacy education support fee.
Present law requires each pharmacy holding a pharmacy permit or a pharmacist license from
the La. Board of Pharmacy (board) to apply for a renewal and pay a renewal fee.
Present law assesses a $100 annual "pharmacy education support fee" on pharmacist license
and pharmacy permit renewals, which is remitted to an accredited school of pharmacy at a
public university.
Proposed law removes the requirement that an eligible university be public.
Present law allows a pharmacist to choose not to pay the pharmacy education support fee by
selecting an option on the license renewal form.
Proposed law directs the board to include a list of eligible schools of pharmacy on the
pharmacist license and pharmacy permit renewal forms.
Proposed law provides that Xavier of La. College of Pharmacy is eligible to receive the
pharmacy education support fee.
Proposed law adds that a licensee or permittee may select an eligible pharmacy school on
the renewal form to receive the pharmacy education support fee.
Proposed law directs the board to allocate the fee to the eligible pharmacy school selected
by the licensee or permittee.
