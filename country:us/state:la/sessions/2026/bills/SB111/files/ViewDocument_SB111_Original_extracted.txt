DIGEST
SB 111 Original 2026 Regular Session Kleinpeter
Present law provides daily take limits for black bass of genus Micropterus and crappie of
genus Pomoxis.
Proposed law retains present law and adds a minimum size limit of 14 inches and daily take
limit of 5 for black bass and a minimum size limit of 8 inches and daily take limit of 25 for
crappie within a specified geographic area including the Atchafalaya Basin and surrounding
tributaries and distributaries.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Adds R.S. 56:325(A)(13))
