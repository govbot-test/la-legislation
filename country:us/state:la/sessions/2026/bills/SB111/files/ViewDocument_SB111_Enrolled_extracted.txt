Section 1:
2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
BY SENATOR KLEINPETER
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 56:325(A)(13), relative to freshwater recreational fish; to provide for daily
3 take and minimum size limits for black bass; to provide for daily take and minimum
4 size limits for crappie; to provide for a geographic area; and to provide for related
5 matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 56:325(A)(13) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §325. Daily take, possession, and size limits; freshwater recreational fish; possession
9 of finfish filets prohibited
10 A. No person shall take in any one day more than the following of each
11 species of fish, of the designated size where specified:
12 * * *
13 (13) Within the area south of US 190 from the West Atchafalaya Basin
14 Protection Levee to the intersection of LA 1 and US 190 due north of Port Allen,
15 east of the West Atchafalaya Basin Protection Levee from US 190 to US 90,
16 north of US 90 from the West Atchafalaya Basin Protection Levee to LA 20,
17 north and west of LA 20 from US 90 to LA 1 in Thibodaux, south and west of
18 LA 1 from LA 20 to US 190, no person shall take, in any one day, more than the
19 following of the designated size:
20 (a) Black bass (Micropterus spp.), or their hybrids - minimum size twelve
21 inches total length - five.
