12 A. The legislature hereby finds and declares that it is vitally important
13 to the public safety, interest, and welfare of the state to protect Louisiana
14 residents and their residences, businesses, and other property, as well as visitors
15 to the state, by reasonably regulating the licensing of persons participating in
16 the horse racing industry in the state.
