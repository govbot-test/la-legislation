HLS 26RS-3649 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 250
BY REPRESENTATIVE MUSCARELLO
COMMENDATIONS: Commends the Tangipahoa Parish Sheriff's Office Mounted
Division and its Rodeo Queens for their service to the parish
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend the Tangipahoa Parish Sheriff's Office Mounted Division on its service to the
3 parish and to recognize the 2025 Tangipahoa Parish Sheriff's Office Mounted
4 Division Rodeo Queens.
5 WHEREAS, the Tangipahoa Parish Sheriff's Office (TPSO) Mounted Division is a
6 volunteer organization that consists of deputies who offer their time, vehicles, horse trailers,
7 and trained horses to serve Tangipahoa Parish and the surrounding area; and
8 WHEREAS, the TPSO Mounted Division volunteer deputies and their equine
9 partners serve year-round, assisting with rescue and recovery operations, providing security
10 at high school athletic events and parish fairs and festivals, and hosting the TPSO Mounted
11 Division Rodeo every September; and
12 WHEREAS, the TPSO Mounted Division Rodeo was established in 2011 as a
13 fundraiser to help volunteer deputies fund bullet-proof vests, horse tack and equipment, and
14 other costs associated with serving the parish; and
15 WHEREAS, the TPSO Mounted Division Rodeo is a Cajun Rodeo Association-
16 sanctioned rodeo event, and it has been awarded "Rodeo of the Year" four times; and
17 WHEREAS, the TPSO Mounted Division Rodeo will see over three thousand
18 spectators, in addition to its participants, over the course of the two-day rodeo; and
19 WHEREAS, the TPSO Mounted Division Rodeo Pageant began in 2016, and the
20 director of the pageant is Rebecca Borras; the pageant features five age divisions: Miss
21 Queen, Teen Miss Queen, Deb Miss Queen, Little Miss Queen, and Petite Miss Queen; and
