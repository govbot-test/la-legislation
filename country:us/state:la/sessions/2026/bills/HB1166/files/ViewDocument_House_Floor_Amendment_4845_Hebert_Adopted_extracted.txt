5 On page 1, line 7, after "exemptions;" and before "to" insert "to provide for the membership
6 of the Louisiana Real Estate Commission;"
7 AMENDMENT NO. 3
8 On page 5, line 1, change "37:1455(A)(introductory paragraph)" to "37:1432(A) and
9 1455(A)(introductory paragraph)"
10 AMENDMENT NO. 4
11 On page 5, between lines 2 and 3, insert the following:
12 "§1432. Louisiana Real Estate Commission; qualifications; terms of office
13 A. The Louisiana Real Estate Commission is hereby created within the office
14 of the governor. The commission shall consist of eleven members appointed by the
15 governor. One member shall be appointed from each of the seven supreme court
16 districts. Two members shall be appointed at large. Two members shall be
17 appointed from the following districts: one appointee from either the Fourth
18 Congressional District or the Fifth Congressional District and one appointee from
19 either the First, Second, Third, or Sixth Congressional District. One member shall
20 be appointed from each congressional district, and the remaining members shall be
21 appointed at large. Each appointment by the governor shall be submitted to the
22 Senate for confirmation.
23 * * *"
24 AMENDMENT NO. 5
25 On page 5, between lines 18 and 19, insert the following:
26 "Section 3. R.S. 37:1432(A) as amended and reenacted by Section 2 of this Act shall
27 not be construed to change the existing term of appointment for any member of the
28 Louisiana Real Estate Commission who is serving as a commissioner on the effective date
29 of this Act."
30 AMENDMENT NO. 6
31 On page 5, line 19, change "Section 3." to "Section 4."
