SLS 26RS-1151 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 73
BY SENATOR HODGES
COMMENDATIONS. Commends and congratulates Ferdie P. Genre on winning the 2026
LEDC Bob Easterly Award.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend and congratulate Fernand "Ferdie" P. Genre upon receiving the Livingston
3 Economic Development Council's Bob Easterly Award.
4 WHEREAS, the Livingston Economic Development Council established the Bob
5 Easterly Award to honor individuals who exemplify outstanding service and commitment
6 to enhancing economic development and growth in Livingston Parish; and
7 WHEREAS, the award is named in honor of Robert "Bob" Easterly, a founding
8 member and past president of the Livingston Economic Development Council whose legacy
9 of leadership, philanthropy, and dedication to community advancement continues to inspire
10 those who work to strengthen Livingston Parish and the state of Louisiana; and
11 WHEREAS, Fernand "Ferdie" P. Genre is a distinguished certified public accountant
12 who has devoted more than four decades of dedicated service to Livingston Parish; and
13 WHEREAS, over the course of his forty-three year career in public accounting,
14 including forty years with Hannis T. Bourgeois, LLP, Ferdie Genre has provided trusted
15 financial guidance to individuals, small businesses, nonprofit organizations, and
16 governmental entities, demonstrating the highest standards of integrity, professionalism, and
17 expertise; and
18 WHEREAS, he previously served as governmental practice leader and office lead

3 WHEREAS, a graduate of Southeastern Louisiana University with a Bachelor of

6 WHEREAS, Ferdie Genre has served as a long-term board member and treasurer of
7 the Livingston Economic Development Council and remains actively engaged in the local
8 community, serving in various capacities on boards, clubs, and in his church; and
9 WHEREAS, his career embodies the very qualities for which the Bob Easterly
10 Award was established, including leadership, service, lifelong commitment to professional
11 excellence, and the economic well being of Livingston Parish.
12 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
13 does hereby commend and congratulate Fernand "Ferdie" P. Genre upon receiving the
14 Livingston Economic Development Council's Bob Easterly Award.
15 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to
16 Fernand "Ferdie" P. Genre.
