13 §141. Policy and definitions
14 A. In order The legislature finds that the practice of architecture by
15 qualified individuals with professional expertise is necessary to safeguard life,
16 health, and property and to promote the public welfare, the practice of architecture
17 in this state is reserved to those persons who have the proper qualifications and have
18 been registered by the board.
19 B. The legislature further finds that the practice of architecture by
20 individuals and firms should be regulated in the public interest.
21 C. Therefore, it is the legislative intent and the purpose of this Chapter
22 to promote, preserve, and protect the public health, safety, and welfare by
23 regulating the practice of architecture and ensuring that any individual or firm
24 practicing or offering to practice architecture shall be registered with and
25 licensed by the board before engaging in the practice. In furtherance of this
26 purpose, this Chapter creates the State Board of Architectural Examiners
27 whose members, authority, functions, and procedures shall be established in

13 §141. Policy and definitions
14 A. In order The legislature finds that the practice of architecture by
15 qualified individuals with professional expertise is necessary to safeguard life,
16 health, and property and to promote the public welfare, the practice of architecture
17 in this state is reserved to those persons who have the proper qualifications and have
18 been registered by the board.
19 B. The legislature further finds that the practice of architecture by
20 individuals and firms should be regulated in the public interest.
21 C. Therefore, it is the legislative intent and the purpose of this Chapter
22 to promote, preserve, and protect the public health, safety, and welfare by
23 regulating the practice of architecture and ensuring that any individual or firm
24 practicing or offering to practice architecture shall be registered with and
25 licensed by the board before engaging in the practice. In furtherance of this
26 purpose, this Chapter creates the State Board of Architectural Examiners
27 whose members, authority, functions, and procedures shall be established in
