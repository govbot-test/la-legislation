HLS 26RS-384 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE BILL NO. 475
BY REPRESENTATIVE BERAULT
HEALTH CARE/PROVIDERS: Requires a healthcare provider to obtain a patient's consent
prior to recording a medical visit
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 37:22.1, relative to artificial intelligence; to require a healthcare professional
3 to obtain consent from a patient to use certain software; to provide an opt-out option;
4 and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 37:22.1 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
7 §22.1. Recordings; artificial intelligence; prohibited uses
8 A healthcare professional licensed by this Title shall obtain verbal consent
9 from a patient before recording any part of an appointment or treatment to be
10 transcribed by artificial intelligence. If the patient does not consent to a recording,
11 the healthcare professional shall conduct the appointment or treatment without the
12 use of an artificial intelligence transcription.

HB 475 Original 2026 Regular Session Berault
Abstract: Requires a healthcare professional to obtain a patient’s verbal consent before
recording a medical visit or treatment for artificial intelligence transcription.
Proposed law requires a healthcare professional licensed under present law to obtain a
patient's verbal consent before recording any part of a medical visit or treatment for
transcription using artificial intelligence.

HLS 26RS-384 ORIGINAL

Proposed law prohibits the use of artificial intelligence transcription software or service
during a medical visit or treatment if the patient does not provide verbal consent to the
recording.
