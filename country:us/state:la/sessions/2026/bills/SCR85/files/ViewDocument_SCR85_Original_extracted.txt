2026 Regular Session
SENATE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 85
BY SENATOR STINE AND REPRESENTATIVE GEYMANN
upon winning the LHSAA 2026 Division I Non-Select state championship.
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 To commend and celebrate the Sam Houston High School Broncos baseball team upon
3 winning the Louisiana High School Athletic Association 2026 Division I Non-Select
4 state championship and to recognize the team for an extraordinary season marked by
5 resilience, unity, determination, and back-to-back state titles.
6 WHEREAS, entering the 2026 season as defending state champions, the Broncos
7 embraced the pressures and expectations that accompany success while remaining grounded
8 in a simple but powerful team philosophy: to embody the spirit of "F.A.M.I.L.Y. — Forget
9 About Me, I Love You", placing team success above individual achievement; and
10 WHEREAS, fueled by belief in one another and driven by a shared goal of "Run it
11 back 2026", the Broncos dedicated themselves throughout the season to continue the proud
12 tradition of Sam Houston baseball; and
13 WHEREAS, through relentless preparation, teamwork, and perseverance, the
14 Broncos advanced once again to the state championship series and found themselves in a
15 familiar and defining moment with the championship on the line in a decisive game three;
16 and
17 WHEREAS, showing the poise and confidence of true champions, Sam Houston
18 responded with a commanding 4-0 victory over Dutchtown to secure back-to-back Division

1 I Non-Select state championships and further cement the Broncos' place among the elite
2 baseball programs in Louisiana; and
3 WHEREAS, the Broncos displayed remarkable resilience throughout the series,
4 overcoming a game one loss and rallying together to win the next two games, proving once
5 again that championship teams are defined not by adversity, but by how they respond to it;
6 and
7 WHEREAS, junior pitchers Brytn Gardner and Coleton Donaldson delivered
8 outstanding performances in the championship-clinching victory, combining for a two-hit
9 shutout and once again rising to the occasion in one of the season's biggest moments; and
10 WHEREAS, senior Carson Christ capped an unforgettable high school career with
11 a historic championship performance, including a record-setting tenth home run of the
12 season and exceptional defensive play, earning Outstanding Player honors for the
13 championship series; and
14 WHEREAS, while individual achievements helped define memorable moments, the
15 success of the Broncos was built upon the collective effort, sacrifice, and determination of
16 every player, coach, manager, student, parent, and supporter who contributed to the team's
17 remarkable journey; and
18 WHEREAS, members of the 2026 Sam Houston High School Broncos varsity
19 baseball team are AK Batterton, Adam Baumgarten, Carson Christ, Jet Conner, Charlie
20 Donaldson, Cameron Heflin, DJ Johnson, Kolton Miller, Austin Richard, Cooper Wiersema,
21 Coleton Donaldson, Owen Galley, Brytn Gardner, Bennett Harlow, Tanner Laughlin, Ty
22 Smith, Peyton Bell, Brock Blessington, Pat Conner, Quinn Fiasconaro, Kannon Keiser,

25 WHEREAS, the Broncos team managers, Jack Gilley, JoLeigh Mistric, Mason
26 Stewart, and Sylar Hymel, provided invaluable support, leadership, and dedication
27 throughout the championship season; and
28 WHEREAS, Head Coach Chad Hebert guided the Broncos with exceptional
29 leadership, vision, and commitment while continuing to build upon the proud tradition of
30 Sam Houston baseball; and

1 WHEREAS, assistant coaches Matt Keller, Robby Norris, Kaden Walker, Cameran
2 Christ, Mike Abshire, and Bart Anderson played an integral role in the team's success
3 through their mentorship, preparation, and unwavering support of the student-athletes; and
4 WHEREAS, the 2026 Sam Houston Broncos inspired the school and community
5 with their energy, humility, sportsmanship, and competitive excellence, bringing immense
6 pride and excitement to Southwest Louisiana and to the entire state of Louisiana; and
7 WHEREAS, by capturing consecutive state championships, the Broncos have
8 secured their place in the proud history of Louisiana high school baseball and created
9 memories that will endure for generations.
10 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of Louisiana does hereby
11 commend, honor, and celebrate the Sam Houston High School Broncos baseball team upon
12 winning the Louisiana High School Athletic Association 2026 Division I Non-Select state
13 championship and upon achieving the extraordinary accomplishment of back-to-back state
14 titles.
15 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Legislature of Louisiana expresses its
16 deepest appreciation to the players, coaches, families, faculty, students, alumni, and loyal
17 supporters whose dedication and passion helped make this championship season possible.
18 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to Sam
19 Houston High School Head Baseball Coach Chad Hebert.
