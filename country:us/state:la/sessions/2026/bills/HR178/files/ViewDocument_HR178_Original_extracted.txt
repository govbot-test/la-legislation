HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 178
BY REPRESENTATIVE BOYER
Francis Regis Church 30th Étouffée Festival Queen
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Emma Renee Bonin on being named the 2026-2027 St. Francis Regis Church
3 30th Étouffée Festival Queen.
4 WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize Emma Renee Bonin for her achievements
5 and to commend her on being named the 2026-2027 30th Étouffée Festival Queen; and
6 WHEREAS, Emma is a nineteen-year-old native of New Iberia, Louisiana, and a
7 2025 graduate of New Iberia Senior High School; and
8 WHEREAS, Emma is freshman at the University of Louisiana at Lafayette, where
9 she studies kinesiology, has a 3.8 GPA, and received President's List honors; and
10 WHEREAS, Emma is a member of the Kinesiology Professional Association, and
11 she participates in the Life on Purpose small group at Our Savior's Church; she works at
12 Tutu Cute Vinyl Creations and is a cheer coach at New Heights Gymnastics; and
13 WHEREAS, Emma is a survivor of a head-on collision caused by a drunk driver; she
14 dedicates her time advocating against impaired driving and educating teens and adults about
15 risks associated with drunk driving; and
16 WHEREAS, Emma volunteers at festivals across Louisiana, and in her free time, she
17 enjoys hunting, fishing, and watching college baseball; she plans to pursue a career as an
18 athletic trainer and hopes to work with special needs athletes competing in the Special
19 Olympics; and
Page 1 of 2
HLS 26RS-2716 ORIGINAL
HR NO. 178
1 WHEREAS, Emma Renee Bonin is an inspiring young woman who is most
2 deserving of the highest recognition for her academic achievements and advocacy and for
3 being named the 30th Étouffée Festival Queen.
4 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
5 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Emma Renee Bonin on being named the
6 2026-2027 St. Francis Regis Church 30th Étouffée Festival Queen; does hereby express
7 enduring appreciation for her advocacy against impaired driving; and does hereby extend
8 sincere wishes that she remain prosperous in all of her endeavors.
