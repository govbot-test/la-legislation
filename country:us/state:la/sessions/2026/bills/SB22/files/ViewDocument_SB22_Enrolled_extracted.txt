SENATE BILL NO. 22
BY SENATOR CARTER
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 11:1732(14)(a)(xiv), relative to participation in the Municipal Employees'
3 Retirement System of Louisiana; to provide for an additional employer in the
4 system; to provide for membership of the office of the constable of the New Orleans
5 Second City Court; to provide for an effective date; and to provide for related
6 matters.
7 Notice of intention to introduce this Act has been published.
8 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:

10 §1732. Definitions
11 The following words and phrases, as used in this Chapter, unless a different
12 meaning is plainly required by the context, shall have the following meanings:
13 * * *
14 (14)(a) "Employer" or "participating employer" shall mean:
15 * * *
16 (xiv) Marshals or constables of city courts, if not otherwise eligible for
17 membership in any other local, state, or statewide retirement system.
18 * * *
