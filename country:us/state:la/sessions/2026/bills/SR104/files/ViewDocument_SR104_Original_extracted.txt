SLS 26RS-2542 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 104
BY SENATOR BOUDREAUX
Firefighter's Appreciation Day.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To recognize Monday, May 4, 2026, as Louisiana Firefighter's Appreciation Day and to
3 commend firefighters across the state for their dedication to public safety and their
4 service to the residents of Louisiana and the citizens of the United States of America.
5 WHEREAS, May fourth is traditionally observed as Saint Florian's Day, honoring
6 Saint Florian who is believed to have been one of the first commanding firefighters of a
7 Roman battalion and is recognized as the patron saint of firefighters; and
8 WHEREAS, firefighting is a vital service to our communities and firefighters play
9 a critical role in preserving life and property; and
10 WHEREAS, firefighting is a high-risk profession exposing firefighters to many
11 dangerous elements and hazardous materials; and
12 WHEREAS, whenever a crisis occurs, firefighters selflessly answer the call, putting
13 their lives at risk in dangerous and hazardous conditions; and
14 WHEREAS, Louisiana firefighters respond to all types of disasters, whether natural
15 or man-made, not only in their respective communities but in those throughout the state and
16 the nation; and
17 WHEREAS, it is appropriate to recognize and support firefighters as they strive to
18 preserve life and property from the ravages of fire and other disasters and to celebrate the
Page 1 of 2

1 proud history of the American fire service and the sacrifices made by firefighters and their
2 families across this great nation.
3 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
4 does hereby recognize Monday, May 4, 2026, as Louisiana Firefighter's Appreciation Day
5 and does hereby commend all firefighters across the state for their dedication to public safety
6 and service to the people of Louisiana and the citizens of the United States of America.
