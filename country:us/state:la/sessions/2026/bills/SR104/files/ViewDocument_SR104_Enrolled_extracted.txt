2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 104
BY SENATOR BOUDREAUX
A RESOLUTION
To recognize Monday, May 4, 2026, as Louisiana Firefighter's Appreciation Day and to
commend firefighters across the state for their dedication to public safety and their
service to the residents of Louisiana and the citizens of the United States of America.
WHEREAS, May fourth is traditionally observed as Saint Florian's Day, honoring
Saint Florian who is believed to have been one of the first commanding firefighters of a
Roman battalion and is recognized as the patron saint of firefighters; and
WHEREAS, firefighting is a vital service to our communities and firefighters play
a critical role in preserving life and property; and
WHEREAS, firefighting is a high-risk profession exposing firefighters to many
dangerous elements and hazardous materials; and
WHEREAS, whenever a crisis occurs, firefighters selflessly answer the call, putting
their lives at risk in dangerous and hazardous conditions; and
WHEREAS, Louisiana firefighters respond to all types of disasters, whether natural
or man-made, not only in their respective communities but in those throughout the state and
the nation; and
WHEREAS, it is appropriate to recognize and support firefighters as they strive to
preserve life and property from the ravages of fire and other disasters and to celebrate the
proud history of the American fire service and the sacrifices made by firefighters and their
families across this great nation.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
does hereby recognize Monday, May 4, 2026, as Louisiana Firefighter's Appreciation Day
and does hereby commend all firefighters across the state for their dedication to public safety
and service to the people of Louisiana and the citizens of the United States of America.
PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE
