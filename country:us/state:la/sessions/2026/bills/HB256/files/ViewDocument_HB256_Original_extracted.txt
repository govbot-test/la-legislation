HB 256 Original 2026 Regular Session Freeman
Abstract: Provides relative to existing requirements applicable to mandatory reporters,
including requirements pertaining to certain training provided by the Dept. of
Children and Family Services.
Present law provides that the definition of a "mandatory reporter" includes a "teaching or
child care provider". Requires each teaching or child care provider to complete an online
training course provided by the Dept. of Children and Family Services between June first
and August 31st annually. Requires that a record of completion of the course be provided
to each entity at which the provider is employed. Proposed law retains present law.
Proposed law provides that each school employee who meets the definition of a mandatory
reporter as provided in present law is subject to all present law provisions relative to
mandatory reporters, including but not limited to such present law training and reporting
requirements. Proposed law is applicable to such employees of all public schools, including
charter schools.
(Adds R.S. 17:421 and 3996(B)(24))
