HLS 26RS-1051 REENGROSSED

BY REPRESENTATIVE CREWS
FUNDS/FUNDING: Authorizes the state treasurer to invest up to ten percent of available
monies in the Revenue Stabilization Trust Fund in gold
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 39:100.112(E)(1), relative to monies in the state treasury; to
3 provide relative to the powers and duties of the state treasurer; to provide relative to
4 investment of certain monies in the state treasury; to provide for definitions; to
5 provide for implementation and administration; to provide for an effective date; and
6 to provide for related matters.
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
8 Section 1. R.S. 39:100.112(E)(1) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as
9 follows:
10 §100.112. Revenue Stabilization Trust Fund
11 * * *
12 E.(1)(a) Except as provided for in Subsection F of this Section, monies
13 deposited into the Revenue Stabilization Trust Fund shall be permanently credited
14 to the trust fund and shall be invested by the treasurer in the same manner as
15 investments of the Millennium Trust, as provided in R.S. 39:98.2.
16 (b) Notwithstanding the provisions of Subparagraph (a) of this Paragraph
17 regarding investment of monies in the fund, an aggregate amount not to exceed ten
18 percent of monies in the fund may be invested in gold bullion, gold specie, or both.

1 For the purposes of this Subparagraph, the term "gold bullion" means gold formed
2 into uniform shapes and quantities such as ingots, bars, or plates, with uniform
3 content and purity as are suitable for or customarily used in the purchase, sale,

12 contracts necessary for the implementation and administration of the provisions of this Act,
13 including contracting with a depository to serve as the custodian, guardian, and administrator
14 of any bullion or specie that may be acquired pursuant to the provisions of this Act.

16 amendment of Article VII, Section 14(B) of the Constitution of Louisiana contained in the
17 Act which originated as House Bill No. 603 of this 2026 Regular Session of the Legislature
18 is adopted at a statewide election and becomes effective.

HB 610 Reengrossed 2026 Regular Session Crews
Abstract: Authorizes the state treasurer to invest up to 10% of monies in the Revenue
Stabilization Trust Fund ("trust fund") in gold bullion, gold specie, or both.
Present constitution (Art. VII, Sec. 10.15) establishes the trust fund as a special fund in the
state treasury and provides for the deposit and use of monies in the trust fund. Further
provides that monies in the trust fund shall be invested by the treasurer in a manner provided
for by law.
Present law (R.S. 39:100.112) requires the treasurer to invest monies in the trust fund in the
same manner as investments of the Millennium Trust. Proposed law adds an authorization
for the treasurer to invest an aggregate amount up to 10% of monies in the trust fund in gold
bullion, gold specie, or both. Defines "gold bullion" as gold formed into uniform shapes and
quantities such as ingots, bars, or plates, with uniform content and purity as are suitable for

transactions in precious metals. Defines "gold specie" as gold stamped into coins of uniform

medium of exchange; or as the medium for purchase, sale, storage, transfer, or delivery of
