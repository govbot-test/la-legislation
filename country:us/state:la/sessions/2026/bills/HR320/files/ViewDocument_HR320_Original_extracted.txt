2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 320
BY REPRESENTATIVE SPELL
Premier League Championship
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Arsenal Football Club on winning the 2025-2026 Premier League
3 championship and to recognize the club as the pride of North London.
4 WHEREAS, Arsenal Football Club (Arsenal F.C.), founded in 1886, stands among
5 the most historic and successful football clubs in the world, having built a global reputation
6 for excellence, class, attacking football, and commitment to winning at the highest levels of
7 competition; and
8 WHEREAS, the Gunners captured the 2025-2026 Premier League title through
9 brilliance, resilience, tactical superiority, and the leadership of manager Mikel Arteta, all of
10 which restored Arsenal Football Club to its place atop English football; and
11 WHEREAS, Arsenal supporters throughout the world witnessed their club rise above
12 every challenger while displaying the style, composure, and ambition expected of the true
13 kings of North London; and
14 WHEREAS, the club's supporters at the legendary Emirates Stadium created one of
15 the most electrifying atmospheres in world football and stood behind the club during its
16 triumphant march toward league glory; and
17 WHEREAS, Arsenal's championship triumph further widened the gulf between the
18 Gunners and their North London rivals, Tottensham Hotspur F.C.; and
19 WHEREAS, Arsenal's dominance over Tottenham serves as a reminder that North
20 London has always been red and shall remain red for the foreseeable future; and
Page 1 of 2

1 WHEREAS, this championship also highlighted Arsenal's return to footballing
2 greatness; and
3 WHEREAS, Arsenal has demonstrated how a properly run football club can build
4 sustainably and compete honorably; and
5 WHEREAS, Arsenal's title victory therefore stands not only as a triumph of talent
6 but also as a triumph of tradition, proper stewardship, organic team-building, and footballing
7 authenticity; and
8 WHEREAS, Arsenal F.C.'s 2025-2026 championship team will forever be
9 remembered by supporters across the globe as the "Unforgettables".
10 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
11 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Arsenal Football Club on winning the
12 2025-2026 Premier League championship; does hereby recognize Arsenal Football Club as
13 the pride of North London and one of the premier sporting institutions in the world; and does
14 hereby extend best wishes for continued success and additional silverware in the years
15 ahead.
