2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 198
BY SENATOR MIZELL
A RESOLUTION
To urge and request the Louisiana Department of Health to submit a report on the
implementation of the Rural Health Transformation Program.
WHEREAS, the Louisiana Rural Health Transformation Program (RHTP) is an
unprecedented five year federally funded investment aimed to increase healthcare access and
improve healthcare outcomes for rural communities across the state; and
WHEREAS, the RHTP will focus on improving prevention and chronic disease
management, strengthening workforce development, technology innovation, improving rural
healthcare infrastructure, and developing innovative care models that deliver high quality,
efficient, and sustainable care; and
WHEREAS, Louisiana was awarded $208.4 million for the first year of the grant,
with an estimated award of $1.4 billion over the grant period that represents one of the top
awards in the nation; and
WHEREAS, the Louisiana Department of Health is implementing multiple initiatives
through the program, including the Rural Tech Catalyst Fund in partnership with Louisiana
Economic Development; and
WHEREAS, the fund is designed to support innovative companies, entrepreneurs,
healthcare organizations, and partners working to improve healthcare access, delivery, and
sustainability across rural Louisiana; and
WHEREAS, pursuant to federal requirements, all RHTP funds must be used by end
of the federal fiscal year following the fiscal year in which funds were allotted; and
WHEREAS, the RHTP presents a tremendous opportunity for every corner of
Louisiana and this program could have long-term impacts for improving our population's
health outcomes; and
WHEREAS, full transparency and accountability are essential when managing this
amount of money and demonstrating a successful return on investment.

THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
does hereby urge and request the Louisiana Department of Health to submit the following
reports to the Senate relative to the implementation of the Rural Health Transformation
Program:
(1) No later than October 15, 2026, a report that provides the proposed program
expenditures for federal Fiscal Year 2026-2027.
(2) No later than November 1, 2027, a report that provides the actual program
expenditures for federal Fiscal Year 2026-2027 and proposed program expenditures for
federal Fiscal Year 2027-2028.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the reports shall include identification of
recipients of funding and the amount of funding allocated, measurable outcomes,
benchmarks, and progress in improving rural health outcomes.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
secretary of the Louisiana Department of Health.
PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE
