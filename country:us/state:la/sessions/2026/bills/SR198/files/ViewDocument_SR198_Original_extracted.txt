SLS 26RS-4534 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 198
BY SENATOR MIZELL
HEALTH DEPARTMENT. Requests the Louisiana Department of Health to submit a report
relative to the implementation of the Rural Health Transformation Program.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To urge and request the Louisiana Department of Health to submit a report on the
3 implementation of the Rural Health Transformation Program.
4 WHEREAS, the Louisiana Rural Health Transformation Program (RHTP) is an
5 unprecedented five year federally funded investment aimed to increase healthcare access and
6 improve healthcare outcomes for rural communities across the state; and
7 WHEREAS, the RHTP will focus on improving prevention and chronic disease
8 management, strengthening workforce development, technology innovation, improving rural
9 healthcare infrastructure, and developing innovative care models that deliver high quality,
10 efficient, and sustainable care; and
11 WHEREAS, Louisiana was awarded $208.4 million for the first year of the grant,
12 with an estimated award of $1.4 billion over the grant period that represents one of the top
13 awards in the nation; and
14 WHEREAS, the Louisiana Department of Health is implementing multiple initiatives
15 through the program, including the Rural Tech Catalyst Fund in partnership with Louisiana
16 Economic Development; and
17 WHEREAS, the fund is designed to support innovative companies, entrepreneurs,
18 healthcare organizations, and partners working to improve healthcare access, delivery, and

1 sustainability across rural Louisiana; and
2 WHEREAS, pursuant to federal requirements, all RHTP funds must be used by end
3 of the federal fiscal year following the fiscal year in which funds were allotted; and
4 WHEREAS, the RHTP presents a tremendous opportunity for every corner of
5 Louisiana and this program could have long-term impacts for improving our population's
6 health outcomes; and
7 WHEREAS, full transparency and accountability are essential when managing this
8 amount of money and demonstrating a successful return on investment.
9 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
10 does hereby urge and request the Louisiana Department of Health to submit the following
11 reports to the Senate relative to the implementation of the Rural Health Transformation
12 Program:
13 (1) No later than October 15, 2026, a report that provides the proposed program
14 expenditures for federal Fiscal Year 2026-2027.
15 (2) No later than November 1, 2027, a report that provides the actual program
16 expenditures for federal Fiscal Year 2026-2027 and proposed program expenditures for
17 federal Fiscal Year 2027-2028.
18 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the reports shall include identification of
19 recipients of funding and the amount of funding allocated, measurable outcomes,
20 benchmarks, and progress in improving rural health outcomes.
21 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
22 secretary of the Louisiana Department of Health.
