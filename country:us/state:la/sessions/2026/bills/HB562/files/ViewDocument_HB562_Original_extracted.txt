2026 Regular Session
HOUSE BILL NO. 562
BY REPRESENTATIVE LAFLEUR
COURTS/COURT COSTS: Provides relative to transcript fee provisions for the Nineteenth
Judicial District Court
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 13:961(F)(1)(w), relative to court reporter transcript fees in the Nineteenth
3 Judicial District Court; to provide for transcript fees; and to provide for related
4 matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 13:961(F)(1)(w) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
7 §961. Court reporters; generally
8 * * *
9 F.(1)
10 * * *
11 (w) In the Nineteenth Judicial District Court, such fee shall not exceed four
12 dollars for routine transcript requests and six dollars and fifty cents for special
13 requests and two dollars for each copied page.

HLS 26RS-1025 ORIGINAL
HB NO. 562
Proposed law provides that the court reporter transcript fees in the 19th Judicial District
Court shall not exceed $4.00 for routine transcripts and $6.50 for special requests. Further
provides that cost for copied pages will be $2 per page.
(Adds R.S. 13:961(F)(1)(w))
