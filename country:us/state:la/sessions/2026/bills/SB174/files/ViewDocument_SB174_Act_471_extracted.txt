Section 1:
2026 Regular Session ACT No. 471 ENROLLED
BY SENATOR PRESSLY
1 AN ACT
2 To authorize and provide for the transfer of certain state property; to authorize the transfer
3 of state property in Rapides Parish and Caddo Parish; to provide for the property
4 description; to provide for reservation of mineral rights; to provide terms and
5 conditions; to provide for an effective date; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. The president of Louisiana Tech University, as authorized by the Board
8 of Supervisors for the University of Louisiana System, and the commissioner of
9 administration, notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, are hereby
10 authorized and empowered to convey, transfer, assign, or deliver any interest, excluding
11 mineral rights, the state may have to all or any portion of the following described parcel of
12 property to the Caddo Parish School Board:
13 Lot 1, Shreve Park Industrial Campus, Unit No. 1, per plat in Conveyance
14 Book 2500, Pages 34-38, recorded June 6, 1997 under Registry No. 1561114
15 of the Records of Caddo Parish, Louisiana.
16 Section 2. The president of Louisiana Tech University, as authorized by the Board
17 of Supervisors for the University of Louisiana System, and the commissioner of
18 administration are hereby authorized to enter into such agreements, covenants, conditions,
19 and stipulations and to execute such documents as necessary to properly effectuate any
20 conveyance, transfer, assignment, lease, or delivery of title, excluding mineral rights, to the
21 property described in Section 1 of this Act, and as more specifically described in any such
22 agreements entered into and documents executed by and between the president of Louisiana
23 Tech University, as authorized by the Board of Supervisors for the University of Louisiana
24 System, and the commissioner of administration and the Caddo Parish School Board, in
25 exchange of consideration proportionate to the appraised value of the property.
26 Section 3. The commissioner of administration, notwithstanding any other provision
27 of law to the contrary, is hereby authorized and empowered to convey, transfer, assign, lease,
28 or deliver any interest, excluding mineral rights, the state may have to all or any portion of
