SLS 26RS-4632 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 204
BY SENATOR BARROW
leadership, guidance, and prayer during the 2026 Regular Session of the Louisiana
Legislature.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Dr. Lawrence Dewayne Hardy for his exceptional leadership, guidance, and
3 prayer during the 2026 Regular Session of the Louisiana Legislature and to recognize
4 his steadfast commitment to serving others.
5 WHEREAS, Dr. Lawrence Dewayne Hardy has faithfully provided spiritual
6 leadership, encouragement, and prayer throughout the 2026 Regular Session of the Louisiana
7 Legislature, offering wisdom, guidance, and inspiration to legislators as they addressed the
8 many complex and challenging issues before them; and
9 WHEREAS, Dr. Hardy has consistently made himself available whenever called
10 upon to pray, encourage, uplift, and exhort others, demonstrating a servant's heart and a
11 sincere commitment to the well-being of those entrusted with public service; and
12 WHEREAS, Dr. Hardy is the Senior Pastor and Founder of Theo House of Judah
13 Ministries in Baton Rouge, Louisiana, a ministry dedicated to fulfilling God's purpose and
14 plan by "Touching Lives and Meeting Needs Through God's Eyes"; and
15 WHEREAS, the vision of Theo House of Judah Ministries is to lead change, equip
16 and empower God's people, and positively impact the communities it serves by transforming
17 lives through ministry both within and beyond the walls of the church; and
18 WHEREAS, Dr. Hardy is an anointed apostle, teacher, prophet, and entrepreneur

1 whose ministry has reached people from diverse backgrounds and walks of life through his
2 preaching of the uncompromised Word of God with clarity, compassion, and truth; and
3 WHEREAS, his unwavering devotion to God, his love for people, and his willingness
4 to serve wherever needed have all earned him the admiration and respect of countless
5 individuals throughout Louisiana and beyond; and
6 WHEREAS, Dr. Hardy is also the proud father of five children, Zoe, Zyon,
7 Shandolyn, Tiffany, and Jeremiah, and he serves as an example of faith, leadership, and
8 dedication both in his family and in his ministry; and
9 WHEREAS, the people of Louisiana have been greatly blessed by Dr. Hardy's
10 faithful service, and it is fitting to recognize his invaluable contributions and express sincere
11 appreciation for the encouragement and spiritual support he has provided during the 2026
12 legislative session.
13 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
14 does hereby commend Dr. Lawrence Dewayne Hardy for his exceptional leadership,
15 guidance, and prayer during the 2026 Regular Session of the Louisiana Legislature and does
16 hereby express profound gratitude for his dedicated service to the citizens of this state.
17 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to Dr.
18 Lawrence Dewayne Hardy.
