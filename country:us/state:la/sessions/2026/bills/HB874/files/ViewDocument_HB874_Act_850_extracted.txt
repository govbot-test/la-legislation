Section 1:
ENROLLED
ACT No. 850
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE MURRAY
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 32:707.2(C)(2) and R.S. 39:17.2(A), relative to digitized
3 credentials; to provide for the authority of the commissioner; to authorize the
4 commissioner to coordinate with the governing bodies of certain professions and
5 occupations for digitized credentials; to implement the availability of certain
6 credentials in digitized format for use in an electronic wallet; to modify the number
7 of motor vehicle transactions required to designate a public tag agent to interface its
8 computer system; and to provide for related matters.
9 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
10 Section 1. R.S. 32:707.2(C)(2) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
11 §707.2. Electronic media system for lien recordation and title information; vehicle
12 titling and registration; participation requirements; fees; required bond;
13 confidentiality of information; rules and regulations
14 * * *
15 C.(1)
16 * * *
17 (2) Each federally insured depository institution that originates more than
18 two hundred fifty motor vehicle transactions per year, and each finance company,
19 lending institution, or other lender shall designate a public tag agent with which such
20 bank, finance company, lending institution, or other lender shall interface its
21 computer system for the purpose of receiving electronic confirmation from the
22 department, of the receipt and the filing of the security interest on the subject motor
23 vehicle. Each federally insured depository institution that originates more than two
24 hundred fifty motor vehicle transactions per year, and each finance company, lending
Page 1 of 2

are additions.
HB NO. 874 ENROLLED
1 institution, or other lender shall also designate such public tag agent when
2 transmitting a release or satisfaction of its lien.
3 * * *
4 Section 2. R.S. 39:17.2(A) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
5 §17.2. Digitized credentials; authorization; implementation; validity
6 A.(1) Any credential authorized or required by the state may be made
7 available in digitized format on a program which a user can utilize through an
8 electronic wallet.
9 (2) The commissioner of administration may coordinate with the governing
10 bodies of the professions and occupations listed below to implement the availability
11 of digitized credentials:
12 (a) The Louisiana State Bar Association for the inclusion of bar membership
13 cards.
14 (b) The Louisiana High School Athletic Association for the inclusion of a
15 referee credential.
16 (c) Public and private universities and colleges in Louisiana for the inclusion
17 of faculty, staff, and student credentials.
18 (d) The Department of Public Safety and Corrections, office of motor
19 vehicles, for the inclusion of mobility impaired identification cards.
