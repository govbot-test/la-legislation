2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 69
BY REPRESENTATIVE BRYANT
COMMENDATIONS: Designates Jeanerette as the French Bread Capital of Louisiana
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate Jeanerette as the French Bread Capital of Louisiana.
3 WHEREAS, the city of Jeanerette in Iberia Parish, Louisiana, was incorporated in
4 1878 and has a culturally rich and colorful history; and
5 WHEREAS, Jeanerette is the home of LeJeune's Bakery, which has produced
6 scratch-made French bread since its founding in 1884; and
7 WHEREAS, five generations of LeJeune family members have carried on the legacy
8 of the family bakery and established the city of Jeanerette as the premier source of French
9 bread in Louisiana and beyond; and
10 WHEREAS, LeJeune's Bakery is the first bakery in Louisiana to be named in the
11 National Register of Historic Places; and
12 WHEREAS, the city of Jeanerette is synonymous with the production of high-quality
13 French bread, and it is most fitting to recognize the city for this distinction.
14 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
15 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby designate Jeanerette as the French Bread Capital of
16 Louisiana.
