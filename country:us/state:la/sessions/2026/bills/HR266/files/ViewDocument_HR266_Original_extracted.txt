HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 266
BY REPRESENTATIVES DEVILLIER AND MILLER
COMMENDATIONS: Commends Paul T. DeRousselle, II, on his accomplishments as a
sports agent, author, scholarship initiator, and citizen of his community
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Paul T. DeRousselle, II, on his many accomplishments as a sports agent,
3 author, scholarship initiator, and active citizen of his community.
4 WHEREAS, Paul T. DeRousselle, II, is a native of Eunice, Louisiana; the son of
5 Sheila and Paul T. DeRousselle, Sr.; the brother of Shelana and Denise DeRousselle; and the
6 father of Alanna DeRousselle; and
7 WHEREAS, Paul is the founder of the Go Be Great Scholarship initiative, which
8 supports local children in their educational pursuits; he is guided by the wisdom of his
9 grandfather, who taught him that "those who walk with God always reach their destination",
10 and continues to make a lasting impact in his community and the sports world; and
11 WHEREAS, Paul was a Spring 2004 initiate of the Beta Sigma Chapter of Alpha Phi
12 Alpha Fraternity, Incorporated, at Southern University and served as president of that
13 chapter; and
14 WHEREAS, Paul earned a bachelor of arts degree in English from Southern
15 University; as an undergraduate at Southern University, his achievements extended beyond
16 academics: he was a four-year letterman in tennis, he played an integral role in leading the
17 Jaguars to back-to-back SWAC titles and two NCAA tournament appearances, and he
18 represented Southern as the national delegate on the Student Athlete Advisory Committee;
19 and
20 WHEREAS, Paul is a proud magna cum laude graduate of Southern University Law
21 Center; during his time at the Law Center, he demonstrated exceptional leadership by

2 chairman of the Recruitment Committee, and associate editor of the Southern University

4 WHEREAS, Paul has established himself as an accomplished National Football

8 WHEREAS, Paul has risen to prominence representing elite talent and has secured
9 many high-profile endorsement deals with global companies, including Nike, Adidas, State
10 Farm, Booking.com, and Snickers; and
11 WHEREAS, Paul has authored a number of articles on sports law and a book entitled
12 Faith. Failure. Football., and he endeavors to assist rising sports agents and provide them
13 with the tools to succeed; he is most deserving of recognition for his outstanding
14 achievements.
15 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
16 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Paul T. DeRousselle, II, on his many
17 accomplishments; does hereby extend enduring appreciation for his support of students in
18 his community; and does hereby express sincere wishes that he remain prosperous in all of
19 his endeavors.
