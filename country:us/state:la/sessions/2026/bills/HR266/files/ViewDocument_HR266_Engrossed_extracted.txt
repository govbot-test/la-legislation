WHEREAS, Paul T. DeRousselle, II, is a native of Eunice, Louisiana; the son of
Sheila and Paul T. DeRousselle, Sr.; the brother of Shelana and Denise DeRousselle; and the
father of Alanna DeRousselle; and
WHEREAS, Paul is the founder of the Go Be Great Scholarship initiative, which
supports local children in their educational pursuits; he is guided by the wisdom of his
grandfather, who taught him that "those who walk with God always reach their destination",
and continues to make a lasting impact in his community and the sports world; and
WHEREAS, Paul was a Spring 2004 initiate of the Beta Sigma Chapter of Alpha Phi
Alpha Fraternity, Incorporated, at Southern University and served as president of that
chapter; and
WHEREAS, Paul earned a bachelor of arts degree in English from Southern
University; as an undergraduate at Southern University, his achievements extended beyond
academics: he was a four-year letterman in tennis, he played an integral role in leading the
Jaguars to back-to-back SWAC titles and two NCAA tournament appearances, and he
represented Southern as the national delegate on the Student Athlete Advisory Committee;
and
WHEREAS, Paul is a proud magna cum laude graduate of Southern University Law
Center; during his time at the Law Center, he demonstrated exceptional leadership by
serving as vice president of the Student Bar Association, co-captain of the Mock Trial Team,

chairman of the Recruitment Committee, and associate editor of the Southern University
Law Review; and
WHEREAS, Paul has established himself as an accomplished National Football
League Players Association Certified Contract Advisor since 2012 and has represented some
of the NFL's most notable players, including Keon Coleman, who is a Louisiana native and
wide receiver for the Buffalo Bills; and
WHEREAS, Paul has risen to prominence representing elite talent and has secured
many high-profile endorsement deals with global companies, including Nike, Adidas, State

WHEREAS, Paul has authored a number of articles on sports law and a book entitled
Faith. Failure. Football., and he endeavors to assist rising sports agents and provide them
with the tools to succeed; he is most deserving of recognition for his outstanding
achievements.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Paul T. DeRousselle, II, on his many
accomplishments; does hereby extend enduring appreciation for his support of students in
his community; and does hereby express sincere wishes that he remain prosperous in all of
his endeavors.
