2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 107
BY REPRESENTATIVE WILDER
A RESOLUTION
To commend Justin Wax for being named the 2026 Principal of the Year in Louisiana.
WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize Justin Wax for his dedication to the
students of Denham Springs Junior High School and to commend him for being named the
2026 Principal of the Year; and
WHEREAS, Principal Wax currently serves in his fourth year as principal of
Denham Springs Junior High School; under his leadership, the school was recognized as a
2024 Top Gains Honoree and featured in U.S. News and World Report's Best Middle
Schools in 2024 and 2025; and
WHEREAS, Principal Wax is a leader in student advocacy; he has taken innovative
steps to address teen vaping by offering educational alternatives to suspension and facilitated
the installation of campus vape detection technology; and
WHEREAS, Principal Wax spoke before the House Committee on Judiciary and
effectively advocated to raise the minimum age requirements for the purchase of vaping
products; and
WHEREAS, Principal Wax is dedicated to STEM education and has pioneered an
aquaponics lab that provides hands-on opportunities to conserve endangered plant species
in Louisiana and teaches sustainable produce cultivation; and
WHEREAS, Principal Wax is committed to addressing students' needs and has
partnered with community leaders to eliminate student fees and ensure access for
economically disadvantaged students; he also supports a rapidly growing English learner
population; and

WHEREAS, Principal Wax earned a bachelor of science degree in business
administration with a minor in leadership development from Louisiana State University; he
pursued a career in education through alternative certification and received a master of
education in educational leadership from Southeastern Louisiana University; and
WHEREAS, Principal Wax is a member of the Louisiana Association of Principals
and vice president of the Livingston Parish Principals Association; he served four years as
president of Mighty Moms, which is a nonprofit that "nurtures hungry students to success";
and
WHEREAS, outside of school, Principal Wax serves as a church deacon, and he is
a married father of three children; and
WHEREAS, Denham Springs Junior High School Principal Justin Wax is a dedicated
advocate of students and their education, and he is most deserving of the highest recognition
for his outstanding service.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Justin Wax for being named the 2026
Principal of the Year in Louisiana and does hereby extend enduring appreciation for his
contributions to students in Louisiana.
