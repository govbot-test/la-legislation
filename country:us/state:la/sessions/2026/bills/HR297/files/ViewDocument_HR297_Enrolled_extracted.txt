HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 297
BY REPRESENTATIVE BOYER
A RESOLUTION
To commend Breaux Bridge resident Jacob Lewis on being selected by Louisiana Public
Broadcasting as the Louisiana representative in the PBS Kids series "America's
Awesome Kids".
WHEREAS, Louisiana Public Broadcasting (LPB) is part of a group of twenty-three
partnering PBS stations across the United States that are participating in the "America's
Awesome Kids" series; the series is a part of the "PBS Kids Across America" initiative and
features twenty-five mini-documentaries that showcase kids from across the country; and
WHEREAS, Jacob Lewis is a ten-year-old from Breaux Bridge, Louisiana, who has
a diverse set of interests and displays incredible leadership skills; he was selected by LPB
to represent the state of Louisiana in the "America's Awesome Kids" series; and
WHEREAS, Jacob enjoys horseback riding, trail riding, playing football, and
participating in a variety of cultural activities in Acadiana; and
WHEREAS, Jacob is a leader among his classmates and serves as "House Chief" at
his school; he developed an anti-bullying campaign for his school; and
WHEREAS, in Jacob's featured episode, he brings the viewer on a friends-and-
family trail ride that includes horses, wagons, zydeco music, and Cajun cooking; and
WHEREAS, Jacob also shares a tour of the Atchafalaya Swamp that includes an
appearance from the Cajun Santa Claus, Papa Noel; and
WHEREAS, Jacob will be recognized by LPB on June 22, 2026, at St. Martinville's
STEAM Academy.

HR NO. 297 ENROLLED
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Breaux Bridge resident Jacob Lewis on
