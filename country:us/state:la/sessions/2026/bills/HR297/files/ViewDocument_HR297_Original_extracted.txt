HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 297
BY REPRESENTATIVE BOYER
by Louisiana Public Broadcasting as the Louisiana representative in the PBS Kids
series "America's Awesome Kids"
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Breaux Bridge resident Jacob Lewis on being selected by Louisiana Public
3 Broadcasting as the Louisiana representative in the PBS Kids series "America's
4 Awesome Kids".
5 WHEREAS, Louisiana Public Broadcasting (LPB) is part of a group of twenty-three
6 partnering PBS stations across the United States that are participating in the "America's
7 Awesome Kids" series; the series is a part of the "PBS Kids Across America" initiative and
8 features twenty-five mini-documentaries that showcase kids from across the country; and
9 WHEREAS, Jacob Lewis is a ten-year-old from Breaux Bridge, Louisiana, who has
10 a diverse set of interests and displays incredible leadership skills; he was selected by LPB
11 to represent the state of Louisiana in the "America's Awesome Kids" series; and
12 WHEREAS, Jacob enjoys horseback riding, trail riding, playing football, and
13 participating in a variety of cultural activities in Acadiana; and
14 WHEREAS, Jacob is a leader among his classmates and serves as "House Chief" at
15 his school; he developed an anti-bullying campaign for his school; and
16 WHEREAS, in Jacob's featured episode, he brings the viewer on a friends-and-
17 family trail ride that includes horses, wagons, zydeco music, and Cajun cooking; and
18 WHEREAS, Jacob also shares a tour of the Atchafalaya Swamp that includes an
19 appearance from the Cajun Santa Claus, Papa Noel; and
Page 1 of 2

1 WHEREAS, Jacob will be recognized by LPB on June 22, 2026, at St. Martinville's

3 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the

5 being selected by Louisiana Public Broadcasting as the Louisiana representative in the PBS

7 continue to prosper in all of his endeavors.
