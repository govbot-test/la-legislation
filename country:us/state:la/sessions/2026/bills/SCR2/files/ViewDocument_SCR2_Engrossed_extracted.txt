SLS 26RS-381 ENGROSSED
2026 Regular Session
SENATE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 2
BY SENATOR MYERS
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 To amend the Louisiana Department of Health rule LAC 48:I.9305(N)(2)(b), to provide that
3 hospital architectural plans shall comply with the most recent edition of the Facility
4 Guidelines Institute, Guidelines for Design and Construction of Hospitals and
5 Outpatient Facilities; and to direct the Louisiana Register to print the amendment in
6 the Louisiana Administrative Code.
7 WHEREAS, the Louisiana Department of Health recently amended and updated the
8 rules and regulations pertaining to licensure requirements of hospitals in Louisiana; and
9 WHEREAS, one of the changes enacted in the recent update included changing the
10 applicable architectural standards to the 2014 Edition of the Facility Guidelines Institute,
11 Guidelines for Design and Construction of Hospitals and Outpatient Facilities; and
12 WHEREAS, the department is using guidelines from twelve years ago, while the
13 Facility Guidelines Institute updates its guidelines every four years with a new edition
14 coming out in 2026; and
15 WHEREAS, R.S. 49:969 provides that the legislature, by concurrent resolution, may
16 suspend, amend, or repeal any rule adopted by a state department, agency, board, or
17 commission.
18 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of Louisiana hereby amends

1 the provisions of LAC 48:I.9305(N)(2)(b) to read as follows:

10 as adopted by the OSFM for building design and construction, the OSFM shall notify
11 the party submitting the plans in writing, the particular items in question and request
12 further explanation and/or confirmation of necessary modifications.

14 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
15 office of the state register, the Louisiana Department of Health, and the office of state fire
16 marshal.
17 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Louisiana Register is hereby directed to have
18 the amendments to LAC 48:I.9305(N)(2)(b) printed and incorporated into the Louisiana
19 Administrative Code and to transmit a copy of the revised rules to the Louisiana Department
20 of Health and the office of state fire marshal.

DIGEST

Changes the La. Dept. of Health's rule to require hospital architectural plans to comply with
the most recent edition of the Facility Guidelines Institute, Guidelines for Design and
Construction of Hospitals and Outpatient Facilities.
Directs the La. Register to print the amendment in the La. Administrative Code.
