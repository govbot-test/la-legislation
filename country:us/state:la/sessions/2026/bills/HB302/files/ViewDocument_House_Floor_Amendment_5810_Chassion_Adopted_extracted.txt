1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, line 2, after "enact" delete the remainder of the line and delete line 3 in its
3 entirety and insert following:
4 "R.S. 26:911(A)(7), relative to vapor products; to prohibit certain acts relative to the sale
5 of"
6 AMENDMENT NO. 2
7 On page 1, line 7, change "R.S. 26:902.1" to "R.S. 26:911(A)(7)"
8 AMENDMENT NO. 3
9 On page 1, delete lines 8 through 20 in their entirety and insert the following:
10 "§911. Acts prohibited
11 A. No person, agent, associate, employee, representative, or servant of any
12 person shall permit any of the following acts to be done on or about any premises
13 which sells or offers for sale tobacco products, alternative nicotine products, or vapor
14 products:
15 * * *
16 (7)(a) When prohibited by municipal or parish ordinance, the sale, offer for
17 sale, storing, or maintaining of stock of vapor products on any licensed premises
18 situated within three hundred feet or less of a school as defined by R.S.
19 17:405(A)(2).
20 (b) In municipalities and in unincorporated areas which are divided into
21 subdivisions with streets, blocks, and sidewalks, subject to the adoption of the
22 alternate method of measurement as provided for in Subparagraph (c) of this
23 Paragraph, the distance of three hundred feet shall be measured as a person walks
24 using the sidewalk from the nearest point of the property line of the school to the
25 nearest point of the licensed premises. The provisions of this Paragraph shall apply
26 to any licensed premises upon the adoption of the municipal or parish ordinance.
27 (c) A municipality or parish may adopt an ordinance establishing an alternate
28 method of measurement of the three hundred foot limitation by measuring in a
29 straight line from the nearest point of the property line of the school to the nearest
30 point of the licensed premises. Such alternate method of measurement applies only
31 prospectively to any new permit issued on or after the date the ordinance establishing
32 the alternate measurement has been adopted.
33 * * *"
