SCASB4 3005 1001
SENATE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
No. 4 by Senator Fesi
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, line 4, after "fluoridation of" insert "certain"
3 AMENDMENT NO. 2
4 On page 1, line 5, after "election;" insert "to provide for a procedure for water systems to
5 cease fluoridation;"
6 AMENDMENT NO. 3
7 On page 1, line 9, change "prohibition" to "requirements"
8 AMENDMENT NO. 4
9 On page 1, line 13, after "Louisiana" insert "that does not have added fluoride as of July
10 1, 2026,"
11 AMENDMENT NO. 5
12 On page 3, between lines 23 and 24, insert the following:
13 "D. Any water system that has added fluoride as of July 1, 2026, may cease
14 fluoridation only if the following conditions are met:
15 (1) A petition requesting to cease fluoridation has been signed by at least fifteen
16 percent of the registered voters who reside in the precincts served by the water system,
17 as certified by the parish registrar of voters, and has been presented to the governing
18 authority of each municipality or parish in whose jurisdiction the system provides
19 service.
20 (2) A local election has been called after the petition has been received by each
21 governing authority pursuant to Paragraph (1) of this Subsection. Participation in the
22 election shall be limited to those precincts in which the water system provides service.
23 The local election shall be called and conducted by the governing authority of each
24 municipality in whose jurisdiction the water system provides service if all of the
25 registered voters served by the water system reside wholly within the jurisdiction of one
26 or more municipalities. However, the local election shall instead be called and
27 conducted by the governing authority of each parish in whose jurisdiction the water
28 system provides service if either of the following applies:
29 (a) The water system also provides service to registered voters in
30 unincorporated areas of one or more parishes.
31 (b) The water system was created or franchised by the parish or parishes.
32 (3) Each local election pertaining to ceasing fluoridation of the water system has
33 been held in accordance with state and local law, and the certified results of each
34 election show that a majority of the registered voters who cast a vote in the election
35 approve the fluoridation."
36 AMENDMENT NO. 6
37 On page 3, line 24, change "D." to "E."
