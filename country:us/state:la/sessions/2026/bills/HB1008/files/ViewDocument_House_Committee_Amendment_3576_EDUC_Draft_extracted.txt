Section 1:
HCAHB1008 224 3576
HOUSE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
by Representative Owen
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 3, delete lines 1 through 5 and insert the following:
3 "(c) Exercising free speech during student instruction consistent with
4 his academic field and the subject matter of the course.
5 (d) Exercising free speech outside the scope of student instruction,
6 including extramural free speech involving public appearances, publications,
7 and other expression on topics outside his academic field."
8 AMENDMENT NO. 2
9 On page 4, delete lines 1 through 5 and insert the following:
10 "(a) Reinstatement, back pay, and restoration of benefits or tenure
11 status if the faculty member proves by a preponderance of the evidence that
12 an adverse action against him was retaliation for conduct provided for in
13 Paragraph (B)(1) of this Section.
14 (b) Compensatory damages, including treble damages for willful
15 violations.
16 (c) Reasonable attorney fees and court costs.
17 (d) Punitive damages of up to five hundred thousand dollars for
18 egregious or repeated violations."
19
20 AMENDMENT NO. 3
21 On page 4, between lines 8 and 9, insert the following:
22 "E. Nothing in this Part shall be construed to do either of the
23 following:
24 (1) Limit or restrict the authority of an institution to make legitimate,
25 merit-based personnel decisions, including the granting or denial of tenure,
26 promotion, or any other employment action.
27 (2) Prohibit an institution from denying tenure, promotion, or any
28 other benefit to a faculty member who fails to satisfy established, codified
29 performance standards, rubrics, teaching effectiveness criteria, research
30 expectations, service requirements, or other legitimate academic or
31 institutional criteria, as long as such decision is not made in retaliation for
32 conduct provided for in Paragraph (B)(1) of this Section."
33 AMENDMENT NO. 4
34 On page 4, at the beginning of line 9, delete "E." and insert "F."
35 AMENDMENT NO. 5
36 On page 4, at the beginning of line 12, delete "F." and insert "G."
