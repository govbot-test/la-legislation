Section 1:
2026 Regular Session ACT No. 600 ENROLLED
BY SENATOR KLEINPETER
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 14:81.1(A)(3), relative to offenses affecting the public morals; to provide
3 relative to child sexual abuse materials; to provide that the crime of child sexual
4 abuse materials does not apply to persons under the age of seventeen under certain
5 circumstances; to provide relative to the crime of "sexting"; to provide relative to
6 definitions; and to provide for related matters.
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
8 Section 1. R.S. 14:81.1(A)(3) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
9 §81.1. Child sexual abuse materials
10 A.(1) * * *
11 (3)(a) When a person who is under the age of seventeen years violates
12 Paragraph (A)(1) of this Section by either of the following acts, the provisions
13 of R.S. 14:81.1.1 relative to the crime of "sexting" shall govern the offense,
14 unless the person committed the offense with the intent to obtain anything of
15 value, secure a privilege or advantage, or harass or cause emotional distress to
16 any person:
17 (i) Using a computer or telecommunication device to knowingly and
18 voluntarily transmit an indecent visual depiction of himself.
19 (ii) Receiving or possessing an indecent visual depiction of a person that
20 was transmitted by the person depicted in the image.
