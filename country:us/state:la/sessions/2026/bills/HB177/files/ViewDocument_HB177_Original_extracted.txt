Section 1:
HLS 26RS-818 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE MUSCARELLO
COURTS/COURT REPORTERS: Excludes court reporters from the two-year waiting
period for rehiring relative to termination of public service
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 42:1121(K), relative to assistance after termination of public service; to
3 provide for the rehiring of retired court reporters; to permit the rehiring of retired
4 court reporters previously employed by a governmental entity or agency; and to
5 provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 42:1121(K) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §1121. Assistance to certain persons after termination of public service
9 * * *
10 K. Nothing in this Section shall prohibit a former official court reporter
11 employed in any judicial district court or city court from being hired on a contractual
12 basis or reemployed with the same court or jurisdiction at any time after retirement
13 or upon leaving the employment of the court.
DIGEST
The digest printed below was prepared by House Legislative Services. It constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument. The keyword, one-liner, abstract, and digest do not constitute
part of the law or proof or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
HB 177 Original 2026 Regular Session Muscarello
Abstract: Excepts a retired court reporter from the two-year prohibition on being
reemployed by a governmental entity or agency.
Present law prohibits a former public employee from assisting another person, for
compensation, for a two-year period following the termination of his public employment.

HLS 26RS-818 ORIGINAL
HB NO. 177
Proposed law retains present law; however, proposed law authorizes a former official court
reporter employed in any judicial district court or city court to be hired on a contractual basis
