area is ranked No. 2 in America for economic development projects; and
WHEREAS, the financial impact that the city and its diverse attractions contribute
to the state's financial position has not been holistically measured; and
Page 1 of 2
HR NO. 370 ENROLLED
WHEREAS, a clear picture of the economic benefit that the city of New Orleans
contributes to the rest of the state will be helpful in determining allocation of state resources
in future years as the city works to continue securing tourism dollars and big ticket events
such as sporting events and championships, national festivals, and conventions.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby authorize and direct the legislative auditor to study the
economic impact that the city of New Orleans has had on the state fisc over the past ten
fiscal years and to report his findings to the House Committee on Appropriations prior to the
convening of the 2027 Regular Session of the Legislature of Louisiana.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the secretary of the Department of Revenue
assist the legislative auditor with this study by supplying historical collections information
as requested by the legislative auditor.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be delivered to the
legislative auditor and the secretary of the Department of Revenue.
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
