1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 13:5554(X), relative to the premium costs of group hospital and
3 health insurance for retired sheriffs and retired deputy sheriffs in DeSoto Parish; to
4 provide for eligibility of premium costs; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 13:5554(X) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
7 §5554. Group insurance; kinds; amounts; subrogation
8 * * *
9 X.(1) Notwithstanding the provisions of Subsection D of this Section, the
10 sheriff of DeSoto Parish shall pay out of the sheriff's general fund the premium costs
11 of group insurance for any retired sheriff and any retired deputy sheriff who retired
12 from the DeSoto Parish Sheriff's Office as follows:
13 (1) (a) One hundred percent of the premium costs of group hospital, surgical,
14 medical expense, and dental insurance, and the first ten thousand dollars of life
15 insurance contracted for under the provisions of this Section if the sheriff or deputy
16 sheriff retired with thirty years or more of creditable service with the sheriff's office
17 of DeSoto Parish.
