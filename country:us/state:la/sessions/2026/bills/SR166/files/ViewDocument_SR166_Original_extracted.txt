SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 166
BY SENATOR BOUDREAUX
for providing reliable public water service to the citizens of St. Landry Parish.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend the Lewisburg-Bellevue Water System for providing reliable public water
3 service to the citizens of St. Landry Parish.
4 WHEREAS, the Lewisburg-Bellevue Water System was established in 1969 for the
5 purpose of providing safe, reliable, and centralized public water service to the rural
6 communities of Lewisburg and Bellevue and surrounding areas of St. Landry Parish,
7 Louisiana; and
8 WHEREAS, prior to the establishment of the system, many residents of these rural
9 communities depended upon private wells and limited water sources that often lacked
10 dependable quality, pressure, and adequate fire protection capabilities; and
11 WHEREAS, local citizens and community leaders organized the Lewisburg-Bellevue
12 Water System as a nonprofit rural water corporation dedicated to improving public health,
13 sanitation, infrastructure, and quality of life for residents throughout northern and western
14 Opelousas and surrounding unincorporated communities; and
15 WHEREAS, through decades of service, the Lewisburg-Bellevue Water System has
16 expanded and modernized its infrastructure to meet the growing needs of the community by
17 investing in groundwater wells, pumping stations, elevated storage facilities, chlorination
18 systems, distribution pipelines, and fire protection infrastructure; and
Page 1 of 3

1 WHEREAS, the Lewisburg-Bellevue Water System has continuously operated in
2 accordance with state and federal drinking water regulations under the oversight of the
3 Louisiana Department of Health and the United States Environmental Protection Agency,
4 while providing annual water quality reporting and maintaining compliance with public
5 health standards; and
6 WHEREAS, the system has played an essential role in supporting residential
7 development, improving sanitation, enhancing fire protection capabilities, strengthening
8 property values, and promoting economic and community growth throughout rural portions
9 of St. Landry Parish; and
10 WHEREAS, the Lewisburg and Bellevue communities possess a rich cultural and
11 agricultural heritage deeply rooted in the history of St. Landry Parish, and the creation of the
12 Lewisburg-Bellevue Water System represented a major advancement in modern public
13 infrastructure for these historic rural communities; and
14 WHEREAS, the continued operation and service of the Lewisburg-Bellevue Water
15 System remains vital to the health, welfare, safety, and prosperity of the citizens it serves.
16 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
17 does hereby recognize and commend the Lewisburg-Bellevue Water System for its
18 longstanding commitment to providing reliable public water service and infrastructure
19 improvements to the citizens of St. Landry Parish since its founding in 1969.
20 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana does
21 hereby express appreciation for the dedication of the board members, employees, community
22 leaders, and residents whose efforts have contributed to the success and continued operation
23 of the Lewisburg-Bellevue Water System.
24 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
25 Lewisburg-Bellevue Water System in recognition of its important contributions to the people
26 and communities of St. Landry Parish and the state of Louisiana.
