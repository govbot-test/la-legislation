Section 1:
SLS 26RS-487 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY SENATOR PRESSLY
MEDICAID. Provides relative to Medicaid. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 46:260.52, relative to Medicaid; to provide for the administration
3 of the Medicaid managed care program; and to provide for related matters.
4 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
5 Section 1. R.S. 46:260.52 is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
6 §460.52. Exemptions
7 The provisions of this Part shall not apply to any entity contracted with the
8 Louisiana Department of Health to provide fiscal intermediary services in processing
9 claims of the health care providers any healthcare provider.
The original instrument and the following digest, which constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument, were prepared by Senate Legislative Services.
The keyword, summary, and digest do not constitute part of the law or proof
or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
DIGEST
SB 158 Original 2026 Regular Session Pressly
Present law establishes requirements relative to the administration of the Medicaid managed
care program and exempts certain entities from the requirements of present law.
Proposed law retains present law and makes technical changes.
