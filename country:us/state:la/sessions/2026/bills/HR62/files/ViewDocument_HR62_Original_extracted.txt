HLS 26RS-1879 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 62
BY REPRESENTATIVE WALTERS
Award for best actor and celebrates his Louisiana roots
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Michael B. Jordan for receiving the 2026 Academy Award for best actor and
3 celebrates his Louisiana roots.
4 WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize Michael B. Jordan for his Louisiana roots
5 and to commend him for receiving the 2026 Academy Award for best actor; and
6 WHEREAS, Michael B. Jordan was born on February 9, 1987, in Santa Ana,
7 California, to Michael A. Jordan and Donna Jordan; his father's family is from Shreveport,
8 Louisiana; and
9 WHEREAS, Mr. Jordan grew up in Newark, New Jersey, and graduated from
10 Newark Arts High School in 2005; as a child, he worked as a model and began acting in
11 1999; and
12 WHEREAS, Mr. Jordan is recognized for his refined and compelling
13 characterizations and for his frequent collaborations with director Ryan Coogler; he gained
14 notoriety as an actor following his critically-acclaimed portrayal of Oscar Grant in the 2013
15 film Fruitvale Station; and
16 WHEREAS, Mr. Jordan rekindled his star status by portraying Adonis Creed in the
17 2015 film Creed; he received praise for his performance as the villain Erik Killmonger in
18 the 2018 film Black Panther; and
19 WHEREAS, Mr. Jordan made his directorial debut in 2023 with the film Creed III
20 and earned widespread acclaim for his work; and
Page 1 of 2

1 WHEREAS, Mr. Jordan reunited with Coogler to star in the 2025 film Sinners, a
2 supernatural horror thriller set in Clarksdale, Mississippi, during the Jim Crow era; he
3 received widespread praise for his performance and won his first Academy Award for best
4 actor; and
5 WHEREAS, Michael B. Jordan is the sixth black man to win best actor at the
6 Academy Awards, joining Will Smith, Forest Whitaker, Jamie Foxx, Denzel Washington,
7 and Sidney Poitier, and he is most deserving of the highest recognition for his achievement.
8 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
9 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Michael B. Jordan for receiving the 2026
10 Academy Award for best actor; does hereby celebrate his Louisiana roots; and does hereby
11 extend sincere wishes that he remain prosperous in all of his endeavors.
