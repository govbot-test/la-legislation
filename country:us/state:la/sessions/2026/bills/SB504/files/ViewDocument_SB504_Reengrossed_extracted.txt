DIGEST
SB 504 Reengrossed 2026 Regular Session Carter
Present law (R.S. 17:2925) provides that every student, by the end of the 8th grade, must
begin to develop an Individual Graduation Plan or Individualized Education Program, if
applicable, to guide the next academic year's course work and assist in exploring educational
and career possibilities.
Proposed law retains present law and further provides that every Individual Graduation Plan
and Individualized Education Program must include vocational training opportunities in the
student's school district.
Proposed law provides that a parent or legal guardian of a student entering 11th grade can
request an update to the student's Individual Graduation Plan or Individualized Education
Program.
Proposed law provides that each school must use existing school staff, counselors, and other
available resources to carry out proposed law.
Present law (R.S. 17:3996) provides that charter schools are exempt from statutory mandates
or requirements applicable to public school except for certain laws listed in present law.
Proposed law retains present law and further provides that individual graduation plans are
applicable to charter schools.
Effective on August 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 17:2925(A)(4), (5), (6), and (7); adds R.S. 17:2925(A)(8) and (9) and (D), and
3996(B)(92))
