HOUSE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
No. 504 by Senator Carter
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, line 2, delete "R.S. 17:2925(A)(4), (5), (6), and (7)" and insert "R.S.
3 17:2925(A)(2) and (4) through (7)"
4 AMENDMENT NO. 2
5 On page 1, line 3, after "Plans;" and before "to provide" insert "to provide relative to
6 requirements pertaining to such plans for students attending certain charter schools;"
7 AMENDMENT NO. 3
8 On page 1, line 8, delete "R.S. 17:2925(A)(4), (5), (6), and (7)" and insert "R.S.
9 17:2925(A)(2) and (4) through (7)"
10 AMENDMENT NO. 4
11 On page 1, between lines 12 and 13, insert the following:
12 "(2)(a) By Except as provided in Subparagraph (b) of this Paragraph,
13 by the end of the eighth grade, each student's Individual Graduation Plan or the
14 student's Individualized Education Program, if applicable, shall list the required core
15 courses to be taken through the tenth grade and shall identify the courses to be taken
16 in the first year of high school. Students who fail to meet the standard for promotion
17 to the ninth grade, pursuant to policies adopted by the State Board of Elementary and
18 Secondary Education, shall have any necessary remedial courses included in their
19 Individual Graduation Plan.
20 (b)(i) A charter school that terminates in the eighth grade shall provide
21 written notice to each eighth grade student and his parent or legal guardian of
22 the purpose of an Individual Graduation Plan, an explanation of the different
23 types of high school diplomas, and the courses required for each type of diploma
24 so that the student is prepared to complete his plan with the list of required
25 courses to be taken upon the student's enrollment in high school but no later
26 than October first of the student's ninth grade year.
27 (ii) A charter school that terminates in the eighth grade may partner
28 with a high school that an eighth grade student plans to attend for assistance in
29 providing for the student's Individual Graduation Plan or Individualized
30 Education Program, if applicable, as provided in Subparagraph (a) of this
31 Paragraph."
32 * * *"
33 AMENDMENT NO. 5
34 On page 1, delete lines 16 and 17 and on page 2, delete line 1 and insert the following:
35 "(5) Each student, with the assistance of his parent or legal guardian and
36 school counselor, shall be offered available vocational training opportunities."
