2026 Regular Session
Bill No. 222 by Senator Pressly
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 2, between lines 2 and 3, insert the following:
3 "§302. Definitions
4 As used in this Chapter, the following terms have the following
5 meanings:
6 (1) "Cardiopulmonary resuscitation" or "CPR" means an emergency
7 procedure combining chest compressions and breaths to maintain blood flow
8 and oxygenation during cardiac arrest.
9 (2) "Community psychiatric support and treatment services" or
10 "CPST" services, means CMS-approved Medicaid mental health rehabilitation
11 services designed to reduce disability from mental illness, restore functional
12 skills of daily living, build natural supports, and achieve identified
13 person-centered goals or objectives through counseling, clinical
14 psycho-education, and ongoing monitoring needs as set forth in an
15 individualized treatment plan.
16 (3) "CMS" means the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services.
17 (4) "Department" means the Louisiana Department of Health.
18 (5) "Psychosocial rehabilitation services" or "PSR" services, means
19 CMS-approved Medicaid mental health rehabilitation services designed to assist
20 the individual with compensating for or eliminating functional deficits and
21 interpersonal or environmental barriers associated with mental illness through
22 skill building and supportive interventions to restore and rehabilitate social and
23 interpersonal skills and daily living skills."
24 AMENDMENT NO. 2
25 On page 2, line 3, change "§302." to "§303."
26 AMENDMENT NO. 3

33 "B. The department may require CPR, first aid certification, or seizure
34 assessment training only for"
35 AMENDMENT NO. 6
36 On page 2, line 22, change "§304." to "§305." and delete "provisionally"
