HB 444 Original 2026 Regular Session Marcelle
Abstract: Changes the membership of the Recreation and Park Commission for the Parish
of East Baton Rouge.
Present law creates the Recreation and Park Commission for the Parish of East Baton Rouge
as a subdivision of the state to plan, own, and operate public recreational and park facilities
in the parish.

Proposed law retains present law.
Present law provides for a nine-member commission comprised of five ex officio members
and four members appointed by the governing authority of city of Baton Rouge, parish of
East Baton Rouge for three-year terms. Provides that the five ex officio members are the
mayors of the following municipalities: Baker, Baton Rouge, Central, St. George, and
Zachary. Authorizes each mayor to designate a person to serve in his place as a member of
the commission. Requires that at least two of the appointed members be residents of the city
of Baton Rouge, and at least one be a resident of an unincorporated area of East Baton
Rouge Parish.
Proposed law adds two ex officio members to provide for an 11-member commission.
Provides that the two additional members are the member of the La. Senate who represents
District No. 14 and the member of the La. House of Representatives who represents District
No. 61. Authorizes each member to designate a person to serve in his place as a member of
the commission.
Present law provides that five members constitute a quorum. Proposed law instead provides
that six members constitute a quorum.
Effective upon signature of governor or lapse of time for gubernatorial action.
(Amends R.S. 33:4570.1)
