SCASB405 3005 3246
SENATE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
No. 405 by Senator Talbot
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, line 2, after "facilities;" delete the remainder of the line and delete line 3
3 AMENDMENT NO. 2
4 On page 1, line 4, delete "rated facilities;" and insert "to provide for a statewide quality
5 oversight initiative for nursing facilities; to provide for goals and strategies;"
6 AMENDMENT NO. 3
7 On page 1, line 5, change "effectiveness" to "reporting"
8 AMENDMENT NO. 4
9 On page 1, line 9, change "One-star" to "Statewide quality oversight for"
10 AMENDMENT NO. 5
11 On page 1, delete lines 10 through 17, delete page 2, and on page 3, delete lines 1 through
12 23, and insert the following:
13 "A. The Louisiana Department of Health shall develop a statewide quality
14 oversight initiative for nursing facilities.
15 (1) The short-term goal of the initiative shall be to stabilize performance of
16 facilities by identifying gaps, deploying targeted incentives, aligning interventions with
17 the most pressing quality risks, and identifying funding for execution of the initiative.
18 (2) The long-term goal shall be to create a more robust statewide quality
19 oversight system capable of supporting and driving continuous improvement and
20 overall system level changes.
21 (3) Stakeholders, including trade and advocacy associations, individuals engaged
22 in the daily operational practice of nursing facility care, and quality improvement
23 organizations shall be identified and utilized by the department to build a coordinated
24 approach centered on collaboration, education, and practical tools.
25 B. The Louisiana Department of Health, through the office of aging and adult
26 services, shall identify and develop internal subject-matter experts to support the
27 statewide quality oversight initiative. The department may utilize such efforts as
28 considered appropriate, including but not limited to:
29 (1) Data dashboards and public-facing online tools.
30 (2) Resident and family satisfaction surveys.
31 (3) Staff training plans and ongoing staff training and development.
32 (4) Participation in the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services Nursing
33 Home Staffing Campaign.
34 C. The department shall submit an initial report to the Senate and House
35 committees on health and welfare on the status of the statewide quality oversight
36 initiative on January 1, 2028, and annually on the first of January thereafter."
