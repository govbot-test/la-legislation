SLS 26RS-674 ENGROSSED
2026 Regular Session
BY SENATOR TALBOT
NURSING HOMES. Provides for a statewide quality oversight initiative for nursing
facilities. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 40:2009.10.2, relative to nursing facilities; to provide for a statewide quality
3 oversight initiative for nursing facilities; to provide for goals and strategies; to
4 provide for the duties and responsibilities of the Louisiana Department of Health; to
5 provide for reporting; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 40:2009.10.2 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §2009.10.2. Statewide quality oversight for nursing facilities; state
9 requirements
10 A. The Louisiana Department of Health shall develop a statewide quality
11 oversight initiative for nursing facilities.
12 (1) The short-term goal of the initiative shall be to stabilize performance
13 of facilities by identifying gaps, deploying targeted incentives, aligning
14 interventions with the most pressing quality risks, and identifying funding for
15 execution of the initiative.
16 (2) The long-term goal shall be to create a more robust statewide quality
17 oversight system capable of supporting and driving continuous improvement

1 and overall system level changes.
2 (3) Stakeholders, including trade and advocacy associations, individuals
3 engaged in the daily operational practice of nursing facility care, and quality
4 improvement organizations, shall be identified and utilized by the department
5 to build a coordinated approach centered on collaboration, education, and
6 practical tools.
7 B. The Louisiana Department of Health, through the office of aging and
8 adult services, shall identify and develop internal subject-matter experts to
9 support the statewide quality oversight initiative. The department may utilize
10 such efforts as considered appropriate, including but not limited to:
11 (1) Data dashboards and public-facing online tools.
12 (2) Resident and family satisfaction surveys.
13 (3) Staff training plans and ongoing staff training and development.
14 (4) Participation in the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services
15 Nursing Home Staffing Campaign.
16 C. The department shall submit an initial report to the Senate and House
17 committees on health and welfare on the status of the statewide quality
18 oversight initiative on January 1, 2028, and annually on the first of January
19 thereafter.
The original instrument and the following digest, which constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument, were prepared by Senate Legislative Services.
The keyword, summary, and digest do not constitute part of the law or proof
or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]

SB 405 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Talbot
Proposed law requires the La. Dept. of Health to develop a statewide quality oversight
initiative for nursing facilities.
Proposed law provides for the short- and long-term goals of the initiative.
Proposed law requires the La. Dept. of Health to identify and utilize stakeholders to build
a coordinated approach on quality improvement.
Proposed law requires the office of aging and adult services to identify and develop internal
subject-matter experts to support the statewide quality oversight initiative.
