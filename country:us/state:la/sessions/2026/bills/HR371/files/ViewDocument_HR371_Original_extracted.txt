2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 371
BY REPRESENTATIVE SPELL
U.S.: Commemorates the commencement of the American Revolution and recognizes the
importance of America's founding documents
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commemorate the commencement of the American Revolution and recognize America's
3 founding documents.
4 WHEREAS, the commencement of the American Revolution in 1775 led to the
5 liberation of the American colonies and people from British rule; and
6 WHEREAS, the American victory at Yorktown and the signing of the Treaty of Paris
7 led to the creation of the United States of America and the founding documents of American
8 liberty, justice, and freedom, the Declaration of Independence, the Constitution of the United
9 States of America, including the Bill of Rights, and the Federalist Papers; and
10 WHEREAS, the constitution creates the framework of our government and
11 establishes the separation of powers and system of checks and balances that protects
12 America's freedoms and system of justice; and
13 WHEREAS, the Legislature of Louisiana has demonstrated its commitment to the
14 education of Louisiana's people on these historical documents and events by enacting laws
15 requiring their inclusion in the education of the state's students.
16 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
17 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commemorate the commencement of the American
18 Revolution and does hereby recognize the importance of America's founding documents.
19 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the Legislature
20 of Louisiana encourages the people of Louisiana to study and acknowledge the significance
