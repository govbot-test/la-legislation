HLS 26RS-5664 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 350
BY REPRESENTATIVE FREEMAN
SPECIAL DAY/WEEK/MONTH: Designates June 7, 2026, as Reggio Family Clock
Donation Day in Louisiana
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate Sunday, June 7, 2026, as Reggio Family Clock Donation Day in Louisiana.
3 WHEREAS, Louisiana citizens are heirs to and benefactors of a rich and diverse
4 historical tapestry; and
5 WHEREAS, each year, communities and museums across Louisiana celebrate the
6 history of the state and its artifacts; and
7 WHEREAS, in museums across Louisiana, precious artifacts are curated and
8 preserved and presented to the citizens of Louisiana and those interested in gaining a broader
9 understanding of the past, present, and hope for the future of the state; and
10 WHEREAS, Godfrey "Frey" Reggio, III, and the Reggio family will present the
11 Reggio Family Clock to the Cabildo in New Orleans for display as a part of its permanent
12 collections during the Gálvez and Louisiana in the American Revolution exhibition; and
13 WHEREAS, this timepiece was crafted around 1751 and has been handed down from
14 its original owner, François Marie, Chevalier de Reggio; the artifact is a small but tangible
15 link to the objects and people present during some of most pivotal moments in Louisiana
16 history.
17 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
18 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby designate Sunday, June 7, 2026, as Reggio Family
19 Clock Donation Day in Louisiana and does hereby encourage all citizens of Louisiana to
20 share in the joy of this special day.
