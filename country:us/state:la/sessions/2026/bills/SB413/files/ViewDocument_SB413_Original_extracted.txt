SLS 26RS-790 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE BILL NO. 413
BY SENATOR MCMATH
PHYSICIANS. Provides relative to the Louisiana State Board of Medical Examiners.
(8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 37:1270(A)(10), relative to the Louisiana State Board of Medical Examiners;
3 to provide for the duties and powers of the board; and to provide for related matters.
4 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
5 Section 1. R.S. 37:1270(A)(10) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
6 §1270. Duties and powers of the board
7 A. The board shall:
8 * * *
9 (10) Appoint an executive director who is not a physician licensed by the
10 board.
11 * * *
