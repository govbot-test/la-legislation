BY REPRESENTATIVE CARPENTER
BAIL: Provides relative to the imposition of a statewide bail schedule for individuals
arrested for certain sexual offenses involving children
1 AN ACT
2 To enact Code of Criminal Procedure Article 315(D), relative to bail; to provide for the
3 minimum fixing of the amount of bail in relation to the type of offense committed;
4 to provide for duties of the court; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. Code of Criminal Procedure Article 315(D) is hereby enacted to read as
7 follows:
8 Art. 315. Schedules of bail
9 * * *
10 D.(1) Each district court with criminal jurisdiction in this state shall set an
11 initial bail amount of not less than one million dollars for an individual arrested for
12 any of the following offenses when committed against a victim under the age of
13 eighteen years:
14 (a) First degree rape (R.S. 14:42).
15 (b) Second degree rape (R.S. 14:42.1).
16 (c) Third degree rape (R.S. 14:43).
17 (d) Sexual battery (R.S. 14:43.1).
18 (e) Oral sexual battery (R.S. 14:43.3).

HB 276 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Carpenter
Abstract: Provides relative to the imposition of a statewide bail schedule for individuals
arrested for certain sexual offenses involving children.
Present law provides for schedules of bail. Further authorizes certain courts to fix bail
amounts in certain cases.
Proposed law retains present law.
Proposed law requires each district court with criminal jurisdiction in the state of La. to set
an initial bail amount of not less than $1,000,000 for an individual arrested for any of the
following offenses when committed against a victim under the age of 18 years:
(1) First degree rape (R.S. 14:42).
(2) Second degree rape (R.S. 14:42.1).
(3) Third degree rape (R.S. 14:43).
(4) Sexual battery (R.S. 14:43.1).
(5) Oral sexual battery (R.S. 14:43.3).
(6) Child sexual abuse materials (R.S. 14:81.1).
(7) Molestation of a juvenile or a person with a physical or mental disability (R.S.
14:81.2).
(8) Aggravated crime against nature (R.S. 14:89.1).
(9) Human trafficking (R.S. 14:46.2)
Proposed law requires the court to consider the factors provided in present law (C.Cr.P. Art.
316) before fixing any initial bail amount pursuant to proposed law.
(Adds C.Cr.P. Art. 315(D))
