HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 306
BY REPRESENTATIVES LYONS AND GREEN
COMMENDATIONS: Commends Brandon Lionel Washington for graduating as
valedictorian of the Class of 2026 at John Ehret High School
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Brandon Lionel Washington for graduating as valedictorian of the Class of
3 2026 at John Ehret High School.
4 WHEREAS, Brandon Lionel Washington ranked first among four hundred sixty-four
5 of his peers with an extraordinary 4.72 GPA, and his success is proof that consistency is the
6 highest form of excellence; and
7 WHEREAS, Brandon is known as "Mr. President" by his peers; he has earned
8 Student of the Year recognition multiple times and excelled as a dual enrollment student; his
9 academic path demonstrates that potential is not determined by circumstances, but by
10 commitment; and
11 WHEREAS, Brandon was selected as Homecoming King and earned the
12 Superintendent's Champion distinction; and
13 WHEREAS, Brandon competed on the basketball court throughout high school and
14 earned a starting position on the baseball team during his senior year; through sports, he
15 developed an understanding of teamwork, perseverance, and how to perform under pressure;
16 and
17 WHEREAS, Brandon selflessly served with the Kelly KW Foundation, where he
18 supported fire awareness events and provided aid to the less fortunate in his community; and
19 WHEREAS, Brandon's love for music and fashion displays his creativity,
20 individuality, and genuine spirit; and
