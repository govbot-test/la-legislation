HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 306
BY REPRESENTATIVES LYONS AND GREEN
A RESOLUTION
2026 at John Ehret High School.
WHEREAS, Brandon Lionel Washington ranked first among four hundred sixty-four
of his peers with an extraordinary 4.72 GPA, and his success is proof that consistency is the
highest form of excellence; and
WHEREAS, Brandon is known as "Mr. President" by his peers; he has earned
Student of the Year recognition multiple times and excelled as a dual enrollment student; his
academic path demonstrates that potential is not determined by circumstances, but by
commitment; and
WHEREAS, Brandon was selected as Homecoming King and earned the
Superintendent's Champion distinction; and
WHEREAS, Brandon competed on the basketball court throughout high school and
earned a starting position on the baseball team during his senior year; through sports, he
developed an understanding of teamwork, perseverance, and how to perform under pressure;
and
WHEREAS, Brandon selflessly served with the Kelly KW Foundation, where he
supported fire awareness events and provided aid to the less fortunate in his community; and
WHEREAS, Brandon's love for music and fashion displays his creativity,
individuality, and genuine spirit; and

2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 306
BY REPRESENTATIVES LYONS AND GREEN
A RESOLUTION
To commend Brandon Lionel Washington for graduating as valedictorian of the Class of
2026 at John Ehret High School.
WHEREAS, Brandon Lionel Washington ranked first among four hundred sixty-four
of his peers with an extraordinary 4.72 GPA, and his success is proof that consistency is the
highest form of excellence; and
WHEREAS, Brandon is known as "Mr. President" by his peers; he has earned
Student of the Year recognition multiple times and excelled as a dual enrollment student; his
academic path demonstrates that potential is not determined by circumstances, but by
commitment; and
WHEREAS, Brandon was selected as Homecoming King and earned the
Superintendent's Champion distinction; and
WHEREAS, Brandon competed on the basketball court throughout high school and
earned a starting position on the baseball team during his senior year; through sports, he
developed an understanding of teamwork, perseverance, and how to perform under pressure;
and
WHEREAS, Brandon selflessly served with the Kelly KW Foundation, where he
supported fire awareness events and provided aid to the less fortunate in his community; and
WHEREAS, Brandon's love for music and fashion displays his creativity,
individuality, and genuine spirit; and
