HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 9
BY REPRESENTATIVE BAYHAM
CONDOLENCES: Expresses the condolences of the House of Representatives on the death
of Charles James "Charlie" Kirk, co-founder of Turning Point USA
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 To express the condolences of the Legislature of Louisiana upon the death of Charles James
3 "Charlie" Kirk, co-founder of Turning Point USA.
4 WHEREAS, Charlie Kirk was born on October 14, 1993, in Arlington Heights,
5 Illinois, where he attended Wheeling High School; he later studied at Harper College, a
6 public community college in the northwest Chicago suburb of Palatine, before leaving to
7 pursue a career in political activism; and
8 WHEREAS, Mr. Kirk co-founded Turning Point USA in 2012 with the help of his
9 early mentor, the late Bill Montgomery; he launched the conservative advocacy group as a
10 shoestring campus effort and built it into a well-funded national organization; he used the
11 success of Turning Point USA to found several affiliated groups, including Turning Point
12 Action and Turning Point Faith; and
13 WHEREAS, Mr. Kirk's success as a conservative political activist increased his
14 professional profile, and he became a widely-known media figure; he hosted "The Charlie
15 Kirk Show" on talk radio, regularly appeared on conservative media outlets, and addressed
16 both the 2024 Republican National Convention and the 2022 Conservative Political Action
17 Conference; and
18 WHEREAS, Mr. Kirk focused on campus activism, youth engagement, and faith-
19 based organizing; he was also the author of several books; and
