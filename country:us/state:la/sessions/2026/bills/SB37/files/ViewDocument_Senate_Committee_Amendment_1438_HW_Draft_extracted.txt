2 On page 3, delete lines 6 through 17 and insert the following:
3 "A.(1) Every early learning center and school with a prekindergarten program
4 that was built, in whole or in part, prior to 1978 shall have a lead risk assessment
5 performed by a certified lead risk assessor and submit the results to the department as
6 a requirement for obtaining any departmental approval of plans and specifications.
7 (2) The lead risk assessment shall include lead testing results which shall include
8 testing of all painted surfaces of the building, dust, water, and soil adjacent to the
9 building and on any property that is regularly utilized by the children enrolled at the
10 early learning center or prekindergarten program.
11 B.(1) If test results identify lead hazards, the department shall not grant
12 approval of plans and specifications for a new early learning center or new school with
13 a prekindergarten program until appropriate lead hazard control methods have been
14 verified by a clearance inspection authorized by the state health officer.
15 (2) If test results identify lead hazards the early learning center or school with
16 a prekindergarten program shall enter into a lead hazard abatement agreement with
17 the department which shall include required compliance with certain timelines and
18 efforts to mitigate danger to children on the premises as required by the state health
19 officer.
