HLS 26RS-1659 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 25
BY REPRESENTATIVE LARVADAIN
SPECIAL DAY/WEEK/MONTH: Designates March 16, 2026, as Opportunity in Action
Day at the capitol
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate Monday, March 16, 2026, as Opportunity in Action Day at the capitol.
3 WHEREAS, the Louisiana Opportunity Youth Alliance is a network of direct service
4 providers and advocacy organizations working to ensure that public policies and institutions
5 support the needs of Louisiana's opportunity youth with a focus on building a stronger
6 connective fabric that supports all young people as they transition from school to the
7 workforce; and
8 WHEREAS, an "opportunity youth" is defined as a person between the ages of
9 sixteen and twenty-four years old who is not enrolled in school and does not work; and
10 WHEREAS, Louisiana has the second-highest youth disconnection rate in the
11 country, with more than eighty-six thousand disconnected youth throughout the state, which
12 is three thousand six hundred more people than the maximum capacity of the Caesar's
13 Superdome; and
14 WHEREAS, by prioritizing opportunity youth, Louisiana will expand education and
15 workforce opportunities, expand the pool of talent that employers can access to meet
16 workforce needs, and avert the societal cost associated with disconnection; and
17 WHEREAS, Opportunity in Action Day at the Capitol provides an opportunity for
18 youth and youth-serving organizations throughout Louisiana to come together in the state
19 capitol in Baton Rouge to learn more about the legislative process, engage with elected
20 officials, and celebrate their commitment to improving the lives of opportunity youth in the
21 state.
