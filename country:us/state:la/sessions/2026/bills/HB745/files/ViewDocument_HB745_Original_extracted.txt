WEIGHTS/MEASURES: Extends the existence of special permits for the operation of a

1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 32:387.2(D), relative to special permits for the operation of a
3 combination of vehicles or tandem loads hauling containers to and from port
4 facilities; to extend the expiration date for the permit requirement; and to provide for
5 related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 32:387.2(D) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
8 §387.2. Special permits; vehicles or tandem loads hauling divisible or nondivisible
9 container imports or exports to port facilities
10 * * *
11 D. The provisions of this Section and all legislative authority for the
12 existence of the permits authorized in this Section shall cease to exist on August 1,
13 2026 2030.

HLS 26RS-1253 ORIGINAL
HB NO. 745
Present law provides relative to special permits issued by the secretary of the Dept. of
Transportation and Development. Further provides that authority for permits under present
law ceases on Aug. 1, 2026.
Proposed law modifies present law by extending the permit authority from Aug. 1, 2026 to
Aug. 1, 2030.
(Amends R.S.32:387.2(D))
