14 §100.56. Water Sector Program
15 * * *
16 C. The Water Sector Program is hereby established to provide grant funding
17 for repairs, improvements, and consolidation of community water and sewer
18 systems. The division of administration, office of facility planning and control, and
19 the office of community development shall administer the program in consultation
20 with the Louisiana Department of Health and the Department of Environmental
21 Quality. The division shall establish a working panel comprised of employees of the
22 office of facility planning and control and the office of community development,
23 within the division of administration, the office of public health within the Louisiana
24 Department of Health, and the Department of Environmental Quality. The working
25 panel shall review and rate applications submitted by community water systems,
26 community sewer systems, and local governing authorities and submit
27 recommendations for funding to the commission.
