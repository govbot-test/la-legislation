HLS 26RS-3255 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 56
BY REPRESENTATIVE LAFLEUR
COMMENDATIONS: Commends the Louisiana Lottery Corporation on the occasion of its
thirty-fifth anniversary and recognizes Rose Hudson for twenty years of service as
the president and CEO of the corporation
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 To commend the Louisiana Lottery Corporation on the occasion of its thirty-fifth
3 anniversary; to acknowledge the contributions of the Louisiana Lottery to public
4 education in the state of Louisiana; and to recognize Rose Hudson for twenty years
5 of distinguished service as president and chief executive officer.
6 WHEREAS, the Louisiana Lottery Corporation was established in 1991 pursuant to
7 Article XII, Section 6 of the Constitution of Louisiana and the Louisiana Lottery Corporation
8 Law, and has faithfully served the citizens of Louisiana for thirty-five years; and
9 WHEREAS, since its inception, the Louisiana Lottery has operated with the highest
10 standards of integrity, accountability, and transparency, and these principles are central to
11 its mission and foundational to the trust it holds with the people of Louisiana; and
12 WHEREAS, over the course of thirty-five years of operation, the Louisiana Lottery
13 Corporation has contributed more than five billion dollars to the state treasury; these funds
14 are dedicated entirely to the Minimum Foundation Program, which is the mechanism for
15 financing elementary and secondary public education across the state of Louisiana; and
16 WHEREAS, Rose Hudson has served as president and chief executive officer of the
17 Louisiana Lottery Corporation for twenty years, providing visionary leadership, operational
18 excellence, and an unwavering commitment to the public interest throughout her tenure; and

8 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Legislature of Louisiana does hereby
9 recognize Rose Hudson for twenty years of distinguished service as president and chief
10 executive officer of the Louisiana Lottery Corporation; does hereby extend enduring
11 appreciation for her transformative leadership and dedication to public service; and does
12 hereby commend her commitment to the values of integrity, accountability, and transparency
13 that define the Louisiana Lottery.
