HLS 26RS-3489 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 130
BY REPRESENTATIVE CHENEVERT
SPECIAL DAY/WEEK/MONTH: Commends Film Louisiana and designates April 15,
2026, as Film Louisiana Day at the state capitol
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Film Louisiana for its significant contributions to the economic development
3 and cultural vitality of Louisiana and to designate Wednesday, April 15, 2026, as
4 Film Louisiana Day at the state capitol.
5 WHEREAS, Film Louisiana is a statewide trade association representing the motion
6 picture, television, and digital media production industry; and
7 WHEREAS, Louisiana has earned national and international recognition as a premier
8 destination for film and television production and is commonly known as "Hollywood
9 South" due to its diverse locations, skilled workforce, and well-developed production
10 infrastructure; and
11 WHEREAS, Film Louisiana plays a vital role in advocating for policies that support
12 and sustain the continued growth of Louisiana's film industry and helps to attract major
13 productions and long-term investment to the state; and
14 WHEREAS, the film and television industry provides substantial economic benefits
15 to Louisiana by creating thousands of jobs for residents, supporting local businesses, and
16 generating significant economic activity throughout the state; and
17 WHEREAS, Film Louisiana actively promotes workforce development, fosters local
18 talent, and strengthens collaboration between industry professionals and state leaders; and
19 WHEREAS, Film Louisiana Day at the state capitol provides an important
20 opportunity for members of the film industry to engage with legislators, highlight their
