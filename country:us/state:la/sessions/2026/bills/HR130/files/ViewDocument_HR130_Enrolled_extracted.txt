ENROLLED
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 130
BY REPRESENTATIVE CHENEVERT
A RESOLUTION
To commend Film Louisiana for its significant contributions to the economic development
and cultural vitality of Louisiana and to designate Wednesday, April 15, 2026, as
Film Louisiana Day at the state capitol.
WHEREAS, Film Louisiana is a statewide trade association representing the motion
picture, television, and digital media production industry; and
WHEREAS, Louisiana has earned national and international recognition as a premier
destination for film and television production and is commonly known as "Hollywood
South" due to its diverse locations, skilled workforce, and well-developed production
infrastructure; and
WHEREAS, Film Louisiana plays a vital role in advocating for policies that support
and sustain the continued growth of Louisiana's film industry and helps to attract major
productions and long-term investment to the state; and
WHEREAS, the film and television industry provides substantial economic benefits
to Louisiana by creating thousands of jobs for residents, supporting local businesses, and
generating significant economic activity throughout the state; and
WHEREAS, Film Louisiana actively promotes workforce development, fosters local
talent, and strengthens collaboration between industry professionals and state leaders; and
WHEREAS, Film Louisiana Day at the state capitol provides an important
opportunity for members of the film industry to engage with legislators, highlight their
contributions, and emphasize the importance of maintaining a strong and competitive film
sector in Louisiana.

HR NO. 130 ENROLLED
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Film Louisiana for its contributions to the
