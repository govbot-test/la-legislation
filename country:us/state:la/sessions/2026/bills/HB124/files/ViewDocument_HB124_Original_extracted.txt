HB 124 Original 2026 Regular Session Dickerson
Abstract: Provides relative to the membership of the Domestic Violence Prevention
Commission.
Present law provides for the membership of the Domestic Violence Prevention Commission.
Proposed law generally retains present law and amends the membership of the commission
as follows:
(1) Permits either a current or former district court judge with experience in criminal law
matters, selected by the president of the La. District Court Judges Assoc., to serve
as a member.
(2) Permits either a current or former district court judge with experience in family law
matters, selected by the president of the La. District Court Judges Assoc., to serve
as a member.
(3) Removes from membership the representative of the Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco,
Firearms and Explosives.
(Amends R.S. 46:2146(A)(intro. para.) and (1)(intro. para.), (h), (i), (p), and (q); Repeals
R.S. 46:2146(A)(1)(r))
