DOMESTIC ABUSE: Provides relative to the membership of the Domestic Violence
Prevention Commission
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 46:2146(A)(introductory paragraph) and (1)(introductory
3 paragraph), (h), and (i) and to repeal R.S. 46:2146(A)(1)(p), relative to the Domestic
4 Violence Prevention Commission; to provide for the membership of the commission;
5 to remove a member from the commission; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 46:2146(A)(introductory paragraph) and (1)(introductory paragraph),
8 (h), and (i) are hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
9 §2146. Composition of the commission
10 A. The commission shall be composed of twenty nineteen members as
11 follows:
12 (1) Eighteen Seventeen of the commission members shall be the following:
13 * * *
14 (h) One current or former district court judge with experience in criminal law
15 matters, selected by the president of the Louisiana District Court Judges Association.
16 (i) One current or former district court judge with experience in family law
17 matters, selected by the president of the Louisiana District Court Judges Association.
18 * * *
19 Section 2. R.S. 46:2146(A)(1)(p) is hereby repealed in its entirety.

HB 124 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Dickerson
Abstract: Provides relative to the membership of the Domestic Violence Prevention
Commission.
Present law provides for the membership of the Domestic Violence Prevention Commission.
Proposed law generally retains present law and amends the membership of the commission
as follows:
(1) Permits either a current or former district court judge with experience in criminal law
matters, selected by the president of the La. District Court Judges Assoc., to serve
as a member.
(2) Permits either a current or former district court judge with experience in family law
matters, selected by the president of the La. District Court Judges Assoc., to serve
as a member.
(3) Removes from membership the representative of the Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco,
Firearms and Explosives.
(Amends R.S. 46:2146(A)(intro. para.) and (1)(intro. para.), (h), and (i); Repeals R.S.
46:2146(A)(1)(p))
