Section 1:
2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
BY SENATOR PRESSLY AND REPRESENTATIVE CHASSION
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 17:3384, relative to postsecondary education institutions; to require a letter-
3 based grading scale for medical doctorate degrees; to provide for an effective date;
4 and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 17:3384 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
7 §3384. Medical school grading scale
8 A. Beginning August 1, 2027, all public postsecondary education
9 institutions offering medical doctorate degrees shall adopt policies ensuring that
10 all curriculum coursework offered for a medical degree or certificate assesses
11 a student's performance on the assigned coursework using a tiered grading
12 system of High-Pass (HP), Pass (P), Low-Pass(LP), or Fail (F), or another tiered
13 grading system with at least four such designations.
14 B. If any provision of this Section or the application thereof is held
15 invalid as to any person or circumstance, such invalidity shall not affect other
16 provisions or applications of this Section which can be given effect without the
17 invalid provisions or applications.
18 Section 2. This Act shall be known as the "Medical School Letter Grade Act".

SB NO. 234 ENROLLED
