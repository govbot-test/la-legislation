SENATE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
by Senator Pressly
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, delete lines 8 through 14, and insert the following:
3 "A. Beginning August 1, 2027, all public postsecondary education
4 institutions offering medical doctorate degrees shall adopt policies ensuring that
5 all curriculum coursework offered for a medical degree or certificate assesses
6 a student's performance on the assigned coursework using a tiered grading
7 system of High-Pass (HP), Pass (P), Low-Pass or Fail (F), or another tiered
8 grading system with at least four such designations."
