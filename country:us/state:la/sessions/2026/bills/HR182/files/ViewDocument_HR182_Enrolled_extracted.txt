ENROLLED
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 182
BY REPRESENTATIVE MELERINE
A RESOLUTION
Louisiana.
WHEREAS, Louisiana State University at Shreveport (LSUS) is an integral part of
the Louisiana State University System, providing high-quality, accessible, and affordable
education to students across northwest Louisiana and beyond; and
WHEREAS, LSUS continues to be one of the fastest-growing universities in
Louisiana, growing one hundred thirty-three percent over the past ten years, placing it in the
top one percent of colleges and universities in the United States for growth in the past
decade; the university has achieved record enrollment five semesters in a row; and
WHEREAS, LSUS crossed the ten thousand student threshold in the Spring of 2024
for the first time in the university's history and surpassed the record in the Fall of 2025 with
enrollment reaching the eleven thousand student mark; and
WHEREAS, LSUS continues to grow in every enrollment category, including
undergraduate, graduate, face-to-face, online, and dual-enrollment; and
WHEREAS, LSUS continues to develop new programs that are meeting student
interests as well as current workforce needs in the community and throughout Louisiana; and
WHEREAS, LSUS graduates more students annually than every other Louisiana
university except for the main campus of LSU, and the university leads the state in the
number of graduate degrees awarded each year; and
WHEREAS, LSUS holds the distinction of being the third-fastest growing regional
public institution in the nation from 2010 to 2023; and

WHEREAS, Louisiana State University at Shreveport is most deserving of
recognition for its outstanding achievements as a higher education institution.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Louisiana State University at Shreveport on
its contributions to the state of Louisiana and does hereby extend sincerest wishes that the
university and its students continue to prosper in all of their endeavors.
