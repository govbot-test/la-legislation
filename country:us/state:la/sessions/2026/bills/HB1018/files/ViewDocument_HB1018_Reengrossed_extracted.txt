HB 1018 Reengrossed 2026 Regular Session Jackson
Abstract: Prohibits the issuance of certain Class B and C permits for the sale of high or low
alcoholic beverages within the boundaries of District 2 of the La. House of
Representatives from Aug. 1, 2026, through Dec. 31, 2027.
Present law provides the issuance and regulation of various high and low alcoholic beverage
permits.
Beginning on Aug. 1, 2026, and ending on Dec. 31, 2027, proposed law prohibits the
issuance of new permits for the sale of high or low alcoholic beverages within the
boundaries of District 2 of the La. House of Representatives.
Proposed law provides that the limitation on the issuance of permits applies only to Class
B or Class C permits for licensed premises that contain less than 5,000 square feet of public
habitable floor area.
Proposed law provides that the limitation on the issuance of permits does not apply to
licensed premises located in the Shreveport Downtown Development District, licensed
premises located in Bossier City, or to a licensed premises purchased by the owner of
another licensed premise if the purchased premises is not in violation of federal, state, or
local laws at the time of the acquisition.
Proposed law provides that valid existing permits may continue and be renewed if the permit
holder remains in compliance with all other provisions of law and local ordinances.
