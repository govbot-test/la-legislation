HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 44
BOYER, BRYANT, CARLSON, CHASSION, DEVILLIER, HEBERT, CHANCE
HENRY, AND JACOB LANDRY AND SENATORS ABRAHAM, ALLAIN,
BOUDREAUX, CLOUD, HENSGENS, KLEINPETER, LAMBERT, MIGUEZ,
MILLER, PRICE, REESE, AND STINE
COMMENDATIONS: Commends Emile Myers on the success of his annual philanthropic
endeavor, "Emile's Hot Cocoa Fundraiser"
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 To commend Emile Myers on the success of his annual philanthropic endeavor, "Emile's
3 Hot Cocoa Fundraiser".
4 WHEREAS, Lafayette resident Emile Myers launched a small neighborhood hot
5 cocoa stand in 2019 at the age of eight, and the stand has evolved into a major community
6 fundraising tradition that is embraced by families across Acadiana; and
7 WHEREAS, over the past seven years, "Emile's Hot Cocoa Fundraiser" has grown
8 into a beloved annual holiday event; the stand has raised over one hundred thousand dollars
9 to benefit numerous local nonprofit organizations, including St. Joseph Diner, Boys & Girls
10 Club of Acadiana, Catholic Charities of Acadiana, American Cancer Society, Junior League
11 of Lafayette, and others; and
12 WHEREAS, Emile's kindhearted nature and leadership ability have inspired
13 widespread community participation and provided meaningful support to vulnerable
14 families, youth programs, cancer patients, and vital social service organizations; and
15 WHEREAS, Emile plans to pass his fundraiser on to the next generation of young
16 philanthropists, and he is committed to serving as a mentor to help the event continue to
17 grow, inspire service, and strengthen the spirit of generosity within the community for years
18 to come; and
Page 1 of 2
HLS 26RS-1501 ORIGINAL
HCR NO. 44
1 WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize Emile Myers for his remarkable
2 compassion, leadership, and commitment to improving the lives of others and to honor him
3 for demonstrating the profound impact that the state's youth can have when they choose to
4 lead with their hearts.
5 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of Louisiana does hereby
6 commend Emile Myers on the success of his annual philanthropic endeavor, "Emile's Hot
7 Cocoa Fundraiser" and does hereby extend enduring appreciation for his lasting community
8 impact and continued legacy of youth-led service.
