HB 185 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Melerine
Abstract: For the purposes of workers' compensation, expands the definition of an
independent contractor by providing additional individuals who shall also be
considered an independent contractor.
Present law defines an individual who is considered an independent contractor for the
purposes of workers' compensation.
Proposed law expands the definition of independent contractor to also include employees of
independent contractors or other independent contractors contracting with an independent
contractor.
(Amends R.S. 23:1021(intro. para.) and (7))
