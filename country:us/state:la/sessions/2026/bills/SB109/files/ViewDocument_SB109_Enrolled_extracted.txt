Section 1:
2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
BY SENATOR JENKINS
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 40:1133.3(B)(1)(a), (b), and (d), relative to the Louisiana
3 Emergency Medical Services Commission; to provide for membership; to provide
4 for qualifications; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 40:1133.3(B)(1)(a), (b), and (d) are hereby amended and reenacted
7 to read as follows:
8 §1133.3. Louisiana Emergency Medical Services Commission; creation;
9 membership; qualifications; terms; vacancies; meetings; officers;
10 compensation; domicile
11 * * *
12 B.(1) The commission shall be composed of twelve voting members
13 appointed by the governor as follows:
14 (a) Two emergency medical services administrators, including one
15 representing the private sector and one representing the public sector, nominated by
16 the Louisiana Ambulance Alliance.
17 (b) Two licensed paramedics and one licensed emergency medical technician
18 who are approved as emergency medical services educators by the bureau. At least
19 one of the members as provided in this Subparagraph shall be appointed by the
20 Louisiana Fire Chiefs Association.
21 * * *
