HB 854 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Walters
Abstract: Creates the "Alzheimer's Awareness", "Lupus Awareness", "Krewe of Sobek",
"Mystic Krewe of Imani", "Krewe of Harambee", "Huntington High School",
"Haynesville Jr./Sr. High School", "Lincoln Preparatory School", and "Green Oaks
High School" special prestige license plates and provides for the creation, issuance,
design, implementation, fees, distributions, and promulgation of rules applicable to
these license plates.
Present law (R.S. 47:463.73) requires the Dept. of Public Safety and Corrections (DPS&C),
office of motor vehicles (OMV), to establish special prestige license plates for the La.
parochial, public, and private high schools, provided there are a minimum of 1,000
applicants for the plates.
Proposed law adds Huntington High School, Haynesville Jr./Sr. High School, Lincoln
Preparatory School, and Green Oaks Performing Arts Academy to the list of high schools
for the establishment of special prestige license plates. Further directs the DPS&C, OMV,
to create these special prestige license plates when the applicable statutory provisions are
met and the department's electronic vehicle and registration system is updated to
accommodate the creation of the new plates.
Proposed law extends existing authority for the secretary to promulgate and adopt rules and
regulations as are necessary to implement the provisions of proposed law.

DIGEST
The digest printed below was prepared by House Legislative Services. It constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument. The keyword, one-liner, abstract, and digest do not constitute
part of the law or proof or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
HB 854 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Walters
Abstract: Creates the "Alzheimer's Awareness", "Lupus Awareness", "Krewe of Sobek",
"Mystic Krewe of Imani", "Krewe of Harambee", "Huntington High School",
"Haynesville Jr./Sr. High School", "Lincoln Preparatory School", and "Green Oaks
High School" special prestige license plates and provides for the creation, issuance,
design, implementation, fees, distributions, and promulgation of rules applicable to
these license plates.
Present law (R.S. 47:463.73) requires the Dept. of Public Safety and Corrections (DPS&C),
office of motor vehicles (OMV), to establish special prestige license plates for the La.
parochial, public, and private high schools, provided there are a minimum of 1,000
applicants for the plates.
Proposed law adds Huntington High School, Haynesville Jr./Sr. High School, Lincoln
Preparatory School, and Green Oaks Performing Arts Academy to the list of high schools
for the establishment of special prestige license plates. Further directs the DPS&C, OMV,
to create these special prestige license plates when the applicable statutory provisions are
met and the department's electronic vehicle and registration system is updated to
accommodate the creation of the new plates.
Proposed law extends existing authority for the secretary to promulgate and adopt rules and
regulations as are necessary to implement the provisions of proposed law.

Summary of Amendments Adopted by House
The Committee Amendments Proposed by House Committee on Transportation,
Highways and Public Works to the original bill:
1. Make technical changes.
2. Delete the requirement that the Department of Public Safety and Corrections
retain one dollar from each annual fee to offset administrative costs from the
Alzheimer's Awareness and Lupus Awareness special prestige license plates.

Fleming Lupus Foundation as a charitable donation.

of special prestige license plates for Louisiana parochial, public, and private high
schools.

of motor vehicles to create the Huntington High School, Haynesville Jr./Sr. High
School, Lincoln Preparatory School, Green Oaks Performing Arts Academy,
Alzheimer's Awareness, Lupus Awareness, Krewe of Sobek, Mystic Krewe of
Imani, and the Krewe of Harambee special prestige license plates when the
applicable statutory provisions are met and the department's electronic vehicle
and registration system is updated to accommodate the creation of the new
plates.
