11 §463.243. Special prestige license plate; Alzheimer's Awareness
12 A. The secretary of the Department of Public Safety and Corrections shall
13 establish a special prestige motor vehicle license plate to be known as the
14 "Alzheimer's Awareness" plate, provided that there is a minimum of one thousand
15 applicants for such plate. The plate shall be restricted to use on passenger cars,
16 pickup trucks, recreational vehicles, motorcycles, and vans.
17 B. The secretary shall work in conjunction with the Alzheimer's Association
18 Louisiana Chapter to select the color and design of the plate, provided it is in
19 compliance with R.S. 47:463(A)(3). The design shall include "Alzheimer's
20 Awareness" and the official purple Alzheimer's Awareness logo.
21 C. The special prestige license plate shall be issued, upon application to any
22 citizen of Louisiana in the same manner as any other motor vehicle license plate.
23 D. The department shall collect an annual royalty fee of twenty-five dollars
24 that shall be disbursed in accordance with Subsection E of this Section. This fee
25 shall be in addition to the standard motor vehicle license tax imposed by Article VII,
26 Section 5 of the Constitution of Louisiana and a handling fee of three dollars and
27 fifty cents for each plate to be retained by the department to offset a portion of
28 administrative costs.

HB 854 Original 2026 Regular Session Walters
Abstract: Creates the "Alzheimer's Awareness", "Lupus Awareness", "Krewe of Sobek",
"Mystic Krewe of Imani", "Krewe of Harambee", "Huntington High School", and
"Green Oaks High School" special prestige license plates and provides for the
creation, issuance, design, implementation, fees, distributions, and promulgation of
rules applicable to these license plates.
Present law (R.S. 47:463.73) requires the Dept. of Public Safety and Corrections (DPS&C),
office of motor vehicles (OMV), to establish special prestige license plates for the La.
parochial, public, and private high schools, provided there are a minimum of 1,000
applicants for the plates.
Proposed law adds Huntington High School and Green Oaks Performing Arts Academy to
the list of high schools for the establishment of special prestige license plates.

Proposed law extends existing authority for the secretary to promulgate and adopt rules and
regulations as are necessary to implement the provisions of proposed law.
Proposed law (R.S. 47:463.243) requires that the DPS&C, OMV, issue a special prestige

recreational vehicles, motorcycles, and vans.

Chapter to select the color and design of the plate. Requires the design include the words
"Alzheimer's Awareness" and the official Alzheimer's symbol.
Proposed law specifies that the plate will be issued, upon application, to any citizen of the
state in the same manner as any other motor vehicle license plate.
Proposed law requires that the dept. collect an annual fee of $25 in addition to the standard

administrative costs. Specifies that the remainder of the fee will be forwarded to the
Alzheimer's Assoc. La. Chapter as a charitable donation to the association by the applicant.

necessary to implement the provisions of proposed law.
