HLS 26RS-4536 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 209
BY REPRESENTATIVE EDMONSTON
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate April as Deaf History Month in Louisiana.
3 WHEREAS, Deaf History Month is celebrated annually in April to commemorate
4 key milestones in Deaf history, including the founding of the first permanent school for the
5 Deaf in the United States and the recognition of Deaf culture and language; and
6 WHEREAS, Deaf individuals have made significant contributions to the cultural,
7 educational, economic, and civic life of our state and nation; and
8 WHEREAS, American Sign Language is a fully developed and linguistically rich
9 language that serves as a primary means of communication for many Deaf and hard-of-
10 hearing individuals; and
11 WHEREAS, access to language, education, and communication is essential for the
12 full inclusion and success of Deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals; and
13 WHEREAS, schools, organizations, and communities across the state work to ensure
14 that Deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals have equitable opportunities to thrive
15 academically, socially, and professionally; and
16 WHEREAS, it is important to recognize and celebrate Deaf culture, promote
17 awareness of the experiences of Deaf individuals, and continue efforts to remove barriers
18 to accessibility and inclusion.

HLS 26RS-4536 ORIGINAL
HR NO. 209
1 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
2 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby designate April as Deaf History Month in Louisiana
3 and does hereby encourage all citizens to recognize the achievements of the Deaf community
4 and to support efforts that promote accessibility, inclusion, and equal opportunity for all.
