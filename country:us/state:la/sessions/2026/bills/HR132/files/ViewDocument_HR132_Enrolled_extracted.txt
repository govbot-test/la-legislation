ENROLLED
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 132
BY REPRESENTATIVES ILLG AND STAGNI
A RESOLUTION
Louisiana High School Athletic Association 2026 Division I Select state
championship.
WHEREAS, the John Curtis Christian School Patriots basketball team captured the
2026 Division I Select state title by defeating district-rival Edna Karr High School, 68-40,
in the championship match held in Lake Charles, Louisiana; the victory gave the boys
basketball program its second state championship; and
WHEREAS, the Patriots were the top-ranked team throughout the season and
finished with an outstanding record of twenty-four wins and only two losses; and
WHEREAS, the Patriots claimed the District 10-5A title after going undefeated in
seven district games; and
WHEREAS, the Patriots were led by Head Coach Biko Paris, and Bryan Hammond,
Malik Rhinehart, and Jonathan English served as assistant coaches; and
WHEREAS, the members of the John Curtis Patriots basketball team are Autrail
Manning, Marlin Johnson, Zaveon Sheperd, Jewellz Tapp, Midnight Martin, Cashmir
Connor, Hayden Joesph, Joshua Willis, Tory Walker, Chance Foster, Jonnie Walker, Scott
Alexander, Kyle Jones, Jarvis Stevenson, Terrance Jones, and Ethan Banks; Mikala
Manganello and Johnes English served as the team managers; and
WHEREAS, the John Curtis Christian School boys basketball team is most deserving
of the highest recognition for their outstanding achievements this season.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend the John Curtis Christian School boys
basketball team on winning the Louisiana High School Athletic Association 2026 Division
I Select state championship and does hereby encourage the members of the team to continue
their excellent work and always strive for greatness in competition.
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
