HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 132
BY REPRESENTATIVES ILLG AND STAGNI
on winning the Louisiana High School Athletic Association 2026 Division I Select
state championship
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend the John Curtis Christian School boys basketball team on winning the
3 Louisiana High School Athletic Association 2026 Division I Select state
4 championship.
5 WHEREAS, the John Curtis Christian School Patriots basketball team captured the
6 2026 Division I Select state title by defeating district-rival Edna Karr High School, 68-40,
7 in the championship match held in Lake Charles, Louisiana; the victory gave the boys
8 basketball program its second state championship; and
9 WHEREAS, the Patriots were the top-ranked team throughout the season and
10 finished with an outstanding record of twenty-four wins and only two losses; and
11 WHEREAS, the Patriots claimed the District 10-5A title after going undefeated in
12 seven district games; and
13 WHEREAS, the Patriots were led by Head Coach Biko Paris, and Bryan Hammond,
14 Malik Rhinehart, and Jonathan English served as assistant coaches; and
15 WHEREAS, the members of the John Curtis Patriots basketball team are Autrail
16 Manning, Marlin Johnson, Zaveon Sheperd, Jewellz Tapp, Midnight Martin, Cashmir
17 Connor, Hayden Joesph, Joshua Willis, Tory Walker, Chance Foster, Jonnie Walker, Scott
18 Alexander, Kyle Jones, Jarvis Stevenson, Terrance Jones, and Ethan Banks; Mikala
19 Manganello and Johnes English served as the team managers; and
Page 1 of 2
HLS 26RS-3529 ORIGINAL
HR NO. 132
1 WHEREAS, the John Curtis Christian School boys basketball team is most deserving
2 of the highest recognition for their outstanding achievements this season.
3 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
4 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend the John Curtis Christian School boys
5 basketball team on winning the Louisiana High School Athletic Association 2026 Division
6 I Select state championship and does hereby encourage the members of the team to continue
7 their excellent work and always strive for greatness in competition.
