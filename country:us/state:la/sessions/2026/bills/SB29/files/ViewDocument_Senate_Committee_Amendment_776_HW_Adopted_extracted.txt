2026 Regular Session
No. 29 by Senator McMath
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, line 2, after "R.S. 13:5713(C)" insert "and to enact R.S. 13:5713(L)"
3 AMENDMENT NO. 2
4 On page 1, line 6, after "reenacted" insert "and R.S. 13:5713(L) is hereby enacted"
5 AMENDMENT NO. 3
6 On page 1, delete lines 10 and 11, and insert the following:
7 "physician an autopsy in all cases of infants under the age of one year who die unexpectedly
8 without explanation."
9 AMENDMENT NO. 4
10 On page 2, after line 19, insert the following:
11 "(6) The inclusion of an immunization record in any report shall not imply cause
12 of death."
13 AMENDMENT NO. 5
14 On page 2, after line 20, insert the following:
15 "L.(1) The death investigation report for any child under the age of fifteen who
16 dies unexpectedly without explanation shall include documentation of any
17 immunizations administered to the child within ninety days before the child's death,
18 as available through the state immunization registry.
19 (2) If the coroner finds that the cause of death was Sudden Infant Death
20 Syndrome, Sudden Unexpected Infant Death, Sudden Arrhythmic Death Syndrome,
21 or Sudden Death in the Young, he shall report the case to the Centers for Disease
22 Control and Prevention and the National Institutes of Health's Sudden Unexpected
23 Infant Death and Sudden Death in the Young Case Registry.
24 (3) The inclusion of an immunization record in any report shall not imply cause
25 of death."
