HB 152 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Mack
Abstract: Adds certain substances to Schedule I of the Uniform Controlled Dangerous
Substances Law.
Present law provides for the Uniform Controlled Dangerous Substances Law (R.S. 40:961
et seq.). Provides for definitions, composition schedules, prohibited acts, and related
requirements and regulations.
Proposed law retains present law.
Present law (R.S. 40:964) names certain opiates under Schedule I of the Uniform Controlled
Dangerous Substances Law.
Proposed law retains present law and adds new entries for opiates to Schedule I of the
Uniform Controlled Dangerous Substances Law.
(Adds R.S. 40:964(A)(108)-(120))
