1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend and recognize Film Louisiana for its contributions to economic development
3 and cultural enrichment of the state of Louisiana and to designate April 15, 2026, as
4 Film Louisiana Day at the Louisiana State Capitol.
5 WHEREAS, Film Louisiana is a statewide trade association representing the motion
6 picture and television production industry; and
7 WHEREAS, Louisiana has established itself as a national leader in film and
8 television production, earning widespread recognition as "Hollywood South" due to its
9 unique locations, experienced workforce, and strong production infrastructure; and
10 WHEREAS, Film Louisiana plays a critical role in advocating for policies that foster
11 growth and sustainability within the state's film industry, ensuring that Louisiana remains
12 competitive in attracting domestic and international productions; and
13 WHEREAS, the film and television industry generates substantial economic benefits
14 for Louisiana by creating thousands of jobs, supporting small businesses, and contributing
15 millions of dollars in economic activity annually; and
16 WHEREAS, Film Louisiana's contributions to the economic development and
17 cultural enrichment of the state of Louisiana are invaluable; and
18 WHEREAS, Film Louisiana works diligently to promote workforce development,
