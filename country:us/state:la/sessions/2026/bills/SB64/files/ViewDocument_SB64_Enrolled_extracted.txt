SENATE BILL NO. 64
BY SENATOR HODGES
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 17:1948(C)(5)(b) and to enact R.S. 17:1948(C)(5)(c), relative
3 to cameras in special education classrooms; to provide relative to circumstances
4 where recordings may be disclosed; to require presence of the superintendent or his
5 designee and parent or legal guardian during viewing of recordings; and to provide
6 for related matters.
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
8 Section 1. R.S. 17:1948(C)(5)(b) is hereby amended and reenacted and R.S.
9 17:1948(C)(5)(c) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
10 §1948. Cameras in certain classrooms; definitions; required policies; confidentiality;
11 authorization of funding
12 * * *
13 C. The policies shall include provisions for the following:
14 * * *
15 (5) Protecting student privacy and for determining to whom and under what
16 circumstances the recordings may be disclosed including:
17 * * *
18 (b) Requiring both the superintendent and his designee or requiring two
19 designees of the superintendent to be present during the initial viewing of the
20 recordings after a request has been made.
