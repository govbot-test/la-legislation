HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 349
BY REPRESENTATIVE BRYANT
Family Reunion, being held July 17 through 19, 2026, and designates Saturday, July
18, 2026, as Esther and Edwards Family Day in St. Martinville
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend the Esther and Edwards families on the occasion of their Family Reunion,
3 being held July 17 through 19, 2026, and to designate Saturday, July 18, 2026, as
4 Esther and Edwards Family Day in St. Martinville.
5 WHEREAS, the Esther and Edwards families have been an integral part of the
6 St. Martinville, Louisiana, community since 1825 and have contributed significantly to the
7 development, spirit, heritage, and history of the city through their involvement in both the
8 private and public sectors; and
9 WHEREAS, the members of the Esther and Edwards Family Reunion Committee
10 have worked tirelessly to preserve and honor their rich history and ensure that the values and
11 traditions of their ancestors continue to thrive through present and future generations; and
12 WHEREAS, the Esther and Edwards Family Reunion brings together family
13 members from communities across Louisiana, including St. Martinville, Lafayette, and New
14 Iberia, and states across the country, including California, Pennsylvania, Rhode Island, and
15 Texas; the family is gathering in St. Martinville to celebrate their shared bond, love, and
16 unity; and
17 WHEREAS, the gathering serves to memorialize the family theme, "Legacy Lives
18 Here. Love Leads Us Forward", and to facilitate learning where the family's journey began
19 in America, honoring the elders and members that have served in the U.S. Armed Forces,
Page 1 of 2

1 inspiring the family's youth, celebrating the memory of those who have passed on, and
2 creating new and cherished memories.
3 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
4 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend the Esther and Edwards family on the
5 occasion of their Family Reunion, being held July 17 through 19, 2026; does hereby
6 recognize the love, legacy, and strength of the family; does hereby designate Saturday,
7 July 18, 2026, as Esther and Edwards Family Day in St. Martinville; and does hereby urge
8 all community members to celebrate the continuous contributions of the families to the
9 enhancement of the city and their unwavering commitment to fostering education and unity
10 within the family, the community, and beyond.
