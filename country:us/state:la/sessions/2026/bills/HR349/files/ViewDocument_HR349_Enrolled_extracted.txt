HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 349
BY REPRESENTATIVE BRYANT
A RESOLUTION
To commend the Esther and Edwards families on the occasion of their Family Reunion,
being held July 17 through 19, 2026, and to designate Saturday, July 18, 2026, as
Esther and Edwards Family Day in St. Martinville.
WHEREAS, the Esther and Edwards families have been an integral part of the
St. Martinville, Louisiana, community since 1825 and have contributed significantly to the
development, spirit, heritage, and history of the city through their involvement in both the
private and public sectors; and
WHEREAS, the members of the Esther and Edwards Family Reunion Committee
have worked tirelessly to preserve and honor their rich history and ensure that the values and
traditions of their ancestors continue to thrive through present and future generations; and
WHEREAS, the Esther and Edwards Family Reunion brings together family
members from communities across Louisiana, including St. Martinville, Lafayette, and New
Iberia, and states across the country, including California, Pennsylvania, Rhode Island, and
Texas; the family is gathering in St. Martinville to celebrate their shared bond, love, and
unity; and
WHEREAS, the gathering serves to memorialize the family theme, "Legacy Lives
Here. Love Leads Us Forward", and to facilitate learning where the family's journey began
in America, honoring the elders and members that have served in the U.S. Armed Forces,
inspiring the family's youth, celebrating the memory of those who have passed on, and
creating new and cherished memories.

HR NO. 349 ENROLLED
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend the Esther and Edwards family on the
occasion of their Family Reunion, being held July 17 through 19, 2026; does hereby
recognize the love, legacy, and strength of the family; does hereby designate Saturday,
July 18, 2026, as Esther and Edwards Family Day in St. Martinville; and does hereby urge
all community members to celebrate the continuous contributions of the families to the
enhancement of the city and their unwavering commitment to fostering education and unity
within the family, the community, and beyond.
