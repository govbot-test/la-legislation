HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 152
BY REPRESENTATIVE LACOMBE
A RESOLUTION
Wilson.
WHEREAS, it is with immense sadness and profound regret that the House of
Representatives of the Legislature of Louisiana has learned of the passing, on April 3, 2026,
of Charles Wiley Wilson; and
WHEREAS, Charles Wiley Wilson was born on March 13, 1941, to Ozeale "Mudge"
Hall and Charles Wesley "Paw-Paw Pete" Wilson; he was a native of Delhi, Louisiana, and
was raised in a hard-working farming family from whom he inherited a legacy of resilience
and a deep devotion to family; and
WHEREAS, after graduating from high school, Mr. Wilson attended Northeast
Louisiana State College in Monroe, Louisiana, where he earned a bachelor of science degree
in agriculture; he went on to complete a master of science degree in agriculture at Louisiana
State University in Baton Rouge; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Wilson worked as a county extension agent for more than thirty-
five years; he served in East Carroll, Texas, and provided more than twenty-five years of
service in West Feliciana Parish; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Wilson was a forestry expert, skilled woodworker, dedicated youth
baseball coach, and loyal LSU fan; he was a fifty-year Mason of Feliciana Lodge #31 and
an avid outdoorsman who cherished hunting, fishing, and gardening; he also loved to cook,
especially while sharing a beer with his friends at Possum Corner Hunting Club; and
WHEREAS, Charles Wiley Wilson is survived by his loving wife of sixty-four years,
Judith Caffery Wilson; children, Angela Wade (Ted), Charles Frank Wilson (Lynn), Susan
Causey, and Teresa Andrews (Charles); grandchildren, Lesleigh Silvey (Ryan), Lauren Pike
