HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 152
BY REPRESENTATIVE LACOMBE
CONDOLENCES: Expresses the condolences of the House of Representatives on the death
of Charles Wilson
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To express the condolences of the House of Representatives upon the death of Charles
3 Wilson.
4 WHEREAS, it is with immense sadness and profound regret that the House of
5 Representatives of the Legislature of Louisiana has learned of the passing, on April 3, 2026,
6 of Charles Wiley Wilson; and
7 WHEREAS, Charles Wiley Wilson was born on March 13, 1941, to Ozeale "Mudge"
8 Hall and Charles Wesley "Paw-Paw Pete" Wilson; he was a native of Delhi, Louisiana, and
9 was raised in a hard-working farming family from whom he inherited a legacy of resilience
10 and a deep devotion to family; and
11 WHEREAS, after graduating from high school, Mr. Wilson attended Northeast
12 Louisiana State College in Monroe, Louisiana, where he earned a bachelor of science degree
13 in agriculture; he went on to complete a master of science degree in agriculture at Louisiana
14 State University in Baton Rouge; and
15 WHEREAS, Mr. Wilson worked as a county extension agent for more than thirty-
16 five years; he served in East Carroll, Texas, and provided more than twenty-five years of
17 service in West Feliciana Parish; and
18 WHEREAS, Mr. Wilson was a forestry expert, skilled woodworker, dedicated youth
19 baseball coach, and loyal LSU fan; he was a fifty-year Mason of Feliciana Lodge #31 and
20 an avid outdoorsman who cherished hunting, fishing, and gardening; he also loved to cook,
21 especially while sharing a beer with his friends at Possum Corner Hunting Club; and
