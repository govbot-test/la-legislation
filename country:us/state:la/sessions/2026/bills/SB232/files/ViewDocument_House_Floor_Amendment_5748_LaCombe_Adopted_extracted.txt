1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, line 2, delete "R.S. 13:10.3(D)" and insert "R.S. 13:10.3(D) and 103"
3 AMENDMENT NO. 2
4 On page 1, line 6, after "reserves;" insert "to provide for the reimbursement of unvouchered
5 expenses; to provide for the adjustment of the amount payable from self-generated funds;"
6 AMENDMENT NO. 3
7 On page 1, line 8, delete "R.S. 13:10.3(D) is" and insert "R.S. 13:10.3(D) and 103 are"
8 AMENDMENT NO. 4
9 On page 2, between lines 13 and line 14, insert the following:
10 "§103. Expenses of chief justice and associate justices of the supreme court;
11 reimbursement
12 A. The chief justice and each associate justice of the supreme court shall be
13 reimbursed for reasonable housing and other otherwise unreimbursed
14 unvouchered expenses incurred while on official duty, payable out of the self-
15 generated funds provided for in R.S. 13:124 in amounts not to exceed the amounts
16 allocated under the authority of this Section as of May 1, 1989.
17 B. The allowance payable from self-generated funds as provided for in
18 this Section shall be adjusted for inflation on January first of each year by the
19 change in the consumer price index - U.S. city average for all urban consumers
20 (CPI-U) as prepared by the United States Department of Labor, Bureau of
21 Labor Statistics, applied annually, and shall reflect the change, if any, since the
22 amount was last modified, provided that any adjustment in a monthly amount
23 shall be made only in whole increments of one hundred dollars."
24 AMENDMENT NO. 5
25 On page 2, line 14, after "Section 2." delete "This" and insert "Section 1 of this"
26 AMENDMENT NO. 6
27 On page 2, after line 18, add the following:
28 "Section 3. Section 2 of this Act shall take effect and become operative if and when
29 the Act which originated as House Bill No. 398 of this 2026 Regular Session of the
30 Legislature is enacted and becomes effective."
