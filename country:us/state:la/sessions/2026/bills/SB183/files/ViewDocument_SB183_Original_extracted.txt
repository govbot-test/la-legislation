8 Section 1. Subpart FF of Part II-A of Chapter 1 of Subtitle I of Title 39 of the

10 as follows:
11 SUBPART FF. RESERVE FUND
12 §100.271. Reserve Fund
13 A. There is hereby established in the state treasury, as a special fund, the
14 Reserve Fund, hereinafter referred to as the "fund".
15 B. Any money transferred, donated, or appropriated to the fund by the
16 legislature shall be deposited into the fund.

DIGEST
SB 183 Original 2026 Regular Session Womack
Proposed law creates the Reserve Fund as a special fund in the state treasury.
Proposed law provides for the deposit of monies into the fund and the withdrawal of monies
out of the fund.
Effective upon signature of the governor or lapse of time for gubernatorial action.
(Adds R.S. 39:100.271)
