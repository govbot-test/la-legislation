2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 105
BY SENATOR JACKSON-ANDREWS
A RESOLUTION
To designate Wednesday, May 6, 2026, as Links Day at the Louisiana State Capitol and to
commend The Links, Incorporated, for their dedicated service and lasting
contributions to the state and its residents.
WHEREAS, established in 1946, The Links, Incorporated, is an international
not-for-profit corporation committed to enriching, sustaining, and ensuring the cultural and
economic survival of African Americans and persons of African ancestry; and
WHEREAS, the organization is one of the nation's oldest and largest volunteer
service organizations of women dedicated to improving the lives of others through a strong
focus on community service and civic engagement; and
WHEREAS, the mission of The Links, Incorporated, is to promote and engage in
educational, civic, and intercultural activities that enhance the lives of members and the
larger community through five program facets: Services to Youth, The Arts, National Trends
and Services, International Trends and Services, and Health and Human Services; and
WHEREAS, The Links, Incorporated, is comprised of more than seventeen thousand
professional women of African descent in two hundred ninety-nine chapters, located in
forty-one states, with seven chapters established in Louisiana, as well as the District of
Columbia; the Commonwealth of the Bahamas; and the United Kingdom; and
WHEREAS, the Louisiana chapters of Baton Rouge, Crescent City, La Capitale,
Monroe-Grambling, New Orleans, Pontchartrain, and Shreveport belong to the Southern
Area of The Links, Incorporated, and together these chapters represent decades of leadership
and service throughout the state; and
WHEREAS, each chapter has significantly contributed to the advancement of
education, health equity, cultural preservation, and community development in Louisiana
through volunteerism and impactful programming; and

WHEREAS, Links Day at the Louisiana State Capitol is an advocacy project of the
Southern Area of The Links, Incorporated, and serves as an important opportunity for
members to engage with lawmakers, highlight issues affecting communities across the state,
and reinforce the importance of civic involvement, leadership, and public service.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
does hereby designate Wednesday, May 6, 2026, as Links Day at the Louisiana State Capitol
and does hereby commend The Links, Incorporated, for their dedicated service and lasting
contributions to the state of Louisiana and its residents.
