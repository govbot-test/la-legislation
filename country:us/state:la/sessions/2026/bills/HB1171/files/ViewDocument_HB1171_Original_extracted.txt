Section 1:
HLS 26RS-1906 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE MACK
WILDLIFE/MGT AREAS: Authorizes the use of airboats in the Maurepas Swamp Wildlife
Management Area
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 56:109.5 and to repeal R.S. 56:109.4, relative to the use of airboats; to provide
3 for the use of airboats within the Maurepas Swamp Wildlife Management Area; and
4 to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 56:109.5 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
7 §109.5. Use of airboats on the Maurepas Swamp Wildlife Management Area
8 Use of airboats within the Maurepas Swamp Wildlife Management Area shall
9 be permitted.
10 Section 2. R.S. 56:109.4 is hereby repealed in its entirety.
DIGEST
The digest printed below was prepared by House Legislative Services. It constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument. The keyword, one-liner, abstract, and digest do not constitute
part of the law or proof or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
HB 1171 Original 2026 Regular Session Mack
Abstract: Provides for the use of airboats within the Maurepas Swamp Wildlife
Management Area.
Proposed law permits the use of airboats within the Maurepas Swamp Wildlife Management
Area.
Proposed law repeals the provisions in present law which terminated in 2019 permitting the
use of airboats in the Maurepas Swamp Wildlife Management Area on airboat trails and
providing for promulgation of rules and regulations and for violations.
(Adds R.S. 56:109.5; Repeals R.S. 56:109.4)
