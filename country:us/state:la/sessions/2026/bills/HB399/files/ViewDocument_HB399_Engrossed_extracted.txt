HLS 26RS-1215 ENGROSSED
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE ZERINGUE
CRIMINAL/PROCEDURE: Provides relative to temporary release of inmates for limited
purposes
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 15:833.2(A), relative to temporary release of an inmate for
3 limited purposes; to provide for the number of days in which death is expected for
4 purposes of authorizing temporary release; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 15:833.2(A) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
7 §833.2. Temporary release of inmate for limited purposes
8 A. Notwithstanding any other provisions of law to the contrary, the secretary
9 may authorize the temporary release of any inmate, except an inmate sentenced to
10 death, for palliative or medical care when an inmate is diagnosed with a terminal
11 illness, and death is expected within sixty one hundred twenty days or when an
12 inmate is to be confined to an acute care hospital or nursing home with a condition
13 that totally prevents mobility, including but not limited to prolonged coma or
14 mechanical ventilation.

DIGEST
The digest printed below was prepared by House Legislative Services. It constitutes no part

HB 399 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Zeringue
Abstract: Changes the number of days in which death is expected for purposes of
temporary release from 60 to 120.
Present law provides that the secretary of the Dept. of Public Safety and Corrections
(DPS&C) may authorize temporary release of an inmate when the inmate is diagnosed with
a terminal illness and death is expected within 60 days.
Proposed law changes the timefame for which death is expected from 60 days to 120 days.
Otherwise retains present law.
(Amends R.S. 15:833.2(A))
