6 §3453. Priority list of projects; public hearings; final program
7 Each quarter, the department shall prepare and shall furnish a prioritized list
8 of projects, based on the applications received by the department during that quarter,
9 to the joint committee. The joint committee shall receive the prioritized list of
10 projects from the department for each of the first three quarters of the year, and shall
11 call a public hearing within thirty days of receiving the list in order to receive public
12 testimony regarding any project on the list. At such hearing, the joint committee
13 shall vote to either accept, reject, or modify the list. Each quarter, the department
14 shall reprioritize the list of projects to reflect the cumulative list of projects
15 recommended by the department. After application recommendations for the last
16 quarter are made by the department, the department shall submit the final port
17 construction and development priority program for the ensuing fiscal year to the
18 joint committee for approval. Prior to the convening of the regular session of the
19 legislature, the joint committee shall hold a public hearing for the purpose of
20 reviewing the final program for the ensuing fiscal year. Prior to such hearing, the
21 department shall publish the appropriate official notice in the necessary journals
22 Official Journal of the State, as provided for in R.S. 43:81, beginning on July 1,
23 2027. The final program shall be based upon the anticipated revenues to be
24 appropriated by the legislature or other funding obligation authority and the projects
25 shall be listed in order of priority. When this final construction program is presented
26 to the legislature for funding or funding obligation authority for the ensuing fiscal
27 year, the legislature shall not add any projects to this final construction program.
28 Any project recommended by the department and approved by the joint committee
29 but for which funds are unavailable in the fiscal year for which it was approved shall
30 remain on the prioritized list of projects and shall be carried forward to the next
