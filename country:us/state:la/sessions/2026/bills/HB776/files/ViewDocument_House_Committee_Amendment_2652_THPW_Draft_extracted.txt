19 "§3451. Definitions
20 As used in this Chapter, unless the context clearly indicates otherwise, the
21 following definitions shall apply:
22 * * *
23 (6) "Benefit cost ratio" means the total benefits divided by the total cost of
24 a project with the total cost being equal to the total investment, both public and
25 private, required to implement the total project and derive the benefits and with total
26 benefits being equal to the revenues and expenses, number of jobs, payroll benefits,
27 spin-off benefits of payroll, shipping costs, or any other benefits that are
28 determinable.
29 (7) "Large project" means a project with a total project cost between forty
30 million dollars and eighty million dollars with a benefit cost ratio greater than or
31 equal to five, a return on the state's investment greater than or equal to three and one
32 half, and a private investment of between twenty-five and fifty percent of the total
33 project cost.
34 (8) "Standard project" means a project with a benefit cost ratio greater than
35 or equal to one and a return on the state's investment greater than or equal to two and
36 three hundred seventy-five thousandths."
