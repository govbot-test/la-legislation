19 "(4) Has aided and abetted a person who is not a licensed radiologic
20 technologist or otherwise authorized by this Chapter to perform the duties of a
21 license holder. Has knowingly trained, aided, abetted, or permitted a person who is
22 not licensed, permitted, or otherwise authorized, in accordance with this Chapter, to
23 perform duties requiring a license or permit in accordance with this Chapter.
