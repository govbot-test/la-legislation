SLS 26RS-373 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY SENATOR FOIL
TRANSPORTATION/DEV DEPT. Provides for the Department of Transportation and
Development to accept duties of the federal government. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 48:212(E), relative to the Department of Transportation and Development and
3 the federal government; to provide for giving authority to the department to take over
4 duties of the federal government; to provide for allowing limited lawsuits against the
5 department; to provide for the department to accept funds from any federal agency;
6 and to provide for related matters.
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
8 Section 1. R.S. 48:212(E) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
9 §212. Agreements with federal government
10 * * *
11 E. The department may enter into any agreements with the federal
12 government or with any federal agency, including memoranda of
13 understanding, to assume responsibility of the United States Department of
14 Transportation, pursuant to 23 USC 326 and 327 as authorized under the
15 federal Moving Ahead for Progress in the 21st Century Act, the federal Safe,
16 Accountable, Flexible, Efficient Transportation Equity Act: A Legacy for Users,
17 and the federal National Environmental Policy Act of 1969.
Page 1 of 2
Coding: Words which are struck through are deletions from existing law;
words in boldface type and underscored are additions.
SLS 26RS-373 ORIGINAL
SB NO. 115
1 (1) State sovereign immunity under the Eleventh Amendment of the
2 United States Constitution is waived for lawsuits in federal court, but only as
3 required by 23 USC 326 and 327. This waiver is limited solely to the compliance,
4 discharge, or enforcement of responsibilities assumed by the department under
5 these agreements and does not create any liability beyond federal law
6 requirements.
7 (2) The department may adopt and promulgate any rules and regulations
8 necessary to implement an agreement pursuant to this Subsection and to carry
9 out any duties imposed by the agreement.
10 (3) The department may accept and make expenditures of money
11 received from the federal government or any agency of the federal government
12 for any transportation purpose.
The original instrument and the following digest, which constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument, were prepared by Senate Legislative Services.
The keyword, summary, and digest do not constitute part of the law or proof
or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
DIGEST
SB 115 Original 2026 Regular Session Foil
Present law provides for DOTD to work with the federal government on transportation
matters including highways, transit, toll roads, emergencies, and rail safety.
Proposed law retains present law and adds permission for DOTD to take over certain federal
transportation approval and environmental review duties. Proposed law further allows
DOTD to agree to be sued, promulgate administrative rules and accept federal money from
any agency of the federal government to carry the agreed upon federal duties.
Proposed law further provides DOTD may accept and make expenditures of money received
from any agency of the federal government for any transportation purpose.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Adds R.S. 48:212(E))
