HLS 26RS-586 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE CARRIER
DISTRICTS/ECONOMIC DEVEL: Creates the Allen Parish Economic Development
District and abolishes the Allen Parish Tourist Commission
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 33:2740.70.11 and to repeal R.S. 33:4574(B)(3), 4574.1.1(A)(3), and 4574.14,
3 relative to Allen Parish; to create the Allen Parish Economic Development District;
4 to provide relative to the boundaries, purpose, governance, and powers and duties of
5 the district; to provide for district funding; to provide relative to the Allen Parish
6 Tourist Commission; to provide relative to the governing board of the commission;
7 to provide relative to the compensation of board members; to provide relative to
8 taxes levied by the commission; to repeal provisions that provide for the creation of
9 the commission; to abolish the commission; to provide relative to the funds and
10 property of the commission; and to provide for related matters.
11 Notice of intention to introduce this Act has been published
12 as provided by Article III, Section 13 of the Constitution of
13 Louisiana.
14 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
15 Section 1. R.S. 33:2740.70.11 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
16 §2740.70.11. Allen Parish Economic Development District
17 A. There is hereby created within the parish of Allen, as more specifically
18 provided in Subsection B of this Section, a body politic and corporate which shall
19 be known as the Allen Parish Economic Development District, referred to in this

1 Section as the "district". The district shall be a political subdivision of the state as
2 defined in the Constitution of Louisiana.
3 B. The boundaries of the district shall encompass all of the territorial
4 jurisdiction of the parish of Allen.
5 C. The district is created to provide for cooperative economic and
6 community development among the district, the parish, the state, and the owners of
7 property in the district; to enhance the development of and improvement to the
8 property within the area of the district; to develop stable and more extensive
9 employment opportunities; to improve infrastructure; and to promote the overall
10 welfare of the citizens of the district.
11 D.(1) In order to provide for the orderly development of the district and
12 effect the purposes of the district, the district shall be administered and governed by
13 a board of commissioners, referred to in this Section as the "board", composed of
14 five members as follows:
15 (a) The president of the Allen Parish Industrial Development Board.
16 (b) The president of the Oakdale Chamber of Commerce.
17 (c) The president of the Kinder Chamber of Commerce.
18 (d) The president of the governing authority of the parish of Allen.
19 (e) The member of the Louisiana House of Representatives whose district
20 encompasses all or the greater portion of the area of the district, or his designee.
21 (2) The members of the board shall serve during their terms of office. Any
22 designee shall serve at the pleasure of the designating authority.
23 (3) The board shall elect from its members a president, a vice president, a
24 secretary, a treasurer, and such other officers as it may deem necessary. The duties
25 of the officers shall be fixed by the bylaws adopted by the board.
26 (4) The minute books and archives of the district shall be maintained by the
27 secretary of the board. The monies, funds, and accounts of the district shall be in the
28 official custody of the board.

Parish Tourist Commission.

district, the parish, the state, and the owners of property in the district; to enhance the

Present law creates the Allen Parish Tourist Commission as a political subdivision of the
state composed of all of the territory of Allen Parish. Provides that the commission is
