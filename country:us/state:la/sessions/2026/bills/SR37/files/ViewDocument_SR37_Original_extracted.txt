SLS 26RS-1094 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 37
BY SENATOR SELDERS
Louisiana State Capitol and commends Thrive Academy and the Thrive Foundation for their
commitment to expanding educational opportunity for students across the state of Louisiana.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To recognize March 23, 2026, as Thrive Day at the Louisiana State Capitol, and to
3 commend Thrive Academy and the Thrive Foundation for their commitment to
4 expanding educational opportunities for students across the state of Louisiana.
5 WHEREAS, Thrive Academy is a public boarding school located in Baton Rouge,
6 Louisiana, that serves students from communities across the state and provides an
7 educational environment designed to prepare young people for academic success, leadership,
8 and lifelong opportunities; and
9 WHEREAS, the students of Thrive Academy representnineteen parishes, twenty-two
10 Senate legislative districts and twenty-nine House legislative districts across Louisiana, and
11 embody the promise, talent, and potential of the state's young people, demonstrating the
12 importance of expanding access to high-quality educational opportunities; and
13 WHEREAS, the Thrive Foundation partners with Thrive Academy to help remove
14 barriers and expand opportunities for students through initiatives such asThrive Essentials,
15 Thrive Within, Thrive Together, Thrive Beyond, and Thrive Tomorrow, to provide resources
16 that support student well-being, family stability, experiential learning, workforce exposure,
17 and college and career readiness; and
18 WHEREAS, the collaborative efforts of Thrive Academy and the Thrive Foundation
Page 1 of 2

1 demonstrate a shared commitment to investing in Louisiana's students and strengthening the
2 future of this state through education, opportunity, and community support; and
3 WHEREAS, the Class of 2026, the largest graduating class in Thrive Academy's
4 history, is present at the Louisiana State Capitol in recognition of Thrive Day and serves as
5 a testament to the school's continued growth and impact on students across Louisiana.
6 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
7 does hereby recognize Monday, March 23, 2026, as Thrive Day at the Louisiana State
8 Capitol, and commend Thrive Academy and the Thrive Foundation for their commitment
9 to expanding educational opportunities for students across the state of Louisiana.
10 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana does
11 hereby commend the students of Thrive Academy, including the students of the class of
12 2026, and extends its best wishes for continued success in their academic pursuits and future
13 endeavors.
14 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to
15 Thrive Academy and the Thrive Foundation.
