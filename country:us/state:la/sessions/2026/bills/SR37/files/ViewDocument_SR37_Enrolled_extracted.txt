2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 37
BY SENATOR SELDERS
A RESOLUTION
To recognize March 23, 2026, as Thrive Day at the Louisiana State Capitol, and to
commend Thrive Academy and the Thrive Foundation for their commitment to
expanding educational opportunities for students across the state of Louisiana.
WHEREAS, Thrive Academy is a public boarding school located in Baton Rouge,
Louisiana, that serves students from communities across the state and provides an
educational environment designed to prepare young people for academic success, leadership,
and lifelong opportunities; and
WHEREAS, the students of Thrive Academy represent nineteen parishes, twenty-two
Senate legislative districts, and twenty-nine House legislative districts across Louisiana, and
embody the promise, talent, and potential of the state's young people, demonstrating the
importance of expanding access to high-quality educational opportunities; and
WHEREAS, the Thrive Foundation partners with Thrive Academy to help remove
barriers and expand opportunities for students through initiatives such as Thrive Essentials,
Thrive Within, Thrive Together, Thrive Beyond, and Thrive Tomorrow, to provide resources
that support student well-being, family stability, experiential learning, workforce exposure,
and college and career readiness; and
WHEREAS, the collaborative efforts of Thrive Academy and the Thrive Foundation
demonstrate a shared commitment to investing in Louisiana's students and strengthening the
future of this state through education, opportunity, and community support; and
WHEREAS, the Class of 2026, the largest graduating class in Thrive Academy's
history, is present at the Louisiana State Capitol in recognition of Thrive Day and serves as
a testament to the school's continued growth and impact on students across Louisiana.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
does hereby recognize Monday, March 23, 2026, as Thrive Day at the Louisiana State
Capitol, and commends Thrive Academy and the Thrive Foundation for their commitment
to expanding educational opportunities for students across the state of Louisiana.
