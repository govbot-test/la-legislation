HB 303 Original 2026 Regular Session Freiberg
Abstract: Excludes certain local revenues for the purposes of computing per pupil funding
amounts for certain charter schools.
Present law, relative to charter school funding, provides for the computation of the per pupil
amount for a Type 1, 2, 3, 3B, or 4 charter school to be equal to the per pupil amount
received by the school district in which the student resides from the following sources:
(1) The state-funded per pupil allocation.
(2) Local revenues received during the prior year by the school district from specified
sources, with certain revenues excluded.
Proposed law retains present law and additionally excludes any amount granted by the state
for the cost of providing transportation to nonpublic school students as provided in present
law (R.S. 17:158).
(Adds R.S. 17:3995(A)(1)(b)(v))
