WHEREAS, it is with immense sadness and profound regret that the House of
Representatives of the Legislature of Louisiana has learned of the tragic mass shooting in

WHEREAS, the state of Louisiana and Bossier Parish stand firmly against domestic
violence and offer prayers and support to the families of Mar'Kaydon Pugh, Kayla Pugh,
Layla Pugh, Jayla Elkins, Shayla Elkins, Sariahh Snow, Khedarrion Snow, and Braylon
Snow.

Legislature of Louisiana does hereby express heartfelt condolences to the families of the
children whose lives were taken in the mass shooting in Shreveport and does hereby affirm
its stance against domestic violence.

HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 203
BY REPRESENTATIVE BAMBURG
A RESOLUTION
To express the condolences of the House of Representatives to the families of the children
whose lives were taken in the mass shooting in Shreveport and to affirm its stance
against domestic violence.
WHEREAS, it is with immense sadness and profound regret that the House of
Representatives of the Legislature of Louisiana has learned of the tragic mass shooting in
Shreveport, Louisiana, that claimed the lives of eight children on April 19, 2026; and
WHEREAS, the state of Louisiana and Bossier Parish stand firmly against domestic
violence and offer prayers and support to the families of Mar'Kaydon Pugh, Kayla Pugh,
Layla Pugh, Jayla Elkins, Shayla Elkins, Sariahh Snow, Khedarrion Snow, and Braylon
Snow.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby express heartfelt condolences to the families of the
children whose lives were taken in the mass shooting in Shreveport and does hereby affirm
its stance against domestic violence.
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
