SLS 26RS-3532 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 134
BY SENATOR HENRY
COMMENDATIONS. Commends Country Day's Isaac Herzenberg on his fourth
championship at the 2A state track and field meet.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend and congratulate Isaac Herzenberg of Metairie Park Country Day School upon
3 winning his fourth-consecutive Class 2A state track and field championship in the
4 javelin and to recognize his outstanding athletic achievements and dedication to
5 excellence.
6 WHEREAS, more than four years ago, Country Day's middle school track and field
7 coach, Jason Abate, encouraged then-eighth-grade student, Isaac Herzenberg, to try the
8 javelin, a moment that sparked a remarkable athletic career; and
9 WHEREAS, as a young student, Isaac embraced the challenge of learning the javelin
10 later reflecting that, "as an eighth grader, throwing a spear—who doesn't want to do that?",
11 demonstrating both enthusiasm and natural curiosity for the sport; and
12 WHEREAS, through years of disciplined training, Isaac has become one of the most
13 accomplished high school javelin throwers in Louisiana, earning four consecutive Class 2A
14 state track and field championships in the event; and
15 WHEREAS, at the 2026 Class 2A state track and field championship meet, held at
16 LSU's Bernie Moore Track Stadium in Baton Rouge, Isaac recorded a throw of 186 feet on
17 his second attempt, securing yet another state title; and
18 WHEREAS, Isaac's competitive standard is exceptionally high, as demonstrated by

1 his lifetime best throw of 219 feet, 2 inches, achieved in the summer of 2025, and his
2 impressive 205-foot, 4-inch throw at the 2026 regional championship meet; and
3 WHEREAS, Isaac attributes his success to his dedication to hard work and consistent
4 training during the spring and summer, acknowledging that his performance improves when
5 he trains diligently and declines when he takes his focus away from the event; and
6 WHEREAS, despite balancing multiple sports—including a pause in training the
7 soccer season, Isaac returned to javelin training in February and continued to excel,
8 demonstrating resilience, focus, and athletic maturity; and
9 WHEREAS, Isaac has signed to compete in track and field for the University of
10 Nebraska, continuing his athletic and academic career at the collegiate level; and
11 WHEREAS, Isaac Herzenberg has brought great pride to his family, his school, his
12 coaches, and the state of Louisiana through his exceptional achievements, sportsmanship,
13 and dedication to excellence.
14 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
15 does hereby commend and congratulate Isaac Herzenberg upon winning his fourth
16 consecutive Class 2A state track and field championship in the javelin and does hereby
17 extend best wishes for continued success in his collegiate career and future endeavors.
18 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to Isaac
19 Herzenberg in recognition of his outstanding accomplishments.
