HCAHB549 2549 4264
HOUSE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
549 by Representative Berault
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 3, delete lines 3 through 17 in their entirety and insert the following:
3 "A. There is hereby established in the state treasury as a special fund the
4 Bayou Growth Opportunity Workforce Fund, hereafter referred to in this Section as
5 the "fund".
6 B. After allocation of money to the Bond Security and Redemption Fund as
7 provided in Article VII, Section 9(B) of the Constitution of Louisiana, the treasurer
8 shall deposit into the fund all donations, gifts, and grants received by the state for the
9 program as well as any other monies which may be provided for the program by law.
10 C. Monies in the fund shall be invested by the treasurer in the same manner
11 as monies in the state general fund and interest earned on investment of the monies
12 shall be credited to the fund. All unexpended and unencumbered monies in the fund
13 at the end of the fiscal year shall remain in the fund.
14 D. Monies in the fund shall be appropriated to the department and used
15 solely to fund the payment of awards to awardees and for the administration of the
16 program."
