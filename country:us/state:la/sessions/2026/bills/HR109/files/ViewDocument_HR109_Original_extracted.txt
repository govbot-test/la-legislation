HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 109
BY REPRESENTATIVE OWEN
on winning the Louisiana High School Athletic Association 2026 Division III Select
state championship
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend the Rosepine High School Lady Eagles basketball team on winning the
3 Louisiana High School Athletic Association 2026 Division III Select state
4 championship.
5 WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize the Rosepine High School Lady Eagles
6 for their impressive season and to commend the team on winning the 2026 Division III
7 Select state championship; and
8 WHEREAS, the Lady Eagles captured the 2026 Division III Select state title by
9 defeating Holy Savior Menard, 42-34, in the championship match at the University Center
10 in Hammond, Louisiana; and
11 WHEREAS, Rosepine's Case James led the team with fourteen points; Marli Jones
12 sealed the victory by scoring five points in overtime; and
13 WHEREAS, the Lady Eagles have three girls basketball state titles over the past four
14 seasons; and
15 WHEREAS, the Rosepine High School Lady Eagles coaches and players are most
16 deserving of the highest recognition for their outstanding achievements.
17 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
18 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend the Rosepine High School Lady Eagles
19 basketball team on winning the Louisiana High School Athletic Association 2026 Division
