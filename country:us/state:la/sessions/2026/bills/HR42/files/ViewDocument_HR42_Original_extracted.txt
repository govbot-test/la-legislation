HLS 26RS-1819 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 42
BY REPRESENTATIVE DICKERSON
COMMENDATIONS: Commends women for the critical role they play in the chemistry
industry and other industries in Louisiana
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend women for the critical role they play in the chemistry industry and other
3 industries in Louisiana.
4 WHEREAS, Louisiana's industrial sector drives the state's economic growth and job
5 creation, and women are leading the way; and
6 WHEREAS, women are shaping industry and building upon Louisiana's rich energy
7 history to pave the way for a cleaner, greener, and more diverse future; and
8 WHEREAS, women of every race, class, and ethnic background have made
9 significant contributions to the growth and strength of the state of Louisiana and continue
10 to play critical economic, cultural, and social roles by contributing to the labor force; and
11 WHEREAS, the Louisiana chemistry industry promotes the employment and
12 advancement of skilled women in the industry and benefits the state of Louisiana through
13 community development and educational programs; and
14 WHEREAS, the Louisiana chemistry industry provides thousands of high-paying
15 jobs to women, including governmental relations professionals, engineers, operators, process
16 safety and human resources professionals, contractors, construction workers, and many
17 more; and
18 WHEREAS, the women in Louisiana industry are most deserving of the highest
19 recognition for paving the way for future generations of women to succeed and advance the
20 reputation of the state's industrial sector.
