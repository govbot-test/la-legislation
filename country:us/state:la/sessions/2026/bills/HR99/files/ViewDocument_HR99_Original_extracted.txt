HLS 26RS-3021 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 99
BY REPRESENTATIVE MIKE JOHNSON
COMMENDATIONS: Commends Melissa Frost and David Atwood on the occasion of the
tenth anniversary of The Melissa & David Show on KRRV-FM in Alexandria
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Melissa Frost and David Atwood, hosts of The Melissa & David Show on
3 KRRV-FM in Alexandria, Louisiana, on the occasion of the tenth anniversary of
4 their program on April 1, 2026, and to recognize their outstanding contributions to
5 broadcasting and to the citizens of Central Louisiana.
6 WHEREAS, The Melissa & David Show, hosted by Melissa Frost and David
7 Atwood, has entertained and informed listeners across Central Louisiana each weekday
8 morning for the past decade and become a staple of daily life for area residents; and
9 WHEREAS, since its inception, the show has provided not only humor and
10 entertainment, but also meaningful engagement with the community through local news,
11 events, and outreach efforts that strengthen the bonds of the region; and
12 WHEREAS, Melissa Frost and David Atwood have demonstrated exceptional talent,
13 professionalism, and dedication to their craft and earned widespread recognition for their
14 work; and
15 WHEREAS, their program has been honored by the Louisiana Association of
16 Broadcasters as both the Best Morning Show in the State and Best Personality Radio Team,
17 which reflects their excellence and popularity among audiences and peers alike; and
18 WHEREAS, they have also received the distinguished Programmer of the Year
19 Award from CDX Traction in Nashville, Tennessee, which further demonstrates their impact
20 and leadership within the broadcasting industry; and
