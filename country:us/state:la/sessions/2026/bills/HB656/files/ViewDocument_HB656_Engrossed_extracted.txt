HLS 26RS-527 ENGROSSED
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE BRYANT
CORRECTIONS: Creates a pilot program that provides certain DPS&C programming and
services to inmates
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 15:830.4, relative to programing for inmates; to provide for the establishment
3 of a pilot program; to provide for programming topics; to provide for attendance and
4 accessibility; to provide for duties of the Department of Public Safety and
5 Corrections; to provide for rulemaking; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 15:830.4 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §830.4. Access to Programming Pilot Program; establishment
9 A. Subject to the availability of funds and appropriate resources, the
10 Department of Public Safety and Corrections is hereby authorized to establish and
11 administer the Access to Programming Pilot Program for the purpose of making
12 programming available to all of the following:
13 (1) Juvenile offenders.
14 (2) Inmates sentenced to the Department of Public Safety and Corrections
15 who are in the custody of the sheriff at a parish prison or jail.
16 B. The programming shall include all of the following topics:
17 (1) Rehabilitation services that encompass educational and vocational
18 opportunities.
19 (2) Mental health services.
20 (3) Suicide awareness and prevention.

6 D. The Department of Public Safety and Corrections shall adopt rules and
7 regulations in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act to implement the
8 provisions of this Section.

HB 656 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Bryant
Abstract: Creates a pilot program that provides certain Dept. of Public Safety and
Corrections (DPS&C) programming and services to inmates.
Proposed law authorizes the DPS&C, subject to the availability of funding and resources,
to establish and administer the Access to Programming Pilot Program for the purpose of
making programming available to certain offenders.
Proposed law provides for services to be included within the programming and options for
attendance.
Proposed law requires the DPS&C to adopt rules and regulations to implement the
provisions of proposed law.
(Adds R.S. 15:830.4)
