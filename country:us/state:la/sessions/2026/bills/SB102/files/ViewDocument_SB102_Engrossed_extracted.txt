DIGEST
SB 102 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Pressly
Proposed law allows a trade association that meets certain requirements to bring a civil
action or intervene in a civil action in its own name or on behalf of one or more of its
members for declaratory or injunctive relief.
Proposed law provides a prevailing trade association may be granted any of the following
remedies: declaratory relief, injunctive relief, and reasonable costs and attorney fees.
Proposed law provides a civil action pursuant to proposed law may be brought before the
commission or in any district court of competent jurisdiction.
(Adds R.S. 32:1253.1)
