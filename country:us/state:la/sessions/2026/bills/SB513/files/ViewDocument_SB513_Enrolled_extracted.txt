Section 1:
2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
BY SENATOR FESI
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 38:2225.2.6(A), relative to public works contracts; to provide for
3 design build contacts; and to provide for related matters.
4 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
5 Section 1. R.S. 38:2225.2.6(A) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
6 §2225.2.6. Design-build contracts; authorized use by airports; air traffic control
7 tower and hangar
8 A. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, a public
9 airport may use the design-build project delivery method to contract for construction
10 and design-build of an air traffic control tower or hangar development and
11 redevelopment project when deemed in the public interest, beneficial to the entity,
12 and in accordance with the procedures set forth by law. vertical facilities on airport
13 property, excluding passenger terminal facilities at commercial service airports
14 and equipment, when deemed in the public interest, beneficial to the entity, and
15 in accordance with the procedures set forth by law. These facilities could
16 include but are not limited to education or workforce development facilities,
17 airport air traffic control towers, parking garages, and aircraft hangars.
18 * * *
PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
GOVERNOR OF THE STATE OF LOUISIANA
