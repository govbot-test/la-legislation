1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, line 2, delete "enact R.S. 38:2211.3 and" and insert "amend and reenact R.S.
3 38:2225.2.6(A) and to enact" and after "provide for" insert "design build contacts;"
4 AMENDMENT NO. 2
5 On page 1, delete line 3 and at the beginning of line 4, delete "method;"
6 AMENDMENT NO. 3
7 On page 1, delete lines 7 through 17, and delete pages 2 and 3, and insert the following:
8 "Section 1. R.S. 38:2225.2.6(A) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
9 §2225.2.6. Design-build contracts; authorized use by airports; air traffic control
10 tower and hangar
11 A. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, a public airport may
12 use the design-build project delivery method to contract for construction and design-build
13 of an air traffic control tower or hangar development and redevelopment project when
14 deemed in the public interest, beneficial to the entity, and in accordance with the procedures
15 set forth by law. vertical facilities on airport property, excluding passenger terminal
16 facilities at commercial service airports and equipment, when deemed in the public
17 interest, beneficial to the entity, and in accordance with the procedures set forth by law.
18 These facilities could include, but are not limited to, education or workforce
19 development facilities, airport air traffic control towers, parking garages, and aircraft
20 hangars.
21 * * *
22 Section 2. R.S. 48:255.9 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
23 §255.9. Average bid method; authority
24 A. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, a pilot
25 program is hereby established to authorize the department and the office of
26 Louisiana highway construction to use the average bid method to determine the
27 bidder to whom a contract for public works shall be awarded. The pilot
28 program shall be limited to four state funded projects per year, two each for the
29 department and the office of Louisiana highway construction, and subject to the
30 approval of the Senate and House committees on transportation, highways and
31 public works.
32 B. (1) For purposes of this Section, if five or more responsive bids are
33 received, the department shall average the bids, excluding the highest and
34 lowest responsive bids. The department shall award the contract to the bidder
35 whose responsive bid is closest to the average. If two or more bids are equally
36 close, award shall be made to the lower bid. If no bid is equal to or less than the
37 average mean, award shall be made to the lowest responsible bidder.
38 (2) If less than three responsive bids are received, all bids will be
39 rejected.
40 C. The provisions of this Section shall not apply to professional services.
41
42 Section 3. The provisions of this Section shall terminate March 1, 2028 unless
43 legislatively extended."
