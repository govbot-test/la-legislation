HLS 26RS-949 ENGROSSED
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE BOYER
ELECTIONS/BOND & TAX: (Constitutional Amendment) Provides for eligible election
dates for bond and tax elections
1 A JOINT RESOLUTION
2 Proposing to amend Article VI, Section 22 of the Constitution of Louisiana, relative to the
3 procedure for certain special elections; to provide for local government bond and tax
4 elections; to provide that local government bond and tax elections may be held only
5 during a regularly scheduled primary election; to authorize the legislature to provide
6 for a special election; to provide for submission of the proposed amendment to the
7 electors; and to provide for related matters.
8 Section 1. Be it resolved by the Legislature of Louisiana, two-thirds of the members
9 elected to each house concurring, that there shall be submitted to the electors of the state of
10 Louisiana, for their approval or rejection in the manner provided by law, a proposal to
11 amend Article VI, Section 22 of the Constitution of Louisiana, to read as follows:
12 §22. Procedure for Certain Special Elections
13 Section 22.(A) When an election is required in a political subdivision under
14 the provisions of this constitution which require submission to the electors of a
15 proposition or question, the election shall be called, conducted, and the returns
16 thereof canvassed, in accordance with the procedures established by the law then in
17 effect pertaining to elections for incurring bonded indebtedness and special taxes
18 relative to local finance, or as may be otherwise provided by law.

1 (B) When an election is required in a political subdivision under the
2 provisions of this constitution or other law regarding bonded indebtedness or special
3 taxes relative to local finance, the election may be held only during a regularly
4 scheduled primary election. A special election may be authorized by law enacted by
5 two-thirds of the elected members of each house of the legislature.
6 Section 2. Be it further resolved that this proposed amendment shall be submitted
7 to the electors of the state of Louisiana at the statewide election to be held on November 3,
8 2026.
9 Section 3. Be it further resolved that on the official ballot to be used at the election,
10 there shall be printed a proposition, upon which the electors of the state shall be permitted
11 to vote YES or NO, to amend the Constitution of Louisiana, which proposition shall read as
12 follows:
13 Do you support an amendment to require that any local bond or tax election
14 be held only during a regularly scheduled primary election? (Amends Article
15 VI, Section 22)

HB 446 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Boyer
Abstract: Provides that local tax and bond elections may be held only during a regularly
scheduled primary election, except as otherwise provided by law enacted by two-
thirds vote of the legislature.
Present constitution (Art. VI, §22) provides that when an election is required in a political
subdivision under the provisions of the constitution which require submission to the electors
of a proposition or question, the election shall be called, conducted, and the returns thereof
canvassed, in accordance with the procedures established by the law then in effect pertaining
to elections for incurring bonded indebtedness and special taxes relative to local finance, or
as may be otherwise provided by law.
Proposed constitutional amendment retains present constitution.
Present constitution (Art. XI, §1) provides that the legislature shall adopt an election code
which shall provide for the conduct of all elections.
Proposed constitutional amendment retains present constitution.
