3 "Louisiana and to repeal Article VI, Section 30(B) of the Constitution of Louisiana,"
4 AMENDMENT NO. 2
5 On page 1, delete lines 4 and 5 and insert the following:
6 "elections, parcel fee elections, and gaming elections; to provide for such elections
7 to be held only during a regularly scheduled open primary election; to repeal
8 provisions prohibiting a political subdivision from submitting the same tax
9 proposition to the electorate more than once in a six month period except in the case
10 of an emergency; to authorize the legislature to provide"
11 AMENDMENT NO. 3
12 On page 2, line 3, after "finance," and before "the election" insert "a fee imposed within a
13 special district, the primary purpose of which is provided for in Article III, Section 13(B) of
14 this Constitution, or gaming, gambling, or wagering,"
15 AMENDMENT NO. 4
16 On page 2, delete lines 4 and 5 and insert the following:
17 "scheduled open primary election. The legislature may provide by law enacted by
18 two-thirds of the elected members of each house of the legislature for the calling of
19 special elections in the case of emergency.
20 Section 2. Be it resolved by the Legislature of Louisiana, two-thirds of the
21 members elected to each house concurring, that there shall be submitted to the
22 electors of the state of Louisiana, for their approval or rejection in the manner
23 provided by law, a proposal to repeal Article VI, Section 30(B) of the Constitution
24 of Louisiana.
25 Section 3. Be it further resolved that this proposed amendment shall become
26 effective on January 1, 2029."
27 AMENDMENT NO. 5
28 On page 2, line 6, change "Section 2." to "Section 4."
29 AMENDMENT NO. 6
30 On page 2, line 9, change "Section 3." to "Section 5."
