2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 238
BY REPRESENTATIVE DEVILLIER
of Jane Couvillion
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To express the condolences of the House of Representatives on the death of Jane Couvillion.
3 WHEREAS, it is with immense sadness and profound regret that the House of
4 Representatives of the Legislature of Louisiana has learned of the passing, on May 1, 2026,
5 of Jane Couvillion; and
6 WHEREAS, Jane was born on May 7, 1942, and her life was defined by her
7 Christian faith and devotion to her family; and
8 WHEREAS, Jane married the love of her life, Dr. John Lee Couvillion, on June 1,
9 1963; they shared fifty-three years of marriage together before his passing; she was a
10 devoted homemaker who raised her children and grandchildren in a home filled with love
11 and laughter; and
12 WHEREAS, Ms. Couvillion gave her family the gift of faith, and she and John set
13 a wonderful example of service to their church; she dedicated many years as a CCD teacher
14 at St. Thomas More, taught confirmation and pre-cana classes, and was involved in
15 St. Thomas More Couples Weekend retreats; and
16 WHEREAS, Jane cherished her monthly gatherings with friends; she shared meals
17 and Christian fellowship with her supper club group for forty years; she also enjoyed
18 meeting with her six sisters for their monthly "sister breakfast", during which they shared
19 many conversations; and
20 WHEREAS, Ms. Couvillion loved spending time with her children and
21 grandchildren, and she was blessed to live long enough to meet her first great-grandchild,
Page 1 of 2
HLS 26RS-4916 ORIGINAL
HR NO. 238
1 Emrie George, ten months before her passing; her great-grandchild was a true blessing that
2 brought much joy and laughter to her life; and
3 WHEREAS, Jane Couvillion is survived by her beloved children, Darren Couvillion
4 (Leah), Sue Andrepont (Chuck), Michelle George (Mike), and Jeanelle Fruge (Nick);
5 grandchildren, Peyton Couvillion, John Christian Lyons, Nathan Andrepont, Jacob George,
6 Ian George, Kaleb George, Grace Fruge, and Zachary Fruge; great-grandchild, Emrie
7 George; and a host of extended relatives and dear friends; she will be deeply missed, but the
8 legacy of her loving spirit shall live on in the hearts and minds of those who were privileged
9 to have known and loved her.
10 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
11 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby express heartfelt condolences on the death of Jane
12 Couvillion and does hereby extend sincere wishes that her family, friends, and loved ones
13 find comfort in their shared memories of her beautiful life.
