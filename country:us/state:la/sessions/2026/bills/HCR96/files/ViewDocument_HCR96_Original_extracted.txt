2026 Regular Session
HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 96
BY REPRESENTATIVE ADAMS
MTR VEHICLE/PUBLIC: Urges and requests the Department of Public Safety and
Corrections, office of motor vehicles, to provide its customers with educational
materials regarding the proper use of roundabouts
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 To urge and request the Department of Public Safety and Corrections, office of motor
3 vehicles (OMV), as a means of promoting public safety, to provide its customers
4 with existing roundabout educational materials prepared by the Louisiana
5 Department of Transportation and Development (DOTD).
6 WHEREAS, roundabouts are one-way, circular intersections designed to improve
7 safety and efficiency for motorists, bicyclists, and pedestrians; and
8 WHEREAS, the DOTD has increasingly utilized roundabouts throughout the state
9 as traffic control solutions and has constructed or initiated numerous roundabout projects
10 across multiple municipalities; and
11 WHEREAS, roundabouts have been shown to significantly improve traffic flow,
12 reduce congestion, and decrease the severity and numerosity of crashes; and
13 WHEREAS, safe navigation of roundabouts requires drivers to understand specific
14 rules and behaviors, including yielding to traffic and pedestrians, reducing speed when
15 entering, and properly signaling when exiting; and
16 WHEREAS, despite the increasing presence of roundabouts in Louisiana, many
17 drivers remain unfamiliar with how to properly navigate them, which may lead to unsafe
18 driving conditions; and
19 WHEREAS, the DOTD has developed educational materials, including brochures
20 and instructional guides, to inform the public about proper roundabout usage; and
Page 1 of 2

1 WHEREAS, dissemination of such educational materials by the OMV to its
2 customers would increase public awareness and knowledge of roundabouts which would
3 improve driving conditions in roundabouts across the state, further enhancing their positive
4 safety and efficiency benefits.
5 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of Louisiana does hereby
6 urge and request the OMV to provide its customers with existing educational materials
7 prepared by the DOTD regarding the proper use of roundabouts in the state.
8 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the commissioner of the OMV and secretary of
9 the DOTD shall work in conjunction with each other to determine which of the existing
10 educational materials prepared by the DOTD should be provided to OMV customers.
11 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the selected educational material shall be printed
12 and provided to OMV customers at any point during their transaction at an OMV customer
13 service center.
14 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be forwarded to the
15 commissioner of the OMV and to the secretary of the DOTD.
