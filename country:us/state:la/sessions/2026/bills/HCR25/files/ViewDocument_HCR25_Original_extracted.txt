2026 Regular Session
HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 25
BY REPRESENTATIVES BERAULT AND GLORIOSO
Team on winning its sixth consecutive Louisiana High School Athletic Association
Division IV state championship
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 To commend the Pope John Paul II Catholic High School Boys' Swim Team on winning its
3 sixth consecutive Louisiana High School Athletic Association Division IV state
4 championship.
5 WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize the Pope John Paul II Catholic High
6 School Boys' Swim Team for its sustained excellence in competition and to commend the
7 team on winning its sixth-consecutive Louisiana High School Athletic Association Division
8 IV state championship; and
9 WHEREAS, the boys' swim team is led by Head Coach Tiffany Hooks Hill, and the
10 team's moderator is Mona Erato; Richard Dieudonne serves as the school's director of
11 athletics, and Kimberlie Kilroy serves as the head of school; and
12 WHEREAS, seniors Michael Delaney, Cole Hutson, Brent Lusich, Eric Ring, and
13 Zayne Sterbenz serve as captains of the team, and the remaining members of the team
14 include James Clements, Dimitrios Fronistas, Chase Hayden, Gabriel Laurente, and Sawyer
15 Smith; and
16 WHEREAS, the boys' swim team began its state championship streak in 2020; the
17 team followed up the impressive season by dominating in Division IV competitions and
18 winning state championships in 2021, 2022, 2023, 2024, and 2025; and
19 WHEREAS, Eric Ring was recognized as the Most Outstanding Swimmer in 2022,
20 2024, and 2025; during the 2025 state meet, he won both the 200M freestyle and the 500M

1 freestyle; Michael Delaney placed sixth in the 100M breaststroke, Dimitrios Fronistas placed
2 sixth in the 100M fly, Cole Hutson placed second in the 200M individual medley and fifth
3 in the 100M backstroke, Brent Lusich placed fourth in the 200M freestyle and sixth in the
4 100M freestyle, and Zayne Sterbenz placed second in the 50M freestyle; and
5 WHEREAS, the relay team of Eric Ring, Brent Lusich, Cole Hutson, and Zayne
6 Sterbenz claimed first place in the 400M freestyle relay; the team of Cole Hutson, Michael
7 Delaney, Eric Ring, and Zayne Sterbenz placed first in the 200M medley relay; and
8 WHEREAS, the Pope John Paul II Catholic High School Boys' Swim Team is most
9 deserving of the highest recognition for their incredible achievements over the past six
10 seasons.
11 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of Louisiana does hereby
12 commend the Pope John Paul II Catholic High School Boys' Swim Team on winning its
13 sixth consecutive Louisiana High School Athletic Association Division IV state
14 championship and does hereby encourage the members of the team to continue their
15 excellent work and always strive for greatness.
