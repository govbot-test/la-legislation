HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 6
BY REPRESENTATIVE KNOX
proceedings
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 To authorize and direct the Louisiana State Law Institute to study intestate succession
3 proceedings and mechanisms to disinherit successors without a will and to report its
4 findings to the Legislature of Louisiana no later than January 4, 2027.
5 WHEREAS, succession is the transmission of the estate of the deceased to his
6 successors; and
7 WHEREAS, there are two kinds of succession, testate and intestate; and
8 WHEREAS, testate succession results from the will of the deceased, contained in a
9 testament executed in a form prescribed by law; and
10 WHEREAS, in the absence of valid testamentary disposition, the undisposed
11 property of the deceased devolves by operation of law in favor of his descendants,
12 ascendants, and collaterals; and
13 WHEREAS, many parents are estranged from their adult children; and
14 WHEREAS, Civil Code Article 1618 provides that a disinherison must be made in
15 one of the forms prescribed by testaments; and
16 WHEREAS, current law does not provide authority to disinherit a child without a
17 will; and
18 WHEREAS, if the deceased dies intestate, estranged children can inherit from the
19 deceased; and
20 WHEREAS, allowing estranged children to inherit property places a burden on
21 surviving spouses who have contributed to the community property.
Page 1 of 2
HLS 26RS-648 ENGROSSED
HCR NO. 6
1 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of Louisiana does hereby
2 authorize and direct the Louisiana State Law Institute to study intestate succession
3 proceedings and mechanisms to disinherit a successor without the formality of a will and
4 report its findings to the Louisiana Legislature no later than January 4, 2027.
5 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
6 director of the Louisiana State Law Institute.
7 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Louisiana State Law Institute shall submit
8 one printed copy and one electronic copy of any report produced pursuant to this Resolution
9 to the David R. Poynter Legislative Research Library as required by R.S. 24:772.
