16 "(c) Restore an eligible employee to the same position and same assignment at
17 the same school held before the taking of paid parental leave upon return from such
18 leave.
19 (d) Allow an eligible employee to use paid parental leave continuously or
20 intermittently, provided that the paid parental leave is used within twelve weeks of the
21 qualifying event.
22 (e) Prohibit an eligible employee who takes fewer than six weeks, or two
23 hundred-forty hours, of paid parental leave reserving his remaining weeks of paid
24 parental leave for a subsequently occurring qualifying event.
25 (f) Compensate a full-time employee at the rate of one hundred percent of the
26 employee's base pay pursuant to the salary schedule provided in R.S. 17:418 for a
27 period not to exceed six weeks during the available leave period."
