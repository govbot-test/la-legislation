SLS 26RS-35 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE BILL NO. 151
BY SENATOR MIZELL
MOTOR VEHICLES. Authorizes the office of motor vehicles to flag the license of an
individual who is in the Combined DNA Index System. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 15:609(K), relative to drawing or taking of DNA samples; to provide for rules
3 to flag a driver's license for certain persons under certain circumstances; and to
4 provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 15:609(K) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
7 §609. Drawing or taking of DNA samples
8 * * *
9 K. No later than January 1, 2027, the state police shall promulgate rules
10 to flag a driver's license for outstanding Combined DNA Index System (CODIS)
11 hits and persons that lawfully owe the state a DNA sample in order to complete
12 their full DNA profile.

DIGEST
SB 151 Original 2026 Regular Session Mizell
Present law describes circumstances in which a person's DNA is required to be taken.
Proposed law requires that no later than January 1, 2027, the state police promulgate rules
to flag a driver's license for outstanding Combined DNA Index System (CODIS) hits and
persons that lawfully owe the state a DNA sample in order to complete their full DNA
profile.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Adds R.S. 15:609(K))
