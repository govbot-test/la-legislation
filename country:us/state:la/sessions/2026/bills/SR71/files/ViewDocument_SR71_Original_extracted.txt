SLS 26RS-1871 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 71
BY SENATOR KLEINPETER
COMMENDATIONS. Commends Olivia Daigle on her achievement of being crowned the
2025-2026 Louisiana State Queen Sugar at the Louisiana Sugar Cane Festival in New Iberia.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Olivia Daigle on being crowned the 2025-2026 Louisiana State Queen Sugar
3 at the Louisiana Sugar Cane Festival in New Iberia.
4 WHEREAS, it is with great pride and admiration that the Senate of the Legislature
5 of Louisiana recognizes Olivia Daigle for her outstanding achievement in being crowned the
6 2025-2026 Louisiana State Queen Sugar at the Louisiana Sugar Cane Festival in New Iberia
7 by the Louisiana Sugar Cane Festival and Fair Association; and
8 WHEREAS, in addition to this distinguished honor, Olivia was also recognized by
9 her peers and judges as Ms. Congeniality, a testament to her grace, kindness, and exemplary
10 character; and
11 WHEREAS, as Queen Sugar, Olivia has embraced a one-year commitment to serve
12 as an ambassador for the Louisiana Sugar Cane Festival and Fair Association, and to
13 represent the organization and the state at numerous events, including the prestigious 2026
14 Washington, D.C. Mardi Gras Ball; and
15 WHEREAS, in this role she will promote Louisiana's sugar cane industry, which has
16 been a cornerstone of Louisiana's economy for more than two centuries, supporting over
17 19,000 jobs and generating an annual economic impact exceeding four billion dollars; and
18 WHEREAS, alongside her ambassadorial duties, Olivia, a dedicated scholar and

15 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to
16 Olivia Daigle and her family as an expression of the Senate's esteem, appreciation, and best
