DIGEST
SB 47 Reengrossed 2026 Regular Session Mizell
Present law requires that certain information be included on the boards and commissions
website.
Proposed law requires that the boards and commissions website include each member's
private email address or an email address accessible through the board or commission and
a phone number for each member.

Proposed law clarifies that this subsection does not require the purchase of mobile or
landline phones for board and commission members.
Proposed law provides that the chairman of a board or commission shall have a publicly
available phone number included with his information and that the chairman is designated
as the emergency contact for the board or commission.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 49:1302(D) and R.S.43:82(5) as redesignated by Acts 2025, No. 374)
