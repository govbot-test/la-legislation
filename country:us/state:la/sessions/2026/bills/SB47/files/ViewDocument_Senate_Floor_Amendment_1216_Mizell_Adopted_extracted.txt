SENATE FLOOR AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, at the end of line 14, insert the following:
3 "The provisions of this Subsection shall not require a member of a board or
4 commission to have a mobile or landline phone provided for their use with public
5 funds. The chairman of the board or commission shall have a publicly available phone
6 number at which the chairman may be reached included with his contact information.
7 The chairman shall serve as the emergency point of contact for the board or
8 commission."
9 AMENDMENT NO. 2
10 On page 2, line 8, delete "direct"
11 AMENDMENT NO. 3
12 On page 2, at the end of line 9, insert the following:
13 "The provisions of this Paragraph shall not require a member of a board or
14 commission to have a mobile or landline phone provided for their use with public
15 funds. The chairman of the board or commission shall have a publicly available phone
16 number at which the chairman may be reached included with his contact information.
17 The chairman shall serve as the emergency point of contact for the board or
18 commission."
