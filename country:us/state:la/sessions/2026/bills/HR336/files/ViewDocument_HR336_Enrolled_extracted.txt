ENROLLED
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 336
BY REPRESENTATIVE BAYHAM
A RESOLUTION
To commend the valedictorians of the Holy Cross School 2026 graduating class.
WHEREAS, it is with great pride that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana recognizes individuals who contribute to the academic excellence
of the state; and
WHEREAS, Jacob William Guichard and Peter Jeffries Schissel are the
valedictorians of the graduating class of 2026 at Holy Cross School in New Orleans,
Louisiana; and
WHEREAS, founded in 1849, the Holy Cross School serves grades pre-kindergarten
through twelve; the school upholds a tradition of academic excellence with a focus on
developing boys of strong character and citizenship; and
WHEREAS, among the 2026 graduating class, Jacob and Peter are the highest-
performing students graduating from the distinguished school; both young men are deserving
of recognition for their outstanding achievements.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Jacob William Guichard and Peter Jeffries
Schissel for graduating as the valedictorians of the Holy Cross School class of 2026 and does
hereby encourage them to continue their excellent work and always strive for greatness in
their scholastic endeavors.
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
