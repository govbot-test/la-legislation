1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 9:2798(B)(1) and to enact R.S. 9:2798(D)(4), relative to youth
3 athletics; to provide for limitation of liability; to provide for definitions; to provide
4 for an injury mitigation course; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 9:2798(B)(1) is hereby amended and reenacted and R.S. 9:2798(D)(4)
7 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §2798. Limitation of liability of a volunteer athletic coach, manager, team volunteer
9 health care provider, or official; definitions
10 * * *
11 B.(1) The provisions of this Section shall not be applicable unless the
12 volunteer athletic coach, manager, athletic trainer, team volunteer health care
13 provider, or sports team official has participated in a safety orientation and training
14 program established by the league or team with which he is affiliated or a youth
15 injury mitigation and information course. Any safety orientation and training
16 program established in compliance with this Subsection may include, as a condition
17 of satisfactory completion, a course in child cardiopulmonary resuscitation.

DIGEST
SB 260 Original 2026 Regular Session Edmonds
Present law provides a limitation of liability for any volunteer athletic coach, manager,
athletic trainer, team volunteer health care provider, or sports team official who has
participated in a safety orientation and training program established by the league or team
with which he is affiliated.
Proposed law defines "youth injury mitigation and information course".
Proposed law adds a limitation of liability for certain volunteer athletic personnel who have
completed a youth injury mitigation and information course.
