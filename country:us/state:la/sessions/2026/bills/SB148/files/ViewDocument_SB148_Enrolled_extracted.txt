Section 1:
2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
BY SENATOR STINE
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 33:103(C)(1)(l), relative to the city of Lake Charles planning
3 commission; to authorize the governing authority of the city of Lake Charles to pay
4 members of the Lake Charles planning commission a per diem; to provide for the
5 rate of per diem paid to commission members and the number of meetings for which
6 per diem shall be paid; and to provide for related matters.
7 Notice of intention to introduce this Act has been published.
8 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
9 Section 1. R.S. 33:103(C)(1)(l) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
10 §103. Planning commission; membership; appointment
11 C.(1) * * *
12 * * *
13 (l) Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, the governing
14 authorities of the municipalities of Addis, Arcadia, Brusly, Delhi, Gramercy,
15 Haughton, Haynesville, Iowa, Jena, Lake Arthur, Lake Charles, Lockport, and
16 Many may pay a per diem to members of their respective municipal planning
17 commissions for attending meetings of any such commission. The rate of per diem
18 to be paid to such members and the number of meetings for which per diem shall be
