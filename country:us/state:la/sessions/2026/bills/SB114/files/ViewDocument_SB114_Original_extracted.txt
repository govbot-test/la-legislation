SENATE BILL NO. 114
BY SENATOR HODGES
MOTOR VEHICLES. Provides for a prestige license plate for the Foundation for Moral
Law. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 47:463.243, relative to motor vehicle special prestige license plate; to provide
3 for the establishment of the "Foundation for Moral Law" special prestige license
4 plate; to provide for creation, issuance, design, fees, distribution, and rule
5 promulgation applicable to such license plates; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 47:463.243 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §463.243. Special prestige license plate; "Foundation for Moral Law"
9 A. The secretary of the Department of Public Safety and Corrections
10 shall establish a special prestige motor vehicle license plate to be known as the
11 "Foundation for Moral Law" plate, provided there is a minimum of one
12 thousand applicants for such plate. The plate shall be restricted to use on
13 passenger cars, pickup trucks, recreational vehicles, motorcycles, and vans.
14 B. The secretary shall determine the design of the plate issued under the
15 provisions of this Section. The design shall include the words "Foundation for
16 Moral Law" and the plate shall be issued, upon application, to any citizen of
17 Louisiana in the same manner as any other motor vehicle license plate when the
