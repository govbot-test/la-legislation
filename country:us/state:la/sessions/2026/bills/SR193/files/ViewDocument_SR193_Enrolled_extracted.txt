2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 193
BY SENATOR BARROW
A RESOLUTION
To urge and request that the division of administration submit to the Senate of the
Legislature of Louisiana a detailed report regarding certain contracts for which the
state paid funds, but for which services were not rendered, or which were
subsequently rescinded, canceled, or otherwise terminated prior to completion of all
deliverables specified in the contract.
WHEREAS, the prudent stewardship of public funds requires transparency and
accountability for all state contracts; and
WHEREAS, the state has entered into thousands of contracts for goods or services
over the past decade; and
WHEREAS, in some instances the state may have paid all or a portion of contract
funds for contracts for which services were not rendered, or contracts may have been
rescinded, cancelled, or otherwise terminated prior to completion, resulting in potential
financial loss or unresolved contractual obligations; and
WHEREAS, the Louisiana Senate seeks to identify, review, and assess the
circumstances, costs, and corrective actions related to such contracts.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
does hereby request that the division of administration submit a report with the following
information for each contract with an original value of one hundred thousand dollars or
greater during the previous ten years for which the state paid funds, but services were not
rendered, or the contracts were rescinded, canceled, or otherwise terminated prior to
completion of all deliverables specified in the contract:
(1) The name of the contracting state entity.
(2) The contractor or vendor legal name.
(3) The contract number, award date, and effective term.
(4) A concise description of the goods or services procured, including the name of
the product or system, if applicable.
(5) The original contract value, any amendments, the means of financing associated

(6) The reason services were not rendered or the contract was rescinded, canceled,

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the report shall be transmitted electronically to
