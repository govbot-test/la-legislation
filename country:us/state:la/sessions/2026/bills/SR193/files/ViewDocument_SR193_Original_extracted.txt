SLS 26RS-4593 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 193
BY SENATOR BARROW
CONTRACTS. Requests the division of administration to report on certain state contracts
executed within the past 10 years.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To urge and request that the division of administration submit to the Senate of the
3 Legislature of Louisiana a detailed report regarding certain contracts for which the
4 state paid funds, but for which services were not rendered, or which were
5 subsequently rescinded, canceled, or otherwise terminated prior to completion of all
6 deliverables specified in the contract.
7 WHEREAS, the prudent stewardship of public funds requires transparency and
8 accountability for all state contracts; and
9 WHEREAS, the state has entered into thousands of contracts for goods or services
10 over the past decade; and
11 WHEREAS, in some instances the state may have paid all or a portion of contract
12 funds for contracts for which services were not rendered, or contracts may have been
13 rescinded, cancelled, or otherwise terminated prior to completion, resulting in potential
14 financial loss or unresolved contractual obligations; and
15 WHEREAS, the Louisiana Senate seeks to identify, review, and assess the
16 circumstances, costs, and corrective actions related to such contracts.
17 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
18 does hereby request that the division of administration submit a report with the following

1 information for each contract with an original value of one hundred thousand dollars or
2 greater during the previous ten years for which the state paid funds, but services were not
3 rendered, or the contracts were rescinded, canceled, or otherwise terminated prior to
4 completion of all deliverables specified in the contract:
5 (1) The name of the contracting state entity.
6 (2) The contractor or vendor legal name.
7 (3) The contract number, award date, and effective term.
8 (4) A concise description of the goods or services procured, including the name of
9 the product or system, if applicable.
10 (5) The original contract value, any amendments, the means of financing associated
11 with the contract, and the total amount paid to date.
12 (6) The reason services were not rendered or the contract was rescinded, canceled,
13 or terminated.
14 (7) Any recoveries, refunds, credits, offsets, or insurance proceeds obtained or
15 expected, and the amounts and dates thereof.
16 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the report shall be transmitted electronically to
17 the president of the Louisiana Senate and the chairman of the Senate Committee on Finance,
18 on or before December 1, 2026.
19 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
20 commission of administration.
21 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of any report produced pursuant to this
22 Resolution be transmitted to the David R. Poynter Legislative Research Library as required
23 by R.S. 24:772.
