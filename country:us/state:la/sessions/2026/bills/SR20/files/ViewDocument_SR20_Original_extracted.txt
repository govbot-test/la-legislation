SLS 26RS-274 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 20
BY SENATOR HODGES
COMMENDATIONS. Commends Maggie Danielle Richardson on being selected as the
"Student of the Year" at Albany High School in Livingston Parish.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend and congratulate Maggie Danielle Richardson on being selected as the 2025-
3 2026 "Student of the Year" at Albany High School in Livingston Parish.
4 WHEREAS, Maggie Danielle Richardson has been selected as the 2025–2026
5 "Student of the Year" at Albany High School, honoring her outstanding academic
6 achievement, leadership, character, and service to her school and community; and
7 WHEREAS, Maggie is a dedicated scholar and honor student who has earned
8 consistent academic recognition while challenging herself through advanced placement
9 coursework and maintaining a strong ACT score; and
10 WHEREAS, beyond the classroom, Maggie is a respected and trusted leader among
11 her peers, serving in the Fellowship of Christian Athletes and the Student Government
12 Association, where she has contributed positively to school culture and student engagement;
13 and
14 WHEREAS, Maggie extends her leadership and service into the community by
15 volunteering as a youth basketball coach and serving as a children's ministry leader at her
16 church, reflecting her strong sense of responsibility, compassion, and service to others; and
17 WHEREAS, as a member of the Albany Lady Hornets basketball team, Maggie has
18 helped lead her team to multiple state championship appearances and has earned district
