DIGEST
SB 281 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Barrow
Present law (R.S. 33:9038.71) established the Baker Economic Development District as a
special taxing district and political subdivision of the state.
Proposed law retains present law.
Present law provides that the purpose of the district is to provide for cooperative economic
development in order to provide for the redevelopment of, and dramatic improvement to, the
property within the district located in the city of Baker.
Proposed law retains present law.
Present law provides for the boundaries of the district.
Proposed law makes changes to the boundaries of the district. Provides that the district shall
encompass only nonresidential parcels.
Effective upon signature of the governor or lapse of time for gubernatorial action.
(Amends R.S. 33:9038.71(B))
