1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 34:3158, relative to the Caddo-Bossier Parishes Port
3 Commission; to provide for confirmation of commissioners by the Senate; to provide
4 for requirements for commissioners; to modify term limits; and to provide for related
5 matters.
6 Notice of intention to introduce this Act has been published.
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
8 Section 1. R.S. 34:3158 is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
9 §3158. Creation of commission; membership; qualifications; tenure
10 A. There is hereby created a commission to be known as the Caddo-Bossier
11 Parishes Port Commission. It shall be composed of nine members who shall:
12 (1) serve Serve without compensation, as follows:.
13 (2) Be appointed subject to Senate confirmation.
14 (3) Have expertise in economic development, transportation, logistics, or
15 commercial real estate.
16 B.(1)(a) Two of the commissioners shall be appointed by the governing
17 authority of the parish of Caddo as follows:
18 (i) One of the commissioners shall reside in the parish of Caddo outside of
19 the city limits of Shreveport.
20 (ii) One of the commissioners shall reside in the parish of Caddo within the
21 city limits of Shreveport.
22 (b) Any commissioner appointed pursuant to this Paragraph shall serve a term
23 of six years.
24 (2) One of the commissioners shall be appointed by the governing authority
25 of the parish of Bossier.
26 (a) The commissioner shall reside in the parish of Bossier.

18 B.C. A vacancy on the commission for any reason shall be filled in the same
19 manner as the original appointments and for the unexpired term of office. Each
20 commissioner shall be a citizen of the United States and a qualified voter and
21 property taxpayer of the parish of Caddo or Bossier, as the case may be, and of the
22 state of Louisiana.
23 C.D. The governing authorities, in making appointments, shall endeavor to
24 provide for racial diversity of the board and to ensure that the racial proportion of the
25 appointees shall reflect the same racial proportion of the geographical area which
26 they represent. Except, the governing authority of the parish of Caddo, in making
27 appointments, shall endeavor to provide for racial diversity of the board and to
28 ensure that the racial proportion of their appointees shall reflect the same racial
29 proportion of the governing authority of the parish of Caddo.
