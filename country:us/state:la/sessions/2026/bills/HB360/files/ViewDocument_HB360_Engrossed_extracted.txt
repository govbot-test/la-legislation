HLS 26RS-654 ENGROSSED
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE BILL NO. 360
BY REPRESENTATIVES EGAN AND GLORIOSO
HIGHWAYS: Designates a portion of Louisiana Highway 41 as the "Sgt. Michael J.
Guillory Memorial Highway"
1 AN ACT
2 To designate a portion of Louisiana Highway 41 in Pearl River, Louisiana, as the "Sgt.
3 Michael J. Guillory Memorial Highway"; to provide for implementation; and to
4 provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. The portion of Louisiana Highway 41 in Pearl River, Louisiana,
7 beginning at the intersection of Louisiana Highway 41 and Lock 1 Road and ending at the
8 intersection of Louisiana Highway 41 and Louisiana Highway 36 shall be known and is
9 hereby designated as the "Sgt. Michael J. Guillory Memorial Highway".
10 Section 2. The Department of Transportation and Development is hereby directed
11 to erect and maintain appropriate signage reflecting this designation, provided local or
12 private monies are received by the department equal to the department's actual costs for
13 material, fabrication, mounting posts, and installation of the signage, not to exceed the sum
14 of seven hundred fifty dollars per sign.
