14 AMENDMENT NO. 7
15 On page 3, between lines 9 and 10, insert the following:
16 "1350.4. Annual reporting
17 A. The commission shall submit an annual report to the House Committee
18 on Commerce, the Senate Committee on Commerce, Consumer Protection and
19 International Affairs, and the governor no later than February fifteenth of each year.
20 The report shall cover the prior calendar year and shall include but not be limited to
21 all of the following:
22 (1) A list of all data centers that have requested or received new electric
23 service, interconnection, or infrastructure upgrades.
24 (2) The total new or expanded electric demand, in megawatts, associated
25 with data centers.
26 (3) A summary of all costs, including generation, transmission, and
27 distribution, allocated to data centers versus household customers, including any
28 special contracts or tariffs approved.
29 (4) Confirmation of compliance with the prohibition on cost recovery from
30 household customers, include results of any prudence reviews.
31 (5) Any instances of proposed rate adjustments or certificates denied or
32 modified pursuant to this Part.
33 (6) Recommendations for any legislative or regulatory improvements.
34 B. The commission may require utilities to submit data necessary to prepare
35 the report outlined in this Section."
