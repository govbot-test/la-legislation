HFASB441 224 5134
FOR OFFICE USE ONLY
HOUSE FLOOR AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
Senator Mizell
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 4, line 26, after "or" delete the remainder of the line and delete line 27 and insert
3 "stand-alone early learning program that is not part of an elementary school as defined
4 in R.S. 17:236."
5 AMENDMENT NO. 2
6 In Amendment No. 16 by the House Committee on Education (#4403), on page 2, delete
7 lines 30 through 33 and insert the following:
8 "(a) All of the following criteria are met:
9 (i) The school remains a member in good standing with an
10 association or organization recognized by the State Board of Elementary
11 Education for the purposes of this Section.
12 (ii) The association or organization's standards include provisions
13 addressing the supervision, health, safety, and welfare of students
14 enrolled in a prekindergarten program operated by the school that meet
15 or exceed the child safety and welfare standards provided for in this
16 Section.
17 (iii) The school provides a written attestation to the Louisiana
18 Department of Education, at the time of its annual school approval
19 application, that the association or organization's standards meet or
20 exceed the child safety and welfare standards provided for in this Section
21 and provides documentation from the association or organization
22 supporting the attestation."
23 AMENDMENT NO. 3
24 In Amendment No. 16 by the House Committee on Education (#4403), on page 3, after line
25 6, insert the following:
26 "(4) Relative to nonaccredited approved schools as provided for
27 in Paragraph (2) of this Subsection, if an association or organization has
28 submitted a complete application for recognition to the state board and
29 the state board has not acted upon such application prior to the
30 beginning of the 2026-2027 school year, the department shall accept the
31 attestation submitted by the school."
