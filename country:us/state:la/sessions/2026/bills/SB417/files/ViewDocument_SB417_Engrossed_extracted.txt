SLS 26RS-249 ENGROSSED
SB NO. 417
The original instrument and the following digest, which constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument, were prepared by Senate Legislative Services.
The keyword, summary, and digest do not constitute part of the law or proof
or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
DIGEST
SB 417 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Allain
Present law (R.S. 38:1751) provides that any parish in the state of La., except Orleans Parish,
may create its own gravity drainage districts.
Proposed law retains present law.
Present law (R.S. 38:1759) provides that the police jury organizing the drainage district shall
appoint five commissioners in each district, two of the commissioners for the term of two
years and three for the term of four years, and all selections or appointments thereafter shall
be for the term of four years. The appointment of commissioners by the police jury shall be
made upon the petition or recommendation of a majority in number of acres or a majority
of the resident land owners in the district where the district contains forty or fewer land
owners.
Proposed law provides that, notwithstanding the provisions of present law, the board of
commissioners of St. Mary Parish Consolidated Gravity Drainage District No. 2A shall
consist of seven members, subject to all other requirements of qualifications for office
provided in present law.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Adds R.S. 38:1759(B)(4))
