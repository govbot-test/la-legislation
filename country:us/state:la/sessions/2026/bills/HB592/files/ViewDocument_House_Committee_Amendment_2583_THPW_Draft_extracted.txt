23 highway, highway, roadway, or street with a posted speed limit in excess of thirty-
24 five not to exceed fifty-five miles per hour at an intersection.
25 * * *
26 D.(1) Any utility terrain vehicle operated upon a parish road or municipal
27 street shall be registered with the state of Louisiana through the Department of
28 Public Safety and Corrections, office of motor vehicles, as an off-road vehicle and
29 shall display a decal issued by the office of motor vehicles.
30 (2) Registration of utility terrain vehicles may include a quick response code
31 (QR) code containing encrypted vehicle and registration information accessible to
32 authorized law enforcement personnel. The provisions of this Paragraph shall take
33 effect when the department's electronic vehicle and title registration system is
34 updated to accommodate the registration of these vehicles and the issuance of QR
35 codes.
