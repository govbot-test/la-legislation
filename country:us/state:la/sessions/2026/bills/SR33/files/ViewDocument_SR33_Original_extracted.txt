1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend and recognize the women who play a critical role in Louisiana's many
3 industries, particularly in the chemistry industry.
4 WHEREAS, industry powers Louisiana through economic growth and job creation
5 and women are leading the way; and
6 WHEREAS, women are shaping industry and building upon Louisiana's rich energy
7 history to pave the way for a modern future that is cleaner, greener, and more diverse; and
8 WHEREAS, women of every race, class, and ethnic background have made
9 countless, significant contributions to the growth and strength of the state of Louisiana, and
10 continue to play critical economic, cultural, and social roles in every facet of life by
11 contributing to the labor force; and
12 WHEREAS, Louisiana's chemistry industry promotes the employment and
13 advancement of skilled women in the industry and benefits the state of Louisiana through
14 its community development and educational programs; and
15 WHEREAS, the industry provides thousands of high-paying jobs to women,
16 including governmental relations professionals, engineers, operators, process safety and
17 human resources professionals, contractors, construction workers, and many more.
18 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
