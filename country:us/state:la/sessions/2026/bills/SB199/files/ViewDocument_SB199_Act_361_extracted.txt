2026 Regular Session ACT No. 361 ENROLLED
BY SENATOR SEABAUGH
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 14:82.2(C)(2) and R.S. 15:541(24)(a), relative to offenses
3 concerning prostitution; to impose a mandatory minimum sentence for a second
4 offense of purchase of commercial sexual activity; to require registration as a sex
5 offender under certain circumstances; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 14:82.2(C)(2) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
8 §82.2. Purchase of commercial sexual activity; penalties
9 * * *
10 C.(1) * * *
11 (2) On a second conviction, the offender shall be imprisoned, with or
12 without hard labor, for not less than six months nor more than two years, with
13 not less than six months being served without the benefit of probation, parole,
14 or suspension of sentence, and in addition may be fined not less than one thousand
15 five hundred dollars nor more than two thousand dollars or be imprisoned, with or
16 without hard labor, for not more than two years, or both, and one-half . One-half of
17 the fines collected shall be distributed in accordance with R.S. 15:539.4.
18 * * *
19 Section 2. R.S. 15:541(24)(a) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
20 §541. Definitions
