SENATE BILL NO. 172
BY SENATOR LUNEAU
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 33:113(A) and (B), relative to the development of parishes and municipalities;
3 to provide relative to planning commissions; to provide relative to the administrative
4 procedures for approving plats by planning commissions; to provide relative to
5 exceptions for certain municipalities; to provide relative to certain requirements or
6 restrictions for approval of plats; to provide relative to the waiver of a public hearing
7 in certain circumstances; and to provide for related matters.
8 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
9 Section 1. R.S. 33:113(A) and (B) are hereby enacted to read as follows:
10 §113. Procedure; legal effect of approval of plat
11 A. A planning commission shall approve or disapprove a plat within sixty
12 days after the submission thereof to it; otherwise such the plat shall be deemed to
13 have been approved, and a certificate to that effect shall be issued by such the
14 commission on demand. The applicant for a commission's approval may, however,
15 waive this requirement and consent to an extension of such the period. The ground
16 of disapproval of any plat shall be stated upon the records of such the commission.
17 Any plat submitted to such the commission shall contain the name and address of a
18 person to whom notice of a hearing shall be sent; and no plat shall be acted on by
19 such the commission without affording a hearing thereon. Notice shall be sent to the
20 said address by certified mail of the time and place of such the hearing not less than
21 five days before the date fixed therefor. A planning commission shall give notice of
22 such the hearings, including the purpose, time, and place, by at least one publication
23 in a newspaper of general circulation in the area surrounding the proposed
24 subdivision, not less than five days prior to the hearing date; provided, however, that
25 in parishes or municipalities with a population in excess of one hundred fifty
26 thousand, the public hearing may be waived by the planning commission or planning
27 authority for subdivisions creating five or less lots not involving the creation of any
