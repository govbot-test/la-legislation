SLS 26RS-700 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 15
BY SENATOR STINE
INTERNATIONAL AFFAIRS. Recognizes the mutual interests of Quebec and Louisiana
and welcomes the Honorable Christopher Skeete, Minister of International Relations and La
Francophonie to the Louisiana State Capitol.
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 To recognize the common bond and mutual interest between Quebec and the state of
3 Louisiana and to welcome the Honorable Christopher Skeete, Minister of
4 International Relations and La Francophonie, to the Louisiana State Capitol.
5 WHEREAS, Quebec and Louisiana share deep historical, cultural, and linguistic
6 bonds rooted in a common Francophone heritage; and
7 WHEREAS, the governments of Quebec and Louisiana are linked by four
8 agreements that strengthen cooperation and collaboration, including the 2017 Joint
9 Declaration on cooperation in education, Francophonie, culture, and youth; and
10 WHEREAS, the Quebec-Louisiana Cooperation Program, established in 2015,
11 promotes exchanges and partnerships between the two jurisdictions in the areas of culture
12 and heritage; and
13 WHEREAS, the Quebec-Louisiana Parliamentary Association, also established in
14 2015, fosters dialogue and cooperation between members of the National Assembly of
15 Quebec and the Louisiana Legislature; and
16 WHEREAS, Quebec and Louisiana both collaborate within the International
17 Organization of La Francophonie, further strengthening their shared commitment to the
18 promotion of the French language and Francophone culture; and

1 WHEREAS, economic ties between Quebec and Louisiana remain strong, and in
2 2024, Louisiana ranked second among United States clients of Quebec for iron ores and fifth
3 for unwrought aluminum; and
4 WHEREAS, it is important to recognize the mutual interests of Quebec and
5 Louisiana in areas such as education, health, environmental stewardship, energy, economic
6 development, and cultural affairs, and to promote cooperation and dialogue among members
7 of their respective legislative assemblies.
8 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of Louisiana does hereby
9 recognize the common bond and mutual interests between Quebec and Louisiana and does
10 hereby welcome the Honorable Christopher Skeete, Minister of International Relations and
11 La Francophonie, to the Louisiana State Capitol.
12 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
13 Honorable Christopher Skeete.
