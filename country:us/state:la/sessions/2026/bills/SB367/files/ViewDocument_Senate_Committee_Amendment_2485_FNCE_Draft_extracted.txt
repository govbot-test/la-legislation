15 B. The purpose of the fund shall be to support the reclamation,
16 restoration, and long-term stewardship of lands and watersheds within
17 the Amite River Basin Drainage and Water Conservation District.
18 C. After allocation of money to the Bond Security and
19 Redemption Fund as provided in Article VII, Section 9(B) of the
20 Constitution of Louisiana, the treasurer shall deposit into the fund any
21 monies transferred, donated, or appropriated to the fund by the
22 legislature.
23 D. Monies in the fund shall be invested in the same manner as
24 monies in the state general fund. Any interest earned on the investment
25 of monies in the fund shall be credited to the fund. Unexpended and
26 unencumbered monies in the fund at the end of the fiscal year shall
27 remain in the fund.
28 E. The legislature may appropriate monies from the fund to the
29 board of commissioners of the Amite River Basin Drainage and Water
30 Conservation District for any purpose authorized pursuant to this
31 Section."
