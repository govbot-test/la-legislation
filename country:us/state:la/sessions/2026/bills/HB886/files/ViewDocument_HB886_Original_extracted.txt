Section 1:
HLS 26RS-519 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE ORGERON
FISHING/COMMERCIAL: Provides for reporting and data collection for commercial
menhaden fishing
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 56:325.5(D), relative to commercial menhaden harvest data reporting; to
3 provide an exception to confidentiality for reports received by a legislative
4 committee; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 56:325.5(D) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
7 §325.5. Commercial menhaden harvest reporting
8 * * *
9 D. Notwithstanding Paragraph (A)(3) of this Section or R.S. 56:301.4, any
10 report received by a legislative committee pursuant to the requirement in Subsection
11 B of this Section shall not be considered confidential.
DIGEST
The digest printed below was prepared by House Legislative Services. It constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument. The keyword, one-liner, abstract, and digest do not constitute
part of the law or proof or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
HB 886 Original 2026 Regular Session Orgeron
Abstract: Provides an exception to confidentiality for reports received by a legislative
committee.
Present law requires the commission to promulgate rules and regulations that require
companies taking menhaden by vessels equipped with purse seines for the purpose of
reduction fishing to submit monthly reports that include location data, estimated volume of
harvest, the size of the menhaden, the form for reporting, and procedures to preserve the
confidentiality of the data in compliance with federal procedures.
