SLS 26RS-264 ENGROSSED
2026 Regular Session
BY SENATOR FOIL
HEALTH/ACC INSURANCE. Provides for the method of dental reimbursement or
payments. (gov sig)
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact the introductory paragraph of R.S. 22:1157(D)(2) and to enact R.S.
3 22:1157(A)(6) and (D)(2)(c) and (4), relative to the method of dental reimbursement
4 or payments; to provide for definitions; to provide for payment of dental insurance
5 claims to a provider; to provide for applicability; to provide for an effective date; and
6 to provide for related matters.
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
8 Section 1. The introductory paragraph of R.S. 22:1157(D)(2) is hereby amended and
9 reenacted and R.S. 22:1157(A)(6) and (D)(2)(c) and (4) are hereby enacted to read as
10 follows:
11 §1157. Dental reimbursement or payments
12 A. As used in this Section, the following definitions shall apply:
13 * * *
14 (6) "Express acceptance" means a clear and direct agreement to the
15 terms of payment method, communicated explicitly by the dental plan to the
16 dentist, in writing, signifying acceptance of the payment method without any
17 ambiguity or implied actions.

3 (2) If initiating or changing payments to a dentist using electronic funds
4 transfers or credit card payments, a dental plan shall do both all of the following:
5 * * *
6 (c) Obtain from the provider or a designee of the provider an election,
7 through express acceptance, to accept a payment of the claim using the credit
8 card or electronic funds transfer payment methodology.
9 * * *
10 (4) Violation of the express acceptance required as provided in
11 Paragraph (2) of this Subsection nullifies any election on claim payment
12 methodology until such time as a new express agreement is executed.
13 Section 2. The provisions of this Act shall apply to any new policy, contract,
14 program, or dental coverage plan issued on or after January 1, 2027. Any policy, contract,
15 or dental coverage plan in effect prior to January 1, 2027, shall convert to conform to the
16 provisions of this Act on or before the renewal date, but no later than January 1, 2028.
17 Section 3. This Act shall become effective upon signature by the governor, or, if not
18 signed by the governor, upon expiration of time for bills to become law without signature
19 by the governor, as provided by Article III, Section 18 of the Constitution of Louisiana. If
20 vetoed by the governor and subsequently approved by the legislature, this Act shall become
21 effective on the day following such approval.

The keyword, summary, and digest do not constitute part of the law or proof
or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
DIGEST
SB 192 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Foil
Present law provides for insurance reimbursement or payments to dentists for dental care
services. Proposed law retains present law.
Proposed law defines "express acceptance" as a written agreement between a dental plan and
a dentist providing for a payment method.
Proposed law provides that a dentist must elect through express acceptance to receive
