SLS 26RS-295 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY SENATOR ABRAHAM
HEALTH CARE. Provides relative to the Local Healthcare Provider Participation Program.
(gov sig)
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 40:1248.3(C), relative to the Local Healthcare Provider Participation Program;
3 to provide for the authorization of assessment payments in Calcasieu Parish; to
4 provide for applicability of parish compliance provisions; to provide for an effective
5 date; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 40:1248.3(C) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §1248.3. Applicability; multi-parish funding district
9 * * *
10 C.(1) If, by June 1, 2026, Calcasieu Parish fails to authorize a local
11 hospital assessment payment pursuant to the provisions of this Subpart, the
12 governing body of any municipality within the parish with a population of
13 greater than sixty thousand shall have the authority to authorize a local hospital
14 assessment payment. Institutional providers included in the assessment
15 payment shall be limited to providers within the jurisdictional boundaries of the
16 municipality.
17 (2) Solely for the purposes of compliance with this Subpart, any such

DIGEST
SB 113 Original 2026 Regular Session Abraham
Present law authorizes the governing body of a parish to require a local hospital assessment
payment from an institutional provider in the parish.
Proposed law provides that if by June 1, 2026, Calcasieu Parish fails to authorize a local
hospital assessment payment, the governing body of any municipality within the parish with
a population of greater than 60,000 shall have the authority to authorize a local hospital
assessment payment.
Proposed law further provides that institutional providers included in the assessment
payment are limited to providers within the jurisdictional boundaries of the municipality.
Proposed law requires a participating municipality to be treated as a parish for purposes of
compliance with present law.
Effective upon signature of the governor or lapse of time for gubernatorial action.
(Adds R.S. 40:1248.3(C))
