Section 1:
2026 Regular Session ACT No. 356 ENROLLED
BY SENATOR ABRAHAM
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 40:1248.3(C), relative to the Local Healthcare Provider Participation Program;
3 to provide for the authorization of assessment payments in Calcasieu Parish; to
4 provide for applicability of parish compliance provisions; to provide for an effective
5 date; and to provide for related matters.
6 Notice of intention to introduce this Act has been published
7 as provided by Article III, Section 13 of the Constitution of
8 Louisiana.
9 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
10 Section 1. R.S. 40:1248.3(C) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
11 §1248.3. Applicability; multi-parish funding district
12 * * *
13 C.(1) If, by June 1, 2026, Calcasieu Parish fails to authorize a local
14 hospital assessment payment pursuant to the provisions of this Subpart, the
15 governing body of any municipality within the parish with a population of
16 greater than sixty thousand shall have the authority to authorize a local hospital
17 assessment payment. Institutional providers included in the assessment
18 payment shall be limited to providers within the jurisdictional boundaries of the
19 municipality.
20 (2) Solely for the purposes of compliance with this Subpart, any such
21 municipality shall be treated as if it were a parish. All requirements that apply
22 to a parish and its governing body shall equally apply to any such municipality
23 and its governing body.
24 Section 2. This Act shall become effective upon signature by the governor or, if not
25 signed by the governor, upon expiration of the time for bills to become law without signature
