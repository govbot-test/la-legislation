Section 1:
2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
BY SENATORS HODGES, BARROW, BASS, EDMONDS, FESI, HENRY, MIGUEZ,
MIZELL, MORRIS, PRESSLY, SEABAUGH, STINE AND TALBOT
AND REPRESENTATIVES AMEDEE, DICKERSON, EGAN,
HORTON, MILLER, OWEN, SCHAMERHORN AND TAYLOR
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 14:112.14 and R.S. 39:1602.3 and to repeal Section 4 of Act No. 670 of the
3 2024 Regular Session of the Legislature, relative to homeland security; to provide
4 an effective date for Act No. 670 of the 2024 Regular Session of the Legislature; to
5 authorize the division of administration to review state contracts involving foreign
6 adversaries or foreign terrorist organizations or their agents; and to provide for
7 related matters.
8 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
9 Section 1. R.S. 14:112.14 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
10 §112.14. Effective date
11 This Subpart shall become effective on August 1, 2026.
12 Section 2. R.S. 39:1602.3 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
13 §1602.3. State contracts; review for foreign adversaries and foreign terrorist
14 organizations
15 The division of administration is authorized to review all state contracts
16 to determine if they are held by:
17 (1) A foreign adversary as defined in R.S. 3:3614.
18 (2) A foreign terrorist organization as designated by the United States
19 secretary of state in accordance with Section 219 of the United States
20 Immigration and Nationality Act, Section 40 of the United States Arms Export
21 Control Act, or Section 620(A) of the United States Foreign Assistance Act of
22 1961.
23 (3) The agents of a foreign adversary or of a foreign terrorist
24 organization.
