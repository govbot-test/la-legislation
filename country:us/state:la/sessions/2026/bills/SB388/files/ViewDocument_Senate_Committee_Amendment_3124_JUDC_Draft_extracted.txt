Section 1:
SCASB388 4769 3124
SENATE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
by Senator Hodges
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, delete lines 2 through 11 and insert:
3 "To enact R.S. 14:112.14 and R.S. 39:1602.3 and to repeal Section 4 of Act No. 670 of the
4 2024 Regular Session of the Legislature, relative to homeland security; to provide
5 an effective date for Act No. 670 of the 2024 Regular Session of the Legislature; to
6 authorize the division of administration to review state contracts involving foreign
7 adversaries or foreign terrorist organizations or their agents; and to provide for
8 related matters."
9 AMENDMENT NO. 2
10 On page 1, delete lines 13 through 17 and insert:
11 "Section 1. R.S. 14:112.14 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
12 §112.14. Effective date
13 This Subpart shall become effective on August 1, 2026.
14 Section 2. R.S. 39:1602.3 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
15 §1602.3. State contracts; review for foreign adversaries and foreign terrorist
16 organizations
17 The division of administration is authorized to review all state contracts
18 to determine if they are held by:
19 1) A foreign adversary as defined in R.S. 3:3614.
20 2) A foreign terrorist organization as designated by the United States
21 secretary of state in accordance with Section 219 of the United States
22 Immigration and Nationality Act, Section 40 of the United States Arms Export
23 Control Act, or Section 620(A) of the United States Foreign Assistance Act of
24 1961.
25 3) The agents of a foreign adversary or of a foreign terrorist
26 organization.
27 * * *
28 Section 3. Section 4 of Act No. 670 of the 2024 Regular Session of the Legislature
29 is hereby repealed."
30 AMENDMENT NO. 3
31 Delete pages 2 though 7
32 AMENDMENT NO. 4
33 On page 8, at the beginning of line 1 delete "4." and insert "Section 4."
