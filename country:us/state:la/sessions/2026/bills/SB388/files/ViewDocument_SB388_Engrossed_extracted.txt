DIGEST
SB 388 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Hodges
Present law provides that the effective date of Act No. 670 of the 2024 Regular Session of
the Legislature is contingent on decisions being issued by the U.S. Supreme Court in certain
cases.
Proposed law repeals present law and provides that Act No. 670 of the 2024 Regular Session
of the Legislature will be effective on August 1, 2026.
Proposed law authorizes the division of administration to review state contracts to determine
if they are held by a foreign adversary, a foreign terrorist organization, or the agents of a
foreign adversary or of a foreign terrorist organization.
(Adds R.S. 14:112.14 and R.S. 39:1602.3; repeals Sec. 4 of Act No. 670 of the 2024 R.S.)
