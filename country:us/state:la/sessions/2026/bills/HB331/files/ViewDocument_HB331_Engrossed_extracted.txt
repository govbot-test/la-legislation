BY REPRESENTATIVE MACK
MTR VEHICLE/LICEN PLATES: Creates the Louisiana GOP special prestige license plate
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 47:463.243, relative to motor vehicle special prestige license plates; to
3 establish the "Louisiana GOP" special prestige license plate; to provide for the
4 creation, issuance, design, implementation, fees, distribution, and rule promulgation
5 applicable to such license plates; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 47.463.243 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §463.243. Special prestige license plate; Louisiana GOP
9 A.(1) The secretary of the Department of Public Safety and Corrections shall
10 establish a special prestige motor vehicle license plate to be known as the "Louisiana
11 GOP" plate, provided there is a minimum of one thousand applicants for such plate.
12 The plates shall be restricted to use on passenger cars, pick up trucks, recreational
13 vehicles, and vans.
14 (2) The Department of Public Safety and Corrections, office of motor
15 vehicles, shall create this special prestige license plate when the applicable statutory
16 provisions are met and the department's electronic vehicle and registration system
17 is updated to accommodate the creation of the new plates.
18 B. The secretary shall work in conjunction with the chair of the Republican
19 Party of Louisiana to select the color and design of the plate, provided it is in
20 compliance with R.S. 47:463(A)(3). The design shall include "Louisiana GOP".

1 C. The prestige license plate shall be issued, upon application, to any citizen
2 of Louisiana in the same manner as any other motor vehicle license plate.
3 D. The department shall collect an annual royalty fee of twenty-five dollars
4 that shall be disbursed in accordance with Subsection E of this Section. This fee
5 shall be in addition to the standard motor vehicle license tax imposed by Article VII,
6 Section 5 of the Constitution of Louisiana, and a handling fee of three dollars and
7 fifty cents for each plate to be retained by the department to offset a portion of
8 administrative costs.
9 E. The annual royalty fee shall be collected by the department and forwarded
10 to the Republican Party of Louisiana. The monies received from the royalty fees
11 shall be used solely for the activities of the Republican Party of Louisiana.
12 F. The secretary shall adopt rules and regulations as are necessary to
13 implement the provisions of this Section.

HB 331 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Mack
Abstract: Creates the "Louisiana GOP" special prestige license plate and provides for the
creation, issuance, design, implementation, fees, distributions, and promulgation of
rules applicable to these license plates.
Proposed law requires that the Dept. of Public Safety and Corrections (DPS&C), office of
motor vehicles (OMV), issue a special prestige license plate to be known as the "Louisiana
GOP" license plate, provided that there is a minimum of 1,000 applicants for such plates.
Proposed law specifies that the plate is restricted to use on passenger cars, pickup trucks,
recreational vehicles, motorcycles, and vans. Further directs the DPS&C, OMV, to create
this special prestige license plate when the applicable statutory provisions are met and the
department's electronic vehicle and registration system is updated to accommodate the
creation of the new plates.
Proposed law requires the secretary work in conjunction with the Republican Party of La.
to select the color and design of the plate. Requires the design include the words "Louisiana
GOP".
Proposed law specifies that the plate will be issued, upon application, to any citizen of the
state in the same manner as any other motor vehicle license plate.

Proposed law requires that the dept. collect an annual fee of $25 in addition to the standard

to be retained by the dept. to offset a portion of administrative costs.
Proposed law requires the annual royalty fee collected by the dept. be forwarded to the
Republican Party of La. and all monies be used solely for the activities of the Republican
Party of La.
Proposed law requires the secretary promulgate and adopt rules and regulations as are
necessary to implement the provisions of proposed law.
