HB 296 Original 2026 Regular Session Knox
Abstract: Repeals the Reentry Advisory Council and Offender Rehabilitation Workforce
Development Act and all related cross-references.
Present law (R.S. 15:1199.1-1199.16) provides for the Reentry Advisory Council and
Offender Rehabilitation Workforce Development Act. Further provides for all of the
following:
(1) The establishment of a Reentry Advisory Council and its powers, duties, and
membership.
(2) Guidelines for eligibility, participation, training, and removal from the program.

are additions.

HB NO. 296
(3) Administration of the program at facilities operated through the Dept. of Public
Safety and Corrections (DPS&C) and parish prisons.
Proposed law repeals present law.
Proposed law further amends provisions of Titles 13, 15, 36, and 47 of the La. R.S. of 1950
to remove any cross-references or eligibility requirements associated with the Reentry
Advisory Council and Offender Rehabilitation Workforce Development Act.
(Amends R.S. 13:5401(B)(1)(g), R.S. 15:827(A)(intro. para.) and (9)(a), 828(D), and
1113(A), R.S. 36:409(A), and R.S. 47:287.750(A) and (B)(intro. para.), (2) and (4); Repeals
R.S. 13:5401(B)(1)(a) and R.S. 15:1199.1-1199.16)
