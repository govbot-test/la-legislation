ENROLLED
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 134
BY REPRESENTATIVE HILFERTY
A RESOLUTION
Orleans.
WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize Robert E. "Bob" Smith Lupo for his
professional achievements and to commend him for his service to the Lakeview
neighborhood and the city of New Orleans; and
WHEREAS, Robert E. "Bob" Smith Lupo began his career at the age of fourteen
working as a laborer unloading railroad boxcars; he then served as a junior executive with
the Southern Railway System, and after a few years, became a salesman for the New Orleans
Land Company; and
WHEREAS, Bob formed a real estate development organization with his wife,
Hettie; he developed single-family homes, designed by his wife, in the Lakeview
neighborhood and ultimately branched out into commercial development; and
WHEREAS, following Hurricane Katrina, Bob took on the significant task of
restoring his commercial properties; through this work, he helped stabilize local commerce
and contributed to the revitalization of many neighborhoods; and
WHEREAS, Bob's generosity and support of public institutions strengthened the
foundations of the Lakeview community; he made valuable contributions to the Lakeview
library, which bears his name, and the New Orleans Fire Department's historic Engine 18
Firehouse; and
WHEREAS, Bob's philanthropic endeavors have inspired his descendants to follow
a similarly civic-minded and charitable path; their work has benefitted many institutions in
New Orleans, including the National World War II Museum, Tulane University, City Park,
and numerous Catholic charities; and
Page 1 of 2
HR NO. 134 ENROLLED
WHEREAS, Robert E. "Bob" Smith Lupo is most deserving of the highest
recognition for his outstanding dedication to Lakeview and the city of New Orleans.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Robert E. "Bob" Smith Lupo for his service
to Lakeview and the city of New Orleans and does hereby encourage citizens to follow his
example of civic-minded service and philanthropic work.
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
