1 AN ACT
2 To enact Subpart E of Part II of Chapter 3 of Title 46 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of
3 1950, to be comprised of R.S. 46:291, relative to food purchase cards; to create the
4 Empower Louisiana Food Purchase Program; to provide for the duties of the
5 Louisiana Department of Health; to provide for criteria for nonprofit organization
6 participation; to provide for funding; to provide for records; to provide for reporting;
7 and to provide for related matters.
8 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
9 Section 1. Subpart E of Part II of Chapter 3 of Title 46 of the Louisiana Revised
10 Statutes of 1950, comprised of R.S. 46:291, is hereby enacted to read as follows:
11 SUBPART E. EMPOWER LOUISIANA FOOD PURCHASE PROGRAM
12 §291. Empower Louisiana Food Purchase Program; Louisiana Department of
13 Health
14 A. The Louisiana Department of Health shall establish and administer
15 the Empower Louisiana Food Purchase Program. The program shall make
16 restricted-use food purchase cards available for issuance through qualified
17 nonprofit organizations.
18 B.(1) The Louisiana Department of Health shall partner with qualified
19 nonprofit organizations that operate food assistance programs in Louisiana.
20 (2) To qualify for participation in the program, the organization shall be
21 recognized under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code, execute a
22 participation agreement, and comply with reporting requirements established
23 by the Louisiana Department of Health.
24 (3) The Louisiana Department of Health may implement the program as
25 a pilot program in one administrative region of the state and may expand the

1 program as determined by the secretary. Upon statewide implementation, the
2 program shall include at least one participating qualified nonprofit organization
3 in each administrative region of the Louisiana Department of Health.
4 C.(1) Each qualified nonprofit organization shall do all of the following:
5 (a) Be responsible for funding any purchase cards issued by the
6 organization.
7 (b) Accept charitable contributions to support the program.
8 (c) Establish a process to allow individuals to provide funding for cards.
9 (2) Cards may be issued directly to the individual providing the funds or
10 to another person identified by the individual providing the funds for the card.
11 D.(1) Nothing in this Section shall be construed to create a public
12 entitlement, require participation, authorize federal funding, or merge the
13 program with Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program.
14 (2) Benefits provided pursuant to this Section shall be considered
15 charitable food assistance and shall not be construed as wages, compensation,
16 or remuneration to the recipient.
17 E. The Louisiana Department of Health shall establish uniform
18 guidelines and operating standards for the program.
19 F. The program shall do all of the following:
20 (1) Operate under a program identifier separate from Supplemental
21 Nutrition Assistance Program.
22 (2) Align food items that are allowable for purchase with food items that
23 are eligible for purchase through Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program.
24 (3) Prohibit cash withdrawals and transfers.
25 (4) Not access Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program eligibility or
26 recipient data.
27 G. Transaction records shall be generated automatically by the payment
28 processor. The Louisiana Department of Health shall generate aggregate annual
29 transaction reports, which participating nonprofits shall reconcile to their
30 internal records. The Louisiana Department of Health may review, monitor,
