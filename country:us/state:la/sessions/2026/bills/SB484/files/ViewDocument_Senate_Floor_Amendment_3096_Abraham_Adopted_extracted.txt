1 H.(1) It is the intent of the Legislature of Louisiana that the governance
2 structure established in this Section, including the creation of the governing
3 board of the consortium, shall increase federal, private, and other external
4 research funding in Louisiana and enhances the state's research competitiveness
5 for the benefit of all public postsecondary education institutions in the state.
6 (2)(a) If any member institution is denied fair, reasonable, and equitable
7 access to the consortium’s facilities, equipment, programs, and resources by the
8 staff of the consortium, the member shall notify the consortium governing
9 board. The consortium governing board shall take corrective action and
10 instruct the staff to provide services to the members institutions in compliance
11 with this Section.
12 (b) If the consortium governing board refuses to act and rectify the
13 denial of access to facilities as provided in Subparagraph (2)(a) of this
14 Paragraph within thirty days of receiving the complaint, then the member
15 institution shall notify the members of the Louisiana Senate and the members
16 of the Louisiana House of Representatives representing the parish in which the
17 member institution is located and shall notify the Senate Committee on
18 Education and the House Committee on Education.
19 I.(1) The governing board shall submit a written report no later than
20 December first of each year, in accordance with R.S. 24:771 and 772, to the
21 Senate Committee on Finance, the Senate Committee on Education, the House
22 Committee on Appropriations, and the House Committee on Education.
23 (2) The report shall include, at a minimum, data for the prior fiscal year,
24 including:
25 (a) The amount of federal, private, and other external research funding
26 obtained by or through the consortium, including a comparison to prior fiscal
27 years to reflect any increase in such funding.
