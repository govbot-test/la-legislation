15 "* * *
16 E.(1) There is hereby created the Louisiana Marine Consortium
17 Efficiency and Research Competitiveness Commission, hereinafter referred to
18 as the "commission", to study and make recommendations regarding the
19 operations, governance, administration, funding structure, and research
20 competitiveness of the Louisiana Universities Marine Consortium for Research
21 and Education.
22 (2) The purpose of the commission shall be to evaluate methods and
23 opportunities to do all of the following:
24 (a) Improve the efficiency and effectiveness of the consortium.
25 (b) Increase collaboration among public and private postsecondary
26 education institutions participating in the consortium.
27 (c) Maximize the consortium’s ability to obtain federal, private, and
28 other external research grants and funding.
29 (d) Increase equitable participation in consortium research opportunities
30 by member institutions.
31 (e) Identify opportunities to reduce administrative duplication and
32 improve operational coordination among participating institutions.
33 (f) Strengthen Louisiana’s competitiveness for marine, coastal,
34 environmental, fisheries, and related scientific research funding.
35 (3) The commission shall be composed of the following voting members:
36 (a) The president of the Louisiana State University System, or his
37 designee.
38 (b) The president of the Southern University System, or his designee.
39 (c) The president of the University of Louisiana System, or his designee.
40 (d) The president of the Louisiana Community and Technical College
41 System, or his designee.
