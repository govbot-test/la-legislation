2026 Regular Session ACT No. 246 ENROLLED
SENATE BILL NO. 140
BY SENATOR MILLER
1 AN ACT
2 To enact Code of Civil Procedure Art. 2813, relative to probate procedure; to provide
3 relative to multiple successions in the same proceeding; to provide relative to
4 successions of more than one deceased person; to provide relative to jurisdiction of
5 the court over successions; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. Code of Civil Procedure Art. 2813 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 Art. 2813. Multiple successions in the same proceeding
9 The same proceeding may be used to open the successions of more than
10 one deceased person regardless of the dates of death of the deceased persons,
11 provided that the court has jurisdiction over each succession pursuant to Article
12 2811.
PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
GOVERNOR OF THE STATE OF LOUISIANA
