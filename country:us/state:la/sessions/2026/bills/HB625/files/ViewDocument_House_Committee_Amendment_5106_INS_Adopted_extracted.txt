7 AMENDMENT NO. 3
8 On page 1, delete lines 12 through 20 in their entirety
9 AMENDMENT NO. 4
10 On page 2, delete lines 1 through 11 in their entirety and insert in lieu thereof the following:
11 "D.(1) If a peer-to-peer car sharing program does not provide a
12 non-insurance contractual physical damage protection package, consistent with R.S.
13 22:1300.10(4), to the shared vehicle during the car sharing period, the program shall
14 own and maintain as the named insured a policy of insurance written by an insurer
15 admitted or approved in this state, covering physical damage for collision and
16 comprehensive losses to the shared vehicle during the car sharing period.
17 (2) The policy of insurance required by this Subsection applies when the
18 policy of insurance maintained by the shared vehicle owner excludes or otherwise
19 does not provide coverage for physical damage for collision and comprehensive
20 losses to the shared vehicle during the car sharing period."
21 AMENDMENT NO. 5
22 On page 2, line 14, change "Subpart" to "Subsection"
