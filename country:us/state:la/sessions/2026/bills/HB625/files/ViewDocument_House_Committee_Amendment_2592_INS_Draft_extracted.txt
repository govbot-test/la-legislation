1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, delete line 2 in its entirety and insert in lieu thereof the following:
3 "To enact R.S. 22:1300.9(D), relative to peer-to-peer car sharing"
4 AMENDMENT NO. 2
5 On page 1, delete line 8 in its entirety and insert in lieu thereof the following:
6 "Section 1. R.S. 22:1300.9(D) is hereby enacted to read as"
7 AMENDMENT NO. 3
8 On page 1, delete lines 12 through 20 in their entirety
9 AMENDMENT NO. 4
10 On page 2, delete lines 1 through 11 in their entirety and insert in lieu thereof the following:
11 "D.(1) If a peer-to-peer car sharing program does not provide a non-insurance
12 contractual physical damage protection package, consistent with R.S. 22:1300.10(4)
13 to the shared vehicle during the car sharing period, the program shall own and
14 maintain as the named insured a policy of insurance written by an insurer admitted
15 or approved in this state, covering physical damage for collision and comprehensive
16 losses to the shared vehicle during the car sharing period.
17 (2) The policy of insurance required by this Subsection applies when the
18 policy of insurance maintained by the shared vehicle owner excludes or otherwise
19 does not provide coverage for physical damage for collision and comprehensive
20 losses to the shared vehicle during the car sharing period."
