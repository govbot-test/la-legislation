2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 133
BY REPRESENTATIVE MENA
A RESOLUTION
WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize Colette Hirstius for her outstanding
professional achievements and to commend her on being named president of Shell USA; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Hirstius is a New Orleans native who grew up in Gentilly and
graduated from St. Mary's Dominican High School and Tulane University; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Hirstius's history is deeply rooted in the state of Louisiana; her
father, Broderick Bagert, was a lawyer and New Orleans City Council member in the 1970s
and served on the Louisiana Public Service Commission in the 1980s; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Hirstius's two-decade career at Shell has placed her at locations
around the world, including West Africa and the company's headquarters in The Hague,
Netherlands; and
WHEREAS, in her new role, Ms. Hirstius will oversee more than thirteen thousand
Shell employees and the European oil giant's portfolio of energy companies; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Hirstius will remain in New Orleans while serving as president of
Shell's U.S. operations, making her the first president of Shell USA to be based in Louisiana;
and
WHEREAS, Colette Hirstius has brought great pride to the state of Louisiana through
her service at Shell, and she is most deserving of the highest recognition for her outstanding
career achievements.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Colette Hirstius on being named president
of the Shell USA and does hereby extend sincere wishes that she remain prosperous in her
endeavors as she begins a new chapter in her career.
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
