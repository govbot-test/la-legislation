Section 1:
2026 Regular Session ACT No. 752 ENROLLED
BY SENATORS BARROW, ABRAHAM, BARTHELEMY, CARTER, DUPLESSIS,
HENRY, JACKSON-ANDREWS, JENKINS, LUNEAU, MIZELL,
PRICE, SELDERS AND WHEAT
1
2 AN ACT
3 To enact R.S. 46:2136.2(H), relative to domestic abuse assistance; to authorize the creation
4 of a statewide portal to allow an online application for a protective order; to provide
5 relative to a petition for a temporary restraining order; and to provide for related
6 matters.
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
8 Section 1. R.S. 46:2136.2(H) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
9 §2136.2. Louisiana Protective Order Registry; statewide portal
10 * * *
11 H. The judicial administrator's office may develop and implement a
12 statewide portal that allows petitioners to file an online petition requesting any
13 protective order authorized by this Part. Notwithstanding any provisions of R.S.
14 46:2134(D), any petition for a protective order filed through a statewide portal
15 shall require an affirmation signed and dated by electronic means by the
16 petitioner that the facts and circumstances contained in the petition are true
17 and correct to the best knowledge, information, and belief of the petitioner,
18 under penalty of perjury pursuant to R.S. 14:123. The affirmation shall be
19 made before a witness who shall sign his name electronically.
PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
GOVERNOR OF THE STATE OF LOUISIANA
