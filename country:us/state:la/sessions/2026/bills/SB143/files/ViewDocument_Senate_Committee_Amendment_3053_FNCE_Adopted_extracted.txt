19 "§2405.2. Commission on Law Enforcement Bulletproof Vest Fund;
20 creation of fund; eligibility
21 A. The legislature hereby finds and declares all of the following:
22 (1) Smaller local law enforcement agencies within this state often
23 lack sufficient financial resources to provide adequate protective
24 equipment, including bulletproof vests, for their commissioned peace
25 officers.
26 (2) The provision of bulletproof vests is essential to protect the
27 lives and safety of those officers who serve and protect the public.
28 (3) The establishment of a dedicated fund is necessary to assist
29 local law enforcement agencies with limited financial capacity in
30 acquiring bulletproof vests, thereby saving lives and enhancing officer
31 safety.
32 B. For the purposes of this Section, the following terms shall
33 mean:
34 (1) "Commission" means the Louisiana Commission on Law
35 Enforcement and Administration of Criminal Justice.
36 (2) "Eligible law enforcement agency" means any local,
37 municipal, or parish law enforcement agency within this state that
38 employs fifteen or fewer full-time commissioned peace officers.
39 (3) "Fund" means the Commission on Law Enforcement
40 Bulletproof Vest Fund established pursuant to this Section.
41 (4) "Peace officer" shall have the same definition as provided in
42 R.S. 40:2402.
43 C.(1) There is hereby established in the state treasury, as a special
44 fund, the "Commission on Law Enforcement Bulletproof Vest Fund"
Page 1 of 2

1 that shall be administered by the Louisiana Commission on Law
2 Enforcement and Administration of Criminal Justice.
3 (2) After allocation of money to the Bond Security and
4 Redemption Fund as provided in Article VII, Section 9(B) of the
5 Constitution of Louisiana, the treasurer shall deposit into the fund any
6 monies transferred, donated, or appropriated to the fund by the
7 legislature. The source of monies deposited into the fund may include
8 grants, donations, gifts, and any federal funds received for the purchase
9 of bulletproof vests, or any other monies that are made available from
10 any other sources that are collected for the purposes of this Section.
11 (3) Monies in the fund shall be invested in the same manner as
12 monies in the state general fund. Any interest earned on the investment
13 of monies in the fund shall be credited to the fund. Unexpended and
14 unencumbered monies in the fund at the end of the fiscal year shall
15 remain in the fund.
16 (4) The legislature may appropriate monies from the fund to the
17 commission for any purpose authorized pursuant to this Section.
18 D.(1) Monies in the fund shall be used solely to provide financial
19 assistance to eligible law enforcement agencies for the purchase of
20 bulletproof vests for commissioned peace officers.
21 (2) In awarding funds pursuant to this Section, the commission
22 shall give priority to eligible law enforcement agencies that demonstrate
23 a lack of adequate bulletproof vests for their commissioned peace
24 officers.
25 (3) The commission may consider the extent to which an agency
26 lacks bulletproof vests, the number of officers without vests, and the
27 agency’s financial needs when determining awards.
28 (4) The commission may establish matching fund requirements
29 or cost-sharing provisions as it deems appropriate."
