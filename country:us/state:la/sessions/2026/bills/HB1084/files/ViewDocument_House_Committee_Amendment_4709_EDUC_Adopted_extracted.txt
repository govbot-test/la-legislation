HCAHB1084 224 4709
HOUSE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
by Representative Turner
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, line 2, after "To" delete the remainder of the line and insert "enact R.S. 17:3384,
3 relative to"
4 AMENDMENT NO. 2
5 On page 1, delete lines 8 through 18 and delete pages 2 and 3 and insert the following:
6 "Section 1. R.S. 17:3384 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
7 §3384. Public postsecondary education institutions; tuition and fee authority and
8 autonomy
9 A. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, each public
10 postsecondary education institution may establish and adjust tuition and fee amounts
11 for all programs offered by the institution, including programs granting certificates,
12 degrees, diplomas, or other credentials of value. This authority includes establishing
13 tuition and fee amounts for part-time students, dual enrollment students, and students
14 enrolled in summer and intersession terms.
15 B. When establishing tuition and fee amounts, each public postsecondary
16 education institution shall, at a minimum, consider the following:
17 (1) Tuition and fee amounts of peer institutions.
18 (2) Median household income in Louisiana and in states where peer
19 institutions are located.
20 (3) Affordability.
21 (4) Total cost of attendance.
22 C. Each public postsecondary education institution shall establish a process
23 for providing adequate notice to students of tuition and fee amounts. Prior to
24 imposing any increase in tuition and fee amounts pursuant to this Section, each
25 institution shall establish criteria for waivers in cases of financial hardship. Such
26 information shall be made available to all current and prospective students affected
27 by the increase in a timely manner.
28 D. Each public postsecondary education institution shall submit a written
29 report to its management board, the Board of Regents, the House Committee on
30 Education, the Senate Committee on Education, the House Committee on
31 Appropriations, and the Senate Committee on Finance not later than February
32 fifteenth annually regarding how the tuition and mandatory fees authorized by this
33 Section are being implemented."
