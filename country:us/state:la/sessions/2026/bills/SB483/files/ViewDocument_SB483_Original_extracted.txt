15 districts as provided in R.S. 37:1173. and one
16 (2) Eight members representing licensed healthcare professionals as
17 provided in R.S. 37:1173.1.

13 A. Except as provided in R.S. 37:1175, pharmacist members of the board and
14 licensed healthcare professionals who are not pharmacists appointed pursuant
15 to R.S. 37:1173.1 shall be appointed for a term of six years, beginning on July first

18 healthcare professionals who are not pharmacists appointed pursuant to R.S.

21 appointed and qualified.

DIGEST
SB 483 Original 2026 Regular Session Reese
Present law provides for the membership of the La. Board of Pharmacy.
Present law provides for 16 pharmacist members on the board, with two members selected
from each of the eight pharmacy districts established in the state following an election and
submission of the top three names to the governor for appointment.
Proposed law changes the number of pharmacists selected from the pharmacy districts from
16 to 8, with one pharmacist elected from each district.
Proposed law provides for the following eight licensed healthcare professional members on
the board:
(1) Three sterile compounding pharmacists.
(2) One chain pharmacy pharmacist.
(3) One hospital pharmacy pharmacist.
(4) One independent community pharmacy pharmacist.
(5) One licensed physician.
(6) One registered nurse.
Present law provides for one consumer member who serves at the pleasure of the governor.
Proposed law retains present law.
Present law provides that the governor shall fill the vacancy of the consumer member at his
pleasure.
