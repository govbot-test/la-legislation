2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 201
BY REPRESENTATIVE YOUNG
A RESOLUTION
To commend Ahmad Hudson of the Ruston High School boys basketball team on being
named 2026 Mr. Basketball in Louisiana.
WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize Ahmad Hudson for his outstanding
athletic achievements at Ruston High School and to commend him on being named 2026
Mr. Basketball in Louisiana; and
WHEREAS, Ahmad is the son of Lincoln Preparatory School boys basketball Coach
Antonio Hudson and a standout junior on the Ruston High School Bearcats basketball team;
and
WHEREAS, Ahmad stands six feet seven inches tall and is one of the country's most
talented two-sport athletes; he is ranked by ESPN as the Class of 2027's nineteenth-ranked
basketball player and twenty-second-ranked football player; and
WHEREAS, Ahmad led the Bearcats to its first boys basketball state championship
appearance in thirty-eight years and became the first high school basketball player from
North Louisiana to be named Mr. Basketball; and
WHEREAS, Ahmad enjoyed an outstanding season in which he averaged 19.7
points, 13.4 rebounds, two assists, and 2.7 blocks per game; in the state championship game,
he scored thirty-two points, grabbed twenty-four rebounds, had four assists, and totaled
seven blocks; and
WHEREAS, Ahmad has received offers from numerous Power 4 schools, including
receiving an offer to play for the Louisiana State University basketball team; and
WHEREAS, Ahmad Hudson is an outstanding young athlete who is most deserving
of recognition for his incredible achievements.

HR NO. 201 ENROLLED
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Ahmad Hudson of the Ruston High School
