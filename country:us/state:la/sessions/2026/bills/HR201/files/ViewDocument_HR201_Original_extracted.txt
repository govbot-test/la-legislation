HLS 26RS-3667 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 201
BY REPRESENTATIVE YOUNG
COMMENDATIONS: Commends Ahmad Hudson of the Ruston High School boys
basketball team on being named 2026 Mr. Basketball in Louisiana
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Ahmad Hudson of the Ruston High School boys basketball team on being
3 named 2026 Mr. Basketball in Louisiana.
4 WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize Ahmad Hudson for his outstanding
5 athletic achievements at Ruston High School and to commend him on being named 2026
6 Mr. Basketball in Louisiana; and
7 WHEREAS, Ahmad is the son of Lincoln Preparatory School boys basketball Coach
8 Antonio Hudson and a standout junior on the Ruston High School Bearcats basketball team;
9 and
10 WHEREAS, Ahmad stands six feet seven inches tall and is one of the country's most
11 talented two-sport athletes; he is ranked by ESPN as the Class of 2027's nineteenth-ranked
12 basketball player and twenty-second-ranked football player; and
13 WHEREAS, Ahmad led the Bearcats to its first boys basketball state championship
14 appearance in thirty-eight years and became the first high school basketball player from
15 North Louisiana to be named Mr. Basketball; and
16 WHEREAS, Ahmad enjoyed an outstanding season in which he averaged 19.7
17 points, 13.4 rebounds, two assists, and 2.7 blocks per game; in the state championship game,
18 he scored thirty-two points, grabbed twenty-four rebounds, had four assists, and totaled
19 seven blocks; and
20 WHEREAS, Ahmad has received offers from numerous Power 4 schools, including
21 receiving an offer to play for the Louisiana State University basketball team; and
