SLS 26RS-283 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 83
BY SENATOR MIZELL
WOMEN. Commends Our Lady of Angels on its work with Project M.O.M.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Our Lady of Angels on its work with Project Maternal Overdose
3 Mortality (Project M.O.M.).
4 WHEREAS, Our Lady of Angels Hospital has served as an inaugural catalyst site for
5 the Project M.O.M. program, dedicating itself to the support and further care of pregnant and
6 postpartum women with substance use disorder; and
7 WHEREAS, accidental opioid overdose has been the leading cause of pregnancy-
8 associated death in Louisiana since 2018; and
9 WHEREAS, every preventable death is a tragedy, but the death of a pregnant woman
10 or young mom doubles the tragedy, as the loss of the mother endangers her child; and
11 WHEREAS, the goal of Project M.O.M. is to reduce pregnancy-associated opioid
12 overdose deaths by 80% within three years by:
13 (1) Scaling best practices and pilot programs across Louisiana that increase substance
14 use screening across prenatal providers and facilities.
15 (2) Increasing availability and access to substance use disorder treatments including
16 medications for opioid use disorder.
17 (3) Improving care coordination for pregnant and postpartum women, including
18 linking emergency department overdoses to rapid outpatient treatment.

1 (4) Increasing accessibility of lifesaving medications including naloxone to prevent
2 overdose mortality.
3 WHEREAS, Our Lady of Angels in Bogalusa developed a specialized Family
4 Medicine-OB Partnership to expand access to medications for opioid use disorder for
5 pregnant women with substance use disorder and to provide integrated primary care
6 throughout their journey; and
7 WHEREAS, Our Lady of Angels has also implemented targeted treatment plans for
8 infants exposed to substances that improved withdrawal care, shortened hospital stays, and
9 supported mother-infant bonding; and
10 WHEREAS, this region experienced a 35% decrease in overdose mortality after the
11 implementation of the program; and
12 WHEREAS, through the Project M.O.M. efforts of hospitals statewide, including Our
13 Lady of Angels, overdose mortality is projected to fall by 80% over the next few years.
14 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
15 does hereby commend Our Lady of Angels on their work with Project M.O.M. and for
16 becoming a model for the state's Project M.O.M. efforts.
17 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to Our
18 Lady of Angels.
