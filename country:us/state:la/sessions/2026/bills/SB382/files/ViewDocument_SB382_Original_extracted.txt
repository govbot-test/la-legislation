WORKERS' COMPENSATION. Repeals provisions relative to the Workers' Compensation
Advisory Council. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To repeal R.S. 23:1294 and R.S. 36:309(B)(2), relative to the Workers' Compensation
3 Advisory Council; to repeal provisions relative to membership, appointment by the
4 governor and confirmation by the Senate, reporting and policy recommendation
5 requirements, and immunity from liability; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 23:1294 and R.S. 36:309(B)(2) are hereby repealed.

DIGEST
SB 382 Original 2026 Regular Session Seabaugh
Present law creates the Workers' Compensation Advisory Council (council) within Louisiana
Works. Present law further provides that the council consists of 17 members who are
domiciled in Louisiana and appointed by the governor to serve at the pleasure of the
governor. Present law further provides relative to the duties of the council and members'
immunity from liability under certain circumstances.
Present law further requires the council to review and make recommendations to the
governor, through Louisiana Works, on any proposed rules affecting the administration or
resolution of claims.
Proposed law repeals present law.
