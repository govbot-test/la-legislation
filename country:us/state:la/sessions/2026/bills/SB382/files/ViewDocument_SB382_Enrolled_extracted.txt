2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
BY SENATOR SEABAUGH
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 23:1034.2(C)(1) and to repeal R.S. 23:1294 and R.S.
3 36:309(B)(2), relative to the Workers' Compensation Advisory Council; to repeal
4 provisions relative to membership, appointment by the governor and confirmation
5 by the Senate, reporting and policy recommendation requirements, and immunity
6 from liability; to require the assistant secretary of Louisiana Works to reassess the
7 workers' compensation medical reimbursement schedule; to provide for certain
8 requirements relative to the reimbursement schedule; and to provide for related
9 matters.
10 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
11 Section 1. R.S. 23:1034.2(C)(1) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
12 §1034.2. Reimbursement schedule

16 and supplies. Except as provided for in Subparagraph (b) of this Paragraph, any
17 Any necessary adjustments to the reimbursement schedule adopted and established
18 in accordance with the provisions of this Section may be made annually.
19 (b) The assistant secretary shall reassess the reimbursement schedule
20 provided for in this Section and shall promulgate and implement any necessary
