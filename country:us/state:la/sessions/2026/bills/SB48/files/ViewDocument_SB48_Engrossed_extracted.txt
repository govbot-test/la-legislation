SLS 26RS-39 ENGROSSED
2026 Regular Session
BY SENATOR MIZELL
MOTOR VEHICLES. Establishes reciprocity between Louisiana and Ireland for driver's
license recognition. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 32:404(F)(1) and to enact R.S. 32:404(G), relative to
3 international driving privileges; to provide for technical corrections; to authorize
4 establishing reciprocity with Ireland relative to driver's licenses; and to provide for
5 related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 32:404(F)(1) is hereby amended and reenacted and R.S. 32:404(G)
8 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
9 §404. Operation of motor vehicles by nonresidents, students, and military personnel
10 and dependents; reciprocity agreements with foreign countries
11 * * *
12 F.(1) The deputy secretary of public safety services of the Department of
13 Public Safety and Corrections is hereby authorized to promulgate rules and
14 regulations as may be necessary to enter into reciprocity agreements with
15 jurisdictions or agencies in foreign countries or with foreign governments that issue
16 driver's licenses or the equivalent.
17 * * *

SLS 26RS-39 ENGROSSED
SB NO. 48
1 G. The Department of Public Safety and Corrections, office of motor
2 vehicles, shall enter a reciprocity agreement with the country of Ireland.
The original instrument and the following digest, which constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument, were prepared by Senate Legislative Services.
The keyword, summary, and digest do not constitute part of the law or proof
or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
DIGEST
SB 48 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Mizell
Present law authorizes the deputy secretary of public safety services of the Department of
Public Safety and Corrections to promulgate rules and regulations relative to reciprocity
agreements with jurisdictions, foreign countries or foreign governments. Proposed law
changes the limited provision of the deputy secretary of public safety services and authorizes
the Department of Public Safety and Corrections.
Proposed law directs the Department of Public Safety and Corrections, office of motor
vehicles, to enter into a reciprocity agreement with the country of Ireland.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 32:404(F)(1); adds R.S. 32:404(G))
