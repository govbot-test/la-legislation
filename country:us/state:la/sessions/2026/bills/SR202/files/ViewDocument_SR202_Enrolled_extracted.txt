2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 202
BY SENATOR OWEN
A RESOLUTION
To commend and congratulate Ed Tillison as a pioneer in professional football and to
recognize his leadership in both business and community service.
WHEREAS, Ed Tillison, a native of Pearl River, Louisiana, and a graduate of Pearl
River High School, has distinguished himself as the first resident of Pearl River to become
a professional football player for the Detroit Lions and to compete in the National Football
League; and
WHEREAS, his performance at the professional level demonstrated exceptional
athleticism, perseverance, and dedication, establishing him as a trailblazer for athletes from
Pearl River and becoming a source of great pride for Pearl River and the surrounding region;
and
WHEREAS, beyond his athletic accomplishments, Ed Tillison has made a lasting
impact, which will inspire future generations, through his commitment to community service
and development, mentorship of youth, and support of local initiatives designed to improve
the quality of life in the Pearl River community; and
WHEREAS, Ed Tillison has contributed to and invested in the economic growth and
vitality of the Pearl River community through business ventures, which include Tillison
Trucking & Bobcat Services, LLC and Tillison Carriages, LLC, providing services and
employment opportunities for residents of Pearl River and beyond; and
WHEREAS, his leadership in both business and community service reflects a deep
commitment to the well-being and advancement of his hometown; and
WHEREAS, Ed Tillison's legacy as an athlete, entrepreneur, and community leader
represents the highest ideals of dedication, integrity, and civic pride and will leave a lasting
impact on the Pearl River community and the state.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
does hereby commend and congratulate Ed Tillison for his pioneering achievements as the
first professional football player from Pearl River, Louisiana, and does hereby recognize his
leadership in both business and community service.
