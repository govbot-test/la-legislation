SLS 26RS-855 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 50
MOORE
COMMENDATIONS. Commends and congratulates the Wossman High School boys'
basketball team on becoming state runner-up.
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 To commend and congratulate the Wossman High School Wildcats boys' basketball team
3 upon being the Louisiana High School Athletic Association 2026 Division II Non-
4 Select state runner-up.
5 WHEREAS, the Wossman High School Wildcats basketball team completed a
6 sensational season, earning the title of Division II Non-Select state runner-up at the
7 Louisiana High School Athletic Association (LHSAA) basketball championship in March
8 2026; and
9 WHEREAS, the Wildcat team finished the 2025-26 season with an impressive 22-8
10 overall record and competed in its third consecutive LHSAA state championship game; and
11 WHEREAS, senior Jontae Turpin Jr. contributed twelve points, six rebounds, and
12 three steals in the championship game, while senior shooting guard, Roosevelt Dean Jr.,
13 added fourteen points; and
14 WHEREAS, Head Coach Casey Jones, along with Assistant Coaches O. Brass, A.
15 Burton, C. J. Jones, D. Riley, D. Comanche, and D. Greely, led the Wildcats deep into the
16 playoffs, while athletic trainer Jody Powell kept the players in top-notch form; and
17 WHEREAS, proudly donning the purple and gold each game are C. J. Williams Jr.,
18 Jontae Turpin Jr., Cam Washington, Deterrius Williams, Dontavious Daggs, Teborious
Page 1 of 2
SLS 26RS-855 ORIGINAL
SCR NO. 50
1 Taylor, Paul Lewis, Teandre Triplett, Kaden Sales, Antoine Austin, Jaterrance Long,
2 Roosevelt Dean Jr., Zalyn Jones, Robert Clark III, and Justin Wilson; and
3 WHEREAS, all members of the team performed at the highest level of excellence
4 throughout the season, each making vital contributions to the team's success; and
5 WHEREAS, the team's outstanding season, built on hard work, commitment, and the
6 relentless pursuit of excellence, upholds the proud tradition of Wossman High School and
7 and brings great pride and honor for the school, community, and state.
8 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of Louisiana does hereby
9 commend and congratulate the Wossman High School Wildcats basketball team upon being
10 the LHSAA 2026 Division II Non-Select state runner-up.
11 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to
12 Casey Jones, head coach of the Wossman High School Wildcats basketball team, and Dena
13 Sampson, principal of Wossman High School.
