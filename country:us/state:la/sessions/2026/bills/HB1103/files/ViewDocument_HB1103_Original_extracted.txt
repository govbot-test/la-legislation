HB 1103 Original 2026 Regular Session Turner
Abstract: Provides relative to the regulation of construction or improvement of industrial
facilities.
Present law provides that, excluding the applicable requirements of the International
Plumbing Code, the provisions of present law shall not apply to the construction or
improvement inside the secured or fenced confines of certain industrial facilities that are
engaged in certain activities.
Proposed law includes 334511 search, detection, navigation, guidance, aeronautical, and
nautical system and instrument manufacturing to the list of activities outlined in present law.
Present law provides relative to the regulation of construction by local governments.
Proposed law adds, notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, no political
subdivision shall require a building permit for, apply the provisions of a building code to,
or otherwise regulate pursuant to present and proposed law, the construction or improvement
inside the secured or fenced confines of the industrial facilities exempted in proposed law.
Effective upon signature of governor or lapse of time for gubernatorial action.
(Adds R.S. 40:1730.29(A)(20) and 1730.40(D))
