SPECIAL DAY/WEEK/MONTH: Designates February 24 as SCN2A awareness day
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 1:58.10, relative to days of remembrance; to provide for SCN2A day in the
3 state; and to provide for related matters.
4 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
5 Section 1. R.S. 1:58.10 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
6 §58.10. SCN2A Day
7 A. February twenty-fourth is designated as SCN2A Day throughout the state
8 of Louisiana.
9 B. This Section shall be known and may be cited as the "Emily Katherine
10 Dedon Act" in memory of the beautiful young child whose passing highlighted the
11 need for awareness of SCN2A.
