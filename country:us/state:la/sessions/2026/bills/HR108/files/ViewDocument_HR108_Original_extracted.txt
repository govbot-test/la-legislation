HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 108
BY REPRESENTATIVE WILDER
COMMENDATIONS: Commends the Denham Springs High School Robotics Team on
winning the FIRST Robotics Pikes Peak Regional Competition
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend the Denham Springs High School Robotics Team on winning the FIRST
3 Robotics Pikes Peak Regional Competition.
4 WHEREAS, the Denham Springs High School Robotics Team, Denham Venom,
5 captured a victory at the FIRST Robotics Pikes Peak Regional Competition in Colorado
6 Springs, Colorado, and secured a place in the 2026 FIRST World Championship in Houston,
7 Texas; and
8 WHEREAS, Denham Venom defeated a field a thirty-three teams, including
9 international competitors from Mexico and Taiwan; with the victory, the team rose to
10 fifteenth in the world rankings, thirteenth in the United States, and first in Louisiana; and
11 WHEREAS, Denham Venom is led by Coach Daniel Eiland, and its major sponsors
12 include NASA, Weyerhauser, DoD STEM, West Coast Products, Fabworks, the Gene Haas
13 Foundation, and Limelight; and
14 WHEREAS, in addition to the victory, Denham Venom earned the Quality Award
15 and was recognized for excellence in robotic design and pit efficiency; and
16 WHEREAS, the Denham Springs High School Robotics Team is most deserving of
17 the highest recognition for its outstanding success and for earning the opportunity to
18 represent the state of Louisiana at the FIRST World Championships.
19 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
20 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend the Denham Springs High School Robotics
21 Team on winning the FIRST Robotics Pikes Peak Regional Competition and does hereby
