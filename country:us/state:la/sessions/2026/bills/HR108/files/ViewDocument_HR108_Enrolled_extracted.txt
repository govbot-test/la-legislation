HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 108
BY REPRESENTATIVE WILDER
A RESOLUTION
To commend the Denham Springs High School Robotics Team on winning the FIRST
Robotics Pikes Peak Regional Competition.
WHEREAS, the Denham Springs High School Robotics Team, Denham Venom,
captured a victory at the FIRST Robotics Pikes Peak Regional Competition in Colorado
Springs, Colorado, and secured a place in the 2026 FIRST World Championship in Houston,
Texas; and
WHEREAS, Denham Venom defeated a field a thirty-three teams, including
international competitors from Mexico and Taiwan; with the victory, the team rose to
fifteenth in the world rankings, thirteenth in the United States, and first in Louisiana; and
WHEREAS, Denham Venom is led by Coach Daniel Eiland, and its major sponsors
include NASA, Weyerhauser, DoD STEM, West Coast Products, Fabworks, the Gene Haas
Foundation, and Limelight; and
WHEREAS, in addition to the victory, Denham Venom earned the Quality Award
and was recognized for excellence in robotic design and pit efficiency; and
WHEREAS, the Denham Springs High School Robotics Team is most deserving of
the highest recognition for its outstanding success and for earning the opportunity to
represent the state of Louisiana at the FIRST World Championships.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend the Denham Springs High School Robotics
Team on winning the FIRST Robotics Pikes Peak Regional Competition and does hereby
encourage the members of the team to continue their excellent work and always strive for
greatness.
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
