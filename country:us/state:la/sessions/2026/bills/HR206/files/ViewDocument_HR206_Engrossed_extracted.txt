HLS 26RS-454 ENGROSSED
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 206
BY REPRESENTATIVE DICKERSON
Louisiana Highway 16 widening project
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To authorize and direct the Department of Transportation and Development to complete the
3 Louisiana Highway 16 widening project from Louisiana Highway 1019 to Louisiana
4 Highway 1023.
5 WHEREAS, approximately twenty-five thousand drivers use Louisiana Highway 16
6 (LA-16) daily; and
7 WHEREAS, there a numerous businesses on Louisiana Highway 1019 (LA-1019),
8 LA-16, and Louisiana Highway 1023 (LA-1023), including but not limited to the Oak Point
9 Fresh Market, Livingston Parish Fire Protection District #4 Station, restaurants, and clothing
10 stores; and
11 WHEREAS, LA-1019 to LA-1023 is only a two-lane road; and
12 WHEREAS, according to the World Population Review, Livingston Parish's
13 population has increased five and one half percent since 2020; and
14 WHEREAS, according to the office of operations, within the Federal Highway
15 Administration, lack of physical capacity contributes to congestion, thus, widening highways
16 is an important strategy and is critical for alleviating congestion; and
17 WHEREAS, widening highways improves commuter satisfaction by reducing
18 congestion and decreasing driving times; and
19 WHEREAS, there is already capital outlay funding from the 2024 Regular Session
20 dedicated to widening LA-16, but the project has yet to begin.
Page 1 of 2
HLS 26RS-454 ENGROSSED
HR NO. 206
1 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
2 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby authorize and direct the Department of Transportation
3 and Development to widen LA-16 from two lanes to four lanes from LA-1019 to LA-1023.
4 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
5 secretary of the Department of Transportation and Development.
DIGEST
The digest printed below was prepared by House Legislative Services. It constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument. The keyword, one-liner, abstract, and digest do not constitute
part of the law or proof or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
HR 206 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Dickerson
Authorizes and directs the Dept. of Transportation and Development to begin the widening
project on LA-16, making it four lanes from LA-1019 to LA-1023.
