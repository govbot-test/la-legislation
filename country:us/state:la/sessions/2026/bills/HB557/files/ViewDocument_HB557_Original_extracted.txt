Section 1:
HLS 26RS-636 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE DEWITT
INSURANCE/HEALTH: Provides relative to long-term care pharmacy
1 AN ACT
2 To enact Part VIII of Chapter 14 of Title 37 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950, to
3 be comprised of R.S. 37:1259, relative long-term care; to provide for a definition;
4 to define long-term care pharmacy; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. Part VIII of Chapter 14 of Title 37 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of
7 1950, comprised of R.S. 37:1259, is hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 Part VIII. LONG-TERM CARE PHARMACY
9 §1259. Long-term care pharmacy
10 For purposes of this Part, "Long-term care pharmacy" means a pharmacy that
11 serves residents of a long-term care facility, as defined in the Older Americans Act,
12 42 U.S.C. 3002(35), and has a national provider identifier associated with taxonomy
13 code 3336L0003X, or a successor code, as maintained by the National Uniform
14 Claim Committee.
DIGEST

of the legislative instrument. The keyword, one-liner, abstract, and digest do not constitute
part of the law or proof or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
