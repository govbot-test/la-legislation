8 accounts; to provide for the dedication of certain monies to the Louisiana Early
9 Childhood Education Fund; to provide for use of monies in the Local Revenue Fund;
10 to provide for use of monies in the Hurricane Ida Recovery Fund; to provide for use
11 of monies in the Louisiana Transportation Infrastructure Fund; to provide for the use
12 of monies in the Higher Education Campus Revitalization Fund; to provide for the
13 use of monies in the Louisiana Economic Development Initiatives Fund; to establish
14 the Criminal Justice Technology Innovation Fund; to establish the Strategic
15 Investments Across Louisiana Fund; to restrict payments of certain monies in the
16 Self-Insurance Fund; to provide for the use of monies in the Regional Maintenance
17 and Improvement Fund; to provide for approvals for the use of certain monies in the
18 Major Events Incentive Fund; to provide for the investment and use of monies in the
19 Site Investment and Infrastructure Improvement Fund; to authorize appropriation
20 from the Revenue Stabilization Trust Fund; to modify threshold amounts and
21 percentages allowable for appropriations from the Revenue Stabilization Trust Fund;
22 to repeal certain treasury funds; to provide an effective date; and to provide for
23 related matters.

15 §100.257. Criminal Justice Technology Innovation Fund
16 There is hereby established in the state treasury, as a special fund, the
17 Criminal Justice Technology Innovation Fund, hereinafter referred to in this Section
18 as the "fund". Any money transferred or appropriated to the fund by the legislature
19 shall be deposited into the fund after compliance with the requirements of Article
20 VII, Section 9(B) of the Constitution of Louisiana relative to the Bond Security and
21 Redemption Fund. All unexpended and unencumbered monies in the fund at the end
22 of the fiscal year shall remain in the fund. The monies in the fund shall be invested
23 by the state treasurer in the same manner as monies in the state general fund, and
24 interest earned on the investment of monies in the fund shall be credited to the fund.
25 Notwithstanding the provisions of Chapter 10 of Title 38 and Part V-A of Chapter
26 1 and Chapter 17 of Title 39 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950, monies in
27 the fund shall be used by offices of the district attorney to leverage technology to
28 collaborate with other entities and facilitate interagency communication regarding
29 care and protection of youth.
