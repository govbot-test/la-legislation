6 On page 1, between lines 17 and 18, insert the following:
7 "(19) "Family support organization" means an organization that provides parent
8 support and training, youth support and training, advocacy, and peer-based services to family
9 members of children and youth with emotional or behavioral health challenges enrolled in
10 the Coordinated System of Care.
11 (20) "Statewide management organization" means an entity contracted by the
12 Louisiana Department of Health to administer the statewide Coordinated System of Care
13 program, including management of care coordination, provider networks, family and youth
14 support services, quality oversight, and system administration."
15 AMENDMENT NO. 4
16 On page 1, between lines 18 and 19, insert the following:
17 "§460.54. Medicaid policies and procedures; procedure for adoption; required
18 content
19 * * *
20 H. The family support organization providing services pursuant to the Coordinated
21 System of Care on January 1, 2026, shall continue operating and providing services without
22 interruption until the Louisiana Department of Health completes a competitive procurement
23 process and selects and contracts with a new statewide management organization.
24 * * *"
