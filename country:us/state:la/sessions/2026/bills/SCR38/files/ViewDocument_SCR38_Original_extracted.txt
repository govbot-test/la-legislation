2 To repeal Joint Rule No. 7 of the Joint Rules of Order of the Senate and House of
3 Representatives that established the Atchafalaya Basin Program Oversight

DIGEST
SCR 38 Original 2026 Regular Session Kleinpeter
Present Joint Rule establishes a special joint committee, the Atchafalaya Basin Program
Oversight Committee to monitor, review, and make recommendations relative to all aspects
of the Atchafalaya Basin Program in the Dept. of Natural Resources which was established
in 1998 pursuant to prior law.
Present Joint Rule provides that the committee is composed of all members of the House of
Representatives and of the Senate who represent in whole or in part the parishes of
Assumption, Avoyelles, Iberia, Iberville, Pointe Coupee, St. Landry, St. Martin, and St.
