2 On page 1, line 2, after "to" and before "relative to" insert the following:
3 "amend and reenact R.S. 44:11.2(C), (E)(1)(introductory paragraph),
4 (H)(introductory paragraph) and (3), to enact R.S. 44:11.2(A)(10) and (11),
5 and to repeal R.S. 44:11.2(G) and (I),"
6
7 AMENDMENT NO. 2
8 On page 1, line 4, after "judges;" insert "to provide relative to publishing of certain
9 information;"
10 AMENDMENT NO. 3
11 On page 1, line 7, after "Section 1." delete the remainder of the line and insert:
12 "R.S. 44:11.2(C), (E)(1)(introductory paragraph), (H)(introductory paragraph)
13 and (3) are hereby amended and reenacted and R.S. 44:11.2(A)(10) and (11) are
14 hereby enacted to read as follows:"
15 AMENDMENT NO. 4
16 On page 1, below line 13, add the following:
17 "C. As used in this Section, the term "publish" shall mean to publicly post or
18 publicly display on the internet personal information of a protected individual who
19 submits a request pursuant to Subsection E of this Section without any legitimate
20 political, economic, or societal interest, or with the intent to publicly intimidate or
21 harass the protected individual.
22 * * *
23 E.(1) A protected individual, or the judicial administrator's office on behalf of
24 a protected individual identified in Paragraphs (A)(1) through (5) of this Section or
25 the Louisiana District Attorneys Association on behalf of a protected individual
26 identified in Paragraph (A)(6) of this Section, may request that a public body or third
27 party:
28 * * *
29 H. A protected individual, the judicial administrator's office, or the Louisiana
30 District Attorneys Association may bring an action for mandamus due to a violation
31 of this Section against a public body or third party for:
32 * * *
33 (3)(a) Reasonable attorney fees.
34 (b) For a third party, an action for a violation of this Section may also be
35 brought for damages incurred as a result of a violation of this Section.
36 Section 2. R.S. 44:11.2(G) and (I) are hereby repealed."
