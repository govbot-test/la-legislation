1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend the Louisiana Tech University Lady Techsters on winning the 2025-2026
3 Conference USA (CUSA) Women's Basketball regular season championship.
4 WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize the Louisiana Tech Lady Techsters
5 basketball team for their outstanding achievements and to commend the team on winning
6 the 2025-2026 CUSA Women's Basketball regular season championship; and
7 WHEREAS, the Lady Techsters secured the 2025-2026 CUSA Women's Basketball
8 regular season title by finishing with an impressive record of seventeen wins and only one
9 loss in regular season conference games; and
10 WHEREAS, the Lady Techsters have thirty-eight women's basketball regular season
11 conference titles in school history; and
12 WHEREAS, the Lady Techsters finished conference play with a perfect road record
13 of eight wins and zero losses; and
14 WHEREAS, the Lady Techsters carried a nineteen-game winning streak into the
15 2026 Air National Guard CUSA Women's Championship game; the streak was the fourth-
16 longest winning streak in the country at the time; and
17 WHEREAS, four Louisiana Tech players averaged double figures in scoring; Paris
18 Bradley averaged 14.8 points per game, Jianna Morris averaged 11.1 points, Jordan Marshall
19 averaged 10.7 points, and Alexia Weaver averaged 10.5 points; and

1 WHEREAS, the Lady Techsters were led by an exceptional coaching staff, including
2 Head Coach Brooke Stoehr, Assistant Coach Scott Stoehr, Assistant Coach Josh Ashley,
3 Assistant Coach Olivia Grayson, and Assistant Coach Caleb Livingston; Mikayla Baillie
4 served as the team's director of basketball operations; and
5 WHEREAS, the Louisiana Tech University Lady Techsters are most deserving of
6 the highest recognition for their outstanding achievements during the 2025-2026 season.
7 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
8 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend the Louisiana Tech University Lady
9 Techsters on winning the 2025-2026 Conference USA Women's Basketball regular season
10 championship and does hereby encourage the coaches and players to continue their excellent
11 work and always strive for greatness in competition.
