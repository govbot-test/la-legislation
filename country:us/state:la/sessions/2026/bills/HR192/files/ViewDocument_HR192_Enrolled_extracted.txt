HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 192
BY REPRESENTATIVE SPELL
A RESOLUTION
To commend the American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists on the occasion of
its seventy-fifth anniversary.
WHEREAS, the American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists (ACOG) was
founded in 1951, and for seventy-five years, has advanced the health and well-being of
patients through excellence in clinical practice, education, advocacy, and research; and
WHEREAS, ACOG represents more than sixty-two thousand health care
professionals nationwide, including more than seven hundred members in Louisiana who
provide essential obstetric and gynecologic care to patients across the state; and
WHEREAS, ACOG and its members in Louisiana have championed initiatives to
improve maternal health, protect the patient-physician relationship, and ensure access to
health care for all individuals; and
WHEREAS, ACOG works to create a stronger, more sustainable practice
environment for obstetrician-gynecologists and their patients; and
WHEREAS, the seventy-fifth anniversary of ACOG offers an opportunity to
recognize its historic contributions and reaffirm its commitment to advancing the health of
patients in Louisiana.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend the American College of Obstetricians and
Gynecologists on the occasion of its seventy-fifth anniversary; does hereby recognize the
dedication of its members to improving the health of patients; and does hereby express
appreciation for the service and leadership the college provides.
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
