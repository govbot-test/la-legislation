12 "Section 2. R.S. 40:2405(B) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
13 §2405. Peace officer training requirements; reimbursement by peace officer
14 * * *
15 B.(1) Additionally, the receipt of supplemental pay from the municipal police
16 officers fund, the deputy sheriffs' fund, the state police fund, or out of the funds appropriated
17 for military police by the Military Department, state of Louisiana, shall be contingent upon
18 the recipient's beginning employment as a peace officer after January 1, 1986, successfully
19 completing a council-approved training program, and passing the council-approved
20 examination within one calendar year from date of initial employment.
21 (2) Certified security personnel of the Supreme Court of Louisiana or of any court
22 of appeal of the state shall not be eligible to receive supplemental pay benefits even though
23 the peace officer has successfully completed a council-approved training program. The
24 commission issued to court security personnel shall remain in force and in effect at the
25 pleasure of the employing court.
26 * * *"
