HB 653 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Bayham
Abstract: Provides that one of the two members appointed to the Oyster Task Force by the
United Commercial Fisherman's Association be a resident of St. Bernard Parish and
that the task force make recommendations to the Dept. of Agriculture and Forestry.
Present law provides for the establishment of the Oyster Task Force and provides for
membership appointments.
Proposed law provides that one of the two existing appointments made by the United
Commercial Fisherman's Association be a resident of St. Bernard Parish.
Present law provides the task force with responsibility to make recommendations relative
to the oyster industry to state agencies with responsibilities related to the oyster industry.
Proposed law adds the Dept. of Agriculture and Forestry to the list of agencies to whom the
task force makes recommendations.
(Amends R.S. 56:421(B)(10) and (E)(4))
Summary of Amendments Adopted by House
The Committee Amendments Proposed by House Committee on Natural Resources and
Environment to the original bill:

Section 2:
1. Remove the provision for additional appointments made by the Dept. of
Agriculture and Forestry.

Section 3:
2. Add a provision that one of the two existing appointments made by the United
Commercial Fisherman's Association be a resident of St. Bernard Parish.

Section 4:
3. Make technical changes.
