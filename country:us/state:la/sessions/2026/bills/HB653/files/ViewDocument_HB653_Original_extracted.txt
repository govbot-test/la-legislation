HB 653 Original 2026 Regular Session Bayham
Abstract: Adds two members to the Oyster Task Force appointed by the commissioner of
Dept. of Agriculture and Forestry.
Present law provides for the establishment of the Oyster Task Force and provides for
membership appointments.
Proposed law provides for two additional members to be appointed by the commissioner of
the Dept. of Agriculture and Forestry. Specifies that the additional members are nonvoting
members. Adds the Dept. of Agriculture and Forestry as an entity that receives
recommendations from the task force pertaining to the oyster industry and production.
(Amends R.S. 56:421(B)(5)-(15), (C), and (E)(4); Adds R.S. 56:421(B)(16))
