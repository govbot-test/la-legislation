12 "§2401.5. Pursuit Safety and Officer Technology Fund
13 A. There is hereby established in the state treasury, as a special fund, the
14 Pursuit Safety and Officer Technology Fund, hereinafter referred to in this Section
15 as the "fund".
16 B. After allocation of monies to the Bond Security and Redemption Fund as
17 provided in Article VII, Section (9)(B) of the Constitution of Louisiana, the treasurer
18 shall deposit into the fund monies transferred, appropriated, or dedicated to the fund.
19 Monies in the fund shall be appropriated by the legislature to the Louisiana
20 Commission on Law Enforcement and the Administration of Criminal Justice and
21 shall be used solely for the following purposes:
22 (1) Provide grants to state, parish, and municipal law enforcement agencies
23 for the acquisition of pursuit intervention technology, including but not limited to
24 GPS tracking projectiles, remote-deployment tire deflation devices, and
25 vehicle-tethering systems.
26 (2) Provide grants to state, parish, and municipal law enforcement agencies
27 for the development and implementation of training programs for the safe
28 deployment of pursuit intervention devices and technology.
29 (3) Administrative costs of the Louisiana Commission on Law Enforcement
30 and the Administration of Criminal Justice associated with the grant program, not to
31 exceed five percent of the annual fund balance.
32 C. Monies in the fund shall be invested in the same manner as monies in the
33 state general fund. Interest earned on investment of monies in the fund shall be
34 credited to the fund. Unexpended and unencumbered monies in the fund at the end
35 of the fiscal year shall remain in the fund."
