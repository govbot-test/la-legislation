5 On page 1, delete lines 4 and 5 in their entirety and insert the following:
6 "law enforcement agency responding to a hit-and-run offense to use a certain system

8 AMENDMENT NO. 3
9 On page 1, line 7, after "Section 1." and before "hereby" delete "R.S. 14:100(D) is" and

12 On page 1, between lines 9 and 10, insert the following:
13 "B. For the purpose of this Section:
14 * * *
15 (2) "Louisiana Law Enforcement Telecommunications System (LLETS)"
16 means a system that is established and overseen by the Department of Public Safety
17 and Corrections, office of state police, and provides for statewide dissemination of
18 system-generated notifications to all Louisiana law enforcement agencies.
19 * * *"
20 AMENDMENT NO. 5
21 On page 1, delete lines 14 through 16 in their entirety and insert the following:
22 "(2) Law enforcement agencies responsible for investigating hit-and-run
23 driving offenses shall adopt protocols and procedures to rapidly notify other law
24 enforcement agencies of all of the following:"
25 AMENDMENT NO. 6
26 On page 1, line 18, delete "Whether" and insert "If known, whether"
27 AMENDMENT NO. 7
28 On page on page 2, after line 2, insert the following:
29 "(3) The law enforcement agency investigating a hit-and-run driving offense
30 shall utilize the Louisiana Law Enforcement Telecommunications System (LLETS)
31 to disseminate the information required by Paragraph (2) of this Subsection."
