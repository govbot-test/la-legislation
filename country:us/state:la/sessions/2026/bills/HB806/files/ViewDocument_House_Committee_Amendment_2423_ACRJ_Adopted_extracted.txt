15 * * *

20 * * *"

22 On page 1, delete lines 14 through 16 in their entirety and insert the following:

27 On page 1, line 18, delete "Whether" and insert "If known, whether"
28 AMENDMENT NO. 7
29 On page 2, after line 2, insert the following:
30 "(3) The law enforcement agency investigating a hit-and-run driving offense
31 shall utilize the Louisiana Law Enforcement Telecommunications System (LLETS)
32 to disseminate the information required by Paragraph (2) of this Subsection.
33 Section 2. The Louisiana State Law Institute is authorized and directed to
34 alphabetize and renumber the definitions contained in R.S. 100(B) and to correct any cross
35 references to the renumbered paragraphs if necessary."
