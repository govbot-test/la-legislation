Section 1:
HLS 26RS-200 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE WYBLE
AGRICULTURE/MEAT: Prohibits the manufacturing, sale, and distribution of cell
cultured food products
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 3:4233(A)(4) through (14) and to enact R.S. 3:4201(24), 4210.1,
3 and 4233(A)(15), relative to cell cultured food products; to provide for definitions;
4 to prohibit the sale, manufacturing, and distribution of cell cultured food products;
5 to direct the Louisiana State Law Institute to change statutory references and
6 renumber certain definitions; and to provide for related matters.
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
8 Section 1. R.S. 3:4233(A)(4) through (14) are hereby amended and reenacted and
9 R.S. 3:4201(24), 4210.1, and 4233(A)(15) are hereby enacted to read as follows:
10 §4201. Definitions
11 As used in this Chapter, except as otherwise specified, the following terms
12 shall have the meanings stated below:
13 * * *
14 (24) The term "cell cultured food product" means any cultured animal tissue
15 produced from in vitro animal cell cultures outside of the organism from which it
16 derived.
17 * * *

HB 859 Original 2026 Regular Session Wyble
Abstract: Prohibits the sale, manufacturing, or distribution of cell cultured food products.
Proposed law defines "cell cultured food product" as any cultured animal tissue produced
from in vitro animal cell cultures outside of the organism from which it derived.
Proposed law specifies that no person, firm, or corporation can manufacture for sale, sell,
hold, or offer, or distribute any cell cultured food product.
Present law (R.S. 3:4233(A)) specifies certain prohibitions under present law.
Proposed law expands present law by prohibiting the sale, manufacturing, or distribution of
any cell cultured food product.
(Amends R.S. 3:4233(A)(4) through (14); Adds R.S. 3:4201(24), 4210.1, and 4233(A)(15))
