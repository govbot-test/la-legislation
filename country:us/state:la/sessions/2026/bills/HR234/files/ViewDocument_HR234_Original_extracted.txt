HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 234
BY REPRESENTATIVE MOORE
of Dr. Johnny Earl Drumgole, Sr.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To express the condolences of the House of Representatives upon the death of Dr. Johnny
3 Earl Drumgole, Sr.
4 WHEREAS, it is with immense sadness and profound regret that the House of
5 Representatives of the Legislature of Louisiana has learned of the passing, on April 27,
6 2026, of Dr. Johnny Earl Drumgole, Sr.; and
7 WHEREAS, Dr. Drumgole was born and raised in Monroe, Louisiana, and was a
8 1969 graduate of Carroll High School, where he starred as a running back on the football
9 team; he maintained a strong connection with the city throughout his life; and
10 WHEREAS, Dr. Drumgole and his wife, Dr. Pamela Cage-Drumgole, began hosting
11 a Bible study in their home in 1979 and founded Liberty Christian Center on August 8, 1982;
12 and
13 WHEREAS, Dr. Drumgole fully transitioned into ministry in 1982, after a
14 distinguished career at General Motors; under his stewardship, Liberty Christian Center
15 enjoyed growth and stability and expanded its facilities; and
16 WHEREAS, outside of ministry, Dr. Drumgole was involved in local education and
17 social services; he founded Excelsior Christian School and advocated for youth mentorship
18 to challenge young men to pursue higher education and personal excellence; he held
19 leadership roles with the Northeast Louisiana Food Bank, Habitat for Humanity, and
20 numerous regional ministerial alliances; and
Page 1 of 2
HLS 26RS-4844 ORIGINAL
HR NO. 234
1 WHEREAS, Dr. Johnny Earl Drumgole, Sr., is survived by his wife, Dr. Pamela
2 Cage-Drumgole; his children, Daphne Drumgole, Jasmine Drumgole, and Johnny Drumgole,
3 Jr.; and his grandchildren; he will be deeply missed, but the legacy of his ministerial and
4 community service shall live on in the hearts and minds of those who were privileged to
5 have known and loved him.
6 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
7 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby express heartfelt condolences on the death of
8 Dr. Johnny Earl Drumgole, Sr., and does hereby extend sincere wishes that his family,
9 friends, and loved ones find comfort in their shared memories of his beautiful life.
