17 §21. State psychiatric hospitals
18 A. For purposes of this Part, "state psychiatric hospital" refers to the hospital
19 at Jackson, known as the Eastern Louisiana Mental Health System, and the hospital
20 at Pineville, known as the Central Louisiana State Hospital, which are designated as
21 the hospitals for persons who have a mental illness or a substance-related or
22 addictive disorder until such time as separate or other hospitals are established. The
23 assistant secretary of the office of behavioral health of office of state health care
24 facilities within the department may reorganize and consolidate the administration
25 of the hospitals or facilities.

10 C.(1) The Louisiana Department of Health shall be composed of the
11 executive office of the secretary, the office of the surgeon general, the office of
12 management and finance, the office of public health, the office of behavioral health,
13 the office for citizens with developmental disabilities, the office of aging and adult
14 services, the office on women's health and community health, the office of state
15 health care facilities, and such other offices as shall be created by law.
16 * * *
17 §253. Secretary of health and hospitals the Louisiana Department of Health
18 There shall be a secretary of health and hospitals the Louisiana Department
19 of Health, who shall be appointed by the governor with consent of the Senate and
20 who shall serve at the pleasure of the governor at a salary fixed by the governor,
21 which salary shall not exceed the amount approved for such position by the
22 legislature while in session. The secretary shall serve as the executive head and chief
23 administrative officer of the Louisiana Department of Health and shall have the
24 responsibility for the policies of the department, except as otherwise provided by this
25 Title, and for the administration, control, and operation of the functions, programs,
26 and affairs of the department; provided that the secretary shall perform his functions
27 under the general control and supervision of the governor.
28 §253.1. Executive office of the secretary; duties and functions
29 A. The secretary shall directly supervise, direct, and control the executive
30 office of the secretary and the office of state health care facilities.

24 while in session.
