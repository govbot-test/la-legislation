HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 77
BY REPRESENTATIVE BUTLER
hundredth birthday
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Rosa Mae Briley Pitre on the occasion of her one hundredth birthday.
3 WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize Rosa Pitre for her incredible longevity and
4 to commend her on the occasion of her one hundredth birthday; and
5 WHEREAS, Rosa was born on June 9, 1926, in Grand Prairie, Louisiana, to Lee and
6 Clemence Briley; her first husband, Calvin Fontenot, passed away at an early age, and she
7 later married the late Harry Pitre; and
8 WHEREAS, Ms. Pitre is the mother of five children: David Paul Fontenot, Arlene
9 Pitre Coreil, Janice Pitre Colligan, Peggy Pitre Tate, and John Pitre; and
10 WHEREAS, Ms. Pitre was a devoted housewife and worked as a seamstress,
11 telephone operator, and department manager for West Bros.; and
12 WHEREAS, Ms. Pitre is a devout Catholic who prays the Rosary on a daily basis and
13 receives Holy Communion every week; she is a dedicated member of Sacred Heart Church
14 in Ville Platte, Louisiana, and currently resides at the Village de Memoire in Ville Platte;
15 and
16 WHEREAS, Rosa Mae Briley Pitre is an inspiring woman who is most deserving of
17 the highest recognition for reaching this incredible milestone in life.
18 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
19 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Rosa Mae Briley Pitre on the occasion of
20 her one hundredth birthday and does hereby extend sincere wishes that she remain
