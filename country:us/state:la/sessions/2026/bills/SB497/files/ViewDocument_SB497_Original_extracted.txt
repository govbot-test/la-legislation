SLS 26RS-1135 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY SENATOR JACKSON-ANDREWS
HEALTH DEPARTMENT. Provides relative to evacuation protocols for certain facilities.
(8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 40:2009.26, relative to evacuation protocols for certain facilities licensed by
3 the Louisiana Department of Health; to provide for communication regarding
4 protocols; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 40:2009.26 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
7 §2009.26. Evacuation protocols; licensed facilities
8 During an emergency, the Louisiana Department of Health shall notify
9 a facility licensed by the department of any evacuation or emergency
10 management response required for the facility. If the notification is made orally,
11 the department shall also provide the information to the facility by electronic
12 mail.

The original instrument and the following digest, which constitutes no part

DIGEST
SB 497 Original 2026 Regular Session Jackson-Andrews
Proposed law requires the La. Dept. of Health to notify a licensed facility of any evacuation
or emergency management response required for the facility.
Proposed law provides that if the notification is made orally, the department shall also
provide the information to the facility by electronic mail.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Adds R.S. 40:2009.26 )
