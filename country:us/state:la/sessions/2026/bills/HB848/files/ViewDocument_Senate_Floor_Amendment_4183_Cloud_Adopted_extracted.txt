15 vehicles sold by the dealer during normal business hours. These facilities shall not consist
16 solely of a location for the intake, storage, or transfer of vehicles to another location for
17 repair or servicing and shall not rely primarily on the shipment of vehicles outside of the
18 local service area for repair."
