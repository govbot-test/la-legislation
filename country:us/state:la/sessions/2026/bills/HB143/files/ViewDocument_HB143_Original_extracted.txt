Present law requires any individual subject to confinement in a state adult penal or
correctional institution to be committed to the Dept. of Public Safety and Corrections
(DPS&C). Additionally authorizes DPS&C to house these inmates at a parish jail or
institution in certain circumstances.
Present law requires DPS&C to pay a daily sum to the sheriff or governing authority that
runs the jail or institution for each of these state inmates. Further establishes the daily sum
as $26.39 for fiscal years after FY 2019-2020. Proposed law increases this daily sum rate
to $29.39 beginning FY 2027-2028. Otherwise retains present law.
Effective upon signature of governor or lapse of time for gubernatorial action.
(Amends R.S. 15:824(B)(1)(a))
