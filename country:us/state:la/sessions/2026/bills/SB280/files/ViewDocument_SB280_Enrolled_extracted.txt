Section 1:
2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
BY SENATOR ABRAHAM
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 37:2150.1(25) and (26) and 2156.5, relative to the state Licensing Board for
3 Contractors; to provide relative to water well drilling; to provide relative to
4 environmental wells or holes; to provide for licensure and registration requirements;
5 to provide for contractor classifications; to provide for terms and definitions; to
6 provide for an effective date; and to provide for related matters.
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
8 Section 1. R.S. 37:2150.1(25) and (26) and 2156.5 are hereby enacted to read as
9 follows:
10 §2150.1. Definitions
11 As used in this Chapter, the following terms have the following meanings:
12 * * *.
13 (25) "Environmental well or hole" is a specialized boring that includes
14 but is not limited to observation, monitor, piezometer, recovery, horizontal
15 boring, cathodic protection, or geotechnical boreholes for the purposes of
16 environmental assessment, sampling, or analyzation.
17 (26) "Water well drilling" means drilling for the purposes of extracting
18 ground water which includes but is not limited to the drilling, construction,
19 maintenance, repair, abandoning, plugging, or sealing water wells for potable
20 and nonpotable water.
21 * * *
22 §2156.5. Water well drilling
23 A. Any person who advertises, solicits, undertakes, attempts, offers, or
24 submits a price, bid, quote, or estimate to construct, supervise, direct, oversee,
25 or perform any construction, modification, repair, or maintenance of any
26 portion of any water well, or environmental well or hole for which the project
27 value is one dollar or more shall hold the appropriate license and classification.
Page 1 of 2

SB NO. 280 ENROLLED
1 B. A water well contractor and environmental well or hole contractor
2 shall pass the appropriate examination, be registered with the State Licensing
3 Board for Contractors, be in good standing with the Department of
4 Conservation and Energy, and meet all other licensure requirements of a
5 contractor pursuant to this Chapter.
6 C. A water well contractor and environmental well or hole contractor
7 shall complete five hours of continuing education annually from a provider
8 approved by the board and maintain well registration with the Department of
9 Conservation and Energy pursuant to R.S. 38:3097.3.
10 Section 2. This Act shall become effective on July 1, 2026.
PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
