CONTRACTORS. Provides relative to the authority of the State Licensing Board for
Contractors to issue certain licenses. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 37:2150.1(25) and 2156.5, relative to the State Licensing Board for
3 Contractors; to provide relative to well drilling; to provide for licensure and
4 registration requirements; to provide for contractor classifications; to provide for
5 terms and definitions; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 2150.1(25) and 2156.5 are hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §2150.1. Definitions
9 As used in this Chapter, the following terms have the following meanings:
10 * * *
11 (25) "Well drilling" means either:
12 Environmental drilling, which is the drilling, construction, maintenance,
13 repair, abandoning, plugging, and sealing of any well or hole drilled or
14 constructed to obtain a sample for environmental assessment, sampling, or
15 analyzation.
16 Water drilling, which is the drilling, construction, maintenance, repair,
17 abandoning, plugging, and sealing of water wells for potable and nonpotable

3 §2156.5. Wells; well drilling
4 A. Beginning July 1, 2026, any person who advertises, solicits,
5 undertakes, attempts, offers, or submits a price, bid, quote, or estimate to
6 construct, supervise, direct, oversee, or perform any construction, modification,
7 repair, or maintenance of any portion of any water well, environmental well, or
8 sampling well for which the project value is one dollar or more shall hold the
9 appropriate license and classification.
10 B. A well contractor shall pass the appropriate examination, be
11 registered and in good standing with the Department of Conservation and
12 Energy, and meet all other licensure requirements of a contractor pursuant to
13 this Chapter.
14 C. A well contractor shall complete five hours of continuing education
15 annually from a provider approved by the board and maintain well registration
16 with the Department of Conservation and Energy pursuant R.S. 38:3097.3.

or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
DIGEST
SB 280 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Abraham
Present law provides for the regulation and licensure of contractors.
Present law provides for definitions.
Proposed law retains present law and defines "well drilling" to include environmental
drilling and water drilling. Provides that "environmental drilling" means the drilling,
construction, maintenance, repair, abandoning, plugging, and sealing of wells or holes drilled
or constructed to obtain samples for environmental assessment or analysis. Provides that
"water drilling" means the drilling, construction, maintenance, repair, abandoning, plugging,
and sealing of water wells for potable and nonpotable water.
Proposed law requires that beginning July 1, 2026, the appropriate license classification be
held by any person who advertises, solicits, undertakes, attempts, offers, or submits a price,
bid, quote, or estimate to construct, supervise, direct, oversee, or perform any construction,
modification, repair, or maintenance of any portion of a water well, environmental well, or
sampling well.
