DIGEST
SB 141 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Morris
Present law establishes the Integrated Criminal Justice Information System Policy Board
(policy board) under the jurisdiction of the La. Commission on Law Enforcement and
Administration of Criminal Justice, office of the governor (commission).
Proposed law places the policy board under the jurisdiction of the La. Supreme Court.
Present law requires that the commission provide for clerical, professional, or technical staff
as the policy board may require. Authorizes the policy board, with the concurrence of the
chief executive officer of the agency involved, to require additional personnel or technical
assistance on a temporary basis from any unit of the executive branch of state government.
Proposed law provides that the present law functions of the commission relative to the policy
board be performed by the La. Supreme Court.
Effective upon signature of the governor or lapse of time for gubernatorial action.
(Amends R.S. 15:1228 and 1228.5(4))
