HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 237

WYBLE
A RESOLUTION
To memorialize the United States Congress to enact a provision in the National Defense
Authorization Act directing the Department of Defense to furnish official personnel
counts directly to the United States Census Bureau for military installations located
in the contiguous United States.
WHEREAS, Article I, Section 2 of the Constitution of the United States requires an
enumeration of the population of the United States every ten years; and
WHEREAS, accurate census counts are essential for fair apportionment of
congressional seats, proper allocation of federal funding, and accurate representation of
communities across the state; and
WHEREAS, Louisiana is home to three major military installations: Fort Polk,
Barksdale Air Force Base, and Naval Air Station Joint Reserve Base New Orleans, that
together house tens of thousands of active-duty military personnel, civilian employees, and
dependent family members; and
WHEREAS, historical census counts conducted by door-to-door enumeration on
these secure military installations have resulted in significant undercounts; and
WHEREAS, the 2020 Census reported seven thousand nine hundred fifty personnel,
spouses, dependents, and contractors residing on Fort Polk when the actual population
exceeded thirty-eight thousand; and
WHEREAS, the Department of Defense and each installation's garrison personnel
offices maintain precise, daily counts of all soldiers, airmen, sailors, marines, civilian
employees, and dependent family members physically residing on base; and
WHEREAS, requiring the Department of Defense to provide these verified numbers
directly to the United States Census Bureau would eliminate the need for door-to-door
census workers on military installations, improve accuracy, create administrative

efficiencies, reduce costs, and allow census resources to be redirected to more complex
enumeration challenges elsewhere.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby memorialize the United States Congress to include in
the National Defense Authorization Act a provision directing the Department of Defense to
furnish to the United States Census Bureau the exact number of military personnel, civilian
employees, and dependent family members residing on each military installation in the
contiguous United States during each decennial census.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
Speaker of the United States House of Representatives, the President of the United States
Senate, each member of the Louisiana congressional delegation, and the Secretary of
Defense.
