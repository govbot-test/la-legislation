HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 407
BY REPRESENTATIVE WALTERS
CONDOLENCES: Expresses the condolences of the House of Representatives on the death
of Spencer Leak, Jr.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To express the condolences of the House of Representatives on the death of Spencer Leak,
3 Jr., vice president of Leak & Sons Funeral Home.
4 WHEREAS, it is with immense sadness and profound regret that the House of
5 Representatives of the Legislature of Louisiana has learned of the passing of Spencer Leak,
6 Jr.; and
7 WHEREAS, Spencer Leak, Jr., was the vice president of Leak & Sons Funeral
8 Homes; he helped lead the business for decades while upholding his family's legacy of
9 service; and
10 WHEREAS, Mr. Leak recently led and planned the funeral of Reverend Jesse
11 Jackson, Jr.; his family has deep ties to Chicago's civil rights movement, and his grandfather
12 led the march to desegregate Chicago's Oakwood Cemetery; and
13 WHEREAS, Mr. Leak was a tireless advocate for families who could not afford a
14 burial, a champion of Chicago businesses, and a generous mentor to entrepreneurs
15 throughout the city; and
16 WHEREAS, Spencer Leak, Jr., was a respected leader, devoted public servant, and
17 pillar of the Black community in Chicago; he will be deeply missed, but his legacy shall live
18 on in the hearts and minds of those who were privileged to have known and loved him.
19 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
20 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby express heartfelt condolences on the death of Spencer
