HB 855 Original 2026 Regular Session Orgeron
Abstract: Limits use of purse seines to water of a certain depth.
Present law provides for use of seines or nets in saltwater areas.
Proposed law limits the use of purse seines to saltwater areas that are at least 22 ft. deep.
(Adds R.S. 56:322(C)(6)(d))
