4 AMENDMENT NO. 2
5 On page 2, delete lines 8 through 10 and insert in lieu thereof the following:
6 "C.(1) A retail business that violates the provisions of this Section shall
7 be liable to the cardholder for actual damages incurred as a result of the
8 violation.
9 (2) Prior to filing a civil action, a cardholder shall provide written notice
10 of the alleged violation to the retail business. If the retail business cures the
11 violation and reimburses the cardholder for any surcharge assessed within
12 thirty days after receipt of written notice, no private right of action shall arise
13 from the alleged violation.
14 (3) A private right of action pursuant to this Section shall apply only to
15 willful violations, repeated violations, or violations that are not cured within the
16 time period provided in Paragraph (2) of this Subsection.
17 (4) Nothing in this Section shall limit the authority of the attorney
18 general to enforce the provisions of this Chapter pursuant to R.S. 51:3082."
