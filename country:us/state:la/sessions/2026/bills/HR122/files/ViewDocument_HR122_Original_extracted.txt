HLS 26RS-3254 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 122
BY REPRESENTATIVE NEWELL
Teen USA
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Heaven Riley Breaux for being named Miss Louisiana Teen USA.
3 WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize Heaven Riley Breaux on her outstanding
4 achievements and to commend her for being named Miss Louisiana Teen USA; and
5 WHEREAS, Heaven is an eleventh-grade student and a varsity cheerleader at
6 Lafayette Renaissance Charter Academy; she is the daughter of Riley and Yolanda Breaux
7 of Lafayette, Louisiana, and the youngest sibling of Justyn and Jasen Breaux; and
8 WHEREAS, Heaven, whose life is deeply rooted in faith and family, serves as a
9 junior usher under the leadership of Reverend Chad Ned at Mt. Pilgrim Baptist Church in
10 Opelousas, Louisiana; and
11 WHEREAS, Heaven found inspiration in the world of pageantry at early age,
12 watching pageants alongside her mother; after witnessing Miss South Africa's crowning as
13 Miss Universe in 2019, she decided to step onto the stage and transform her admiration into
14 ambition; and
15 WHEREAS, Heaven's journey in pageants is defined by growth, discipline, and
16 purpose; she has earned numerous titles over the years, including Our Little Miss State
17 Preteen Queen, Miss Lafayette Louisiana Preteen USA, Miss Pelican State Preteen, Miss
18 Opelousas Teen USA, and Miss Greater Baton Rouge Teen USA; and
19 WHEREAS, Heaven was most recently crowned Miss Louisiana Teen USA, and
20 each stage of her journey has strengthened her confidence and commitment to using her
21 platform to make a positive impact on the community; and
Page 1 of 2

1 WHEREAS, Heaven is the founder of Bows of Confidence, which reflects her
2 passion for empowering young girls to embrace their individuality, build self worth, and lead
3 with confidence; she is active in mentorship and community initiatives, through which she
4 strives to create spaces where every girl feels seen, valued, and empowered to shine; and
5 WHEREAS, Heaven displays incredible humility and credits her successes to her
6 unwavering faith in God and the support she receives from her family, friends, coaches, and
7 mentors; and
8 WHEREAS, Heaven plans to attend Mississippi State University to study
9 broadcasting on a full four-year scholarship, and she will continue to use her voice to inspire
10 others and make a lasting impact; and
11 WHEREAS, Heaven Riley Breaux leads with grace, purpose, and a heart for service,
12 and she is most deserving of the highest recognition for her incredible achievements.
13 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
14 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Heaven Riley Breaux on being named Miss
15 Louisiana Teen USA and does hereby extend sincere wishes that she remain prosperous in
16 all of her endeavors.
