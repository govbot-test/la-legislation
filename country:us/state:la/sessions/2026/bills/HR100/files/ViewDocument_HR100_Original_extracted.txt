HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 100
BY REPRESENTATIVE ADAMS
of Betty Davis King
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To express the condolences of the House of Representatives on the death of Betty Davis
3 King.
4 WHEREAS, it is with immense sadness and profound regret that the House of
5 Representatives of the Legislature of Louisiana has learned of the passing, on March 19,
6 2026, of Betty Davis King; and
7 WHEREAS, Betty was born to loving parents and was a cherished wife, mother,
8 grandmother, great-grandmother, and friend whose devotion to family and community
9 earned her the admiration and respect of all who knew her; and
10 WHEREAS, Ms. King spent most of her life in her beloved community of Jackson,
11 Louisiana, where she was deeply involved in community service, engaged in many of the
12 ministries of McKowen Baptist Church and Visions Church, and devoted to the East
13 Feliciana School System; and
14 WHEREAS, Betty Davis King was exceptional, capable, conscientious, and
15 compassionate, and all of her actions were guided by three great loves: her relationship with
16 Jesus Christ as her Lord and Savior, whom she loved intentionally and unconditionally; her
17 deep devotion to her family; and her abiding love for her hometown of Minden, Louisiana;
18 and
19 WHEREAS, Betty Davis King leaves to cherish her memory her husband, Charles
20 Earl King; son, Adrian; daughters, Towanda Taylor, Keashu King, and Ashley Armstrong;
21 grandsons, Charleston Taylor and John Charles King; granddaughters, Peyton King,
Page 1 of 2
HLS 26RS-2963 ORIGINAL
HR NO. 100
1 Adrienne King, Ashlynn Taylor, and Jacelyn Armstrong; great-granddaughter, Chloe Taylor;
2 and a host of sisters, brothers, sisters-in-law, and brothers-in-law; four generations of nieces
3 and nephews; and extended relatives and friends; her passing leaves a void in the lives of her
4 family and friends in the community she served so well; she will be deeply missed, but the
5 legacy of her loving spirit shall live on in the hearts and minds of those who were privileged
6 to have known and loved her.
7 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
8 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby express heartfelt condolences on the death of Betty
9 Davis King and does hereby extend sincere wishes that her family, friends, and loved ones
10 find comfort in their shared memories of her beautiful life.
