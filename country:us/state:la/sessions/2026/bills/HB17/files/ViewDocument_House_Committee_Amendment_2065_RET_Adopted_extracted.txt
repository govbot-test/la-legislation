HCAHB17 4267 2065
HOUSE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
by Representative Illg
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, line 2, after "reenact" delete the remainder of the line and delete line 3 in its
3 entirety and insert the following:
4 "R.S. 11:1581(11), 1631(F)(1) and (2)(introductory paragraph) and to enact R.S.
5 11:1631(F)(2)(d), relative to the District Attorneys' Retirement System;"
6 AMENDMENT NO. 2
7 On page 1, line 4, after "retirees;" insert "to require certain information to be reported to the
8 retirement system; to provide for definitions;"
9 AMENDMENT NO. 3
10 On page 1, line 10, after "Section 1." delete the remainder of the line and delete line 11 in
11 its entirety and insert the following:
12 "R.S. 11:1581(11), 1631(F)(1) and (2)(introductory paragraph) are hereby amended
13 and reenacted and R.S. 11:1631(F)(2)(d) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
14 §1581. Definitions
15 The following words and phrases, as used in this Chapter, unless a different
16 meaning is plainly required by the context, shall have the following meanings:
17 * * *
18 (11) "Employer" shall mean any parish in the state of Louisiana; the state of
19 Louisiana, or the police jury or any other governing body of a parish or political
20 corporation or subdivision of the state of Louisiana which employs and pays persons
21 as district attorneys or assistant district attorneys. "Employer" shall also mean this
22 retirement system and the Louisiana District Attorneys' Association.
23 * * *"
24 AMENDMENT NO. 4
25 On page 2, delete lines 23 and 24 in their entirety and insert the following:
26 "(d)(i) Beginning January 31, 2027, through June 30, 2031, each employer
27 shall submit an annual report to the retirement system concerning retirees employed
28 under the provisions of this Subsection for the previous calendar year.
29 (ii) The report shall be submitted to the retirement system no later than
30 March first.
31 (iii) The report shall include each employee's name, date of retirement, date
32 of reemployment, annual earnings paid by local funds, and annual earnings paid by
33 state funds. The retirement system may adopt a uniform template for the report."
