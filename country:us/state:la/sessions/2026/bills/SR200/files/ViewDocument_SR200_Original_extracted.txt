1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend and congratulate the Zachary High School boys basketball team on winning
3 the Division I Non-Select state championship and on earning statewide honors
4 following its championship season.
5 WHEREAS, the Louisiana Senate desires to recognize and commend the
6 student-athletes and coaches whose dedication, discipline, and perseverance bring distinction
7 to their school, the city of Zachary, and the state of Louisiana; and
8 WHEREAS, the Zachary High School Broncos completed an outstanding 2025-2026
9 basketball season by capturing the Louisiana High School Athletic Association Division I
10 Non-Select state championship; and
11 WHEREAS, the Broncos secured their second consecutive state championship with
12 a hard-fought 63-60 victory over Ruston High School in the championship game played on
13 March 14, 2026, at Burton Coliseum in Lake Charles; and
14 WHEREAS, under the leadership of Head Coach Jon McClinton, the Broncos
15 compiled an exceptional 34-3 record during the 2025-2026 season; and
16 WHEREAS, Coach McClinton was named the Louisiana High School Athletic
17 Association Class 5A Coach of the Year in recognition of his leadership and the continued
18 excellence of the Zachary High School basketball program; and

1 WHEREAS, the championship marked the fourth state title won under Coach
2 McClinton's leadership in six seasons and the second consecutive championship for the
3 Broncos; and
4 WHEREAS, junior, Ian Edmond, earned first-team all-state honors and junior, Ethan
5 Kimmie, received all-state honorable mention recognition for their consistently outstanding
6 performances throughout the season; and
7 WHEREAS, the accomplishments of the Zachary High School boys basketball team
8 inspire great pride by reflecting the highest standards of teamwork, sportsmanship,
9 determination, and commitment to excellence.
10 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
11 does hereby commend and congratulate the Zachary High School boys basketball team both
12 on its second consecutive championship season and on receiving statewide recognition by
13 the Louisiana High School Athletic Association for its outstanding accomplishments.
14 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to
15 Zachary High School Head Coach Jon McClinton.
