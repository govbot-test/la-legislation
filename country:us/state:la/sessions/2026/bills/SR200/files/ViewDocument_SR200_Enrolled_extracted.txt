2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 200
BY SENATOR BARROW
A RESOLUTION
To commend and congratulate the Zachary High School boys basketball team on winning
the Division I Non-Select state championship and on earning statewide honors
following its championship season.
WHEREAS, the Louisiana Senate desires to recognize and commend the
student-athletes and coaches whose dedication, discipline, and perseverance bring distinction
to their school, the city of Zachary, and the state of Louisiana; and
WHEREAS, the Zachary High School Broncos completed an outstanding 2025-2026
basketball season by capturing the Louisiana High School Athletic Association Division I
Non-Select state championship; and
WHEREAS, the Broncos secured their second consecutive state championship with
a hard-fought 63-60 victory over Ruston High School in the championship game played on
March 14, 2026, at Burton Coliseum in Lake Charles; and
WHEREAS, under the leadership of Head Coach Jon McClinton, the Broncos
compiled an exceptional 34-3 record during the 2025-2026 season; and
WHEREAS, Coach McClinton was named the Louisiana High School Athletic
Association Class 5A Coach of the Year in recognition of his leadership and the continued
excellence of the Zachary High School basketball program; and
WHEREAS, the championship marked the fourth state title won under Coach
McClinton's leadership in six seasons and the second consecutive championship for the
Broncos; and
WHEREAS, junior, Ian Edmond, earned first-team all-state honors and junior, Ethan
Kimmie, received all-state honorable mention recognition for their consistently outstanding
performances throughout the season; and
WHEREAS, the accomplishments of the Zachary High School boys basketball team
inspire great pride by reflecting the highest standards of teamwork, sportsmanship,
determination, and commitment to excellence.

THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
does hereby commend and congratulate the Zachary High School boys basketball team both
on its second consecutive championship season and on receiving statewide recognition by
the Louisiana High School Athletic Association for its outstanding accomplishments.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to
Zachary High School Head Coach Jon McClinton.
PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE
