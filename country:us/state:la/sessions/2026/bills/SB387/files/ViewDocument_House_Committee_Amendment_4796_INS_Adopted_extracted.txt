HCASB387 4902 4796
HOUSE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
387 by Senator Bass
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 6, between lines 17 and 18, insert the following:
3 "(23) "Net acquisition cost" means the amount paid to acquire a prescription
4 drug after deducting all rebates, price concessions, administrative fees, and any
5 other payments or economic benefits received directly or indirectly by the
6 pharmacy benefit manager or any related entity in connection with the
7 selection, placement, or dispensing of that drug, calculated from the perspective
8 of the health benefit plan and not the pharmacy benefit manager."
9 AMENDMENT NO. 2
10 On page 9, line 6, after "on" and before "a" delete "either"
11 AMENDMENT NO. 3
12 On page 9, line 7, after "per-prescription" and before "basis" insert "or per-event"
13 AMENDMENT NO. 4
14 On page 12, at the beginning of line 4, after "(5)" insert "(a)"
15 AMENDMENT NO. 5
16 On page 12, between lines 19 and 20, insert the following:
17 "(b) Nothing in this Paragraph shall be construed to prohibit the
18 commissioner from disclosing information to a Prescription Drug Affordability
19 Board established pursuant to this Title if such information is subject to the
20 confidentiality protections applicable to that board."
21 AMENDMENT NO. 6
22 On page 12, line 23, change "2027" to "2028"
23 AMENDMENT NO. 7
24 On page 14, line 7, after "provided" delete the remainder of the line and insert in lieu
25 thereof the following:
26 "in the same formulary tier and cost-sharing structure for the"
27 AMENDMENT NO. 8
28 On page 16, after line 16, add the following:
29 "Section 5. The provisions of this Act shall apply to any new policy, contract,
30 program, or health coverage plan issued on or after January 1, 2028. Any policy, contract,
31 program, or health coverage plan in effect prior to January 1, 2028, shall convert to conform
32 to the provisions of this Act on or before the renewal date, but no later than January 1,
33 2029."
