ENROLLED
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 173
BY REPRESENTATIVE CHANCE HENRY
A RESOLUTION
commend the Louisiana Association of Wholesalers for its contributions to the
economy and citizens of Louisiana.
WHEREAS, wholesalers play a critical role in Louisiana's economy by serving as
the vital link between manufacturers and retailers and ensuring the efficient distribution of
goods across the state; and
WHEREAS, the Louisiana Association of Wholesalers represents a diverse network
of businesses that support local economies, create jobs, and contribute significantly to the
state's economic growth and stability; and
WHEREAS, members of the Louisiana Association of Wholesalers are committed
to maintaining high standards of service, professionalism, and integrity while supporting
Louisiana's businesses and communities; and
WHEREAS, wholesalers help ensure that essential goods, including food, beverages,
medical supplies, and other necessities are delivered safely and reliably to communities
throughout Louisiana; and
WHEREAS, Wholesalers Day at the state capitol provides an opportunity to
recognize the hard work and dedication of those in the wholesale industry and to highlight
their importance to the state's continued prosperity, and it is fitting to recognize the
Louisiana Association of Wholesalers for its valuable contributions.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby recognize Tuesday, April 21, 2026, as Wholesalers
Day at the state capitol and does hereby commend the Louisiana Association of Wholesalers
for its exemplary service and contributions to the state.
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES

HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 173

commend the Louisiana Association of Wholesalers for its contributions to the
economy and citizens of Louisiana.

the vital link between manufacturers and retailers and ensuring the efficient distribution of
goods across the state; and
WHEREAS, the Louisiana Association of Wholesalers represents a diverse network
of businesses that support local economies, create jobs, and contribute significantly to the
state's economic growth and stability; and
WHEREAS, members of the Louisiana Association of Wholesalers are committed
to maintaining high standards of service, professionalism, and integrity while supporting
Louisiana's businesses and communities; and
WHEREAS, wholesalers help ensure that essential goods, including food, beverages,
medical supplies, and other necessities are delivered safely and reliably to communities
throughout Louisiana; and
WHEREAS, Wholesalers Day at the state capitol provides an opportunity to
recognize the hard work and dedication of those in the wholesale industry and to highlight
their importance to the state's continued prosperity, and it is fitting to recognize the
Louisiana Association of Wholesalers for its valuable contributions.
