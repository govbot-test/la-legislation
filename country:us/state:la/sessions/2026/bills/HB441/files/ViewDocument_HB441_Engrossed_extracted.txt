HB 441 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Freeman
Abstract: Repeals provisions requiring the State Civil Service Commission and the Civil
Service Commission of the City of New Orleans to develop a plan for the transfer
of employees of the board to the state civil service.
Present law (Act 384 of the 2024 R.S.) requires the State Civil Service Commission and the
Civil Service Commission of the City of New Orleans to cooperatively develop a plan for
the transfer of all employees of the sewerage and water board of New Orleans to the state
civil service. Provides that the plan shall be fully implemented and all such employees shall
be subject to the administration of state civil service within one year of the effective date of
present law (Aug. 1, 2024). House Resolution No. 334 of the 2024 R.S. directs the State
Civil Service Commission and the Civil Service Commission of the City of New Orleans to
disregard present law and not to proceed with the plan contemplated therein.
Proposed law repeals present law.
Effective upon signature of governor or lapse of time for gubernatorial action.
(Repeals §2 of Act No. 384 of the 2024 R.S.)
