Section 1:
ENROLLED
ACT No. 301
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE MCMAKIN
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 48:773, relative to the Capital Area Road and Bridge District;
3 to provide relative to the board of commissioners of the district; to provide relative
4 to the membership of the board; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 48:773 is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
7 §773. Appointment and term of board of commissioners
8 The management and control of the district shall be vested in a board of
9 commissioners composed of seven members as follows:
10 (1) The secretary of the Department of Transportation and Development or
11 his designee.
12 (2) The president of each parish comprising the district or the president's
13 designee.
14 (3) The governor shall appoint one resident of the district as a member who
15 shall serve at the pleasure of governor.
16 (4) The speaker of the House of Representatives shall appoint at least one
17 and not more than two representatives whose representative district includes a
18 portion of at least one of the parishes comprising the district. The speaker shall
19 appoint, as practicable, one representative from each of the parishes comprising the
20 district.
21 (5) The president of the Senate shall appoint at least one and not more than
22 two senators whose senate district includes a portion of at least one of the parishes
