HLS 26RS-5235 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 256
BY REPRESENTATIVE AMEDEE
COMMENDATIONS: Commends the members and volunteers of the 2026 Concerned
Women for America of Louisiana's Encourage-A-Legislator Project
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend the members and volunteers of the 2026 Concerned Women for America of
3 Louisiana's Encourage-A-Legislator Project.
4 WHEREAS, Concerned Women for America is a legislative action committee whose
5 mission is to protect and promote biblical values and constitutional principles through
6 prayer, education, and advocacy; the vision of the committee is to lead a movement
7 dedicated to Christian causes through education and public policy; and
8 WHEREAS, the Prayer/Action chapters of Concerned Women for America help to
9 give its members a voice in their communities by meeting regularly to encourage informed
10 prayer and action for our nation and its leaders; and
11 WHEREAS, the Prayer/Action chapters educate Christian members on issues that
12 affect the Bible-based standards of Concerned Women for America; and
13 WHEREAS, on May 7, 2026, Concerned Women for America of Louisiana's
14 Encourage-A-Legislator volunteers will be visiting the capitol to meet and engage with
15 legislators; and
16 WHEREAS, Concerned Women for America of Louisiana's Encourage-A-Legislator
17 volunteers are most deserving of recognition for their active participation in state politics and
18 government and for exercising their right to discuss and support legislation about which they
19 are passionate.
20 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
21 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend the members and volunteers of the 2026
