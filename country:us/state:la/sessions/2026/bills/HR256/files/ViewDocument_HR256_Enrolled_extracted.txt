2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 256
BY REPRESENTATIVE AMEDEE
A RESOLUTION
To commend the members and volunteers of the 2026 Concerned Women for America of
Louisiana's Encourage-A-Legislator Project.
WHEREAS, Concerned Women for America is a legislative action committee whose
mission is to protect and promote biblical values and constitutional principles through
prayer, education, and advocacy; the vision of the committee is to lead a movement
dedicated to Christian causes through education and public policy; and
WHEREAS, the Prayer/Action chapters of Concerned Women for America help to
give its members a voice in their communities by meeting regularly to encourage informed
prayer and action for our nation and its leaders; and
WHEREAS, the Prayer/Action chapters educate Christian members on issues that
affect the Bible-based standards of Concerned Women for America; and
WHEREAS, on May 7, 2026, Concerned Women for America of Louisiana's
Encourage-A-Legislator volunteers will be visiting the capitol to meet and engage with
legislators; and
WHEREAS, Concerned Women for America of Louisiana's Encourage-A-Legislator
volunteers are most deserving of recognition for their active participation in state politics and
government and for exercising their right to discuss and support legislation about which they
are passionate.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend the members and volunteers of the 2026
Concerned Women for America of Louisiana's Encourage-A-Legislator Project and does
hereby extend sincere wishes that they continue their active participation in state
government.
