SLS 26RS-1306 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 53
BY SENATOR BOUDREAUX
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Reverend Eradly Ben Sr. on his Golden Jubilee celebration.
3 WHEREAS, Reverend Eradly Ben Sr. has demonstrated a lifetime of devotion to
4 ministry, having served as a beacon of light, hope, and compassion to his congregation and
5 the broader Ville Platte community; and
6 WHEREAS, Reverend Ben is recognized as the oldest active Baptist Pastor in the
7 city of Ville Platte, where he continuously brings spiritual nourishment and stability to
8 generations of families; and
9 WHEREAS, with sixty-two years at Mt. Pilgrim Baptist Church and fifty years at
10 Antioch Baptist Church, Reverend Ben's many years of service are a testament to his
11 steadfast commitment to his community; and
12 WHEREAS, alongside his beloved wife, First Lady Lois Ben, Reverend Ben has
13 uplifted countless lives through his ministry, exemplifying the values of love, humility, and
14 selfless service; and
15 WHEREAS, the occasion of Reverend Ben's Golden Jubilee celebration is not only
16 to remember the remarkable fifty years of pastoral leadership at Antioch Baptist Church, but
17 to also serve as a reflection of his lifelong impact, deeply rooted in faith and community.
18 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
Page 1 of 2

1 does hereby commend Reverend Eradly Ben Sr. on his Golden Jubilee celebration and for
