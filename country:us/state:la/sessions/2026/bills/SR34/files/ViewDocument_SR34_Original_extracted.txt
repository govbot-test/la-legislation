SLS 26RS-1146 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 34
BY SENATOR MIZELL
2026, as Geaux Far Louisiana Day at the Louisiana State Capitol.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Geaux Far Louisiana for its leadership in creating an equitable and unified
3 early childhood system and ensuring that families have access to seamless, high-
4 quality early child development, health, and educational services in Louisiana and
5 to designate Tuesday, March 24, 2026, as Geaux Far Louisiana Day at the Louisiana
6 State Capitol.
7 WHEREAS, Geaux Far Louisiana was established in 2021, as a cross-sector coalition
8 with intentional focus on parents and early childhood providers, that advocates for the needs
9 of Louisiana's children from prenatal through age four; and
10 WHEREAS, Geaux Far Louisiana believes in a unified early childhood system and
11 has identified five goals to ensure families have access to seamless, high-quality early
12 childhood development, health, and educational services; and
13 WHEREAS, Geaux Far Louisiana collaborates with Louisiana-based partner
14 organizations dedicated to carrying out specific objectives within each target area that
15 compliments the work the organizations are already implementing; and
16 WHEREAS, all families in Louisiana want their children to be healthy, happy, and
17 to thrive; and
18 WHEREAS, healthy families and young children, prenatal through age four, are the
Page 1 of 2
SLS 26RS-1146 ORIGINAL
SR NO. 34
1 backbone of thriving communities and economic vitality for the state of Louisiana; and
2 WHEREAS, by engaging parents, educators, policymakers, and community leaders,
3 Geaux Far Louisiana aims to shape policies, advocate for change, and improve outcomes for
4 young learners statewide; and
5 WHEREAS, Geaux Far Louisiana encourages individuals to become "Champions
6 for Children" by lending their voices, time, and talents to advocate for legislative policies.
7 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
8 does hereby commend Geaux Far Louisiana for its leadership in creating an equitable and
9 unified early childhood system and ensuring that families have access to seamless, high-
10 quality early child development, health, and educational services in Louisiana and does
11 hereby designate Tuesday, March 24, 2026, as Geaux Far Louisiana Day at the Louisiana
12 State Capitol.
13 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
14 Geaux Far Louisiana Coalition.
The original instrument and the following digest, which constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument, were prepared by Senate Legislative Services.

or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
