HOUSE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
No. 17 by Representative Turner
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, line 6, after "Education" delete the remainder of the line and delete line 7 and
3 insert a comma "," and insert "the House Committee on Appropriations, and the House
4 Committee on Labor and Industrial Relations not later than February 15, 2027."
5 AMENDMENT NO. 2
6 On page 2, line 15, after "Education" delete the remainder of the line and delete line 16 and
7 insert a comma "," and insert the following:
8 "the House Committee on Appropriations, and the House Committee on Labor and Industrial
9 Relations not later than February 15, 2027.
10 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that these agencies shall use information provided
11 by the Louisiana Office of Student Financial Assistance when conducting the study."
12 AMENDMENT NO. 3
13 On page 2, after line 30, insert the following:
14 "(5) A comparative analysis of the largest state-funded financial assistance programs
15 for students enrolled in postsecondary education institutions.
16 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the report may be incorporated into the 2027
17 Launch Louisiana report developed and published by the Kathleen Babineaux Blanco Public
18 Policy Center for Louisiana's Foundational Integrated Research System for Transformation
19 as provided in R.S. 17:3138.12(D)(1)."
20 AMENDMENT NO. 4
21 On page 3, line 6, after "Works," delete the remainder of the line and insert "secretary of
22 Louisiana Economic Development, state superintendent of education, and presidents of the
23 Louisiana State University System, Southern University System, University of Louisiana
24 System, and Louisiana Community and Technical College System."
