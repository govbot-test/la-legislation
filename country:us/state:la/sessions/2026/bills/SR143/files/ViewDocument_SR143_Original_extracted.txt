SLS 26RS-3013 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 143
BY SENATOR MIZELL
SPECIAL DAY/WEEK/MONTH. Designates Wednesday, May 20, 2026, as Tourism Day
at the Louisiana State Capitol.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate Wednesday, May 20, 2026, as Tourism Day at the Louisiana State Capitol.
3 WHEREAS, travel plays an essential role in preserving Louisiana's culture, propels
4 the economy, and is pivotal in shaping Louisiana's success; and
5 WHEREAS, travel is vital in creating quality job opportunities with two hundred
6 twenty-five thousand six hundred people employed in the leisure and hospitality industry,
7 maintaining its status as the fourth highest employer in the state; and
8 WHEREAS, Louisiana attracted over forty-five million domestic and international
9 visitors in 2025; and
10 WHEREAS, domestic and international travelers spent an estimated nineteen billion
11 five hundred million dollars while visiting Louisiana in 2025, with a 5.2 percent increase
12 over 2024; and
13 WHEREAS, tourism is funded exclusively by a dedication of three one-hundredths
14 of a cent of sales tax, and the funds are used by the office of tourism, under the direction of
15 Lieutenant Governor Billy Nungesser, to promote tourism to the state, creating an enormous
16 economic impact in Louisiana; and
17 WHEREAS, members of the travel and tourism community will gather together at
18 the Louisiana State Capitol on Wednesday, May 20, 2026, to celebrate tourism in Louisiana.

1 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
2 does hereby designate Wednesday, May 20, 2026, as Tourism Day at the Louisiana State
3 Capitol and does hereby express appreciation and support for the fine work of the Louisiana
4 travel industry in its mission to bring visitors to Louisiana and provide ambassadorial and
5 financial benefits to the state.
6 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
7 assistant secretary of tourism of the Department of Culture, Recreation and Tourism.
