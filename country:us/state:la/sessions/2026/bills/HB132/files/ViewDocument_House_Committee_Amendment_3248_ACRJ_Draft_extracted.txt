15 (D)"
16 AMENDMENT NO. 6
17 On page 1, between lines 9 and 10, insert the following:
18 "§33. Battery defined
19 Battery is the intentional use of force or violence upon the person of another;
20 or the intentional administration of a poison or other noxious liquid or substance to
21 another; or the intentional directing of sound at another, in close proximity to
22 another's ear or head, under circumstances in which the sound is capable of causing
23 physical pain, hearing impairment, or bodily injury, and in such a manner that a
24 reasonable person would foresee that the conduct would cause physical pain, hearing
25 impairment, or bodily injury."
26 AMENDMENT NO. 7
27 On page 1, delete lines 11 through 18 in their entirety and on page 2, delete lines 1 through
28 3 in their entirety
29 AMENDMENT NO. 8

31 "E." to "D."
