Section 1:
HCAHB392 224 2935
HOUSE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
by Representative Boyd
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, line 5, after "requirements;" and before "and" insert "to require signage at schools
3 about the national suicide prevention hotline number and a state suicide prevention website;"
4 AMENDMENT NO. 2
5 On page 1, between lines 6 and 7, insert the following:
6 "Section 1. This Act shall be known and may be cited as the "Student Suicide
7 Prevention Awareness Act.""
8 AMENDMENT NO. 3
9 On page 1, at the beginning of line 7, change "Section 1." to "Section 2."
10 AMENDMENT NO. 4
11 On page 1, between lines 16 and 17, insert the following:
12 "(c) A link to Louisiana988.org, a suicide prevention initiative of the
13 Louisiana Department of Health."
14 AMENDMENT NO. 5
15 On page 2, between lines 2 and 3, insert the following:
16 "(3)(a) Each middle school and high school that receives federal
17 funds shall post signage to inform students about the National Suicide
18 Prevention Lifeline hotline number, "988", with an explanation that this
19 number may be called or texted. The signage shall include a brief message
20 encouraging students experiencing emotional distress to seek help as well as
21 an explanation that the hotline is free, confidential, and available twenty-four
22 hours a day and seven days a week.
23 (b) The signage shall also include information about
24 Louisiana988.org and an explanation that additional information and
25 resources, including the ability to chat online, are available at this website.
26 (c) The signage shall be placed in locations easily visible to students
27 such as school entrances and exits, student common areas, school counselor
28 offices, school nurse offices, near restrooms and locker rooms, and digital
29 school bulletin boards if available."
30
