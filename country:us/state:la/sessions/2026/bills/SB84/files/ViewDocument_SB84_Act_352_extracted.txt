1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 22:1028(B)(1) and to enact R.S. 22:1028(B)(3), relative to
3 prostate cancer screening; to provide for insurance coverage for screening of men
4 over the age of forty in accordance with the guidelines from the National
5 Comprehensive Cancer Network; to prohibit out-of-pocket expenses for cancer
6 screening services; to provide for applicability; to provide for an effective date; and
7 to provide for related matters.
8 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
9 Section 1. R.S. 22:1028(B)(1) is hereby amended and reenacted and R.S.
10 22:1028(B)(3) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
11 §1028. Early screening and detection requirements; examination; coverage
12 * * *
13 B.(1) Any health coverage plan which is delivered or issued for delivery in
14 the state shall provide coverage for detection of prostate cancer, including digital
15 rectal examination and prostate-specific antigen testing for men over the age of fifty
16 years and as medically necessary and appropriate for men over between the age ages
17 of forty to forty-nine years in accordance with the most recently published
18 guidelines from the National Comprehensive Cancer Network.
19 * * *
20 (3) Any coverage required pursuant to the provisions of this Subsection
21 shall not be subject to any cost-sharing amount, including deductibles,
22 coinsurance, copayment, or similar out-of-pocket expenses.

2 Section 2. The provisions of this Act shall apply to any new policy, contract,
3 program, or health coverage plan issued on or after January 1, 2027. Any policy, contract,
4 or health coverage plan in effect prior to January 1, 2027, shall convert to conform to the
5 provisions of this Act on or before the renewal date, but no later than January 1, 2028.
6 Section 3. This Act shall become effective upon signature by the governor, or, if not
7 signed by the governor, upon expiration of time for bills to become law without signature
8 by the governor, as provided by Article III, Section 18 of the Constitution of Louisiana. If
9 vetoed by the governor and subsequently approved by this legislature, this Act shall become
10 effective on the day following such approval.
