HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 155
BY REPRESENTATIVE BAYHAM
A RESOLUTION
To memorialize the observance of April 14, 2026, as Holocaust Martyrs' and Heroes'
Remembrance Day.
WHEREAS, Holocaust Martyrs' and Heroes' Remembrance Day, or Yom HaShoah,
is a day of remembrance for the six million Jewish lives lost during the Holocaust; it is also
a day to acknowledge the acts of heroism and resistance made during this period; and
WHEREAS, in Israel, Yom HaShoah is observed on the twenty-seventh day of the
Hebrew month of Nisan, which falls in late April or early May and marks the anniversary
of the Warsaw Ghetto Uprising; on this day, sirens sound for one minute at 10 a.m., and all
public activities cease in observance; the main ceremony takes place at Yad Vashem, the
World Holocaust Remembrance Center in Jerusalem; and
WHEREAS, the central theme for Holocaust Martyrs' and Heroes' Remembrance
Day in 2026 is "The Jewish Family during the Holocaust", which recognizes the renewal of
family ties as a pivotal factor in the rehabilitation of survivors, their return to life after the
Holocaust, and the rebirth of the Jewish people as a whole; and
WHEREAS, many countries and communities around the world also observe a day
of remembrance for the Holocaust, often aligning with Yom HaShoah.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby memorialize the observance of April 14, 2026, as
Holocaust Martyrs' and Heroes' Remembrance Day and does hereby encourage all
Louisianians to join in honoring the legacy of the victims of the Holocaust.
