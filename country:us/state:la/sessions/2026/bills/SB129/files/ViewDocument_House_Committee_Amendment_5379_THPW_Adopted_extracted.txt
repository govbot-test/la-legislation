HCASB129 4877 5379
HOUSE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
to Engrossed Senate Bill No. 129 by Senator Foil
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, line 2, after "To" and before "enact" insert "amend and reenact the Section
3 heading of R.S. 47:463.191, (A), and (C) and to" and after "plates;" and before "to" insert
4 "to modify the member of congress special prestige license plate;"
5 AMENDMENT NO. 2
6 On page 1, line 5, after "plate;" and before "and" insert "to provide for an effective date;"
7 AMENDMENT NO. 3
8 On page 1, line 7, after "Section 1." and before "R.S. 47:463.243" insert "The Section
9 heading of R.S. 47:463.191, (A), and (C) are hereby amended and reenacted and"
10 AMENDMENT NO. 4
11 On page 1, between lines 7 and 8, insert the following:
12 "§463.191. Special prestige license plate; member of congress; designated state
13 officials
14 A. Notwithstanding the provisions of R.S. 47:463(A)(3)(b), the secretary of
15 the Department of Public Safety and Corrections shall establish a special prestige
16 license plate for motor vehicles, restricted to passenger cars, pickup trucks,
17 motorcycles, recreational vehicles, and vans, which may be issued upon application
18 of any Louisiana member of congress, to members of the Louisiana Supreme
19 Court, or upon written request of the clerk of court, for official vehicles used by
20 duly commissioned officers in the course and scope of their official duties.
21 * * *
22 C. The secretary shall promulgate rules and regulations necessary to
23 implement the provisions of this Section, including rules and regulations governing
24 the issuance, transfer, and disposition of the license plates upon the death of the
25 recipient and governing the design of the plate.
26 * * *"
27 AMENDMENT NO. 5
28 On page 2, after line 24, insert the following:
29 "Section 2. This Act shall become effective upon signature by the governor
30 or, if not signed by the governor, upon expiration of the time for bills to become law
31 without signature by the governor, as provided by Article III, Section 18 of the
32 Constitution of Louisiana. If vetoed by the governor and subsequently approved by
33 the legislature, this Act shall become effective on the day following such approval."
