HLS 26RS-643 ENGROSSED
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE BILL NO. 190
BY REPRESENTATIVE SCHLEGEL
(On Recommendation of the Louisiana State Law Institute)
LIABILITY/TORTS: Provides for a standard of care in certain activities related to software
and computer programs
1 AN ACT
2 To enact Civil Code Article 2317.2, relative to software and computer programs; to provide
3 for a standard of care in design, development, licensing, manufacture, and sale; and
4 to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. Civil Code Article 2317.2 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
7 Art. 2317.2. Software and computer programs
8 Any person who designs, develops, licenses, manufactures, or sells a
9 computer program or software that provides an interactive or personalized user
10 experience based on the user's data or information owes a duty to exercise reasonable
11 care in the design, development, licensing, manufacture, or sale of the computer
12 program or software.
13 Revision Comments - 2026
14 This Article does not foreclose the availability of any other cause of action
15 or theory of liability pursuant to this Code or other applicable law, including the
16 Louisiana Products Liability Act, R.S. 9:2800.51 et seq.

HB 190 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Schlegel
Abstract: Provides relative to the standard of care for activities related to computer
programs and software.
Proposed law (C.C. Art. 2317.2) requires a person to exercise reasonable care in the design,
development, manufacture, and sale of certain computer programs and software.
(Adds C.C. Art. 2317.2)
