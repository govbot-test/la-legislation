SLS 26RS-577 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY SENATOR MILLER
CONTRACTORS. Provides relative to the State Licensing Board for Contractors. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 37:2153(F)(7), relative to the State Licensing Board for Contractors; to
3 provide for powers and duties of the board; to provide for hearings conducted by the
4 board; to provide for the suspension or revocation of licenses; and to provide for
5 related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 37:2153(F)(7) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §2153. Powers of the board
9 * * *
10 F. In addition to any other duties and powers granted by this Chapter, the
11 board shall:
12 * * *
13 (7) Have plenary authority to review all standard construction practices
14 performed within the state and shall have the authority to hold hearings for the
15 purpose of suspending or revoking any license for substandard construction
16 practices in violation of this Chapter.
17 * * *

SLS 26RS-577 ORIGINAL
SB NO. 370
The original instrument and the following digest, which constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument, were prepared by Senate Legislative Services.
The keyword, summary, and digest do not constitute part of the law or proof
or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
DIGEST
SB 370 Original 2026 Regular Session Miller
Present law provides relative to the powers and duties of the State Licensing Board for
Contractors (board).
Present law requires the board to do the following:
(1) Grant licenses to qualified persons.
(2) Hold hearings in accordance with the provisions of the Administrative Procedure Act to
consider violations of present law and the rules and regulations of the board.
(3) Suspend or revoke any license for any cause as provided in present law and refuse to
grant any license for any cause which would be grounds for revocation or suspension of a
license.
(4) Issue fines, penalties, and other costs for violations of present law.
(5) Waive the trade portion of the examination for any applicant holding a similar
certification from another state that the applicant passed the examination upon a written
certification from the licensing state.
(6) Publish and distribute materials containing information as it deems proper to accomplish
the purpose of present law.
Proposed law retains present law and adds that the board is authorized to review all
substandard construction practices within the state and hold hearings for the purpose of
suspending and revoking any license for substandard construction practices in violation of
present law.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Adds R.S. 37:2153(F)(7))
