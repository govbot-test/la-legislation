12 §1749.12. Definitions
13 As used in this Part, the following terms have the meanings ascribed to them
14 in this Section:
15 (1) "Agricultural excavator" means a person who owns or operates a farm and
16 is directly involved in the cultivation of land or crops or who raises livestock.
17 (2) "Clear or no conflict" means a response provided by an owner or
18 operator of underground utilities or facilities when the owner or operator
19 determines that its facilities or utilities are not in conflict with the proposed area
20 of excavation.
21 (2)(3) "Commissioner" means the commissioner of conservation.
22 (3)(4) "Damage" means any defacing, scraping, gouging, breaking, cutting,
23 or displacement of, impact upon or removal of an underground facility or utility or
24 its means of primary support.
25 (4)(5) "Demolisher" means any person engaged in the act of demolishing as
26 defined in Paragraph (2)(6) of this Section.
27 (5)(6) "Demolition" means the total or partial wrecking, razing, rendering,

2 (6)(7) "Emergency" means any crisis situation which poses an imminent
3 threat or danger to life, health, or property, which requires immediate action, if such
4 action is taken. The term also includes an unplanned utility outage, which requires
5 immediate action, if such action is taken.
6 (7)(8) "Excavation" or "excavate" means any operation causing movement
7 or removal of earth, rock, or other materials in or on the ground or submerged in a
8 marine environment that could reasonably result in damage to underground or
9 submerged utilities or facilities by the use of powered or mechanical or manual
10 means, including but not limited to pile driving, digging, blasting, augering, boring,
11 back filling, dredging, compaction, plowing-in, trenching, ditching, tunneling,
12 land-leveling, grading, and mechanical probing. "Excavation" or "excavate" shall not
13 include manual probing, normal commercial farming operations, or any activity

15 of God or an act of nature.
16 (8)(9) "Excavator" means any person who engages in excavation operations.
17 (9)(10) "Forestry excavator" means an excavator who is a logger, prescribed
18 burner, site preparation operator, or tree planter for commercial forestry operations.
19 (10)(11) "Inclement weather" means weather that prohibits or impedes a
20 worker's use of his locating equipment or causes undue risk to himself or his
21 equipment such as lightning, heavy rain, tornadoes, hurricanes, floods, sleet, snow,
22 or flooding conditions.

24 demolition activity within a contiguous area that cannot reasonably be completed
25 within twenty calendar days for routine demolition or excavation or within thirty
26 calendar days for agricultural, forestry, or marine excavation or demolition activity.
27 (13) "Locate request" means a communication between an excavator or
28 demolisher and the regional notification center in which a request for locating
29 underground utilities or facilities is processed.
