3 AMENDMENT NO. 13
4 On page 8, between lines 2 and 3, insert the following:
5 "(2) If the operator does not visibly mark the location of these utilities or
6 facilities, the operator shall provide information to enable an excavator using
7 reasonable and prudent means to determine the approximate location of the utility
8 or facility including but not limited to written or electronic information in the
9 form of maps, drawings, or GPS coordinates showing any potential conflicts
10 within the area of planned excavation. The information provided by the operator
11 shall include a contact person and a specific telephone number for the excavators to
12 call. After the operator has received the notification request, the information on
13 location, size, and type of underground utility or facility must shall be provided by
14 the operator to the excavator prior to excavation."
15 AMENDMENT NO. 14
16 On page 9, line 3, change "under" to "in accordance with"
17 AMENDMENT NO. 15
18 On page 10, after line 25, add the following:
19 "§1749.28. Dispute resolution for BEAD Program-funded projects
20 A. This Section applies solely to broadband construction projects funded
21 pursuant to the federal Broadband Equity, Access, and Deployment, or BEAD,
22 Program as administered by the office of broadband, development and
23 connectivity, which will be referred to in this Section as the office, pursuant to
24 National Telecommunications and Information Administration of the United
25 States Department of Commerce, NTIA, requirements.
26 B.(1) For the projects outlined in Subsection A of this Section, a stop-
27 work order issued solely for alleged marking noncompliance or excavation zone
28 disputes shall not take immediate effect unless the issuing authority makes a
29 written determination that an imminent threat to life or public safety exists.
30 (2) Except as outlined in Paragraph (1) of this Subsection, enforcement
31 of a stop-work order shall be postponed for a four-hour review period during
32 which construction may continue. A rapid dispute resolution panel, consisting
33 of representatives of the office, the underground facility operator, the governing
34 authority of the impacted area, and the excavating contractor, shall review
35 relevant documentation and attempt resolution. If no resolution is reached, the
36 office or its designated neutral third party shall issue an independent field
37 determination that is binding for purposes of the stop-work order.
38 C. Nothing in this Section shall be construed to modify federal BEAD
39 Program obligations."
