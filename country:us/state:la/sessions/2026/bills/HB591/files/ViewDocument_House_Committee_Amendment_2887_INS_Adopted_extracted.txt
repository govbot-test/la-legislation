Section 1:
HCAHB591 4145 2887
HOUSE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
by Representative Green
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, line 16, before "The" insert "A."
3 AMENDMENT NO. 2
4 On page 3, delete lines 3 through 14 in their entirety and insert in lieu thereof the following:
5 "(4) "Family leave insurance" means an insurance policy issued to an employer
6 related to a benefit program provided to an employee to pay for a percentage or portion of
7 the employee’s income loss due to any of the following:
8 (a) The birth of a child or adoption of a child by the employee.
9 (b) Placement of a child with the employee for foster care.
10 (c) Care of a family member of the employee who has a serious health condition.
11 (d) Circumstances arising out of the fact that the employee's family member who is
12 a service member is on active duty or has been notified of an impending call or order
13 to active duty.
14 (5) "Family member" means a child, spouse, parent, or any other person defined as
15 a "family member" pursuant to R.S. 40:2024.2(5)."
16 AMENDMENT NO. 3
17 On page 4, line 1, after "benefits;" and before "payment" change "Eligibility" to "eligibility"
18 AMENDMENT NO. 4
19 On page 4, line 19, change "B.(1)" to "B."
20 AMENDMENT NO. 5
21 On page 5, line 4, change "C.(1)" to "C."
22 AMENDMENT NO. 6
23 On page 6, after line 28, insert the following:
24 "H. Payment of benefits under any policy or rider providing paid family
25 leave benefits pursuant to this Subpart shall not be deemed wages in lieu of workers'
26 compensation benefits and shall not interrupt prescription for any claim arising under
27 Chapter 10 of Title 23 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950."
