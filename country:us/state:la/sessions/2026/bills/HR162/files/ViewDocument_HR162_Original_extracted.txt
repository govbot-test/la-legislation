1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate the month of May 2026 as Motorcycle Safety Awareness Month in Louisiana
3 and to commend American Bikers Active Towards Education of Louisiana, Inc., and
4 New Orleans StreetHogs Motorcycle Club.
5 WHEREAS, American Bikers Active Towards Education of Louisiana, Inc. is a
6 nonprofit organization dedicated to motorcycle safety, awareness, and reasonable motorcycle
7 legislation; and
8 WHEREAS, New Orleans StreetHogs is an official Motorcycle Club that is
9 committed to motorcycle safety, awareness, and education with respect to motorcycle riders;
10 and
11 WHEREAS, motorcycles are less visible than cars and are often unseen by motorists,
12 which contributes to thousands of riders and passengers being injured or killed in motorcycle
13 accidents each year; and
14 WHEREAS, the number of vehicles, including motorcycles, is expected to increase
15 on Louisiana roads in the coming years; and
16 WHEREAS, a concerted effort is being made to bring motorcycle safety and rider
17 awareness to the forefront; and
18 WHEREAS, all licensed vehicle operators have the same rights and privileges on
19 roads and highways, and all motorists should be aware of their surroundings while driving;
20 and
