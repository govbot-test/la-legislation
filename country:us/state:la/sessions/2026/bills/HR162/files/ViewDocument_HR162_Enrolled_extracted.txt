HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 162
BY REPRESENTATIVE BRASS
A RESOLUTION
To designate the month of May 2026 as Motorcycle Safety Awareness Month in Louisiana
and to commend American Bikers Active Towards Education of Louisiana, Inc., and
New Orleans StreetHogs Motorcycle Club.
WHEREAS, American Bikers Active Towards Education of Louisiana, Inc. is a
nonprofit organization dedicated to motorcycle safety, awareness, and reasonable motorcycle
legislation; and
WHEREAS, New Orleans StreetHogs is an official Motorcycle Club that is
committed to motorcycle safety, awareness, and education with respect to motorcycle riders;
and
WHEREAS, motorcycles are less visible than cars and are often unseen by motorists,
which contributes to thousands of riders and passengers being injured or killed in motorcycle
accidents each year; and
WHEREAS, the number of vehicles, including motorcycles, is expected to increase
on Louisiana roads in the coming years; and
WHEREAS, a concerted effort is being made to bring motorcycle safety and rider
awareness to the forefront; and
WHEREAS, all licensed vehicle operators have the same rights and privileges on
roads and highways, and all motorists should be aware of their surroundings while driving;
and
WHEREAS, it is fitting to recognize National Motorcycle Safety Awareness Month
in May and the importance of road safety for all riders and drivers.

HR NO. 162 ENROLLED
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby designate the month of May 2025 as Motorcycle Safety
Awareness Month in Louisiana and does hereby commend American Bikers Active Towards
Education of Louisiana, Inc., and New Orleans StreetHogs Motorcycle Club for its work
promoting motorcycle safety, awareness, and education.
