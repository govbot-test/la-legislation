2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
SENATE BILL NO. 397
BY SENATOR REESE
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 18:586(C), relative to vacancies; to provide for vacancies when an elected
3 official has retired, resigned, or been removed from office; to prohibit an official
4 who has retired, resigned, or been removed from office from being appointed to or
5 qualifying for election to the same office in which the official resigned, retired, or
6 was removed; to provide for certain exceptions; and to provide for related matters.
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
8 Section 1. R.S. 18:586(C) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
9 §586. Vacancies caused by retirement, resignation, or removal; prohibitions
10 * * *
11 C. This Section does not purport to impact the constitutional authority
12 of the supreme court to appoint a sitting or retired judge to any court, and the
13 provisions of Subsection A of this Section shall not be applicable to judgeships.
PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE
