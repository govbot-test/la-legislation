HLS 26RS-2519 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 82
BY REPRESENTATIVES FREEMAN AND FREIBERG
SPECIAL DAY/WEEK/MONTH: Designates March 30, 2026, as Ellevate Louisiana Day
at the state capitol
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate Monday, March 30, 2026, as Ellevate Louisiana Day at the state capitol.
3 WHEREAS, Ellevate Louisiana is a nonpartisan, statewide organization dedicated
4 to elevating women's leadership, fostering civic engagement, and advancing policies that
5 strengthen Louisiana's communities and economy; and
6 WHEREAS, Since its founding in 2020, Ellevate Louisiana has united women
7 leaders across sectors to promote dialogue, mutual respect, and cross-partisan collaboration
8 on key issues; and
9 WHEREAS, Ellevate Louisiana Day at the state capitol provides an opportunity for
10 members to engage with legislators and participate in the legislative process.
11 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
12 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby designate Monday, March 30, 2026, as Ellevate
13 Louisiana Day at the state capitol and does hereby commend the organization for its
14 contributions to the state.
