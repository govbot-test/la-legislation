SLS 26RS-4621 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 194
BY SENATOR PRESSLY
COMMENDATIONS. Commends and congratulates the Chief Executive Officer of the
YMCA of Northwest Louisiana, Gary Lash, on his retirement.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Gary Lash upon his retirement as chief executive officer of the YMCA of
3 Northwest Louisiana and to recognize his outstanding contributions to the
4 Shreveport-Bossier community and the state of Louisiana.
5 WHEREAS, Gary Lash has devoted more than two decades of faithful leadership and
6 service to the YMCA of Northwest Louisiana, transforming it into the premier community
7 organization in the region; and
8 WHEREAS, Gary Lash accepted the position of chief executive officer of the YMCA
9 of Northwest Louisiana in 2003 at a time when the organization faced severe financial
10 hardship and uncertainty; and
11 WHEREAS, through vision, determination, and faith in God, Gary Lash led the
12 YMCA from severe debt and financial instability to unprecedented growth and success; and
13 WHEREAS, under his leadership, the YMCA of Northwest Louisiana expanded its
14 reach and impact through the development and improvement of numerous facilities; and
15 WHEREAS, these improvements included the revitalization of the historic downtown
16 YMCA, the construction of the BHP Billiton YMCA, the opening of the Lash Family
17 YMCA, and establishment of the Caddo Parish Premier Park baseball complex; and
18 WHEREAS, Gary Lash consistently emphasized that the true mission of the YMCA

1 is not merely the construction of buildings, but the building of people, families, friendships,
2 and community bonds; and
3 WHEREAS, his leadership fostered opportunities for youth development, healthy
4 living, sportsmanship, mentorship, and community engagement for countless children and
5 families throughout northwest Louisiana; and
6 WHEREAS, Gary Lash worked tirelessly to ensure that the YMCA remained a
7 welcoming place for people of all backgrounds and ages and continually promoted the
8 values of faith, service, teamwork, and compassion; and
9 WHEREAS, his commitment to the YMCA became a true family legacy shared with
10 his wife of forty-six years Renee, and their sons, Dr. Austin Lash and Dr. Tyler Lash, all of
11 whom devoted years of service and involvement to the YMCA's programs and activities; and
12 WHEREAS, the citizens of northwest Louisiana have greatly benefitted from Gary
13 Lash's leadership, optimism, humility, faith, and steadfast commitment to improving the
14 quality of life in the Shreveport-Bossier community; and
15 WHEREAS, it is appropriate to recognize and honor Gary Lash for his exceptional
16 service, leadership, and lasting contributions to the people of Louisiana.
17 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
18 commends Gary Lash upon his retirement and does hereby express sincere appreciation for
19 his many years of dedicated service, and does hereby extend best wishes for continued
20 happiness and success in all future endeavors.
21 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to Gary
22 Lash.
