2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 194
BY SENATOR PRESSLY
A RESOLUTION
To commend Gary Lash upon his retirement as chief executive officer of the YMCA of
Northwest Louisiana and to recognize his outstanding contributions to the
Shreveport-Bossier community and the state of Louisiana.
WHEREAS, Gary Lash has devoted more than two decades of faithful leadership and
service to the YMCA of Northwest Louisiana, transforming it into the premier community
organization in the region; and
WHEREAS, Gary Lash accepted the position of chief executive officer of the YMCA
of Northwest Louisiana in 2003 at a time when the organization faced severe financial
hardship and uncertainty; and
WHEREAS, through vision, determination, and faith in God, Gary Lash led the
YMCA from severe debt and financial instability to unprecedented growth and success; and
WHEREAS, under his leadership, the YMCA of Northwest Louisiana expanded its
reach and impact through the development and improvement of numerous facilities; and
WHEREAS, these improvements included the revitalization of the historic downtown
YMCA, the construction of the BHP Billiton YMCA, the opening of the Lash Family
YMCA, and establishment of the Caddo Parish Premier Park baseball complex; and
WHEREAS, Gary Lash consistently emphasized that the true mission of the YMCA
is not merely the construction of buildings, but the building of people, families, friendships,
and community bonds; and
WHEREAS, his leadership fostered opportunities for youth development, healthy
living, sportsmanship, mentorship, and community engagement for countless children and
families throughout northwest Louisiana; and
WHEREAS, Gary Lash worked tirelessly to ensure that the YMCA remained a
welcoming place for people of all backgrounds and ages and continually promoted the
values of faith, service, teamwork, and compassion; and

WHEREAS, his commitment to the YMCA became a true family legacy shared with
his wife of forty-six years Renee, and their sons, Dr. Austin Lash and Dr. Tyler Lash, all of
whom devoted years of service and involvement to the YMCA's programs and activities; and
WHEREAS, the citizens of northwest Louisiana have greatly benefitted from Gary
Lash's leadership, optimism, humility, faith, and steadfast commitment to improving the
quality of life in the Shreveport-Bossier community; and
WHEREAS, it is appropriate to recognize and honor Gary Lash for his exceptional
service, leadership, and lasting contributions to the people of Louisiana.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
commends Gary Lash upon his retirement and does hereby express sincere appreciation for
his many years of dedicated service, and does hereby extend best wishes for continued
happiness and success in all future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to Gary
Lash.
PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE
