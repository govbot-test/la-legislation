DIGEST
SB 105 Original 2026 Regular Session Cathey
Present law provides that effective for the 2020-2021, 2021-2022, and 2022-2023 academic
years, an individual who was honorably discharged from a branch of the United States
Armed Forces from a military installation in Louisiana must be eligible to receive a TOPS-
Tech Award under certain circumstances.
Proposed law retains present law but removes the effective academic years of 2020-2021,
2021-2022, and 2022-2023.
Present law requires an individual to have earned a 17 or higher on the ACT or an equivalent
concordant value on the SAT, or attained a silver level score or higher on the ACT
WorkKeys system of assessment in order to be eligible to receive a TOPS-Tech Award.
Proposed law removes the requirement and instead requires an individual provide a copy of
the individual's Certificate of Release or Discharge from Active Duty, a DD Form 214, to
verify qualifying military service.
Effective on August 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 17:5029(E)(1)(intro para) and 5029(E)(1)(d))
