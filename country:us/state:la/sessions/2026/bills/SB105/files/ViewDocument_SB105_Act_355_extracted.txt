2 To amend and reenact the introductory paragraph of R.S. 17:5029(E)(1) and 5029(E)(1)(d),
3 relative to the Taylor Opportunity Program for Students; to provide relative to
4 eligibility requirements for certain military veterans to receive a TOPS-Tech Award;
5 and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. The introductory paragraph of R.S. 17:5029(E)(1) and 5029(E)(1)(d) are
8 hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
9 §5029. Alternative initial eligibility requirements
10 * * *
11 E.(1) Notwithstanding the initial eligibility requirements established by this
12 Chapter, effective for the 2020-2021, 2021-2022, and 2022-2023 academic years, an
13 individual who was honorably discharged from a branch of the United States Armed
14 Forces from a military installation in Louisiana shall be eligible to receive a
15 TOPS-Tech Award, provided he meets all of the following requirements:
16 * * *
17 (d) Earned a 17 or higher score on the ACT or an equivalent concordant value
18 on the SAT, or attained a silver level score or higher on the ACT WorkKeys system
