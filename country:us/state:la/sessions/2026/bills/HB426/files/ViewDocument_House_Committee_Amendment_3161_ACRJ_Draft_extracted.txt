HCAHB426 4274 3161
HOUSE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
Original House Bill No. 426 by Representative Phelps
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, line 2, change "R.S. 14:107.3(B)" to "R.S. 14:107.3(B) and (C)(1) through (3)"
3 AMENDMENT NO. 2
4 On page 1, line 3, after "culpability;" and before "and" insert "to provide relative to
5 penalties;"
6 AMENDMENT NO. 3
7 On page 1, line 5, change "R.S. 14:107.3(B) is" to "R.S. 14:107.3(B) and (C)(1) through (3)
8 are"
9 AMENDMENT NO. 4
10 On page 1, after line 15, add the following:
11 "C.(1) On a first conviction, the offender shall be fined not more than five
12 hundred dollars per violation. Imposition of a fine may be suspended and in lieu
13 thereof, the court may require the offender, or any person managing or operating the
14 property on behalf of the offender, to correct all existing housing violations on the
15 blighted property within a timely manner determined by the court.
16 (2) On a second conviction, or if the offender fails to correct violations after
17 ordered to do so by the court, the offender shall be fined not more than one thousand
18 dollars per violation and imprisoned for not more than six months. Additionally, the
19 court shall require that the offender, or any person managing or operating the
20 property on behalf of the offender, correct all existing housing violations on the
21 blighted property.
22 (3) On any third or subsequent conviction, or if the offender fails to correct
23 all violations after a second conviction, the offender, or any person managing or
24 operating the property on behalf of the offender, shall be fined not more than two
25 thousand dollars per violation and imprisoned for not more than one year, with or
26 without hard labor.
27 * * *"
