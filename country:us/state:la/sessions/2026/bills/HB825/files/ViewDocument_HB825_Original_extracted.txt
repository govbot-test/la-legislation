HLS 26RS-1213 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE BRAUD
INSURANCE: Provides relative to the Stated Value Homeowner's Policy Act
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 22:1346.5(B)(1), relative to the Stated Value Homeowner's
3 Policy Act; to provide for the dissemination of information; and to provide for
4 related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 22:1346.5(B)(1) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
7 §1346.5. Dissemination of information on risks associated with stated value
8 policies; commissioner
9 * * *
10 B.(1) The commissioner shall establish a dedicated section on the
11 department's website for consumers to access the informational materials described
12 in this Section. and shall distribute the informational materials to homeowners at
13 least once every two years through either direct mail, email, or other means deemed
14 appropriate by the commissioner.
15 * * *

HB 825 Original 2026 Regular Session Braud
Abstract: Removes the distribution of informational materials to homeowners requirement.
Present law requires the commissioner of insurance to distribute informational materials to
homeowners.
Proposed law removes the distribution of informational materials to homeowners
requirement.
(Amends R.S. 22:1346.5(B)(1))
