HEALTH/HOSPITALS DEPT. Establishes a Perinatal Bereavement Care Initiative within
the Louisiana Department of Health. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact the heading of Part XII-A of Chapter 5-B of Title 40 of the Louisiana
3 Revised Statutes of 1950 and to enact R.S. 40:1124.3, relative to perinatal care; to
4 provide for the Perinatal Bereavement Care Initiative; to provide for the duties of the
5 Louisiana Department of Health; to provide for funding; and to provide for related
6 matters.
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
8 Section 1. The heading of Part XII-A of Chapter 5-B of Title 40 of the Louisiana
9 Revised Statutes of 1950 is hereby amended and reenacted and R.S. 40:1124.3 is hereby
10 enacted to read as follows:
11 PART XII-A. PERINATAL PHYSICAL HEALTH POLICY
12 * * *
13 §1124.3. Perinatal bereavement care
14 A. There is hereby established within the Louisiana Department of
15 Health, office on women's health and community health, the Perinatal
16 Bereavement Care Initiative. The department shall administer the initiative to
17 improve access to and the quality of perinatal bereavement care in the state.

1 B. The office shall coordinate with the Commission on Perinatal Care
2 and Prevention of Infant Mortality and nonprofit organizations to provide the
3 following resources to hospitals that provide maternity care:
4 (1) Training for hospital personnel on interacting with parents and
5 family members following an intrauterine fetal demise, neonatal death, or
6 stillbirth.
7 (2) Perinatal bereavement devices.
8 (3) Identifying available perinatal bereavement services for parents and
9 family members, including grief counseling and support groups.
10 C. The office shall prioritize hospitals that lack access to a perinatal
11 bereavement device, treat a high number of high-risk maternal patients, and
12 deliver a high number of babies.
13 D. The department may seek and receive voluntary monies from any
14 sources, including federal funds, gifts, grants, and donations, which shall be
15 expended for the purposes provided for in this Section.

DIGEST
SB 32 Original 2026 Regular Session McMath
Proposed law establishes a Perinatal Bereavement Care Initiative within the La. Dept. of
Health, office on women's health and community health.
Proposed law requires the office to coordinate with the Commission on Perinatal Care and
Prevention of Infant Mortality and nonprofit organizations to provide perinatal bereavement
resources to hospitals that provide maternity care.
Proposed law requires the office to prioritize hospitals that lack access to a perinatal
bereavement device, treat a high number of high-risk maternal patients, and deliver a high
number of babies.
Proposed law authorizes the La. Dept. of Health to seek and receive federal funds, gifts,
grants, and donations for purposes of implementing proposed law.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Amends the heading of Part XII-A of Ch. 5-B of Title 40 of the La. Revised Statutes of
1950; adds R.S. 40:1124.3)
