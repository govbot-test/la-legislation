HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 116
BY REPRESENTATIVE WYBLE
A RESOLUTION
Association of Independent Schools (MAIS) 2026 Class 3A state championship.
WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize the Ben's Ford Christian School girls
basketball team for their outstanding season and to commend the team on winning the MAIS
2026 Class 3A state championship; and
WHEREAS, Ben's Ford Christian School is a private educational institution that was
founded in 1983 and provides a comprehensive education rooted in Christian values; and
WHEREAS, this year's girls basketball team became the first team in school history
to play for and win a state championship; ten years ago, the girls basketball team finished
with a record of zero wins and twenty-five losses, and this year, the squad finished with
twenty-five wins and seven losses; and
WHEREAS, the Ben's Ford Christian School girls basketball team is led by Coaches
Mark McGehee and Derek Dantin, and the team's statistician is Mitchell Castleberry; and
WHEREAS, the members of the MAIS Class 3A state champion girls basketball
team are Avery Bell, Scarlet Barber, Zoe Nastasia, Abby Robichaux, Kinley Stewart,
Matilda Golby, Annabelle Knight, Delaney Perrette, Avery Dantin, Alivia Harrington,
Rebecca Cyuzuzo, Zoe Houston, and Maddie Willis; and
WHEREAS, the players, coaches, and staff of the Ben's Ford Christian School girls
basketball team are most deserving of the highest recognition for their incredible
determination and outstanding achievements this season.

HR NO. 116 ENROLLED
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend the Ben's Ford Christian School girls
basketball team on winning the Midsouth Association of Independent Schools 2026 Class
3A state championship and does hereby encourage the players, coaches, and team staff to
continue their excellent work and always strive for greatness in competition.
