HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 67
BY REPRESENTATIVE LACOMBE
CONDOLENCES: Expresses the condolences of the House of Representatives on the death
of Tyler Marie Grezaffi
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To express the condolences of the House of Representatives on the death of Tyler Marie
3 Grezaffi.
4 WHEREAS, it is with immense sadness and profound regret that the House of
5 Representatives of the Legislature of Louisiana has learned of the tragic passing, on
6 March 18, 2026, of Tyler Marie Grezaffi; and
7 WHEREAS, Tyler Marie Grezaffi was born on July 14, 1998, in Baton Rouge,
8 Louisiana, to Scott and Marie Grezaffi; and
9 WHEREAS, Tyler was a dedicated law enforcement and corrections professional;
10 she served as a deputy with the Pointe Coupee and Iberville Parish Sheriff's Offices, and at
11 the time of her passing, was employed as a master sergeant at the Louisiana State
12 Penitentiary; and
13 WHEREAS, Tyler was known for her compassion, professionalism, and commitment
14 to public service, and she touched the lives of many throughout her promising career; and
15 WHEREAS, Tyler enjoyed new adventures with her fiancé Brandon, going on the
16 Grezaffi girls' trips, spending New Years Eve with family at Uncle PJ's Lodge in Sicily
17 Island, and serving as an investigator at the Louisiana State Penitentiary; and
18 WHEREAS, Tyler is survived by her loving father, Scott Grezaffi (DeeDee); mother,
19 Marie Grezaffi; sister, Shannon Grezaffi Legendre (Eric); brother, Ryan Grezaffi; Nana,
20 Toni Foret Grezaffi; Nanny, Andi Grezaffi Perkins; uncle, Paul "PJ" Perkins; cousin, Darby
