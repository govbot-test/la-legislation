2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 32
BY SENATOR JACKSON-ANDREWS
A RESOLUTION
To commend the members of the Louisiana Athletic Trainers Association (LATA) for their
dedicated hard work on behalf of the athletes of Louisiana and to designate March
24, 2026, as LATA Day at the Louisiana State Capitol.
WHEREAS, Louisiana Athletic Trainers Association members are skilled healthcare
professionals trained in the prevention, emergency management, evaluation, and therapeutic
intervention of sports-related injuries and conditions; and
WHEREAS, the mission statement of the national association is to represent, engage,
and foster the continued growth and development of the athletic training profession and
athletic trainers as unique healthcare providers; and
WHEREAS, athletic training is an allied healthcare profession recognized by the
American Medical Association, and certification in the profession requires strict educational
standards based on specific knowledge and skills; certified athletic trainers have a long
history of providing quality healthcare for athletes and individuals engaged in physical
activity; and
WHEREAS, the hard work of the men and women of the LATA has allowed citizens
of Louisiana to participate in meaningful and rewarding athletic competitions throughout the
state and nation.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Louisiana Senate does hereby commend
the members of the Louisiana Athletic Trainers Association for their outstanding
achievements, does hereby recognize and record for posterity their unwavering commitment
to excellence in service to the citizens of the state; does hereby extend enduring appreciation
for the pride and honor they bring to Louisiana, and does hereby designate March 24, 2026,
as Louisiana Athletic Trainers Association Day at the Louisiana State Capitol.
