SLS 26RS-4106 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 139
BY SENATOR JACKSON-ANDREWS
SPECIAL DAY/WEEK/MONTH. Recognizes Wednesday, May 20, 2026, as White Coat
Wednesday at the Louisiana State Capitol.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To recognize Wednesday, May 20, 2026, as White Coat Wednesday at the Louisiana State
3 Capitol.
4 WHEREAS, cities, towns, communities, and residents across the state of Louisiana
5 are proud of the state's physicians for their commitment, dedication, and personal sacrifice
6 as they strive to provide comprehensive and compassionate medical care to all Louisianians;
7 and
8 WHEREAS, society owes a debt of gratitude to physicians for their sympathy and
9 compassion in ministering to the sick and in alleviating human suffering; and
10 WHEREAS, society commends physicians for their contributions in enlarging the
11 reservoir of scientific knowledge, increasing the number of scientific tools, and expanding
12 the ability of health care professionals to use knowledge and tools effectively in the
13 never-ending fight against disease; and
14 WHEREAS, Louisiana physicians continue to play a vital and significant role in
15 caring for families and individuals; and
16 WHEREAS, White Coat days allow physicians to discuss healthcare issues with
17 legislators and relay the issues that affect their patients on a daily basis.
18 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
