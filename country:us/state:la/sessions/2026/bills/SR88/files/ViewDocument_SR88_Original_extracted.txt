2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 88
BY SENATOR HODGES
Day at the Louisiana State Capitol.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To recognize Monday, April 20, 2026, as Livingston Parish Day at the Louisiana State
3 Capitol.
4 WHEREAS, Livingston Parish is a proud and rapidly growing parish in the state of
5 Louisiana, known for its strong communities, deep-rooted values, and unwavering spirit; and
6 WHEREAS, the citizens of Livingston Parish consistently demonstrate resilience,
7 hard work, and a commitment to faith, family, and service, which contribute to the strength
8 and stability of the state as a whole; and
9 WHEREAS, Livingston Parish plays a vital role in Louisiana's economy through its
10 workforce, small businesses, and continued development, while maintaining a culture that
11 reflects the heritage and traditions of this state; and
12 WHEREAS, faced with challenges, including infrastructure, flood risks, and rapid
13 community growth, the leadership of Livingston Parish has displayed great fortitude and
14 tenacity in addressing these challenges while continuing to invest in its future; and
15 WHEREAS, Livingston Parish Day at the Louisiana State Capitol provides an
16 opportunity to recognize the achievements, contributions, and residents of Livingston Parish
17 and to celebrate its continued progress.
18 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
