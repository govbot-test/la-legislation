Section 1:
2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
BY SENATOR MCMATH
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 36:251(C)(1), 254(B)(1)(a)(i), 257(A), and 258(A) and (B) and
3 R.S. 46:450.3(A) and to enact R.S. 36:253.1, 258(N), and 258.1, relative to the
4 Louisiana Department of Health; to create the office of health and nutrition within
5 the Louisiana Department of Health; to provide for purposes, duties, and functions
6 of the office; to provide for an executive director of the office; to provide for offices
7 under the direct supervision of the secretary; to provide for an effective date; and to
8 provide for related matters.
9 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
10 Section 1. R.S. 36:251(C)(1), 254(B)(1)(a)(i), 257(A), and 258(A) and (B) are hereby
11 amended and reenacted and R.S. 36:253.1, 258(N), and 258.1 are hereby enacted to read as
12 follows:
13 §251. Louisiana Department of Health; creation; domicile; composition; purpose
14 and functions
15 * * *
16 C.(1) The Louisiana Department of Health shall be composed of the
17 executive office of the secretary, the office of the surgeon general, the office of
18 management and finance, the office of public health, the office of behavioral health,
19 the office for citizens with developmental disabilities, the office of aging and adult
20 services, the office on women's health and community health, the office of health
21 and nutrition, and such other offices as shall be created by law.
22 * * *
23 §253.1. Executive office of the secretary; certain offices
24 A. The provisions of this Section shall apply to the following offices
25 within the Louisiana Department of Health:
26 (1) The executive office of the secretary.
27 (2) The office of health and nutrition.
28 B. The offices shall be under the direct supervision, direction, and

1 control of the secretary.
2 C. The duties and functions of these offices shall be determined by the
3 secretary and shall be exercised under the direct supervision and control of the
4 secretary.
5 §254. Powers and duties of the secretary of the Louisiana Department of Health
6 * * *
7 B. The secretary shall have authority to:
8 (1)(a) Except as otherwise specifically provided in R.S. 36:801 and 803:
9 (i) Employ, appoint, remove, assign, and promote such personnel as is
10 necessary for the efficient administration of the executive office of the secretary and
11 the office of health and nutrition and the performance of its the offices' powers,
12 duties, functions, and responsibilities and such other personnel, who are not assigned
13 to an office, as may be necessary for the efficient administration of the department
14 and for the performance of the responsibilities, powers, duties, and functions of
15 agencies transferred to it.
16 * * *
17 §257. Assistant secretaries
18 A. Each office within the Louisiana Department of Health, except the
19 executive office of the secretary, the office of management and finance, the office
20 of health and nutrition, and the office of the surgeon general, shall be under the
21 immediate supervision and direction of an assistant secretary, who shall be appointed
22 by the governor with consent of the Senate. Each shall serve at the pleasure of the
23 governor and shall be paid a salary which shall be fixed by the governor, which
24 salary shall not exceed the amount approved for such position by the legislature
25 while in session.
26 * * *
27 §258. Offices; purposes and functions
28 A. The purposes for which the offices of the Louisiana Department of Health,
29 other than the executive office of the secretary and the office of management
30 and finance, are created shall be set forth in this Section.

1 B. The office of public health shall perform the functions of the state which
2 relate to the general health of the people of the state, including but not limited to
3 responsibility for the preparation and supervision of the Sanitary Code, local health
4 units, sewage treatment and disposal within the state, physical fitness, supplemental
5 food programs for women, infants, and children, emergency medical services, and
6 related functions affecting the public health. It shall also perform those functions of
7 the state provided by law relating to environmental quality and pollution control
8 which are related to the public health and which are specifically assigned to the
9 department, including but not limited to functions relating to the treatment and
10 disposal of sewage within the state, with the exception of those functions assigned
11 by law to the Department of Environmental Quality including, but not limited to, the
12 licensing of sewage sludge transporters or haulers.
13 * * *
14 N. The office of health and nutrition shall be responsible for leading and
15 coordinating efforts within the Louisiana Department of Health that are
16 intended to promote health and nutrition, healthcare access, preventative
17 healthcare services, chronic disease prevention and management programs,
18 physical fitness, and health outcomes, including supplemental nutrition
19 assistance services and commodity programs for women, infants, and children.
20 §258.1. Office of health and nutrition
21 A. The department shall have an executive director for the office of
22 health and nutrition that reports directly to the secretary.
23 B. The office of health and nutrition shall be responsible for:
24 (1) Leading and coordinating the Governor's Council on Physical Fitness
25 and Sports, the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program for Women,
26 Infants, and Children (WIC), and the Commodity Supplemental Food Program
27 (CSFP).
28 (2) Developing and strengthening policy to increase access to and
29 consumption of healthy foods among recipients of the Supplemental Nutrition
30 Assistance Program (SNAP).

1 (3) Leading and coordinating department initiatives that include
2 addressing health and nutrition, healthcare access, preventative healthcare
3 services, and chronic disease prevention and management programs.
4 (4) Coordinating with the office of aging and adult services and the office
5 of public health to provide public education on risk reduction strategies for
6 Alzheimer's and related dementias, including the diseases' links to other chronic
7 diseases.
8 Section 2. R.S. 46:450.3(A) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
9 §450.3. WIC participants; homeless
10 A. The office of public health health and nutrition within the Louisiana
11 Department of Health shall adopt rules and regulations for the WIC program in
12 accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act to provide nonperishable food
13 items to women and children. Such rules shall provide for the distribution of WIC
14 vouchers, in small quantities on a frequent basis, for food items that do not require
15 refrigeration or storage to eligible WIC participants that have been identified by
16 qualified organizations as being homeless. Such rules shall comply with
17 requirements of federal laws and regulations in order to ensure receipt of federal
18 funding for the program.
19 * * *
20 Section 3. The Louisiana Department of Health is authorized to transfer funding and
21 positions within the department to establish and implement the office of health and nutrition.
22 Section 4. This Act shall become effective on October 1, 2026.
