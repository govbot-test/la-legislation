HLS 26RS-4921 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 289
BY REPRESENTATIVE MACK
policies and practices related to the redaction of proposed well site locations
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To urge and request the Department of Conservation and Energy to review policies and
3 practices related to the redaction of proposed well site locations.
4 WHEREAS, pursuant to R.S. 30:1 et seq., the Department of Conservation and
5 Energy is charged with regulating the siting, construction, and operation of wells; and
6 WHEREAS, the department is required to provide the public and interested parties
7 with an opportunity to participate in the permitting process; and
8 WHEREAS, a recent trend has emerged among Class VI permit applications that
9 include a request from the applicant that proposed well site locations not be made public;
10 and
11 WHEREAS, a prior version of the Louisiana Geologic Storage of Carbon Dioxide
12 Act contained a provision, R.S. 30:1104(F), which provided for confidentiality of
13 information contained in Class VI permit applications; and
14 WHEREAS, this provision was repealed by Act 458 of the 2025 Regular Session;
15 and
16 WHEREAS, R.S. 44:3.2 regarding proprietary and trade secret information is also
17 cited by applicants as justification for requesting the redaction of proposed well sites; and
Page 1 of 3
HLS 26RS-4921 ORIGINAL
HR NO. 289
1 WHEREAS, the department has previously notified Class VI applicants that the
2 location of their proposed Class VI wells should not be claimed as confidential and that the
3 department plans to upload the well locations on its website; and
4 WHEREAS, the department requires that the Class VI well location be publicly
5 available prior to progressing its review of a Class VI application towards the required public
6 notice and comment period that precedes the department's permit decision; and
7 WHEREAS, the department continues to look into ways to make the well locations
8 within Class VI applications publicly available; and
9 WHEREAS, the redaction of proposed well sites for any class of permit prevents the
10 public and parties who may have a direct interest in the site from being able to participate
11 in the permitting process; and
12 WHEREAS, public notice, hearing, and comment periods required by law cannot
13 perform their intended function if the public is not permitted to know about a proposed well
14 site; and
15 WHEREAS, it is imperative that the department review the current laws, regulations,
16 and policies regarding confidentiality of information contained in well permit applications
17 as well as the practices of permit applicants so that the department may effectively carry out
18 its duties and the public can participate in an informed way.
19 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
20 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby urge and request the Department of Conservation and
21 Energy to review all applicable and relevant laws, regulations, policies, and practices
22 regarding the redaction of proposed well sites in permit applications and to submit a written
23 report of its findings to the House Committee on Natural Resources and Environment,
24 together with any recommendations for legislative action required to allow the department
25 to carry out its mandates and the public to have a meaningful opportunity to participate in
26 the permitting process by March 1, 2027.
27 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
28 secretary of the Department of Conservation and Energy.
