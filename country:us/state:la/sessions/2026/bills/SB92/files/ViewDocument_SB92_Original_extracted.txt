DIGEST
SB 92 Original 2026 Regular Session Mizell
Present law provides that state police will create and operate a statewide sexual assault
collection kit tracking system and that all entities, including law enforcement and healthcare
providers having custody of sexual assault collection kits, will provide certain information
to the tracking system and fully participate in it.
Proposed law requires the district attorney to submit sexual assault collection kits to the
tracking system.
Proposed law otherwise retains present law.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 15:624.1(D))
