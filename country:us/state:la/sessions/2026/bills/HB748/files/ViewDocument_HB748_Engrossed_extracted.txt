BY REPRESENTATIVE BRAUD
HIGHWAYS: Provides that any Louisiana school board-owned vehicles be exempt from
paying tolls on any bridges and highways in the state
1 AN ACT
2 To amend R.S. 17:157 (A) and (B), relative to toll exemptions on ferries, bridges, and roads
3 for all Louisiana school board-owned or leased vehicles; to provide for the free
4 passage of all Louisiana school board-owned or leased vehicles over all public tolled
5 ferries operated by the state; to provide for the free passage of all Louisiana school
6 board-owned or leased vehicles over all public tolled roads and bridges operated by
7 the state; to provide for an effective date; and to provide for related matters.
8 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
9 Section 1. R.S. 17:157(A) and (B) are hereby enacted to read as follows:
10 §157. Ferries, roads, and toll bridges; free passage to students; free passage to
11 school board-owned or leased vehicles
12 A. The free right of passage over all public ferries which are leased out or
13 controlled by the state, parish, or municipality, excluding the Greater New Orleans
14 Expressway, for which license is paid or toll exacted, shall provide for free passage
15 of all students in their passage to and from school between the hours of 6:00 a.m. and
16 9:30 a.m., and between 2:30 p.m. and 9:30 p.m., and any other vehicle owned, or
17 leased, and operated by Louisiana school boards, provided that rules are adopted in
18 accordance with the provisions of Subsection C of this Section.

HB 748 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Braud
Abstract: Exempts any motor vehicle owned or leased by Louisiana school boards from
paying tolls on all ferries, bridges, and roads in Louisiana, except the Greater New
Orleans Expressway.
Present law requires free right of passage over all public tolled ferries which are leased out
and controlled by the state, parish, or municipality, excluding the Greater N.O. Expressway,
to all students in their passage to and from school between 6:00 a.m and 9:30 a.m. and
between 2:30 p.m. and 9:30 p.m., provided that rules are adopted in accordance with present
law.
Proposed law retains present law and requires that all La. school board-owned or leased
vehicles traveling over all public tolled ferries operated by the state have free right of
passage. Maintains the exception applicable to the Greater N.O. Expressway.
Present law requires free right of passage over all public tolled bridges and roads which are
leased out and controlled by the state, parish, or municipality, excluding the Greater N.O.
Expressway, to all students in their passage to and from school between 6:00 a.m and 9:30
a.m. and between 2:30 p.m. and 9:30 p.m., provided that rules are adopted in accordance
with present law.
