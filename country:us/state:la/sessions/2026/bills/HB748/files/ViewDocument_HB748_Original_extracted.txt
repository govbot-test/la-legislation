17 operated by Louisiana school boards, provided that rules are adopted in accordance
18 with the provisions of Subsection C of this Section.

HB 748 Original 2026 Regular Session Braud
Abstract: Exempts any motor vehicle owned by Louisiana school boards from paying tolls
on all bridges and highways in Louisiana, except the Greater New Orleans
Expressway.
Present law requires free right of passage over all public tolled ferries which are leased out
and controlled by the state, parish, or municipality, excluding the Greater N.O. Expressway,
to all students in their passage to and from school between 6:00 a.m and 9:30 a.m. and
between 2:30 p.m. and 9:30 p.m., provided that rules are adopted in accordance with present
law.
Proposed law retains present law and requires that all La. school board-owned vehicles
traveling over all public tolled ferries operated by the state have free right of passage.
Maintains the exception applicable to the Greater N.O. Expressway.
Present law requires free right of passage over all public tolled bridges and roads which are
leased out and controlled by the state, parish, or municipality, excluding the Greater N.O.
Expressway, to all students in their passage to and from school between 6:00 a.m and 9:30
a.m. and between 2:30 p.m. and 9:30 p.m., provided that rules are adopted in accordance
with present law.
Proposed law retains present law and requires that all La. school board-owned vehicles
traveling over all public tolled bridges and roads operated by the state have free right of
passage.
(Amends R.S. 17:157(A) and (B))
