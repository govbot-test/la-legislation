SLS 26RS-163 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE BILL NO. 55
BY SENATOR STINE
MOTOR VEHICLES. Provides for clarification on the proper way to wear a safety belt.
(8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 32:295.1(A)(1), relative to seatbelts; to provide for the proper
3 way to wear a seatbelt; and to provide for related matters.
4 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
5 Section 1. R.S. 32:295.1(A)(1) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
6 §295.1. Safety belt use; tags indicating exemption
7 A.(1) Each driver of a passenger car, van, sports utility vehicle, or truck
8 having a gross weight of twenty-six thousand pounds or less in this state shall have
9 a safety belt properly fastened about his or her body and worn across both the lap
10 and shoulder as designed, and not placed behind the body or under the arm at
11 all times when the vehicle is in forward motion. The provisions of this Section shall
12 not apply to those cars, vans, sports utility vehicles, or pickups manufactured prior
13 to January 1, 1981.

DIGEST
SB 55 Original 2026 Regular Session Stine
Present law provides for a seat belt to be properly fastened. Proposed law adds clarifying
language that the seatbelt shall be worn as designed.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 32:295.1(A)(1))
