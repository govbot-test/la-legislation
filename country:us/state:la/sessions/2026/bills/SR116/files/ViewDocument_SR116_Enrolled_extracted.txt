2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 116
BY SENATOR KLEINPETER
A RESOLUTION
To express the sincere condolences of the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana upon the
untimely passing of Tyler Marie Grezaffi.
WHEREAS, it is with immense sadness and profound regret that the Senate of the
Legislature of Louisiana has learned of the tragic passing, on March 18, 2026, of Tyler
Marie Grezaffi; and
WHEREAS, Tyler Marie Grezaffi was born on July 14, 1998, in Baton Rouge,
Louisiana, to Scott and Marie Grezaffi; and
WHEREAS, Tyler was a dedicated law enforcement and corrections professional;
she served as a deputy with the Pointe Coupee and Iberville Parish Sheriff's offices, and at
the time of her passing, was employed as a master sergeant at the Louisiana State
Penitentiary; and
WHEREAS, Tyler was known for her compassion, professionalism, and commitment
to public service, and she touched the lives of many throughout her promising career; and
WHEREAS, Tyler was at the beginning of her promising career and while she had
not worked very long at the Louisiana State Penitentiary, her dedication, energy, and
kindness have left an unforgettable mark on everyone she encountered; and
WHEREAS, Tyler enjoyed new adventures with her fiancé Brandon, going on the
Grezaffi girls' trips, spending New Years Eve with family at Uncle PJ's Lodge in Sicily
Island, and serving as an investigator at the Louisiana State Penitentiary; and
WHEREAS, Tyler is survived by her loving father, Scott Grezaffi (DeeDee); mother,
Marie Grezaffi; sister, Shannon Grezaffi Legendre (Eric); brother, Ryan Grezaffi; Nana,
Toni Foret Grezaffi; Nanny, Andi Grezaffi Perkins; uncle, Paul "PJ" Perkins; cousin, Darby
McIntosh Carriere (Nick); nephew, Bishop Greene; niece, Kullen Greene; and fiancé,
Brandon Blackburn; and
WHEREAS, Tyler Marie Grezaffi was a truly beautiful soul and dedicated public
servant whose memory shall live in perpetuity in the hearts and minds of those who were
privileged to have known and loved her.

THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
does hereby express heartfelt condolences on the passing of Tyler Marie Grezaffi and does
hereby extend our sincere wishes that her family, friends, and loved ones find comfort in
their shared memories of her beautiful life.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
family of Tyler Marie Grezaffi.
