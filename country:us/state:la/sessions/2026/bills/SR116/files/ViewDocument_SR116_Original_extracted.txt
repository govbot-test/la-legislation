SLS 26RS-2613 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 116
BY SENATOR KLEINPETER
CONDOLENCES. Expresses the condolences of the Senate on the death of Tyler Marie
Grezaffi.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To express the sincere condolences of the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana upon the
3 untimely passing of Tyler Marie Grezaffi.
4 WHEREAS, it is with immense sadness and profound regret that the Senate of the
5 Legislature of Louisiana has learned of the tragic passing, on March 18, 2026, of Tyler
6 Marie Grezaffi; and
7 WHEREAS, Tyler Marie Grezaffi was born on July 14, 1998, in Baton Rouge,
8 Louisiana, to Scott and Marie Grezaffi; and
9 WHEREAS, Tyler was a dedicated law enforcement and corrections professional;
10 she served as a deputy with the Pointe Coupee and Iberville Parish Sheriff's offices, and at
11 the time of her passing, was employed as a master sergeant at the Louisiana State
12 Penitentiary; and
13 WHEREAS, Tyler was known for her compassion, professionalism, and commitment
14 to public service, and she touched the lives of many throughout her promising career; and
15 WHEREAS, Tyler was at the beginning of her promising career and while she had
16 not worked very long at the Louisiana State Penitentiary, her dedication, energy, and
17 kindness have left an unforgettable mark on everyone she encountered; and
18 WHEREAS, Tyler enjoyed new adventures with her fiancé Brandon, going on the

11 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
12 does hereby express heartfelt condolences on the passing of Tyler Marie Grezaffi and does
13 hereby extend our sincere wishes that her family, friends, and loved ones find comfort in
14 their shared memories of her beautiful life.
15 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
16 family of Tyler Marie Grezaffi.
