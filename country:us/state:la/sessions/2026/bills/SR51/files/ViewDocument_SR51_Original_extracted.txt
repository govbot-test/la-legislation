SLS 26RS-1416 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 51
BY SENATOR EDMONDS
Republican Women Day at the Louisiana State Capitol.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend the Louisiana Federation of Republican Women and designate March 31,
3 2026, as Louisiana Federation of Republican Women Day at the Louisiana State
4 Capitol.
5 WHEREAS, the Louisiana Federation of Republican Women (LFRW) is a member
6 of the National Federation of Republican Women, which represents over one hundred
7 thousand Republican women throughout the fifty states; and
8 WHEREAS, chartered in 1953, LFRW is the largest volunteer women's political
9 organization in the state with thirty-five local clubs statewide and more than one thousand
10 eight hundred members; and
11 WHEREAS, LFRW is a grassroots organization that meets regularly at local,
12 regional, and statewide conferences and conventions; and
13 WHEREAS, LFRW develops and organizes efforts and initiatives that promote,
14 support, and preserve the conservative ideals and principles of the Republican Party at all
15 levels of government; and
16 WHEREAS, the objectives of LFRW are to promote an informed public through
17 political education and activity; increase the effectiveness among women in the cause of
18 good government; encourage and facilitate cooperation and communication among local

1 clubs, their members and the LFRW; foster loyalty to the Republican Party and promote its
2 principles and candidates in all elections; and support policies of the Republican National
3 Committee and work for the election of the national Republican Party nominee; and
4 WHEREAS, the members of the LFRW merit a special measure of commendation
5 for their outstanding accomplishments, including their tireless work as participants in the
6 democratic process, their focused dedication to the democratic process, and their continuing
7 efforts to ensure that citizens are aware of that process and participate in it.
8 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
9 does hereby commend the Louisiana Federation of Republican Women and designates
10 March 31, 2026, as Louisiana Federation of Republican Women Day at the Louisiana State
11 Capitol.
12 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to Roby
13 Dyer, President of the Louisiana Federation of Republican Women.
