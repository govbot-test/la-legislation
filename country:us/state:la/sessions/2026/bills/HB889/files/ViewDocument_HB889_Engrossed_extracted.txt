HB 889 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Orgeron
Abstract: Provides for a bulk harvest tagging process for alternative oyster cultivation.
Present law provides that oysters and other molluscan species taken from La. waters for sale
which are sacked or otherwise containerized while on board the fishing vessel must be
tagged prior to removal from the vessel, with a tag that includes all information required by
the La. Dept. of Health, office of public health, and the Dept. of Wildlife and Fisheries.
Present law provides that all oysters and other molluscan species which are to be sacked or
otherwise containerized while in the shell at dockside must be containerized and tagged
immediately upon arrival at the dock prior to shipment.
Proposed law retains present law requirements for the tag information but requires the dept.
to establish a bulk tagging process for alternative oyster cultivation which may be utilized
to represent all oysters on board the vessel between the harvest area and the dockside
facility.
(Amends R.S. 56:449(C))
