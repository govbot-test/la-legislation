2026 Regular Session ACT No. 378 ENROLLED
SENATE BILL NO. 290
BY SENATOR ABRAHAM
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 17:3911(C)(5), relative to the data collection system; to provide
3 relative to the process required for the state Department of Education to change
4 certain information assigned to a student; to provide for an effective date; and to
5 provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 17:3911(C)(5) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
8 §3911. Data collection system; establishment
9 * * *
10 C. The department shall:
11 * * *
12 (5) Obtain concurrence of the city, parish, or other local public school system
13 prior to changing the assigning an exit code to a student that differs from the exit
14 code assigned to a student by the school system.
15 * * *
16 Section 2. This Act shall become effective upon signature by the governor or, if not
17 signed by the governor, upon expiration of the time for bills to become law without signature
18 by the governor, as provided by Article III, Section 18 of the Constitution of Louisiana. If
19 vetoed by the governor and subsequently approved by the legislature, this Act shall become
20 effective on the day following such approval.
