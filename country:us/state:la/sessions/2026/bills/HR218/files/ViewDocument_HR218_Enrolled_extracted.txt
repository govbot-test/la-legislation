HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 218
BY REPRESENTATIVE WALTERS
A RESOLUTION
To commend Shreveport native EJ Fields for his historic achievements in gospel music.
WHEREAS, EJ Fields is a native of Shreveport, Louisiana, who has demonstrated
exceptional talent, dedication, and faith through his contributions to gospel music; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Fields's single, "Mercy Endureth", reached the top position on both
the Billboard and Mediabase Gospel Airplay Charts, which marks a significant national
accomplishment; and
WHEREAS, the single maintained the top position for two consecutive weeks, from
March 29 through April 12, 2026, which further affirms its widespread impact and resonance
with audiences across the country; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Fields has made history as the first male solo gospel artist from
Shreveport, Louisiana, to achieve this extraordinary milestone, and his achievement has
brought honor and recognition to the city and its rich musical heritage; and
WHEREAS, the success of Mr. Fields serves as an inspiration to aspiring artists and
young people throughout the community and demonstrates the power of faith, perseverance,
and commitment to excellence; and
WHEREAS, the city of Shreveport and the state of Louisiana take great pride in
celebrating EJ Fields and his accomplishments, which place the community on a national
stage and contribute positively to its cultural and spiritual legacy.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend EJ Fields for his historic achievements in
gospel music and does hereby express enduring appreciation for his contributions to uplifting
communities through his ministry and artistry.
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
