HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 218
BY REPRESENTATIVE WALTERS
COMMENDATIONS: Commends Shreveport native EJ Fields for his historic
achievements in gospel music
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Shreveport native EJ Fields for his historic achievements in gospel music.
3 WHEREAS, EJ Fields is a native of Shreveport, Louisiana, who has demonstrated
4 exceptional talent, dedication, and faith through his contributions to gospel music; and
5 WHEREAS, Mr. Fields's single, "Mercy Endureth", reached the top position on both
6 the Billboard and Mediabase Gospel Airplay Charts, which marks a significant national
7 accomplishment; and
8 WHEREAS, the single maintained the top position for two consecutive weeks, from
9 March 29 through April 12, 2026, which further affirms its widespread impact and resonance
10 with audiences across the country; and
11 WHEREAS, Mr. Fields has made history as the first male solo gospel artist from
12 Shreveport, Louisiana, to achieve this extraordinary milestone, and his achievement has
13 brought honor and recognition to the city and its rich musical heritage; and
14 WHEREAS, the success of Mr. Fields serves as an inspiration to aspiring artists and
15 young people throughout the community and demonstrates the power of faith, perseverance,
16 and commitment to excellence; and
17 WHEREAS, the city of Shreveport and the state of Louisiana take great pride in
18 celebrating EJ Fields and his accomplishments, which place the community on a national
19 stage and contribute positively to its cultural and spiritual legacy.
20 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
21 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend EJ Fields for his historic achievements in
