HLS 26RS-5320 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 318
BY REPRESENTATIVE WALTERS
WATER/DRINKING WATER: Urges and requests the city of Shreveport to report
quarterly updates to the House Committee on Natural Resources and Environment
regarding the measures it has taken to address its failing grade for water issued by
the Department of Health
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To urge and request the city of Shreveport to provide quarterly reports to the House
3 Committee on Natural Resources and Environment of the Legislature of Louisiana
4 and provide updates to the public regarding its efforts to improve the water quality
5 in Shreveport.
6 WHEREAS, Shreveport's water system recently received an "F" grade (45/100) from
7 the Louisiana Department of Health (LDH) for 2026, marking the third consecutive year of
8 failing grades; and
9 WHEREAS, the city of Shreveport's water has been under an administrative order
10 issued by LDH for several years to correct deficiencies, ensure safe drinking water, enforce
11 compliance, and address public health concerns; and
12 WHEREAS, Shreveport's water system received the biggest point deductions for
13 infrastructure deficiencies noted during inspections, which the report says could lead to
14 unsafe drinking water and possible water service disruption; and
15 WHEREAS, LDH's Office of Public Health ordered Shreveport to repair flow meters,
16 sensors, and actuators, which are all components that help control the flow of water; and
17 WHEREAS, citizens in the Shreveport area are concerned about the safety of the
18 water for drinking, bathing, and washing clothes and dishes and are requesting information
19 on the status of the repairs being made to the water system; and

1 WHEREAS, despite the proximity in location, Bossier City received a grade of "A"
2 for its water system, as it has consistently in the past few years; and
3 WHEREAS, the city of Shreveport is working on required upgrades, including
4 refurbishing water towers and replacing generators, which have already been ordered
5 according to the mayor, with completion expected by 2027; and
6 WHEREAS, the increased demand on water resources, both by residential and
7 commercial users in the Shreveport area, requires proper functionality of the city's water
8 system and necessitates that efforts be made to improve water infrastructure and quality; and
9 WHEREAS, residents in Shreveport deserve to have clean, safe water to drink, to
10 bathe their children, and to wash their dishes, and consecutive years of failing scores and
11 continuing administrative orders related to deficiencies of the water system show that more
12 oversight is needed into the measures being taken to improve the system.
13 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
14 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby urge and request the city of Shreveport to provide
15 quarterly reports to the House Committee on Natural Resources and Environment for three
16 years beginning in October of 2026 and to provide updates to the public regarding its efforts
17 to improve the water quality in Shreveport.
18 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
19 mayor of the city of Shreveport.
