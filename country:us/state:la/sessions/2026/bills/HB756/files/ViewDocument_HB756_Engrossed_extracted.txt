4 §855.2. Definitions
5 As used in this Part, "personal watercraft" means a vessel which uses an
6 inboard motor powering a water jet pump or other machinery as its primary source

8 or kneeling on the vessel, rather than the conventional manner of sitting or standing
9 inside the vessel.
10 §855.3. Regulation of personal watercraft
11 A. A person shall not operate a personal watercraft unless each person
12 aboard is wearing a type I, type II, type III, or type V personal flotation device
13 approved by the United States Coast Guard. Every person on board a personal
14 watercraft shall wear a personal flotation device and no operator of a personal
15 watercraft shall allow any person who is not wearing a personal flotation device to
16 be on board his personal watercraft. The use of an inflatable personal flotation
17 device while on board of a personal watercraft shall not constitute compliance with
18 this Section.

Abstract: Provides relative to personal watercraft.
Present law (R.S. 34:851.4) provides for the careless operation of a watercraft.
Proposed law retains present law generally and changes the phrase "right-of-way" to "give
way". Further changes the term "collision" to "boating incident" relative to approaching a
vessel.
Present law provides for a reference to non-motored powered boats relative to the duty of
motorboats to yield.
