Section 1:
2026 Regular Session ACT No. 245 ENROLLED
BY SENATOR FESI
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 48:21(A) and to enact R.S. 48:1(25) and 23.1, relative to the
3 preservation of bridges by the Department of Transportation and Development; to
4 provide for terms and definitions; to provide for functions and responsibilities of the
5 department; to provide for reports to the legislature; and to provide for related
6 matters.
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
8 Section 1. R.S. 48:21(A) is hereby amended and reenacted and R.S. 48:1(25) and
9 23.1 are hereby enacted to read as follows:
10 §1. Terms defined
11 For purposes of this Chapter, the following terms have the meanings ascribed
12 to them by this Section, except where the context clearly indicates otherwise:
13 * * *
14 (25) "Bridges" means bridges, overpasses, culverts, and related
15 structures, together with their structural, load-bearing, safety, and operational
16 components, that are located on or carry roadways designated as part of the
17 state highway system and for which the department has responsibility pursuant
18 to federal and state law.
19 * * *
20 §21. Functions
21 A.(1) The functions of the department shall be to study, administer, construct,
22 improve, maintain, repair, and regulate the use of public transportation systems and
23 to perform such other functions with regard to public highways, roads, and other
Page 1 of 3

1 transportation related facilities as may be conferred on the department by applicable
2 law.
3 (2) The Department of Transportation and Development shall be
4 responsible for the following:
5 (a) The inspection of all bridges that are part of the state highway system
6 or as applicable pursuant to the terms as defined in R.S. 48:1(10) and (11).
7 (b) The operation, maintenance, repair, rehabilitation, preservation, and
8 replacement of all bridges owned by the department.
9 (c) Oversight of maintenance projects aimed at restoring structural
10 integrity, improving safety, and extending the lifespan of bridges, including
11 addressing deterioration, implementing rehabilitation strategies, and managing
12 emergency repairs to ensure bridges remain safe and operational for the public.
13 (d) The responsibility of the department under this Subsection shall not
14 apply to bridges owned or operated by another state agency, a political
15 subdivision, special district, toll authority, or the federal government, unless
16 otherwise expressly provided by law.
17 * * *
18 §23.1. Bridge review and report to the legislature
19 A.(1) The department shall prepare and furnish a report detailing the
20 condition of all bridges maintained, operated or replaced by the department to
21 the Senate Committee on Transportation, Highways and Public Works and the
22 House Committee on Transportation, Highways and Public Works in a joint
23 public meeting of the two committees on or before October 1, 2026.
24 (2) The report shall include at minimum the following:
25 (a) The number of bridges within the department's responsibility as
26 defined in R.S. 48:1.
27 (b) The status of the condition of the bridge.
28 (c) The type of bridge and the parish where it is located.
29 (d) The source of funds to maintain the bridge.
30 (e) The Specifications for the National Bridge Inventory condition rating.

1 B. Beginning January 1, 2027, the department shall present a biannual
2 proposed maintenance plan for the preservation of all bridges maintained by
3 the department to the Senate Committee on Transportation, Highways and
4 Public Works and the House Committee on Transportation, Highways and
5 Public Works in a joint meeting of the two committees.
6 Section 2. The Louisiana State Law Institute is hereby authorized and directed to
7 arrange in alphabetical order and renumber the definitions provided in R.S. 48:1.
