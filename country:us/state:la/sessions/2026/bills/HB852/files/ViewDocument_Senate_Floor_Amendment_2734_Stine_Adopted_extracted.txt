12 §120.391. Income tax checkoff; donation for Holden's Hope
13 A. For tax years beginning on and after January 1, 2023, every
14 individual who files an individual income tax return for the current tax year
15 and who is entitled to a refund may designate on his current year return that
16 all or any portion of the total amount of the refund to which he is entitled
17 shall be donated to Holden's Hope, in lieu of that amount being paid to him
18 as a refund, in which case the refund shall be reduced by the amount so
19 designated. The designation shall be made at the time of the filing of the
20 current year tax return and shall be made on the income tax return form as
21 prescribed by the secretary of the Department of Revenue. Donated monies
22 shall be administered by the secretary and distributed to Holden's Hope in
23 accordance with the provisions of R.S. 47:120.37. No donation made under
24 the provisions of this Section shall be invalid for lack of an authentic act.
25 B. Notwithstanding the limitations of R.S. 47:120.37(B), the
26 designation of the donee shall be reinstated for the tax year beginning on
27 January 1, 2026. Thereafter, if the total amount of tax refunds donated
28 pursuant to this Section is less than five thousand dollars per year for two
29 consecutive years, the designation shall be removed from the individual
30 income tax return."
