ENROLLED
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 43
BY REPRESENTATIVE MIKE JOHNSON
A RESOLUTION
at the Church of Pentecost in Ball, Louisiana.
WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize individuals whose leadership, faith, and
dedication have made a meaningful and lasting impact upon their communities; and
WHEREAS, Bishop Lonnie Weidner faithfully served as lead pastor of the Church
of Pentecost in Ball, Louisiana, for many years and provided spiritual leadership, guidance,
and encouragement to countless individuals and families throughout Rapides Parish and
Central Louisiana; and
WHEREAS, through his years of ministry, Bishop Weidner helped strengthen the
church's mission of faith, fellowship, and community outreach and left a lasting legacy of
service and compassion; and
WHEREAS, Bishop Weidner has been faithfully supported in his ministry by his
wife, Jeana Weidner, whose partnership, devotion, and service have been a blessing to the
congregation and the community; and
WHEREAS, the citizens of Rapides Parish are grateful for the many years of
leadership and spiritual guidance provided by Bishop Weidner and the positive influence that
he and his family have had upon the lives of others.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Bishop Lonnie Weidner for his many years
of faithful ministry and leadership at the Church of Pentecost in Ball, Louisiana, and does
hereby express sincere appreciation for his service to the church and the people of Central
Louisiana.
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
