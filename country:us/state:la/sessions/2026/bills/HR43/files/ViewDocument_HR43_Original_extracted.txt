HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 43
BY REPRESENTATIVE MIKE JOHNSON
leadership at the Church of Pentecost in Ball, Louisiana
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Bishop Lonnie Weidner for his many years of faithful ministry and leadership
3 at the Church of Pentecost in Ball, Louisiana.
4 WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize individuals whose leadership, faith, and
5 dedication have made a meaningful and lasting impact upon their communities; and
6 WHEREAS, Bishop Lonnie Weidner faithfully served as lead pastor of the Church
7 of Pentecost in Ball, Louisiana, for many years and provided spiritual leadership, guidance,
8 and encouragement to countless individuals and families throughout Rapides Parish and
9 Central Louisiana; and
10 WHEREAS, through his years of ministry, Bishop Weidner helped strengthen the
11 church's mission of faith, fellowship, and community outreach and left a lasting legacy of
12 service and compassion; and
13 WHEREAS, Bishop Weidner has been faithfully supported in his ministry by his
14 wife, Jeana Weidner, whose partnership, devotion, and service have been a blessing to the
15 congregation and the community; and
16 WHEREAS, the citizens of Rapides Parish are grateful for the many years of
17 leadership and spiritual guidance provided by Bishop Weidner and the positive influence that
18 he and his family have had upon the lives of others.
19 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
20 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Bishop Lonnie Weidner for his many years
21 of faithful ministry and leadership at the Church of Pentecost in Ball, Louisiana, and does
22 hereby express sincere appreciation for his service to the church and the people of Central
23 Louisiana.
