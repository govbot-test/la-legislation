1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate Monday, April 13, 2026, as Knock Knock Children's Museum Day at the
3 Louisiana State Capitol and to recognize the museum's contributions to early
4 childhood education, family engagement, and economic vitality in Louisiana.
5 WHEREAS, Knock Knock Children's Museum, a nonprofit educational and cultural
6 organization located in BREC's City-Brooks Park in Baton Rouge, opened in August 2017
7 with the support of one million twenty-five thousand dollars in state funding appropriated
8 in 2013; and
9 WHEREAS, the museum is grounded in research demonstrating the critical role of
10 play in early brain development and focuses on unique and impactful, playful learning
11 experiences, and bonding with supportive adults for children from birth through age eight;
12 and
13 WHEREAS, since opening, Knock Knock Children's Museum has welcomed more
14 than one million five hundred thousand visitors, including more than one hundred forty
15 thousand annually, with families traveling from all sixty-four Louisiana parishes, all fifty
16 states, and numerous countries; and
17 WHEREAS, the museum strengthens the quality of life and economy of the Capital
18 Region while helping close knowledge and opportunity gaps through its Access for All

1 programs, which serve more than twenty thousand children each year, including children
2 with disabilities, special needs, and families with limited financial resources; and
3 WHEREAS, through its eighteen interactive learning zones, the museum provides
4 a safe and inclusive space where children and families learn together while building early
5 literacy, critical thinking, creativity, STEAM skills, and exploring career pathways; and
6 WHEREAS, Knock Knock Children's Museum also supports educators across
7 Louisiana by providing professional learning opportunities that promote innovative teaching
8 strategies in early childhood and elementary education; and
9 WHEREAS, the museum serves as a valuable community resource that connects
10 families to regional experts and expertise, strengthens family bonds, and prepares
11 Louisiana's youngest learners for future success.
12 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
13 does hereby designate Monday, April 13, 2026, as Knock Knock Children's Museum Day
14 at the Louisiana State Capitol and commends the museum for its outstanding contributions
15 to early childhood education, community engagement, and economic development in
16 Louisiana.
17 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Senate expresses its appreciation for the
18 museum's ongoing mission to be a community spark for engaging, playful learning
19 experiences that inspire and support lifelong learning, and in opening doors for children to
20 a world of opportunity and possibility.
21 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
22 chairman of the board, Alexandra Layfield, and the executive director of the Knock Knock
23 Children's Museum.
The original instrument and the following digest, which constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument, were prepared by Senate Legislative Services.
