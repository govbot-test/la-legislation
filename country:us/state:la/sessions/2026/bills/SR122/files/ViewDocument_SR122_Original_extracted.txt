SLS 26RS-3073 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 122
BY SENATOR HENRY
COMMENDATIONS. Commends and congratulates Metairie Park Country Day School on
winning its 9th Louisiana High School Athletic Association State Championship in boys
basketball.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Metairie Park Country Day School upon winning the 2026 Louisiana High
3 School Athletic Association (LHSAA) Division III Select Boys' Basketball State
4 Championship and to recognize the outstanding accomplishments of its players,
5 coaches, and staff.
6 WHEREAS, it has been said that history does not repeat itself, but it often rhymes,
7 and for the Metairie Park Country Day School Cajuns, Louisiana high school boys'
8 basketball is starting to feel like poetry; and
9 WHEREAS, Metairie Park Country Day School captured the 2026 LHSAA Division
10 III Select Boys' Basketball State Championship, defeating Calvary Baptist Academy by a
11 final score of 58-49 on March 14, 2026, at Burton Coliseum in Lake Charles, Louisiana; and
12 WHEREAS, this hard-fought victory secured the second consecutive boys' basketball
13 state championship for the Cajuns and a remarkable ninth boys' basketball state
14 championship for Country Day; and
15 WHEREAS, the number three seed Cajuns' championship run demonstrated
16 extraordinary determination, discipline, and teamwork, as they first claimed the District
17 10-2A title with wins over Sarah T. Reed High School and Isidore Newman School, and then
18 advanced through the playoffs with victories over St. Thomas Aquinas and St. Louis

1 Catholic; and
2 WHEREAS, at Marsh Madness in Lake Charles, the Cajuns defeated the Dunham
3 Tigers in the semi-finals for the second year running, by a score of 51-49, sealed with a
4 game-winning shot by Herm Dyson; and
5 WHEREAS, the Cajuns completed their romp by vanquishing the Calvary Baptist
6 Cavaliers for the crown, also for the second consecutive year, solidifying the program's
7 reputation as a dynasty in high school competition; and
8 WHEREAS, senior Brennan White, who went four-for-four from three-point range,
9 delivered a riveting performance in the championship game, scoring thirteen of his sixteen
10 points in a clutch second half and earning game MVP and All-Tournament Team honors;
11 and
12 WHEREAS, junior Kellen Brewer brought the heat with fourteen points, five
13 rebounds, and six blocks in the title game and was also recognized as the Class 2A Boys
14 Basketball Outstanding Player, All-Tournament, and First Team All-State; and
15 WHEREAS, under the leadership of Head Coach Mike McGuire, who earned his
16 seventh state championship and sixteenth Marsh Madness Final Four appearance with
17 Country Day, the team demonstrated the highest standards of sportsmanship, perseverance,
18 and competitive excellence; and
19 WHEREAS, the players, coaches, faculty, families, and supporters of Metairie Park
20 Country Day School are to be honored on this extraordinary achievement.
21 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
22 does hereby commend Metairie Park Country Day School for winning the 2026 LHSAA
23 Division III Select Boys' Basketball State Championship, congratulates the Cajuns on their
24 second consecutive state title and ninth championship title in school history, and extends best
25 wishes for continued success in all their future endeavors.
26 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to
27 Metairie Park Country Day School and Head Coach Mike McGuire.
