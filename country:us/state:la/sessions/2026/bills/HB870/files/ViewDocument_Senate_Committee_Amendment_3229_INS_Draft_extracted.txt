11 AMENDMENT NO. 5
12 On page 2, at the beginning of line 17, change "(7)" to "(8)"
13 AMENDMENT NO. 6
14 On page 3, after line 26, insert the following:
15 "D.(1) A health insurance issuer shall notify the commissioner in writing
16 within thirty days if the health insurance issuer opts to utilize the net cost calculation
17 for a branded prescription drug in a drug formulary, in lieu of placing a generic or
18 biosimilar on the drug formulary.
19 (2) For each National Drug Code (NDC), the notification shall provide the
20 following information for both the branded prescription drug and any available
21 generic or biosimilar:
22 (a) Wholesale acquisition cost.
23 (b) Net cost.
24 (3) The health insurance issuer shall provide the branded prescription drug
25 cost-sharing amount and the cost-sharing amount if the generic or biosimilar was
26 added to the drug formulary at a more favorable coverage tier.
27 (4) The health insurance issuer shall include in the notification how the issuer
28 is utilizing the rebate provided by the manufacturer of the branded prescription
29 drug."
