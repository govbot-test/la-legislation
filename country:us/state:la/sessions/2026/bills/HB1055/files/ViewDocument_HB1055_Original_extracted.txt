9 §1664.22. Urban search and rescue program fund
10 A. The Louisiana Urban Search and Rescue Program fund, referred to in this
11 Subpart as the "fund", is hereby created within the state treasury.
12 B. The source of monies deposited into the fund shall be legislative
13 appropriation. Monies in the fund shall be subject to appropriation by the legislature
14 and shall be available exclusively for the administration of the Louisiana Urban
15 Search and Rescue Program.
16 C. All unexpended and unencumbered monies in the fund at the end of the
17 fiscal year shall remain in the fund. These monies shall be invested by the state
18 treasurer in the same manner as monies in the state general fund, and interest earned
19 on the investment of these monies shall be credited to the fund after compliance with
20 the requirements of Article VII, Section 9(B) of the Constitution of Louisiana,
21 relative to the Bond Security and Redemption Fund.

HB 1055 Original 2026 Regular Session Zeringue
Abstract: Establishes the La. Urban Search and Rescue Program within the office of state
fire marshal.
Proposed law creates the La. Urban Search and Rescue Program in the office of state fire
marshal within the Dept. of Public Safety and Corrections, which shall exercise the functions
of the state for the development, coordination, response, and provision of resources to locate
and extricate victims entrapped by man-made or natural disasters.

Proposed law provides for the selection and employment of a director of the program, an
assistant to the director, and other personnel who may be needed to implement the program.
Proposed law directs the office of state fire marshal to develop policies and procedures
related to the staffing, organization, and logistics of the program.
Proposed law directs the office of state fire marshal to promulgate rules and regulations for
reimbursement of agencies participating in the program for expenses related to response
team deployment and any other issues concerning the program's operation.
Proposed law creates the La. Urban Search and Rescue Program Fund within the state
treasury, and provides that the source of monies deposited into the fund shall be legislative
appropriation.
Proposed law provides for the treatment of unexpended or unencumbered monies in the fund
and interest earned on investment of fund monies.
