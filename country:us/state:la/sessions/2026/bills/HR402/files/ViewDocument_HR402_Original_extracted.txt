1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Loretta Wiley for completing a master's degree at the age of seventy-five.
3 WHEREAS, Loretta Wiley is a Christian, mother, grandmother, and widow who
4 resides in the unincorporated community of Princeton in Bossier Parish, Louisiana; and
5 WHEREAS, Ms. Wiley attended elementary and high school in the Bossier Parish
6 School System; in 1967, she had the privilege of being a member of the first class enrolled
7 at Southern University at Shreveport; and
8 WHEREAS, Ms. Wiley completed her undergraduate education at Southern
9 University at Baton Rouge and graduated in 1971 with a bachelor of science degree in
10 secondary education; and
11 WHEREAS, Ms. Wiley resumed her education in 2006, enrolling at Louisiana Tech
12 University to pursue a master's degree; after a pause in her educational journey, she re-
13 enrolled at Louisiana Tech as a post-baccalaureate student in 2021 to pursue an
14 undergraduate certification in forensic psychology; and
15 WHEREAS, after completing the undergraduate certificate, Ms. Wiley enrolled as
16 a graduate student and earned a graduate certificate in domestic violence; she continued her
17 higher educational pursuits by working on a master's degree in human services, which she
18 received on May 24, 2025; and
19 WHEREAS, Ms. Wiley served as a substitute teacher prior to and throughout her
20 graduate studies, and she has supported her students and served her community with
