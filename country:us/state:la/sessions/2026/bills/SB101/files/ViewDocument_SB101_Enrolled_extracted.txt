2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
SENATE BILL NO. 101
BY SENATOR ABRAHAM
1 AN ACT
2 To provide relative to state highways; to designate a portion of Louisiana Highway 384 in
3 Cameron Parish as the "Daniel Edward Didelot Memorial Highway"; to designate
4 a portion of Louisiana Highway 436 in Washington Parish as the "Bill "Bleu" Evans
5 Memorial Highway"; to provide for location and placement of signage; to provide
6 for restrictions on costs of materials to the department; and to provide for related
7 matters.
8 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
9 Section 1. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, the
10 portion of Louisiana Highway 384, beginning at Big Diamond Disposal Road extending
11 to N. Precht Road, situated within Cameron Parish, is hereby designated as the "Daniel
12 Edward Didelot Memorial Highway".
13 Section 2. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, the
14 portion of Louisiana Highway 436, beginning at Louisiana Highway 21, extending to
15 E. Old Columbia Road, situated within Washington Parish, is hereby designated as the
16 "Bill "Bleu" Evans Memorial Highway".
17 Section 3. The Department of Transportation and Development is hereby
18 directed to erect and maintain appropriate signs of these designations provided local
