HCASB389 4469 5705
HOUSE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
Senate Bill No. 389 by Senator Connick
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 3, line 16, after "thing of value" delete the remainder of the line and at the
3 beginning of line 17, delete "broadest possible construction"
4 AMENDMENT NO. 2
5 On page 6, delete lines 3 through 16 in their entirety and insert the following:
6 "(9) "Educational institution" means any institution of higher education
7 in this state, including any postsecondary institution, community college,
8 college, institution of higher learning, or university, whether public or private.
9 (10) "Endorsement activities" means any activity or activities related to
10 advertising or sponsorship in which an athlete, for consideration or other value,
11 uses or permits the use or association of the athlete's name, image, likeness,
12 publicity, reputation, following, fame, or other personal or commercial
13 attributes, whether or not related to the athlete's athletic ability or
14 performance.
15 (11) "Endorsement contract" means an agreement under which an
16 athlete is employed or receives anything of value to permit the use, promotion,
17 or association of the athlete's name, image, and likeness, publicity, reputation,
18 following, fame, or other personal or commercial attributes, whether or not
19 related to the athlete's athletic ability or performance."
20 AMENDMENT NO. 3
21 On page 7, line 16, change "all of" to "both of"
22 AMENDMENT NO. 4
23 On page 14, line 6, change "G.(1)" to "G."
