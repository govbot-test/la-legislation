SENATE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
Original Senate Bill No. 25 by Senator Kleinpeter
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, line 3, after "(E)," add the following:
3 "and to enact R.S. 18:55(G) and 59(N),"
4 AMENDMENT NO. 2
5 On page 1, line 8, after "reenacted" add the following:
6 "and R.S. 18:55(G) and 59(N) are hereby enacted"
7 AMENDMENT NO. 3
8 On page 3, delete lines 1 through 3 and insert the following:
9 "the most recent census taken by the United States Government, Louisiana Tech University
10 population estimates for Louisiana parishes, or upon the population figures of each parish
11 population figures as shown by the latest federal decennial census, population estimates
12 published pursuant to the United States Bureau of the Census Federal State
13 Cooperative Program for Population Estimates, as determined by its governing authority
14 as provided by of each parish in accordance with law."
15 AMENDMENT NO. 4
16 On page 3 between lines 4 and 5, insert the following:
17 "G. Notwithstanding any other provisions of this Chapter to the contrary, the
18 Department of State shall not adjust the annual salary of registrars of voters except
19 once decennially one year after receiving the federal census.
20 * * *"
21 AMENDMENT NO. 5
22 On page 5, line 24, after "recent", delete the remainder of the line and delete lines 25 through
23 27 and insert the following:
24 census taken by the United States Government, the Louisiana Tech University population
25 estimates for Louisiana parishes,"population figures as shown by the latest federal
26 decennial census, population estimates published pursuant to the United States Bureau
27 of the Census Federal State Cooperative Program for Population Estimates, or upon
28 the population figures of each parish as determined by its governing authority as provided
29 by of each parish in accordance with law.
30 AMENDMENT NO. 6
31 On page 5, between lines 28 and 29, insert the following:
32 "N. Notwithstanding any other provisions of this Chapter to the contrary, the
33 Department of State shall not adjust the annual salaries of the chief deputy registrars
34 of voters and confidential assistant to the registrars of voters except once decennially
35 one year after receiving the federal census.
36 * * *"
