1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 56:1855(Q), relative to the Louisiana Scenic Rivers Act; to provide for natural

5 Section 1. R.S. 56:1855(Q) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
6 §1855. Exceptions
7 * * *
8 Q. Notwithstanding any other provision of the law to the contrary,
9 beginning August 1, 2026, through August 1, 2031, no provision of this Part
10 shall:
11 (1) Prohibit the channelization, clearing and snagging, channel
12 realignment, reservoir construction, or dredging operations for drainage
13 purposes in the West Pearl River and its tributaries from Lock 1 to the Rigolets.
14 (2) Require a permit pursuant to R.S. 56:1849 for the channelization,
15 clearing and snagging, channel realignment, reservoir construction, or dredging
16 operations for drainage purposes in the West Pearl River and its tributaries
17 from Lock 1 to the Rigolets.

DIGEST
SB 331 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Owen
Present law creates the Natural and Scenic Rivers System to protect, conserve, and replenish
state water bodies and designates certain rivers, streams, and bayous that are part of the
system.
Present law prohibits channelization, clearing and snagging, channel realignment and
reservoir construction on water bodies in the system.
Proposed law retains present law and creates certain exceptions for the portion of the West
Pearl River and tributaries lying between Lock 1 and the Rigolets, from August 1, 2026, to
August 1, 2031.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Adds R.S. 56:1855(Q))
