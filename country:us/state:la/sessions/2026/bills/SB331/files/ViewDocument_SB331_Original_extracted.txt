SLS 26RS-551 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY SENATOR OWEN
WATER/WATERWAYS. Provides relative to the Louisiana Scenic Rivers Act. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 56:1855(Q), relative to the Louisiana Scenic Rivers Act; to provide for natural
3 and scenic rivers; to provide for exceptions; and to provide for related matters.
4 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
5 Section 1. R.S. 56:1855(Q) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
6 §1855. Exceptions
7 * * *
8 Q. Notwithstanding any other provision of the law to the contrary,
9 beginning August 1, 2026, through August 1, 2033, no provision of this Part
10 shall:
11 (1) Prohibit the channelization, clearing and snagging, channel
12 realignment, reservoir construction, or dredging operations for drainage
13 purposes in the West Pearl River from Lock 1 to the Rigolets.
14 (2) Require a permit pursuant to R.S. 56:1849 for the channelization,
15 clearing and snagging, channel realignment, reservoir construction, or dredging
16 operations for drainage purposes in the West Pearl River from Lock 1 to the
17 Rigolets.

SLS 26RS-551 ORIGINAL
SB NO. 331
The original instrument and the following digest, which constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument, were prepared by Senate Legislative Services.
The keyword, summary, and digest do not constitute part of the law or proof
or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
DIGEST
SB 331 Original 2026 Regular Session Owen
Present law creates the Natural and Scenic Rivers System to protect, conserve, and replenish
state water bodies and designates certain rivers, streams, and bayous that are part of the
system.
Present law prohibits channelization, clearing and snagging, channel realignment and
reservoir construction on water bodies in the system.
Proposed law retains present law and creates certain exceptions for the portion of the West
Pearl River lying between Lock 1 and the Rigolets, from August 1, 2026, to August 1, 2033.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Adds R.S. 56:1855(Q)
