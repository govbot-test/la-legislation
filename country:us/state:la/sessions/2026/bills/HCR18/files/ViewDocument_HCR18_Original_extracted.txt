HLS 26RS-1690 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 18
BY REPRESENTATIVES BERAULT AND GLORIOSO
COMMENDATIONS: Commends the Pope John Paul II Catholic High School Lady
Jaguars Soccer Team on winning the Louisiana High School Athletic Association
2026 Division IV state championship
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 To commend the Pope John Paul II Catholic High School Lady Jaguars Soccer Team on
3 winning the Louisiana High School Athletic Association 2026 Division IV state
4 championship.
5 WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize the Pope John Paul II Catholic High
6 School Lady Jaguars Soccer Team for capturing the 2026 Division IV state title by defeating
7 Isidore Newman School in the championship match held at Strawberry Stadium in
8 Hammond, Louisiana; and
9 WHEREAS, the Lady Jaguars finished the 2025-2026 season with an outstanding
10 record of nineteen wins, two draws, and one loss and demonstrated incredible resilience,
11 skill, and teamwork in each match; and
12 WHEREAS, the Lady Jaguars remained composed in the state championship match
13 and claimed the title following a dramatic penalty shootout; and
14 WHEREAS, the Lady Jaguars are led by Head Coach Meaux Homburg and Assistant
15 Coaches Ben Riviere and Jason Schexnaydre; the team embraced a culture of winning
16 founded on dedication, accountability, and belief in one another, and its success is a true
17 reflection of the players' character and sportsmanship; and
18 WHEREAS, the Pope John Paul II Catholic High School Lady Jaguars'
19 championship season stands as a testament to the importance of teamwork and perseverance
20 and the impact of family, school, and community support; the team is most deserving of the
