2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 5
BY SENATOR HODGES
A RESOLUTION
To commend and congratulate Kaidyn McLean on being selected as the 2025-2026 "Student
of the Year" at Springfield High School in Livingston Parish.
WHEREAS, Kaidyn McLean has been selected as the 2025-2026 "Student of the
Year" at Springfield High School, an honor that reflects her exceptional dedication to
academic excellence, leadership, and personal achievement; and
WHEREAS, Kaidyn earned an exceptional 31 composite score and 32 super score
on the ACT, demonstrating academic aptitude and a strong commitment to rigorous
scholarship; and
WHEREAS, Kaidyn aspires to attend medical school and pursue a career as a
medical researcher, with the goal of advancing scientific discovery and improving human
health; and
WHEREAS, Kaidyn has been an active member of the National Beta Club for three
years, holding a leadership position each year and exemplifying scholarship, leadership,
character, and service; and
WHEREAS, Kaidyn is a dedicated member of her school's robotics team, which has
achieved remarkable success, earning two national awards, eleven local awards, and two
state awards, reflecting teamwork, innovation, and technical excellence; and
WHEREAS, Kaidyn has also demonstrated long-term commitment to the arts as a
clarinet player in the Bulldog Band, contributing her musical talents and discipline to her
school community; and
WHEREAS, Kaidyn McLean's academic accomplishments, leadership roles, and
diverse extracurricular involvement distinguish her as an exemplary student and a young
person of exceptional promise.
