SLS 26RS-229 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 5
BY SENATOR HODGES
COMMENDATIONS. Commends Kaidyn McLean on being selected "Student of the Year"
at Springfield High School in Livingston Parish.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend and congratulate Kaidyn McLean on being selected the 2025-2026 "Student
3 of the Year" at Springfield High School in Livingston Parish.
4 WHEREAS, Kaidyn McLean has been selected as the 2025-2026 "Student of the
5 Year" at Springfield High School, an honor that reflects her exceptional dedication to
6 academic excellence, leadership, and personal achievement; and
7 WHEREAS, Kaidyn earned an exceptional 31 composite score and 32 super score
8 on the ACT, demonstrating academic aptitude and a strong commitment to rigorous
9 scholarship; and
10 WHEREAS, Kaidyn aspires to attend medical school and pursue a career as a
11 medical researcher, with the goal of advancing scientific discovery and improving human
12 health; and
13 WHEREAS, Kaidyn has been an active member of the National Beta Club for three
14 years, holding a leadership position each year and exemplifying scholarship, leadership,
15 character, and service; and
16 WHEREAS, Kaidyn is a dedicated member of her school's robotics team, which has
17 achieved remarkable success, earning two national awards, eleven local awards, and two
18 state awards, reflecting teamwork, innovation, and technical excellence; and
