SLS 26RS-214 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 7
BY SENATOR WHEAT
Strawberry Festival Day at the Louisiana State Capitol.
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 To designate Tuesday, April 7, 2026, as Ponchatoula Strawberry Festival Day at the state
3 capitol.
4 WHEREAS, the Louisiana strawberry is the official state fruit of Louisiana; and
5 WHEREAS, strawberries comprise about $8,300,000 in total gross farm value in
6 Tangipahoa Parish and more than $12,000,000 statewide; and
7 WHEREAS, the fifty-fourth annual Ponchatoula Strawberry Festival will be held
8 April 10-12, 2026, at Memorial Park in the beautiful and historic city of Ponchatoula; and
9 WHEREAS, organized in 1971, the first Ponchatoula Strawberry Festival was held
10 in April of 1972, co-sponsored by the Ponchatoula Jaycees and the Ponchatoula Chamber
11 of Commerce, as each organization provided three of its members to serve on the initial
12 festival board; and
13 WHEREAS, the initial intent of the board was to host a festival to provide an
14 opportunity for local charitable organizations to raise money for their clubs, to honor all of
15 the hardworking local strawberry farmers, and to promote the strawberry industry; and
16 WHEREAS, for over fifty years, the Ponchatoula Strawberry Festival has become
17 a venue for fund-raising by nonprofit and charitable organizations while celebrating the rich
18 agricultural heritage and vibrant community spirit of Ponchatoula, the "Strawberry Capital
Page 1 of 3

1 of the World"; and
2 WHEREAS, each year, distinguished individuals are chosen to serve as festival
3 royalty, representing the festival and its traditions with honor, pride, and enthusiasm; and
4 WHEREAS, the 2026 Louisiana Strawberry Queen LIV, Aubrey Renee' Brumfield,
5 is honored with this esteemed title to signify her role as an ambassador of the festival and
6 the values it upholds; and
7 WHEREAS, the 2026 Ponchatoula Strawberry Festival King LIV is David Atkins,
8 who was selected for his dedication to the festival and the community; and
9 WHEREAS, the 2026 Ponchatoula Strawberry Festival Blossom is Heather
10 Robertson, who exemplifies grace and community involvement and is particularly
11 recognized for her contributions and commitment to the festival and the farming industry;
12 and
13 WHEREAS, the 2026 Ponchatoula Strawberry Festival Grand Marshal is Emily
14 McKneely Matise; she is given the prestigious role of leading the festival parade, an honor
15 reserved for those who have demonstrated exceptional dedication to the Ponchatoula
16 community; and
17 WHEREAS, the 2026 Festival Chairman, Rueben Auter, has provided invaluable
18 leadership and direction in ensuring the continued success of the Ponchatoula Strawberry
19 Festival, making this event a cherished tradition for locals and visitors alike; and
20 WHEREAS, the contributions of these individuals to the Ponchatoula Strawberry
21 Festival reflect their passion for preserving the culture and history of this renowned event,
22 which continues to bring joy and economic benefits to the region; and
23 WHEREAS, the Ponchatoula Strawberry Festival attracts both young and old to its
24 annual celebration of Ponchatoula's legendary delicacy, with food booths featuring popular
25 choices like strawberry shortcakes and deep-fried strawberries; and
26 WHEREAS, the Ponchatoula Strawberry Festival has the unique status as one of the
27 only festivals at which nonprofit organizations are the only vendors on the official festival
28 grounds.
29 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of Louisiana does hereby
30 designate Tuesday, April 7, 2026, as Ponchatoula Strawberry Festival Day at the Louisiana
