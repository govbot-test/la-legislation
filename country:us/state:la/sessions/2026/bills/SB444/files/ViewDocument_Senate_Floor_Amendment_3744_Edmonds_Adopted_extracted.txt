Edmonds
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 2, between lines 20 and 21, insert the following:
3 "D. Nothing in this Part authorizes the city of St. George to acquire by
4 declaration of taking any facility, utility plant, servitude, right-of-way, easement, or
5 other immovable property right owned, leased, operated, maintained, or used by a
6 utility subject to the jurisdiction of the Louisiana Public Service Commission to furnish
7 electric, gas, water, or sewerage service. Nothing in this Part shall affect applicable
8 utility relocation law or any authority existing under R.S. 19:102 or other law."
