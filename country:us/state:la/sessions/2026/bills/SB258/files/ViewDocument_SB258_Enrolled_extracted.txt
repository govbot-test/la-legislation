2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
SENATE BILL NO. 258
BY SENATOR LAMBERT
1 AN ACT
2 To enact Children's Code Art. 951.1, relative to juvenile traffic violations; to provide for
3 definitions; and to provide for related matters.
4 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
5 Section 1. Children's Code Art. 951.1 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
6 Art. 951.1. Definitions
7 As used in this Title, "child" means any person under the age of twenty-
8 one, including an emancipated minor, who commits a traffic violation before
9 attaining seventeen years of age.
