HLS 26RS-1939 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 56
BY REPRESENTATIVE TURNER
SPECIAL DAY/WEEK/MONTH: Designates March 24, 2026, as Louisiana Athletic
Trainers Day at the capitol and commends the members of the Louisiana Athletic
Trainers Association
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate March 24, 2026, as Louisiana Athletic Trainers Day at the capitol and to
3 commend the members of the Louisiana Athletic Trainers Association (LATA) for
4 their dedicated hard work on behalf of the athletes of Louisiana.
5 WHEREAS, LATA members are skilled healthcare professionals trained in the
6 prevention, emergency management, evaluation, and therapeutic intervention of
7 sports-related injuries and conditions; and
8 WHEREAS, the mission of the national association is to represent, engage, and foster
9 the continued growth and development of the athletic training profession and athletic trainers
10 as unique healthcare providers; and
11 WHEREAS, athletic training is an allied healthcare profession recognized by the
12 American Medical Association, and certification in the profession requires strict educational
13 standards based on specific knowledge and skills; certified athletic trainers have a long
14 history of providing quality healthcare for athletes and individuals engaged in physical
15 activity; and
16 WHEREAS, citizens of Louisiana are allowed to participate in meaningful and
17 rewarding athletic competitions throughout the state and nation as a result of the hard work
18 of the men and women of LATA; and
19 WHEREAS, the members of LATA are most deserving of the highest recognition
20 for their dedicated work.

1 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
2 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend the members of the Louisiana Athletic

4 for posterity their unwavering commitment to excellence in service to the citizens of this
5 state; does hereby extend enduring appreciation for the pride and honor they bring to
6 Louisiana; and does hereby designate March 24, 2026, as Louisiana Athletic Trainers Day
7 at the capitol.
