2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 198
BY REPRESENTATIVE BOYER
COMMENDATIONS: Commends Karaline Elizabeth Leblanc for being named the 2026
Breaux Bridge Crawfish Festival Queen
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Karaline Elizabeth Leblanc for being named the 2026 Breaux Bridge Crawfish
3 Festival Queen.
4 WHEREAS, it is fitting to recognize Karaline Elizabeth Leblanc for her
5 achievements and to commend her for being named the 2026 Breaux Bridge Crawfish
6 Festival Queen; and
7 WHEREAS, Karaline is the nineteen-year-old daughter of Kelly and Bryce Leblanc;
8 with the support of her parents, she has grown into a strong and confident young woman who
9 beautifully reflects the heart, culture, and traditions of the Breaux Bridge community; and
10 WHEREAS, Karaline is pursuing her passion for the beauty industry at the
11 Cosmetology Training Center of Lafayette, and she is dedicated to perfecting her craft while
12 building a career centered on helping others feel beautiful and confident; and
13 WHEREAS, Karaline strives to make a positive impact within her community and
14 beyond; as the Crawfish Festival Queen, she strongly supports efforts to get younger
15 generations involved and interested in the festival, and she works to preserve the traditions
16 and culture of Breaux Bridge by inspiring youth to take pride in their heritage and actively
17 participate in local events; and
18 WHEREAS, Karaline is excited to celebrate Breaux Bridge's unique and rich Cajun
19 heritage, music, food, and traditions; she proudly embraces her role as the ambassador of the
20 Breaux Bridge Crawfish Festival; and
