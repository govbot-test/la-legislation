2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 112
BY SENATOR STINE
A RESOLUTION
To commend Chennault International Airport on the occasion of its 40th anniversary.
WHEREAS, over the past forty years, Chennault International Airport has evolved
into a nationally recognized aviation hub known for excellence in maintenance, repair,
overhaul, and innovation; and
WHEREAS, the airport has proven itself to be a significant creator of new jobs,
providing thousands of high-quality employment opportunities by consistently attracting
leading aviation and non-aviation companies as on-site business partners, while fostering
investment and strengthening the regional economy; and
WHEREAS, Chennault International Airport proudly continues its distinguished
military heritage as a former Strategic Air Command base and remains an active training and
exercise site for B-52 Stratofortress aircraft and other military aviation operations; and
WHEREAS, the airport has contributed to the resilience and diversification of the
local economy, supporting growth beyond traditional industries; and
WHEREAS, Chennault International Airport has demonstrated a strong commitment
to workforce development through partnerships with local college training programs; and
WHEREAS, the leadership, on-site businesses, and workforce of the Chennault
International Airport have exhibited dedication, innovation, and vision, ensuring its
continued success and positive impact on the community.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
does hereby commend Chennault International Airport on its 40th anniversary, celebrating
its outstanding achievements as a national aviation center and a cornerstone of economic
development in Southwest Louisiana, and does hereby express sincere appreciation to its
leadership, employees, and partners for their enduring contributions, as well as best wishes
for its continued growth and success in years to come.
PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE
