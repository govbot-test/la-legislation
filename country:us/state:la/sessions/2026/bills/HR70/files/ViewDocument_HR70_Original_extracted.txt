HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 70
BY REPRESENTATIVE CHASSION
run to the Louisiana High School Athletic Association 2026 Division II Select state
championship game
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend the Northside High School boys basketball team on their run to the Louisiana
3 High School Athletic Association 2026 Division II Select state championship game.
4 WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize the members of the Northside High School
5 Vikings boys basketball team for their incredible efforts and to commend their run to the
6 2026 Division II Select state championship game; and
7 WHEREAS, Head Coach Troy Jones led the twelve-seed Vikings on an incredible
8 run to the 2026 Division II Select title game and an overall record of twenty-four wins and
9 fourteen losses; and
10 WHEREAS, the Vikings upset five-seed Lake Charles College Prep, 66-65, in the
11 regional round; in the semi-finals, the team ousted top-ranked Madison Prep by a score of
12 65-61; and
13 WHEREAS, in the semi-final game, Northside's Jaydon Francis scored a game-high
14 twenty-four points, including twenty-two points in the second half, and Treylon Angelle
15 added twenty-three points; and
16 WHEREAS, in the championship game, Jaydon Francis scored a game-high twenty-
17 five points, grabbed four rebounds, made three assists, and blocked one shot; Jayden Jones
18 added eleven points; and
