17 The board's papers, documents, reports, or evidence related to the
18 subject of an investigation in accordance with this Chapter shall not be subject
19 to disclosure until the completion of the investigation. Such papers, documents,
20 reports, or evidence relative to the subject of an investigation in accordance
21 with this Chapter shall not be subject to subpoena until the investigation has
