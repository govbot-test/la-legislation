HOUSE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
Amendments proposed by House Committee on House and Governmental Affairs to

1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 In House Committee Amendment No. 20 by the House Committee on Commerce (#4951),
3 on page 2, delete lines 19 through 28 and insert the following:
4 "C.(1) A licensee engaged as a general contractor on a mega project, or
5 an owner acting on an exemption as a general contractor on a mega project,
6 shall annually submit to the board a detailed listing of all subcontractors
7 engaged to work on the mega project, including licensure information or
8 evidence of approval by the secretary of state as a foreign entity registered to
9 do business in this state.
10 (2) A licensee engaged as a general contractor or an owner acting on an
11 exemption as a general contractor on a broadband project administered in
12 accordance with the BEAD Program issued by the National
13 Telecommunications and Information Administration of the United States
14 Department of Commerce shall not be required to submit the listing provided
15 for in Paragraph (1) of this Subsection, but shall notify the board when a
16 contract has been let."
17 AMENDMENT NO. 2
18 On page 20, line 25, after "violation." insert the following:
19 "The penalties provided for in this Subsection shall not apply to a
20 licensee engaged as a general contractor on a mega project, or to an owner
21 acting on an exemption as a general contractor on a mega project."
