1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 17:416.14.1, relative to elementary and secondary schools; to prohibit the use
3 of deepfake material against students; to provide for a definition; to provide for
4 rulemaking authority; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S.17:416.14.1 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
7 §416.14.1. Deepfake material; prohibition
8 A. No student, faculty member, member of the administrative staff, or
9 other employee at an elementary or secondary school shall use material
10 considered to be a deepfake against a student enrolled in kindergarten through
11 grade twelve, with the intent to coerce, harass, intimidate, or maliciously
12 disseminate or sell the material.
13 B. For the purposes of this Section, "deepfake" means any audio or
14 visual media in an electronic format, including any motion picture film or video
15 recording, that is created, altered, or digitally manipulated in a manner that
16 would falsely appear to a reasonable observer to be an authentic record of the
17 actual speech or conduct of the individual or that replaces an individual's
18 likeness with another individual as depicted in the recording. "Deepfake" does
19 not include any material that constitutes a work of political, public interest, or
20 newsworthy value, including commentary, criticism, satire, or parody, or that
21 includes content, context, or a clear disclosure visible throughout the duration
22 of the recording that would cause a reasonable person to understand that the
23 audio or visual media is not a record of a real event.
