HLS 26RS-4413 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 90
BY REPRESENTATIVE SPELL AND SENATOR MCMATH
Month in Louisiana
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 To designate May 2026 as Mental Health Awareness Month in Louisiana.
3 WHEREAS, mental health wellness is a key component of an individual's ability to
4 manage emotions, build relationships, contribute to society, and cope with the challenges life
5 presents; and
6 WHEREAS, mental health conditions can significantly disrupt an individual's life
7 and lead to serious and life-threatening outcomes when left untreated; and
8 WHEREAS, each year, one in five adults experiences a mental health concern, and
9 symptomatic individuals often experience delays with access to effective treatment and care;
10 and
11 WHEREAS, individuals, families, communities, and organizations, including local
12 mental health partners such as NAMI Southeast Louisiana, each play a role in supporting
13 mental wellness and reducing the stigma around mental illness; and
14 WHEREAS, by supporting community-wide initiatives and public education focused
15 on mental health wellness, Louisiana can improve the lives of individuals and families
16 affected by mental health conditions.
17 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of Louisiana does hereby
18 designate May 2026 as Mental Health Awareness Month in Louisiana; does hereby
19 recognize the need for appropriate and accessible services for all people with mental health
20 conditions; does hereby urge citizens, government agencies, public and private institutions,
21 businesses, and schools in Louisiana to recommit to increasing awareness and understanding
22 of mental health; and does hereby encourage Louisianians to take steps to protect their
23 mental health.
