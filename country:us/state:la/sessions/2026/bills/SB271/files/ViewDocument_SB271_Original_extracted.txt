DIGEST
SB 271 Original 2026 Regular Session Cathey
Proposed law establishes permits for hauling grain and cotton provided that there are no
objections raised by the federal government.
Proposed law further provides permits issued are limited to harvest season operations, as
defined by administrative rule of the Department of Transportation and Development.
Proposed law further provides any vehicle issued a special permit may travel on the segment
of Interstate 20 between its interchange with US highway 165 in the city of Monroe and its
interchange with Louisiana Highway 193 in Delta, Louisiana, as an alternate route to US 80.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Adds R.S. 32:387.9.2)
