SLS 26RS-360 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY SENATOR FESI
SCHOOL BOARDS. Removes requirement for publication of local notice by school boards
under certain circumstances. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 17:56(A)(1) and (E), relative to notice required by school boards
3 for changes in compensation; and to provide for related matters.
4 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
5 Section 1. R.S. 17:56(A)(1) and (E) are hereby amended and reenacted to read as
6 follows:
7 §56. Compensation of board members and executive committee members
8 A.(1) Members of all parish and city school boards are authorized to receive
9 compensation not to exceed fifty dollars per day for every meeting of the school
10 board. School board members shall not be paid for more than one hundred forty-four
11 days in any one year; provided that no member shall be compensated for more than
12 twelve meetings per month, or, in lieu of such per diem, the school board may elect,
13 by a vote of a majority of the elected members, to go on an expense allowance basis,
14 in which event the maximum allowance shall be eight hundred dollars per month for
15 each member of the school board and nine hundred dollars per month for the school
16 board president. Additionally, members of all parish and city school boards are
17 authorized to receive the same mileage allowance as provided to state elected

14 based on per diem basis to an expense allowance basis as is authorized in Subsection
15 A hereof of this Section, shall be adopted only upon prior publication only after
16 posting on the official website of the school board of such the proposed change
17 in the official journal of the parish on at least two separate occasions within a period
18 of for fifteen days preceding the meeting.
The original instrument and the following digest, which constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument, were prepared by Senate Legislative Services.
The keyword, summary, and digest do not constitute part of the law or proof
or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
DIGEST
SB 224 Original 2026 Regular Session Fesi
Present law requires that school board meetings where changes to mileage and compensation
are to be voted on must be advertised in the official journal of the parish on two separate
days during the 15 days immediately preceding the meeting.
Proposed law retains present law but changes the notice requirement from the official journal
of the parish to the official website of the school board making the change.
Present law requires that any proposal to change the board from compensation based on per
diem basis to an expense allowance basis be adopted only after prior publication of the
proposed change in the official journal of the parish on at least two separate days within the
period of 15 days preceding the meeting.
