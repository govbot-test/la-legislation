HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 140
BY REPRESENTATIVE HILFERTY
the Lakeview Civic Improvement Association in New Orleans
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Freddy Yoder for more than thirty years of service with the Lakeview Civic
3 Improvement Association in New Orleans.
4 WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize Freddy Yoder for his dedication to the
5 Lakeview community and to commend him for more than thirty years of service with the
6 Lakeview Civic Improvement Association; and
7 WHEREAS, throughout Mr. Yoder's decades-long tenure with the Lakeview Civic
8 Improvement Association, he has inspired civic engagement and fostered unity,
9 volunteerism, and public service; and
10 WHEREAS, Mr. Yoder is an inspiring leader who champions infrastructure funding
11 and unites communities, businesses, and government to improve education, safety, and
12 development in the Lakeview neighborhood; and
13 WHEREAS, Mr. Yoder co-founded the Lakeview Crime Prevention District,
14 Louisiana's first crime prevention district; he also helped restore and beautify New Basin
15 Canal Park after Hurricane Katrina and transformed the area into a vibrant community space;
16 and
17 WHEREAS, Freddy Yoder is most deserving of the highest recognition for his
18 enduring contributions to Lakeview and its residents.
19 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
20 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Freddy Yoder for more than thirty years of
21 service with the Lakeview Civic Improvement Association in New Orleans and does hereby
