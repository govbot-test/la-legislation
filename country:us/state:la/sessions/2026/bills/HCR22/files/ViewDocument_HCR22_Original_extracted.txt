HLS 26RS-1751 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 22
COMMENDATIONS: Commends W.H. Ward Properties, Inc., on being named Tree
Farmer of the Year by the Louisiana Forestry Association
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 To recognize W.H. Ward Properties, Inc., on being named Tree Farmer of the Year by the
3 Louisiana Forestry Association.
4 WHEREAS, the Ward family has owned a six hundred eighteen-acre tree farm in
5 LaSalle Parish, Louisiana, since 1915; and
6 WHEREAS, the cornerstones of W.H. Ward Properties, Inc., are timber, wildlife,
7 stewardship, and family; and
8 WHEREAS, the members of the Ward family have been dedicated to the stewardship
9 of their land for over a century, and they have adapted through the years with a shared vision
10 despite changes to timber markets and land management practices; and
11 WHEREAS, in addition to its significant impact on the area's economic development,
12 the company gives back to the community through the sponsorship of a yearly college
13 scholarship and the donation of materials to the local band of Choctaw Indians; and
14 WHEREAS, W.H. Ward Properties was named Tree Farmer of the Year by the
15 Louisiana Forestry Association; and
16 WHEREAS, on the memorable occasion of receiving this deserved recognition, W.H.
17 Ward Properties, Inc., merits a special measure of commendation for its success in tree
18 farming and its contributions to the state's forestry industry.
19 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of Louisiana does hereby
20 commend W.H. Ward Properties, Inc., on its recognition by the Louisiana Forestry
21 Association as Tree Farmer of the Year and does hereby extend enduring appreciation for
