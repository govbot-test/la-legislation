SLS 26RS-554 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY SENATOR DUPLESSIS
INSURANCE DEPARTMENT. Allows nonprofit organizations to provide additional
resources to cover the cost of installing a fortified roof. (gov sig)
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 22:1483.1(G), relative to the Louisiana Fortify Homes Program; to authorize
3 a nonprofit organization to assist a grant recipient in covering the cost of installing
4 a fortified roof; to provide for definitions; to provide for an effective date; and to
5 provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 22:1483.1(G) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §1483.1. Louisiana Fortify Homes Program
9 * * *
10 G.(1) Nothing in this Section shall be construed to prohibit a nonprofit
11 organization from providing additional support and resources to a recipient of
12 a grant to assist in covering the cost of installing a fortified roof including those
13 costs incurred pursuant to Subsection B of this Section.
14 (2) No rule or regulation promulgated by the commissioner pursuant to
15 Subsection A of this Section to govern eligibility requirements for grants and
16 the administration of the program shall prohibit a nonprofit organization from
17 providing additional support and resources to a recipient of a grant to assist in

7 Section 2. This Act shall become effective upon signature by the governor or, if not
8 signed by the governor, upon expiration of the time for bills to become law without signature

11 effective on the day following such approval.
The original instrument and the following digest, which constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument, were prepared by Senate Legislative Services.
The keyword, summary, and digest do not constitute part of the law or proof
or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
DIGEST
SB 299 Original 2026 Regular Session Duplessis
Present law establishes the Louisiana Fortify Homes Program and authorizes the
commissioner of insurance to provide financial grants for insurable properties that have a
homestead exemption to retrofit a roof that meets or exceeds the fortified roof standard of
the Insurance Institute for Business and Home Safety. Proposed law retains present law.
Proposed law authorizes a nonprofit organization to provide additional support and resources
to a recipient of a grant to assist in covering the cost of installing a fortified roof.
Effective upon signature of the governor or lapse of time for gubernatorial action.
(Adds R.S. 22:1483.1(G))
