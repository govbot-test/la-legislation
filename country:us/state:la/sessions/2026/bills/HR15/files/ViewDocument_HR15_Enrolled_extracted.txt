ENROLLED
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 15
CARRIER, CARVER, CHENEVERT, DOMANGUE, EGAN, FIRMENT,
FONTENOT, GALLE, HORTON, MCCORMICK, OWEN, SAWYER,
VENTRELLA, AND WILDER
A RESOLUTION
To memorialize the United States Congress to take such actions as are necessary to pass the
SAVE America Act requiring voter identification in federal elections to protect
election integrity and public confidence in the democratic process.
WHEREAS, free, fair, and secure elections are the cornerstone of American
democracy and are essential to maintaining public trust in the electoral process; and
WHEREAS, the state of Louisiana currently requests voters to present a valid form
of identification prior to casting a ballot to ensure that elections are conducted with integrity
and accountability; and
WHEREAS, thirty-six states across the nation either request or require some form
of voter identification for in-person voting, which demonstrate that voter identification laws
are widely accepted and are constitutionally permissible safeguards; and
WHEREAS, documented cases of election fraud, illegal voting, and administrative
errors have occurred in various jurisdictions across the country, underscoring the need for
consistent national standards to deter unlawful practices and strengthen election security; and
WHEREAS, requiring voter identification helps prevent impersonation at the polls,
protects the lawful votes of eligible citizens, and reinforces confidence in election outcomes;
and
WHEREAS, inconsistent election laws among states create confusion for voters and
may undermine public trust in federal elections; and
Page 1 of 2
HR NO. 15 ENROLLED
WHEREAS, the SAVE America Act would establish uniform voter identification
requirements for federal elections, ensuring that every vote is cast by an eligible voter and
that election procedures are transparent and secure; and
WHEREAS, ensuring election integrity is not a partisan issue but a fundamental
responsibility owed to every American citizen.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby memorialize the United States Congress to take such
actions as are necessary to pass the SAVE America Act requiring voter identification for
federal elections.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the Legislature
of Louisiana hereby expresses its strong support for policies that safeguard election integrity,
prevent fraud, and strengthen public confidence in the democratic process.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
presiding officers of the Senate and the House of Representatives of the Congress of the
United States of America and to each member of the Louisiana congressional delegation.
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
