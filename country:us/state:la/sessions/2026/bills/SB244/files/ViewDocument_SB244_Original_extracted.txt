SLS 26RS-504 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY SENATOR ALLAIN
HUNTERS/HUNTING. Provides for recreational hunting of alligators. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 56:256, relative to recreational alligator hunting; to provide for a special
3 season; to provide for taking alligators; to provide for possession; to provide for
4 quotas; to provide for season modifications; to provide for residency; to provide for
5 methods of taking; to provide for harvest hours; to provide for licenses; to provide
6 for tags; to provide for prohibitions; to provide for violations; to provide for
7 Louisiana Wildlife Commission authority; and to provide for related matters.
8 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
9 Section 1. R.S. 56:256 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
10 §256. Recreational alligator harvest; penalties
11 A. Notwithstanding the provisions of R.S. 56:8(92), which defines
12 alligators as nongame quadrupeds, the commission may establish a special
13 recreational alligator season, independent of the traditional commercial
14 alligator season set pursuant to this Subpart.
15 B. The commission may establish the season for the recreational taking
16 or possession of alligators by area within the state. The commission may also
17 establish harvest quotas or tag allotments per area of the state. The commission

3 C. Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, the commission
4 may prescribe residency requirements, recreational methods of taking alligators
5 and hours within which alligators may be taken.
6 D. Any person taking an alligator recreationally shall possess a valid

9 E. Recreational taking of alligators under any of the following
10 circumstances is prohibited:
11 (1) Taking outside of the special season, as designated by the commission.
12 (2) Taking by a person who is without valid licenses or tags.
13 (3) Taking by illegal methods.
14 F. Violation of this Section constitutes a class three violation.

The keyword, summary, and digest do not constitute part of the law or proof

DIGEST
SB 244 Original 2026 Regular Session Allain
Present law provides for a commercial hunting season for alligators.
Proposed law retains present law and authorizes the commission to establish a special
recreational alligator hunting season.
Proposed law provides that the season may allow for taking and possession of alligators by
area. Provides for creation of quotas and tag allotments. Further authorizes the commission
to modify the length of the season by area.
Proposed law authorizes creation of residency requirements and methods and hours of
taking.
Proposed law provides for licensing and tagging requirements. Prohibits the taking of
alligators out of season, by persons without licenses or tags, or by illegal methods. Provides
that violation of proposed law is a class three violation.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Adds R.S. 56:256)
