HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 230
BY REPRESENTATIVE CARLSON
A RESOLUTION
To commend Pamela Granger and McBade Engineers & Consultants, LLC, for ten years of
business in Youngsville and the Lafayette area.
WHEREAS, McBade Engineers & Consultants, LLC, is a Youngsville, Louisiana-
based civil engineering firm that specializes in water, wastewater, coastal systems, flood risk
reduction, and transportation infrastructure; and
WHEREAS, the firm was founded in 2016 and is strengthened by decades of senior
engineering leadership; its team brings more than one hundred eighty years of combined
experience delivering infrastructure projects for municipal, parish, state, and federal
agencies; and
WHEREAS, Pamela Granger is the owner and principal of McBade Engineers &
Consultants; she works primarily with government clients and has embraced her role as a
problem solver through her company's work; prior to founding her company, she spent more
than two decades rising through the professional ranks at engineering firms in Louisiana and
Florida; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Granger and McBade Engineers & Consultants are responsible for
some of the biggest flood retention projects in Louisiana; not long after its founding, the firm
played a pivotal role in addressing drainage issues in her home community of Youngsville
after the August 2016 floods; and
WHEREAS, in addition to high-profile drainage projects, McBade Engineers &
Consultants helps its clients, which include Lafayette Consolidated Government, the city of
St. Martinville, the town of Church Point, numerous independent water districts, and other
entities, with water, sewer, and roadway initiatives; and
