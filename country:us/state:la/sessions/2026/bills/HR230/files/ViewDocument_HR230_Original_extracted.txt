HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 230
BY REPRESENTATIVE CARLSON
COMMENDATIONS: Commends Pamela Granger and McBade Engineering for ten years
of business in Youngsville and the Lafayette area
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Pamela Granger and McBade Engineers & Consultants, LLC, for ten years of
3 business in Youngsville and the Lafayette area.
4 WHEREAS, McBade Engineers & Consultants, LLC, is a Youngsville, Louisiana-
5 based civil engineering firm that specializes in water, wastewater, coastal systems, flood risk
6 reduction, and transportation infrastructure; and
7 WHEREAS, the firm was founded in 2016 and is strengthened by decades of senior
8 engineering leadership; its team brings more than one hundred eighty years of combined
9 experience delivering infrastructure projects for municipal, parish, state, and federal
10 agencies; and
11 WHEREAS, Pamela Granger is the owner and principal of McBade Engineers &
12 Consultants; she works primarily with government clients and has embraced her role as a
13 problem solver through her company's work; prior to founding her company, she spent more
14 than two decades rising through the professional ranks at engineering firms in Louisiana and
15 Florida; and
16 WHEREAS, Ms. Granger and McBade Engineers & Consultants are responsible for
17 some of the biggest flood retention projects in Louisiana; not long after its founding, the firm
18 played a pivotal role in addressing drainage issues in her home community of Youngsville
19 after the August 2016 floods; and
20 WHEREAS, in addition to high-profile drainage projects, McBade Engineers &
21 Consultants helps its clients, which include Lafayette Consolidated Government, the city of
