HOUSE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
971 by Representative Stagni
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, line 18, after "later than" and before "the Louisiana" delete "October 1, 2026,"
3 and insert "ninety days after the effective date of this Act"
4 AMENDMENT NO. 2
5 On page 1, after line 19 insert the following:
6 "Section 3.(A) The provisions of Sections 1 and 2 of this Act shall become effective
7 when an Act of the Louisiana Legislature containing a specific appropriation of monies for
8 the implementation of the provisions of this Act becomes effective.
9 (B) The provisions of this Section shall become effective upon signature by the
10 governor or, if not signed by the governor, upon expiration of the time for bills to become
11 law without signature by the governor, as provided by Article III, Section 18 of the
12 Constitution of Louisiana. If vetoed by the governor and subsequently approved by the
13 legislature, the provisions of this Section shall become effective on the day following such
14 approval."
