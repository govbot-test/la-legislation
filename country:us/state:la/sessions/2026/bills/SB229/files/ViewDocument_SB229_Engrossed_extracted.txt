SB 229 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Bass
Authorizes the Board of Supervisors of Louisiana State University and Agricultural and
Mechanical College and the commissioner of administration to transfer certain property in
Bossier Parish. Provides for the reservation of mineral rights to the state.
Requires sales to be conducted in accordance with present law methods for sales of surplus
state-owned property, which includes auction, sealed bids, and by licensed real estate broker.
Effective upon signature of the governor or lapse of time for gubernatorial action.
