10 PART V. CELL CULTURED FOOD PRODUCTS
11 §4750. Short title
12 This Part shall be known and may be cited as the "Cell Cultured Food
13 Products Act".
14 §4750.1. Cell cultured food products; prohibition
15 A. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, no person
16 shall manufacture, distribute, sell, or offer for sale, any cell cultured food
17 product for human consumption in this state.

DIGEST
SB 152 Reengrossed 2026 Regular Session Fesi
Proposed law prohibits a person from manufacturing, distributing, selling, or offering for
sale any cell cultured food product for human consumption in Louisiana.
Proposed law defines "cell cultured food product" as any cultured animal tissue produced
from in vitro animal cell cultures outside of the organism from which it is derived.
Proposed law provides that any person who violates proposed law will be subject to a civil
penalty of not more than $500 for each violation. Each day on which a violation occurs will
be considered a separate offense.
Proposed law provides that penalties may be assessed only by a ruling of the commissioner
of the Dept. of Agriculture and Forestry based on an adjudicatory hearing and the
commissioner may assess the proportionate costs of the adjudicatory hearing against the
offender.
Proposed law provides that the commissioner may institute civil proceedings to enforce his
rulings and to seek injunctive relief to restrain and prevent the violation, which must be
instituted in the district court for the parish in which the violation occurred.
Proposed law authorizes the commissioner to adopt rules or regulations as necessary to
implement proposed law and all rules or regulations must be adopted in accordance with the
provisions of the Administrative Procedure Act.
Proposed law provides that proposed law shall become effective only upon a final ruling by
the U.S. Supreme Court holding that prohibitions on cell cultured food products do not
violate the U.S. Constitution.
Proposed law further provides that proposed law does not prohibit institutions of higher
education from conducting research on cell cultured food products.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Adds R.S. 3:4750-4750.4)
