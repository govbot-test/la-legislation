HCAHB268 224 1500
1 AMENDMENT NO. 10
2 On page 2, after line 25, insert the following:
3 "§2926. Student counseling; training and professional development
4 A. The State Board of Elementary and Secondary Education shall
5 develop an advisement policy for the middle and high school grades that
6 local school districts can use to equip school counselors with the skills and
7 information needed to:
8 * * *
9 (5) Document at least one middle school career-related activity in the
10 student's Individual Graduation Plan as provided in R.S. 17:183.2(A) and
11 ensure that career interest inventories are updated in seventh and eighth
12 grades as provided in R.S. 17:2925(B).
13 * * *
14 §3005. Elementary school counselors; availability; guidelines; requirements;
15 counseling services; work time; exceptions; reporting; funding
16 * * *
17 C. Such school counselors shall provide preventive and
18 developmental counseling services to all public elementary school students
19 in kindergarten through grade six in order to prepare them for middle, junior,
20 and senior high school responsibilities and their social and physical
21 development. In providing such counseling services, the counselors may:
22 * * *
23 (8) Consult with teachers in implementing a career development
24 program which, at the elementary school level, includes self-awareness, job
25 awareness, and prevocational orientation and in providing that students in
26 kindergarten through fifth grade participate in at least one career awareness
27 experience or activity during each school year as provided in R.S.
28 17:183.2(A).
29 * * *"
