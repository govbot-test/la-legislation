2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 18
BY SENATOR FOIL
allowing an exception to the homestead exemption from seizure for certain unpaid or
accelerated costs subject to the Louisiana Condominium Act.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To urge and request the Louisiana State Law Institute to study the feasibility of allowing an
3 exception to the homestead exemption from seizure for certain unpaid or accelerated
4 costs subject to the Louisiana Condominium Act.
5 WHEREAS, Louisiana law establishes a bona fide homestead which consists of a
6 residence occupied by the owner and the land on which the residence is located, including
7 any building and appurtenances located thereon, and any contiguous tracts up to a total of
8 five acres if the residence is within a municipality, or up to a total of two hundred acres of
9 land if the residence is not located in a municipality; and
10 WHEREAS, the homestead is exempt from seizure and sale under any writ, mandate,
11 or process whatsoever, except in limited circumstances prescribed by law; and
12 WHEREAS, the homestead exemption generally extends to thirty-five thousand
13 dollars in value of the homestead with limited exceptions in the case of obligations arising
14 directly as a result of a catastrophic or terminal illness or injury; and
15 WHEREAS, Louisiana law protects the right of condominium associations to collect
16 unpaid assessments and fees from condominium owners and creates a privilege for the
17 condominium association on a condominium parcel for all unpaid or accelerated sums
18 assessed by the association, any fines or late fees in excess of two hundred fifty dollars, and
Page 1 of 2

1 interest at the rate provided in the condominium declaration or, in the absence, at the legal
2 interest rate; and
3 WHEREAS, a properly recorded claim of privilege by the association preserves the
4 privilege against the condominium parcel for a period of five years from the date of
5 recordation, unless a notice of filing of suit on the claim is recorded within five years from
6 the date of the recordation of the inscription of the claim; and
7 WHEREAS, the privilege ranks superior to all other liens and encumbrances on a
8 condominium unit except privileges, mortgages, and encumbrances recorded before the
9 recordation of the declaration, privileges, mortgages, and encumbrances on the unit recorded
10 before the recordation of the privilege; immovable property taxes; and governmental
11 assessments in which the unit is specifically described; and
12 WHEREAS, the homestead exemption does not include an exception for certain
13 unpaid or accelerated condominium association costs.
14 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
15 does hereby urge and request the Louisiana State Law Institute to study and recommend
16 legislation relative to the feasibility of allowing an exception to the homestead exemption
17 from seizure for certain unpaid or accelerated costs subject to the Louisiana Condominium
18 Act.
The original instrument and the following digest, which constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument, were prepared by Senate Legislative Services.
The keyword, summary, and digest do not constitute part of the law or proof
or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
