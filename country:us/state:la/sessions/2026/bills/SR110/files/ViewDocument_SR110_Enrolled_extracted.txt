SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 110
BY SENATOR MYERS
A RESOLUTION
To commend and congratulate the students and faculty of the Lafayette Parish School
System for their placement of first-overall in the Louisiana Governor's Games' thirty-
first Annual Elementary State Championship Fitness Meet.
WHEREAS, the Louisiana Governor's Games are the state's premier amateur
sporting event; and
WHEREAS, the Lafayette Parish School System finished first-overall in the thirty-
first Annual Elementary State Championship Fitness Meet; and
WHEREAS, Lafayette Parish secured first place with a total of 2,479 points ahead
of second-place St. Tammany Parish with 2,459 points, and third-place Rapides Parish with
2,428 points; and
WHEREAS, the elementary fitness meet competition involves seven fitness-related
events, including the 50-yard dash, the shuttle run, pull-ups, the standing long-jump, curl-ups
or sit-ups, the 600-yard run, and the V-sit and reach; and
WHEREAS, the top two boys and girls from each parish travel to Alexandria
annually to compete for the fitness title; and
WHEREAS, Lafayette Parish's top performer, Tianna Franks, led the way with 648
individual points; and
WHEREAS, Tianna's teammates Aubrey Porter, Brennon Poullard, and Amir
Alsayed anchored their parish to victory; and
WHEREAS, since 1997, the elementary fitness meet has served as the Olympic
Games for elementary school-aged children in thirty parishes across the state, with more than
200,000 students competing for the state fitness crown.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
does hereby extend a warm welcome to the members, coaches, and parents of the members
of the Lafayette Parish School System's Elementary State Championship Fitness Meet team,
and does hereby commend and congratulate them on this amazing achievement.
