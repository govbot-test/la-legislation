HLS 26RS-1006 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 295
BY REPRESENTATIVE JACOB LANDRY
LEGISLATIVE AFFAIRS: Establishes the Energy Caucus in the Louisiana House of
Representatives
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To establish and recognize the Energy Caucus of the House of Representatives and to
3 provide relative to the caucus.
4 WHEREAS, the energy industry is vital to Louisiana's economy; and
5 WHEREAS, the energy sector is multifaceted, involving fossil fuels, renewable
6 energy, and nuclear power; and
7 WHEREAS, several members of the House of Representatives work or have worked
8 in the energy sector; and
9 WHEREAS, the Louisiana House of Representatives recognizes the importance of
10 fostering a specialized and subject-specific caucus dedicated to matters involving the energy
11 industry.
12 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
13 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby establish and recognize the Energy Caucus of the
14 House of Representatives.
15 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that every member of the House of Representatives
16 who works in, has worked in, or has an interest in promoting the energy sector may be a
17 member of the Energy Caucus.
