A RESOLUTION
To express the condolences of the House of Representatives on the tragic loss of eight
children in Shreveport, Louisiana.
WHEREAS, it is with immense sadness and profound regret that the House of
Representatives of the Legislature of Louisiana has learned of the tragic passing, on
April 19, 2026, of Mar'Kaydon Pugh, Sariahh Snow, Khedarrion Snow, Braylon Snow,
Layla Pugh, Kayla Pugh, Shayla Elkins, and Jayla Elkins; and
WHEREAS, Mar'Kaydon Pugh was a fourth-grade student at Linwood Public
Charter School; he arrived at school with a Bible in hand each day and was passionate about
his faith; he frequently read his Bible during lunch or enrichment class, and poured his heart
into every hug that he gave; and
WHEREAS, Sariahh Snow was a fourth-grade student at Summer Grove Elementary
School; she was a friend to everyone she met, was not afraid of hard work, and gave her best
effort in all that she did; she was always excited to learn, had the most beautiful smile, and
showed kindness to students and teachers alike; and

WHEREAS, Khedarrion Snow was a kindergarten student at Summer Grove
Elementary School; he was a vibrant soul with a curious mind and fearless heart and had a
way of bringing a smile to anyone he met; he found joy in every moment and had a thirst for
adventure and desire to learn more about the world; he brought unique energy to every circle
he joined and cherished the time he spent with his friends and classmates; and
WHEREAS, Braylon Snow was a pre-kindergarten student at Johnnie Cochran Head
Start Center; and
WHEREAS, Layla Pugh was a second-grade student at Linwood Public Charter
School; she was a quiet girl who always strived to do the right thing; she was a friend to
everyone and a constant help to her teachers and classmates; and
WHEREAS, Kayla Pugh was a first-grade student at Linwood Public Charter School;
although she was a soft-spoken child, her smile spoke louder than any words; she lit up
every room she entered and gave the biggest hugs when greeting her teachers and principal;
and
WHEREAS, Shayla Elkins was a kindergarten student at Linwood Public Charter
School; she was soft spoken, had a quiet presence, and gave warm and welcoming hugs; and
WHEREAS, Jayla Elkins was a sweet child who was only three years old; and
WHEREAS, although the children will be deeply missed, the memory of their spirits
shall live on in the hearts and minds of those who were privileged to have known and loved
them.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby express heartfelt condolences on the loss of
Mar'Kaydon Pugh, Sariahh Snow, Khedarrion Snow, Braylon Snow, Layla Pugh, Kayla
Pugh, Shayla Elkins, and Jayla Elkins and does hereby extend sincere wishes that their
family, friends, and loved ones find comfort in their shared memories of these eight beautiful
angels.
