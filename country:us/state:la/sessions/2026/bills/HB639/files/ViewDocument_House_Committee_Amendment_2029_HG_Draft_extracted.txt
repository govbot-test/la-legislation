15 R.S. 18:1463.1(F) and (G) are hereby enacted to"

18 4, delete lines 1 through 3

20 On page 4, after line 26 insert the following:
21 "G. The Board of Ethics shall administer and enforce the provisions of this
22 Section.

24 follows:
25 §1132. Board of Ethics

27 D. Additional Jurisdiction. The Board of Ethics shall administer and enforce
28 the provisions of R.S. 18:1463.1, R.S. 27:63, 96, 226, 261, 316, and 373, Part III of
29 Chapter 1 of Title 24 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950, Part IV of Chapter
30 1 of Title 49 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950, Chapter 46 of Title 33 of the
31 Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950, and R.S. 47:9072."
