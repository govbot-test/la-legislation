SCASB270 3005 1620
SENATE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
No. 270 by Senator Jackson-Andrews
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, line 11 after "R.S. 40:2006(A)(2)." insert the following:
3 "Healthcare facility shall not mean any freestanding or distinct part unit of a hospital
4 providing behavioral health services or any emergency or outpatient department of a
5 hospital."
6 AMENDMENT NO. 2
7 On page 1, delete lines 14 and 15 in their entirety and insert the following:
8 "(3) "Patient" means a person who has a terminal and irreversible condition as
9 defined by R.S. 40:1151.1 and maintains a current recommendation to use medical
10 marijuana."
11 AMENDMENT NO. 3
12 On page 1, line 16, after "permit" delete the remainder of the line and insert "patient use
13 of"
14 AMENDMENT NO. 4
15 On page 2, delete lines 6 through 19 in their entirety and insert the following:
16 "(4) Require a patient or a primary caregiver to be responsible for acquiring,
17 retrieving, administering, and removing medical marijuana.
18 (5) Subject to the policy adopted by the healthcare facility, require medical
19 marijuana to be stored securely at all times in a locked container provided by the
20 patient in the patient's room, other designated area, or with the patient's primary
21 caregiver.
22 (6) Prohibit health care professionals, healthcare facility staff, including but not
23 limited to physicians, nurses, and pharmacists, from administering, storing, retrieving,
24 or assisting the patient with the medical marijuana.
25 (7) Upon discharge, all remaining medical marijuana shall be removed by the
26 patient or patient's primary caregiver. If a patient cannot remove the medical
27 marijuana and does not have a primary caregiver that is available to remove the
28 medical marijuana, the medical marijuana shall be disposed of in accordance with the
29 health facility policy and procedure governing medical marijuana.
30 (8) Develop, disseminate, and train health facility staff on the written guidelines
31 developed by the facility for patient use of medical marijuana within the health care
32 facility pursuant to this Section."
33 AMENDMENT NO. 5
34 On page 2, line 20 change "E." to "C."
35 AMENDMENT NO. 6
36 On page 2, line 23 change "F." to "D."
37 AMENDMENT NO. 7
38 On page 2, line 25 change "G." to "E."
