HB 400 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Fontenot
Abstract: Provides that bond or tax elections shall be held only on the regularly scheduled
fall election dates.
Present law provides that every bond, tax, or other election at which a proposition or
question is to be submitted to the voters shall be held only on the regularly scheduled spring
or fall election dates.
Proposed law retains present law as it applies to elections at which a proposition or question
is to be submitted to the voters. However, proposed law provides that bond or tax elections
shall be held only on the regularly scheduled fall election dates.
Effective January 1, 2028, if the proposed amendment of Article VI, Section 22 of the
Constitution of La. contained in the Act which originated as House Bill No. 393 of this
2026 R.S. of the Legislature is adopted at a statewide election and becomes effective.
(Amends R.S. 18:402(F))
