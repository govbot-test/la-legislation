SCASB253 3005 780
SENATE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
No. 253 by Senator McMath
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, line 3, after "providers" insert "and compounding pharmacies"
3 AMENDMENT NO. 2
4 On page 1, line 8, change "A." to "A.(1)"
5 AMENDMENT NO. 3
6 On page 1, line 13, change "B." to "(2)"
7 AMENDMENT NO. 4
8 On page 1, after line 14, insert the following:
9 "B. No professional or occupational licensing board shall prohibit a
10 state-licensed compounding pharmacy from compounding and dispensing peptides,
11 provided that the peptide is not included in the FDA's list of substances prohibited for
12 compounding or the FDA Category 2 list of bulk drug substances. Once a peptide is
13 removed from the FDA Category 2 list, a state-licensed compounding pharmacy may
14 compound such peptide provided the active pharmaceutical ingredient is obtained from
15 an FDA-registered manufacturer and the pharmacy otherwise complies with all
16 applicable federal and state compounding laws and regulations."
