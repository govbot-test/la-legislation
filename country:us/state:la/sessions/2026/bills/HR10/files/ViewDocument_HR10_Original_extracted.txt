HLS 26RS-1493 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 10
BY REPRESENTATIVE RISER
SPECIAL DAY/WEEK/MONTH: Designates March 11, 2026, as Funeral Directors Day
at the capitol, recognizes National Funeral Directors and Morticians Day, and
commends the Louisiana Funeral Directors Association
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate Wednesday, March 11, 2026, as Funeral Directors Day at the capitol, to
3 recognize National Funeral Directors and Morticians Day, and to commend the
4 Louisiana Funeral Directors Association for the vital services provided by funeral
5 directors and morticians across the state of Louisiana.
6 WHEREAS, funeral directors and morticians provide compassionate care and
7 guidance to families during some of the most difficult moments of their lives and help them
8 honor the memory of loved ones with dignity and respect; and
9 WHEREAS, these dedicated professionals serve communities throughout Louisiana
10 by assisting families in making final arrangements, preserving cherished traditions, and
11 ensuring that memorial services are conducted with professionalism and reverence; and
12 WHEREAS, funeral directors and morticians also play an important role in public
13 health and safety by adhering to strict professional standards and regulations, maintaining
14 facilities, and providing services essential to the well-being of communities; and
15 WHEREAS, the Louisiana Funeral Directors Association has long worked to
16 promote excellence in funeral service through education, professional development, ethical
17 standards, and advocacy on behalf of funeral service professionals throughout Louisiana; and
18 WHEREAS, the association serves as a valuable resource for funeral homes,
19 directors, embalmers, and morticians by providing training, guidance, and support to ensure
20 the highest level of care for Louisiana families; and

1 WHEREAS, Funeral Directors Day at the capitol provides an opportunity to
2 recognize the contributions of funeral service professionals and highlight the important role

4 WHEREAS, National Funeral Directors and Morticians Day further honors the
5 commitment, professionalism, and compassion demonstrated daily by these individuals who
6 serve families during times of loss; and
7 WHEREAS, the members of the Louisiana House of Representatives take great pride
8 in recognizing the men and women of the funeral service profession and expressing gratitude
9 for their dedication to serving the citizens of the state with care, integrity, and respect.
10 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
11 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby designate Wednesday, March 11, 2026, as Funeral
12 Directors Day at the capitol; does hereby recognize National Funeral Directors and
13 Morticians Day; does hereby commend the Louisiana Funeral Directors Association; and
14 does hereby extend sincere appreciation to funeral directors and morticians throughout the
15 state for their unwavering service to Louisiana families and communities.
