FORESTRY. Provides relative to prescribed burning by noncertified prescribed burn
managers. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 3:17.1(C), relative to prescribed burning by noncertified
3 prescribed burn managers; to provide relative to authorization to promulgate rules
4 and regulations; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 3:17.1(C) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
7 §17.1. Prescribed burning by noncertified prescribed burn managers
8 * * *
9 C. The commissioner of agriculture and forestry is authorized to may
10 promulgate rules and regulations in accordance with the Administrative Procedure
11 Act to carry out the intent and purposes of this Section.
12 * * *

SLS 26RS-377 ORIGINAL
SB NO. 126
The original instrument and the following digest, which constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument, were prepared by Senate Legislative Services.
The keyword, summary, and digest do not constitute part of the law or proof
or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
DIGEST
SB 126 Original 2026 Regular Session Allain
Present law provides that an applicant must provide the office of forestry with the location,
date, and time of the prescribed burn prior to conducting a prescribed burn and authorizes
the commissioner of agriculture and forestry to promulgate rules and regulations in
accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act.
Proposed law retains present law and further clarifies that the commissioner may promulgate
rules and regulations in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act.
Effective on August 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 3:17.1(C))
