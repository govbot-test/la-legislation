HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 156
BY REPRESENTATIVES WALTERS, JACKSON, AND PHELPS
A RESOLUTION
To express the condolences of the House of Representatives upon the death of Kirby Shawn
Dawson.
WHEREAS, it is with immense sadness and profound regret that the House of
Representatives of the Legislature of Louisiana has learned of the passing of Kirby Shawn
Dawson; and
WHEREAS, Kirby Shawn Dawson was born on May 28, 1971, to Otis and Sara
Dawson and was a lifelong resident of Caddo Parish; he graduated from Huntington High
School in 1989, attended Southern University, and earned a bachelor's degree in criminal
justice from Wiley College; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Dawson began his law enforcement career in 2002 and served with
the Caddo Parish Sheriff's Office before joining the Shreveport City Marshal's Office in
2005; he served in numerous roles throughout his career, including court deputy, outside
deputy, court supervisor, and assistant chief deputy; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Dawson was passionate about his work with the D.A.R.E. Program,
with which he held leadership roles across Louisiana; he possessed a special gift for
connecting with young people and always took time to encourage, uplift, and speak to them;
and
WHEREAS, Mr. Dawson cared deeply about his community and found purpose in
serving the people around him; he was respected for his leadership and dedication and
beloved for the genuine care and concern he had for others; and

WHEREAS, Kirby was a devoted husband to his wife, Tasha Dawson, with whom
he shared more than twenty-eight years of love, laughter, and partnership; he was a proud
father to Chris, Loghan, and Shawn, and his family was his most cherished gift in life; and
WHEREAS, Kirby Shawn Dawson was an inspiring community servant, and while
he will be deeply missed, his legacy of love shall live on in the hearts and minds of those
who were privileged to have known and loved him.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby express heartfelt condolences on the death of Kirby
Shawn Dawson; does hereby offer enduring appreciation for his dedicated service and
community leadership throughout his law enforcement career; and does hereby extend
sincere wishes that his family, friends, colleagues, and loved ones find comfort in their
shared memories of his beautiful life.
