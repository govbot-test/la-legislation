SLS 26RS-4306 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 149
BY SENATOR BARTHELEMY
review the proposed reactivation of the Norfolk Southern Railway originating at the Alabo
Street Wharf.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To direct the Department of Transportation and Development to review and study any
3 potential hazards of the increasing railroad activity on the Norfolk Southern Railway
4 originating at the Alabo Street Wharf.
5 WHEREAS, Norfolk Southern Railway is a Class I freight railroad operating in the
6 Eastern United States, with key infrastructure that includes approximately a six mile rail
7 bridge on the Lake Pontchartrain Bridge; and
8 WHEREAS, the Alabo Street Wharf in New Orleans was originally a slaughterhouse
9 that was purchased by the Port of New Orleans to transport river and train cargo, including
10 lumber, copper, and sugar; and
11 WHEREAS, this rail corridor has been largely dormant for decades; and
12 WHEREAS, the Port of New Orleans has entered into a fifteen-year lease agreement
13 with Sunrise Foods International to convert the Alabo Street Wharf in the Lower Ninth
14 Ward, Holy Cross, and other St. Bernard Parish neighborhoods into a specialized terminal
15 for importing organic grain, transported by Norfolk Southern trains; and
16 WHEREAS, in 2026, there were multiple incidents involving Norfolk Southern trains
17 colliding with vehicles and eighteen-wheeler trucks; and
18 WHEREAS, some tracks run within fifteen feet of the resident's front doors; and

SLS 26RS-4306 ORIGINAL
SR NO. 149
1 WHEREAS, grain dust is an S-1 high-hazard classified material and can become
2 combustible under certain conditions and poses a potential environmental and safety hazard;
3 and
4 WHEREAS, R.S. 48:390 authorizes the secretary of the Department of
5 Transportation and Development to complete a study of all public railroad grade crossings
6 to establish priorities for improvement, relocation, or closure in compliance with federal
7 guidelines and requires the development of a prioritized plan for implementing railroad
8 grade crossing improvements, relocations or closures; and
9 WHEREAS, there is a potential environmental and public safety issue arising out of
10 expansion of rail activity in this area.
11 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
12 does hereby direct the Department of Transportation and Development to review and study
13 any potential hazards with increasing activity on the Norfolk Southern Railway originating
14 at the Alabo Street Wharf, pursuant to R.S. 48:390 et seq.
15 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Department of Transportation and
16 Development shall submit a written report that includes potential ways to prevent any
17 foreseeable harm to the community with detailed findings to the Senate Committee on
18 Transportation, Highways and Public Works prior to January 1, 2027.
19 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
20 secretary of the Department of Transportation and Development.
The original instrument and the following digest, which constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument, were prepared by Senate Legislative Services.
The keyword, summary, and digest do not constitute part of the law or proof
or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
