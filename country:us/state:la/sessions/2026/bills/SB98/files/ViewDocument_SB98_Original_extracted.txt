Section 1:
SLS 26RS-416 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY SENATOR KLEINPETER
CRIME/PUNISHMENT. Adds certain school employees to the definition of "educator"
relative to prohibited sexual conduct between an educator and student. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 14:81.4(B)(1), relative to offenses affecting the public morals;
3 to provide relative to prohibited sexual conduct between an educator and student; to
4 provide relative to the definition of "educator"; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 14:81.4(B)(1) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
7 §81.4. Prohibited sexual conduct between educator and student
8 * * *
9 B. As used in this Section:
10 (1) "Educator" means any administrator, coach, instructor, paraprofessional,
11 student aide, teacher, or teacher aide, school resource officer, or security guard at
12 any public or private school, assigned, employed, or working at the school or school
13 system where the victim is enrolled as a student on a full-time, part-time, or
14 temporary basis.
15 * * *
