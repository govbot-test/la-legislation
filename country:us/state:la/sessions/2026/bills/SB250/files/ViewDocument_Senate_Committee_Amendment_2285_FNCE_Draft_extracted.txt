14 A.
15 * * *
16 (9) To administer or provide for the administration of an Internal Revenue
17 Service qualified cafeteria plan, including any weight management plan, if
18 applicable. The plan shall be developed, negotiated, and implemented by the office

23 Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950 shall not apply.

25 §851. Authority for employee benefit programs; payroll deduction for payment of
26 premiums
27 * * *
28 W. Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, any enrollee
29 or dependent enrolled in a comprehensive weight management plan offered by
30 the Office of Group Benefits shall pay one hundred percent of the premium for
31 the weight management plan.
32 * * *"
33 AMENDMENT NO. 5
34 On page 1, delete lines 8 through 13 and insert the following:
35 "A. Beginning January 1, 2027, the Office of Group Benefits shall offer
36 enrollment in a comprehensive weight management plan to plan participants
37 for enrollees and dependents. Any such plan shall include the following
38 comprehensive weight management and metabolic health services:
39 (1) FDA-approved medications.
40 (2) Metabolic health therapies compounded in compliance with 21 USC
41 353a and b.
42 (3) Medical evaluations.
43 (4) Prescribing oversight.
44 (5) Follow-up care.

1 (6) Related support services.
2 (7) Longitudinal metabolic health outcome measurements.
3 B. Enrollment shall be voluntary and shall not be a condition of
4 enrollment in any health benefit plan.
5 C. Nothing in this Section shall be construed to require any health
6 benefit plan administered by the Office of Group Benefits to alter existing
7 medical necessity standards, utilization management protocols, or formulary
8 requirements.
9 §855.1. Weight loss medications; notification to enrollees
10 A. No later than October 1, 2026, the Office of Group Benefits shall
11 notify all enrollees of available options for direct purchasing of FDA-approved
12 medications through pharmaceutical manufacturers.
13 B. The Office of Group Benefits shall provide the notification by posting
14 information on its website and by electronic distribution.
15 C. Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, any enrollee or
16 dependent in the Office of Group Benefits plans who purchases a weight loss
17 pharmaceutical product directly through a pharmaceutical manufacturer shall
18 pay one hundred percent of the cost of the medications."
