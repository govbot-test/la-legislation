5 §855. Office of Group Benefits; comprehensive weight management services
6 A. Beginning January 1, 2027, the Office of Group Benefits shall offer
7 enrollment in a comprehensive weight management plan to plan participants
8 for enrollees and dependents. Any such plan shall include the following
9 comprehensive weight management and metabolic health services:
10 (1) FDA-approved medications.
11 (2) Metabolic health therapies compounded in compliance with 21 U.S.C.
12 353a.
13 (3) Medical evaluations.
14 (4) Prescribing oversight.
15 (5) Follow-up care.
16 (6) Related support services.
17 (7) Longitudinal metabolic health outcome measurements.
18 B. Enrollment shall be voluntary and shall not be a condition of
19 enrollment in any health benefit plan.
20 C. Nothing in this Section shall be construed to require any health
21 benefit plan administered by the Office of Group Benefits to alter existing
22 medical necessity standards, utilization management protocols, or formulary
23 requirements.
24 §855.1. Weight loss medications; notification to enrollees
25 A. No later than October 1, 2026, the Office of Group Benefits shall
26 notify all enrollees of available options for direct purchasing of FDA-approved
27 medications through pharmaceutical manufacturers.
28 B. The Office of Group Benefits shall provide the notification by posting
29 information on its website and by electronic distribution.
