Section 1:
HLS 26RS-1907 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE ROBBY CARTER
ENERGY/PIPELINES: Prohibits the transport of carbon dioxide in rights-of-way used for
petroleum transportation
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 30:1116, relative to carbon dioxide sequestration pipelines; to prohibit the
3 transport of carbon dioxide for sequestration in the same right-of-way as petroleum
4 pipelines; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 30:1116 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
7 §1116. Use of petroleum pipeline right-of-way prohibited
8 Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, no pipeline
9 transporting carbon dioxide for sequestration shall be located in the same right-of-
10 way as any pipeline transporting petroleum products.
DIGEST
The digest printed below was prepared by House Legislative Services. It constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument. The keyword, one-liner, abstract, and digest do not constitute
part of the law or proof or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
HB 1136 Original 2026 Regular Session Robby Carter
Abstract: Prohibits the transport of carbon dioxide for sequestration within the same right-
of-way as petroleum pipelines.
Proposed law prohibits carbon dioxide sequestration pipelines from being located in the
same right-of-way as a petroleum product pipeline.
(Adds R.S. 30:1116)
