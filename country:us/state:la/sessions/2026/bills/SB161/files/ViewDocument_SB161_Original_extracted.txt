Section 1:
SLS 26RS-276 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY SENATOR SEABAUGH
CIVIL SERVICE. Removes certain employment eligibility requirements for certain
unclassified state employees relative to the registration of motor vehicles. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To repeal R.S. 42:31, relative to unclassified state employment; to repeal certain
3 employment eligibility requirements regarding motor vehicle registration and driver's
4 licenses; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 42:31 is hereby repealed in its entirety.
The original instrument and the following digest, which constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument, were prepared by Senate Legislative Services.
The keyword, summary, and digest do not constitute part of the law or proof
or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
DIGEST
SB 161 Original 2026 Regular Session Seabaugh
Present law provides that any person employed in an unclassified position as defined by the
State Civil Service Commission with an annual salary of $100,000 or more shall provide
proof to his public employer that he has been issued an La. driver's license and that all
vehicles registered in his name are registered in La. Further provides that any person
employed in an unclassified position who does not meet the requirements shall be removed
and terminated.
Proposed law repeals present law.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Repeals R.S. 42:31)
