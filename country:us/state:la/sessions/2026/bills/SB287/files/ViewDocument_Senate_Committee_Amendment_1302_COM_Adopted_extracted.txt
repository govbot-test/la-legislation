Section 1:
SCASB287 2810 1302
SENATE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
International Affairs to Original Senate Bill No. 287 by Senator Reese
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, line 3, change "R.S. 6:1389(C)," to "R.S. 6:1389(B), (C),"
3 AMENDMENT NO. 2
4 On page 1, at the end of line 10, change "R.S. 6:1389(C)," to "R.S. 6:1389(B), (C),"
5 AMENDMENT NO. 3
6 On page 1, between lines 14 and 15, insert:
7 "B.(1) The owner or operator of a virtual currency kiosk shall wait
8 seventy-two hours before processing a user's virtual currency kiosk transaction or
9 shall, at such owner's or operator's cost and within seventy-two hours after a virtual
10 currency kiosk transaction, allow the user to cancel and receive a full refund for the
11 virtual currency kiosk transaction.
12 (2) Notwithstanding the provisions of Paragraph (1) of this Subsection
13 or any other provision of law to the contrary, the owner or operator of a virtual
14 currency kiosk shall, at the owner or operator's cost, allow the user to cancel
15 and receive a full refund for a virtual currency kiosk transaction at any time if
16 the owner or operator was not licensed under the provisions of this Chapter at
17 the time of the transaction."
18 AMENDMENT NO. 4
19 On page 1, end of line 17, insert:
20 "A virtual currency kiosk owner or operator's response shall clearly disclose all
21 requirements for a refund.
22 (2) For a refund request for suspected fraudulent activity, an owner or
23 operator may require a user to provide proof of a police report or other
24 governmental entity report of the suspected fraudulent activity and proof of
25 identification. Submitting either a police report or proof of a complaint
26 submitted to the Internet Crime Complaint Center to the owner or operator of
27 a virtual currency kiosk shall constitute suspected fraudulent activity."
28 AMENDMENT NO. 5
29 On page 2, delete lines 1 and 2, and insert:
30 "(3) Any refund requested pursuant to this Subsection shall be
31 processed and completed either within ninety calendar days from the user's
32 initial request or ninety calendar days from when the user submitted proof
33 of a police or other governmental entity report of the suspected fraudulent
34 activity and proof of identification, if such requirements are part of the
35 owner or operator's refund policy and these requirements were clearly
36 communicated to the user in compliance with Paragraph (1) of this
37 Subsection, whichever is later."
