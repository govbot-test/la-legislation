HB 596 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session McCormick
Abstract: Creates a plugging credit that operators may earn by plugging wells that are at
least 10 years old, which can be applied to reduce the operator's inactive well
assessment obligations.
Present law authorizes the department to charge operators an inactive well assessment for
wells that have been inactive for a certain period of time.
Proposed law retains present law.
Proposed law establishes a plugging credit that operators can apply against the amount of
inactive well assessments owed.
Proposed law provides that an operator can obtain a credit for plugging any well that is at
least 10 years old, calculated at $4.50 per foot of well plugged, which will be applied against
the inactive well assessment the operator owes on a dollar-for-dollar basis.
