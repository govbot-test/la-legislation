BY SENATOR BASS
PHARMACEUTICALS. Provides for prescription drug pricing. (1/1/27)
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 22:1867.1, relative to the pricing of drugs; to provide for restrictions; to
3 provide for an effective date; and to provide for related matters.
4 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
5 Section 1. R.S. 22:1867.1 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
6 §1867.1. Drug pricing
7 A. A pharmaceutical manufacturer shall not offer for the use of an Office
8 of Group Benefits insurance plan or any other state sponsored insurance plan
9 any drug at a price greater than the price Medicare pays for the same drug.
10 B. An insurer, pharmacy benefit manager, or person acting on behalf of
11 a pharmacy benefit manager shall not provide reimbursement on behalf of an
12 Office of Group Benefits insurance plan or any other state sponsored insurance
13 plan for any name brand drug, biosimilar, or generic drug that is less than the
14 price Medicare pays for the same drug.
15 C. The commissioner shall enforce the provisions of this Section and
16 promulgate any rules and regulations that are necessary to carry out the
17 provisions.

DIGEST
SB 369 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Bass
Proposed law prohibits a drug manufacturer from selling a drug in this state at a higher price
than the price Medicare pays for the same drug of an Office of Group Benefits (OGB) or
other state sponsored insurance plan.
Proposed law prohibits an insurer or pharmacy benefit manager from providing
reimbursement for any drug less than the price Medicare pays for the same drug of an OGB
or other state sponsored insurance plan.
Effective January 1, 2027.
(Adds R.S. 22:1867.1)
