HLS 26RS-1854 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 114
BY REPRESENTATIVE MACK
COMMENDATIONS: Commends Hannuh Ray of Livingston, Louisiana, on being named
2026 National Little Miss International Girl Queen
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Hannuh Ray of Livingston, Louisiana, on being named 2026 National Little
3 Miss International Girl Queen.
4 WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize Hannuh Ray for her achievements and to
5 commend her on being named the 2026 National Little Miss International Girl Queen; and
6 WHEREAS, the International Girl Pageant is a premier youth development program
7 designed to empower, inspire, and celebrate young leaders; the core values of the program
8 include kindness, friendship, compassion, and respect; and
9 WHEREAS, the International Girl Pageant is a three-tier system that begins at the
10 local level and advances through regional and state events, and the program concludes with
11 the prestigious International Grand Finals; and
12 WHEREAS, the International Girl Pageant is recognized as one of the most sought-
13 after programs in youth development, and the program provides a platform for its
14 participants to shine on stage, in their communities, and at school; and
15 WHEREAS, Hannuh Ray is a resident of Livingston, Louisiana, who has been named
16 the 2026 National Little Miss International Girl Queen; she is truly deserving of the highest
17 recognition for her incredible achievement.
18 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
19 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Hannuh Ray of Livingston, Louisiana, on
20 being named 2026 National Little Miss International Girl Queen and does hereby extend
21 sincere wishes that she continues her excellent work and prospers in all of her endeavors.
