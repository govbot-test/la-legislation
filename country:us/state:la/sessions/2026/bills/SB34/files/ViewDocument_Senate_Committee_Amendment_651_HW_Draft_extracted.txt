7 On page 1, between lines 15 and 16 insert the following:
8 "C. A Level II Endangered Missing Child Advisory shall be identified as
9 "Bryan's Call" if the missing child is:
10 (1) Aged seventeen or younger.
11 (2) Not a risk to the general public.
12 (3) Believed to be suffering from any of the following:
13 (a) A developmental or cognitive impairment, including but not limited to
14 autism spectrum disorder (ASD), traumatic brain injury (TBI), Down syndrome, or
15 Sanfilippo syndrome.
16 (b) An irreversible deterioration of intellectual faculties that makes them unable
17 to meet their own needs or to seek help without assistance.
18 D. In addition to the procedures established pursuant to R.S. 40:2030.1 et seq.,
19 a Silver Alert may be issued for missing persons aged eighteen and older who are
20 believed to be suffering from a developmental or cognitive impairment, including
21 Alzheimer's Disease, Dementia, ASD, TBI, or an irreversible deterioration of
22 intellectual faculties that makes them unable to meet their own needs or to seek help
23 without assistance, and that is not a risk to the general public."
