1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 29:43, relative to foreign adversaries and agents of foreign adversaries; to
3 provide for the protection of military bases; to provide with respect to the ownership
4 of immovable property by foreign adversaries and agents of foreign adversaries; to
5 provide for the expropriation of property owned or controlled by foreign adversaries
6 or agents of foreign adversaries under certain circumstances; to provide with respect
7 to the constitutional police powers of the state; to provide with respect to threats to
8 public health and safety; to provide for processes; to provide for voluntary
9 divestment of immovable property; to provide for definitions; to provide for
10 declarations; to provide for an effective date; and to provide for related matters.
11 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
12 Section 1. R.S. 29:43 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
13 §43. Protection of military bases; prohibited ownership of immovable property
14 by foreign adversaries and agents of foreign adversaries
15 A. The legislature hereby declares it is the policy of this state that
16 ownership of immovable property in proximity to military bases by foreign
17 adversaries or agents of foreign adversaries presents a threat to public health
18 and safety that warrants reasonable statutory restrictions and the reasonable
19 exercise of the constitutional police powers of the state.
20 B. Any immovable property that is purchased, leased, possessed, owned,
21 held in interest, or in any manner under the control of a foreign adversary or
22 an agent or mandatory of a foreign adversary that is located within fifty miles
23 of a military base may be expropriated by the Military Department, state of
24 Louisiana, regardless of the date that the immovable property was acquired.

1 C. If the Military Department, state of Louisiana, files a petition to

10 D. Property acquisitions conducted pursuant to this Section shall be
11 subject to the provisions of R.S. 38:3101 et seq.
12 E.(1) Notwithstanding any provision of this Section to the contrary, any
13 foreign adversary or any agent or mandatary of a foreign adversary, as defined
14 in this Section, that owns or holds any immovable property subject to the
15 provisions of Subsection (B) of this Section may, prior to the final judgement of
16 expropriation, voluntarily divest itself of such property by bona fide sale,
17 transfer, or conveyance to a natural person or juridical person that is not a
18 foreign adversary and is not an agent or mandatary of a foreign adversary.
19 (2) Upon completion of a valid transfer of the immovable property in
20 accordance with this Paragraph, the Military Department, state of Louisiana
21 shall notify the court that the property shall no longer be subject to
22 expropriation under this Section and the court shall dismiss the suit, provided
23 that the transferee is not acting, directly or indirectly, on behalf of or under the
24 direction or control of a foreign adversary.
25 F. Notwithstanding any provision of this Section to the contrary, no
26 immovable or movable property shall be taken, damaged, or expropriated by
27 the state or any of its political subdivisions pursuant to this Section except in
28 accordance with Louisiana Constitution Article I, Section 4, which requires that
29 property shall not be taken or damaged except for public purposes and with just
30 compensation paid to the owner. Therefore, any expropriation authorized

4 G. Unless otherwise indicated, the following definitions shall apply to this
5 Section:
6 (1) "Agent of a foreign adversary" or "mandatary of a foreign
7 adversary" means a juridical person or a natural person that serves as a
8 mandatary, as provided in Louisiana Civil Code Article 2989, for a principal
9 that is a foreign adversary and meets one or more of the following criteria:
10 (a) Is under direction of a foreign adversary that has the power to direct
11 or cause the direction of the management or policies of the juridical person
12 whether through ownership of securities, by contract, or otherwise.
13 (b) Directly or indirectly has the right to vote fifty percent or more of the
14 voting interests of an entity that is owned or controlled by a foreign adversary
15 or is entitled to fifty percent or more of its profits and is, therefore, presumed
16 to have the power to direct or cause the direction of the management or policies
17 of the juridical person.
18 (c) Is a juridical person that meets any of the following:
19 (i) Is headquartered in a country identified as a foreign adversary.
20 (ii) Is held or controlled, either directly or indirectly, by a government
21 of a foreign adversary.
22 (iii) Is owned by, or the majority of stock or other ownership interest is
23 held or controlled by, any person who is a citizen of a foreign adversary, unless
24 that person is a legal permanent resident of the United States.
25 (d) Is a juridical person or a subsidiary that is owned by, or the majority
26 of stock or other ownership interest of which is held or controlled by, a
27 corporation or entity described in Subparagraph (c) of this Paragraph.
28 (e) Any individual person who is a citizen of a foreign adversary, unless
29 that person is a legal permanent resident of the United States and does not
30 otherwise meet any of the criteria provided in Subparagraphs (a) through (d)
