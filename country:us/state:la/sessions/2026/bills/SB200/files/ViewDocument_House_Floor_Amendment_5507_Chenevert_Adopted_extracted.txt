6 AMENDMENT NO. 3
7 On page 2, between lines 18 and 19, insert the following:
8 "E.(1) Notwithstanding any provision of this Section to the contrary, any
9 foreign adversary or any agent or mandatary of a foreign adversary, as defined
10 in this Section, that owns or holds any immovable property subject to the
11 provisions of Subsection (B) of this Section may, prior to the final judgement of
12 expropriation, voluntarily divest itself of such property by bona fide sale,
13 transfer, or conveyance to a natural person or juridical person that is not a
14 foreign adversary and is not an agent or mandatary of a foreign adversary.
15 (2) Upon completion of a valid transfer of the immovable property in
16 accordance with this Paragraph, the Military Department, state of Louisiana
17 shall notify the court that the property shall no longer be subject to
18 expropriation under this Section and the court shall dismiss the suit, provided
19 that the transferee is not acting, directly or indirectly, on behalf of or under the
20 direction or control of a foreign adversary.
21 F. Notwithstanding any provision of this Section to the contrary, no
22 immovable or movable property shall be taken, damaged, or expropriated by
23 the state or any of its political subdivisions pursuant to this Section except in
24 accordance with Louisiana Constitution Article I, Section 4, which requires that
25 property shall not be taken or damaged except for public purposes and with just
26 compensation paid to the owner. Therefore, any expropriation authorized
27 herein shall be conditioned upon the payment of just compensation to the full
28 extent of the owner’s loss and no transfer of ownership shall be effective until
29 such compensation has been paid."
