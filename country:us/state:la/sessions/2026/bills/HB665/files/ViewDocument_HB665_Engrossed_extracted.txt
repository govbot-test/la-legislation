HLS 26RS-860 ENGROSSED
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE MACK
FISHING/NETS: Prohibits hoop nets and seines from being utilized in North Pass and
Manchac Pass
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 56:322.3 and to repeal R.S. 56:322.3, relative to hoop nets and seines; to
3 prohibit the use of hoop nets and seines in North Pass and Manchac Pass; and to
4 provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 56:322.3 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
7 §322.3. Hoop nets and seines; prohibition in North Pass and Manchac Pass
8 Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, the use of hoop
9 nets and seines shall be prohibited in North Pass and Manchac Pass from Interstate
10 55 to Lake Pontchartrain from May first to August thirty-first.
11 Section 2. R.S. 56:322.3 is hereby repealed in its entirety.
12 Section 3.(A) This Section and Section 1 shall become effective August 1, 2026.
13 (B) Section 2 shall become effective August 1, 2028.

2. Add a two-year sunset on the prohibition.
