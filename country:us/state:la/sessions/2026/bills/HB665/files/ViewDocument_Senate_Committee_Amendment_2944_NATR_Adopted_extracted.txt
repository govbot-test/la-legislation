7 On page 1, delete lines 7 through 13 and insert:
8 "§322.3. Hoop nets and seines; restrictions in North Pass and Manchac Pass
9 A. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, dumping or
10 releasing of dead fish from hoop nets or seines is prohibited in North Pass and Pass
11 Manchac from Interstate 55 to Lake Pontchartrain.
12 B. Tending of hoop nets and seines is restricted to the period of time from
13 thirty minutes before sunrise to thirty minutes after sunset.
14 C. Violation of any provision of this Section constitutes a class 2-A
15 violation."
