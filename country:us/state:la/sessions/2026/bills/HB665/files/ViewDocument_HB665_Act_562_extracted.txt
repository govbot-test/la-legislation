Section 1:
ENROLLED
ACT No. 562
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE MACK
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 56:322.3, relative to hoop nets and seines; to restrict the use of hoop nets and
3 seines in North Pass and Manchac Pass; and to provide for related matters.
4 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
5 Section 1. R.S. 56:322.3 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
6 §322.3. Hoop nets and seines; restrictions in North Pass and Manchac Pass
7 A. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, dumping or
8 releasing of dead fish from hoop nets or seines is prohibited in North Pass and Pass
9 Manchac from Interstate 55 to Lake Pontchartrain.
10 B. Tending of hoop nets and seines is restricted to the period of time from
11 thirty minutes before sunrise to thirty minutes after sunset.
12 C. Violation of any provision of this Section constitutes a class 2-A violation.
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE
GOVERNOR OF THE STATE OF LOUISIANA
