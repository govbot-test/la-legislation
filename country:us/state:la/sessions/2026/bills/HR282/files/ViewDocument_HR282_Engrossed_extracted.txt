HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 282
BY REPRESENTATIVE CHANCE HENRY
with Louisiana State Police and other appropriate stakeholders
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To create the Task Force on Utility Terrain Vehicles (UTVs) to study the feasibility of
3 implementing safety regulations regarding UTVs.
4 WHEREAS, some UTVs are capable of going upwards of sixty miles per hour; and
5 WHEREAS, UTVs can be made street legal and travel on public roads alongside
6 motor vehicles; and
7 WHEREAS, the UTV market rose above ten billion dollars in 2025; and
8 WHEREAS, the Consumer Federation of America reported seventy-eight UTV
9 fatalities in 2024.
10 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
11 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby establish and provide for the Task Force on Utility
12 Terrain Vehicles to study laws and regulations for the safe operation of utility terrain
13 vehicles and to provide recommendations or proposals for legislation.
14 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that any recommendations or proposals shall be
15 submitted by written report to the House Committee on Transportation, Highways and
16 Public Works and to the David R. Poynter Legislative Research Library as required by R.S.
17 24:772 before the convening of the 2027 Regular Session.
18 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the task force shall consist of the following
19 members:
20 (1) The superintendent of Louisiana State Police or his designee.
21 (2) The executive director of Louisiana Sheriffs' Association or his designee.

13 later than August 1, 2026, and once every two months thereafter.
14 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a majority of the task force constitutes a quorum
15 for the transaction of business and all official actions of the task force require the affirmative
16 vote of a majority of the members.
17 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the members of the task force shall serve
18 without compensation, except per diem or expenses reimbursement to which they may be
19 individually entitled as members of their constituent organizations.
20 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that meetings of the task force are subject to the
21 Open Meetings Law, R.S. 42:11 et seq., and records of the task force are subject to the
22 Public Records Law, R.S. 44:1 et seq.
