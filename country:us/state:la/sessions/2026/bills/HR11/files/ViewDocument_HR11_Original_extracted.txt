HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 11
BY REPRESENTATIVE CARVER
COMMENDATIONS: Commends The Krewe of Eve on the occasion of its fortieth
anniversary
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend the Krewe of Eve on the occasion of its fortieth anniversary.
3 WHEREAS, the Krewe of Eve was formed in 1986 by six women with a shared
4 vision to form an all-female Mardi Gras krewe for the Northshore; the krewe began with two
5 hundred sixty members and rode in its inaugural parade on February 14, 1987; and
6 WHEREAS, today, the Krewe of Eve has over seven hundred members and is
7 recognized for hosting one of the Northshore's premier parades; as a night parade, it is
8 renowned for its vibrant displays of color, music, and pageantry, and its family-friendly
9 atmosphere draws massive crowds of eager parade goers; and
10 WHEREAS, in addition to the Krewe of Eve's iconic parade, it supports numerous
11 community organizations, including the Mandeville Police Foundation Toy Drive, Granny
12 Gerties Christmas Stocking Giveaway, Safe Harbor, the Northshore Food Bank, and more;
13 the krewe also supports local businesses and has a great relationship with local law
14 enforcement and city officials; and
15 WHEREAS, Dr. Melody Menard serves as Queen Eve XL for the Krewe of Eve's
16 fortieth anniversary season; she attended both Louisiana State University and Southeastern
17 Louisiana University, earning a bachelor's degree and master's degree plus thirty hours; she
18 also holds a Ph.D. in educational leadership and administration, which she received from the
19 University of Southern Mississippi; she dedicated many years of service as an eleventh-

25 his beloved wife, Melody; and
26 WHEREAS, the Krewe of Eve and its members are an integral piece of the
27 Northshore's cultural fabric and are most deserving of the highest recognition for their
28 contributions to the community.
29 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
