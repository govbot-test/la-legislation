Section 1:
SLS 26RS-341 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY SENATOR REESE
PUBLIC DEFENDER. Provides relative to the Louisiana Public Defender Fund. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 15:167(C), (D), and (E) and to enact R.S. 15:167(F) and (G),
3 relative to the Louisiana Public Defender Fund; to provide for calculations to
4 determine the minimum amount deposited into the fund; to provide for the authority
5 to allocate funds; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 15:167(C), (D), and (E) are hereby amended and reenacted and R.S.
8 15:167(F) and (G) are hereby enacted to read as follows:
9 §167. Louisiana Public Defender Fund
10 * * *
11 C.(1) The minimum amount appropriated to the LPD Fund shall be
12 determined by multiplying the combined number of district attorney and
13 assistant district attorney warrants, as described in R.S. 16:51, by the actual
14 assistant district attorney warrant value in addition to a supplement of fifteen
15 thousand dollars per district attorney and assistant district attorney warrant.
16 (2) The actual assistant district attorney warrant value includes the
17 warrant amount described in R.S. 16:11 for each assistant district attorney plus

1 the value of retirement and benefits.
2 (3) The supplement per district attorney and assistant district attorney
3 warrant may be used to fund the office's administrative and contractual
4 obligations.
5 D. Monies in the fund shall be appropriated and used solely and exclusively
6 to provide for the implementation of the Louisiana Public Defender Act and the
7 program.
8 D. E. The LPD Fund shall be administered by the office as authorized by the
9 provisions of the Louisiana Public Defender Act. The office is hereby authorized to
10 establish such accounts or sub-accounts within the LPD Fund as deemed necessary
11 to comply with the provisions of the Louisiana Public Defender Act and the program.
12 E. F. The office shall dedicate and disburse at least seventy-five percent of
13 the entirety of its annual budget and its funds in the LPD Fund as defined in
14 Subsection A of this Section each fiscal year to the offices of the district public
15 defenders and their indigent defender funds as defined in R.S. 15:168(A) in the
16 various judicial districts throughout the state. The provisions of this Subsection shall
17 not apply to statutorily dedicated funds or funds received through the awarding of
18 grants.
19 G. The state public defender shall have the authority to allocate the LPD
20 Fund to provide district supplemental assistance as required by R.S.
21 15:152(B)(1).
The original instrument and the following digest, which constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument, were prepared by Senate Legislative Services.
The keyword, summary, and digest do not constitute part of the law or proof
or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
DIGEST
SB 316 Original 2026 Regular Session Reese
Present law created the La. Public Defender Fund (LPD Fund) in the state treasury, with
monies in the fund appropriated, administered, and used solely and exclusively for purposes
of the La. Public Defender Act.
Proposed law requires the minimum amount appropriated to the LPD Fund be determined
by multiplying the combined number of district attorney and assistant district attorney
warrants by the actual assistant district attorney warrant value in addition to a supplement

Proposed law authorizes the supplement of $15,000 per district attorney and assistant district

Proposed law grants the state public defender the authority to allocate the LPD Fund to
provide supplemental assistance as required by present law.
