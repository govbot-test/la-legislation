10 "Art. 573.4. Running of time limitations; exception; third degree rape
11 The time limitations established by Article 572 shall not commence to run

13 victim. Notwithstanding the provisions of Article 572(A) and except as provided by
14 Articles 571.1, 572(B), or any other provision of law that establishes a longer period
15 of limitation, the time within which to institute prosecution of the crime of third
16 degree rape (R.S. 14:43) shall be ten years. This period shall not commence to run
17 until the crime is discovered by the victim."
