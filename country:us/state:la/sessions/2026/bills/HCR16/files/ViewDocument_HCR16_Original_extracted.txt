HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 16
DICKERSON, EDMONSTON, FREIBERG, LACOMBE, TERRY LANDRY,
MARCELLE, MCMAKIN, VENTRELLA, AND WILDER AND SENATORS
EDMONDS, FOIL, HODGES, LAMBERT, LUNEAU, AND MORRIS
COMMENDATIONS: Commemorates the one hundredth anniversary of Guaranty
Corporation
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 To commemorate the one hundredth anniversary of Guaranty Corporation.
3 WHEREAS, the origins of Guaranty Corporation can be traced to the front porch of
4 George A. Foster, Sr.'s home in Pollock, Louisiana, where he first imagined building a
5 company centered on trust, service, and opportunity for the people of the entire state of
6 Louisiana; and
7 WHEREAS, Mr. Foster served as a member of the Louisiana House of
8 Representatives more than a century ago, proudly representing the same Grant Parish and
9 Pollock-area communities that make up House District No. 22; and
10 WHEREAS, Mr. Foster founded Guaranty Income Life Insurance Company in 1926;
11 for nearly ninety years the company remained under Foster family ownership and provided
12 trusted insurance and financial protection to families across Louisiana, further strengthening
13 the company's legacy of stability and service; and
14 WHEREAS, Mr. Foster also created Guaranty Corporation in 1926; from its humble
15 beginnings in Pollock, Guaranty Corporation has grown into a respected holding company
16 with operations in broadcasting, digital media, healthcare marketing, senior living, and real
17 estate, all while remaining deeply grounded in Louisiana values; and
Page 1 of 2

HCR NO. 16
1 WHEREAS, Guaranty Media has served as a pillar of Louisiana broadcasting,
2 operating Eagle 98.1, Talk 107.3, 104.5 ESPN Baton Rouge, and 100.7 The Tiger and
3 delivering trusted local news, sports, entertainment, and community storytelling; and
4 WHEREAS, the company has expanded beyond radio, developing nationally
5 recognized digital marketing capabilities with Gatorworks, its digital marketing agency, and
6 Guaranty Health Solutions, which bridges the gap between healthcare and hospitality,
7 demonstrating that a Louisiana-born business can lead and innovate on a national stage; and
8 WHEREAS, Guaranty Corporation has consistently fulfilled its mission to "Enrich
9 Lives, Serve Others" by contributing to charitable causes, youth programs, education
10 initiatives, and disaster relief efforts across Louisiana; and
11 WHEREAS, the Foster family, now in its third and fourth generations of leadership,
12 continues to guide the company with the same integrity, resilience, and belief in Louisiana's
13 people that inspired George A. Foster, Sr., on his Pollock front porch; and
14 WHEREAS, Guaranty's employees, both past and present, are the backbone of its
15 success; the year 2026 marks the one hundredth anniversary of Guaranty Corporation, a
16 milestone achieved by only a select few American businesses; the company's longevity is
17 a testament to its enduring strength, innovation, and commitment to Louisiana.
18 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of Louisiana does hereby
19 commemorate the one hundredth anniversary of Guaranty Corporation; does hereby
20 recognize the legacy of its founder, George A. Foster, Sr., and the Foster family; does hereby
21 express appreciation for the company's century-long impact on Louisiana's economy,
22 culture, and communities; and does hereby extend sincere wishes for its continued growth,
23 prosperity, and service to the people of this state.
