DIGEST
SB 11 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Price
Present law provides that, in addition to the actuarially required employer contribution rate,
a permanent benefit increase account funding contribution rate be established and
determined.
Proposed law retains present law.
Present law further provides that, beginning July 1, 2024, and using the June 30, 2023
actuarial valuation, the AFC rate increases on a permanent basis in any fiscal year in which
the projected employer contribution rate declines, with the increase equal to the lesser of one
half of the reduction in the projected employer contribution rate or the amount needed for
the AFC rate to reach 2.5%.
Proposed law retains present law but changes the AFC rate from 2.5% to 3.5%.
Effective upon signature of the governor or lapse of time for gubernatorial action.
(Amends R.S. 11:102(F)(4)(b))
