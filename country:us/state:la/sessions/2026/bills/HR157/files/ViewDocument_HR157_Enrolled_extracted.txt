HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 157
BY REPRESENTATIVE DICKERSON
A RESOLUTION
WHEREAS, it is fitting to recognize Baron Baker for his inspiring determination and
resilience and to commend him for his achievements in competitive weightlifting; and
WHEREAS, Baron is the seventeen-year-old son of Caroline Baker and the late
Brandon Baker and the grandson of Richard and Ann Baker; his father was an international
weightlifter on the Gayle Hatch Weightlifting Team and was on the path to compete in the
Olympics before a brain injury impacted his career; his grandfather served with distinction
for many years as a Louisiana state representative and United States congressman; and
WHEREAS, Baron followed in his father's footsteps as a competitive weightlifter
and began training at Buhanda Strong Weightlifting Club in Baton Rouge, Louisiana; he
competed in the 2026 VIRUS Weightlifting Series 1 in Columbus, Ohio, from March 5
through March 8, 2026; and
WHEREAS, Baron overcame an eleven-hour flight delay and twenty-four hours
without sleep or food prior to arriving in Columbus; despite these challenges, he set
American records with a two hundred seventy-seven pound snatch; three hundred fifty-nine
pound clean and jerk, and six hundred thirty-six pounds of total weight lifted; and
WHEREAS, Baron earned the top position on the United States Youth World Team
and will represent the country at the Youth World Championship in Cali, Columbia, at the
beginning of July; and
WHEREAS, Baron is described by Coach Will Bujanda as an exceptional young
athlete whose dedication, discipline, and character distinguish him as one of the top youth
Olympic weightlifters in the United States; he has grown from a tall, lean, and quiet athlete
into the strongest youth male weightlifter in the country; and

WHEREAS, outside of competition, Baron is an Honors Society student at Episcopal
High School, where he balances academic excellence with the demands of high-level athletic
training; and
WHEREAS, Baron Baker embodies the values of discipline, humility, and purpose,
and his success is a product of not only his natural talent but also his character and
commitment to growth; he is truly deserving of recognition for his outstanding achievements
and for proudly representing the state of Louisiana on the national and international stages.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Baron Baker for his achievements in
competitive weightlifting and does hereby encourage him to continue his excellent work and
always strive for greatness.
