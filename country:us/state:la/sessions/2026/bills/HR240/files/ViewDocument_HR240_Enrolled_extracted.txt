ENROLLED
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 240
BY REPRESENTATIVE CARPENTER
A RESOLUTION
WHEREAS, for more than thirty years, AMIkids Baton Rouge has been an
unwavering force of transformation by empowering young individuals to discover their true
potential and forge a path towards a successful future; and
WHEREAS, AMIkids is a nonprofit organization that has strong ties to the
community of Baton Rouge, Louisiana, and serves as a guiding light for those facing
academic struggles, learning disabilities, or behavioral challenges; and
WHEREAS, AMIkids offers innovative programming and engages in collaborative
efforts with local school districts to instill a sense of citizenship, elevate academic
performance, and provide alternative education to vulnerable youth; and
WHEREAS, AMIkids employs an evidence-based approach and provides
comprehensive services that equip young people with the tools they need to thrive
academically, modify their behavior, and receive the treatment they deserve; and
WHEREAS, ninety-two percent of AMIkids participants have clear education or
career plans their first year after high school, eighty-five percent of the students who enroll
complete the Youth Build Program, and ninety percent of students receive OSHA-10,
OSHA-30, and forklift certifications; and
WHEREAS, AMIkids is deserving of recognition for supporting the community by
empowering local youth.
Page 1 of 2
HR NO. 240 ENROLLED
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend AMIkids Baton Rouge for its work to
empower youth and does hereby extend sincerest wishes that the organization continue to
prosper in its endeavors to provide a strong foundation that helps Louisiana youth build a
successful life.
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
