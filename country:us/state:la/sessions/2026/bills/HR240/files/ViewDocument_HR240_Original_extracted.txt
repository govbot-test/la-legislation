2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 240
BY REPRESENTATIVE CARPENTER
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend AMIkids Baton Rouge for its work to empower youth.
3 WHEREAS, for more than thirty years, AMIkids Baton Rouge has been an
4 unwavering force of transformation by empowering young individuals to discover their true
5 potential and forge a path towards a successful future; and
6 WHEREAS, AMIkids is a nonprofit organization that has strong ties to the
7 community of Baton Rouge, Louisiana, and serves as a guiding light for those facing
8 academic struggles, learning disabilities, or behavioral challenges; and
9 WHEREAS, AMIkids offers innovative programming and engages in collaborative
10 efforts with local school districts to instill a sense of citizenship, elevate academic
11 performance, and provide alternative education to vulnerable youth; and
12 WHEREAS, AMIkids employs an evidence-based approach and provides
13 comprehensive services that equip young people with the tools they need to thrive
14 academically, modify their behavior, and receive the treatment they deserve; and
15 WHEREAS, ninety-two percent of AMIkids participants have clear education or
16 career plans their first year after high school, eighty-five percent of the students who enroll
17 complete the Youth Build Program, and ninety percent of students receive OSHA-10,
18 OSHA-30, and forklift certifications; and
19 WHEREAS, AMIkids is deserving of recognition for supporting the community by
20 empowering local youth.
