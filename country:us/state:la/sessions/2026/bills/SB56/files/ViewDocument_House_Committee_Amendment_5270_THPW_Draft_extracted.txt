12 "§330.7. Police officers
13 * * *
14 D.(1) The superintendent of police shall serve as the chief law
15 enforcement officer of the Southeast Louisiana Flood Protection Authority East
16 and its constituent levee districts pursuant to R.S. 38:330.1(B)(1)(a), and shall
17 be responsible solely for the execution, direction, and supervision of law
18 enforcement operations within the territorial jurisdiction of the flood authority.
19 (2) The authority of the superintendent of police shall be limited
20 exclusively to matters directly related to law enforcement operations, including
21 the prevention, detection, and investigation of crime and the enforcement of
22 applicable criminal and motor vehicle traffic laws and ordinances.
23 (3) The superintendent of police shall not exercise any authority over
24 any administrative, fiscal, personnel, procurement, policy-making, or other
25 non-law enforcement functions of the Southeast Louisiana Flood Protection
26 Authority East or its constituent levee districts.
27 (4) Nothing in this Section shall be construed to grant the
28 superintendent of police authority beyond operational law enforcement
29 responsibilities, nor to diminish the authority of any other official or entity
30 lawfully responsible for administrative governance.
