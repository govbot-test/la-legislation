2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
SENATE BILL NO. 289
BY SENATOR ABRAHAM
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 44:3.7 and to repeal R.S. 44:4(56), relative to public records; to provide for
3 exemptions to the public records law for certain university records; to provide for
4 confidentiality of applicants for certain executive-level university positions; to
5 provide for the privacy of university donors; to provide for the confidentiality of
6 research and proprietary information at universities; and to provide for related
7 matters.
8 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
9 Section 1. R.S. 44:3.7 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
10 §3.7. Certain records of public postsecondary education institutions
11 A. Nothing in this Chapter shall be construed to require the disclosure
12 of records, or the information contained therein, held by a public postsecondary
13 education institution, which records are:
14 (1) Applications, resumes, or the personally identifiable information of
15 an applicant for president, chancellor, senior vice chancellor, or athletic coach
16 at the public postsecondary education institution, unless the applicant has
17 authorized the disclosure of such records and information. This exemption shall
18 expire once an applicant is approved for the position by the governing board,
19 at which time the records of the top three finalists, including the approved
20 applicant, shall become public. This exemption shall continue for any applicant
21 that is not among the top three finalists for the position, unless the applicant has
22 authorized the disclosure of such records and information.
23 (2) Any portion of a record that identifies the name of a donor to the
24 public postsecondary education institution or its affiliated foundation if the
