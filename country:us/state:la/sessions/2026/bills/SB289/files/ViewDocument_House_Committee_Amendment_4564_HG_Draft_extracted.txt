12 "§3.7. Certain records of public postsecondary education institutions
13 A. Nothing in this Chapter shall be construed to require the disclosure
14 of records, or the information contained therein, held by a public postsecondary
15 education institution, which records are:
16 (1) Applications, resumes, or the personally identifiable information of
17 an applicant for president, chancellor, senior vice chancellor, or athletic coach
18 at the public postsecondary education institution, unless the applicant has
19 authorized the disclosure of such records and information. The records of the
20 applicant selected by the institution's governing board as the finalist for the
21 position and records of the remaining top two finalists shall be subject to
22 disclosure pursuant to this Chapter once the board selects the finalist. This
23 exemption shall continue for any applicant that is not among the top three
24 finalists for the position, unless the applicant has authorized the disclosure of
25 such records and information.
26 (2) Any portion of a record that identifies the name of a donor to the
27 public postsecondary education institution or its affiliated foundation if the
28 donor has requested that his name remain confidential.
29 (3) Records that contain proprietary, unpublished, or incomplete
30 academic research and unsubmitted grant applications. This exception shall
31 expire upon the disclosure, publication, public announcement, or public
32 knowledge of the research or the grant application, upon the submission of the
33 related grant application, or upon the grant or denial of related intellectual
34 property rights.
35 (4)(a) Records of negotiations for industry partnerships with the
36 institution upon a determination by the custodian of records that disclosure of
37 the information would have a detrimental effect on the negotiations. The
38 custodian's determination shall be disclosed in response to a request for the
39 records.
40 (b) No information made confidential pursuant to this Paragraph shall
41 remain confidential for more than twelve months from the date of the
42 custodian's determination that disclosure would be detrimental; however, if the
43 negotiation remains active and the custodian makes a new determination that
44 the disclosure of the information would be detrimental to the negotiations, such
