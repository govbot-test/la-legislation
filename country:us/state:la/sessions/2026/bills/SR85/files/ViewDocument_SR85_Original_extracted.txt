SLS 26RS-2236 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 85
BY SENATOR BOUDREAUX
SPECIAL DAY/WEEK/MONTH. Recognizes Tuesday, April 21, 2026, as Wholesalers Day
at the Louisiana State Capitol.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To recognize Tuesday, April 21, 2026, as Wholesalers Day at the Louisiana State Capitol
3 and to commend the Louisiana Association of Wholesalers for its contributions to
4 the economy and residents of Louisiana.
5 WHEREAS, wholesalers play a critical role in Louisiana's economy by serving as
6 the essential link between manufacturers and retailers, ensuring the efficient and reliable
7 distribution of goods throughout the state; and
8 WHEREAS, the Louisiana Association of Wholesalers represents a broad network
9 of businesses that support local economies, create jobs, and contribute significantly to the
10 economic strength and stability of Louisiana; and
11 WHEREAS, members of the Louisiana Association of Wholesalers are committed
12 to maintaining high standards of service, professionalism, and integrity while supporting
13 businesses and communities across the state; and
14 WHEREAS, wholesalers help ensure that essential goods, including food, beverages,
15 medical supplies, and other necessities, are delivered safely and efficiently to communities
16 throughout Louisiana; and
17 WHEREAS, Wholesalers Day at the Louisiana State Capitol provides an opportunity
18 to recognize the dedication and contributions of those in the wholesale industry and to
