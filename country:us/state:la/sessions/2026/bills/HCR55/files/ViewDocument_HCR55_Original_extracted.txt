HLS 26RS-3063 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 55
BY REPRESENTATIVE BRASS AND SENATOR PRICE
COMMENDATIONS: Commends the Lutcher High School girls and boys power lifting
teams for winning the Louisiana High School Powerlifting Association 2026
Division II state championships
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 To commend the Lutcher High School girls and boys power lifting teams for winning the
3 Louisiana High School Powerlifting Association 2026 Division II state
4 championships.
5 WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize the Lutcher High School boys and girls
6 powerlifting teams for their outstanding achievements at the Louisiana High School
7 Powerlifting Association State Meet and to commend both teams on winning the 2026
8 Division II state titles; and
9 WHEREAS, the Lutcher High School boys powerlifting team captured the Division
10 II boys state title by narrowly outperforming the talented teams from Lakeshore High School
11 and Northwood High School; and
12 WHEREAS, led by Head Coach Chris Daigle, the Lutcher boys delivered incredible
13 individual performances during the state meet; Coy Ruiz placed first in the one hundred
14 ninety-eight pound weight class, and Colin Hastings placed first in the two hundred seventy-
15 five pound weight class; in the one hundred thirty-two pound weight class, Chase Oubre
16 placed fifth, and Ethan Albert placed eighth; Blayr St. Pierre placed eighth in the one
17 hundred sixty-five pound weight class, and Elijah Fobb placed third in the two hundred
18 twenty pound weight class; in the one hundred eighty-one pound weight class, Drey
19 Szubinski placed fourth, and Aiden Louque placed tenth; Nicholas Magendie placed second
20 in the super heavyweight class; and

1 WHEREAS, the Lutcher girls dominated the competition and captured the Division
2 II girls state title by a significant margin; and
3 WHEREAS, led by co-Head Coaches Jon and Kelly Magendie, the Lutcher girls
4 showcased the team's incredible talent during the state meet; Avery St. Pierre placed first in
5 her weight class, received the Best Lifter award, set the state record for squat and deadlift,
6 and set the composite state record; Addison St. Pierre placed first in her weight class and set
7 state records in squat and bench; Danielle Louque, Colbee Mitchell, and Gabriella Bivona
8 each placed first in their respective weight classes; Ava Martinez, Kerianne Louque, Zae'li
9 Florian, and Kelis Washington each placed second in their respective weight classes; and
10 Chloe Lorio and Rilee Roussel placed third in their weight classes; and
11 WHEREAS, the Lutcher High School athletic program is led by Dwain Jenkins, who
12 also serves as the school's head football coach; and
13 WHEREAS, the Lutcher High School boys and girls powerlifting teams are most
14 deserving of the highest recognition for their outstanding achievements during the 2026
15 Louisiana High School Powerlifting Association State Meet.
16 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of Louisiana does hereby
17 commend the Lutcher High School girls and boys power lifting teams for winning the
18 Louisiana High School Powerlifting Association 2026 Division II state championships and
19 does hereby encourage the members of both teams to continue their excellent work and
20 always strive for greatness in competition.
