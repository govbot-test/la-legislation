ENROLLED
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 55
BY REPRESENTATIVE BRASS AND SENATOR PRICE
Louisiana High School Powerlifting Association 2026 Division II state
championships
A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
To commend the Lutcher High School girls and boys power lifting teams for winning the
Louisiana High School Powerlifting Association 2026 Division II state
championships.
WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize the Lutcher High School boys and girls
powerlifting teams for their outstanding achievements at the Louisiana High School
Powerlifting Association State Meet and to commend both teams on winning the 2026
Division II state titles; and
WHEREAS, the Lutcher High School boys powerlifting team captured the Division
II boys state title by narrowly outperforming the talented teams from Lakeshore High School
and Northwood High School; and
WHEREAS, led by Head Coach Chris Daigle, the Lutcher boys delivered incredible
individual performances during the state meet; Coy Ruiz placed first in the one hundred
ninety-eight pound weight class, and Colin Hastings placed first in the two hundred seventy-
five pound weight class; in the one hundred thirty-two pound weight class, Chase Oubre
placed fifth, and Ethan Albert placed eighth; Blayr St. Pierre placed eighth in the one
hundred sixty-five pound weight class, and Elijah Fobb placed third in the two hundred
twenty pound weight class; in the one hundred eighty-one pound weight class, Drey
Szubinski placed fourth, and Aiden Louque placed tenth; Nicholas Magendie placed second
in the super heavyweight class; and
WHEREAS, the Lutcher girls dominated the competition and captured the Division
II girls state title by a significant margin; and
Page 1 of 2
HCR NO. 55 ENROLLED
WHEREAS, led by co-Head Coaches Jon and Kelly Magendie, the Lutcher girls
showcased the team's incredible talent during the state meet; Avery St. Pierre placed first in
her weight class, received the Best Lifter award, set the state record for squat and deadlift,
and set the composite state record; Addison St. Pierre placed first in her weight class and set
state records in squat and bench; Danielle Louque, Colbee Mitchell, and Gabriella Bivona
each placed first in their respective weight classes; Ava Martinez, Kerianne Louque, Zae'li
Florian, and Kelis Washington each placed second in their respective weight classes; and
Chloe Lorio and Rilee Roussel placed third in their weight classes; and
WHEREAS, the Lutcher High School athletic program is led by Dwain Jenkins, who
also serves as the school's head football coach; and
WHEREAS, the Lutcher High School boys and girls powerlifting teams are most
deserving of the highest recognition for their outstanding achievements during the 2026
Louisiana High School Powerlifting Association State Meet.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of Louisiana does hereby
commend the Lutcher High School girls and boys power lifting teams for winning the
Louisiana High School Powerlifting Association 2026 Division II state championships and
does hereby encourage the members of both teams to continue their excellent work and
always strive for greatness in competition.
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE
