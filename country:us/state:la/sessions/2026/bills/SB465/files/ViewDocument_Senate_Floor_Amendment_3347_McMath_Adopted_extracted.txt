10 "§1155. Denial of claims; appeal; prior authorization; preexisting conditions
11 * * *
12 C. Any recoupment by a dental service contractor shall be in accordance with R.S.
13 22:1838. A dental service contractor shall not retroactively deny, adjust, or seek
14 recoupment or refund of a paid claim for dental services submitted by a dental
15 provider for dental services rendered in good faith and pursuant to the benefit plan for
16 any reason after the expiration of eighteen months from the date the initial claim was
17 paid. The contractor shall not recoup a claim solely due to a patient's loss of coverage or
18 ineligibility if, at the time of treatment, the contractor erroneously confirms coverage and
19 eligibility, but had sufficient information available to it indicating that the patient was no
20 longer covered or was ineligible for coverage.
21 * * *"
