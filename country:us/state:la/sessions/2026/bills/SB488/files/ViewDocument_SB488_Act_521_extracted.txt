3 To enact R.S. 17:416.16.2, relative to school crisis management and response plans; to
4 provide for the School Safety Drone Response Pilot Program; to establish eligibility
5 to participate in the pilot program; to provide for administration of the program; and
6 to provide for related matters.
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
8 Section 1. R.S. 17:416.16.2 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
9 §416.16.2. School Safety Drone Response Pilot Program
10 A.(1) The state Department of Education may establish a School Safety
11 Drone Response Pilot Program to evaluate the use of drone technology as a
12 supplement to existing school safety and law enforcement response protocols at
13 each city, parish, and other local public school.
14 (2) The State Board of Elementary and Secondary Education shall
15 establish criteria for participation by each city, parish, and other local public
16 school.
17 (3) The department may choose a vendor to operate the pilot program
18 and provide the drone response technology.
19 B. The program shall be conducted as part of the crisis management and
20 response plan in accordance with the provisions of R.S. 17:416.16.
21 C. Each city, parish, and other local public school shall be eligible to
22 participate in the pilot program. The department shall select at least one but no
23 more than four schools to participate in the pilot program, based upon a first
24 come, first serve basis.
25 D. As a condition to participate in the program, a school shall provide
26 school mapping data to the vendor that is chosen by the department to operate

16 G. Beginning December 31, 2027, the department shall submit an annual
