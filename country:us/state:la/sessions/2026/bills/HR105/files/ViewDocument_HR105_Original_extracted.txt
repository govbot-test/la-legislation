HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 105
BY REPRESENTATIVE MARTINEZ
contributions to middle school students
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend the Youth Legislature of Louisiana and to recognize its exceptional
3 contributions to civic education and leadership development among middle school
4 students in this state.
5 WHEREAS, the Youth Legislature of Louisiana is a premier and highly
6 distinguished civic education program for middle school students in this state that provides
7 participants with an unparalleled opportunity to learn about the legislative process through
8 active and meaningful participation; and
9 WHEREAS, students gain invaluable firsthand experience in the workings of state
10 government and the responsibilities of public service through mock legislative sessions,
11 debate, and committee deliberations; and
12 WHEREAS, the program cultivates excellence in critical thinking, public speaking,
13 collaboration, and leadership and equips students with essential skills for lifelong civic
14 engagement; and
15 WHEREAS, the Youth Legislature of Louisiana has earned widespread recognition
16 for its profound and lasting impact on young students by fostering a deep understanding of
17 government and inspiring the next generation of leaders in this state; and
18 WHEREAS, the dedication and commitment of the program's organizers, educators,
19 and participants exemplify the highest standards of educational excellence and civic
20 responsibility.
