2026 Regular Session ACT No. 516 ENROLLED
BY SENATOR BASS

2 AN ACT
3 To amend and reenact R.S. 33:2491 and 2492(7)(h) and to enact R.S. 33:2492(7)(d)(iii) and
4 (i), 2494(C)(8), and 2498(E), relative to the city of Bossier; to provide relative to the
5 classified police service; to provide relative to department and promotional seniority;
6 to provide relative to the classification of promotions for certain positions; to provide
7 relative to testing and competitive employment in certain circumstances; to provide
8 relative to the establishment and maintenance of employment lists; to provide
9 relative to the certification and appointment of eligible persons in the police
10 department; to provide relative to the abolition of classes; to provide for an effective
11 date; and to provide for related matters.
12 Notice of intention to introduce this Act has been published.
13 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
14 Section 1. R.S. R.S. 33:2491 and 2492(7)(h) are hereby amended and reenacted and
15 R.S. 33:2492(7)(d)(iii) and (i), 2494(C)(8), and 2498(E) are hereby enacted to read as
16 follows:

3 classes for which the operation and maintenance of radio, alarm, or signal systems
4 for the respective fire or police service is the primary duty, the board shall establish
5 and maintain employment lists containing the names of persons eligible for
6 appointment to the various classes of positions in the classified service, as follows:
7 A. Names The names of regular employees who are demoted from any class
8 for a reason other than disciplinary action shall be placed upon the reinstatement list
9 for the class from which they were demoted in the order in which the demotions
10 were made.
11 B. Only the names of regular employees who have been laid off in
12 accordance with the provisions of R.S. 33:2499 shall be eligible for entry upon the
13 reemployment list. The names of such persons shall be entered upon the
14 reemployment list for the class from which they were laid off in the order in which
15 the lay-offs were made.
16 C. A name placed upon either the reinstatement or the reemployment list
17 shall remain thereon on the list for a period of four years. The board, however, may
18 remove the name of any person who refuses an offer of employment following a
19 certification from either list.
20 D.(1) Except as provided for in Paragraphs (2), and (3), and (4) of this
21 Subsection, names of persons attaining a passing score on a promotion test shall be
22 placed upon the promotion employment list for the class for which they were tested,
23 from highest to lowest, according to their total departmental seniority. The names of
24 persons attaining a passing score on a competitive test shall be placed upon the
25 competitive employment list for the class for which they were tested, from highest
26 to lowest, according to their final test scores.
27 (2)(a) Names The names of persons attaining a passing score on the
28 promotional test in the Lafayette Police Department for the rank of lieutenant or
29 captain, or in the Lake Charles Police Department, shall be placed upon the

4 seniority, from highest to lowest.
5 (b) An employee with the Lake Charles Police Department who is a member
6 of the armed forces of the United States and is at an official training, active training
7 exercise, or active duty deployment on the date of a promotional test for which he
8 is qualified and eligible shall have his name placed upon the promotion employment

17 according to their total promotional seniority in the next lower class. If two or more
18 persons possess an equal amount of promotional seniority, the names of those
19 persons shall be placed on the promotional list in the order of departmental seniority,
20 from highest to lowest.

24 (8) Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, in the
25 city of Bossier a vacant position in the police department shall be filled in the
26 following manner:
27 (a) If a vacancy cannot be filled by reinstatement or by reemployment
28 as provided in Subsections A and B of this Section, the board shall next certify

1 (b) The appointing authority shall select and appoint to the first vacancy
2 to be filled the one person certified to him who has the greatest promotional
3 seniority in the next lower class. Any remaining positions to be filled in the same
4 class shall be filled by appointing to each such successive vacancy the one of the
5 remaining persons certified therefor who has the next highest promotional
6 seniority in the next lower class.
7 (c) If any one or more persons so certified should refuse the appointment,
8 the appointing authority shall then select and appoint one of the persons
9 certified by the board with the next highest promotional seniority in the next
10 lower class. This procedure shall be followed until the position has been filled
11 by appointment of the one person who has the greatest promotional seniority
12 in the next lower class and who is willing to accept the appointment or until
13 each person whose name appears on the list, has in this order been certified and
14 offered an appointment to the vacancy.
15 (d) If two or more persons possess an equal amount of promotional
16 seniority, those persons shall be reinstated or listed on the promotional list and
17 offered promotions in the order of departmental seniority, from highest to
18 lowest.

26 the class, from highest to lowest. If two or more persons possess an equal
27 amount of promotional seniority, the names of such persons shall be placed on
28 the reinstatement list in the order of departmental seniority, from highest to
29 lowest.
