15 entirety and insert the following:
16 "Except for the positions of entrance firefighter, entrance police officer,
17 entrance jailer, secretary to the chief, departmental records clerk, and for the entrance
18 classes for which the operation and maintenance of radio, alarm, or signal systems
19 for the respective fire or police service is the primary duty, the board shall establish
20 and maintain employment lists containing the names of persons eligible for
21 appointment to the various classes of positions in the classified service, as follows:
22 A. Names The names of regular employees who are demoted from any class
23 for a reason other than disciplinary action shall be placed upon the reinstatement list
24 for the class from which they were demoted in the order in which the demotions
25 were made.
26 B. Only the names of regular employees who have been laid off in
27 accordance with the provisions of R.S. 33:2499 shall be eligible for entry upon the
28 reemployment list. The names of such persons shall be entered upon the
29 reemployment list for the class from which they were laid off in the order in which
30 the lay-offs were made.
31 C. A name placed upon either the reinstatement or the reemployment list
32 shall remain thereon on the list for a period of four years. The board, however, may
33 remove the name of any person who refuses an offer of employment following a
34 certification from either list.
35 D.(1) Except as provided for in Paragraphs (2), and (3), and (4) of this
36 Subsection, names of persons attaining a passing score on a promotion test shall be
37 placed upon the promotion employment list for the class for which they were tested,

1 from highest to lowest, according to their total departmental seniority. The names of
2 persons attaining a passing score on a competitive test shall be placed upon the
3 competitive employment list for the class for which they were tested, from highest
4 to lowest, according to their final test scores.
5 (2)(a) Names The names of persons attaining a passing score on the
6 promotional test in the Lafayette Police Department for the rank of lieutenant or
7 captain, or in the Lake Charles Police Department, shall be placed upon the
8 promotion employment list for the class for which they were tested, from highest to
9 lowest, according to their total promotional seniority in the next lower class. If two
10 or more persons possess an equal amount of promotional seniority, the names of
11 those persons shall be placed on the promotional list in the order of departmental
12 seniority, from highest to lowest.
13 (b) An employee with the Lake Charles Police Department who is a member
14 of the armed forces of the United States and is at an official training, active training
15 exercise, or active duty deployment on the date of a promotional test for which he
16 is qualified and eligible shall have his name placed upon the promotion employment
17 list with promotional seniority credit back to the original testing date provided that
18 the employee takes and attains a passing score on the promotion test upon his return
19 from training or active duty deployment. In addition, his name shall remain on the
20 promotional employment list for forty-eight months following his attainment of a
21 passing score on the promotion test.
22 (3) Names The names of persons attaining a passing score on the
23 promotional test in the Crowley Police Department shall be placed on the promotion
24 employment list for the class for which they were tested, from highest to lowest,
25 according to their total promotional seniority in the next lower class. If two or more
26 persons possess an equal amount of promotional seniority, the names of those
27 persons shall be placed on the promotional list in the order of departmental seniority,
28 from highest to lowest."
29 AMENDMENT NO. 6
30 On page 2, delete lines 19 through 25, and insert the following:
31 "E. Any person whose name is placed upon the competitive employment list
32 for the entrance or lowest ranking class in the classified service who has served in
33 the armed forces of the United States of America during the times a time of war, and
34 has been honorably discharged or discharged under honorable conditions, shall have
35 added to his final test score a total of five points at the time of placing his name upon
36 the list. Proof of such service and discharge shall be required by the board or the
37 state examiner in any manner it deems advisable.
38 F.(1) The minimum and maximum period of time for which a name may
39 remain upon a competitive employment list established and maintained by the board
40 for any of the various classes of positions in the classified fire and police service
41 shall be twelve and eighteen months, respectively, for each list.
42 (2)(a) Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, the minimum
43 and maximum period of time for which a name may remain upon a promotional
44 employment list established and maintained by the board for any of the various
45 classes of positions in the classified fire and police service shall be twelve and forty-
46 eight months, respectively, for each list.
47 (b) Except as provided in R.S. 33:2491.4(D), the provisions of this Paragraph
48 shall apply to any promotional employment list that is established and maintained
49 by the board pursuant to this Part for any of the various classes of positions in the
50 classified fire and police service containing the names of eligible persons on and
51 after August 1, 2013.
52 G. The employment lists for which eligibles are obtained from the results of
53 tests given for that purpose shall be reestablished in the manner further provided for
54 by this Part.
55 H.(1) Except as provided in Paragraphs (2), and (3), and (5) of this
56 Subsection, when new names are to be placed upon a promotion list for a given class,
