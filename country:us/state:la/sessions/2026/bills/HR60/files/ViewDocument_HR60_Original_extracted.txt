HLS 26RS-2117 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 60
BY REPRESENTATIVE MARCELLE
COMMENDATIONS: Commends student parents pursuing higher education
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend student parents pursuing higher education and to recognize the importance of
3 support during their academic endeavors.
4 WHEREAS, student parents are a significant and growing population within
5 institutions of higher education, and they demonstrate exceptional determination and
6 resilience while balancing the responsibilities of raising children and pursuing academic and
7 career advancement; and
8 WHEREAS, by contributing to their own educational growth, student parents secure
9 the well-being and future stability of their families and communities; and
10 WHEREAS, access to supportive resources, including academic support and safe
11 environments for children, plays a critical role in helping student parents complete their
12 educational goals; and
13 WHEREAS, organizations such as Generation Upward Foundation work to support
14 and advocate for student parents as they navigate the challenges of higher education while
15 caring for their families; and
16 WHEREAS, recognition and support of student parents strengthens families,
17 promotes educational attainment, and contributes to a stronger and more equitable
18 workforce.
19 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
20 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend student parents pursuing higher education;
