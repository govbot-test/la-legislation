ENROLLED
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 247
BY REPRESENTATIVE BRASS
A RESOLUTION
WHEREAS, the House of Representatives of the Legislature of Louisiana takes great
pride in recognizing and celebrating Laura Lewis, whose life embodies the spirit, strength,
and resilience that defines the state and its citizens; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Lewis was born on June 20, 1922, and is the oldest citizen of
St. James Parish and the oldest member of Mount Calvary Baptist Church; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Lewis lived in the Brookstown community of St. James Parish until
her husband built a family home on Communi Street; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Lewis is the mother of fourteen children and lives with two of her
granddaughters; and
WHEREAS, despite her age, Ms. Lewis cooks gumbo, prepares stove-top shrimp
boils, and washes and folds the household laundry; the walls of her home are decorated with
awards, plaques, and remembrances from friends, relatives, parish officials, and a former
United States President; and
WHEREAS, Laura Lewis offers invaluable wisdom, inspiration, and perspective to
current and future generations in her community; she is one of the most treasured daughters
of the state of Louisiana and is deserving of the highest recognition for her longevity.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Laura Lewis on the occasion of her one
hundred fourth birthday; does hereby express enduring appreciation for her service as a
living link to the collective past of St. James Parish; and does hereby extend sincere wishes
that she remain happy, healthy, and prosperous.
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
