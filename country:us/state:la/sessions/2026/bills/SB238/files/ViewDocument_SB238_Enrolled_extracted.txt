Section 1:
2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
BY SENATOR MILLER
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 47:2121, relative to the assessment, payment, and collection of ad valorem
3 taxes; to provide for the collection of delinquent statutory impositions; to provide for
4 tax notice requirements; to provide relative to notice and enforcement requirements
5 for certain tax sales; to provide for an effective date; and to provide for related
6 matters.
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
8 Section 1. R.S. 47:2121 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
9 §2121. Tax sale held prior to January 1, 2026; applicability procedures
10 A.(1) All delinquent statutory impositions for which tax sale title was not
11 sold prior to January 1, 2026, shall be subject to the collection procedures set
12 forth in this Chapter.
13 (2) Notwithstanding the provisions of Paragraph (1) of this Subsection,
14 if, prior to January 1, 2026, a tax collector has provided notice to all tax notice
15 parties, notice of statutory impositions due in accordance with the law in effect
16 at the time the statutory impositions were assessed, this notice shall be deemed
17 sufficient to satisfy the requirements of R.S. 47:2127(E), provided that all steps
18 taken toward the collection of the statutory impositions after December 31,
19 2025, comply with the provisions of this Chapter, including the use of the tax
20 lien auction procedures set forth in R.S. 47:2154.
21 B. For tax sales held prior to January 1, 2026, the rights of the tax sale
22 purchaser, or his successors or assigns, shall be subject to the notice and
23 enforcement procedures provided for in the law in effect at the time of the tax
24 sale.

1 Section 2. This Act shall become effective upon signature by the governor or, if not
