Section 1:
SLS 26RS-1107 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY SENATOR JACKSON-ANDREWS
CORRECTIONAL FACILITIES. Requires the Department of Public Safety and Corrections
to provide state inmates housed in local facilities with the same clothing and healthcare they
would be provided at a state facility. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 15:824(E), relative to commitment of persons to the Department of Public
3 Safety and Corrections; to provide for cases in which the person is confined in a
4 parish jail; to provide for clothing and healthcare products; and to provide for related
5 matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 15:824(E) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §824. Commitment of persons to the Department of Public Safety and Corrections
9 * * *
10 E. Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, for any
11 individual subject to confinement in a state adult penal or correctional
12 institution that is confined to a parish jail or institution, the department shall
13 provide all clothing and healthcare products that the individual would
14 otherwise receive at a state adult penal or correctional institution.

SLS 26RS-1107 ORIGINAL
SB NO. 498
The original instrument and the following digest, which constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument, were prepared by Senate Legislative Services.
The keyword, summary, and digest do not constitute part of the law or proof
or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
DIGEST
SB 498 Original 2026 Regular Session Jackson-Andrews
Present law authorizes the Dept. of Public Safety and Corrections (DPS&C) to assign
prisoners under its custody to parish jails.
Proposed law retains present law and requires that DPS&C provide all clothing and
healthcare products that the individual would otherwise receive at a state adult penal or
correctional institution.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Adds R.S. 15:824(E))
