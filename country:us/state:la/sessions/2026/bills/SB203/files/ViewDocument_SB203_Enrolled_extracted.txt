Section 1:
2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
BY SENATOR LAMBERT
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 56:325(B)(4) and 325.1(A)(4), relative to possession of
3 recreational fish; to provide for a geographic location; to provide for possession
4 limits; to provide for fishing licenses; to provide for largemouth bass and spotted
5 bass; to provide for red drum and spotted sea trout; and to provide for related
6 matters.
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
8 Section 1. R.S. 56:325(B)(4) and 325.1(A)(4) are hereby amended and reenacted to
9 read as follows:
10 §325. Daily take, possession, and size limits; freshwater recreational fish; possession
11 of finfish filets prohibited
12 * * *
13 B.(1) * * *
14 * * *
15 (4) The possession limit for largemouth bass (Micropterus salmoides) and
16 spotted bass (Micropterus punctulatus) taken south of U.S. Highway 90 shall be
17 three times the daily take limit when the fisherman holds and is in possession of a
18 valid recreational fishing license and can show demonstrate to the department's
19 satisfaction a landing receipt from a public boat launch located south of U.S.
20 Highway 90 that demonstrates to the satisfaction of the department that the
21 fisherman has been actively on the water or at a remote camp that can be accessed
22 only by water for two days or more. The fish shall be kept whole or whole gutted in
23 separate bags for each species of fish. The bags shall be marked with the date the fish
24 were taken, the species, the number of fish contained in the bag, and the name and
25 license number of the person taking the fish. The fish shall only be in the possession
26 of the person who took the fish. However, no fisherman shall be actively fishing or
27 engaged in fishing while in possession of more than the daily take limit.
