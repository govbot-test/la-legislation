HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 107
BY REPRESENTATIVE JACKSON
MEDICAID MANAGED CARE: Provides for a Managed Care Incentive Payment (MCIP)
transparency report
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 To urge and request the Louisiana Department of Health (LDH) to require enhanced
3 reporting and legislative oversight of the Medicaid Care Incentive Payment program.
4 WHEREAS, the Medicaid Care Incentive Payment (MCIP) program is designed to
5 improve healthcare quality and outcomes through performance-based incentive payments
6 to participating providers; and
7 WHEREAS, the program utilizes public funds and involves multiple entities,
8 including managed care organizations and quality networks, necessitating clear and ongoing
9 accountability; and
10 WHEREAS, the legislature has a compelling interest in ensuring that incentive
11 payments are utilized in a manner that achieves measurable improvements in healthcare
12 quality and patient outcomes; and
13 WHEREAS, current information available to the legislature regarding the
14 performance and financial activity of the MCIP program is limited and often delayed; and
15 WHEREAS, routine reporting will allow the legislature to better evaluate program
16 effectiveness and make informed policy decisions in a timely manner; and
17 WHEREAS, periodic legislative briefings will further strengthen oversight and
18 ensure accountability of all entities participating in the MCIP program.
19 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of Louisiana does hereby
20 urge and request the Louisiana Department of Health to require the preparation and
21 submission of biannual MCIP reports.

1 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that such reports shall be prepared separately by each
2 Quality Improvement Network (QN), the Louisiana Quality Network (LQN), and each
3 Medicaid managed care organization (MCO) participating in the MCIP program in this state.
4 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that each report shall be submitted to LDH and
5 transmitted by LDH to the House and Senate committees on health and welfare, the Joint
6 Legislative Committee on the Budget, and the David R. Poynter Legislative Research
7 Library.
8 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that each report shall include, at a minimum, all of
9 the following:
10 (1) The total amount of incentive payments received, distributed, retained, or
11 recaptured through the MCIP program.
12 (2) Identification of participating hospital networks and participating hospitals.
13 (3) Performance results for MCIP measures, including baseline performance,
14 established targets, and current results on all initiatives.
15 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the purpose of this Resolution is to ensure that
16 the legislature receives timely, consistent, and comprehensive information regarding the
17 performance of the MCIP program and the use of incentive funds, thereby enhancing
18 oversight between formal audit cycles.
19 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
20 secretary of the Louisiana Department of Health.
