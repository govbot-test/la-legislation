ENROLLED
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 180
BY REPRESENTATIVE SPELL
A RESOLUTION
To designate Wednesday, April 22, 2026, as Counseling Day at the Capitol and to commend
the Louisiana Counseling Association for its outstanding contributions to the citizens
of Louisiana.
WHEREAS, Counseling Day at the Capitol serves as an important occasion to
highlight the essential role that professional counselors play in strengthening the mental
health and well-being of individuals, families, and communities throughout Louisiana; and
WHEREAS, the Louisiana Counseling Association is a leading organization
dedicated to advancing the counseling profession, supporting licensed counselors, and
promoting access to high quality mental health services across the state; and
WHEREAS, professional counselors work diligently to address a wide range of
challenges including anxiety, depression, trauma, substance abuse, and family-related issues,
helping individuals cope with life's most difficult circumstances; and
WHEREAS, through compassion, expertise, and a commitment to service, counselors
provide safe and supportive environments where Louisianians can find comfort, healing, and
peace of mind; and
WHEREAS, the Louisiana Counseling Association advocates for policies that
expand access to mental health care, reduce the stigmas associated with counseling, and
ensure that individuals in all communities receive the support they need; and
WHEREAS, the Louisiana Counseling Association is most deserving of the highest
recognition for its invaluable contributions to the people of this state.
Page 1 of 2

HR NO. 180 ENROLLED
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby designate Wednesday, April 22, 2026, as Counseling
Day at the Capitol and does hereby commend the Louisiana Counseling Association for its
exemplary efforts in helping Louisianians find comfort and peace of mind in some of life's
most challenging times.
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
