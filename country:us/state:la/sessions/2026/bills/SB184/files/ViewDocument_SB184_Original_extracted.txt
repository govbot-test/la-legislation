SLS 26RS-271 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE BILL NO. 184
BY SENATOR LUNEAU
PUBLIC BUILDINGS/GROUNDS. Names the LSU Gymnastics Training Facility in honor
of Coach D-D Breaux. (gov sig)
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 49:149.37, relative to the names of state buildings; to name the LSU
3 Gymnastics Training Facility building in Baton Rouge; to provide for an effective
4 date; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 49:149.37 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
7 §149.37. Coach D-D Breaux Gymnastics Training Facility
8 Notwithstanding the provisions of R.S. 42:267, or any other law to the
9 contrary, the state building situated on the campus of Louisiana State
10 University and Agricultural and Mechanical College in the city of Baton Rouge,
11 located on Cypress Lane, and generally known as the LSU Gymnastics Training
12 Facility, is hereby named and shall hereafter be known as the Coach D-D
13 Breaux Gymnastics Training Facility.
14 Section 2. This Act shall become effective upon signature by the governor or, if not
15 signed by the governor, upon expiration of the time for bills to become law without signature
16 by the governor, as provided by Article III, Section 18 of the Constitution of Louisiana. If
17 vetoed by the governor and subsequently approved by the legislature, this Act shall become
