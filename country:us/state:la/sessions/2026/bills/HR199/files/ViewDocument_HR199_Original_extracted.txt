HLS 26RS-3664 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 199
BY REPRESENTATIVE YOUNG
COMMENDATIONS: Commends the Lincoln Preparatory School boys basketball team on
winning the Louisiana High School Athletic Association 2026 Division IV Select
state championship
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend the Lincoln Preparatory School boys basketball team on winning the Louisiana
3 High School Athletic Association 2026 Division IV Select state championship.
4 WHEREAS, the Lincoln Preparatory School Panthers captured the 2026 Division IV
5 Select state title by defeating Southern University Laboratory School, 69-42, in the
6 championship game held at Burton Coliseum in Lake Charles, Louisiana; and
7 WHEREAS, with the victory, Lincoln Prep claimed its first state championship in
8 thirty-four years; the program's most recent state championship came in 1992 during the
9 tenure of the legendary Coach Michael Lyons, who won seven state titles with the school
10 when it was known as Grambling University Laboratory School; and
11 WHEREAS, Lincoln Prep's Jabari Levingston was recognized as the Most Valuable
12 Player of the tournament and scored twenty-eight points in the championship game; Trey
13 Spann scored sixteen points and Zion Hicks added twelve points; and
14 WHEREAS, Panthers Head Coach Antonio Hudson guided the Panthers basketball
15 team to an incredible record of thirty wins and only three losses and claimed his first state
16 championship as the leader of the program; he was a former Grambling Lab standout who
17 averaged over thirty points and ten rebounds per game during his senior year and was the
18 school's first graduate to attend Louisiana State University on a basketball scholarship; and
19 WHEREAS, the Lincoln Prep boys basketball team is most deserving of the highest
20 recognition for its incredible resilience and success this season.
