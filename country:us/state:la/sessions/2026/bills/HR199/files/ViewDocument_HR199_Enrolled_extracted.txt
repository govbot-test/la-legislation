HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 199
BY REPRESENTATIVE YOUNG
A RESOLUTION
To commend the Lincoln Preparatory School boys basketball team on winning the Louisiana
High School Athletic Association 2026 Division IV Select state championship.
WHEREAS, the Lincoln Preparatory School Panthers captured the 2026 Division IV
Select state title by defeating Southern University Laboratory School, 69-42, in the
championship game held at Burton Coliseum in Lake Charles, Louisiana; and
WHEREAS, with the victory, Lincoln Prep claimed its first state championship in
thirty-four years; the program's most recent state championship came in 1992 during the
tenure of the legendary Coach Michael Lyons, who won seven state titles with the school
when it was known as Grambling University Laboratory School; and
WHEREAS, Lincoln Prep's Jabari Levingston was recognized as the Most Valuable
Player of the tournament and scored twenty-eight points in the championship game; Trey
Spann scored sixteen points and Zion Hicks added twelve points; and
WHEREAS, Panthers Head Coach Antonio Hudson guided the Panthers basketball
team to an incredible record of thirty wins and only three losses and claimed his first state
championship as the leader of the program; he was a former Grambling Lab standout who
averaged over thirty points and ten rebounds per game during his senior year and was the
school's first graduate to attend Louisiana State University on a basketball scholarship; and
WHEREAS, the Lincoln Prep boys basketball team is most deserving of the highest
recognition for its incredible resilience and success this season.
