HLS 26RS-410 ENGROSSED
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVES GLORIOSO AND BERAULT AND SENATOR OWEN
HIGHWAYS: Designates a portion of United States Highway 190 as the "Senior Police
Officer Trevor Abney Memorial Highway"
1 AN ACT
2 To designate a portion of United States Highway 190 Business East in Slidell, Louisiana, as
3 the "Senior Police Officer Trevor Abney Memorial Highway"; to provide for
4 implementation; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. The portion of United States Highway 190 Business East in Slidell,
7 Louisiana, beginning at the intersection of Beech Avenue and East Interstate 10 Service
8 Road and United States Highway 190 and ending at the intersection of United States
9 Highway 190 East and Military Road shall be known and is hereby designated as the "Senior
10 Police Officer Trevor Abney Memorial Highway".
11 Section 2. The Department of Transportation and Development is hereby directed
12 to erect and maintain appropriate signage reflecting this designation provided local or private
13 monies are received by the department equal to the department's actual costs for material,
14 fabrication, mounting posts, and installation of the signage, not to exceed the sum of two
15 thousand two hundred fifty dollars per sign.
