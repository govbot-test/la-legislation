4 WHEREAS, the University of Louisiana System (UL System) is crucial for both
5 Louisiana's academic and economic prosperity, thanks to its fundamental mission to
6 emphasize teaching, research, and community outreach to improve the well-being of
7 Louisiana's residents; and
8 WHEREAS, the UL System is Louisiana's largest system of higher education and its
9 universities have a $13.6 billion annual economic impact; and
10 WHEREAS, the UL System is composed of nine distinctly vital and geographically
11 diverse member institutions, including Grambling State University, Louisiana Tech
12 University, McNeese State University, Nicholls State University, Northwestern State
13 University, Southeastern Louisiana University, University of Louisiana at Lafayette,
14 University of Louisiana at Monroe and the University of New Orleans; and
15 WHEREAS, the UL System supports more than thirteen thousand dual enrollment

17 thousand highly motivated students; and

2 music, allied health, engineering, social studies, pharmacy, business administration, and
3 public administration; and
4 WHEREAS, the member institutions of the UL System award more than fifteen

20 challenges; and
21 WHEREAS, the researchers of the UL System have persistently made innovative
22 strides resulting in numerous invention disclosures and patent applications; and
23 WHEREAS, the University of Louisiana System is committed to creating the most
24 educated generation in Louisiana's history, and the system is deserving of the highest
25 recognition for its many achievements.
26 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
27 does hereby designate Tuesday, March 24, 2026, as University of Louisiana System Day at
28 the Louisiana State Capitol and does hereby extend enduring appreciation to the system for
29 its many contributions to the state of Louisiana.
