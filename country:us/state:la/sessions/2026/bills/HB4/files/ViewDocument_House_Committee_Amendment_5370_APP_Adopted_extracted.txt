1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 4, delete lines 3 through 16 in their entirety and insert the following:
3 "(C)(1)(a) The election for the delegates to the convention shall be held at
4 the open primary election to be held on Saturday, March 25, 2028. The governor
5 shall make proclamation and give notice of the election to be held under this Section
6 in accordance with R.S. 18:402(E)(1). Each person desiring to become a candidate
7 for election as a delegate from a district shall qualify as a candidate from the district
8 in which he resides by filing a notice of candidacy beginning Wednesday, December
9 15, 2027, and ending Friday, December 17, 2027.
10 (b) For the delegates to be elected from each of the districts from which
11 members of the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana are elected, the two delegates
12 that receive the highest number of votes shall be elected. If more than two candidates
13 receive the highest number of votes, the candidates who received the same number
14 of votes shall be selected by a public drawing of lots conducted by the State Board
15 of Election Supervisors from among the candidates who received the same number
16 of votes in the election.
17 (c) For the delegates to be elected from each of the districts from which
18 commissioners of the Public Service Commission are elected, the candidate that
19 receives the highest number of votes shall be elected. If more than one candidate
20 receives the highest number of votes, the delegate shall be selected by a public
21 drawing of lots conducted by the State Board of Election Supervisors from among
22 the candidates who received the highest number of votes in the election.
23 (d) The public drawing of lots shall be conducted at the state capitol on a day
24 and at a time fixed by the board within one week after the results of the election
25 become official. The candidates involved shall be given at least three days' written
26 notice of the time and place of the public drawing of lots."
