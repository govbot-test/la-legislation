WHEREAS, Stine Home & Yard, originally founded as Stine Lumber Company,
traces its beginnings to 1946 when lifelong friends J.W. Stine and J.C. Carlin returned home
to southwest Louisiana after serving our country as decorated combat pilots during World
War II; and
WHEREAS, J.W. Stine served as a B-26 bomber pilot in the Mediterranean theater
during World War II, flying approximately forty combat missions over Europe and
demonstrating the courage, perseverance, and leadership that would later define his business
and personal legacy; and
WHEREAS, after returning to Sulphur, Louisiana, J.W. Stine and J.C. Carlin
invested wartime earnings into the creation of Starlin Construction Company, helping build
homes for Louisiana families during the post-war housing boom and contributing to the
growth and development of southwest Louisiana; and
WHEREAS, after several successful years in the construction business, the partners
amicably parted ways and J.W. Stine entered the retail lumber industry, establishing what
would become one of the most respected and successful family-owned home improvement
companies in the Gulf South; and
WHEREAS, built upon the principles of faith, family, integrity, customer service,
safety, and community, Stine Home & Yard grew from a single lumber yard in Sulphur into
a Louisiana-based regional leader in home improvement retail and building materials supply
serving homeowners, contractors, and businesses throughout Louisiana and Mississippi; and
WHEREAS, the Stine family embraced innovation and evolving consumer needs by
transforming the traditional lumber yard model into a modern home improvement

WHEREAS, during the 1970s, the company adopted its well known slogan, "The

also the company's commitment to treating employees, customers, and communities as part
of the Stine family tradition; and
WHEREAS, under the leadership of J.W. Stine, and later his six sons, the company

family-owned, and operated throughout its eighty-year history, with multiple generations of
the Stine family continuing the vision, work ethic, and servant leadership established by its
founder; and
WHEREAS, the company has demonstrated an unwavering commitment to the
communities it serves, particularly during times of crisis and disaster recovery, including its
extraordinary efforts to assist southwest Louisiana residents following hurricanes Rita and
Laura, reopening stores quickly and helping to rebuild families homes and lives; and
WHEREAS, throughout decades of growth and success, Stine Home & Yard has
remained dedicated to supporting charitable causes, educational initiatives, local
organizations, veterans, food pantries, youth programs, and community outreach efforts
through programs such as Stine Helping Hands and numerous volunteer and philanthropic
initiatives; and
WHEREAS, the company has consistently recognized that its success is rooted in the
dedication and hard work of its employees and associates, whose commitment to customer
service, professionalism, and community engagement has helped build Stine Home & Yard
into one of Louisiana's most respected businesses; and
WHEREAS, Stine Home & Yard has earned numerous honors and distinctions over
the years, including national recognition as a leading home improvement retailer and
building materials supplier, while continuing to uphold the values of integrity,
accountability, and service upon which the company was founded; and

WHEREAS, eighty years of continuous operation represents a remarkable
achievement and stands as a testament to the vision, resilience, leadership, and enduring
legacy of J.W. Stine, the Stine family, and the generations of employees who have
contributed to the company's success; and
WHEREAS, the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana is pleased to recognize and
honor Stine Home & Yard for eight decades of business excellence, community leadership,
economic impact, and dedicated service to the citizens of Louisiana and the Gulf South.
