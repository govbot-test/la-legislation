HLS 26RS-1931 REENGROSSED
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 118
BY REPRESENTATIVES WRIGHT AND JACKSON
establishment of a state income tax credit for certain water utility customers
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To create and provide with respect to a select committee of the House of Representatives to
3 study the potential establishment of a state income tax credit for water utility
4 customers who pay excessive rates for residential water service and to report findings
5 and recommendations to the members of the House of Representatives no later than
6 thirty days prior to the convening of the 2027 Regular Session.
7 WHEREAS, the Legislature of Louisiana places a high priority on ensuring the
8 availability of safe and affordable drinking water for the citizens of this state; and
9 WHEREAS, the Louisiana Public Service Commission (PSC) regulates privately
10 owned water utilities in this state through oversight of those utilities' service quality,
11 regulatory compliance, and rates; and
12 WHEREAS, despite PSC oversight of water utility rates, many utility customers pay
13 excessive charges for their residential water service; and
14 WHEREAS, the legislature frequently considers bills proposing tax preferences for
15 individuals facing various forms of financial hardship; and
16 WHEREAS, it is appropriate to call for a study to determine whether a viable
17 solution involving tax policy exists for the problem of excessive rates charged to residential
18 water utility customers.
19 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
20 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby create a select committee to study the potential
21 establishment of a state income tax credit for water utility customers who pay excessive rates
22 for residential water service.

1 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the select committee shall be named the "Select
2 House Committee on Water Utility Tax Credits" and shall be composed of the following
3 seven members:
4 (1) Three members from the House Committee on Commerce appointed by the
5 speaker of the House of Representatives.
6 (2) Three members from the House Committee on Ways and Means appointed by
7 the speaker of the House of Representatives.
8 (3) One state representative from the Northshore Legislative Delegation appointed
9 by the speaker of the House of Representatives.
10 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the state representative from the Northshore
11 Legislative Delegation shall serve as chair of the select committee.
12 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the speaker of the House of Representatives
13 shall designate staff to assist the committee in its work.
14 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the select committee shall submit a report of its
15 findings and recommendations to the members of the House of Representatives of the
16 Legislature of Louisiana and the David R. Poynter Legislative Research Library no later than
17 thirty days prior to the convening of the 2027 Regular Session.
18 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that legislative authority for the select committee
19 shall terminate upon the convening of the 2027 Regular Session.
