HLS 26RS-1931 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 118
BY REPRESENTATIVE WRIGHT
TAX/INCOME-CREDIT: Creates a legislative subcommittee to study the potential
establishment of a state income tax credit for certain water utility customers
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To create a subcommittee comprising members of the House Committee on Commerce and
3 House Committee on Ways and Means to study the potential establishment of a state
4 income tax credit for water utility customers who pay excessive rates for residential
5 water service and to report findings and recommendations to the members of the
6 House of Representatives no later than thirty days prior to the convening of the 2027
7 Regular Session.
8 WHEREAS, the Legislature of Louisiana places a high priority on ensuring the
9 availability of safe and affordable drinking water for the citizens of this state; and
10 WHEREAS, the Louisiana Public Service Commission (PSC) regulates privately
11 owned water utilities in this state through oversight of those utilities' service quality,
12 regulatory compliance, and rates; and
13 WHEREAS, despite PSC oversight of water utility rates, many utility customers pay
14 excessive charges for their residential water service; and
15 WHEREAS, the legislature frequently considers bills proposing tax preferences for
16 individuals facing various forms of financial hardship; and
17 WHEREAS, it is appropriate to call for a study to determine whether a viable
18 solution involving tax policy exists for the problem of excessive rates charged to residential
19 water utility customers.
20 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
21 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby create a subcommittee to study the potential
