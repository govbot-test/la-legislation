SLS 26RS-690 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 23
BY SENATOR MYERS
SPECIAL DAY/WEEK/MONTH. Recognizes Tuesday, March 17, 2026, as Nurse
Practitioner Day at the Louisiana State Capitol.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To recognize Tuesday, March 17, 2026, as Nurse Practitioner Day at the Louisiana State
3 Capitol.
4 WHEREAS, nurse practitioners play a critical role as trusted providers of health care
5 for patients in our state; and
6 WHEREAS, nurse practitioners work to expand access to health care in communities
7 across our state, promote health equity in care, and improve health outcomes for all; and
8 WHEREAS, patients depend on more than four hundred thirty-one thousand licensed
9 nurse practitioners in the United States, with more than six thousand two hundred fifty
10 licensed in Louisiana, to diagnose, manage, and treat chronic health conditions; and
11 WHEREAS, nurse practitioners provide high-quality primary, acute, and specialty
12 healthcare services while emphasizing health promotion, disease prevention, health
13 education, and counseling to help guide patients to make smarter health and lifestyle
14 decisions every day; and
15 WHEREAS, patients' confidence in health care that is delivered by nurse
16 practitioners is evident through more than one billion visits made annually to nurse
17 practitioners across the country; and
18 WHEREAS, more than five decades of research demonstrate the high quality of care

1 provided by nurse practitioners; and
2 WHEREAS, according to the American Association of Nurse Practitioners, better
3 utilization of nurse practitioners through modernized state laws and improved policies
4 creates better health outcomes through a more accessible, efficient, cost-effective, and
5 higher-quality healthcare system; and
6 WHEREAS, more than half the nation, including twenty-seven states, the District of
7 Columbia, Guam, and the Northern Mariana Islands, has adopted full practice authority
8 legislation that offers patients full and direct access to the outstanding care provided by
9 nurse practitioners; and
10 WHEREAS, leading governmental and policy entities, including the National
11 Academy of Medicine, the National Council of State Boards of Nursing, the National
12 Governors Association, and the Federal Trade Commission, have taken notice of the benefits
13 of providing patients full and direct access to health care delivered by nurse practitioners;
14 and
15 WHEREAS, nurse practitioners serve as the provider of choice for many Louisiana
16 residents and play a pivotal role in the health and welfare of our communities.
17 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
18 does hereby commend nurse practitioners of this state for faithfully providing the highest
19 quality of patient-centered health care and does hereby recognize Tuesday, March 17, 2026,
20 as Nurse Practitioner Day at the Louisiana State Capitol.
21 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
22 Louisiana Association of Nurse Practitioners.
