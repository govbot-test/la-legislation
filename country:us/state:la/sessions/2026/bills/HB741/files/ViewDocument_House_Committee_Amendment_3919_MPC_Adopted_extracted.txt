HCAHB741 4024 3919
HOUSE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
to Original House Bill No. 741 by Representative Boyd
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 2, delete lines 5 through 13 and insert the following:
3 "(2) Is a human trafficking survivor as indicated in a law enforcement report
4 or protective order, or as verified by a healthcare professional, a government agency,
5 an attorney, or a qualified third party as defined by R.S. 9:3261.1(B)(6).
6 D. A local housing authority shall not disclose the address associated with
7 any voucher given pursuant to this Section without the informed consent of the
8 person about whom information is sought, or as directed by a court order. Consent
9 for release may not be given by the abuser or alleged abuser of any minor or
10 incapacitated person, or the abuser or alleged abuser of the other parent of the
11 minor."
