HOUSE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
to Original House Bill No. 506 by Representative Chassion
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, line 2, after "reenact" and before "and" change "R.S. 47:463.73(G)" to "R.S.
3 47:463.73(A) and (G)"
4 AMENDMENT NO. 2
5 On page 1, line 10, after "design," and before "fee" insert "implementation,"
6 AMENDMENT NO. 3
7 On page 1, line 14, after "Section 1." and before "hereby" change "R.S. 47:463.73(G) is" to
8 "R.S. 47:463.73(A) and (G) are"
9 AMENDMENT NO. 4
10 On page 1, between lines 17 and 18, insert the following:
11 "A.(1) The secretary of the Department of Public Safety and Corrections
12 shall establish a special prestige license plate for the Louisiana parochial, public, and
13 private high schools which have a minimum of one hundred applicants for such
14 plate. The license plate shall be restricted to passenger cars, pickup trucks, vans, and
15 recreational vehicles. The license plate shall be of a color and design selected by the
16 respective high school student council subject to the approval of the respective high
17 school principal, provided that it is in compliance with R.S. 47:463(A)(3).
18 (2) The Department of Public Safety and Corrections, office of motor
19 vehicles, shall create these special prestige license plates when the applicable
20 statutory provisions are met and the department's electronic vehicle and registration
21 system is updated to accommodate the creation of the new plates."
22 AMENDMENT NO. 5
23 On page 2, line 14, after "A." and before "The" insert "(1)"
24 AMENDMENT NO. 6
25 On page 2, between lines 18 and 19, insert the following:
26 "(2) The Department of Public Safety and Corrections, office of motor
27 vehicles, shall create these special prestige license plates when the applicable
28 statutory provisions are met and the department's electronic vehicle and registration
29 system is updated to accommodate the creation of the new plates."
30 AMENDMENT NO. 7
31 On page 2, at the end of line 20, delete "a blue", delete line 21 in its entirety, and insert "the
32 word "LOUISIANA" centered at the top or bottom of the plate."
