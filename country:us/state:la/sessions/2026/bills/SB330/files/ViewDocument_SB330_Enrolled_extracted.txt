Section 1:
2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
BY SENATOR OWEN
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 32:47(A)(4) and to repeal 32:47(A)(5), relative to speed
3 enforcement measures; to provide for pavement markings in school zones; to provide
4 for school legends; to provide for compliance language with federal requirements;
5 and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 32:47(A)(4) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
8 §47. Use of speed recording devices; restrictions
9 A. Automated speed enforcement devices or mobile speed cameras that are
10 used to issue a citation of an alleged violation by mail shall only be used by local
11 municipal authorities or local parish authorities in the following instances:
12 * * *
13 (4)(a) Upon the entrance to each school zone, there shall be painted a two-
14 foot-wide yellow stripe completely across the roadway with "Entering School Zone"
15 painted in twelve-inch white lettering visible therein. Upon entrance to any school
16 zone equipped with automated speed enforcement devices, the word
17 "SCHOOL" shall be proportionately striped across the lane adjacent to the
18 S1-1 school sign and the lettering shall be white in color in accordance with the
19 current edition of the Manual on Uniform Traffic Control Devices. The word
20 "SCHOOL" shall not be required on nonasphaltic, nonconcrete roads, or any
21 roads determined unsuitable by the appropriate state agency.
22 (b) Pavement markings installed before August 1, 2026, on a roadway
23 not in the state highway system and in compliance with R.S. 32:47(A)(4) and (5)
24 as in effect before August 1, 2026, shall satisfy the pavement-marking
25 requirements of Subparagraph (4)(a) of this Subsection until the roadway is
