HOUSE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS

Bill No. 366 by Representative Edmonston
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1

5 "If the defendant has not been served and fails to appear at the hearing
6 pursuant to Subsection B or D of this Section, the hearing may be continued and
7 reset within twenty-one days from the date of service of the petition. Any order
8 made or extended pursuant to this Subsection shall remain in effect until the
9 hearing."
