HLS 26RS-2574 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE VILLIO
PUBLIC CONTRACT/BIDS: Provides relative to contracts for garbage collection in
parishes and municipalities
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 33:4169.1(A)(1)(d), relative to municipal and parish authority
3 over collection and disposal of garbage and trash; to provide relative to time
4 contracts for the collection, transportation, and disposal of garbage or trash; to
5 provide relative to the term of such contracts; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 33:4169.1(A)(1)(d) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as
8 follows:
9 §4169.1. Municipal and parish authority over collection and disposal of garbage and
10 trash; franchising, permitting, and licensing; assessment and collection of
11 service charge
12 A.(1) The governing authority of every parish or municipality shall have the
13 following powers:
14 * * *
15 (d) To enter into time contracts for the collection and transportation of
16 garbage or trash for a term of up to ten years, with an option to extend for an
17 additional five-year term, and for disposal of garbage or trash for a term of up to
18 twenty-five years, with an option to extend for an additional five-year term.
19 * * *

DIGEST
The digest printed below was prepared by House Legislative Services. It constitutes no part

HB 1068 Original 2026 Regular Session Villio
Abstract: Authorizes parish and municipal governing authorities to enter into contracts for
the collection, transportation, and disposal of garbage or trash that include an option
to extend the term of the contract.
Present law grants parish and municipal governing authorities certain powers with respect
to the collection and disposal of garbage and trash, including but not limited to the
following:
(1) To engage in the collection and disposal of garbage and trash within its jurisdiction
to the exclusion of other garbage and trash collectors.
(2) To grant permits, licenses, exclusive or nonexclusive franchises, or any combination
thereof to garbage and trash collectors and disposers.
(3) To enter into time contracts for the collection and transportation of garbage or trash
for a term of up to 10 years, and for disposal of garbage or trash for a term of up to
25 years.
(4) To assess a service charge against any person provided any service pursuant to
present law.
(5) To regulate the collection and disposal of garbage and trash.
Proposed law authorizes parish and municipal governing authorities to enter into contracts
for the collection and transportation of garbage or trash and for the disposal of garbage or
trash that include an option to extend the term of the contract for five years. Otherwise
retains present law.
(Amends R.S. 33:4169.1(A)(1)(d))
