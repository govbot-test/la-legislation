HCAHB1068 4024 3439
HOUSE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
to Original House Bill No. 1068 by Representative Villio
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, line 2, after "reenact" and before "relative" delete "R.S. 33:4169.1(A)(1)(d)," and
3 insert "R.S. 33:4169.1(A)(1)(b) through (e) and to repeal R.S. 33:4169.1(A)(1)(f),"
4 AMENDMENT NO. 2
5 On page 1, line 5, after "contracts;" and before "and" insert "to repeal requirements relative
6 to issuing a request for proposals or advertising for bids for exclusive contracts;"
7 AMENDMENT NO. 3
8 On page 1, line 7, after "R.S." delete "33:4169.1(A)(1)(d) is" and insert "33:4169.1(A)(1)(b)
9 through (e) are"
10 AMENDMENT NO. 4
11 On page 1, delete lines 15 through 19 in their entirety and insert the following:
12 "(b) To grant permits, licenses, exclusive or nonexclusive franchises, exclusive
13 contracts, or any combination thereof to garbage and trash collectors and disposers. Any
14 exclusive franchise shall be granted only after advertising for bids, reception of bids, and
15 awarding of the contract or contracts in accordance with the public bid laws of the state and
16 other provisions of law.
17 (c) To grant exclusive contracts to garbage and trash collectors and disposers. Any
18 exclusive contract shall be granted only after issuing a request for proposals, or advertising
19 for bids and receiving bids, and awarding of the contract in accordance with the public bid
20 laws of the state and other provisions of law.
21 (d)(c) To enter into time contracts for the collection and transportation of garbage
22 or trash for a term of up to ten years, with an option to extend for an additional five-year
23 term, and for disposal of garbage or trash for a term of up to twenty-five years, with an
24 option to extend for an additional five-year term.
25 (e)(d) To assess a service charge against any person provided any service pursuant
26 to Subparagraph (a) or (b) of this Paragraph. However, in the parishes of Lafourche,
27 Vermilion, Rapides, and Washington, such charge shall be imposed only after the question
28 of its imposition has been submitted to the qualified electors of the parish at an election to
29 be conducted in accordance with the general election laws of the state, and a majority of
30 those voting in the election shall have voted in favor of the imposition of the charge.
31 (f)(e) To otherwise regulate the collection and disposal of garbage and trash.
32 * * *
33 Section 2. R.S. 33:4169.1(A)(1)(f) is hereby repealed in its entirety."
