DIGEST
SB 516 Original 2026 Regular Session Myers
Proposed law applies to the licensing of hospitals with emergency services.
Proposed law does not apply to an off-site campus of a hospital, critical access hospitals,
rural hospitals, physician-owned hospitals not providing 24-hour emergency medical
services, or hospitals owned or operated by a political subdivision.
Proposed law requires hospitals maintain 30 inpatient and 15 emergency department beds
to either be issued a hospital license or renew its license. Further requires full compliance
with federal Emergency Treatment and Active Labor Act of 1986 (EMTALA).
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Adds R.S. 40:2115.34)
