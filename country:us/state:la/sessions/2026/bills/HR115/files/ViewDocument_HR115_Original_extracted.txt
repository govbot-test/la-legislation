HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 115
SPECIAL DAY/WEEK/MONTH: Designates April 8, 2026, as Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity,
Incorporated, Day at the state capitol
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate Wednesday, April 8, 2026, as Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity, Incorporated, Day
3 at the state capitol.
4 WHEREAS, Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity, Incorporated, is celebrating one hundred
5 fifteen years of achievement; the great state of Louisiana has fashioned two grand
6 polemarchs: Richard B. Millspaugh, the seventeenth Grand Polemarch, and Dwayne M.
7 Murray, Esq., the thirty-first Grand Polemarch; and
8 WHEREAS, Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity, Incorporated, is comprised of undergraduate
9 and alumni chapters on major college campuses and in cities throughout the country; the
10 fraternity was established on January 5, 1911, on the campus of Indiana University at
11 Bloomington, Indiana, and its establishment effectively planted the seed of a fraternal tree
12 whose fruit is now available to college men everywhere, regardless of color, religion, or
13 national origin; and
14 WHEREAS, the constitution of Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity, Incorporated, is dedicated
15 to the principles of achievement and service; from its inception, every endeavor of the
16 fraternity was directed towards establishing a strong foundation before embarking on plans
17 of expansion; and
18 WHEREAS, Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity was originally chartered and incorporated
19 as Kappa Alpha Nu on May 15, 1911, and the name was changed to Kappa Alpha Psi by a
20 resolution offered and adopted at the Grand Chapter meeting in December 1914, which
21 became effective April 15, 1915; and
Page 1 of 2
HLS 26RS-3360 ORIGINAL

1 WHEREAS, except for the years of World War I and World War II, during which
2 time several Grand Chapter meetings were suspended, Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity embarked
3 upon an era of expansion and growth; and
4 WHEREAS, Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity, Incorporated, is now organizationally and
5 administratively mature, and the fraternity steadily moves toward a tomorrow of promise,
6 productivity, and influence.
7 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
8 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby designate Wednesday, April 8, 2026, as Kappa Alpha
9 Psi Fraternity, Incorporated, Day at the state capitol and does hereby extend sincerest wishes
10 that the organization continue to prosper in all of its endeavors for many years to come.
