8 "Section 1. R.S. 15:587.1.2(A)(2)(a)(i) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as
9 follows:
10 §587.1.2. Provision of information to protect children who receive services at a
11 therapeutic group home
12 A.(1) As provided in R.S. 40:2008.10, the Louisiana Department of Health
13 shall request in writing that the bureau supply information to ascertain whether the
14 persons listed in Paragraph (2) of this Subsection have been arrested for or convicted
15 of, or pled nolo contendere to, any criminal offense. The request shall be on a form
16 prepared by the bureau and signed by a responsible official of the Louisiana
17 Department of Health making the request. The request shall include a statement
18 signed by the person about whom the request is made which gives his permission for
19 such information to be released.
20 (2)(a) The persons to which this Section applies include:
21 (i) Any person who owns, operates, or manages a licensed therapeutic group
22 home individual who has any ownership interest in a therapeutic group home or who
23 has managerial authority over its business operations or its provision of client care.
24 * * *"
