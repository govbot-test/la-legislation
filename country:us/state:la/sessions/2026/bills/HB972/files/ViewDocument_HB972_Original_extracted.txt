HLS 26RS-1601 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE BILL NO. 972
BY REPRESENTATIVE BOUDREAUX
HIGHWAYS: Designates a portion of Louisiana Highway 93 in Lafayette Parish as the
"Desert Shield-Desert Storm Memorial Highway"
1 AN ACT
2 To designate a portion of Louisiana Highway 93 in Lafayette Parish, Louisiana, as the
3 "Desert Shield-Desert Storm Memorial Highway"; to provide for implementation;
4 and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. The portion of Louisiana Highway 93 in Lafayette Parish, Louisiana,
7 beginning at the intersection of Louisiana Highway 723 and Wyman Road and ending at the
8 intersection of Louisiana Highway 93 and Destination Pointe Lane shall be known and is
9 hereby designated as the "Desert Shield-Desert Storm Memorial Highway".
10 Section 2. The Department of Transportation and Development is hereby directed
11 to erect and maintain appropriate signage reflecting this designation provided local or private
12 monies are received by the department equal to the department's actual costs for material,
13 fabrication, mounting posts, and installation of the signage, not to exceed the sum of seven
14 hundred fifty dollars per sign.

appropriate signage reflecting this highway designation provided local or private monies are
