Section 1:
2026 Regular Session ACT No. 743 ENROLLED
BY SENATOR TALBOT AND REPRESENTATIVES BERAULT, CARRIER,
CHASSION, DEWITT, FISHER, FREEMAN, FREIBERG, HORTON,
ILLG, KERNER, LAFLEUR, LYONS, MACK AND ZERINGUE
1
2 AN ACT
3 To enact R.S. 22:1077.6, relative to dental care and cancer treatment; to provide for
4 healthcare coverage for certain medically necessary dental procedures as a result of
5 cancer treatment; to clarify healthcare insurers are not required to provide coverage
6 for routine preventative dental care; to provide for applicability; to provide for an
7 effective date; and to provide for related matters.
8 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
9 Section 1. R.S. 22:1077.6 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
10 §1077.6. Coverage for dental services for cancer patients
11 A.(1) Except as provided in Subsections B and C of this Section, an
12 insurer offering a health coverage plan in this state shall provide coverage for
13 evaluation, imaging, clinical examination, and dental extractions that are
14 medically necessary to reduce the risk of infection, eliminate infection, or treat
15 tooth loss or decay as part of a pre-cancer treatment screening for an insured
16 who has been diagnosed with cancer.
17 (2) The dental services shall be covered when performed as part of a pre-
18 treatment medical clearance protocol for insureds scheduled to undergo cancer
19 treatment.
20 B. A healthcare insurer is not required to provide coverage for routine
21 preventive dental care, including cleaning and sealants.
22 C. The provisions of this Section shall not apply to a plan providing
23 coverage for excepted benefits as defined in R.S. 22:1061, limited benefit health
24 insurance plans, and short-term policies that have a term of less than twelve
25 months.
26 Section 2. The provisions of this Act shall apply to any new policy, contract,
