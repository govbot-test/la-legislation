1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 22:1077.6, relative to dental care and cancer treatment; to provide for
3 healthcare coverage for certain medically necessary dental procedures as a result of
4 cancer treatment; to clarify healthcare insurers are not required to provide coverage
5 for routine preventative dental care; to provide for applicability; to provide for an
6 effective date; and to provide for related matters.
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
8 Section 1. R.S. 22:1077.6 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
9 §1077.6. Coverage for dental services for cancer patients
10 A.(1) Except as provided in Subsections B and C of this Section, an
11 insurer offering a health coverage plan in this state shall provide coverage for
12 evaluation, imaging, clinical examination, and dental extractions that are
13 medically necessary to reduce the risk of infection, eliminate infection, or treat
14 tooth loss or decay as part of a pre-cancer treatment screening for an insured
15 who has been diagnosed with cancer.
16 (2) The dental services shall be covered when performed as part of a pre-
17 treatment medical clearance protocol for insureds scheduled to undergo cancer
18 treatment.
19 B. A healthcare insurer is not required to provide coverage for routine
20 preventive dental care, including cleaning and sealants.
21 C. The provisions of this Section shall not apply to a plan providing
22 coverage for excepted benefits as defined in R.S. 22:1061, limited benefit health
23 insurance plans, and short-term policies that have a term of less than twelve
24 months.
25 Section 2. The provisions of this Act shall apply to any new policy, contract,
26 program, or health coverage plan issued on or after January 1, 2027. Any policy, contract,
