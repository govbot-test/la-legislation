HLS 26RS-1226 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 150
BY REPRESENTATIVE PHELPS
COMMENDATIONS: Commends the Shreveport Fire Department on being awarded the
American Heart Association's 2026 Mission: Lifeline EMS - Gold Achievement
Award
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend the Shreveport Fire Department on being awarded the American Heart
3 Association's 2026 Mission: Lifeline EMS - Gold Achievement Award.
4 WHEREAS, the American Heart Association's Mission: Lifeline EMS program
5 focuses on quality of life care and outcomes for pre-hospital stroke, heart attack, and cardiac
6 arrest patients by connecting all care components into a seamlessly integrated care system;
7 the key tenets of these systems of care reinforce evidence-based guidelines and measure
8 performance, identify gaps, and engage in quality improvement; and
9 WHEREAS, the Mission: Lifeline EMS recognition program is designed to showcase
10 pre-hospital agencies across the nation for their excellence in heart attack and stroke care;
11 and
12 WHEREAS, the Shreveport Fire Department has more than five hundred firefighters
13 who are certified as an EMT; and
14 WHEREAS, some months there are as many as fifty cardiac arrests within the city's
15 jurisdictional boundaries; the Shreveport Fire Department managed to revive 60% of its
16 cardiac arrest patients in one month; and
17 WHEREAS, the Shreveport Fire Department fields nearly fifty thousand calls for
18 service each year, and its response time averages around three minutes; the department uses
19 evidence-based diagnosis in the field to coordinate its care with hospitals; and

1 WHEREAS, the Shreveport Fire Department provides national health standards for
2 all of its parish members; the department's firefighters and paramedics are trained to

5 WHEREAS, the Shreveport Fire Department has received the 2026 Mission:
6 Lifeline EMS - Gold Achievement Award from the American Heart Association for the
7 second year in a row, and the department and its members are most deserving of the highest
8 recognition for their commitment to providing the highest level of pre-hospital cardiac arrest
9 and stroke care.
10 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
11 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend the Shreveport Fire Department on being
12 awarded the American Heart Association's 2026 Mission: Lifeline EMS - Gold Achievement
13 Award and does hereby extend enduring appreciation for the department's lifesaving efforts,
14 which exceed expectations set by national standards.
