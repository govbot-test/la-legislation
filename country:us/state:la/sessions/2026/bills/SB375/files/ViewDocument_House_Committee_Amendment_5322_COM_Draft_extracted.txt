HCASB375 4875 5322
HOUSE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
375 by Senator Mizell
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, line 2, change "R.S. 40:1615(A)(4)" to "R.S. 40:1614(A)(4), (B), and (C)(1)"
3 AMENDMENT NO. 2
4 On page 1, line 4, after "chemicals;" and before "to" insert "to provide for declared
5 emergencies;"
6 AMENDMENT NO. 3
7 On page 1, line 6, change "R.S. 40:1615(A)(4)" to "R.S. 40:1614(A)(4), (B), and (C)(1)"
8 AMENDMENT NO. 4
9 On page 2, between lines 7 and 8, insert the following:
10 "B. On and after January 1, 2022, no person shall discharge or otherwise use
11 Class B fire fighting foam that contains intentionally added PFAS chemicals unless
12 such discharge or other use occurs in fire prevention or in response to an a declared
13 emergency fire fighting operation.
14 C. Nothing in this Section shall be construed to do any of the following:
15 (1) Restrict the manufacture, sale, or distribution of Class B fire fighting
16 foam that contains intentionally added PFAS chemicals or restrict the discharge or
17 other use of Class B fire fighting foam in response to an a declared emergency fire
18 fighting operation."
