SLS 26RS-1040 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE BILL NO. 438
BY SENATOR FESI
PORTS/HARBORS/TERMINALS. Authorizes the board of the Greater Lafourche Port
Commission (Commission) to increase member per diems. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 34:1651(F), relative to the Greater Lafourche Port Commission;
3 to provide for commission per diem; to allow the commission to fix per diem for
4 members; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 34:1651(F) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
7 §1651. Greater Lafourche Port Commission; creation; members; election by
8 divisions; vacancies; qualifications; officers; meetings; per diem;
9 removal for excessive absences
10 * * *
11 F. The commission may fix a per diem not to exceed one two hundred fifty
12 dollars to be paid to members of the commission for each day the member attends
13 a meeting of the commission and each day the member spends on business of the
14 commission authorized by the commission, not to exceed six per diem payments per
15 month.

DIGEST
SB 438 Original 2026 Regular Session Fesi
Present law provides the commission may fix a per diem not to exceed $150.00 to members
of the commission for each day the member attends a meeting or spends on business of the
commission. Proposed law changes the amount the commission may fix a per diem from no
more than $150.00 to no more than $250.00.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 34:1651(F))
