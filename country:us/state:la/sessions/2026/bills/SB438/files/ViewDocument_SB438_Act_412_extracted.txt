2026 Regular Session ACT No. 412 ENROLLED
SENATE BILL NO. 438
BY SENATOR FESI
1
2
3 AN ACT
4 To amend and reenact R.S. 34:1651(F), relative to the Greater Lafourche Port Commission;
5 to provide for commission per diem; to allow the commission to fix per diem for
6 members; and to provide for related matters.
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
8 Section 1. R.S. 34:1651(F) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
9 §1651. Greater Lafourche Port Commission; creation; members; election by
10 divisions; vacancies; qualifications; officers; meetings; per diem;
11 removal for excessive absences
12 * * *
13 F. The commission may fix a per diem not to exceed one two hundred fifty
14 dollars to be paid to members of the commission for each day the member attends
15 a meeting of the commission and each day the member spends on business of the
16 commission authorized by the commission, not to exceed six per diem payments per
17 month.
PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE
