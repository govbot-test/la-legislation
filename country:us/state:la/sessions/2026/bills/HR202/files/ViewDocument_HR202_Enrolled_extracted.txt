ENROLLED
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 202
BY REPRESENTATIVE MCFARLAND
A RESOLUTION
WHEREAS, rural communities in Louisiana face significant barriers to accessing
mental health care, including provider shortages and limited resources; and
WHEREAS, individuals with serious mental illness and youth with serious emotional
disturbance, particularly those served by Medicaid, rely on mental health rehabilitation
providers for essential, home and community-based care; and
WHEREAS, mental health rehabilitation providers are the backbone of Louisiana's
behavioral health system, and their work improves stability, reduces hospitalizations, and
strengthens families and communities; and
WHEREAS, the Louisiana Rural Mental Health Alliance plays a vital role in
advocating for rural mental health, supporting providers, and expanding access to care across
underserved areas.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby designate Monday, April 27, 2026, as Rural Mental
Health Day at the capitol and does hereby recognize the Louisiana Rural Mental Health
Alliance and mental health rehabilitation providers for their critical contributions to the well-
being of Louisiana's rural communities.
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
