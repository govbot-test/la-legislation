SLS 26RS-730 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 49
BY SENATOR CONNICK
COMMENDATIONS. Commends and congratulates Melissa Adams on the occasion of her
retirement and recognizes her many career accomplishments.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend and congratulate Melissa Adams on the occasion of her retirement from
3 Ochsner Medical Center - West Bank and to recognize her many accomplishments
4 throughout her career.
5 WHEREAS, after fifty-two years of dedicated service to the healthcare profession,
6 Melissa Adams is celebrating the occasion of her retirement; and
7 WHEREAS, Melissa has been a steadfast advocate for patients, a trusted leader for
8 nurses, a guiding force for the organization, and most importantly, a friend to everyone she
9 encounters; and
10 WHEREAS, her career has been nothing short of extraordinary; and
11 WHEREAS, while there is hardly a department at Ochsner Medical Center - West
12 Bank that she has not touched, it was the collaborative and team-focused environment of the
13 operating room where she found her true calling; and
14 WHEREAS, she started her career as a staff nurse in the operating room before being
15 promoted to director of surgical services, which included OR, PACU, anesthesia, endoscopy,
16 same-day surgery, and the sterile processing department; and
17 WHEREAS, when promoted to assistant vice president, Melissa assumed
18 responsibilities for the emergency department and critical care before becoming chief

2 WHEREAS, Melissa was instrumental in developing Senate Bill No. 136 of the 2022
3 Regular Session to address workplace violence in healthcare settings; and
4 WHEREAS, her career reflects an unwavering commitment to excellence,
5 compassion, and the advancement of nursing practice; and
6 WHEREAS, as chief nursing officer, Melissa shaped clinical care, mentored
7 hundreds of nurses, and led with integrity, vision, and heart; and
8 WHEREAS, while Melissa is retiring from the profession, she certainly has no plans
9 to slow down, and is looking forward to celebrating her parents' upcoming seventy-fifth
10 wedding anniversary, traveling with family, and spending time with her four young
11 grandchildren.
12 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
13 does hereby commend and congratulate Melissa Adams on the occasion of her retirement
14 from Ochsner Medical Center - West Bank and to recognize her many accomplishments
15 throughout her career.
16 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to
17 Melissa Adams.
