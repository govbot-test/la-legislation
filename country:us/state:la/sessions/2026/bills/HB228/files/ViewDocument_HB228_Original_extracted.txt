HB 228 Original 2026 Regular Session Stagni
Abstract: Re-creates the Dept. of State Civil Service for six years. Changes termination
date from July 1, 2027, to July 1, 2033.
Present law (sunset law) provides that the Dept. of State Civil Service and all the statutory
entities made a part of that department by law shall begin to terminate operations on July 1,
2026, and that all legislative authority for such entities shall cease as of July 1, 2027, unless
the legislature enacts a bill authorizing the re-creation of the department and its statutory
entities prior thereto.
Proposed law provides for the general re-creation of the Dept. of State Civil Service and its
statutory entities, effective June 30, 2026, in accordance with the sunset law. Supersedes the
provisions of the sunset law which set out the procedure for review and re-creation and
which require a separate bill to re-create each statutory entity within the department along
with additional provisions. July 1, 2033, is the new termination date, and the termination
would begin July 1, 2032, unless the department is again re-created.
Effective June 30, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 49:191(4); Repeals R.S. 49:191(1)(d))
