ENROLLED
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE BILL NO. 982
BY REPRESENTATIVE THOMPSON
1 AN ACT
2 To designate a portion of Louisiana Highway 133 North in the Village of Oak Ridge, as the
3 "Erle McKoin 'Niney' Barham Memorial Highway"; to designate a portion of
4 Louisiana Highway 133 South in the Village of Oak Ridge, as the "Joseph Sidney
5 Carter Memorial Highway"; to designate a portion of Louisiana Highway 134 West
6 in the Village of Oak Ridge, as the "Abner Wimberly Memorial Highway"; to
7 designate a portion of Louisiana Highway 134 East in the Village of Oak Ridge, as
8 the "James Wallace McLendon Memorial Highway"; to provide for implementation;
9 and to provide for related matters.
10 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
11 Section 1. The portion of Louisiana Highway 133 North in the Village of Oak Ridge,
12 between south of Fluker Lane and the Village of Oak Ridge corporate limits, shall be known
13 and is hereby designated as the "Erle McKoin 'Niney' Barham Memorial Highway".
14 Section 2. The portion of Louisiana Highway 133 South in the Village of Oak Ridge,
15 between north of Buck Shepard Road and the Village of Oak Ridge corporate limits, shall
16 be known and is hereby designated as the "Joseph Sidney Carter Memorial Highway".
17 Section 3. The portion of Louisiana Highway 134 West in the Village of Oak Ridge
18 between east of Lake Erwin Road and the Village of Oak Ridge corporate limits, shall be
19 known and is hereby designated as the "Abner Wimberly Memorial Highway".
20 Section 4. The portion of Louisiana Highway 134 East in the Village of Oak Ridge,
21 between west of Pipes Lane and the Village of Oak Ridge corporate limits, shall be known
22 and is hereby designated as the "James Wallace McLendon Memorial Highway".
