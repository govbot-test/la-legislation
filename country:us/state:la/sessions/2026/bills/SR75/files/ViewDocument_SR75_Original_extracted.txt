SLS 26RS-1830 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 75
BY SENATOR KLEINPETER
SPECIAL DAY/WEEK/MONTH. Commends and recognizes Iberville Parish for its rich
history and designates April 15, 2026, as Iberville Parish Day at the Louisiana State Capitol.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend and recognize Iberville Parish on its historic, cultural, and economic
3 significance, while expressing support for critical infrastructure investments,
4 including a new Mississippi River Bridge and designate April 15, 2026, as Iberville
5 Parish Day at the Louisiana State Capitol.
6 WHEREAS, Iberville Parish, established on March 31, 1807, is one of Louisiana's
7 original nineteen parishes and remains a vital contributor to the history and development of
8 this state; and
9 WHEREAS, named in honor of French explorer Pierre Le Moyne d'Iberville, the
10 parish embodies a legacy of leadership, resilience, and cultural pride that continues to define
11 its people and communities; and
12 WHEREAS, Iberville Parish serves as a gateway to the Atchafalaya Basin, the largest
13 river swamp in the United States, offering significant ecological, recreational, and cultural
14 value that enhances Louisiana's identity and tourism economy; and
15 WHEREAS, Iberville Parish is home to a major industrial corridor along the
16 Mississippi River, including world-class chemical and manufacturing facilities that provide
17 thousands of jobs, strengthen the state's tax base, and supports both regional and national
18 economic interests; and

1 WHEREAS, Iberville Parish continues to experience significant industrial expansion
2 and economic growth, positioning it as a key driver in Louisiana's future prosperity; and
3 WHEREAS, the celebration of Iberville Parish Day at the Louisiana State Capitol
4 serves as an important opportunity to highlight the parish's achievements, strengthen
5 collaboration with state leadership, and advocate for continued investment in infrastructure
6 and economic development; and
7 WHEREAS, the development of a new Mississippi River Bridge in the Baton Rouge
8 region, inclusive of Iberville Parish, is a critical infrastructure priority that will improve
9 traffic congestion, enhance public safety, support industrial expansion, and serve as a
10 transformative catalyst for regional and statewide economic growth.
11 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
12 does hereby commend and recognize Iberville Parish on the occasion of the second annual
13 Iberville Parish Day at the Louisiana State Capitol and expresses appreciation for the parish's
14 enduring contributions to the state of Louisiana.
15 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana does
16 hereby express support for continued strategic investment in infrastructure, including the
17 development of a new Mississippi River Bridge, to ensure sustained economic growth,
18 improved connectivity, and enhanced quality of life for the citizens of Iberville Parish and
19 the state of Louisiana.
20 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
21 Iberville Parish President and the Iberville Parish Council.
