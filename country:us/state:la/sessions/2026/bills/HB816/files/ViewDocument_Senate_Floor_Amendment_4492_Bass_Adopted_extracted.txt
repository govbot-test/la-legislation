18 "§2717.1. Prohibition of acquisition of immovable property by a foreign adversary
19 or a person connected with a foreign adversary
20 * * *
21 F. The Unless the person is an agent of a foreign adversary or a mandatary
22 of a foreign adversary, the provisions of this Section shall not apply to any of the
23 following:
24 * * *
25 G. For the purposes of this Section:
26 (1) "Agent of a foreign adversary" or "mandatary of a foreign adversary"
27 means a juridical person or a natural person that serves as a mandatary, as provided
28 in Louisiana Civil Code Article 2989, for a principal that is a foreign adversary and
29 meets one or more of the following criteria:
30 (a) Is under direction of a foreign adversary that has the power to direct or
31 cause the direction of the management or policies of the juridical person whether
32 through ownership of securities, by contract, or otherwise.
33 (b) Directly or indirectly has the right to vote fifty percent or more of the
34 voting interests of an entity that is owned or controlled by a foreign adversary or is
35 entitled to fifty percent or more of its profits and is, therefore, presumed to have the
36 power to direct or cause the direction of the management or policies of the juridical
37 person.
38 (c) Is a juridical person that meets any of the following:
39 (i) Is headquartered in a country identified as a foreign adversary.
40 (ii) Is held or controlled, either directly or indirectly, by a government of a
41 foreign adversary.
42 (iii) Is owned by, or the majority of stock or other ownership interest is held
43 or controlled by, any person who is a citizen of a foreign adversary, unless that
44 person is a legal permanent resident of the United States.
