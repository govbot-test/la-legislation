DIGEST
SB 455 Original 2026 Regular Session Talbot
Proposed law provides for the submission of a plan for any district court or parish court to
allow their employees to become members of the Parochial Employees' Retirement System.
Present law provides the specific conditions used by the board of trustees to approve a plan
or any amended plan. Proposed law retains present law.
Present law stipulates that a plan must cover all services performed for the entity and extend
benefits to all of its employees. Proposed law creates an exception for district court or parish
court employees which would allow the board to approve plans even if they do not provide
coverage for all services or employees.
Proposed law allows for certain prior district court or parish court employees to make an
irrevocable election to join the system if they are currently in another public retirement plan
and have not yet retired or used the deferred retirement option plan. The system is required
to provide all necessary forms for this election.
Proposed law specifies that no person who transfers service credit to the system has the right
to execute a reverse transfer.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 11:1903(B)(2); adds R.S. 11:1903(A)(5) and (H))
