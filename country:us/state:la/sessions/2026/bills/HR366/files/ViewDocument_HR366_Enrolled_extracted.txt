2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 366
BY REPRESENTATIVE ADAMS
A RESOLUTION
To commend Noah Shotwell for winning the one hundred twenty-kilogram Powerlifting
National Championship, East Regional Championship, and Division IV State
Championship and for being invited to represent the United States in the 2026 Junior
World Powerlifting Championships in South Africa.
WHEREAS, Noah Shotwell is a 2026 graduate of Slaughter Community Charter
School who is distinguished as the Powerlifting National Champion, East Regional
Champion, and Division IV State Champion in the one hundred twenty-kilogram weight
classification; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Shotwell completed a perfect powerlifting season with a three-lift
total of more than one thousand six hundred pounds and has cemented himself as the most
dominant powerlifter in Slaughter Community Charter School history; he holds the school's
overall records in squat, deadlift, and total weight; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Shotwell began powerlifting in high school and has established
himself as the strongest eighteen-year-old in the United States; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Shotwell received an invitation to represent the United States
during the 2026 World Championships in South Africa this August and will lift in the Junior
World Championship as part of the U.S. Junior National Team; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Shotwell has overcome physical struggles during his early life,
during which he spent years in pediatric physical and occupational therapy for low muscle
tone, poor core strength, and fine motor skill delays, and established himself as a dominant
competitive powerlifter; and
WHEREAS, Noah Shotwell is an inspiring young man who is deserving of the
highest recognition for his outstanding work ethic and for proudly representing the state of
Louisiana on the international stage.

HR NO. 366 ENROLLED
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Noah Shotwell for winning the one hundred
twenty-kilogram Powerlifting National Championship, East Regional Championship, and
Division IV State Championship and for being invited to represent the United States in the
2026 Junior World Powerlifting Championships in South Africa and does hereby encourage
him to continue his outstanding work and always strive for greatness in competition.
