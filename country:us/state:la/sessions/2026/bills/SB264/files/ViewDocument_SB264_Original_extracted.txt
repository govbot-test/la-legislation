SLS 26RS-529 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE BILL NO. 264
BY SENATOR LUNEAU
NURSES. Provides relative to the administration of anesthetics. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 37:930(G)(1)(a), relative to the administration of anesthetics; to
3 provide for the administration of anesthetics by CRNAs; to provide for legislative
4 findings; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 37:930(G)(1)(a) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
7 §930. Anesthetics; authority to administer; penalty
8 * * *
9 G.(1) The Louisiana Legislature hereby finds that:
10 (a) Certified Registered Nurse Anesthetists (CRNAs) have been selecting and
11 administering anesthesia in Louisiana and the United States for over more than one
12 hundred years.

DIGEST
SB 264 Original 2026 Regular Session Luneau
Present law provides legislative findings relative to the authority of Certified Registered
Nurse Anesthetists (CRNAs) to administer anesthetics.
Present law makes technical changes to the findings.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 37:930(G)(1)(a))
