1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 17:5067.1, relative to financial aid; to provide relative to the Taylor
3 Opportunity Program for Students; to provide relative to student data; to require the
4 establishment of a data administration system; to require certain postsecondary
5 education institutions to report certain data; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 17:5067.1 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §5067.1. Data administration system
9 The administering agency shall establish and maintain a data administration
10 system for the collection of uniform data on all state financial aid programs to
11 conduct and report on statewide financial aid trends, effectiveness, outcomes
12 assessment, and other factors. The administering agency shall determine the uniform
13 data set necessary for such reports. Any postsecondary education institution that
14 admits students who are recipients of Taylor Opportunity Program for Students
15 awards shall submit to the administering agency the uniform data set as established
16 pursuant to this Section.

HB 1058 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Bamburg
Abstract: Requires the Bd. of Regents to establish a data administration system for certain
data.
Present law provides for the Taylor Opportunity Program for Students (TOPS), a merit-
based scholarship program for postsecondary education, administered through the La. Office
of Student Financial Assistance, a program under the Bd. of Regents.
Proposed law requires the Bd. of Regents to establish and maintain a data administration
system to collect data on financial aid usage at postsecondary education institutions that
accept students who receive TOPS.
(Adds R.S. 17:5067.1)
