12 (2) The date of payment required of the owner pursuant to this Section shall
13 change from thirty-five days after the date the owner receives the payment request
14 to the fifth day after the date the owner receives loan proceeds when all of the
15 following occurs:
16 (a) The owner has obtained a loan intended to pay for all or part of a contract
17 to improve real property.
18 (b) The owner has timely and properly requested disbursement of proceeds
19 from that loan.
20 (c) The lender is obligated to disburse the proceeds to the owner but has
21 failed to do so within thirty-five days after the date the owner received the
22 contractor's payment request.
23 (3) This Section does not apply to any of the following:
24 (a) Agreements authorizing the exploration, production, or development of
25 oil, natural gas, natural gas liquids, synthetic gas, sulphur, ore, or other mineral
26 substances, including any lease or royalty agreement, joint interest agreement,
27 production or production-related agreement, operating agreement, farmout
28 agreement, area of mutual interest agreement, or other related agreement.
29 (b) Well or mine services.
30 (c) The purchase, sale, gathering, storage, or transport of oil, natural gas,
31 natural gas liquids, synthetic gas, or other hydrocarbon substances by pipeline or by
32 a fixed associated facility.
33 (d) For the purposes of this exemption, "agreement" includes a written or oral
34 agreement or understanding that does any of the following:
35 (i) Provides work or services, including any construction, operating, repair,
36 or maintenance services.
37 (ii) Performs a part of the services covered by Subparagraph (a) of this
38 Paragraph or an act collateral to those services, including furnishing or renting
39 equipment, incidental transportation, or other goods and services furnished in
40 connection with those services.
41 (e) For the purposes of this exemption, "well or mine services" includes
42 either of the following:
43 (i) Drilling, deepening, reworking, repairing, improving, testing, treating,
44 perforating, acidizing, logging, conditioning, purchasing, gathering, storing, or
45 transporting oil or natural gas, brine water, fresh water, produced water, condensate,
46 petroleum products, or other liquid commodities, or otherwise rendering services in
47 connection with a well drilled to produce or dispose of oil, gas, or other minerals or
48 water.
49 (ii) Designing, excavating, constructing, improving, or otherwise rendering
50 services in connection with an oil, gas, or other mineral production platform or
51 facility, mine shaft, drift, or other structure intended directly for use in exploring for
52 or producing a mineral."
