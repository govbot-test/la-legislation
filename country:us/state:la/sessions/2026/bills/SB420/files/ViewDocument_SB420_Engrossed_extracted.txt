DIGEST
SB 420 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Hensgens
Present law provides for 11 commissioners of the Lafayette Metropolitan Expressway
Commission. Proposed law changes total number of commissioners from 11 to 13.
Present law provides for the appointment of members. Proposed law retains present law and
adds members appointed by the Vemilion Parish Police Jury President and president of
Iberia Parish.
Present law provides for the boundary and jurisdiction of the commission to be coextensive
with the boundaries of Lafayette Parish, Louisiana. Proposed law adds Vermilion and Iberia
parishes to the boundaries and jurisdiction of the commission.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 48:2094(C)(intro para) and 2094(H); adds R.S. 48:2094(C)(8) and (9))
