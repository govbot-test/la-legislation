Section 1:
2026 Regular Session ACT No. 264 ENROLLED
BY SENATOR HENSGENS
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact the introductory paragraph of R.S. 48:2094(C) and 2094(H) and to
3 enact R.S. 48:2094(C)(8) and (9), relative to the Lafayette Metropolitan Expressway
4 Commission; to provide for commissioners of the Lafayette Metropolitan
5 Expressway Commission; to provide for the boundary and jurisdiction of the
6 commission; and to provide for related matters.
7 Notice of intention to introduce this Act has been published.
8 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
9 Section 1. The introductory paragraph of R.S. 48:2094(C) and 2094(H) are hereby
10 amended and reenacted and R.S. 48:2094(C)(8) and (9) are hereby enacted to read as
11 follows:
12 §2094. Lafayette Metropolitan Expressway Commission; creation; board of
13 directors; meetings; quorum
14 * * *
15 C. The board shall be composed of eleven thirteen directors who shall be the
16 governing body of the commission with full power to promulgate rules and
17 regulations for the maintenance and operation of said authority as follows:
18 * * *
19 (8) One member appointed by the Vermilion Parish Police Jury
20 President.
21 (9) One member appointed by the Iberia Parish President.
22 * * *
23 H. The boundary and jurisdiction of the commission shall be coextensive
