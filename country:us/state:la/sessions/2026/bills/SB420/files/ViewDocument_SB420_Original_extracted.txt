13 * * *

DIGEST
SB 420 Original 2026 Regular Session Hensgens
Present law provides for eleven commissioners of the Lafayette Metropolitan Expressway
Commission.
Proposed law removes total number of commissioners.
Present law provides for the boundary and jurisdiction of the commission to be coextensive
with the boundaries of Lafayette Parish, Louisiana. Proposed law adds Vermilion and Iberia
parishes to the boundaries and jurisdiction of the commission.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 48:2094(C)(intro para) and 2094(H))
