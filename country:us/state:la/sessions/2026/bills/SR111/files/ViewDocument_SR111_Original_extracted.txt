SLS 26RS-3015 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 111
BY SENATOR CLOUD
Women for America's Encourage-A-Legislator Project.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend and acknowledge the volunteers and members of the 2026 Concerned Women
3 for America's Encourage-A-Legislator Project.
4 WHEREAS, Concerned Women for America (CWA) is the nation's largest public
5 policy women's organization with a rich history of over forty years of helping its members
6 across the country bring Biblical principles into all levels of public policy; and
7 WHEREAS, 1 Timothy 2:1-2 instructs us to pray for our leaders, and CWA's
8 Encourage-A-Legislator (EAL) program offers members and friends the vehicle to do this
9 in an easy and meaningful way by ensuring consistent prayer for each legislator; and
10 WHEREAS, as prayers are offered on behalf of the legislators, hearts are changed
11 and encouraged; and
12 WHEREAS, the goal of the EAL program is to encourage each member of the state
13 legislature, as well as statewide office holders, by praying for them on a regular basis; and
14 WHEREAS, the EAL program is a great way to lift up persons in authority and
15 encourage them in their responsibilities; and
16 WHEREAS, it is highly appropriate to commend the volunteers and members of the
17 2026 Concerned Women for America's Encourage-A-Legislator Project.
18 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
Page 1 of 2

1 does hereby commend and acknowledge the volunteers and members of the 2026 Concerned
2 Women for America's Encourage-A-Legislator Project.
