HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 187
BY REPRESENTATIVE WALTERS
A RESOLUTION
To express the condolences of the House of Representatives on the death of Dr. Cerina
Wanzer Fairfax.
WHEREAS, it is with immense sadness and profound regret that the House of
Representatives of the Legislature of Louisiana has learned of the tragic passing, on
April 16, 2026, of Dr. Cerina Wanzer Fairfax; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Fairfax was a beloved and accomplished dentist who ran a thriving
family dental practice, Dr. Fairfax and Associates Family Dentistry, which served the
Fairfax community in Virginia; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Fairfax earned a bachelor of science degree from Duke University;
she obtained a doctorate of dental surgery degree and graduated magna cum laude from
Virginia Commonwealth University (VCU) School of Dentistry in 2005; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Fairfax embodied the ideals of the dentistry profession and took
great pride in her work; she was dedicated to her patients, committed to growth, and
displayed a deep sense of purpose in her daily work; she was a mentor, role model, and
friend to many in the VCU School of Dentistry; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Fairfax was a member of the American Dental Association, Virginia
Dental Association, Northern Virginia Dental Society, and Omicron Kappa Upsilon Dental
Society; she was recently presented with a VCU Alumni Association Award recognizing her
as an Outstanding Graduate of the Last Decade; and
WHEREAS, outside of work, Cerina was a devoted mother to her children; she loved
to read, practice yoga, run trails with her dogs, travel, and spend time with her family; and

Legislature of Louisiana does hereby express heartfelt condolences on the death of
Dr. Cerina Wanzer Fairfax and does hereby extend sincere wishes that her family, friends,
