Section 1:
HLS 26RS-2709 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE JORDAN
STUDENTS: Requires the administration of trauma screenings to public school students
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 17:2111, relative to student screenings; to require schools to administer
3 trauma screenings to students; to require the Louisiana Department of Health to
4 provide a screening tool to each school board; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 17:2111 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
7 §2111. Trauma screenings
8 Each public school shall administer trauma screenings to each student in
9 grades prekindergarten through twelve during each school year. The Louisiana
10 Department of Health shall select and provide a screening instrument for this purpose
11 to each school board.
DIGEST
The digest printed below was prepared by House Legislative Services. It constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument. The keyword, one-liner, abstract, and digest do not constitute
part of the law or proof or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
HB 1150 Original 2026 Regular Session Jordan
Abstract: Requires the administration of trauma screenings to public school students.
Proposed law requires each public school to administer trauma screenings to each student
in grades prekindergarten through 12 during each school year. Requires the La. Department
of Health to select and provide a screening instrument for this purpose to each school board.
(Adds R.S. 17:2111)
