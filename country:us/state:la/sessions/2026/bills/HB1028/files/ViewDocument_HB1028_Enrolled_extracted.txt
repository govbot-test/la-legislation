2026 Regular Session
HOUSE BILL NO. 1028
BY REPRESENTATIVES LYONS, CHASSION, EGAN, FISHER, JACKSON, AND
SPELL
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 40:1257.2(C) through (E), relative to nonemergency medical transportation;
3 to provide for Medicaid reimbursement rates; to require the Louisiana Department
4 of Health to establish a minimum trip and mileage reimbursement rate; to provide
5 for an effective date; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 40:1257.2(C) through (E) are hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §1257.2. Nonemergency medical transportation services
9 * * *
10 C. The department shall promulgate and adopt rules establishing a Medicaid
11 reimbursement rate methodology that provides for a minimum rate of fourteen
12 dollars and fifty cents per trip and two dollars and ten cents per mile for NEMT
13 services, including wheelchair-accessible NEMT.
14 D. The department shall promulgate rules to establish a NEMT rate
15 methodology for multiple passenger trips based on the number of passengers being
16 transported.
17 E. The department shall not allow an individual to be transported via NEMT
18 to a Medicaid covered service outside of the individual's parish of residence or
19 contiguous parishes unless there is a determination of medical necessity by the
20 Medicaid managed care organization or the department.
21 Section 2. Not later than ninety days after the effective date of this Act the Louisiana
22 Department of Health shall take all necessary actions including but not limited to filing a
23 Medicaid state plan amendment, to implement the provisions of this Act.
