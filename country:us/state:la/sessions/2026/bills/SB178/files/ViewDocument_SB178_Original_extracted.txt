SLS 26RS-372 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY SENATOR ALLAIN
COLLEGES/UNIVERSITIES. Provides relative to the Louisiana State University
Agricultural Center. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 17:3215(8), relative to the Louisiana State University
3 Agricultural Center; to provide relative to authority; and to provide for related
4 matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 17:3215(8) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
7 §3215. Louisiana State University system
8 The Louisiana State University system is composed of the institutions under
9 the supervision and management of the Board of Supervisors of Louisiana State
10 University and Agricultural and Mechanical College as follows:
11 * * *
12 (8) The Center for Agricultural Sciences and Rural Development, generally
13 known as the Louisiana State University Agricultural Center, which administers
14 shall administer the agricultural extension and research programs of the board
15 throughout the state. The center shall also be responsible for conducting river water
16 research and agricultural youth development and education programs, including FFA
17 and 4-H.

SLS 26RS-372 ORIGINAL
SB NO. 178
1 * * *
The original instrument and the following digest, which constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument, were prepared by Senate Legislative Services.
The keyword, summary, and digest do not constitute part of the law or proof
or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
DIGEST
SB 178 Original 2026 Regular Session Allain
Present law provides that the Louisiana State University Agricultural Center administers the
agricultural extension and research programs.
Proposed law retains present law and clarifies that the Louisiana State University
Agricultural Center must administer the agricultural extension and research programs.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 17:3215(8))
