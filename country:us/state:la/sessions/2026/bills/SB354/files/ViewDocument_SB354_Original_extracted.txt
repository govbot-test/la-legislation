Section 1:
SLS 26RS-581 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY SENATOR JACKSON-ANDREWS
GAMING. Removes sports-related proposition betting from the definition of permitted
sports wagers and prohibits micro-bets and proposition bets. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 27:602(23) and to enact R.S. 27:602(29) and (30) and 608(C)(5)
3 and (6), relative to sports betting: to provide for definitions; to prohibit proposition
4 bets; to prohibit micro-bets; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 27:602(23) is hereby amended and reenacted and R.S. 27:602(29) and
7 (30) and 608(C)(5) and (6) are hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §602. Definitions
9 For purposes of this Chapter, the following terms have the following meanings
10 ascribed to them unless the context clearly indicates otherwise:
11 * * *
12 (23) "Sports wager" or "sports bet" means a sum of money or representation
13 of value risked by a player on an occurrence associated with a sports event for which
14 the outcome is uncertain. The term includes but is not limited to single-game bets,
15 teaser bets, parlay bets, over-under bets, moneyline bets, pools, exchange wagering,
16 in-game wagering, in-play bets, proposition bets, and straight bets.
17 * * *

SLS 26RS-581 ORIGINAL
SB NO. 354
1 (29) "Proposition bet" or "prop bet" means a side wager on a part of a
2 sport or athletic event that does not concern the final outcome of the sport or
3 athletic event.
4 (30) "Sports micro-bet" means a proposition bet which is wagered live,
5 while a sport or athletic event is ongoing, and concerns the outcome of a play or
6 action occurring in the sport or athletic event.
7 * * *
8 §608. Limitations on wagering
9 * * *
10 C. No sports wagers may be accepted or paid by any operator on any of the
11 following:
12 * * *
13 (5) On any sports micro-bet.
14 (6) On any proposition bet.
15 * * *

DIGEST
SB 354 Original 2026 Regular Session Jackson-Andrews
Present law defines a "sports wager" or "sports bet" as a sum of money or representation of
value risked by a player on an occurrence associated with a sports event for which the
outcome is uncertain. Present law further defines the term to include but not be limited to
single-game bets, teaser bets, parlay bets, over-under bets, moneyline bets, pools, exchange
wagering, in-game wagering, in-play bets, proposition bets, and straight bets.
Proposed law retains present law, but removes "proposition bets" from the definition.
Proposed law defines a "proposition bet" or "prop bet" as a side wager on a part of a sport
or athletic event that does not concern the final outcome of the sport or athletic event.
Proposed law defines a "sports micro-bet" as a proposition bet which is wagered live, while
a sport or athletic event in ongoing, and concerns the outcome of a play or action occurring
in the sport or athletic event.
Present law provides for limitations on wagering.
Proposed law retains present law limitation on wagering and adds sports micro-bets and
proposition bets to the list.
