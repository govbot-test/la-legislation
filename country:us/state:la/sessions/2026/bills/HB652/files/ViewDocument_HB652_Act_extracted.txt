HOUSE BILL NO. 652
BY REPRESENTATIVE BRAUD
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 56:421(B)(5) through (15), (C), and (E)(4) and to enact R.S.
3 56:421(B)(16), relative to the Oyster Task Force; to add one member to the task
4 force appointed by the commissioner of the Department of Agriculture and Forestry;
5 to add the Department of Agriculture and Forestry as an entity that receives
6 recommendations from the task force relative to the oyster industry and production;
7 and to provide for related matters.
8 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
9 Section 1. R.S. 56:421(B)(5) through (15), (C), and (E)(4) are hereby amended and
10 reenacted and R.S. 56:421(B)(16) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
11 §421. Oyster Task Force
12 * * *
13 B. The task force shall be composed as follows:
14 * * *
15 (5) One member appointed by the commissioner of the Department of
16 Agriculture and Forestry.
17 (5) (6) Four members appointed by the Louisiana Oyster Dealers and
18 Growers Association. Two members appointed under the provisions of this
19 Paragraph shall be selected at-large. Of the other two members, one shall be from
20 Jefferson Parish and one shall be selected from either Lafourche or Terrebonne
21 Parish.
