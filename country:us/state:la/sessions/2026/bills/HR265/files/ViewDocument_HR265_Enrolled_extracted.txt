HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 265
BY REPRESENTATIVE MIKE JOHNSON
A RESOLUTION
To commend the Pineville High School Lady Rebels softball team on winning the Louisiana
High School Athletic Association 2026 Class 5A state championship and to
recognize the efforts of the team members, coaches, and supporters.
WHEREAS, the Lady Rebels completed an outstanding season with a record of
twenty-three wins and nine losses, ranking among the top teams in Louisiana Class 5A; and
WHEREAS, the team demonstrated exceptional skill, determination, and teamwork
throughout the season, advanced through a highly competitive playoff field, and ultimately
secured the state championship with a victory over John Curtis Christian by a score of 12-1;
and
WHEREAS, the Lady Rebels dedicated countless hours of practice and displayed an
inspiring degree of discipline and perseverance; these student-athletes exemplify the highest
standards of sportsmanship, commitment, and excellence both on and off the field; and
WHEREAS, the members of the 2026 Pineville High School Lady Rebels softball
team include Savanna Johnson, Adi Floyd, Ava Moore, Briley Byrnes, Jadin Basco, Chelsie
Simpson, Kaylee Ramos, Kate Herron, Katie Kelley, Aniyah Jackson, Abigail Daniels,
Zariah Hicks, Katie Oney, Haedyn Phillips, Berkley Byrnes, Analiese Holmes, Harper
Wallace, Anistyn Lee, Anna Kate Ulrich, Ashlynn Weatherford, Ayvah Norris, and Abbie
Flowers; and
WHEREAS, the success of the team is due in large part to the leadership of head
coach Allison Frye and assistant coaches Kayla Delao and Ethan Penfield; their dedication
and mentorship have helped guide these student-athletes to championship excellence; and

WHEREAS, the support of families, faculty, school administrators, and the Pineville
community played an essential role in the success of the team and has contributed to an
environment that fosters excellence in athletics and academics; and
WHEREAS, the achievements of the Pineville High School Lady Rebels softball
team serve as an inspiration to students across the state and reflect the strength and spirit of
high school athletics in Louisiana.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend the Pineville High School Lady Rebels
softball team on winning the Louisiana High School Athletic Association 2026 Class 5A
state championship and does hereby extend sincere congratulations to the players, coaches,
and supporters on their remarkable accomplishment.
