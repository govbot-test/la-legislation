HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 265
BY REPRESENTATIVE MIKE JOHNSON
winning the Louisiana High School Athletic Association 2026 Class 5A state
championship
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend the Pineville High School Lady Rebels softball team on winning the Louisiana
3 High School Athletic Association 2026 Class 5A state championship and to
4 recognize the efforts of the team members, coaches, and supporters.
5 WHEREAS, the Lady Rebels completed an outstanding season with a record of
6 twenty-three wins and nine losses, ranking among the top teams in Louisiana Class 5A; and
7 WHEREAS, the team demonstrated exceptional skill, determination, and teamwork
8 throughout the season, advanced through a highly competitive playoff field, and ultimately
9 secured the state championship with a victory over John Curtis Christian by a score of 12-1;
10 and
11 WHEREAS, the Lady Rebels dedicated countless hours of practice and displayed an
12 inspiring degree of discipline and perseverance; these student-athletes exemplify the highest
13 standards of sportsmanship, commitment, and excellence both on and off the field; and
14 WHEREAS, the members of the 2026 Pineville High School Lady Rebels softball
15 team include Savanna Johnson, Adi Floyd, Ava Moore, Briley Byrnes, Jadin Basco, Chelsie
16 Simpson, Kaylee Ramos, Kate Herron, Katie Kelley, Aniyah Jackson, Abigail Daniels,
17 Zariah Hicks, Katie Oney, Haedyn Phillips, Berkley Byrnes, Analiese Holmes, Harper
18 Wallace, Anistyn Lee, Anna Kate Ulrich, Ashlynn Weatherford, Ayvah Norris, and Abbie
19 Flowers; and

1 WHEREAS, the success of the team is due in large part to the leadership of head
2 coach Allison Frye and assistant coaches Kayla Delao and Ethan Penfield; their dedication
3 and mentorship have helped guide these student-athletes to championship excellence; and
4 WHEREAS, the support of families, faculty, school administrators, and the Pineville
5 community played an essential role in the success of the team and has contributed to an
6 environment that fosters excellence in athletics and academics; and
7 WHEREAS, the achievements of the Pineville High School Lady Rebels softball
8 team serve as an inspiration to students across the state and reflect the strength and spirit of
9 high school athletics in Louisiana.
10 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
11 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend the Pineville High School Lady Rebels
12 softball team on winning the Louisiana High School Athletic Association 2026 Class 5A
13 state championship and does hereby extend sincere congratulations to the players, coaches,
14 and supporters on their remarkable accomplishment.
