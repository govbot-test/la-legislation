HCAHB804 4563 3410
1 AMENDMENT NO. 10
2 On page 2, at the beginning of line 20, delete "C. "Climate change" means" and insert "(1)
3 "Climate change" means any actual or alleged change in global or regional climate
4 conditions, weather patterns, temperature, sea levels, storm frequency or severity, or
5 atmospheric composition, whether characterized as caused by human activity, natural forces,
6 or both. For purposes of this Chapter, climate change shall include"
7 AMENDMENT NO. 11
8 On page 2, delete lines 23 through 29 and insert the following:
9 "(2) "Covered civil liability action for climate change damages from
10 greenhouse gas emissions" means any claim or action for damages, penalties, or any
11 other form of relief whether legal or equitable or otherwise, or any cause of action
12 for fraud, misrepresentation, deception, or failure to warn, whether brought under
13 statute or under the Louisiana Civil Code or any other source of Louisiana law, that
14 purports to arise out of or relate to, or seek relief of any kind arising out of or relating
15 to, climate change or the alleged effects of climate change from greenhouse gas
16 emissions or any debate, public controversy, or discussion arising out of or relating
17 to climate change, its causes, or the alleged effects of climate change from
18 greenhouse gas emissions.
19 (3) "Greenhouse gases" means those gaseous constituents of the atmosphere,
20 both natural and anthropogenic, that absorb and emit infrared radiation including but
21 not limited to carbon dioxide, methane, nitrous oxide, water vapor, ozone, or
22 fluorinated gases, such as chlorofluorocarbons, perfluorocarbons, sulfur
23 hexafluoride, and nitrogen trifluoride."
24 AMENDMENT NO. 12
25 On page 3, at the beginning of line 1, change "F." to "(4)"
26 AMENDMENT NO. 13
27 On page 3, delete lines 4 through 28 and delete pages 4 and 5 in their entirety and insert the
28 following:
29 "A. No covered civil liability action for climate change damages from
30 greenhouse gas emissions, as defined in R.S. 30:1603, shall be brought, filed, or
31 maintained by any person against any person in any court or proceeding in this state,
32 including any claim, cause of action, or judicial or administrative proceeding. This
33 prohibition shall not apply to claims, actions, or judicial or administrative
34 proceedings brought for unlawful acts arising out of:
35 (1) A violation of an enforceable Louisiana or federal statutory limitation or
36 restriction against emissions of greenhouse gas, or a violation of the express terms
37 of a valid, enforceable operating, air, or other permit issued by a Louisiana or federal
38 regulatory agency that has jurisdiction over greenhouse gas emissions.
39 (2) A violation of an enforceable standard expressly established under the
40 federal Occupational Safety and Health Act, 29 U.S.C. 651 et seq., where such
41 violation directly and proximately causes the specific injury alleged.
42 B. The provisions of this Chapter shall apply to all covered civil liability
43 actions for climate change damages from greenhouse gas emissions filed on or after
44 the effective date of this Chapter, regardless of when the conduct giving rise to such
45 action occurred.
46 §1605. Construction
47 No provision of this Chapter shall be construed to create a public or private
48 claim, cause of action, or remedy.
49 Section 2. If any provision of this Act or the application thereof is held invalid, such
50 invalidity shall not affect other provisions or applications of this Act which can be given
