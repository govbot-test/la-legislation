11 §1472.9. Civil penalties
12 A. After notice and an opportunity for a hearing held in accordance with the
13 Administrative Procedure Act, the deputy secretary may assess a civil penalty not to
14 exceed ten thousand dollars for each violation against any person or explosives
15 business he determines to have violated any rule or regulation adopted pursuant to
16 this Part. In determining the amount of the penalty the deputy secretary shall take
17 into account the nature, circumstances, extent, and gravity of the violation, the
18 degree of culpability of the person or explosives business violating the rule or
19 regulation, the person's or explosives business's history of previous offenses, the
20 person's or explosives business's ability to pay and the effect of the penalty on his
21 ability to continue to operate, and any other matter that justice requires.
