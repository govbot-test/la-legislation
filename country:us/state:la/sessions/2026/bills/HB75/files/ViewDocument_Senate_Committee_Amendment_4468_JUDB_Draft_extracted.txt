9 §44. Definitions
10 When used in this Chapter, the following terms shall mean:
11 * * *
12 (15) "Net gaming proceeds" means the total of all cash and property,
13 including checks received by a licensee, whether collected or not, received by the
14 licensee from gaming operations, less the total of all cash paid out as winnings to
15 patrons and five seven million dollars annually directly attributable to promotional
16 play wagers.
17 * * *
18 §205. Definitions
19 When used in this Chapter, the following terms have these meanings:
20 * * *
21 (16) "Gross revenue" means the total of all value received by the casino
22 gaming operator from gaming operations, including cash, checks, vouchers,
23 instruments and anything received in payment for credit extended to a patron for
24 purposes of gaming, and compensation received for conducting any game in which
25 the casino gaming operator is not party to a wager, less the total of all value or
26 amounts paid out as winnings to patrons and credit instruments or checks which are
27 uncollected as determined by rule of the corporation and five seven million dollars
28 annually directly attributable to promotional play wagers.
29 * * *
30 Section 2. R.S. 27:353(9) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
31 §353. Definitions
32 When used in this Chapter, the following terms shall have these meanings:
33 * * *
34 (9) "Net slot machine proceeds" means the total of all cash and property
35 received by a licensee from slot machine gaming operations minus the amount of
36 cash or prizes paid to winners and five seven million dollars annually directly
37 attributable to promotional play wagers.
