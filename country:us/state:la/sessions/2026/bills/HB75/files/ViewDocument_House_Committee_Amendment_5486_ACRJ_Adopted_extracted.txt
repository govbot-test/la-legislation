HOUSE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
Original House Bill No. 75 by Representative Jackson
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, line 2, change "R.S. 27:44(introductory paragraph) and (15) and 205(16)" to
3 "R.S. 27:353(introductory paragraph) and (9)"
4 AMENDMENT NO. 2
5 On page 1, line 6, change "R.S. 27:44(introductory paragraph) and (15) and 205(16) are" to
6 "R.S. 27:353(introductory paragraph) and (9) are"
7 AMENDMENT NO. 3
8 On page 1, delete lines 8 through 19 in their entirety and delete pages 2 and 3 in their
9 entirety and insert the following:
10 "§353. Definitions
11 When used in this Chapter, the following terms shall have these meanings:
12 * * *
13 (9) "Net slot machine proceeds" means the total of all cash and property
14 received by a licensee from slot machine gaming operations minus the amount of
15 cash or prizes paid to winners and five million dollars annually directly attributable
16 to promotional play wagers or the percentage cap, whichever is greater. For the
17 purposes of this Section, the percentage cap shall be as follows:
18 (a) For tax year 2027, equal to four percent of a licensee's taxable revenue
19 for that tax year.
20 (b) For tax year 2028, equal to seven percent of a licensee's taxable revenue
21 for that tax year.
22 (c) For tax year 2029 and every year thereafter, equal to ten percent of a
23 licensee's taxable revenue for that year.
24 * * *"
