HLS 26RS-6208 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 377
BY REPRESENTATIVES PHELPS AND TURNER
Student of the Year Sanil Savita Thorat
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Louisiana Department of Education 2026 Elementary Student of the Year
3 Sanil Savita Thorat.
4 WHEREAS, the Louisiana Department of Education (LDOE) honored twenty-four
5 regional finalists and named three Louisiana Students of the Year during a ceremony at The
6 Cook Hotel and Conference Center in Baton Rouge; and
7 WHEREAS, each year, the LDOE and the Louisiana Board of Elementary and
8 Secondary Education (BESE) recognize students who exemplify academic excellence,
9 leadership, and service; finalists include one fifth, eighth, and twelfth grader from each of
10 the state's eight BESE regions; and
11 WHEREAS, the 2026 overall honorees are Sanil Savita Thorat, Kaejha Mary-Anne
12 Christine Stewart, and Melissa An Le; and
13 WHEREAS, Sanil Savita Thorat is the Louisiana Elementary School Student of the
14 Year; he attends Fairfield Magnet School in Caddo Parish and is an accomplished speller
15 who earned a place at the Scripps National Spelling Bee and received top honors at the
16 regional level; he also completed the 1,000 Books Before Kindergarten challenge, has earned
17 recognition in music competitions, competes as a runner, and performs piano in recitals at
18 nursing homes, churches, and other venues; he aspires to become a doctor and volunteers
19 with organizations focused on community wellness; and
20 WHEREAS, Sanil Savita Thorat is most deserving of recognition for bringing great
21 pride to Caddo Parish and the state of Louisiana through his outstanding achievements.
