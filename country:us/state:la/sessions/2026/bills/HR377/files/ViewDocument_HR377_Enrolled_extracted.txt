ENROLLED
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 377
BY REPRESENTATIVES PHELPS AND TURNER
A RESOLUTION
Sanil Savita Thorat.
WHEREAS, the Louisiana Department of Education (LDOE) honored twenty-four
regional finalists and named three Louisiana Students of the Year during a ceremony at The
Cook Hotel and Conference Center in Baton Rouge; and
WHEREAS, each year, the LDOE and the Louisiana Board of Elementary and
Secondary Education (BESE) recognize students who exemplify academic excellence,
leadership, and service; finalists include one fifth, eighth, and twelfth grader from each of
the state's eight BESE regions; and
WHEREAS, the 2026 overall honorees are Sanil Savita Thorat, Kaejha Mary-Anne
Christine Stewart, and Melissa An Le; and
WHEREAS, Sanil Savita Thorat is the Louisiana Elementary School Student of the
Year; he attends Fairfield Magnet School in Caddo Parish and is an accomplished speller
who earned a place at the Scripps National Spelling Bee and received top honors at the
regional level; he also completed the 1,000 Books Before Kindergarten challenge, has earned
recognition in music competitions, competes as a runner, and performs piano in recitals at
nursing homes, churches, and other venues; he aspires to become a doctor and volunteers
with organizations focused on community wellness; and
WHEREAS, Sanil Savita Thorat is most deserving of recognition for bringing great
pride to Caddo Parish and the state of Louisiana through his outstanding achievements.
Page 1 of 2
HR NO. 377 ENROLLED
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Louisiana Department of Education 2026
Elementary Student of the Year Sanil Savita Thorat; does hereby recognize him for building
a strong foundation for the future success of the state; and does hereby encourage him to
continue his excellent work and always strive for greatness.
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
