SLS 26RS-2436 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 99
BY SENATOR FOIL
Renewable Energy Industries Association Day at the Louisiana State Capitol.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate Wednesday, April 29, 2026, as Gulf States Renewable Energy Industries
3 Association Day at the Louisiana State Capitol.
4 WHEREAS, the Gulf States Renewable Energy Industries Association (GSREIA)
5 is the trade association of member companies leading in all parts of the renewable energy
6 supply chain; and
7 WHEREAS, GSREIA members provide high quality Louisiana jobs, local property
8 tax revenue, and economic benefits to local communities; and
9 WHEREAS, Project Vanguard is the nation's premier veteran-led network,
10 connecting and transitioning service members and veterans with meaningful careers across
11 the full spectrum of the American energy sector, from oil and gas, to renewable and nuclear,
12 across every field of the industry; and
13 WHEREAS, GSREIA and Project Vanguard collaborate with a diverse array of
14 companies throughout the supply chain, including manufacturers, suppliers, distributors, and
15 developers of energy systems and components, as well as providers of installation,
16 maintenance, and grid integration services; and
17 WHEREAS, GSREIA and Project Vanguard support the continued deployment of
18 renewable energy resources, strengthening grid reliability, and contributing to long-term
Page 1 of 2

SR NO. 99
1 economic investment and job creation in the state; and
2 WHEREAS, GSREIA and Project Vanguard have fostered expansion of the sector
3 across utility-scale, commercial, and residential energy generation and storage, creating jobs,
4 and supporting economic development; and
5 WHEREAS, GSREIA and Project Vanguard promote the expansion of new energy
6 generation, the strengthening of the region's renewable energy infrastructure, and
7 contributing to Louisiana's energy security and resilience in the face of natural disasters; and
8 WHEREAS, GSREIA includes companies across the nation and the Gulf Coast
9 region, from small businesses to Fortune 500 companies, and stimulates sustainable
10 economic growth by supporting workforce development, removing market barriers, and
11 promoting innovation.
12 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
13 does hereby extend sincere wishes that the association continue to cultivate a prosperous
14 future, create jobs, and bolster innovation in the energy sector in Louisiana and across the
15 country.
16 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana does
17 hereby commend Gulf States Renewable Energy Industries Association and Project
18 Vanguard and designates Wednesday, April 29, 2026, as Gulf States Renewable Energy
19 Industries Association Day.
20 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to Gulf
21 States Renewable Energy Industries Association and Project Vanguard.
The original instrument and the following digest, which constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument, were prepared by Senate Legislative Services.
The keyword, summary, and digest do not constitute part of the law or proof
or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
