Section 1:
SLS 26RS-591 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY SENATOR BARROW
MOTOR VEHICLES. Provides for regulation of prices for driving courses by the
Department of Public Safety. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 32:402.1(A)(2)(d) and R.S. 40:1461(C)(3), relative to pricing for
3 driving courses; to provide for administrative rules; to provide for pricing of driving
4 courses; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 32:402.1(A)(2)(d) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as
7 follows:
8 §402.1. Driver education; required
9 A. No application for a license for the operation of a motor vehicle shall be
10 received from any person making application for the first time unless there is also
11 submitted with the application, on a form approved by the secretary of the
12 Department of Public Safety and Corrections, public safety services, written
13 evidence of the successful completion by the applicant of one of the following:
14 * * *
15 (2) A "prelicensing training course" administered by a Class "D" and "E"
16 driver education provider licensed and contracted pursuant to R.S. 40:1461 for any
17 person eighteen years or older if a driver education course is not completed. The
Page 1 of 3

1 prelicensing training course shall consist of a minimum of six hours of classroom
2 instruction and a minimum of eight hours of actual driving instruction, including the
3 administering of the skills test. A person shall not be allowed to receive more than
4 four hours of actual driving instruction on any single calendar day. A properly
5 licensed third-party examiner may, at his discretion, administer the skills test to the
6 student prior to the conclusion of the eight hours of actual driving instruction. Upon
7 successful completion of the instruction and skills test, the third-party tester shall
8 provide the student with a department-approved certificate of successful completion
9 in a sealed envelope to be delivered to the department. If the student fails to
10 successfully complete the skills test, the third-party examiner shall issue a
11 department-approved certificate indicating completion of the driver education course
12 but failure of the skills test. The Department of Public Safety and Corrections, public
13 safety services, shall establish rules and regulations to administer and define the
14 requirements of the course. Oversight review of these rules shall be conducted by the
15 House and Senate committees on transportation, highways and public works in
16 accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act. The rules shall provide for:
17 * * *
18 (d) Classroom size, pricing, and facilities.
19 * * *
20 Section 2. R.S. 40:1461(C)(3) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
21 §1461. Requirements for private training and driving instructor schools
22 * * *
23 C. The commissioner of the office of motor vehicles shall establish rules and
24 regulations in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act subject to oversight
25 review by the House and Senate committees on transportation, highways, and public
26 works, for the following:
27 * * *
28 (3) The establishment of the terms, manner, price and location of instruction
29 and testing for driving courses.

SB 309 Original 2026 Regular Session Barrow
Present law provides for driver's education courses.
Present law provides for administrative rules by the DPS. Proposed law retains present law
to provide for pricing in their administrative rules for driving courses.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 32:402.1(A)(2)(d) and R.S. 40:1461(C)(3))
