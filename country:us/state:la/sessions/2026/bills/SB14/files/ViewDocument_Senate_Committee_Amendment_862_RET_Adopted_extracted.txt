SCASB14 4226 862
SENATE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
by Senator Price
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 7, line 3, delete the ";" after "certification"
3 AMENDMENT NO. 2
4 On page 7, line 10, after "hearing" insert a comma
5 AMENDMENT NO. 3
6 On page 7, line 17, change "credit" to "credits"
7 AMENDMENT NO. 4
8 On page 7, delete lines 22 through 29 and on page 8, delete lines 1 through 17, and insert the
9 following:
10 “E. The provisions of this Section, other than Paragraph (C)(3) and
11 Subsection G of this Section, shall be applicable to any retiree who returns to
12 active service with an employer covered by the provisions of this Chapter
13 whose reemployment is based on a contract or corporate contract under the
14 following circumstances:
15 (1) For nonpostsecondary employers, when the services provided could
16 be performed by an employee of a public school district and the services
17 provided fall under the following areas:
18 (a) Instructional programs, including regular and special education
19 instruction across all age and grade levels, and vocational and other
20 instructional programs.
21 (b) Pupil support services, including child welfare and attendance
22 services, guidance services, health services, and pupil assessment and appraisal
23 services.
24 (c) Instructional staff services, including curriculum development
25 services, parish-wide directors, supervisors and coordinators of instructional
26 programs, media-based instructional staff, educational media or instructional
27 staff, and clerical and support staff that support instructional staff services
28 positions. The provisions of this Paragraph shall not include staff training
29 services.
30 (d) School administration, including principals, assistant principals and
31 related administrator positions at school sites, and clerical and support staff
32 that support school administration positions.
33 (e) Food services operations, including school food service supervisors
34 and assistant supervisors, cafeteria managers and assistant managers, lunch
35 room workers, aides, food services personnel, and clerical and support staff
36 that support food services positions.
37 (2) For public postsecondary education institutions, when the retiree is
38 reemployed as a professor, adjunct professor, instructor, or researcher.”
