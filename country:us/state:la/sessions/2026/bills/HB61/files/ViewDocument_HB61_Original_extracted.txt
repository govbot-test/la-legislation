HLS 26RS-642 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE BILL NO. 61
BY REPRESENTATIVE BAYHAM
HOLIDAYS: Creates the holiday of the Battle of New Orleans Day
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 1:58.10, relative to holidays; to provide for the Battle of New Orleans Day;
3 and to provide for related matters.
4 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
5 Section 1. R.S. 1:58.10 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
6 §58.10. Battle of New Orleans Day
7 The eighth day of January is hereby designated as the Battle of New Orleans
8 Day throughout the state of Louisiana to honor those who served in this historic
9 battle.

Abstract: Provides for the Battle of New Orleans Day.

served in this historic battle.
