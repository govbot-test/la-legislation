ENROLLED
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 246
BY REPRESENTATIVE LAFLEUR
A RESOLUTION
WHEREAS, the Louisiana Art Education Association (LAEA) inspires and
empowers art educators by fostering creativity, advancing professional growth, championing
leadership, and advocating for the transformative power of the arts in every community; and
WHEREAS, LAEA annually presents eight awards to art educators who demonstrate
exceptional dedication to fostering creativity, community, advocacy, and dynamic learning
for Louisiana scholars; and
WHEREAS, award candidates are nominated by their colleagues and supervisors,
and nominations are reviewed by the LAEA Board of Directors; the board consists of fifteen
members who represent a diverse range of art education experience, from pre-K through the
university level, across the state; and
WHEREAS, LAEA honorees are recognized at the State Fall Conference, the
National Art Education Convention, and the state capitol; and
WHEREAS, the 2025 LAEA award recipients are Kelly Adkinson of East Baton
Rouge Parish, Southeastern Region Elementary Art Educator of the Year; Ange Riehl of
Lafayette Parish, LAEA Art Educator of the Year; Ashley Comeaux-Foret of St. Charles
Parish, LAEA Elementary Art Educator of the Year; Savannah Dominique of St. Tammany
Parish, LAEA New to the Profession Educator of the Year; Alix Rogers of Lafayette Parish,
LAEA Preservice Art Educator of the Year; Aubrey Garcia of Caddo Parish, LAEA
Secondary Art Educator of the Year; Shelly Breaux of Lafayette Parish, LAEA Southeastern
Region Educator of the Year; and Chantell Nabonne of Orleans Parish, LAEA Museum Art
Educator of the Year; and
Page 1 of 2
HR NO. 246 ENROLLED
WHEREAS, it is with great pride that the House of Representatives recognizes
Artists at the Capitol Day and honors the 2025 LAEA award recipients for their service.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby designate Monday, May 11, 2026, as Artists at the
Capitol Day and does hereby commend the Louisiana Art Education Association 2025 award
recipients for their outstanding dedication and service to art education.
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES

HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 246
BY REPRESENTATIVE LAFLEUR
A RESOLUTION
To designate Monday, May 11, 2026, as Artists at the Capitol Day.
WHEREAS, the Louisiana Art Education Association (LAEA) inspires and
empowers art educators by fostering creativity, advancing professional growth, championing
leadership, and advocating for the transformative power of the arts in every community; and
WHEREAS, LAEA annually presents eight awards to art educators who demonstrate
exceptional dedication to fostering creativity, community, advocacy, and dynamic learning
for Louisiana scholars; and
WHEREAS, award candidates are nominated by their colleagues and supervisors,
and nominations are reviewed by the LAEA Board of Directors; the board consists of fifteen
members who represent a diverse range of art education experience, from pre-K through the
university level, across the state; and
WHEREAS, LAEA honorees are recognized at the State Fall Conference, the
National Art Education Convention, and the state capitol; and
WHEREAS, the 2025 LAEA award recipients are Kelly Adkinson of East Baton
Rouge Parish, Southeastern Region Elementary Art Educator of the Year; Ange Riehl of
Lafayette Parish, LAEA Art Educator of the Year; Ashley Comeaux-Foret of St. Charles
Parish, LAEA Elementary Art Educator of the Year; Savannah Dominique of St. Tammany
Parish, LAEA New to the Profession Educator of the Year; Alix Rogers of Lafayette Parish,
LAEA Preservice Art Educator of the Year; Aubrey Garcia of Caddo Parish, LAEA
Secondary Art Educator of the Year; Shelly Breaux of Lafayette Parish, LAEA Southeastern
Region Educator of the Year; and Chantell Nabonne of Orleans Parish, LAEA Museum Art
Educator of the Year; and
