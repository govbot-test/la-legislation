HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 232
BY REPRESENTATIVE TAYLOR
A RESOLUTION
To commend Pastor Neil J. Bernard on his legacy of pastoral leadership and community
service to St. John the Baptist Parish.
WHEREAS, Pastor Neil J. Bernard is a devoted leader and community advocate who
was born in the vibrant city of New Orleans, Louisiana; after graduating from high school,
he continued his education at Xavier University of New Orleans and Bethel Bible College,
where he developed his skills in ministry and community service; and
WHEREAS, Pastor Bernard founded New Wine Christian Church in LaPlace,
Louisiana, in 1995, and he has faithfully served as its pastor for over twenty-nine years;
under his leadership, the church embraces a mission to renew lives, restore families, and
rebuild communities; and
WHEREAS, New Wine Christian Church offers a range of programs, including after-
school initiatives, summer camps, recreational activities, and financial assistance, which
benefit numerous families in the area; and
WHEREAS, beyond his clerical role, Pastor Bernard serves as chairman of St. John
the Baptist Parish Long-Term Recovery; he has played a critical role in assisting the
community's recovery efforts following three major hurricanes and a tornado and
demonstrated his unwavering commitment to the well-being of his neighbors; and
WHEREAS, Pastor Bernard also serves as chairman of the St. John Pastors Coalition
and believes in the power of collaboration and teamwork to address the pressing needs of
the community; and
WHEREAS, Pastor Bernard has traveled extensively and preached the Gospel in
several nations overseas; his church supports nineteen missionaries who serve around the
world; and

WHEREAS, Pastor Bernard has shared forty-one happy years of marriage with his
wife, Angela, and they are the proud parents of four children and grandparents of seven
grandchildren; and
WHEREAS, Pastor Neil J. Bernard embodies a commitment to service, faith, and
community building, and he is deserving of recognition for his outstanding work.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Pastor Neil J. Bernard on his legacy of
pastoral leadership and community service to St. John the Baptist Parish and does hereby
extend sincere wishes that he remain prosperous in all of his endeavors.
