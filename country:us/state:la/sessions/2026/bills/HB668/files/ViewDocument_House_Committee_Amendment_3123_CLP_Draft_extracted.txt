13 AMENDMENT NO. 6
14 On page 2, line 4, change "Article 4607.1 is" to "Articles 4602.1 and 4607.1 are"
15 AMENDMENT NO. 7
16 On page 2, between lines 5 and 6, insert the following:
17 "Art. 4602.1. Judicial partition of immovable property; posting of notice
18 When the property sought to be partitioned is a corporeal immovable, the
19 petitioner, contemporaneous with causing service of the petition, shall affix notice
20 of the institution of the proceeding in at least one prominent location on the
21 immovable. This notice shall set forth the title and docket number of the proceeding,
22 the name and address of the court, a description of the property sought to be
23 partitioned, and shall notify the public that the plaintiff is seeking to have the
24 property partitioned. Defects in the notice required by this Article shall not result in
25 the nullification of the partition.
26 * * *
27 Art. 4605. Preference; appointment of notary; discretion of court
28 A. A partition proceeding shall be tried with preference over other ordinary
29 proceedings.
30 B. After the trial of the proceeding, if the court finds that the plaintiff is
31 entitled to a partition of the property, the court shall may appoint a notary to make
32 the partition in accordance with law.
33 C. Except as otherwise provided in Article 4606, the court has discretion to
34 direct the manner and conditions of effecting the partition, so that it will be most
35 advantageous and convenient to the parties.
36 * * *"
