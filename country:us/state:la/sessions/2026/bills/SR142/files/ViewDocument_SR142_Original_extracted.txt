SLS 26RS-3279 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 142
BY SENATOR HODGES
COMMENDATIONS. Commends and congratulates Braxton Whitesell as the reserve
champion at the Hondo Rodeo Fest in New Orleans, Louisiana.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend and congratulate Braxton Whitesell on being named reserve champion of the
3 Hondo Rodeo Fest in New Orleans, Louisiana.
4 WHEREAS, Braxton Whitesell, a proud resident of the city of Walker in Livingston
5 Parish, Louisiana, has demonstrated exceptional talent, discipline, and determination in the
6 sport of rodeo; and
7 WHEREAS, Braxton has distinguished himself as a dedicated athlete and
8 representative of his community through years of hard work and commitment to excellence;
9 and
10 WHEREAS, Braxton has compiled an extraordinary list of accomplishments in high
11 school rodeo, including being crowned champion bull rider at the International Finals Youth
12 Rodeo in 2025; placing sixth in bull riding at the 2025 National High School Finals Rodeo,
13 as well as qualifying in 2022, 2023, and 2024; winning three Mississippi High School Rodeo
14 Association bull riding championships, two Mississippi High School Rodeo Association
15 saddle bronc riding championships, and being named the 2025 Tri-State Rodeo High School
16 Champion; and
17 WHEREAS, Braxton Whitesell has also excelled at the professional level through
18 the Professional Cowboys Rodeo Association, currently sitting ninth in the world standings,

1 leading the Resistol Rookie of the year all-around standings, placing second in the Resistol
2 Rookie of the Year bull riding competition, earning first place at the Fort Worth Stock Show
3 and Rodeo Rookie Rally and the Brighton Field Days Festival Extreme Bulls event, and
4 earning third place at the Dixie National Rodeo; and
5 WHEREAS, in 2026, Braxton Whitesell earned the prestigious title of reserve
6 champion at the Hondo Rodeo Fest in New Orleans, Louisiana, one of the region's most
7 competitive and respected rodeo events; and
8 WHEREAS, this remarkable achievement brings great pride and recognition to
9 Livingston Parish and the city of Walker, showcasing the strength, character, skill, and
10 perseverance of its young athletes; and
11 WHEREAS, Braxton's success serves as an inspiration to others, reflecting the values
12 of faith, hard work, and determination instilled through his family, education, and
13 community; and
14 WHEREAS, Braxton represents the city of Walker and Livingston Parish with
15 excellence and distinction through his outstanding accomplishment as reserve champion of
16 the 2026 Hondo Rodeo Fest in New Orleans, Louisiana.
17 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
18 does hereby commend and congratulate Braxton Whitesell on earning the title of reserve
19 champion at the 2026 Hondo Rodeo Fest.
20 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to
21 Braxton Whitesell.
