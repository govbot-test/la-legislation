SCASB386 2810 1630
1 "A. The attorney general shall enforce the provisions of this Chapter.
2 B. The attorney general shall post on his website, information relating
3 to the responsibilities of a controller and a processor and consumers' rights
4 pursuant to this Chapter.
5 C. Any violation of the provisions of this Chapter shall constitute an
6 unfair and deceptive trade practice pursuant to the Unfair Trade Practices and
7 Consumer Protection Law, R.S. 51: 1401 et seq., excluding private rights of
8 action as provided in R.S. 51:1409 and 1409.1. Notwithstanding any other
9 provision of law to the contrary, any monies received related to the attorney
10 general's enforcement of this Chapter shall be used by the attorney general for
11 consumer protection efforts or to promote consumer protection and education.
12 D. Beginning January 1, 2027, and ending July 31, 2027, before bringing
13 an action pursuant to R.S. 51:1780, the attorney general shall notify a person
14 in writing, not later than the thirtieth calendar day before initiating an
15 investigation, identifying the specific provisions of this Chapter the attorney
16 general alleges is being violated. The attorney general shall not initiate an
17 investigation against the person if the person does all of the following:
18 (1) Cures the alleged violation identified by the attorney general within
19 the thirty-day period.
20 (2) Provides the attorney general with a written statement that the
21 person cured the alleged violation and notified the consumer that the
22 consumer's privacy violation was addressed provided the consumer's contact
23 information has been made available to the person.
24 (3) Submits supportive documentation to the attorney general to show
25 how the privacy violation was cured.
26 (4) Changes are made to the internal policy, if necessary, to ensure that
27 no further violations occur.
28 Section 2. This Act shall become effective on January 1, 2027."
