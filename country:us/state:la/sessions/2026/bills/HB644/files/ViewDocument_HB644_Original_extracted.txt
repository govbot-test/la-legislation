3 schools from being members of, or participating in any competition sponsored by,
4 associations or organizations that do not provide for certain student eligibility

15 eligibility to participate in interscholastic athletics for students in grades seven
16 through twelve:
17 (1) An otherwise eligible student who has not previously been enrolled in

HB 644 Original 2026 Regular Session Wyble
Abstract: Prohibits schools from being members of, or participating in competition
sponsored by, any intrastate interscholastic extracurricular athletic association or
organization that does not provide for certain student eligibility procedures,
including regarding transfers.
Proposed law prohibits schools from being members of, or participating in competition
sponsored by, any intrastate interscholastic extracurricular athletic association or
organization that does not provide for the following with respect to student eligibility:
(1) An otherwise eligible student who has not previously been enrolled in any member
school is eligible to participate without delay upon enrollment in a member school
(a "member school" is a school that participates in such competition).
(2) An otherwise eligible student may transfer from one member school to another
without delay in eligibility to participate as follows:
