1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Sabrina Schmidt for her artistic contributions to the cultural landscape of
3 St. Bernard Parish and the state of Louisiana.
4 WHEREAS, Sabrina Schmidt grew up in the Delacroix Island community in
5 St. Bernard Parish, Louisiana, where she developed a lifelong passion for art; and
6 WHEREAS, Sabrina received a formal education in art and design and began work
7 as a web designer early in her professional career; in the wake of Hurricane Katrina, she
8 moved to Tennessee and found new inspiration in the mountain scenery; and
9 WHEREAS, Sabrina is incredibly talented with a brush and paint, and she has
10 developed her own style of plein air art; and
11 WHEREAS, as a plein air oil painter, Sabrina connects deeply with her surroundings
12 and focuses on light, atmosphere, and subject matter; her art captures the emotional essence
13 of a landscape through exploration, connection, and design; and
14 WHEREAS, Ms. Schmidt earned the Best in Show award at her first plein air festival
15 in 2022 and has since received recognition at regional and national events; she won Best in
16 Show, Artist Choice, and Best Main Street at a 2025 plein air event in West Reading,
17 Pennsylvania; she also won Artist Choice and Best in Show at Teche Plein Air in 2026; and
18 WHEREAS, Ms. Schmidt was one of ten artists selected for the Art in the Park: En
19 Plein Air Public Arts Project in New Orleans; she also designed and painted the official
20 artwork for two consecutive Grand Isle Fishing Rodeos, which is the oldest fishing
