2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 128
BY REPRESENTATIVE CHASSION
SPECIAL DAY/WEEK/MONTH: Designates April 14, 2026, as Acadiana's Red and White
Day at the state capitol
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate Tuesday, April 14, 2026, as Acadiana's Red and White Day at the state capitol.
3 WHEREAS, One Acadiana, the University of Louisiana at Lafayette, and the UL
4 Lafayette Alumni Association are bringing Acadiana's economic development, chamber,
5 civic, business, and higher education partners together for "Acadiana's Red and White Day"
6 to promote the shared interests of the Acadiana Region; and
7 WHEREAS, Acadiana's Red and White Day is a celebration of the region's unique
8 identity which unites citizens, civic leaders, educators, business owners, and students to
9 promote the interests and accomplishments of Acadiana, highlight regional priorities, foster
10 dialogue between state lawmakers and local stakeholders, and advocate for continued
11 investment and development in the region; and
12 WHEREAS, for one hundred and twenty-seven years, the University of Louisiana
13 at Lafayette has supported and improved the educational, social, and economic well-being
14 of Acadiana and beyond; and
15 WHEREAS, the university has an annual statewide economic impact of $4.4 billion
16 and supports more than 47,500 jobs; for every dollar invested in the University of Louisiana
17 at Lafayette, the university generates $7.30 in statewide economic impact; and
18 WHEREAS, the university serves to educate our citizens and grow and strengthen
19 our talent pipeline; ninety percent of current students enrolled at the university hail from
20 Louisiana, and seventy-two percent of these students remain in state for ten years after
21 earning their bachelor's degree; and

1 WHEREAS, the University of Louisiana at Lafayette was designated for the second
2 time as a Carnegie Research I university, which recognizes the institution as a very high
3 spending and doctorate production university; the university has spent $1.7 billion on
4 groundbreaking research since 2013 and ranks fourth in the nation for industry-funded
5 research; and
6 WHEREAS, in March 2026, the university welcomed its seventh president, Dr.
7 Ramesh Kolluru, who is an "Honorary Cajun" with a bold vision for his alma mater and its
8 students; and
9 WHEREAS, the continued success of the University of Louisiana at Lafayette, the
10 Acadiana Region, and the state of Louisiana is contingent upon capable leadership, a shared
11 vision, and strong collaboration with partners throughout the region and the state.
12 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
13 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby designate Tuesday, April 14, 2026, as Acadiana's Red
14 and White Day at the state capitol and does hereby commend the people of Acadiana for
15 their ongoing contributions to the cultural, economic, and social fabric of Louisiana.
