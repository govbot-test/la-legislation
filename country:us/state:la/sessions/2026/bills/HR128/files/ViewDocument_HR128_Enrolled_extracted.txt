2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 128
BY REPRESENTATIVE CHASSION
A RESOLUTION
WHEREAS, One Acadiana, the University of Louisiana at Lafayette, and the UL
Lafayette Alumni Association are bringing Acadiana's economic development, chamber,
civic, business, and higher education partners together for "Acadiana's Red and White Day"
to promote the shared interests of the Acadiana Region; and
WHEREAS, Acadiana's Red and White Day is a celebration of the region's unique
identity which unites citizens, civic leaders, educators, business owners, and students to
promote the interests and accomplishments of Acadiana, highlight regional priorities, foster
dialogue between state lawmakers and local stakeholders, and advocate for continued
investment and development in the region; and
WHEREAS, for one hundred and twenty-seven years, the University of Louisiana
at Lafayette has supported and improved the educational, social, and economic well-being
of Acadiana and beyond; and
WHEREAS, the university has an annual statewide economic impact of $4.4 billion
and supports more than 47,500 jobs; for every dollar invested in the University of Louisiana
at Lafayette, the university generates $7.30 in statewide economic impact; and
WHEREAS, the university serves to educate our citizens and grow and strengthen
our talent pipeline; ninety percent of current students enrolled at the university hail from
Louisiana, and seventy-two percent of these students remain in state for ten years after
earning their bachelor's degree; and

HR NO. 128 ENROLLED
WHEREAS, the University of Louisiana at Lafayette was designated for the second
time as a Carnegie Research I university, which recognizes the institution as a very high
spending and doctorate production university; the university has spent $1.7 billion on
groundbreaking research since 2013 and ranks fourth in the nation for industry-funded
research; and
WHEREAS, in March 2026, the university welcomed its seventh president, Dr.
Ramesh Kolluru, who is an "Honorary Cajun" with a bold vision for his alma mater and its
students; and
WHEREAS, the continued success of the University of Louisiana at Lafayette, the
Acadiana Region, and the state of Louisiana is contingent upon capable leadership, a shared
vision, and strong collaboration with partners throughout the region and the state.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby designate Tuesday, April 14, 2026, as Acadiana's Red
and White Day at the state capitol and does hereby commend the people of Acadiana for
their ongoing contributions to the cultural, economic, and social fabric of Louisiana.
