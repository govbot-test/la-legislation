Section 1:
ENROLLED
ACT No. 150
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE MELERINE
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 23:921(P), relative to noncompete contracts or agreements; to prohibit
3 noncompete contracts, agreements, or provisions for interns and apprentices; to
4 provide for confidentiality agreements and certain employment information and
5 agreements; to provide a definition; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 23:921(P) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §921. Restraint of business prohibited; restraint on forum prohibited; competing
9 business; contracts against engaging in; provisions for
10 * * *
11 P.(1) Notwithstanding any provision of this Section to the contrary, there
12 shall be no contract or agreement or provision entered into by an intern, whether paid
13 or unpaid, or apprentice and his employer restraining the intern or apprentice from
14 engaging in a business or an employment similar to that of the employer. The
15 contract or agreement or provision shall not restrict an intern or apprentice from
16 pursuing employment, educational, or professional opportunities following the
17 completion of the internship or apprenticeship. The intern or apprentice shall,
18 however, remain subject to the employer's confidentiality, intellectual property, and
19 data protection agreements.
