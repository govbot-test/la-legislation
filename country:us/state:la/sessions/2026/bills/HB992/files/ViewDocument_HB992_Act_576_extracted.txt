1 (ii) The state Department of Education may assign a early childhood
2 education identification number to every child participating in a program that does
3 not receive public funds at a prekindergarten program at a school or an early learning
4 center as defined in R.S. 17:407.33 whose parent or legal guardian has agreed to

6 (b) Each local public school board shall assign such a student identification
7 number to every student enrolled in a public elementary or secondary school, unless
8 the student already has a number assigned to him from his enrollment in an early
9 childhood program. By no later than December 31, 2027, each local public school
10 board shall link each early childhood education identification number assigned by
11 the state Department of Education to the corresponding student's student
12 identification number for the purposes of research and analysis of early childhood
13 programs. Student identification numbers shall not include or be based on social
14 security numbers, and a student shall retain his student identification number for his
15 tenure in Louisiana public elementary and secondary schools.
