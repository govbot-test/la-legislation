FEES/LICENSES/PERMITS: Provides relative to reinstatement fees for certain offenses
assessed by the office of motor vehicles
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 32:414(I)(1), relative to reinstatement fees for certain suspended
3 licenses; to provide for an increase in reinstatement fees applicable to a person
4 whose license was suspended for operating a motor vehicle while under the influence
5 of alcoholic beverages, narcotic drugs, or certain stimulants; and to provide for
6 related matters.
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
8 Section 1. R.S. 32:414(I)(1) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
9 §414. Suspension, revocation, renewal, and cancellation of licenses; judicial review
10 * * *
11 I.(1) The reinstatement fee for a license of a person who has been convicted
12 of or forfeited bond on or entered a plea of guilty or nolo contendere to a charge of
13 operating a motor vehicle while under the influence of beverages of high alcoholic
14 content, of low alcoholic content, of narcotic drugs, or of central nervous system
15 stimulants shall be one two hundred dollars if one conviction appears listed on his
16 record prior to application for reinstatement; two four hundred dollars if two such
17 convictions appear on his record prior to application for reinstatement; and three six
18 hundred dollars for each conviction on his record after the second conviction prior

DIGEST
The digest printed below was prepared by House Legislative Services. It constitutes no part

HB 845 Original 2026 Regular Session Bayham
Abstract: Increases the reinstatement fees for persons whose licenses were suspended,
revoked, or withdrawn for operating a motor vehicle while under the influence of
alcoholic beverages and narcotic drugs.
Present law requires a person convicted of or forfeited bond on or plead guilty or nolo
contendere to the charge of operating a motor vehicle while under the influence of beverages
of high or low alcoholic content, of narcotic drugs, or of central nervous system stimulants
to pay a drivers license reinstatement fee of $100 for a first offense, $200 for a second
offense, and $300 for any subsequent offense.
Proposed law modifies present law by changing the reinstatement fee for a first offense from
$100 to $200, for a second offense from $200 to $400, and for any subsequent offense from
$300 to $600.
(Amends R.S. 32:414(I)(1))
