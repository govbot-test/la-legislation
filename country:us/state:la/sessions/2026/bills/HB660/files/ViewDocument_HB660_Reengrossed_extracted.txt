2 To amend and reenact R.S. 16:10(A)(2) and 11(A)(1), relative to the annual salary of
3 assistant district attorneys and district attorneys paid by the state; to provide for an

5 by the state; to provide for an effective date; and to provide for related matters.

8 as follows:

16 throughout the state of Louisiana is hereby fixed at fifty-five thousand dollars
17 payable monthly by the state treasurer upon the warrant of the district attorney.

HB 660 Reengrossed 2026 Regular Session Egan
Abstract: Increases the salary of assistant district attorneys and district attorneys on July
1, 2026.
Present law provides that, effective July 1, 2021, assistant district attorneys statewide are to
receive an annual salary of $50,000 payable monthly.
Proposed law increases the salary of the assistant district attorneys to $60,000 beginning July
1, 2026.
Present law provides that, effective July 1, 2021, district attorneys statewide are to receive
an annual salary of $55,000 payable monthly.
