15 works for the city of New Orleans, or his designee, to develop a coordinated

19 emergency response priorities. The plan shall be presented to the members of the
20 New Orleans Delegation and the city council of New Orleans annually on May
21 fifteenth, beginning May 15, 2025.

6 §4073. Officers of sewerage and water board; compensation of members
7 The mayor shall be ex officio president of the board shall elect from its
8 membership a president and a president pro tempore. The board shall also elect an
9 executive director, whose salary and bond shall be fixed by the board. The executive
10 director shall hold office at the pleasure of the board. The election or removal of the
11 executive director, the amount of his salary, and the acceptance of his bond shall be
12 determined by a majority vote of the entire board at one of its regular monthly
13 meetings. The duties of the executive director shall be fixed by the board. No
14 member of the board shall receive any salary or compensation for his services,
15 except actual expenses incurred in travelling traveling by authority of, or for the
16 benefit of the board. The board shall have power to elect one of its members
17 president pro tempore who shall act in the absence or disability of the president.
18 §4074. Meetings of the board
19 All meetings of the board shall be held in accordance with rules adopted by
20 the board and shall be open and public as provided by ordinance adopted pursuant
21 to R.S. 33:4159.2. All board meetings shall be conducted in accordance with the
22 Open Meetings Law. All its transactions shall be recorded in the minutes to be kept
23 in writing by the executive director, and its records shall be public. Six members
24 thereof shall constitute a quorum.
