HLS 26RS-65 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE ROBBY CARTER
FLOODING/CONTROL: Prohibits expropriation by the board of commissioners of the
Amite River Basin Drainage and Water Conservation District in East Feliciana and
St. Helena Parishes
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 38:3306(F), relative to expropriation authority of the Amite
3 River Basin Drainage and Water Conservation District board of commissioners; to
4 prohibit expropriation by the board within the parishes of East Feliciana and St.
5 Helena; and to provide for related matters.
6 Notice of intention to introduce this Act has been published
7 as provided by Article III, Section 13 of the Constitution of
8 Louisiana.
9 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
10 Section 1. R.S. 38:3306(F) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
11 §3306. General powers and duties of the board
12 * * *
13 F. The board may expropriate property subject to and in accordance with
14 R.S. 48:441-460 R.S. 48:441 through 460, where applicable; however, the board
15 shall not expropriate property within the parishes of East Feliciana and St. Helena.
16 The owner of residential property or commercial property, including homes,
17 businesses, barns, outbuildings and churches, shall be paid the replacement cost of
18 any such property expropriated by the board.
19 * * *

DIGEST
The digest printed below was prepared by House Legislative Services. It constitutes no part

HB 493 Original 2026 Regular Session Robby Carter
Abstract: Prohibits the board of the Amite River Basin Drainage and Water Conservation
District from expropriating property in East Feliciana and St. Helena Parishes.
Present law authorizes the board of the Amite River Basin Drainage and Water Conservation
District to expropriate property and provides for compensation to the owners of property
expropriated.
Proposed law limits the expropriation authority of the board by prohibiting any expropriation
of property within the parishes of E. Feliciana and St. Helena.
(Amends R.S. 38:3306(F))
