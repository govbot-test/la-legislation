HB 884 Original 2026 Regular Session Spell
Abstract: Provides relative to survivors of sexually-oriented criminal offenses.
Present law (R.S. 15:555) provides for the La. Sexual Assault Oversight Commission and
its membership.
Proposed law retains present law and adds a member with experience in forensic medical
examinations provided to victims of sexually-oriented criminal offenses who is selected by
the attorney general.
Present law (R.S. 15:622) provides relative to sexual assault collection kits.
Proposed law generally retains present law.
Proposed law makes technical revisions to the definitions of "criminal justice agency",
"forensic medical examination", and "sexually-oriented criminal offense". Further makes
technical revisions to the legislative committee reporting requirements in present law.
Present law (R.S. 40:1216.1) provides for procedures for survivors of a sexually-oriented
criminal offense.
Proposed law retains present law and makes a technical revisions.
Present law (R.S. 46:2166) provides for the La. Human Trafficking Prevention Commission
and its membership.
Proposed law retains present law and adds a member with experience in forensic medical
examinations provided to victims of sexually-oriented criminal offenses who is selected by
the attorney general.
(Amends R.S. 15:555(A)(intro. para.) and 622(A)(intro. para.), (1), (2), and (4) and (C) and
R.S. 40:1216.1(A)(intro. para.); Adds R.S. 15:555(A)(18) and R.S. 46:2166(A)(19))
