HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 93
BY REPRESENTATIVE MARCELLE
COMMENDATIONS: Commends Myrtle V. Willis on the occasion of her one hundredth
birthday
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Myrtle V. Willis on the occasion of her one hundredth birthday.
3 WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize Myrtle V. Willis for her inspiring
4 longevity and to commend her on the occasion of her one hundredth birthday; and
5 WHEREAS, Myrtle V. Willis was born on April 14, 1926, in Assumption Parish,
6 Louisiana, where she is one of the oldest residents and a living testament to strength,
7 resilience, and God's enduring grace; and
8 WHEREAS, Ms. Willis is a constant source of love, wisdom, and guidance for her
9 family; she is the only living child of Winnie and Charles Hall, Sr., and carries a unique and
10 treasured legacy which honors the generations before her and continues to inspire those who
11 follow her; and
12 WHEREAS, throughout her life, Ms. Willis embraced her role as a devoted wife and
13 mother with grace and dignity; she and her late husband built a family rooted in faith and
14 love, and she cherishes the memories of those who passed before her, including her husband
15 and children: James Jr., Annette, James III, and Vivian; and
16 WHEREAS, Ms. Willis is deeply loved by her grandchildren, great-grandchildren,
17 and great-great-grandchildren, and they are blessed by her kindness, gentle spirit, and
18 unwavering presence as they continue to honor her legacy of love; and
19 WHEREAS, Ms. Willis is a devoted Christian who has trusted in God through every
20 stage of her life and found strength, comfort, and peace in her faith; her life is a testimony
