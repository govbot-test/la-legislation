HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 200
BY REPRESENTATIVE YOUNG
A RESOLUTION
To commend Lincoln Preparatory School boys basketball head coach Antonio Hudson on
being named the Louisiana High School Athletic Association 2026 Class A Coach
of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting to recognize Coach Antonio Hudson for his leadership of the
Lincoln Preparatory School boys basketball team and to commend him on being named the
2026 Class A Coach of the Year; and
WHEREAS, Coach Hudson led the Lincoln Prep Panthers to an outstanding record
of thirty wins and only three losses, the District 1-1A title, and the 2026 Division IV Select
state championship; and
WHEREAS, under Coach Hudson's leadership, Lincoln Prep claimed its first state
basketball title since 1992, when the school was known as Grambling University Laboratory
School and led by the legendary Coach Michael Lyons; and
WHEREAS, Coach Hudson is a former Grambling Lab basketball standout who
averaged over thirty points and ten rebounds per game during his senior season; he also
holds the distinction as the school's first graduate to attend Louisiana State University on an
athletic scholarship; and
WHEREAS, Coach Hudson was also recognized as the Coach of the Year on the All-
Lincoln Parish Journal Team and the All-District 1-1A Team; and
WHEREAS, Lincoln Prep Coach Antonio Hudson is deserving of recognition for his
excellence in leadership and his coaching achievements.

HR NO. 200 ENROLLED
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
