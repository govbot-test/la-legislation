HLS 26RS-3666 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 200
BY REPRESENTATIVE YOUNG
COMMENDATIONS: Commends Lincoln Preparatory School boys basketball head coach
Antonio Hudson on being named the Louisiana High School Athletic Association
2026 Class A Coach of the Year
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Lincoln Preparatory School boys basketball head coach Antonio Hudson on
3 being named the Louisiana High School Athletic Association 2026 Class A Coach
4 of the Year.
5 WHEREAS, it is fitting to recognize Coach Antonio Hudson for his leadership of the
6 Lincoln Preparatory School boys basketball team and to commend him on being named the
7 2026 Class A Coach of the Year; and
8 WHEREAS, Coach Hudson led the Lincoln Prep Panthers to an outstanding record
9 of thirty wins and only three losses, the District 1-1A title, and the 2026 Division IV Select
10 state championship; and
11 WHEREAS, under Coach Hudson's leadership, Lincoln Prep claimed its first state
12 basketball title since 1992, when the school was known as Grambling University Laboratory
13 School and led by the legendary Coach Michael Lyons; and
14 WHEREAS, Coach Hudson is a former Grambling Lab basketball standout who
15 averaged over thirty points and ten rebounds per game during his senior season; he also
16 holds the distinction as the school's first graduate to attend Louisiana State University on an
17 athletic scholarship; and
18 WHEREAS, Coach Hudson was also recognized as the Coach of the Year on the All-
19 Lincoln Parish Journal Team and the All-District 1-1A Team; and
