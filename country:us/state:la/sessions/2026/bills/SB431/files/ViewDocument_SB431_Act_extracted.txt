2026 Regular Session ACT No. 768 ENROLLED
SENATE BILL NO. 431
BY SENATOR FOIL
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 32:1734(F) and to enact R.S. 32:1734(G) through (I), relative to
3 towing and storage company hours; to provide for requirements for towing and
4 storage companies; to provide for business hours relative to towing and storage of
5 vehicles; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 32:1734(F) is hereby amended and reenacted and R.S. 32:1734(G)
8 through (I) are hereby enacted to read as follows:
9 §1734. Gate fees; other fees; excessive charges; prohibitions; cause of action
10 * * *
11 F. For purposes of this Section, any towing or storage company
12 conducting nonconsensual towing operations from private property shall make
13 themselves available for the release of personal property or the vehicle, outside
14 normal business hours, exclusive of legal holidays and certain exempted hours.
15 G. For purposes of this Section, any towing or storage company
16 conducting nonconsensual towing operations from private property that is
17 unavailable to the public for the timely release of personal property or the
18 vehicle shall not charge the requester a storage fee the day of the facility closure
19 or unavailability, exclusive of legal holidays and certain exempted hours,
20 suspending future storage fees until the next day of normal business hours.
21 H. The office of state police may adopt rules and regulations in
22 accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act as necessary to implement
23 the provisions of this Section, including but not limited to establishing the
24 exempted hours.
