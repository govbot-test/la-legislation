HLS 26RS-1181 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE RISER
WILDLIFE/FEES: Authorizes recreational alligator hunting license and lottery fees
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 56:268 and 3002(A)(7), relative to recreational alligator hunting licenses; to
3 provide for recreational alligator harvest lottery; to provide for the fee and dedication
4 to the Conservation Fund; to provide for a resident recreational license; to provide
5 for effectiveness; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 56:268 and 3002(A)(7) are hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §268. Recreational alligator harvest lottery
9 A. The commission shall promulgate and adopt rules and regulations to
10 establish a lottery for the selection and issuance of alligator harvest tags. There shall
11 be a fifty-dollar nonrefundable entry fee for alligator harvest tag lottery applications.
12 The fee may be applied to the license fee for successful applicants. Alligator harvest
13 tags may be issued to successful and eligible lottery applicants at no cost.
14 B. All revenues derived from lottery applications and auction proceeds shall
15 be deposited into the Conservation Fund.

HB 767 Original 2026 Regular Session Riser
Abstract: Provides for an alligator lottery license and a resident recreation alligator license
and provides for fees.
Proposed law creates an alligator license lottery, establishes the fee, and provides for the
dedication of the fee to the Conservation Fund.
Present law provides for recreational hunting licenses for residents and annual fees.
Proposed law adds a resident recreational hunting license for alligators and establishes a $25
annual fee.
Eff. when Senate Bill No. ____ of the 2026 Regular Session becomes effective.
(Adds R.S. 56:268 and 3002(A)(7))
