HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 213
BY REPRESENTATIVE SAWYER
of First Presbyterian Church of Baton Rouge on the occasion of his designation as
pastor emeritus of the church
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend the Reverend Doctor Gerrit Scott Dawson for his spiritual leadership on the
3 occasion of his designation as pastor emeritus of First Presbyterian Church of Baton
4 Rouge.
5 WHEREAS, Pastor Gerrit Scott Dawson joined the First Presbyterian family as
6 senior pastor in 2004; and
7 WHEREAS, Pastor Dawson and his wife Rhonda have four children and seven
8 grandchildren; and
9 WHEREAS, Pastor Dawson earned an undergraduate degree at Vanderbilt
10 University, a master of divinity at Princeton Theological Seminary, and a doctorate of
11 ministry at the Reformed Theological Seminary in Charlotte, North Carolina; and
12 WHEREAS, Pastor Dawson is the author of eight books, including The Blessing Life
13 and Raising Adam: Why Jesus Descended into Hell; and
14 WHEREAS, Pastor Dawson's interests include J.R.R. Tolkien, movies from the
15 1940s, poet and minister George Herbert, the Miami Hurricanes, and Harry Potter; and
16 WHEREAS, Pastor Dawson has tirelessly provided scriptural preaching, counseling,
17 visitation and mentoring, and administrative guidance for the past twenty-two years; and
18 WHEREAS, Pastor Dawson guided First Presbyterian Church into its Evangelical
19 Presbyterian Church (EPC) denominational home; and
Page 1 of 2

1 WHEREAS, Pastor Dawson has provided rich and meaningful Lenten devotional
2 guides, national leadership through timely and challenging matters for the EPC, and writings
3 on spiritual formation that have guided and blessed the church across denominational lines;
4 and
5 WHEREAS, First Presbyterian Church has experienced significant spiritual and
6 numerical growth of all generations and has become increasingly generous and creative in
7 its local and global mission endeavors; and
8 WHEREAS, the congregation of First Presbyterian Church of Baton Rouge gratefully
9 bestowed the title of pastor emeritus on the Reverend Doctor Gerrit Scott Dawson on
10 April 26, 2026.
11 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
12 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend the Reverend Doctor Gerrit Scott Dawson
13 of First Presbyterian Church of Baton Rouge on the occasion of his designation as pastor
14 emeritus of the church and does hereby extend enduring appreciation for his spiritual
15 leadership throughout his career in ministry.
