SCAHB797 4893 4489
SENATE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
797 by Representative Crews
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 3, line 8, change "metals:" to "metals are all of the following:"
3 AMENDMENT NO. 2
4 On page 4, between lines 10 and 11, insert the following:
5 "(f) An attestation from the electronic payment platform that the information
6 it provides to the department is accurate."
7 AMENDMENT NO. 3
8 On page 4, line 23, change "under" to "pursuant to"
9 AMENDMENT NO. 4
10 On page 5, after line 1, add the following:
11 "I. The department shall not be liable for any action of an electronic payment
12 platform that is certified pursuant to this Chapter.
13 Section 2.(A) The provisions of Section 1 of this Act shall become effective
14 when an Act of the Louisiana Legislature containing a specific appropriation of
15 monies for the implementation of the provisions of this Act becomes effective.
16 (B) The provisions of this Section shall become effective upon signature by
17 the governor or, if not signed by the governor, upon expiration of the time for bills
18 to become law without signature by the governor, as provided by Article III, Section
19 18 of the Constitution of Louisiana. If vetoed by the governor and subsequently
20 approved by the legislature, the provisions of this Section shall become effective on
21 the day following such approval."
