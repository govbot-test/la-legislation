2026 Regular Session
SENATE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 4
BY SENATOR MIZELL
cultural and historic links between Ireland and Louisiana.
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 To commemorate the celebration of St. Patrick's Day and recognize the cultural and historic
3 links between Ireland and Louisiana and the establishment of the American Irish
4 State Legislators Caucus.
5 WHEREAS, on March 17, during the annual celebration of the Feast of Saint Patrick,
6 the Patron Saint of Ireland, Irish Americans join with men, women, and children of all ethnic
7 origins who, for one day, become Irish and celebrate Saint Patrick and love for Ireland; and
8 WHEREAS, on Saint Patrick's Day, people throughout the state of Louisiana and
9 around the world wear green and live for a day in the spirit of Saint Patrick, Saint Brigid, and
10 Saint Colmcille; and
11 WHEREAS, Irish immigrants in the United States helped form the cultural
12 foundation of the nation, and those of Irish lineage today proudly sing their support for
13 Ireland; and
14 WHEREAS, there has always been a strong cultural connection between Ireland and
15 Louisiana, especially in New Orleans where the first St. Patrick's Day celebration was held
16 in 1806; and
17 WHEREAS, between 1832 and 1838, an important new shipping channel, the New
18 Basin Canal in New Orleans, was built with a workforce consisting almost entirely of Irish
Page 1 of 2

1 immigrants, which led to the building of several cohesive Irish communities in the city such
2 as the Irish Channel Historic District and Irish Bayou; and
3 WHEREAS, the songs of Ireland are the tragic songs of love, joyous songs of battle,
4 nostalgic reveries of the sorrows, and the glories that are the Emerald Isle, and lamentations
5 of life's myriad travails, and the odes of joy and the life eternal; and
6 WHEREAS, with leadership in all 50 states, the American Irish State Legislators
7 Caucus was established with the aim of fostering and strengthening the longstanding
8 relationship that exists between the United States of America and Ireland to the mutual
9 benefit of both countries.
10 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of Louisiana does hereby
11 commemorate the celebration of St. Patrick's Day and recognizes the cultural and historic
12 links between Ireland and Louisiana and the establishment of the American Irish State
13 Legislators Caucus.
14 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
15 National Co-chairs of the American Irish State Legislators Caucus: Senator Beth Mizell of
16 Louisiana; Senator Mark Daly, Chair of the Senate of Ireland; Representative Brian Patrick
17 Kennedy of Rhode Island; Speaker Robin Vos of Wisconsin; Assemblywoman Carol
18 Murphy of New Jersey; Senator Shannon O'Brien of Montana; Representative Killian
19 Timoney of Kentucky; Representative Fran Hurley of Illinois; Representative James
20 Gallagher of California; Senator Ogden Driskill of Wyoming; Senator Wayne Harper of
21 Utah; Representative Bridget Walsh Moore of Missouri; Representative Maureen Terry of
22 Maine; Representative John Lawn of Massachusetts; Delegate Roger Hanshaw of West
23 Virginia; Representative Marcus Evan of Illinois; and Representative Mia Costello of
24 Alaska.
