2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 44
BY SENATOR CATHEY
Walsworth on the occasion of his seventieth birthday.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend and congratulate former Louisiana State Senator Michael A. Walsworth on the
3 occasion of his seventieth birthday.
4 WHEREAS, the Honorable Michael A. Walsworth rendered twenty-four years of
5 exemplary public service in the Legislature of Louisiana, faithfully representing the citizens
6 of Ouachita, Claiborne, Lincoln, Morehouse, Union, and West Carroll parishes and
7 contributing meaningfully to the advancement of the state; and
8 WHEREAS, he served with distinction in both chambers of the legislature,
9 representing District No. 15 in the Louisiana House of Representatives from 1996 until 2008
10 and District No. 33 in the Louisiana State Senate from 2008 until 2020, demonstrating
11 unwavering commitment, integrity, and diligence throughout his tenure; and
12 WHEREAS, a lifelong resident of northeast Louisiana, former Senator Walsworth
13 is a graduate of West Monroe High School and Northeast Louisiana University, where he
14 formed his roots and a steadfast advocacy for the people of the region; and
15 WHEREAS, a man of faith, he is known for his warm demeanor, even temperament,
16 and collegial spirit, bringing camaraderie and goodwill to the legislature; and
17 WHEREAS, on March 27, 2026, Michael A. Walsworth will celebrate his seventieth
18 birthday with his beloved family and continues to look toward the future.
