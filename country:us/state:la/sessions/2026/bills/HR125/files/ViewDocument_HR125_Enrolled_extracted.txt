2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 125
BY REPRESENTATIVE MUSCARELLO
A RESOLUTION
To commend the members of the 2026 Independence Sicilian Heritage Festival Royal Court
on the occasion of the festival's eighteenth anniversary.
WHEREAS, the Sicilian Heritage Festival celebrates the beautiful and rich Sicilian
history that is prominent in the Tangipahoa Parish town of Independence; and
WHEREAS, the Sicilian Heritage Festival is held annually in downtown
Independence; and
WHEREAS, this year's festival honors the 2026 royal court members: King XVII,
Guy Recotta; Queen XVII, Caidence Mckinney; Madre, Angie Domiano; and Grand
Marshal, Nick Gagliano; and
WHEREAS, features of the Sicilian Heritage Festival include street dancing, live
entertainment, a parade, a spaghetti cook-off, and a meatball toss competition; and
WHEREAS, the Sicilian Heritage Festival is a cherished local event that honors the
cultural influence of Louisiana's Sicilian communities and is most worthy of recognition.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend the 2026 Sicilian Heritage Festival in
Independence, Louisiana, and its royal court on the occasion of the festival's eighteenth
anniversary; does hereby recognize the festival's royal court for serving honorably as
representatives of the Sicilian community in Independence; and does hereby express great
pride in the town honoring its Sicilian heritage.
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
