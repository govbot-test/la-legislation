2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 50
BY SENATOR BOUDREAUX
A RESOLUTION
Capitol and to commend the physical therapists, physical therapist assistants, and
physical therapy students across Louisiana for their dedication to improving the
health and well-being of the citizens of this state.
WHEREAS, physical therapists and physical therapist assistants are highly educated,
licensed healthcare professionals who play a vital role in the prevention, diagnosis, and
treatment of movement dysfunction and pain; and
WHEREAS, physical therapy services help individuals of all ages recover from
injury, manage chronic conditions, improve mobility, and maintain independence, thereby
enhancing overall quality of life; and
WHEREAS, physical therapists are integral members of the healthcare profession,
working collaboratively with physicians and other providers to deliver patient-centered care
that may reduce the need for more invasive and costly treatments; and
WHEREAS, physical therapy contributes significantly to reducing healthcare costs
by preventing injuries, minimizing the need for surgery, decreasing reliance on prescription
medications, and facilitating faster recovery times; and
WHEREAS, the physical therapy profession is committed to evidence-based
practice, continuing education, and innovation to ensure the highest standard of care for
patients across Louisiana; and
WHEREAS, Physical Therapy Day at the Louisiana State Capitol provides an
opportunity to raise awareness among legislators and the public about the important role of
physical therapy in promoting health, wellness, and recovery; and
WHEREAS, this day also recognizes the dedication and service of physical
therapists, physical therapist assistants, and physical therapy students who work tirelessly
to improve the lives of others throughout the state.

SR NO. 50 ENROLLED
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
does hereby recognize Wednesday, April 1, 2026, as Physical Therapy Day at the Louisiana
State Capitol and does hereby commend the physical therapy professionals and students of
Louisiana for their outstanding contributions to the health and well-being of the people of
this state.
PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE
