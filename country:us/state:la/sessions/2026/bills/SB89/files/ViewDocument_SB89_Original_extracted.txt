DIGEST
SB 89 Original 2026 Regular Session Miller
Present law requires tax assessors in certain parishes to provide forms to property owners
for permanent registration of the homestead exemption.
Proposed law adds St. Charles Parish to those parishes in which the assessor is required to
provide such forms.
Effective upon signature of the governor or lapse of time for gubernatorial action.
(Amends R.S. 47:1703.1(A))
