HB 179 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Muscarello
Abstract: Provides relative to retention of court reporter notes and recordings with respect
to criminal and civil proceedings.
Present law provides for the retention and ownership of the notes, recordings, and other
records of civil and criminal court matters.
Proposed law retains present law and provides that the custody and control of the notes,
recordings, and other records of the court proceedings remains with the court when a court
reporter retires or leaves the employ of the court.
(Amends R.S. 15:511 and C.C.P. Art. 372(C) and (D))
