Section 1:
2026 Regular Session ACT No. 473 ENROLLED
BY SENATOR MIZELL
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 40:2009.10.2, relative to nursing facilities; to provide for the Centers for
3 Medicare and Medicaid Services Special Focus Facility Program; to provide for state
4 requirements for nursing facilities; to provide for the duties and responsibilities of
5 the Louisiana Department of Health; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 40:2009.10.2 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §2009.10.2. Special Focus Facility Program; state requirements
9 A. Any nursing facility licensed by the Louisiana Department of Health
10 that is selected for participation in the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid
11 Services Special Focus Facility Program shall be subject to enhanced state
12 oversight.
13 B. Any nursing facility identified in Subsection A of this Section shall do
14 all of the following:
15 (1) Provide notice to both prospective and current residents that the
16 facility is in the Special Focus Facility Program due to the scope and severity of
17 noncompliance with state and federal requirements.
18 (2) Provide current residents the opportunity to transfer to another
19 available facility if the resident can be transferred safely and there is an
20 alternative nursing facility with availability that meets the resident's
21 geographical, physical health, and mental health needs.
22 (3) Comply with all of the following state requirements in addition to any
23 requirements imposed by the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services:
24 (a) Attend an in-person meeting with the Louisiana Department of
25 Health within forty-five days of being selected to participate in the Special
26 Focus Facility Program to discuss increased state monitoring and other
27 state-imposed requirements.

1 (b) Enter into an agreement with the Louisiana Department of Health to
2 address the noncompliance of the nursing facility. At the discretion of the
3 department, the agreement may include any of the following requirements for
4 the nursing facility:
5 (i) Being subject to additional state monitoring, including additional
6 in-person unannounced inspections at the expense of the nursing facility.
7 (ii) Implementing directed plans of correction.
8 (iii) Implementing directed, in-service trainings.
9 (iv) Hiring consultants or subject matter experts, subject to approval by
10 the department, at the expense of the nursing facility.
11 (v) Being subject to a root cause analysis to determine the basis for the
12 noncompliance.
13 (vi) Engaging with a Quality Improvement Organization approved by
14 the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services.
15 (vii) Being subject to an owner or license holder analysis to identify a
16 pattern of poor performance.
17 (viii) Implementing staffing adjustments and increases as directed by the
18 department to address deficiencies.
19 (ix) Being subject to fines and penalties.
20 (x) Complying with alternate remedies or other provisions intended to
21 deter noncompliance, correct deficiencies, and address quality, health, safety,
22 and welfare issues, imposed by the department.
23 C. If a nursing facility is selected for the Special Focus Facility Program
24 and fails to meet the requirements for graduation from the program and the
25 additional requirements imposed by the department in accordance with
26 Subsection B of this Section within eighteen months, the Louisiana Department
27 of Health shall revoke the facility's license. However, nothing in this Section
28 shall prohibit the department from issuing a license revocation to a nursing
29 facility prior to the expiration of the eighteen-month period, if the nursing
30 facility violates the provisions of the settlement agreement and the violations or
