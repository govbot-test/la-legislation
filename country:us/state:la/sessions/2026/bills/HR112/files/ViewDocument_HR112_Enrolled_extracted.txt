2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 112
BY REPRESENTATIVE MURRAY
A RESOLUTION
To commend Father Anthony M. Bozeman, SSJ, on the occasion of the twenty-fifth
anniversary of his ordination as a priest in the Society of St. Joseph.
WHEREAS, Father Anthony M. Bozeman, SSJ, is a priest in the Society of
St. Joseph who has dedicated his life to the service of his community; and
WHEREAS, Father Bozeman has guided the people of New Orleans with divine
inspiration and an unwavering spirit for the majority of his career; and
WHEREAS, Father Bozeman celebrates the twenty-fifth anniversary of his
ordination on Sunday, April 12, 2026.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Father Anthony M. Bozeman, SSJ, on the
occasion of the twenty-fifth anniversary of his ordination as a priest in the Society of
St. Joseph; does hereby recognize his successful career as a spiritual community leader and
pastor; and does hereby extend sincere wishes that he remain prosperous in his endeavors
to serve the spiritual needs of his parishioners.
