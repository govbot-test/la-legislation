7 * * *
8 B.(1) A state maritime academy, the Universities of Louisiana Maritime

HLS 26RS-1008 ORIGINAL
HB NO. 905
Present law provides for the University of La. System, composted of the postsecondary
education institutions under the supervision and management of the system's board of
supervisors.
Present law establishes a state maritime academy under the supervision and management of
the board of supervisors for the following purposes:
(1) Coordinating existing maritime programs and facilities.
(2) Training merchant marine officers.
(3) Providing additional educational pathways aligned with the workforce needs of the
maritime industry.
Proposed law retains present law.
Proposed law changes name of academy from Universities of Louisiana Maritime Academy
to Louisiana Maritime Academy at Nicholls State University.
(Amends R.S. 17:3217(B)(1))
