WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize Doris Voitier for her dedication to public
education and to commend her for more than fifty years of service to St. Bernard Parish
public schools; and
WHEREAS, Doris is a proud graduate of Mount Carmel Academy; she attended the
University of New Orleans, where she earned a bachelor's degree in mathematics education
and a master's degree in curriculum and instruction; she completed additional postgraduate
course work in educational leadership; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Voitier began her career in St. Bernard Parish as a math teacher and
later moved into administration, where she served as an assistant principal, principal,
supervisor of secondary education, assistant superintendent, and superintendent; and
WHEREAS, Hurricane Katrina devastated St. Bernard Parish just one year after
Ms. Voitier began serving as superintendent; she played an instrumental role in assisting the
rapid reopening of schools, providing stability and hope for returning families, and helping
lead the recovery of the parish; she received national recognition for her leadership during
the recovery efforts and was presented with the John F. Kennedy Profile in Courage Award;
and
WHEREAS, under Ms. Voitier's leadership, the St. Bernard Parish School District
has consistently demonstrated academic growth and innovation and a strong commitment
to student success; and

WHEREAS, Ms. Voitier has served as a member and past president of the Louisiana
Board of Elementary and Secondary Education, where she helped shape education policy
for students across the state; and
WHEREAS, Doris Voitier's career reflects her belief that strong public schools are
the foundation of strong communities, and she is deserving of recognition for her
outstanding commitment to serving students, families, and educators of St. Bernard Parish.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Doris Voitier for more than fifty years of
service to St. Bernard Parish public schools and does hereby extend sincere wishes that she
remain prosperous in all of her endeavors.
