Section 1:
ENROLLED
ACT No. 877
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE SCHAMERHORN
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 39:330.1(A)(3), relative to the sale of surplus state-owned property; to provide
3 for preference of in-state auctioneers; to provide for an effective date; and to provide
4 for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 39:330.1(A)(3) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
7 §330.1. Sale of surplus property
8 A.(1)
9 * * *
10 (3) Where both in-state and out-of-state auctioneers are bidding, in-state
11 auctioneers shall be given preference, provided the services are equal in quality and
12 do not exceed cost by more than ten percent of services available from outside the
13 state. The provisions of this Paragraph shall apply to auctions conducted pursuant to
14 Paragraphs (1) and (2) of this Subsection.
15 * * *
