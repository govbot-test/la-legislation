BY SENATOR PRICE
MUNICIPAL EMPLOYEE RET. Repeals certain exceptions for retirement benefits of
retirees who return to part-time employment in a position covered by the system. (2/3-
CA10s(29)(F)) (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 11:1762(A) and to repeal Section 2 of Act 634 of the 2024
3 Regular Session of the Legislature, relative to retirees who return to part-time
4 employment in a position covered by the Municipal Employees' Retirement System;
5 to provide for retirement benefits under certain conditions; to provide for the
6 termination date of certain provisions; and to provide for related matters.
7 Notice of intention to introduce this Act has been published.
8 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
9 Section 1. R.S. 11:1762(A) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
10 §1762. Part-time reemployment of retirees
11 A.(1) Whenever a retiree receiving normal retirement benefits becomes
12 reemployed by an employer but the retiree does not meet the definition of an
13 employee and either his monthly earnings are equal to or less than the difference
14 between his monthly average final compensation and his monthly retirement benefit
15 or the retiree has more than thirty years of creditable service and is at least age sixty,
16 his retirement benefits shall continue without any reduction, his retirement benefits
17 shall continue, and the retiree shall not be a member of the system.

1 (2) Whenever a retiree who has thirty years or less of creditable service and
2 who is receiving normal retirement benefits becomes reemployed by an employer but
3 the retiree does not meet the definition of an employee and his monthly earnings
4 exceed the difference between his monthly average final compensation and his
5 monthly retirement benefit, his retirement benefits shall be reduced by the amount
6 that his monthly earnings exceed the difference between his monthly average final
7 compensation and his monthly retirement benefit for every month of such
8 employment, and the retiree shall not be a member of the system. The calculation of
9 monthly earnings shall not include income attributable to service as a part-time
10 elected official listed in R.S. 11:164.
11 * * *
12 Section 2. Section 2 of Act 634 of the 2024 Regular Session of the Legislature is
13 hereby repealed.
14 Section 3. A retiree serving as a part-time reemployed retiree under the provisions
15 of Act 634 of the 2024 Regular Session of the Legislature before July 1, 2026, may continue
16 to serve as a part-time reemployed retiree without any reduction in his retirement benefits
17 so long as his part-time service remains continuous.
The original instrument and the following digest, which constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument, were prepared by Senate Legislative Services.
The keyword, summary, and digest do not constitute part of the law or proof
or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
DIGEST
SB 18 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Price
Present law provides for exceptions allowing qualifying retirees of the Municipal
Employees' Retirement System of Louisiana (MERS), at least age 60 with 30 years of
creditable service, to return to employment without a benefit reduction. Proposed law repeals
these exceptions.
Proposed law allows a part-time retiree who was reemployed before July 1, 2026, to
continue reemployment without a benefit reduction as long as there was no break in service.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 11:1762(A); repeals Section 2 of Act 634 of the 2024 RS)
