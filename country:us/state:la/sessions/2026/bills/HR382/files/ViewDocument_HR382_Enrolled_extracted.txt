HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 382
BY REPRESENTATIVE LARVADAIN
A RESOLUTION
To commend Pastor Joshua Joy Dara, Sr., on the occasion of the celebration of his thirtieth
anniversary as pastor of Zion Hill Church.
WHEREAS, Dr. Joshua Joy Dara, Sr. was installed as pastor of Zion Hill Church on
June 2, 1996; he accepted the sacred responsibility of ministering with faith, humility,
wisdom, and unwavering commitment; and
WHEREAS, throughout thirty years of dedicated pastoral service, Pastor Dara has
faithfully proclaimed the Gospel of Jesus Christ and led with integrity and a steadfast
devotion to spiritual growth, discipleship, and kingdom building; and
WHEREAS, under Pastor Dara's visionary leadership and anointing, Zion Hill
Church has experienced remarkable growth and development, increasing its membership
from seventy-five to more than three thousand five hundred, which has made it the largest
African American church in Central Louisiana; and
WHEREAS, Pastor Dara's charismatic and unique style of preaching and teaching
fills the Pineville, Louisiana, sanctuary every Sunday and reaches thousands of followers
nationally and internationally on social media; he speaks every weekday morning on
KALB-News Channel 5 and spreads hope, joy, and love throughout the community and
beyond; and
WHEREAS, Pastor Dara has cultivated and nurtured spiritual leadership by guiding
and mentoring more than forty associate ministers and supporting the development of Sons
and Daughters of the House, which has created a legacy of leadership and ministry that
extends to future generations; and
WHEREAS, Pastor Dara's ministry has transcended the walls of the church and
reached into the broader community through his dedicated service and involvement with
numerous organizations, including the Chamber of Commerce, Rapides Foundation, Hope
House, the Manna House, the Food Bank of Cenla, Salvation Army, American Red Cross,
Rapides Symphony Orchestra, and the United Educational Missionary Baptist Association;
