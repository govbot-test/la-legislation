HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 382
BY REPRESENTATIVE LARVADAIN
COMMENDATIONS: Commends Pastor Joshua Joy Dara, Sr., on the occasion of the
celebration of his thirtieth anniversary as pastor of Zion Hill Church
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Pastor Joshua Joy Dara, Sr., on the occasion of the celebration of his thirtieth
3 anniversary as pastor of Zion Hill Church.
4 WHEREAS, Dr. Joshua Joy Dara, Sr. was installed as pastor of Zion Hill Church on
5 June 2, 1996; he accepted the sacred responsibility of ministering with faith, humility,
6 wisdom, and unwavering commitment; and
7 WHEREAS, throughout thirty years of dedicated pastoral service, Pastor Dara has
8 faithfully proclaimed the Gospel of Jesus Christ and led with integrity and a steadfast
9 devotion to spiritual growth, discipleship, and kingdom building; and
10 WHEREAS, under Pastor Dara's visionary leadership and anointing, Zion Hill
11 Church has experienced remarkable growth and development, increasing its membership
12 from seventy-five to more than three thousand five hundred, which has made it the largest
13 African American church in Central Louisiana; and
14 WHEREAS, Pastor Dara's charismatic and unique style of preaching and teaching
15 fills the Pineville, Louisiana, sanctuary every Sunday and reaches thousands of followers
16 nationally and internationally on social media; he speaks every weekday morning on
17 KALB-News Channel 5 and spreads hope, joy, and love throughout the community and
18 beyond; and
19 WHEREAS, Pastor Dara has cultivated and nurtured spiritual leadership by guiding
20 and mentoring more than forty associate ministers and supporting the development of Sons

1 and Daughters of the House, which has created a legacy of leadership and ministry that
2 extends to future generations; and
3 WHEREAS, Pastor Dara's ministry has transcended the walls of the church and
4 reached into the broader community through his dedicated service and involvement with
5 numerous organizations, including the Chamber of Commerce, Rapides Foundation, Hope
6 House, the Manna House, the Food Bank of Cenla, Salvation Army, American Red Cross,
7 Rapides Symphony Orchestra, and the United Educational Missionary Baptist Association;
8 he serves as a commissioner on the England Authority, where he provides leadership
9 promoting economic development; and
10 WHEREAS, Pastor Dara has previously served the community through his
11 engagement with various organizations, including Turning Point Shelter, Shepherd Center,
12 and Cenla Interfaith, which exemplifies his commitment to service and meeting the needs
13 of humanity; and
14 WHEREAS, Pastor Joshua Joy Dara, Sr., has served as a pastor, mentor, counselor,
15 teacher, leader, and servant whose ministry has strengthened families, empowered
16 individuals, and transformed countless lives.
17 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
18 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Pastor Joshua Joy Dara, Sr., for thirty years
19 of faithful service as pastor of Zion Hill Church; does hereby express gratitude for his
20 steadfast leadership, tireless dedication, and enduring commitment to the church and the
21 community; and does hereby offer sincere wishes that he continue to prosper as he fulfills
22 his call to ministry.
