SLS 26RS-852 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 38
BY SENATOR PRICE
Louisiana's Eleventh Annual Caregiver Day at the Louisiana State Capitol.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate Wednesday, April 15, 2026, as AARP Louisiana's Eleventh Annual Caregiver
3 Day at the Louisiana State Capitol.
4 WHEREAS, Louisiana ranks forty-fifth in the nation on the Long Term Services and
5 Supports State Scorecard which evaluates how states perform in long-term care services for
6 older adults and individuals with disabilities; and
7 WHEREAS, an estimated nine hundred seventy-three thousand family caregivers in
8 Louisiana provide care to adult relatives or friends, with the economic value of this care
9 estimated to be roughly six billion nine hundred million dollars annually; and
10 WHEREAS, eleven thousand eight hundred and twenty individuals are on waiting
11 lists to receive assistance with activities of daily living in their homes and to live in the
12 setting of their choice; and
13 WHEREAS, almost ninety percent of older adults in Louisiana who need assistance
14 with activities of daily living wish to remain in their homes and communities with the
15 support of friends and family as they age; and
16 WHEREAS, almost three-fourths of older people living in a community who receive
17 personal assistance rely exclusively on unpaid family caregivers for help.
18 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
