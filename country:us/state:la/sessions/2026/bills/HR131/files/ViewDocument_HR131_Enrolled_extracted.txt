2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 131
BY REPRESENTATIVES ILLG AND STAGNI
A RESOLUTION
To commend the John Curtis Christian School girls basketball team on winning the
Louisiana High School Athletic Association 2026 Division I Select state
championship.
WHEREAS, the John Curtis Christian School Lady Patriots basketball team captured
the 2026 Division I Select state title by defeating St. Joseph's Academy, 82-38, in the
championship match in Hammond, Louisiana; the victory gave the girls basketball program
its eleventh state championship; and
WHEREAS, the Lady Patriots were the top-ranked team throughout the season and
finished with an incredible record of twenty-one wins and only one loss; and
WHEREAS, the Lady Patriots won the District 10-5A title after going undefeated
in five district games; and
WHEREAS, the Lady Patriots are led by Head Coach Alendra Brown, and Sadie
Branch, Jane Wright, and Jerkaila Jordan serve as assistant coaches; and
WHEREAS, the members of the 2025-2026 John Curtis Lady Patriots basketball
team are Janiyah Williams, Jayla Albert, Payton Jones, Malie Moore, Katie Kelly, Ke'Sonja
Nelson, Elaine Fagin, Kaliyah Mathews-Francis, Natalia Klein, Mia Duncan, Bailey
Timmons, Elaysia Noel, Jamari Anderson, Raven Bolds, and Jamya Albert; the team
managers are Ahmari Joesph and Sidra Mitchell; and
WHEREAS, the John Curtis Christian School girls basketball team is most deserving
of the highest recognition for its outstanding achievements this season.

HR NO. 131 ENROLLED
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
