HLS 26RS-3528 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 131
BY REPRESENTATIVES ILLG AND STAGNI
COMMENDATIONS: Commends the John Curtis Christian School girls basketball team
on winning the Louisiana High School Athletic Association 2026 Division I Select
state championship
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend the John Curtis Christian School girls basketball team on winning the
3 Louisiana High School Athletic Association 2026 Division I Select state
4 championship.
5 WHEREAS, the John Curtis Christian School Lady Patriots basketball team captured
6 the 2026 Division I Select state title by defeating St. Joseph's Academy, 82-38, in the
7 championship match in Hammond, Louisiana; the victory gave the girls basketball program
8 its eleventh state championship; and
9 WHEREAS, the Lady Patriots were the top-ranked team throughout the season and
10 finished with an incredible record of twenty-one wins and only one loss; and
11 WHEREAS, the Lady Patriots won the District 10-5A title after going undefeated
12 in five district games; and
13 WHEREAS, the Lady Patriots are led by Head Coach Alendra Brown, and Sadie
14 Branch, Jane Wright, and Jerkaila Jordan serve as assistant coaches; and
15 WHEREAS, the members of the 2025-2026 John Curtis Lady Patriots basketball
16 team are Janiyah Williams, Jayla Albert, Payton Jones, Malie Moore, Katie Kelly, Ke'Sonja
17 Nelson, Elaine Fagin, Kaliyah Mathews-Francis, Natalia Klein, Mia Duncan, Bailey
18 Timmons, Elaysia Noel, Jamari Anderson, Raven Bolds, and Jamya Albert; the team
19 managers are Ahmari Joesph and Sidra Mitchell; and
