HB 520 Original 2026 Regular Session Wilford Carter
Abstract: Requires clerks of court to provide officers of the court with information
regarding criminal cases including but not limited to violent offenses and sexual
offenses.
Present law requires each district clerk of court responsible for maintaining criminal records
to provide the public electronic access to all minute entries or summaries involving all
matters in criminal court for any case filed on or after Jan. 1, 2020, by transmission to the
online portal maintained by the La. Clerks' Remote Access Authority pursuant to present law
(R.S. 13:754).
Present law provides that no other records or images, other than the minute entries or
summaries, are required to be transmitted in accordance with present law.
Present law does not apply to traffic violations.
Proposed law retains present law and requires each district clerk of court to provide officers
of the court with electronic access to all minute entries or summaries involving all matters
in criminal court including but not limited to violent offenses and sexual offenses for any
case filed on or after Jan. 1, 2027, by transmission to the online portal maintained by the La.
Clerks' Remote Access Authority.
Proposed law provides that no other records or images, other than the minute entries or
summaries, are required to be transmitted in accordance with proposed law.
(Amends R.S. 13:5992(B)(1))
