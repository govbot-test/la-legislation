HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 355
BY REPRESENTATIVE LYONS
A RESOLUTION
To commend Dr. Boise Kimber, president of the National Baptist Convention, USA, Inc.
WHEREAS, Dr. Boise Kimber was elected to serve as the nineteenth president of
the National Baptist Convention, USA, Inc., on September 5, 2024; he is the distinguished
pastor of the First Calvary Baptist Church in New Haven, Connecticut, and New Hope
Baptist Church in Bridgeport, Connecticut; and
WHEREAS, President Kimber is married to Reverend Shevelle T. Kimber and is a
proud father and grandfather; he is a native of Phenix City, Alabama, and the son of the late
Milburn and Ruthie Kimber; and
WHEREAS, President Kimber holds a bachelor of arts in sociology from Johnson
C. Smith University in Charlotte, North Carolina; a master of religious education in religious
life and education from Hartford Seminary; a doctor of ministry from United Theological
Seminary in Dayton, Ohio; and a master of sacred theology from Yale University Divinity
School; and
WHEREAS, President Kimber is a prominent national faith leader, strategist,
community leader, and advocate for social justice and economic empowerment; he
seamlessly blends faith and activism and addresses the most pressing issues in society with
conviction and purpose; and
WHEREAS, President Kimber is widely respected for his ability to articulate
complex social issues with clarity, which has earned him recognition in the religious and
civic spheres; he embodies the power of faith-driven activism, and his lifelong commitment
to justice, equality, and empowerment serves as an inspiration to all; and
WHEREAS, President Kimber focuses his ministry on uplifting marginalized
communities, and he has devoted his life to guiding his congregation through spiritual,
social, and economic challenges; outside of his ministry, he extends his influence to create
pathways for justice, equality, and opportunity across communities; and

WHEREAS, President Kimber is a prominent civil rights advocate who has
spearheaded initiatives that tackle racial inequality, poverty, and educational disparities; he
emphasizes unity and collaboration and unites religious, civic, and governmental
organizations to address systemic challenges and create lasting change; and
WHEREAS, President Kimber mentors and passes on his knowledge and
commitment to social justice to the next generation of young clergy and emerging leaders;
his commitment to education and leadership development has left an indelible mark on the
faith community and ensured that his legacy of service will endure; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Boise Kimber has transformed countless lives and equipped
individuals to overcome adversity and reach their potential; he is deserving of recognition
for his outstanding work as president of the National Baptist Convention, USA, Inc.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Dr. Boise Kimber, president of the National
Baptist Convention, USA, Inc., and does hereby express enduring appreciation for his
lifelong dedication to ministry, social justice, and community service.
