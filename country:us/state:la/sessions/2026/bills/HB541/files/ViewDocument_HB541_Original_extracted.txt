HB 541 Original 2026 Regular Session LaCombe
Abstract: Provides for a permit and cost of permit for a microdistiller processing a certain
number of gallons per year.
Present law provides for the issuance of a Class A permit at a cost of $1,000 to a distiller
who distills, makes, blends, rectifies or processes alcoholic beverages of no more than
12,000 gallons per year.
Proposed law retains present law and provides for a new permit for the issuance of a Class
A permit at a cost of $100 to a distiller who distills, makes, blends, rectifies, or processes
alcoholic beverages of no more than 1,000 gallons per year.
(Amends R.S. 26:71(A)(3)(d))
