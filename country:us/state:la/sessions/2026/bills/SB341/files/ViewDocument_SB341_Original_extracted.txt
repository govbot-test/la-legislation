SLS 26RS-622 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY SENATOR EDMONDS
SELF INSURANCE. Provides for the Louisiana Churches and Nonprofit Religious
Organizations Self-Insured Fund. (gov sig)
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 12:1853 and 1854(3) and (7), relative to the Louisiana Churches
3 and Nonprofit Religious Organizations Self-Insured Fund; to provide for definitions;
4 to provide for initial financial requirements; to provide for technical changes by the
5 Louisiana State Law Institute; to provide for an effective date; and to provide for
6 related matters.
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
8 Section 1. R.S. 12:1853 and 1854(3) and (7) are hereby amended and reenacted to
9 read as follows:
10 §1853. Authorization; requirements; regulation
11 A. Two or more churches or nonprofit religious organizations or one or more
12 religious denominations may agree to pool their liabilities for the purposes of
13 providing property commercial coverage for their buildings and other properties,
14 so long as they have a positive net worth, are financially solvent, and are capable of
15 assuming the obligations set forth in this Chapter.
16 B. Pools may also be created for commercial coverages that are
17 analogous to commercial property insurance in the private market as approved

2 B.C. The department shall promulgate necessary rules in accordance with the

5 §1854. Definitions
6 Wherever used in this Chapter, unless a different meaning clearly appears in
7 the context, the following terms, whether used in the singular or plural, shall have
8 the following meanings:
9 * * *
10 (3) "Fund" or "self-insurance fund" means the self-insurance fund a pool
11 of monies established pursuant to this Chapter from contributions of its members
12 to provide commercial property and other types of insurance for churches and
13 nonprofit religious organizations and shall be known as the Louisiana Churches and
14 Nonprofit Religious Organizations Self-Insured Fund.
15 * * *
16 (7) "Property Commercial coverage" means coverage for the damage or loss
17 of a member's immovable structures or building and may also include any or all of
18 the following:
19 (a) Premises liability coverage.
20 (b) Contents coverage for furniture or equipment.
21 (c) Wind and hail coverage.
22 (d) Loss of use coverage.
23 (e) Medical payments coverage.
24 * * *
25 Section 2. The Louisiana State Law Institute, pursuant to its statutory authority, is
26 hereby directed to change "property coverage" to "commercial coverage" in Chapter 28 of
27 Part IV of Title 12.
28 Section 3. This Act shall become effective upon signature by the governor or, if not
29 signed by the governor, upon expiration of the time for bills to become law without signature

DIGEST
SB 341 Original 2026 Regular Session Edmonds
Present law allows churches and nonprofit religious organizations to form self-insurance
pools for property insurance. Proposed law changes this to allow self-insurance pools for
commercial coverages.
Proposed law provides a definition for "commercial coverage".
Proposed law allows churches and nonprofit religious organizations to also form self-
insurance pools for other types of risk with approval of the commissioner of insurance.
Effective upon signature of the governor or lapse of time for gubernatorial action.
(Amends R.S. 12:1853 and 1854(3) and (7))
