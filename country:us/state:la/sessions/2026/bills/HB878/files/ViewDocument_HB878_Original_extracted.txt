1 AN ACT

3 of carbon dioxide beneath a scenic river system; and to provide for related matters.

5 Section 1. R.S. 30:1104(F) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
6 §1104. Duties and powers of the secretary; rules and regulations; permits
7 * * *
8 F. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, the secretary
9 shall not permit the geologic storage of carbon dioxide beneath a natural and scenic

HB 878 Original 2026 Regular Session Robby Carter
Abstract: Prohibits the geologic storage of carbon dioxide beneath a scenic river system.
Present law provides for the duties and powers of the secretary with regard to carbon dioxide
pipelines and storage facilities.
Proposed law prohibits the secretary from permitting the operation of a carbon dioxide storage
facility beneath a natural and scenic river system as designated by present law (R.S. 56:1840
et al.).
(Adds R.S. 30:1104(F))
