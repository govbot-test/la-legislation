1 The register of the State Land Office, with the concurrence and approval of
2 the governor, is hereby authorized and empowered to lease on behalf of the state any
3 portion or part of any meandered state-owned lake, bay, cove or the bed and bottom
4 thereof or state-owned lands reclaimed therefrom to any governing authority of a
5 parish or municipality or any combination thereof, the Louisiana Wildlife and
6 Fisheries Commission, the State Parks and Recreation Commission, and the
7 Louisiana State Department of Highways for use primarily for public recreational
8 purposes; provided, however, that said leases may only be entered into with the
9 aforementioned municipal and parish governing authorities or any combination
10 thereof, when the geographical territory encompassed by said governing authorities
11 is directly adjacent to the lake, bay, cove or any part thereof or the bed and bottom
12 thereof or lands reclaimed therefrom. In the case of leases to the State Parks and
13 Recreation Commission, the State Wildlife and Fisheries Commission, and/or the
14 State Department of Highways, said leases may be granted on a statewide basis
15 subject to all conditions and requirements as provided for under the provisions of this
16 Chapter. Subject to the approval of the register of the State Land Office, and with
17 the concurrence of the attorney general, the political body who has been granted a
18 lease under the provisions of this Chapter may place improvements in and on the
19 leased property, and after obtaining approval of the register may assign or sublease
20 such lease; provided, however, that any political body subleasing or assigning its
21 lease as herein authorized shall supervise its sublessee or assign to insure that the
22 terms and conditions of the original lease are observed, and provided, further, such
23 assignment or sublease shall be only to the highest responsible bidder after
24 advertisement in accordance with R.S. 41:1213 through 1215.1.
25 Any lease made by the register of the State Land Office pursuant to the
26 provisions of this chapter shall be on behalf of the state of Louisiana except that, as
27 to any lake, bay, cove and the bed and bottom thereof and land reclaimed therefrom,
28 or part thereof, not under the jurisdiction and control of the state of Louisiana, the
29 lease shall be made on behalf of the political body or entity having such jurisdiction
30 and control and only at its request.
