HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 189
BY REPRESENTATIVE WALTERS
A RESOLUTION
To commend Kathy Taylor on the occasion of her retirement from Windsor Village
Houston.
WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize Kathy Taylor for her musical
achievements and community activism and to commend her on the occasion of her
retirement from Windsor Village Houston; and
WHEREAS, Kathy Taylor is an accomplished national recording artist, minister of
music, songwriter, producer, and community activist; she has received critical acclaim for
her soul-stirring and inspirational performances; and
WHEREAS, Kathy began her music career as a soloist with James Cleveland's
Gospel Music of America and The Mass Choir; she was a featured vocalist on songs such
as "Anticipation", "It's Your Decision", "He Did It", and "I Got My Joy Back", and her
captivating live performances catapulted her into the national spotlight; and
WHEREAS, Kathy's musical talents have afforded her opportunities to perform
before national and world leaders, including Queen Elizabeth, President Bill Clinton, Senator
Hillary Clinton, Congresswoman Sheila Jackson Lee, Reverend Jesse Jackson, Cicely Tyson,
and Maya Angelou; and
WHEREAS, Kathy founded Kathy Taylor and Favor, which is a multi-talented
singing ensemble that performs meticulously-crafted and breathtaking harmonies; the group
has performed with acclaimed contemporary gospel artists and released three albums; the
most recent album was produced by GRAMMY Award-winning gospel music producer,
Sanchez Harley, and received a Stellar Award nomination; and
WHEREAS, Kathy Taylor is an inspiring minister of music and is most deserving
of recognition for her outstanding achievements.
