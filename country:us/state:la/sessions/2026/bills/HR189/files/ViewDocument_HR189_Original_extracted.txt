HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 189
BY REPRESENTATIVE WALTERS
Windsor Village Houston
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Kathy Taylor on the occasion of her retirement from Windsor Village
3 Houston.
4 WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize Kathy Taylor for her musical
5 achievements and community activism and to commend her on the occasion of her
6 retirement from Windsor Village Houston; and
7 WHEREAS, Kathy Taylor is an accomplished national recording artist, minister of
8 music, songwriter, producer, and community activist; she has received critical acclaim for
9 her soul-stirring and inspirational performances; and
10 WHEREAS, Kathy began her music career as a soloist with James Cleveland's
11 Gospel Music of America and The Mass Choir; she was a featured vocalist on songs such
12 as "Anticipation", "It's Your Decision", "He Did It", and "I Got My Joy Back", and her
13 captivating live performances catapulted her into the national spotlight; and
14 WHEREAS, Kathy's musical talents have afforded her opportunities to perform
15 before national and world leaders, including Queen Elizabeth, President Bill Clinton, Senator
16 Hillary Clinton, Congresswoman Sheila Jackson Lee, Reverend Jesse Jackson, Cicely Tyson,
17 and Maya Angelou; and
18 WHEREAS, Kathy founded Kathy Taylor and Favor, which is a multi-talented
19 singing ensemble that performs meticulously-crafted and breathtaking harmonies; the group
20 has performed with acclaimed contemporary gospel artists and released three albums; the
Page 1 of 2
HLS 26RS-2947 ORIGINAL

1 most recent album was produced by GRAMMY Award-winning gospel music producer,
2 Sanchez Harley, and received a Stellar Award nomination; and
3 WHEREAS, Kathy Taylor is an inspiring minister of music and is most deserving
4 of recognition for her outstanding achievements.
5 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
6 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Kathy Taylor on the occasion of her
7 retirement from Windsor Village Houston and does hereby extend sincere wishes that she
8 remain prosperous as she begins a new phase in her life.
