HLS 26RS-1199 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE BILL NO. 696
BY REPRESENTATIVE FARNUM
ELECTION CODE: Provides relative to early voting
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 18:1309(O), relative to early voting; to provide relative to early voting
3 procedures; to provide for the duties of registrars of voters related to early voting;
4 to authorize the secretary of state to establish policies and procedures to provide for
5 the implementation of procedures related to early voting; and to provide for related
6 matters.
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
8 Section 1. R.S. 18:1309(O) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
9 §1309. Early voting; verification
10 * * *
11 O. The secretary may establish policies and procedures to provide for the
12 implementation of this Section.
