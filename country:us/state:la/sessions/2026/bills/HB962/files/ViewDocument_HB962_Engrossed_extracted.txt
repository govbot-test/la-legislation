HLS 26RS-1408 ENGROSSED
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE MILLER
NURSES: Provides relative to reconstitution of medications for intravenous therapy
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 37:930.1, relative to the administration of medication by a nurse; to provide
3 for administering reconstituted medication; to provide for the authority to administer
4 certain medications; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 37:930.1 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
7 §930.1. Administering reconstituted medication
8 A registered nurse may execute preparation of medication, mixing,
9 reconstitution, or similar acts for immediate administration when carrying out the
10 orders of a healthcare professional authorized to prescribe medication in this state.
DIGEST
The digest printed below was prepared by House Legislative Services. It constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument. The keyword, one-liner, abstract, and digest do not constitute
part of the law or proof or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
HB 962 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Miller
Abstract: Provides that a registered nurse may administer a reconstituted medication when
directed by a licensed healthcare professional with prescribing authority.
Present law establishes guidelines and procedures relative to licensing and governing the
nursing profession.
Proposed law provides that a registered nurse may administer a reconstituted medication
when directed by a licensed healthcare professional with prescribing authority.
(Adds R.S. 37:930.1)
