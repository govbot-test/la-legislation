Section 1:
2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
BY SENATOR OWEN
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 4:715(A)(7) and 719(B) and to repeal R.S. 4:727, relative to the
3 Charitable Raffles, Bingo and Keno Licensing Law; to provide for premises rental
4 agreements; to provide for terms; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 4:715(A)(7) and 719(B) are hereby amended and reenacted to read
7 as follows:
8 §715. Personnel to hold games; commissions or salaries; equipment and supplies;
9 expenses
10 A. * * *
11 (7) No lease or other agreement providing for a rental arrangement for
12 premises or equipment shall provide for payment in excess of the reasonable market
13 rental rate as mutually agreed to in writing for such the premises or equipment and
14 in no case shall any payment be based on a percentage of gross receipts or profits
15 derived from a game of chance.
16 * * *
17 §719. Commercial lessor license
18 * * *
19 B. No lease or other agreement of any premises by a commercial lessor to
20 any charitable organization for any charitable gaming session shall provide for
21 payment in excess of the reasonable market rental rate as mutually agreed to in
22 writing for such the premises as provided in the local licensing ordinance. No lease
