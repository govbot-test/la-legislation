DIGEST
SB 487 Original 2026 Regular Session Owen
Present law prohibits a lease providing for a rental arrangement for charitable gaming
premises or equipment from payment in excess of the reasonable market rental rate for the
premises or equipment and Present law prohibits any payment from being based on a
percentage of gross receipts or profits derived from a game of chance.
Proposed law requires the lease providing for a rental arrangement for charitable gaming
premises or equipment be mutually agreed to by the parties and that it be in writing.
Present law prohibits a lease of any premises by a commercial lessor to any charitable
organization for any charitable gaming session that provides for payment in excess of the
reasonable market rental rate for the premises as provided in the local licensing ordinance.
Present law prohibits a lease from providing a rental for less than a two-hour session.
Proposed law retains present law, but requires the lease to be mutually agreed to by the
parties and that it be in writing.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 4:715(A)(7) and 719(B))
