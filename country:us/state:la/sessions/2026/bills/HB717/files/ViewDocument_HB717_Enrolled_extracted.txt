ENROLLED
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE BILL NO. 717
BY REPRESENTATIVE SCHAMERHORN
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 3:4744(Section heading) and (A), relative to the labeling of cell
3 cultured food products; to require cell cultured food products contain a disclosure
4 identifying the product in a clear and conspicuous manner; and to provide for related
5 matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 3:4744(Section heading) and (A) are hereby amended and reenacted
8 to read as follows:
9 §4744. Applicability; prohibited activities; labeling
10 A.(1) The provisions of this Part shall apply only to a person that places a
11 label on a food product as defined in R.S. 3:4743.
12 (2) Any cell cultured food product shall bear a clear and conspicuous
13 disclosure identifying the product in a bold typed print of not less than thirty-point
14 font.
