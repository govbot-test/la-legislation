Section 1:
HLS 26RS-945 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE SCHAMERHORN
AGRICULTURAL COMMODITIES: Provides relative to labeling requirements for cell
cultured food products
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 3:4744(A), relative to the labeling of cell cultured food products;
3 to require cell cultured food products contain a disclosure identifying the product in
4 a clear and conspicuous manner; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 3:4744(A) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
7 §4744. Applicability; prohibited activities; labeling
8 A.(1) The provisions of this Part shall apply only to a person that places a
9 label on a food product as defined in R.S. 3:4743.
10 (2) Any cell cultured food product shall bear a clear and conspicuous
11 disclosure identifying the product in a bold typed print of not less than thirty-point
12 font.

HLS 26RS-945 ORIGINAL
HB NO. 717
Present law prohibits any person from intentionally misbranding or misrepresenting certain
food products as an agricultural product through various activities, including intentionally
misbranding or representing a cell cultured food product as a meat product.
Proposed law retains present law and further requires that any cell cultured food product bear
a clear and conspicuous disclosure identifying the product in a bold typed print of not less
than 30-point font.
(Amends R.S. 3:4744(A))
