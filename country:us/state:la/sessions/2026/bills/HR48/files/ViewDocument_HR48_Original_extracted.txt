HLS 26RS-2133 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 48
BY REPRESENTATIVE BUTLER
SPECIAL DAY/WEEK/MONTH: Designates the week of March 23-27, 2026, as Louisiana
Agriculture Week at the state capitol and in the state of Louisiana and commends the
farmers, ranchers, and agribusiness leaders who participate in this initiative
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate the week of March 23-27, 2026, as Louisiana Agriculture Week at the state
3 capitol and in the state of Louisiana and to commend the farmers, ranchers, and
4 agribusiness leaders who participate in this vital educational initiative.
5 WHEREAS, agriculture is the backbone of Louisiana's economy, contributing
6 billions of dollars annually and providing the essential food, fiber, and fuel that sustains our
7 citizens and drives our state's prosperity; and
8 WHEREAS, Louisiana Agriculture Week is dedicated to providing an opportunity
9 for citizens across the state to honor the essential role agriculture plays in providing food,
10 maintaining a strong economy, and driving innovation for a sustainable future and fostering
11 a deeper connection between consumers and the sources of their food, fuel, and fiber; and
12 WHEREAS, this week serves as a critical bridge between the field and the capitol,
13 allowing lawmakers and the public to gain firsthand insight into the daily operations,
14 technological advancements, and economic challenges faced by our agricultural community;
15 and
16 WHEREAS, this week provides a unique opportunity for legislators to walk the land,
17 visit with producers, and understand the direct impact of legislative decisions on the
18 sustainability of family farms and rural life; and

1 WHEREAS, the House Committee on Agriculture, Forestry, Aquaculture, and Rural
2 Development continues to emphasize the importance of "boots-on-the-ground" advocacy and
3 the strengthening of personal relationships between farmers and their elected officials; and
4 WHEREAS, fostering a deeper understanding of the labor and dedication required
5 to maintain Louisiana's diverse agricultural landscape, from timber and sugarcane to rice,
6 poultry, and crawfish, is essential for the development of sound public policy.
7 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
8 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby designate the week of March 23-27, 2026, as Louisiana
9 Agriculture Week at the state capitol and in the state of Louisiana and does hereby commend
10 the farmers, ranchers, and agribusiness leaders of this state for their participation in this
11 initiative and for their invaluable contributions to Louisiana's economy, food security, and
12 cultural heritage.
13 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the Legislature
14 of Louisiana does hereby encourage all citizens and lawmakers to participate in activities
15 that celebrate and support the hardworking men and women of Louisiana agriculture.
16 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
17 commissioner of agriculture and the president of the Louisiana Farm Bureau Federation.
