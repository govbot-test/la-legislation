Amendments proposed by House Committee on Health and Welfare to Original House Bill
No. 930 by Representative Coates
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1

3 AMENDMENT NO. 2
4 On page 2, line 7, delete "gross"
5 AMENDMENT NO. 3
6 On page 2, delete lines 11 through 15 in their entirety and insert the following:
7 "(1) Manufacture any of the following products:
8 (a) A cosmetic product that regularly comes into contact with the mucus
9 membrane of the eye under conditions that are customary or usual.
10 (b) Cosmetic products that are injected.
11 (d) Cosmetic products that are intended for internal use.
12 (e) Cosmetic products that are intended to alter appearance for more than
13 twenty-four hours under conditions that are customary or usual and removal by the
14 consumer is not part of such conditions of use that are customary and usual."
15 AMENDMENT NO. 4
16 On page 2, between lines 22 and 23, insert the following:
17 "E. Facilities that sell cosmetic products directly to consumers shall be
18 regulated in accordance with the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act, including
19 the Modernization of Cosmetic Regulation Act of 2022, and are not subject to the
20 facility permitting and inspection requirements of this Chapter. Nothing in this
21 Section shall be construed to exempt a person from applicable state and local sales
22 and use tax laws, including registration requirements."
