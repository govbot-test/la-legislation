20 AMENDMENT NO. 10
21 On page 2, delete lines 11 through 15 in their entirety and insert the following:
22 "(1) Manufacture any of the following products:
23 (a) A cosmetic product that regularly comes into contact with the mucus
24 membrane of the eye under conditions that are customary or usual.
25 (b) Cosmetic products that are injected.
26 (c) Cosmetic products that are intended for internal use.
27 (d) Cosmetic products that are intended to alter appearance for more than
28 twenty-four hours under conditions that are customary or usual and removal by the
29 consumer is not part of such conditions of use that are customary and usual."
