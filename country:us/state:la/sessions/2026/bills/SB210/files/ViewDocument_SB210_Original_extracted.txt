ELECTIONS DEPARTMENT. Provides for the increase in the number of election
commissioners for closed party primaries. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 18:425(A)(1), relative to election commissioners; to provide for
3 election commissioners at precincts during an election; to provide party primary
4 elections; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 18:425(A)(1) is hereby amended to read as follows:
7 §425. Commissioners
8 A. Number. (1) In addition to the commissioner-in-charge, at the following
9 elections there shall be the following number of additional commissioners at each
10 precinct:
11 (a) For the gubernatorial primary and general elections, the congressional
12 primary and general elections, the presidential preference primary election, primary
13 and general elections for party primary offices, fall general elections held on the
14 sixth Saturday after the first Tuesday after the first Monday in November of a
15 congressional election year, and the primary and general elections for municipal
16 officers in a parish containing a municipality with a population of four hundred
17 seventy-five thousand or more held every four years beginning in 1994:

DIGEST
SB 210 Original 2026 Regular Session Kleinpeter
Present law provides elections that shall have additional commissioners at each precinct.
Proposed law provides additional elections that shall have additional commissioners at each
precinct for primary and general elections for party primary offices and fall general elections
held on the sixth Saturday after the first Tuesday after the first Monday in November of a
congressional election year.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 18:425(A)(1))
