HOUSE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
Amendments proposed by House Committee on House and Governmental Affairs to
Original House Bill No. 210 by Representative Melerine
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, at the end of line 11, insert the following:
3 "Any member of a school board or parish or municipal governing authority who
4 prior to June 5, 2024, was employed with a person that had or was seeking a
5 contractual or other business or financial relationship with the member's
6 governmental entity or an agency under the jurisdiction or supervision of the
7 member's governmental entity shall not be required to comply with the disclosure
8 requirements in R.S. 42:1114 for such employment. However, a person shall be
9 required to file a disclosure for any employment on or after June 5, 2024, as required
10 by R.S. 42:1111(C)(6)."
