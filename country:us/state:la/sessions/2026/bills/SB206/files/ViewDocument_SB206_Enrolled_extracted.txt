6 Section 1. R.S. 17:440.4(C) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
7 §440.4. Cardiac health; student athletes, parents; communication
8 * * *
9 C.(1) Beginning in the 2026-2027 school year, a qualified healthcare
10 professional who performs a physical examination of a student athlete shall
11 include in the examination a cardiovascular prescreening that follows nationally
12 recognized, evidence-based guidelines recommended by the American College
13 of Cardiology, American Heart Association, or other nationally recognized
14 organizations focused on evidence-based cardiovascular care.
15 (2) A student athlete with a positive finding shall be referred for further
16 evaluation and testing.
17 (3) Educational materials that provide information about causes of
18 sudden cardiac arrest in minor children that follow evidence-based science shall
