BY REPRESENTATIVE PHELPS
HEALTH/SICKLE CELL ANEM: Provides for the membership of the Sickle Cell
Commission
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 40:1125.1(B)(1)(introductory paragraph) and (2) and to repeal
3 R.S. 40:1125.1(B)(1)(d) through (h) and (10), relative to the Louisiana Sickle Cell
4 Commission; to provide for the members of the Louisiana Sickle Cell Commission;
5 to provide for the initial meeting of the Louisiana Sickle Cell Commission; and to
6 provide for related matters.
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
8 Section 1. R.S. 40:1125.1(B)(1)(introductory paragraph) and (2) are hereby amended
9 and reenacted to read as follows:
10 §1125.1. Louisiana Sickle Cell Commission
11 B.(1) Thirteen members shall be appointed by the governor, subject to
12 Senate confirmation, as follows: The following members shall be appointed by the
13 governor and subject to Senate confirmation:
14 * * *
15 (2) Two of the members shall be as follows: The following shall be members
16 of the commission:
17 (a) The secretary of the Louisiana Department of Health or his designee.
18 (b) The Medicaid director of the Louisiana Department of Health or his
19 designee.
20 (c) The executive director of the Sickle Cell Association of South Louisiana.

HB 193 Original 2026 Regular Session Phelps
Abstract: Provides that the executive directors of certain Louisiana sickle cell disease
organizations are members of the Louisiana Sickle Cell Commission.
Present law provides that one member from a list of nominees provided by the following
organizations shall be a member of the La. Sickle Cell Commission appointed by the
governor and subject to Senate confirmation:
(1) The Sickle Cell Association of South Louisiana.
(2) The Northeast Louisiana Sickle Cell Anemia Technical Resource Foundation, Inc.
(3) The Sickle Cell Disease Association of America, Inc., Northwest Louisiana Chapter.
(4) The Sickle Cell Anemia Research Foundation, Alexandria.
(5) Southwest Sickle Cell Anemia, Inc.
Proposed law repeals present law and provides that the executive directors of the following
organizations shall be members of the La. Sickle Cell Commission:
(1) The Sickle Cell Association of South Louisiana.
(2) The Northeast Louisiana Sickle Cell Anemia Technical Resource Foundation, Inc.
(3) The Sickle Cell Disease Association of America, Inc., Northwest Louisiana Chapter.
(4) The Sickle Cell Anemia Research Foundation, Alexandria.
(5) Southwest Sickle Cell Anemia, Inc.

Present law provides that the appointment of the La. Sickle Cell Commission shall take place
no later than October 1, 2013, and the commission shall convene its first meeting no later
than November 1, 2013.
Proposed law repeals present law.
