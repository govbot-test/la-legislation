28 * * *
29 C.(1) No later than the first of May of each year, the 911 districts shall submit
30 a consolidated report of statewide 911 communication activity to both the House
31 Committee on Commerce and the Senate Committee on Commerce, Consumer
32 Protection and International Affairs of the Louisiana Legislature. A copy of the
33 consolidated report shall be submitted to the governing authority of each parish in
34 which a 911 district included in the report is located. Each communications district
35 shall post the consolidated report on its public website, if it maintains a public
36 website, within ten calendar days after submission.
37 (2) The report shall include, at a minimum, all of the following from the
38 previous calendar year:
39 * * *
40 (b) All expenditures for each 911 district and all expenditures statewide. This
41 accounting shall identify, at a minimum and where applicable, expenditures for all
42 of the following:
43 (i) PSAP personnel and administrative costs.
