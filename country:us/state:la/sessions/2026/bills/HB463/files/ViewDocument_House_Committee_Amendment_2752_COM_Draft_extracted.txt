HCAHB463 4463 2752
HOUSE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
by Representative McMakin
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, line 2, change "33:9109(C)(2)(a)" to "33:9109(C)(2)"
3 AMENDMENT NO. 2
4 On page 1, line 5, after "charge;" insert "to provide for an annual report; to provide for
5 requirements to levy a service charge;"
6 AMENDMENT NO. 3
7 On page 1, line 8, change "33:9109(C)(2)(a)" to "33:9109(C)(2)"
8 AMENDMENT NO. 4
9 On page 1, delete line 19, and insert in lieu thereof the following:
10 "(b) Notwithstanding the provisions of Subparagraph (a) of this Paragraph,
11 the governing authority of the Terrebonne Parish Communications District may levy
12 a CMRS emergency telephone service charge of not more than one dollar and eighty-
13 five cents per month per wireless CMRS connection.
14 (c)(i) Each communications district that levies a service charge pursuant to
15 this Subsection shall submit an annual report to the legislative committees on
16 commerce and to the governing authority of the parish or municipality in which the
17 district is located.
18 (ii) The report shall include but not be limited to all of the following:
19 (aa) The total amount of service charge revenues collected.
20 (bb) A detailed accounting of expenditures of such revenues.
21 (cc) Performance metrics, including average call answer times and dispatch
22 times.
23 (dd) A description of any improvements to emergency communications
24 systems funded by such revenues.
25 (d) A communications district shall not levy a service charge in excess of
26 one dollar and twenty-five cents per month unless both of the following occur:
27 (i) The proposed increase is approved by a majority vote of the governing
28 authority of the parish or municipality in which the district is located.
29 (ii) The district conducts at least one public hearing on the proposed
30 increase, with notice published in the official journal of the parish or municipality
31 at least ten days prior to the hearing.
