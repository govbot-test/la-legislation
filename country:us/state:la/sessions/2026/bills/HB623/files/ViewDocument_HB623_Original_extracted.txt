BY REPRESENTATIVE HENRY
TOBACCO/TOBACCO PRODUCTS: Provides for a three-tier system of permitting for the
producers, manufacturers, and wholesalers of tobacco and vapor products
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 26:914.1 and 914.2, relative to permits for tobacco or vapor products; to
3 provide for a three-tier system for the issuance of permits; to require in-state
4 wholesale distribution of products; to provide for a tracking system; to provide for
5 penalties; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 26:914.1 and 914.2 are hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §914.1. Three-tier system for tobacco and vapor products
9 A.(1) No manufacturer of tobacco or vapor products shall do any of the
10 following:
11 (a) Hold a retail dealer permit issued pursuant to this Chapter.
12 (b) Hold a wholesale dealer permit issued pursuant to this Chapter.
13 (c) Possess any direct or indirect financial interest in a permitted wholesaler
14 or retailer in this state.
15 (2) No wholesaler shall do either of the following:
16 (a) Hold a retail dealer permit.
17 (b) Be a manufacturer of tobacco or vapor products.
18 (3) No retail dealer shall do any of the following:
19 (a) Hold a wholesaler permit.
20 (b) Be a manufacturer of tobacco or vapor products.

17 (e) Tax verification status.

19 violation of this Section and subjects the violator to a penalty pursuant to R.S.

22 A. Violations of the provisions of R.S. 26:913 shall result in the following:
23 (1) For a first offense, a fine of not less than five thousand dollars nor more
24 than twenty-five thousand dollars, and a suspension of the permit for up to ninety
25 days.
26 (2) A second offense shall result in revocation of the permit.
27 B. All penalties collected shall be retained by the commissioner.

HB 623 Original 2026 Regular Session Henry
Abstract: Provides for a three-tier system of permitting for the producers, manufacturers,
and wholesalers of tobacco and vapor products.
Present law (R.S. 26:902) provides for the issuance of retail dealer and wholesale dealer
permits for the sale of tobacco and vapor products in the state of La.
Proposed law retains present law and provides for a three-tier system for the issuance of
permits for the sale of tobacco and vapor products in the state of La.
Proposed law provides that a manufacturer of tobacco or vapor products cannot:
(1) Hold a retail dealer permit.
(2) Hold a wholesale dealer permit.
(3) Possess any direct or indirect financial interest in a permitted wholesaler or retailer
in the state of La.
Proposed law provides that a wholesaler of tobacco or vapor products cannot:
(1) Hold a retail dealer permit.
(2) Be a manufacturer of tobacco or vapor products.
Proposed law provides that a retailer of tobacco or vapor products cannot:
(1) Hold a wholesaler permit.
(2) Be a manufacturer of tobacco or vapor products.
(3) Purchase tobacco or vapor products from any person other than a wholesaler with a
valid permit issued in the state of La.
Proposed law provides that all tobacco and vapor products must be sold by a manufacturer
to a wholesaler with a valid permit. Further prohibits the direct shipment of products from
a manufacturer to a retailer. Permits the direct shipment to a consumer provided that the
seller has a valid wholesale permit and complies with tax and reporting requirements.

Proposed law requires the commissioner of the office of alcohol and tobacco control (ATC)
to establish and approve a digital product registry and chain of custody tracking system to
track the transfer of tobacco and vapor products between tiers.
Proposed law provides for penalties of not less than $5,000 nor more than $25,000 for a first
offense and suspension of a permit for 90 days. For a second offense, a permit is revoked.
Further provides that all penalties shall be retained by the commissioner.
Proposed law provides that the purchase of products from an unauthorized source is a
violation of proposed law and that the products are subject to forfeiture.
(Adds R.S. 26:914.1 and 914.2)
