15 "CHAPTER 1-D. TERMINATING RESTRICTIVE ENFORCEMENT OF
16 YOUTH SETTLEMENTS LAW
17 §2717.21. Short title
18 A. This Chapter shall be known and may be cited as the ``Terminating
19 Restrictive Enforcement of Youth Settlements Law'' or "TREY'S Law''.
20 §2717.22. Definitions
21 As used in this Chapter, the following terms have the following
22 meanings:
23 (1) "Minor" means a person who has not attained eighteen years of age.
24 (2) "Nondisclosure clause" means a provision in a contract or agreement
25 that prohibits one or more parties to the contract or agreement from disclosing
26 conduct or information covered by the terms and conditions of the contract or
27 agreement.
28 (3) "Sexual abuse" means:
29 (a) Any sexual act or conduct constituting a crime under the laws of this
30 state, including a sex offense or sexual offense as defined in R.S. 15:541,
31 regardless of whether the conduct results in a criminal charge, conviction,
32 adjudication, or sentence.
33 (b) Any sexual act or conduct constituting abuse as defined in Children's
34 Code Article 603, including the aiding or toleration of a minor's involvement in
35 any sexual act or sexual activity.
36 §2717.23. Nondisclosure agreements; victims of child sexual abuse; void and
37 unenforceable
38 A. A nondisclosure clause shall be void and unenforceable as against
39 public policy only to the extent that the nondisclosure clause prohibits the
40 following:
41 (1) A victim or alleged victim of sexual abuse against a minor from
42 disclosing that act of sexual abuse, or facts related to that act of sexual abuse.
43 (2) Any other person from disclosing facts related to sexual abuse against
44 a minor in support of, in furtherance of, or consistent with the right of a victim
45 or alleged victim to disclose under Paragraph (A)(1) of this Section.
46 B. Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, nothing in this
47 Section shall be construed to prohibit a person, including a victim or alleged
