SLS 26RS-426 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY SENATOR BOUDREAUX
BANKS/BANKING. Provides relative to the name of a bank. (gov sig)
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 6:412(D) and to enact R.S. 6:412(E), relative to banks; to
3 provide relative to the use of a trade or assumed name of an FDIC-insured depository
4 institution; to provide for terms, conditions, and exceptions; to provide for an
5 effective date; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 6:412(D) is hereby amended and reenacted and R.S. 6:412(E) is
8 hereby enacted to read as follows:
9 §412. Unauthorized use of the word "bank" or similar words
10 * * *
11 D. Nothing in this Section or any other provision of law shall be
12 construed to prevent any FDIC-insured depository institution operating within
13 the state from using a trade or assumed name that is different from the
14 institution's corporate name or title, provided the corporate name or title is
15 used together with the trade or assumed name and is in compliance with
16 applicable federal laws and regulations.
17 E. Nothing in this Section shall be construed to prevent the use of the word

The keyword, summary, and digest do not constitute part of the law or proof
or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
DIGEST
SB 175 Original 2026 Regular Session Boudreaux
Present law prohibits any person other than a licensed financial institution from using certain
terms and words as part of its name or title.
Proposed law retains present law and allows an FDIC-insured bank operating in the state to
use a trade or assumed name different from its corporate name, as long as the corporate
name is used together with the trade name and the bank complies with applicable federal
laws and regulations.
Effective upon signature of the governor or lapse of time for gubernatorial action.
(Amends R.S. 6:412(D); adds R.S. 6:412(E))
