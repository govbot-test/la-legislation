2 A. There is hereby created in the state treasury a special fund designated as

HB 109 Original 2026 Regular Session Domangue
Abstract: Allocates sports wagering net gaming proceeds to the Imported Seafood Safety
Fund.
Present law provides for the allocation of revenue from the sports wagering tax.
Proposed law provides that 2.5% of net sports wagering proceeds shall be allocated to the
Imported Seafood Safety Fund found in present law (R.S. 40.5.10.1).
(Amends R.S. 27:625(G)(10) and R.S. 40:5.10.1(A); Adds R.S. 27:625(G)(11))
