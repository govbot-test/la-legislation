Section 1:
HCAHB6 3209 5691
HOUSE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
Original House Bill No. 6 by Representative Mike Johnson
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, at the end of line 5, insert "provide for revenue generated by carbon dioxide
3 sequestration activity; to"
4 AMENDMENT NO. 2
5 On page 2, between lines 24 and 25, insert the following:
6 "(3)(a) If a parish prohibits activity related to carbon dioxide sequestration
7 pursuant to the local option procedures provided in this Chapter, that parish shall be
8 prohibited from receiving any state revenues or monies collected from the activity
9 prohibited for the duration of the prohibition. Any state revenue or monies that
10 would otherwise be payable to the parish from the prohibited activity shall be
11 deposited into the state general fund.
12 (b) The prohibition provided by this Paragraph shall apply to all of the
13 following:
14 (i) Any and all state revenue and monies generated by or from taxes, fees,
15 rentals, bonuses, leases, operating agreements, contracts for the geologic storage of
16 carbon dioxide, or other sources of public funds.
17 (ii) Any revenue and monies generated by carbon dioxide sequestration
18 activity and facilities, including Class V and VI wells, storage facilities, and
19 pipelines transporting carbon dioxide for sequestration purposes, or by any energy-
20 generating, manufacturing, or other industrial facility or activity whose operations
21 include the use of carbon dioxide sequestration, whether on- or off-site."
