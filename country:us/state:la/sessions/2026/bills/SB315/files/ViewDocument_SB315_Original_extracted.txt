PUBLIC CONTRACTS. Increases budget cost minimums for professional services contract

1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 38:2316, relative to professional service contract exemptions;
3 to provide for thresholds; to provide for an effective date; and to provide for related

8 Except as provided in R.S. 38:2311(C), the provisions of this Part shall not

DIGEST
SB 315 Original 2026 Regular Session Reese
Present law (R.S. 38:2310 et seq.) provides requirements for agencies seeking to enter into
professional services contracts for architect, engineer, or landscape architect services.
Present law exempts from such requirements professional services contracts in which the
estimated budget is $1,000,000 or less. Proposed law increases this exemption threshold
from $1,000,000 to $2,000,000.
Effective upon signature of the governor or lapse of time for gubernatorial action.
(Amends R.S. 38:2316)
