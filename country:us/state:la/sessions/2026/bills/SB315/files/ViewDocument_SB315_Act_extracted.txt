Section 1:
2026 Regular Session ACT No. 260 ENROLLED
BY SENATOR REESE
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 38:2316, relative to professional service contract exemptions;
3 to provide for thresholds; to provide for contract requirements; to provide for an
4 effective date; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 38:2316 is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
7 §2316. Applicability
8 Except as provided in R.S. 38:2311(C), the provisions of this Part shall not
9 apply to professional service contracts in which the estimated project budget cost is
10 one two million dollars or less. Any agency that enters into more than one
11 professional services contract in any given fiscal year shall ensure that not less
12 than fifty percent of the total number of such contracts are awarded to separate,
13 distinct, and unaffiliated professional services firms if such contracts are
14 exempt pursuant to the provisions of this Section.
15 Section 2. This Act shall become effective on July 1, 2026; if vetoed by the governor
16 and subsequently approved by the legislature, this Act shall become effective on the day
17 following such approval by the legislature or July 1, 2026, whichever is later.
PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
GOVERNOR OF THE STATE OF LOUISIANA
