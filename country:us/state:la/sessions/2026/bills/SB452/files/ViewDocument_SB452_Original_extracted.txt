SLS 26RS-805 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE BILL NO. 452
BY SENATORS WHEAT AND HODGES
WATER/WATERWAYS. Prohibits issuance of certain coastal use permits on Lake
Maurepas. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 49:214.30(J), relative to issuance of coastal use permits on Lake Maurepas;
3 to provide for coastal use permits; to provide for prohibitions; to provide for
4 exceptions; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 49:214.30(J) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
7 §214.30. Coastal use permits
8 A.(1) * * *
9 J. Notwithstanding the provisions of this Section, neither the secretary
10 nor the approved local program of any parish shall issue a coastal use permit
11 for any work or construction on Lake Maurepas, except for the following:
12 (1) Construction, creation, alteration, improvement, extension, or
13 maintenance of any wharf, pier, dock, bulkhead, landfill, or structure.
14 (2) Repair or replacement of an existing pipeline or well facility.
15 (3) Dredging or disposal of dredge material.

DIGEST
SB 452 Original 2026 Regular Session Wheat
Present law authorizes Conservation and Energy to issue coastal use permits for certain
activities that occur within the coastal zone, including construction and dredging.
Proposed law retains present law and prohibits issuance of coastal use permits on Lake
Maurepas, except for construction and maintenance of a wharf, pier, dock, landfill, or other
structure, repair and maintenance of an existing pipeline or well, or dredging operations.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Adds R.S. 49:214.30(J))
