SLS 26RS-280 ENGROSSED
SB NO. 133
Present law provides the Department of Transportation and Development (DOTD) is the
agency with jurisdiction over autonomous commercial motor vehicles and automated driving
systems. Proposed law provides DOTD is the lead agency with the assistance of Department
of Public Safety and Corrections, office of state police over autonomous commercial motor
vehicles and automated driving systems.
Present law provides a written statement must be presented before using a driverless,
autonomous, commercial vehicle certifying the vehicle meets DOTD requirements. Proposed
law retains present law and changes the agency from DOTD to Department of Public Safety
and Corrections, office of state police.
Present law provides before operating a driverless commercial motor vehicle with a
teleoperation system, a written statement must be submitted by the vehicle's owner, a remote
driver or the remote drivers employer certifying the vehicle meets certain requirements.
Proposed law retains present law and changes the agency from DOTD to Department of
Public Safety and Corrections, office of state police.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 32:296(A)(intro para), 400.2(B), 400.3(C) and 400.7(B))
