Present law defines "tactical medical professional" as a licensed paramedic or physician who

Present law also provides that a tactical medical professional may carry a firearm in the same
manner as a law enforcement officer in certain circumstances and that the appointing law
enforcement agency must issue any firearm or ammunition that the tactical medical
professional carries.
Proposed law provides that the appointing law enforcement agency must approve any
firearm or ammunition that a tactical medical professional carries but that it will not be
required to issue it.
Proposed law otherwise retains present law.
Effective August 1, 2026.
