HB 747 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Miller
Abstract: Combines the licensing and certification of medical psychologists and authorizes
the board to establish a fee for the licensing.
Present law requires medical psychologists to be licensed by the La. St. Bd. of Medical
Examiners to practice as a medical psychologist in this state. Present law further provides
that when a medical psychologist meets certain requirements, he may obtain a certificate of
advanced practice.
Present law provides that a medical psychologist with a certificate of advanced practice has
more autonomy and responsibility.
Proposed law combines the medical psychologist license and the medical psychologist
certificate of advance practice and eliminates the certificate of advanced practice.
Proposed law provides that the board may establish a fee not to exceed $325.
Present law provides that a medical psychologist shall provide the board with the name and
credentials of a psychological technician or psychometerician under his supervision.
Proposed law repeals present law.
