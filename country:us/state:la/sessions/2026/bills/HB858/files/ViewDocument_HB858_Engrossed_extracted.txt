2026 Regular Session
HOUSE BILL NO. 858
BY REPRESENTATIVE RISER
WILDLIFE & FISHERIES: Establishes a public records exemption for Global Positioning
System data collected on wildlife and aquatic life
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 44:4(65), relative to global positioning data for individual animals; to
3 establish a public records exemption for Department of Wildlife and Fisheries data
4 that identifies the location of individual animals; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 44:4(65) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
7 §4. Applicability
8 This Chapter shall not apply:
9 * * *
10 (65) To any global positioning data on wildlife or aquatic life collected by
11 or in the possession of the Department of Wildlife and Fisheries that would reveal
12 the location of individual animals.

Abstract: Establishes a public records exemption for Dept. of Wildlife and Fisheries'
Global Positioning System (GPS) data on wildlife and aquatic life.
Present law establishes a public records law and exemptions from the public records
requirements.

HLS 26RS-1184 ENGROSSED
HB NO. 858
Proposed law retains present law.
Proposed law adds an exemption to the public records laws for GPS data on wildlife and
aquatic life that is collected by or in the possession of the Dept. of Wildlife and Fisheries
that would reveal the location of individual animals.
(Adds R.S. 44:4(65))
