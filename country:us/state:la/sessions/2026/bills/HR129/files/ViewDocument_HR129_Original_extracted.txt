2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 129
BY REPRESENTATIVE HILFERTY
Annual Family Caregiver Day at the state capitol
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate Wednesday, April 15, 2026, as AARP Louisiana's 11th Annual Family
3 Caregiver Day at the state capitol.
4 WHEREAS, Louisiana ranks forty-fifth in the nation on the Long Term Services and
5 Supports State Scorecard; and
6 WHEREAS, an estimated nine hundred seventy three thousand family caregivers in
7 Louisiana provide care to adult relatives or friends, with the economic value of this care
8 estimated to be roughly six billion nine hundred million dollars annually; and
9 WHEREAS, eleven thousand eight hundred-twenty individuals are on waiting lists
10 to receive assistance with activities of daily living in their homes and live in the setting of
11 their choice; and
12 WHEREAS, almost ninety percent of older adults in Louisiana who need assistance
13 with activities of daily living wish to remain in their homes and communities with the
14 support of friends and family as they age; and
15 WHEREAS, almost three-fourths of older people living in a community who receive
16 personal assistance rely exclusively on unpaid family caregivers for help; and
17 WHEREAS, it is fitting to recognize the outstanding efforts and selfless dedication
18 of family caregivers throughout Louisiana.
19 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
20 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby designate Wednesday, April 15, 2026, as AARP
21 Louisiana's 11th Annual Family Caregiver Day at the state capitol and does hereby express

1 enduring appreciation to the individuals who serve as caregivers and improve the quality of
