2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 129
BY REPRESENTATIVE HILFERTY
A RESOLUTION
To designate Wednesday, April 15, 2026, as AARP Louisiana's 11th Annual Family
Caregiver Day at the state capitol.
WHEREAS, Louisiana ranks forty-fifth in the nation on the Long Term Services and
Supports State Scorecard; and
WHEREAS, an estimated nine hundred seventy three thousand family caregivers in
Louisiana provide care to adult relatives or friends, with the economic value of this care
estimated to be roughly six billion nine hundred million dollars annually; and
WHEREAS, eleven thousand eight hundred-twenty individuals are on waiting lists
to receive assistance with activities of daily living in their homes and live in the setting of
their choice; and
WHEREAS, almost ninety percent of older adults in Louisiana who need assistance
with activities of daily living wish to remain in their homes and communities with the
support of friends and family as they age; and
WHEREAS, almost three-fourths of older people living in a community who receive
personal assistance rely exclusively on unpaid family caregivers for help; and
WHEREAS, it is fitting to recognize the outstanding efforts and selfless dedication
of family caregivers throughout Louisiana.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby designate Wednesday, April 15, 2026, as AARP
Louisiana's 11th Annual Family Caregiver Day at the state capitol and does hereby express
enduring appreciation to the individuals who serve as caregivers and improve the quality of
life for the elderly in the state.
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
