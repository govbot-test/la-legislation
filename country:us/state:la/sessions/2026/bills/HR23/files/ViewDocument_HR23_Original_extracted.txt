HLS 26RS-1361 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 23
BY REPRESENTATIVE DICKERSON
COMMENDATIONS: Commends Livingston Parish Sheriff Jason Ard and his law
enforcement team for their work in stopping child predators
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Livingston Parish Sheriff Jason Ard and his law enforcement team for their
3 work in stopping child predators.
4 WHEREAS, Sheriff Jason Ard, Chief Detective Calvin Bowden, and the Livingston
5 Parish Sheriff's Office criminal investigations detectives and undercover agents have gone
6 above and beyond to ensure that Louisiana is protected from the threat of child predators;
7 and
8 WHEREAS, the Livingston Parish Sheriff's Office law enforcement officers have put
9 their lives on the line to protect and serve their community and to ensure that predatory and
10 violent criminals cannot harm children; and
11 WHEREAS, the Livingston Parish Sheriff's Office recently worked with Chris
12 Hansen to conduct a child predator sting, and their efforts resulted in the arrest of thirteen
13 men; and
14 WHEREAS, Sheriff Jason Ard, Chief Detective Bowden, and the law enforcement
15 officers with the Livingston Parish Sheriff's Office are most deserving of the highest
16 recognition for honoring their commitment to protect the children of Louisiana.
17 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
18 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Livingston Parish Sheriff Jason Ard and his
19 law enforcement team for their work in stopping child predators and does hereby express
20 enduring appreciation for ensuring the safety of children across the state.
