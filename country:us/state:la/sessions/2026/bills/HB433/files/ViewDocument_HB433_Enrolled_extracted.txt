Section 1:
ENROLLED
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE FREIBERG
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 33:2740.8(D)(1)(b), 2740.19(D)(1)(e), 2740.53(B)(1)(b),
3 2740.67.1(C)(1)(f), and 4720.151(G)(1)(b) and R.S. 39:1482(4), relative to East
4 Baton Rouge Parish; to provide relative to the Downtown Development District of
5 the City of Baton Rouge, the Baton Rouge Inner City Economic Development
6 District, the Florida Boulevard Economic Development District, and the Plank Road
7 Business Economic Development District, and the East Baton Rouge Redevelopment
8 Authority; to provide relative to nominations or appointments made to such entities
9 by the Baton Rouge Area Chamber of Commerce; to provide relative to the
10 Louisiana Competes Regional Economic Development Program; to provide relative
11 to definitions for the program; to change the name of the Baton Rouge Area
12 Chamber of Commerce; and to provide for related matters.
13 Notice of intention to introduce this Act has been published
14 as provided by Article III, Section 13 of the Constitution of
15 Louisiana.
16 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
17 Section 1. R.S. 33:2740.8(D)(1)(b), 2740.19(D)(1)(e), 2740.53(B)(1)(b),
18 2740.67.1(C)(1)(f), and 4720.151(G)(1)(b) are hereby amended and reenacted to read as
19 follows:

21 composition and powers; preparation of plans, levy of special ad valorem
22 taxes, and issuance of bonds and other instruments of indebtedness
23 * * *

25 qualified voters of the parish of East Baton Rouge, and shall have their principal

18 Rouge Area Chamber Greater Baton Rouge Economic Partnership.
