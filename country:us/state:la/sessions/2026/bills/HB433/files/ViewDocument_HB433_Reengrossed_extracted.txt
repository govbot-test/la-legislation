HB 433 Reengrossed 2026 Regular Session Freiberg
Abstract: Changes the name of the Baton Rouge Area Chamber to the Greater Baton
Rouge Economic Partnership in certain provisions.
Present law creates the following entities as political subdivisions in East Baton Rouge
Parish: the Downtown Development District of the city of Baton Rouge, the Baton Rouge
Inner City Economic Development District, the Florida Boulevard Economic Development
District, the Plank Road Business Economic Development District, and the East Baton
Rouge Redevelopment Authority. Provides that the Baton Rouge Area Chamber of
Commerce makes nominations or appointments to the governing boards of the districts.
Proposed law changes the name of the chamber to the Greater Baton Rouge Economic
Partnership.
Present law creates the Louisiana Competes Regional Economic Development Program to
be administered by Louisiana Economic Development, to provide grants to regional
economic development organizations. Provides that the definition of "regional economic
development organization" includes the Baton Rouge Area Chamber, or its successor.
Proposed law changes the name of the chamber to the Greater Baton Rouge Economic
Partnership.
