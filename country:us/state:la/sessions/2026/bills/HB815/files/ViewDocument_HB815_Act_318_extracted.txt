Section 1:
ENROLLED
ACT No. 318
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE CARVER
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 40:41(C)(1)(k), relative to vital records in the custody of the state registry; to
3 provide for the disclosure of records to federally insured financial institutions; and
4 to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 40:41(C)(1)(k) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
7 §41. Disclosure of records
8 * * *
9 C.(1) The state registrar shall not permit inspection of the records or issue
10 a certificate, or any part thereof, unless he is satisfied that the applicant is any of the
11 following:
12 * * *
13 (k) A federally insured bank or federally insured credit union, or its duly
14 authorized agent.
15 * * *
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE
GOVERNOR OF THE STATE OF LOUISIANA
