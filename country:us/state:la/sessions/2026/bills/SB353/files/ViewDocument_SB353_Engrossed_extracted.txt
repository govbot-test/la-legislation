SLS 26RS-572 ENGROSSED
2026 Regular Session
SENATE BILL NO. 353
BY SENATOR SELDERS
POSTSECONDARY ED. Authorizes each postsecondary system management board to
digitize student IDs for use in LA Wallet. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 17:3351(H), relative to student identification cards; to authorize each
3 postsecondary system management board to make student identification cards
4 available as a digitized credential; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 17:3351(H) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
7 §3351. General powers, duties, and functions of postsecondary education
8 management boards
9 * * *
10 H. In addition to any other powers and duties authorized by this Section,
11 each board shall make all student identification cards, within their respective
12 system, available as a digitized credential as authorized by Subpart E of Part
13 I of Chapter 1 of Title 39 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950, commonly
14 referred to as "LA Wallet".
15 * * *
