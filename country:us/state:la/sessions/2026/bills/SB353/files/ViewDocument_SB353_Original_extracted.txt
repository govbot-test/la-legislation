SLS 26RS-572 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE BILL NO. 353
BY SENATOR SELDERS
STUDENTS. Authorizes the Board of Supervisors of LSU to digitize student IDs for use in
LA Wallet. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 17:3351(H), relative to student identification cards; to authorize the Board of
3 Supervisors of the Louisiana State University and Agricultural and Mechanical
4 College of the Louisiana State University System to make student identification
5 cards available as a digitized credential; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 17:3351(H) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §3351. General powers, duties, and functions of postsecondary education
9 management boards
10 * * *
11 H. In addition to any other powers and duties authorized by this Section,
12 the Board of Supervisors of the Louisiana State University and Agricultural and
13 Mechanical College with supervision and management of the Louisiana State
14 University System shall make all student identification cards, within the system,
15 available as a digitized credential as authorized by Subpart E of Part I of
16 Chapter 1 of Title 39 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950, commonly
17 referred to as "LA Wallet", by August 1, 2027.

SB 353 Original 2026 Regular Session Selders
Proposed law authorizes the LSU Board of supervisors to make student identification cards
available as a digitized credential as part of LA Wallet for all students enrolled in LSU
System schools.
Effective August 1, 2026.
