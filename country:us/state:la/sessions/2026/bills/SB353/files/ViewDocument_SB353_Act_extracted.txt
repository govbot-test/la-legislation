2026 Regular Session ACT No. 760 ENROLLED
SENATE BILL NO. 353
BY SENATOR SELDERS AND REPRESENTATIVES CHASSION AND TERRY
LANDRY
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 17:3351(H), relative to student identification cards; to authorize each
3 postsecondary system management board to make student identification cards
4 available as a digitized credential; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 17:3351(H) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
7 §3351. General powers, duties, and functions of postsecondary education
8 management boards
9 * * *
10 H. In addition to any other powers and duties authorized by this Section,
11 each board may make student identification cards, within their respective
12 system, available as a digitized credential as authorized by Subpart E of Part
13 I of Chapter 1 of Title 39 of the Louisiana Revised Statutes of 1950, commonly
14 referred to as "LA Wallet". Student identification cards authorized by the
15 provisions of this Subsection shall be obtained by a student at no charge. A
16 biometric identification platform shall not charge any fee or payment for its use
17 to any student, university, retail establishment, partnership, corporation, or any
18 business.
19 * * *
