2026 Regular Session ACT No. 387 ENROLLED
SENATE BILL NO. 424
BY SENATOR FOIL
1 AN ACT
2 To enact Code of Civil Procedure Art. 1313(E), relative to service of pleadings; to provide
3 relative to service of certain pleadings by electronic means; to provide for counsel
4 of record; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. Code of Civil Procedure Art. 1313(E) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
7 Art. 1313. Service by mail, delivery, or electronic means
8 * * *
9 E. For the purposes of this Article, "counsel of record" means a counsel
10 who has enrolled in the action for a party.
