HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 66
BY REPRESENTATIVE WALTERS
of ShaMonica Michelle Bryant Huggins
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To express the condolences of the House of Representatives upon the death of ShaMonica
3 Michelle Bryant Huggins.
4 WHEREAS, it is with immense sadness and profound regret that the House of
5 Representatives of the Legislature of Louisiana has learned of the passing, on February 17,
6 2026, of ShaMonica Michelle Bryant Huggins; and
7 WHEREAS, ShaMonica was born on August 21, 1989, in Shreveport, Louisiana, to
8 Robert Jett, Sr., and Stephanie Hawkins; she accepted Christ at an early age and joined Faith
9 Baptist Fellowship; and
10 WHEREAS, ShaMonica graduated from Huntington High School in 2007 and always
11 strived for excellence in her academic endeavors; and
12 WHEREAS, ShaMonica was passionate about her work as a hair stylist and found
13 joy in using her creative artistry to make others feel beautiful; she cherished her work in
14 retail, where her caring spirit impacted many lives; and
15 WHEREAS, ShaMonica Michelle Bryant Huggins is survived by her husband,
16 Jacorey Huggins; daughters, Zikara Woodard, Ziaira Woodard, and Ja'Niyah Huggins;
17 parents, Stephanie Hawkins (Greg) and Robert Jett, Sr.; grandparents, Larry Black (Alice)
18 and Lodis Bryant; sisters, TraNiqua Bryant, TraShayla Bryant, and Raven Eneh
19 (Ifeanyichukwu); brother, Robert Jett, Jr.; mother-in-law, LaShonda Huggins Douglas;
20 sister-in-law, Jatarie Huggins; and a host of aunts, uncles, nieces, nephews, extended
Page 1 of 2

1 relatives and friends; she will be deeply missed, but the legacy of her loving spirit shall live
2 on in the hearts and minds of those who were privileged to have known her.
3 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
4 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby express heartfelt condolences on the death of
5 ShaMonica Michelle Bryant Huggins and does hereby extend sincere wishes that her family,
6 friends, and loved ones find comfort in their shared memories of her beautiful life.
