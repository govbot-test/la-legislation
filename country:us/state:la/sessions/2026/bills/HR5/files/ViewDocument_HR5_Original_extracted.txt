HLS 26RS-301 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 5
BY REPRESENTATIVE DEVILLIER
CONDOLENCES: Expresses the condolences of the House of Representatives on the death
of Derek Scott
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To express the sincere and heartfelt condolences of the House of Representatives upon the
3 death of Derek Scott.
4 WHEREAS, it is with the utmost sorrow and deepest regret that the House of
5 Representatives of the Legislature of Louisiana has learned of the passing, on December 21,
6 2025, of Derek Scott; and
7 WHEREAS, Derek was the inspiring and cherished son of State Representative
8 Rhonda Gaye Butler; he was known for his gentle spirit and inimitable personality and the
9 way he made powerful connections with people simply by being himself; and
10 WHEREAS, Derek brought an immeasurable amount of joy, purpose, and
11 unconditional love into his family's life; though his life was too short, it radiated with an
12 unparalleled degree of meaning; he gave his family and loved ones countless moments that
13 will be treasured for generations to come, and his memory will provide them with guidance
14 and comfort as they mourn his passing; and
15 WHEREAS, Derek was beloved by the staff and caretakers at St. Mary's Training
16 School in Alexandria, Louisiana, where he was faithfully cared for with compassion,
17 dedication, and kindness for more than twenty years; and
18 WHEREAS, Derek is survived by his loving parents, State Representative Rhonda
19 Gaye Butler and Frank Butler; stepsisters, Millicent Johnson and Demi Butler; stepbrother,
20 Jared Leger; and grandparents, Samuel and Cheryl Leger; and
