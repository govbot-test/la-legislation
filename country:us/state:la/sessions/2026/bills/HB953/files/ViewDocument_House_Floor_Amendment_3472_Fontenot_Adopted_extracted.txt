FOR OFFICE USE ONLY
HOUSE FLOOR AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
Representative Fontenot
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 In House Floor Amendment No. 2 by Representative Fontenot (#3137), on page 5, delete
3 lines 32 through 50 in their entirety and insert the following:
4 "A.(1) There is hereby established within the board the Plumbing
5 Contractors Subcommittee, hereinafter referred to as "plumbing subcommittee",
6 consisting of the following eleven members appointed by the governor, each of
7 whom shall be a resident of this state, and each of whom shall have been actively
8 engaged in plumbing or contracting for at least five years prior to his appointment
9 by the governor.
10 (a) Three members, each of whom shall be appointed from the membership
11 of the Plumbing-Heating-Cooling Contractors Association of Louisiana.
12 (b) Three members, each of whom shall be appointed from the membership
13 of The United Association of Journeymen and Apprentices of the Plumbing and
14 Pipefitting Industry of the United States and Canada.
15 (c) One member who shall be appointed from the membership of the
16 Louisiana Associated General Contractors.
17 (d) One member who shall be appointed from the membership of the
18 Associated Builders and Contractors of Louisiana.
19 (e) One member who shall be appointed from the membership of the
20 Louisiana Home Builders Association.
21 (f) Two members, each of whom shall not be associated with the plumbing
22 industry, to serve as members-at-large.
23 (2) Each State Board of Elementary and Secondary Education district shall
24 be represented among the membership provided in this Subsection."
