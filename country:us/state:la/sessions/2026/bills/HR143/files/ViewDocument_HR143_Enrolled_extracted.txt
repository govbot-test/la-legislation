HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 143
BY REPRESENTATIVE FONTENOT

capitol, commends the Louisiana Automobile Dealers Association on its unwavering

pricing, highly trained service technicians, exceptional customer service, and strong

of this state, including a skilled workforce and a strong business climate, and in turn

citizens, support tens of thousands of additional jobs in related industries, and maintain a

WHEREAS, Louisiana's franchised dealers generate more than $1.8 billion dollars
annually in state and local sales tax revenue; and

WHEREAS, Louisiana's franchised dealers demonstrated their commitment to their
communities by contributing more than $20 million dollars to charitable organizations
during the previous year; and
WHEREAS, since 1937, the Louisiana Automobile Dealers Association has united
automobile dealers across the state, advocated for their interests, promoted ethical business
practices, and supported policies that foster economic growth and the long-term vitality of
Louisiana's automotive marketplace.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby designate Tuesday, April 14, 2026, as Louisiana
Automobile Dealer Day at the state capitol; does hereby recognize the unwavering
commitment of the Louisiana Automobile Dealers Association's members to excellence in
service; does hereby commend the next generation of new car and heavy truck dealers of the
Louisiana Automobile Dealers Association for their leadership, service, and contributions
to the state of Louisiana; and does hereby extend enduring appreciation for all that these
individuals do for Louisiana.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
chairman of the Louisiana Automobile Dealers Association.
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES

franchised new car and heavy truck dealers for shaping the automotive industry and

WHEREAS, Louisiana's franchised dealers play a vital role in the state's economy
by fostering a competitive marketplace that prioritizes consumer satisfaction through fair
pricing, highly trained service technicians, exceptional customer service, and strong
employment opportunities; and
WHEREAS, Louisiana's franchised dealers depend upon the people and resources
of this state, including a skilled workforce and a strong business climate, and in turn
