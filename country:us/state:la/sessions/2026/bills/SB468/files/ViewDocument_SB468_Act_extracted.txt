Section 1:
2026 Regular Session ACT No. 771 ENROLLED
BY SENATOR SEABAUGH AND REPRESENTATIVE JACKSON
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 51:422.1(F), relative to fuel points discount limits; to provide for the sale of
3 motor fuel to participants of fuel rewards programs; to prohibit a limit on the
4 allowable discount of fuel purchased with fuel rewards points; to prohibit a limit on
5 the value of fuel rewards points earned through a fuel rewards program; and to
6 provide for related matters.
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
8 Section 1. R.S. 51:422.1(F) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
9 §422.1. Unfair sales of motor fuel; impairment of competition; exemptions; records
10 to support exemptions
11 * * *
12 F. Nothing in this Section or any other provision of law that regulates the
13 price of motor fuel shall limit or be construed to limit the allowable discount or
14 the value of fuel points or rewards earned by a customer through participation
15 in a loyalty program, fuel rewards program, or other fuel discount program in
16 which earned rewards can be exchanged for discounts on motor fuel at the
17 retailer providing the program or at other fuel retailers, even if fuel sold
18 pursuant to this Subsection is sold at a price that would otherwise be prohibited
19 by this Section or any other provision of law regulating the price of motor fuel.
PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
GOVERNOR OF THE STATE OF LOUISIANA
