DIGEST
SB 468 Original 2026 Regular Session Seabaugh
Present law provides that fuel sold at a price below the retailer's cost constitutes an unfair
trade practice.
Proposed law provides for an exemption to present law when fuel is purchased using fuel
rewards points earned through a loyalty program, fuel rewards program, or other discount
fuel program.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Adds R.S. 51:422.1(F))
