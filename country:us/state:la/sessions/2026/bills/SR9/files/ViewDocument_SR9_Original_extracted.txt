SLS 26RS-404 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 9
BY SENATOR FOIL
CONDOLENCES. Expresses the condolences of the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
upon the passing of Barbara Vastine Hughes.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To express the sincere condolences of the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana upon the
3 passing of Barbara Vastine Hughes, and to recognize her lifetime of services, faith,
4 and dedication to her family and community.
5 WHEREAS, the Senate of Louisiana joins with the family and friends of Barbara
6 Vastine Hughes in mourning her passing on January 11, 2026, and in recognizing a life
7 marked by kindness, integrity, and a deep personal commitment to serving others; and
8 WHEREAS, born on August 31, 1935, in Baton Rouge, Louisiana, to Marvin and
9 Ethel Vastine, Barbara was raised with values that guided her lifelong dedication to family,
10 faith, and service; and
11 WHEREAS, she attended University High School and later Louisiana State
12 University, where she was an active campus leader, a member of Chi Omega, and was
13 honored as Freshman of the Year; and
14 WHEREAS, she earned a Bachelor of Science in Business from Louisiana State
15 University and applied her talents in budget and finance roles across a variety of industries,
16 including work with a historic sugar cane farm and the Louisiana State University
17 Agriculture Center; and
18 WHEREAS, Barbara met her husband, William J. Hughes Jr., on a blind date, and

1 their marriage in 1980 marked the beginning of a loving and enduring partnership; and
2 WHEREAS, her love of people, events, and history, together with her husband's love

4 friendships both at home and abroad; and
5 WHEREAS, Barbara was preceded in death by her husband, William J. Hughes Jr.;
6 her parents, Marvin and Ethel Vastine; and her sister, Sandra Dennis and her husband, Jack;
7 and
8 WHEREAS, she is survived by her brother, Boone Vastine and his wife, Carolyn;
9 her daughter, Cheryl Jones and her husband, Rowley, and their children, Shelby Neuman and
10 her husband, Nick, and Grayson Jones; her son, Richard Wood and his wife, Dr. Rebecca
11 Adcock; her stepdaughter, Nancy Readinger and her children, Alexa Alexander and Andrew
12 Readinger; her stepson, Jeff Hughes and his wife, Tammy, and his children, Jessica Stewart
13 and her husband, Chad, and Campbell Hughes and his wife, Amy Paige; as well as five
14 great-granddaughters, a future great-grandson, and numerous nieces and nephews; and
15 WHEREAS, Barbara leaves behind a legacy of service and will be remembered for
16 her warmth, wit, and sincere interest in others, qualities that enriched the lives of all who
17 knew her.
18 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
19 does hereby express its sincere condolences upon the passing of Barbara Vastine Hughes.
20 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
21 family of Barbara Vastine Hughes.
