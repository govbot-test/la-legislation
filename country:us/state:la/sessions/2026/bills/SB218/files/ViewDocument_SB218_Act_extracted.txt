Section 1:
2026 Regular Session ACT No. 254 ENROLLED
BY SENATOR TALBOT
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 18:59.4(D)(1), relative to certification programs; to provide for
3 training programs approved by the State Board of Election Supervisors; and to
4 provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 18:59.4(D)(1) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
7 §59.4. The Louisiana Voter Registration Administrators' Certification Program;
8 requirements; compensation
9 * * *
10 D.(1) The requisite education and training will be provided through courses
11 of the Certified Elections Registration Administrator program administered through
12 Auburn University and the Election Center, a national certification designation, or
13 a substantially equivalent program approved by the State Board of Election
14 Supervisors. Any course taken by a registrar of voters, chief deputy registrar, or
15 confidential assistant shall be a course that is required for the attainment of
16 certification or maintenance of such certification.
17 * * *
PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
GOVERNOR OF THE STATE OF LOUISIANA
