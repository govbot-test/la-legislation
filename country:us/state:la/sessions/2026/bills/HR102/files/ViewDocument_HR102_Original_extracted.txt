HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 102
BY REPRESENTATIVE CARPENTER
president of Phi Delta Kappa, Incorporated
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Noreen E. Little on the occasion of her election as national president of Phi
3 Delta Kappa, Incorporated, and to recognize her service, leadership, and
4 contributions to education.
5 WHEREAS, Noreen E. Little has dedicated thirty-four years of service to the New
6 York City Department of Education, where she has served as an early childhood teacher,
7 assistant principal, and principal of kindergarten through eighth grade; she holds
8 certifications in elementary education, reading specialist, and school administration and
9 supervision; and
10 WHEREAS, Ms. Little has been a committed member of the National Sorority of Phi
11 Delta Kappa, Incorporated, Eastern Region, Beta Omicron Chapter in Jamaica, New York,
12 since 1986; she has held numerous chapter offices including basileus, second anti-basileus,
13 tamiouchos, grammateus, and dean of pledgees; and
14 WHEREAS, Ms. Little has provided distinguished leadership at the regional level
15 by serving as kudos chair, tamiouchos, audit chair, and parliamentarian; she served as
16 elected eastern region director in 2015 and played an instrumental role in reactivating the
17 Epsilon Sigma Chapter in Gastonia, North Carolina; and
18 WHEREAS, Ms. Little co-chaired the 2009 Youth Leadership Conference, chaired
19 the 2024 Eastern Region Conference, and was named a Diamond Life Member in 2019; after
20 being elected first supreme anti-basileus, she launched the NSPDK, Incorporated, e-Learning
Page 1 of 2

1 Academy and implemented a town hall series to deliver timely professional content to
2 members; and
3 WHEREAS, Mrs. Little is supported by her husband, Allan, and their three adult
4 children; her election as national president of the National Sorority of Phi Delta Kappa,
5 Incorporated, is a testament to her sustained commitment to educational excellence,
6 organizational service, and the development of youth and professional members.
7 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
8 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Noreen E. Little on the occasion of her
9 election as national president of Phi Delta Kappa, Incorporated; does hereby express
10 enduring appreciation for her exemplary service, visionary leadership, and contributions to
11 education; and does hereby encourage the support of her peers for her ongoing work on
12 behalf of students, educators, and sorority members.
