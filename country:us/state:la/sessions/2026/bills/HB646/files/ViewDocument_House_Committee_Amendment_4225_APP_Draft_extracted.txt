3 "10.18. Louisiana Income Tax Elimination Fund
4 Section 10.18. (A) There is hereby established in the state treasury the

6 "fund".
7 (B) After allocation of money to the Bond Security and Redemption Fund
8 as provided in Article VII, Section 9(B) of this Constitution, the treasurer shall
9 annually deposit in and credit to the fund all recurring state general fund revenues
10 received by the state in excess of the Government Growth Limit and below the
11 official forecast for that fiscal year.
12 (C) Monies in the fund shall be invested in the same manner as monies in the
13 state general fund. All interest earned from the investment of monies in the fund
14 shall be deposited in and credited to the fund. All unexpended and unencumbered
15 monies in the fund at the end of the fiscal year shall remain in the fund.
16 (D) Monies in the fund shall be appropriated only for the following:
17 (1) For the same purposes and under the same conditions as appropriation
18 of monies in the Budget Stabilization Fund.
19 (2) For the retiring or defeasance of bonds in advance or in addition to the
20 existing amortization requirements of the state.
21 (3) For payments against the unfunded accrued liability of one or more state
22 retirement systems which are in addition to any payments required for the annual
23 amortization of the unfunded accrued liability of the system; however, any such
24 payments shall not be used, directly or indirectly, to fund any benefit increase for
25 such system.
26 (4) For costs associated with actual project delivery, construction, and
27 maintenance of transportation and capital transit infrastructure projects of the state
28 and local government."
