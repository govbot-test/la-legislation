SLS 26RS-226 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE BILL NO. 104
BY SENATOR KLEINPETER
ROADS/HIGHWAYS. Designates a portion of Louisiana Highway 1 in Pointe Coupee
Parish as the "Ernest J. Gaines Memorial Highway". (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To provide relative to state highways; to designate a portion of Louisiana Highway 1 in
3 Pointe Coupee Parish as the "Ernest J. Gaines Memorial Highway"; to provide for
4 location and placement of signage; to provide for restrictions on costs of materials
5 to the department; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, the
8 portion of Louisiana Highway 1, beginning where Louisiana Highway 1 intersects with
9 Louisiana Highway 78, and ending at the Pointe Coupee Parish line before crossing
10 into Avoyelles Parish, is hereby designated as the "Ernest J. Gaines Memorial
11 Highway".
12 Section 2. The Department of Transportation and Development is hereby
13 directed to erect and maintain appropriate signs of this designation provided local or
14 private monies are received by the department equal to the department's actual costs
15 for material, fabrication, mounting posts, and installation of each sign, not to exceed
16 the sum of seven hundred fifty dollars per sign.
