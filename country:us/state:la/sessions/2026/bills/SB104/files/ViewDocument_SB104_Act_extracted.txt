1 AN ACT
2 To provide relative to state highways; to designate a portion of Louisiana Highway 1 in
3 Pointe Coupee Parish as the "Ernest J. Gaines Memorial Highway"; to designate a
4 portion of Louisiana Highway 16 in Livingston Parish as the "Deputy Katherine "Ms.
5 Katy" Wells Memorial Highway"; to provide for location and placement of signage;
6 to provide for restrictions on costs of materials to the department; and to provide for
7 related matters.
8 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
9 Section 1. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, the
10 portion of Louisiana Highway 1, beginning where Louisiana Highway 1 intersects with
11 Louisiana Highway 78, and ending at the Pointe Coupee Parish line before crossing
12 into Avoyelles Parish, is hereby designated as the "Ernest J. Gaines Memorial
13 Highway".
14 Section 2. Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, the
15 portion of Louisiana Highway 16, between Louisiana Highway 1033 and Louisiana
16 Highway 1026 in the city of Denham Springs, Livingston Parish, is hereby designated
17 as the "Deputy Katherine "Ms. Katy" Wells Memorial Highway".
18 Section 3. The Department of Transportation and Development is hereby
19 directed to erect and maintain appropriate signs of these designations provided local
