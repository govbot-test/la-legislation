2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 101
BY SENATOR KLEINPETER
A RESOLUTION
To commend the Honorable Judge Alvin Batiste Jr. on the occasion of his retirement from
the Eighteenth Judicial District Court and to recognize his distinguished service to
the state of Louisiana.
WHEREAS, the Honorable Judge Alvin Batiste Jr. is retiring after twenty-three years
of distinguished service as the presiding judge of Division "C" of the Eighteenth Judicial
District Court, serving the citizens of three parishes, Iberville, Pointe Coupee, and West
Baton Rouge with integrity, fairness, and dedication; and
WHEREAS, Judge Batiste has been on the bench since January 2003, is currently
serving as Chief Judge, and has presided over the Iberville Parish Drug Court for fifteen
years, demonstrating a deep commitment to justice, accountability, and rehabilitation; and
WHEREAS, a proud product of the Southern University School System, Judge
Batiste graduated from Southern Lab with honors in 1973, earned his Bachelor of Science
in Economics from Southern University and A&M College in 1976, and his Juris Doctor
from the Southern University Law Center in 1981 and was admitted to the Louisiana State
Bar that same year; and
WHEREAS, Judge Batiste was later inducted into the Southern University Law
Center's Hall of Fame in recognition of his outstanding contributions and dedicated service
to the legal profession, Southern University Law Center, and his community; and
WHEREAS, his professional career reflects a lifetime of public service, including
honorable service as an officer and lawyer in the United States Navy Judge Advocate
General Corps, as assistant public defender in East Baton Rouge Parish, assistant attorney
general handling cases for the Department of Environmental Quality, and the first chief
administrative officer for Iberville Parish; and
WHEREAS, Judge Batiste has demonstrated a steadfast commitment to civic
engagement and youth mentorship through initiatives such as Judges in the Classroom in
Iberville Parish, inspiring students and strengthening public trust in the judicial system; and

WHEREAS, Judge Batiste is married to Barbara Thomas Batiste and they are the
proud parents of nine children, whereby his devotion to family further exemplifies the
highest ideals of leadership and enriched values.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
does hereby commend the Honorable Judge Alvin Batiste Jr. on the occasion of his
retirement as judge of the Eighteenth Judicial District Court; and records and recognizes his
exemplary service to the state of Louisiana; extends to him sincere appreciation for his long,
dedicated, and distinguished service; and expresses sincere wishes for health and happiness
in all of his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
Honorable Judge Alvin Batiste Jr.
