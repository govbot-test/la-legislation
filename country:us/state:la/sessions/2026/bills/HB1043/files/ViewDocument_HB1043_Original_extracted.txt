HLS 26RS-1753 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE BILL NO. 1043
BY REPRESENTATIVE GREEN
COURTS/JUSTICE OF PEACE: Provides relative to civil jurisdictional amounts for parish
courts
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact Code of Civil Procedure Article 4842(A), relative to the jurisdictional
3 amount for parish courts; to provide for a jurisdictional amount of fifty thousand
4 dollars for parish courts; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. Code of Civil Procedure Article 4842(A) is hereby amended and
7 reenacted to read as follows:
8 Art. 4842. Parish court jurisdiction; amount in dispute; injunctive actions by a
9 political subdivision
10 A. Except as otherwise provided by law, the civil jurisdiction of a parish
11 court is concurrent with the district court in cases where the amount in dispute, or the
12 value of the property involved, does not exceed twenty fifty thousand dollars.

HB 1043 Original 2026 Regular Session Green
Abstract: Increases the civil jurisdictional amount for parish courts.
Present law provides that the parish courts have concurrent jurisdiction with the district
courts in cases where the amount in dispute does not exceed $20,000.
