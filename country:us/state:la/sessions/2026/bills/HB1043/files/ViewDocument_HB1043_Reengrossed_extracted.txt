HB 1043 Reengrossed 2026 Regular Session Green
Abstract: Increases the civil jurisdictional amount for the 1st and 2nd Parish Courts of
Jefferson Parish from $20,000 to $35,000.
Present law (R.S. 13:2561.2(A) and 2652.2(A)) provides for the civil jurisdictional limit of
$20,000 for the 1st and 2nd Parish Courts of Jefferson Parish.
Proposed law increases the jurisdictional limit for the 1st and 2nd Parish Courts of Jefferson
Parish from $20,000 to $35,000. Otherwise retains present law.
(Amends R.S. 13:2561.2(A) and 2562.2(A))

Summary of Amendments Adopted by House
The Committee Amendments Proposed by House Committee on Civil Law and
Procedure to the original bill:
1. Remove language that would revise the civil jurisdictional limits for all parish
courts.
2. Change the civil jurisdictional limit of the 1st and 2nd Parish Courts of Jefferson
Parish from $20,000 to $35,000.
3. Make technical changes.
The House Floor Amendments to the engrossed bill:
1. Remove references to present law regarding jurisdictional limits for eviction
proceedings that no longer apply to proposed law.
