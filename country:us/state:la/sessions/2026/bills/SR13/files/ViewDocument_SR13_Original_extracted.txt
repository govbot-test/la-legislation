SLS 26RS-272 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 13
BY SENATOR HODGES
Holden High School in Livingston Parish.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend and congratulate Parker Posey on being selected the 2025-2026 "Student of
3 the Year" at Holden High School in Livingston Parish.
4 WHEREAS, Parker Posey has been selected as the 2025–2026 "Student of the Year"
5 at Holden High School, an honor reserved for a student who exemplifies academic
6 excellence, leadership, character, and service; and
7 WHEREAS, Parker has distinguished himself as an exceptional scholar, maintaining
8 a 4.0385 grade point average, earning a composite ACT score of 30, and completing
9 thirty-six hours of college credit while still enrolled in high school; and
10 WHEREAS, Parker has demonstrated remarkable leadership through active
11 involvement in numerous student and civic organizations including Beta Club, Library Club,
12 Fellowship of Christian Athletes (FCA), 4-H, Future Farmers of America (FFA), and Student
13 Council, where he was selected by peers and faculty to serve in key leadership roles such as
14 FFA Voting Delegate, FCA Executive Leader, and Student Council Executive Officer; and
15 WHEREAS, Parker has excelled in athletics and mentorship, earning recognition as
16 a three-time state qualifier and first-place finisher in his weight class on the Holden High
17 School powerlifting team, while also serving as a coaching assistant for the school's baseball
18 team; and

1 WHEREAS, Parker's passion for history and government has led to exceptional
2 academic and civic accomplishments, including service as a page intern for the Louisiana
3 Legislature, recognition as Louisiana Beta Club State Speech Winner at the National Beta
4 Club Convention, and honors as a Louisiana Literary Rally District Winner and state
5 qualifier in history and government; and
6 WHEREAS, Parker's commitment to service and community well-being is further
7 reflected in his receipt of a Joe Burrow Foundation Grant to expand mental health wellness
8 initiatives at Holden High School, as well as his dedicated volunteer service with the Mighty
9 Moms Program and the USDA Food Bank at Killian Baptist Church; and
10 WHEREAS, Parker plans to pursue a degree in political science at either Tulane
11 University or Louisiana State University and to continue his education by earning a law
12 degree, with the goal of serving the people of the state of Louisiana; and
13 WHEREAS, inspired by his grandmother's military service and supported by his
14 parents, Darrel and Jessica Posey, Parker remains deeply rooted in his Holden High School
15 community and he embodies the values of service, integrity, and leadership.
16 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
17 does hereby commend and congratulate Parker Posey on being selected the 2025-2026
18 "Student of the Year" at Holden High School in Livingston Parish.
19 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to
20 Parker Posey.
