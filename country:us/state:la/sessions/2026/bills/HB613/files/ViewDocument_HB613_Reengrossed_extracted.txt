HB 613 Reengrossed 2026 Regular Session Horton
Abstract: Requires the office of motor vehicles to display an image of an eagle to signify
United States citizenship status on all driver's licenses and special identification
cards.
Present law (R.S. 32:410 and R.S. 40:1321) outlines the required information to be included
on driver's licenses and special identification cards issued by the office of motor vehicles
(OMV). Specifies that the required information include the individual's name, address,
photograph, physical description, and other identifying information.
Proposed law retains present law and requires the OMV include an image of an eagle in
flight to signify U.S. citizenship status on all driver's licenses and special identification cards
issued, provided an applicant's citizenship has been verified. Further requires the OMV
promulgate rules in accordance with the Administrative Procedure Act in implementing the
design, placement, and documentation requirements for verifying citizenship status.
Additionally, requires that any future upgrades or new card implementations by the OMV
accommodate new credentials on driver's licenses and special identification cards if the
current system is unable to do so.
(Adds R.S. 32:410(A)(3)(a)(x) and R.S. 40:1321(E)(3))
