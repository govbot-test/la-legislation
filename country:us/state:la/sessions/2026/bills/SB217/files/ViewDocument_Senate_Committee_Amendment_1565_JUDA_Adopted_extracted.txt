10 On page 7, delete lines 1 through 7, and insert the following:
11 "B.(1) Effective January 1, 2027, there shall be nine divisions of the
12 Criminal District Court. The divisions of the first four judges, if any, serving on
13 the effective date of this Paragraph, that provide written notice to the supreme
14 court and secretary of state by July 1, 2026, that they will not qualify to seek re-
15 election, shall be abolished.
16 (2) If a sufficient number of judges do not provide notice pursuant to
17 Paragraph (1) of this Subsection, then the requisite divisions comprising the
18 judges who have served on the court the least amount of time shall be
19 designated to be abolished at the end of their current term of office. If there is
20 any dispute concerning this calculation, the supreme court shall issue an order
21 to the secretary of state designating what divisions shall be abolished pursuant
22 to this Subsection.
23 (3) No qualifying shall be opened for any successor to a judgeship that
24 is abolished pursuant to this Subsection, and no election shall be held for any
25 judgeship abolished pursuant to this Subsection."
