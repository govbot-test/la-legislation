10 A. There shall be one civil district court for the parish of Orleans. It

15 B. Divisions K and L of the civil district court for the parish of Orleans are
16 hereby created for the purpose of nomination and election of the judges elected to fill
17 the two additional judgeships created by this Section. Judges elected to those
18 divisions shall be elected by the qualified voters of Orleans Parish and shall have

27 jurisdiction throughout the parish.
28 E. The judges elected to divisions M and N shall be elected for a six-year
29 term at the congressional election held in 1996 and every sixth year thereafter.
