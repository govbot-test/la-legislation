2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 57
BY REPRESENTATIVE LACOMBE
for Children's Miracle Network
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Jensen LeBlanc for being named a National Champion for Children's Miracle
3 Network.
4 WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize Jensen LeBlanc for promoting the
5 importance of children's healthcare and to commend him for being named a National
6 Champion for Children's Miracle Network; and
7 WHEREAS, Jensen LeBlanc is a vibrant and resilient eleven-year-old resident of
8 Addis, Louisiana, who has been a patient of Our Lady of the Lake (OLOL) Children's
9 Hospital since he was seven months old; and
10 WHEREAS, Jensen is a powerful advocate for pediatric patients, and his heartfelt
11 efforts have brought awareness to the importance of children's healthcare and the life-
12 changing impact that OLOL Children's Hospital has made in the greater Baton Rouge area;
13 and
14 WHEREAS, through his role as the Children's Miracle Network Champion for
15 OLOL Children's Hospital, Jensen serves as the main patient ambassador and has become
16 a local celebrity; he attends community events and corporate partner meetings and shares his
17 healthcare story and OLOL's impact to promote community and corporate fundraising for
18 the hospital; and
19 WHEREAS, Jensen has inspired countless individuals through his outreach efforts
20 and contributed significantly to increased community involvement and fundraising support
21 for the hospital's programs and services; and
Page 1 of 2
HLS 26RS-1760 ORIGINAL
HR NO. 57
1 WHEREAS, Addis Mayor David Toups presented Jensen with a key to the city in
2 recognition of his remarkable leadership and sense of civic responsibility; and
3 WHEREAS, Jensen serves as a 2026 National Champion for Children's Miracle
4 Network; he is one of nineteen patients across the United States who represent all one
5 hundred seventy local Children's Miracle Network hospitals in the country; and
6 WHEREAS, Jensen LeBlanc is a truly inspiring young man who is most deserving
7 of the highest recognition for his work to create positive change in the state and across the
8 country.
9 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
10 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Jensen LeBlanc for being named a National
11 Champion for Children's Miracle Network; does hereby express enduring appreciation for
12 his exceptional sense of civic duty in advocating for the importance of children's healthcare;
13 and does hereby extend sincere wishes that he remain prosperous in all of his endeavors.
