2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 141
BY REPRESENTATIVE HILFERTY
City Council District A
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Joseph Giarrusso, III, for his service to New Orleans City Council District A.
3 WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize Joseph Giarrusso, III, for his civic
4 leadership and to commend him for his service to New Orleans City Council District A; and
5 WHEREAS, Mr. Giarrusso dedicated eight years of service as the councilman
6 representing New Orleans City Council District A, and he previously served as president of
7 the Lakeview Civic Improvement Association; and
8 WHEREAS, Mr. Giarrusso served as New Orleans City Council's budget chair,
9 where he managed the city's finances, saved taxpayers hundreds of millions of dollars, and
10 protected vital public services; and
11 WHEREAS, Mr. Giarrusso's service was defined by his leadership, accessibility, and
12 commitment to fairness; he left an indelible mark on the Lakeview community and all of
13 District A; and
14 WHEREAS, Joseph Giarrusso, III, is most deserving of the highest recognition for
15 his integrity, vision, and devotion to the community he served.
16 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
17 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Joseph Giarrusso, III, for his service to New
18 Orleans City Council District A and does hereby extend sincere wishes that he remains
19 prosperous in his new role as first deputy mayor and chief administrative officer of the City
20 of New Orleans.
