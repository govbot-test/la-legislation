8 CHAPTER 43-B. PUBLIC SCHOOL LEARNING PODS
9 §4036.1. Learning Public school learning pods
10 A.(1) Each public school governing authority may establish public school
11 learning pods as an extension of any school under its jurisdiction.
12 (2) For purposes of this Chapter, "public school learning pod" means a group
13 of at least ten students enrolled in the school who receive instruction in a small group
14 setting.
15 B. A student shall be assigned to a public school learning pod only if the
16 assignment is requested by his parent or authorized by his parent upon the
17 recommendation of school officials.
