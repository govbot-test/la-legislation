DIGEST
SB 486 Original 2026 Regular Session Luneau
Present law provides for regulation of attorney advertising and prohibits certain misleading
or deceptive communications, including the use of nicknames, monikers, mottos, or trade
names that imply an ability to obtain results, and advertisements that promise results.
Proposed law retains present law and prohibits any advertisement for legal services through
unsolicited direct, live, or real-time telephone communication, including but not limited to
a phone bank, call center, or an automated dialing system when a significant motive for the
communication is pecuniary gain.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Adds R.S. 37:223(E)(3))
