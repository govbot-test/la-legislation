SENATE FLOOR AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
Representative Cox
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 6, between lines 9 and 10, insert the following:
3 "(7) Any dues, benefits, products, services, or programs provided by a
4 nonprofit corporation or trade association organized and operated for professional,
5 trade, business, or industry purposes to its members, when such dues, benefits,
6 products, services, or programs are provided in connection with the member's
7 professional, trade, occupational, commercial, or business activities and are not
8 offered primarily for personal, family, or household purposes.
9 (8) Any multiple listing service, real estate listing service, lockbox service,
10 data service, or other professional subscription service provided to subscribers,
11 participants, members, licensees, brokers, or other persons engaged in commercial,
12 professional, or business activities, when such service is not offered primarily for
13 personal, family, or household purposes."
