10 §416.20.1. Information; dissemination, sale, or possession of images of another
11 created by artificial intelligence; Louisiana Department of Education; public
12 school distribution
13 A. The principal of each public school shall disseminate information during
14 the first five days of each school year to each student grades six and above regarding
15 the crime and consequences of unlawful dissemination or sale of images of another
16 created by artificial intelligence as provided in R.S. 14:73.14 and unlawful
17 possession of images of another created by artificial intelligence as provided in R.S.
18 14:73.14.1. This information may be incorporated into an existing orientation
19 program. In addition to each student, the information shall be provided to each
20 student's parent or legal guardian on a written form containing a signature line for
21 each student's parent or legal guardian to sign to acknowledge receipt and
22 understanding of the information, which the parent or legal guardian shall return to
23 the school. The information required by this Section shall be age and grade
24 appropriate and shall give full consideration as to whether the student is in a regular
25 or special program of education.
26 B. The Louisiana Department of Education shall develop the information
27 prescribed by this Section and provide it to each public school governing authority,
28 which shall disseminate it to each principal for this purpose.
