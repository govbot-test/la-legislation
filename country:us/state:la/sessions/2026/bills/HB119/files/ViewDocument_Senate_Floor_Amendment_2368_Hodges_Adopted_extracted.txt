18 §73.13. Unlawful deepfakes
19 A.(1) Any person who, with knowledge that the material is a deepfake
20 depicting a minor, knowingly creates or possesses material that depicts a minor
21 engaging in sexual conduct shall be punished by imprisonment at hard labor for not
22 less than five nor more than twenty years, or a fine of not more than ten thousand
23 dollars, or both. At least five years of the sentence of imprisonment imposed shall
24 be served without benefit of parole, probation, or suspension of sentence.
25 (2) If the offender is an educator and the victim is a student, the offender
26 may be imprisoned for an additional two years with or without hard labor, to be
27 served consecutively to the sentence imposed under this Section.
28 * * *
29 C. For the purposes of this Section, the following terms have the following
30 meanings:
31 * * *
32 (3) "Educator" means any administrator, coach, instructor, paraprofessional,
33 student aide, teacher, or teacher aide at any public or nonpublic elementary school,
34 secondary school, or high school in this state who is assigned, employed, or working
35 at the school or school system where the victim is enrolled as a student on a
36 full-time, part-time, or temporary basis.
37 (3)(4) "Minor" means a person under the age of eighteen years.
38 (4)(5) "Sexual conduct" means any of the following, whether actual or
39 simulated: sexual intercourse, oral copulation, anal intercourse, anal oral copulation,
40 masturbation, bestiality, sexual sadism, sexual masochism, penetration of the vagina
41 or rectum by any object in a lewd or lascivious manner, exhibition of the genitals or
42 pubic or rectal area for the purpose of sexual stimulation of the viewer, or excretory
43 functions performed in a lewd or lascivious manner, whether or not any of the
44 conduct is performed alone or between members of the same or opposite sex or
45 between humans and animals. An act is simulated when it gives the appearance of
46 being actual sexual conduct.
