19 §73.13. Unlawful deepfakes
20 A.(1) Any person who, with knowledge that the material is a deepfake
21 depicting a minor, knowingly creates or possesses material that depicts a minor
22 engaging in sexual conduct shall be punished by imprisonment at hard labor for not
23 less than five nor more than twenty years, or a fine of not more than ten thousand
24 dollars, or both. At least five years of the sentence of imprisonment imposed shall
25 be served without benefit of parole, probation, or suspension of sentence.

7 §73.14.1. Unlawful possession of images of another created by artificial intelligence
8 A. It is unlawful for any person to possess any video or still image created
9 by artificial intelligence that depicts another person who is under the age of
10 seventeen years and is totally nude or in a state of undress so as to expose the
11 genitals, pubic area, buttocks, or female breasts, when the person possessing the
12 video or still image knows or has reason to know that the person is not licensed or
13 authorized to possess such video or still image.
14 B. For purposes of this Section, the following terms have the following
15 meanings:
16 (1) "Another person" includes a person whose image was used in creating,
17 adapting, or modifying a video or still image with the intent to depict an actual
18 person and who is recognizable as an actual person by the person's face, likeness, or
19 other distinguishing characteristic.
20 (2) "Artificial intelligence" means an artificial system developed in computer
21 software, physical hardware, or other context that solves tasks requiring human-like
22 perception, cognition, planning, learning, communication, or physical action.
23 (3) "Educator" means any administrator, coach, instructor, paraprofessional,
24 student aide, teacher, or teacher aide at any public or nonpublic elementary school,

27 full-time, part-time, or temporary basis.
28 (4) "Electronic mail service provider" means any person or entity, including
