HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 224
BY REPRESENTATIVE WRIGHT
occasion of his return visit to the La. House of Representatives
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend the Honorable Paul Hollis, director of the United States Mint, on the occasion
3 of his return visit to the Louisiana House of Representatives and to express enduring
4 gratitude for his outstanding contributions to the state of Louisiana.
5 WHEREAS, Paul Hollis served as a distinguished member of the House of
6 Representatives from 2012 to 2024; he is among Louisiana's most outstanding public
7 servants and holds a special place in the history of the state; and
8 WHEREAS, Mr. Hollis was nominated to serve as the director of the United States
9 Mint by President Donald J. Trump on July 16, 2025, and the United States Senate
10 confirmed him for the position on December 18, 2025; and
11 WHEREAS, Mr. Hollis took the oath of office on January 5, 2026, and became the
12 forty-first director of the United States Mint and the first Louisianian to hold this
13 distinguished position; and
14 WHEREAS, the Honorable Paul Hollis is most deserving of the highest recognition
15 for his dedicated service to the citizens of the United States and the state of Louisiana.
16 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
17 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend the Honorable Paul Hollis, director of the
18 United States Mint, on the occasion of his return visit to the Louisiana House of
19 Representatives; does hereby express enduring gratitude for his contributions to the nation
20 and to the state of Louisiana, particularly during his tenure as a distinguished member of the
21 House and during his service on the State Board of Elementary and Secondary Education;
22 and does hereby extend to him best wishes for continued success in his future endeavors.
