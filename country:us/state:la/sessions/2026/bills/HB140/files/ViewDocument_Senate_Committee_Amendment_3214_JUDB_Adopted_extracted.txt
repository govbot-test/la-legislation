17 "Art. 412. Confidentiality of records; disclosure exceptions; sanctions
18 * * *
19 Q. Nothing in this Section shall prohibit law enforcement officers,
20 prosecuting agencies, or judicial officers from obtaining records relating to
21 delinquency or status offenses proceedings which are relevant to an ongoing
22 delinquency or criminal investigation or proceeding. A court order shall not be
23 necessary for such access. However, the custodian of such records may seek a
24 protective order prohibiting or limiting the disclosure upon a showing of good cause
25 that such protection is necessary in the interests of justice. All such records produced
26 and the information contained therein shall be protected from disclosure by the
27 recipient outside of what is necessary to conduct the investigation, prosecution, or
28 adjudicatory action.
29 * * *"
