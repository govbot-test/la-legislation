HLS 26RS-404 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE BILL NO. 355
BY REPRESENTATIVE WYBLE AND SENATOR MIZELL
HIGHWAYS: Designates a portion of Louisiana Highway 10 from the Washington-
Tangipahoa Parish line extending east to the Louisiana-Mississippi state line as the
"Veterans Park Highway"
1 AN ACT
2 To designate a portion of Louisiana Highway 10 in Washington Parish as the "Veterans Park
3 Highway"; to provide for implementation; and to provide for related matters.
4 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
5 Section 1. The portion of Louisiana Highway 10 beginning at the Washington-
6 Tangipahoa parish line and extending east to the Louisiana-Mississippi state line shall be
7 known and is hereby designated as the "Veterans Park Highway".
8 Section 2. The Department of Transportation and Development is hereby directed
9 to erect and maintain appropriate signage reflecting this designation provided local or private
10 monies are received by the department equal to the department's actual costs for material,
11 fabrication, mounting posts, and installation of the signage, not to exceed the sum of seven
12 hundred fifty dollars per sign.

HB 355 Original 2026 Regular Session Wyble
Designates a portion of La. Hwy. 10, beginning at the boundary between Washington and
Tangipahoa Parishes and extending eastward to the state line between La. and MS., as the
"Veterans Park Highway".
Directs the Dept. of Transportation and Development (DOTD) to erect and maintain
appropriate signage reflecting the highway designation provided local or private monies are
received by the DOTD equal to its actual costs for material, fabrication, mounting posts, and
installation of the signage, not to exceed the sum of $750 per sign.
