HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 245
BY REPRESENTATIVE BAYHAM
CRIME: Condemns political violence in the United States
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To condemn political violence in the United States.
3 WHEREAS, it is appropriate to recognize the often volatile political division in the
4 United States and to condemn acts of politically-motivated violence; and
5 WHEREAS, attempts on any candidate or elected official's life cannot be tolerated;
6 curtailing political violence is essential for the health of the country; and
7 WHEREAS, unfortunately, there is a history of political violence in the United
8 States; and
9 WHEREAS, President Donald Trump has been the target of three separate
10 assassination attempts; he was grazed by a bullet during a July 2024 rally in Butler,
11 Pennsylvania, and faced a second attempt on his life in Florida later that year; the third
12 attempt came during the 2026 White House Correspondents' Dinner in Washington, D.C.;
13 and
14 WHEREAS, Minnesota House of Representatives Speaker Emerita Melissa Hortman
15 and her husband, Mark, were the victims of a targeted political assassination at their home
16 in June 2025; and
17 WHEREAS, Charlie Kirk, an American right-wing political activist, was assassinated
18 during a Turning Point USA event on the campus of Utah Valley University in Orem, Utah,
19 in September, 2025; and
20 WHEREAS, the state of Louisiana is familiar with acts of political violence; U.S.
21 Senator and former Louisiana Governor Huey P. Long was assassinated in the halls of the

1 state capitol on September 8, 1935; a bomb exploded in the Louisiana Senate Chamber in
2 the 1970s; U.S. House majority leader Steve Scalise was shot and wounded during the
3 annual Congressional Baseball Game in 2017; and
4 WHEREAS, political violence subverts the will of democracy and creates greater
5 division within the state and country, and it is vitally important that American citizens stand
6 against such acts.
7 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
8 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby condemn political violence in the United States and
9 does hereby encourage Americans to embrace discourse in the spirit of democracy as a
10 necessary alternative to acts of violence against elected officials and other political figures.
