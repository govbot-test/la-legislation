1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 17:3217.6, relative to community and technical education; to authorize any
3 institution under the supervision and management of the Board of Supervisors of
4 Community and Technical Colleges to establish a pilot program in partnership with
5 a local public school system; to provide for the purpose of the program; to subject
6 the partnership to the approval of the board of supervisors and the applicable city,
7 parish, or other local public school board; and to provide for related matters.
8 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
9 Section 1. R.S. 17:3217.6 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
10 §3217.6. Louisiana Community and Technical College System institutions;
11 partnership with local school systems; pilot program
12 Any institution under the supervision and management of the Board of
13 Supervisors of Community and Technical Colleges may establish a pilot program
14 in partnership with a city, parish, or other local public school system for the purpose
15 of increasing awareness of and access to commercial driver's license training
16 programs, entry level driver training programs, and transportation, distribution, and
17 logistics programs. Such partnership shall be subject to the approval of the board of
18 supervisors and the city, parish, or other local public school board.

HB 380 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Travis Johnson
Abstract: Authorizes any institution under the supervision and management of the Bd. of
Supervisors of Community and Technical Colleges to establish a pilot program with
a school system to increase awareness of and access to certain training programs.
Proposed law authorizes any system under the supervision and management of the Bd. of
Supervisors of Community and Technical Colleges to establish a pilot program in
partnership with a city, parish, or other local public school system for the purpose of
increasing awareness of and access to commercial driver's license training programs, entry
level driver training programs, and transportation, distribution, and logistics programs.
Subjects such partnership to the approval of the board of supervisors and the school board.
(Adds R.S. 17:3217.6)
Summary of Amendments Adopted by House
The Committee Amendments Proposed by House Committee on Education to the
original bill:
1. Apply proposed law authority to establish a pilot program with a public school
system to any institution under the supervision and management of the Bd. of
Supervisors of Community and Technical Colleges instead of only applying to
Sowela Technical Community College.
