Section 1:
HLS 26RS-1061 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE DOMANGUE
EDUCATION: Provides for students at École Pointe-au-Chien
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 17:1977.1, relative to École Pointe-au-Chien; to provide for the
3 grade levels to be served; and to provide for related matters.
4 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
5 Section 1. R.S. 17:1977.1 is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
6 §1977.1. École Pointe-au-Chien; creation; location; governance
7 There is hereby created École Pointe-au-Chien, a public French immersion
8 school for students in grades prekindergarten through four. eight. The school shall
9 be located in Terrebonne Parish. The school shall be independent of the control of
10 the state superintendent of education and of all local and state education boards
11 except its board of directors.
DIGEST
The digest printed below was prepared by House Legislative Services. It constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument. The keyword, one-liner, abstract, and digest do not constitute
part of the law or proof or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
HB 350 Original 2026 Regular Session Domangue
Abstract: Provides relative to École Pointe-au-Chien.
Present law creates École Pointe-au-Chien, an independent public French immersion school
in Terrebonne Parish for students in grades prekindergarten through four. Proposed law
increases the highest grade at the school from four to eight.
(Amends R.S. 17:1977.1)
