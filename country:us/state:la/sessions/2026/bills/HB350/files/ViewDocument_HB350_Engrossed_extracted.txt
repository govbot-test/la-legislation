HB 350 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Domangue
Abstract: Provides relative to École Pointe-au-Chien.
Present law creates École Pointe-au-Chien, an independent public French immersion school
in Terrebonne Parish for students in grades prekindergarten through four. Proposed law
increases the highest grade at the school from four to eight.
(Amends R.S. 17:1977.1)
