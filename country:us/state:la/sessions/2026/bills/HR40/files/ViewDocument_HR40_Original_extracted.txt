HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 40
BY REPRESENTATIVE MIKE JOHNSON
COMMENDATIONS: Commends Joe and Ann Blackwell on the occasion of their
seventieth wedding anniversary
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Joe and Ann Blackwell of Pineville, Rapides Parish, Louisiana, on the
3 occasion of their seventieth wedding anniversary.
4 WHEREAS, it is with great pride and admiration that the Louisiana House of
5 Representatives recognizes Joe and Ann Blackwell of Pineville in Rapides Parish as they
6 celebrate the extraordinary milestone of seventy years of marriage on June 29, 2026; and
7 WHEREAS, Joe and Ann Blackwell began a lifelong partnership when they united
8 in marriage on June 29, 1956, and their union stands as a shining example of love, devotion,
9 and unwavering commitment to one another; and
10 WHEREAS, throughout the Blackwell's seven decades of marriage, they have built
11 a beautiful life firmly rooted in faith in God, deep love for one another, steadfast dedication
12 to family, and the enduring values that strengthen both their home and community; and
13 WHEREAS, their marriage has been richly blessed with a growing family; they are
14 the proud parents of three children, cherished grandparents to twelve grandchildren, and
15 beloved great-grandparents to thirty-three great-grandchildren; each generation of their
16 family carries forward the legacy of love and guidance they have faithfully provided; and
17 WHEREAS, the Blackwell's seventy years together represent a remarkable testament
18 to perseverance, partnership, and the enduring power of family; they are an inspiration to all
19 who know them and serve as a meaningful example for future generations; and
