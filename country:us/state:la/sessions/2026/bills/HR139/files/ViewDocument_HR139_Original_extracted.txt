HLS 26RS-3091 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 139
BY REPRESENTATIVE HILFERTY
COMMENDATIONS: Commends Guy Williams, president of Gulf Coast Bank & Trust,
for his assistance to the Lakeview community in New Orleans following Hurricane
Katrina
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Guy Williams, president of Gulf Coast Bank & Trust, for his assistance to the
3 Lakeview community in New Orleans following Hurricane Katrina.
4 WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize Guy Williams for his work as president
5 of Gulf Coast Bank & Trust and to commend him for his assisting the Lakeview community
6 following Hurricane Katrina; and
7 WHEREAS, Mr. Williams made an exceptional personal effort to assist the
8 Lakeview neighborhood and its residents in the aftermath of Hurricane Katrina; and
9 WHEREAS, Mr. Williams provided much needed space for community meetings,
10 created a temporary office for the Lakeview Civic Improvement Association, and supported
11 fundraising efforts for local schools and charitable organizations; and
12 WHEREAS, Mr. Williams offered Gulf Coast Bank & Trust facilities and personnel
13 when the Lakeview community was in need; despite a lack of computers and functioning
14 systems, he opened the bank and ensured that residents were provided with immediate access
15 to financial relief; he also facilitated essential business and mortgage lending during the
16 crisis; and
17 WHEREAS, Mr. Williams and Gulf Coast Bank & Trust assisted the Lake
18 Pontchartrain Basin Foundation with the restoration of the New Canal Lighthouse, which
19 serves as the logo of the Lakeview Civic Improvement Association; and
