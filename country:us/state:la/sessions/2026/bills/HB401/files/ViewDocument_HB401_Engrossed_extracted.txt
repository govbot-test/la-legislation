HLS 26RS-1003 ENGROSSED
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE WRIGHT AND SENATOR MCMATH
BRIDGES/FERRIES: Designates the elevated portion of United States Highway 190 as the
"Corporal Justin D. McLeese Memorial Bridge"
1 AN ACT
2 To designate the elevated portion on United States Highway 190 over the Bogue Falaya
3 River in St. Tammany Parish as the "Corporal Justin D. McLeese Memorial Bridge";
4 to provide for implementation; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. The elevated portion on United States Highway 190 over the Bogue
7 Falaya River in the city of Covington, parish of St. Tammany shall be known and is hereby
8 designated as the "Corporal Justin D. McLeese Memorial Bridge".
9 Section 2. The Department of Transportation and Development or its contractors are
10 hereby directed to erect and maintain appropriate signage reflecting this designation
11 provided local or private monies are received by the department equal to the department's
12 actual cost for material, fabrication, mounting posts, and installation of the signage, not to
13 exceed the sum of two thousand two hundred fifty dollars per sign.
DIGEST
The digest printed below was prepared by House Legislative Services. It constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument. The keyword, one-liner, abstract, and digest do not constitute
part of the law or proof or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
HB 401 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Wright
Designates the bridge on U.S. Hwy. 190 over the Bogue Falaya River in the city of
Covington, parish of St. Tammany, as the "Corporal Justin D. McLeese Memorial Bridge".
Directs the Dept. of Transportation and Development (DOTD) or its contractors to erect and
maintain appropriate signage reflecting this highway designation provided local or private
monies are received by the DOTD equal to its actual costs for material, fabrication,
mounting posts, and installation of the signage, not to exceed the sum of $2,250 per sign.
