HB 803 Original 2026 Regular Session Bayham
Abstract: Increases the membership of the Board of Commissioners of the Port of New
Orleans.
Present law requires that the Board of Commissioners of the Port of New Orleans be
comprised of seven members, with four members having his principal place of business in
the parish of Orleans, with two members having his principal place of business in the parish
of Jefferson, and with one member having his principal place of business in the parish of St.
Bernard.
Proposed law increases the Board of Commissioners membership from seven to eight.
Further increases the parish of St. Bernard's membership from one to two.
Present law requires that all members be experienced in the commerce or industry, or both,
of the port area, when appointed prior to Aug. 1, 2018.
Proposed law modifies present law by requiring members be experienced in the commerce
or industry, or both, of the port area, who are appointed on or after Aug. 1, 2026.
Present law provides that the provisions of present law in effect on the date of any members
appointment prior to Aug. 1, 2018 must apply for their terms.
Proposed law modifies present law by changing the date from Aug. 1, 2018 to Aug. 1, 2026.
Present law requires that the seven present members appointed prior to Aug. 1, 2018
continue to serve on the board for the duration of their respective terms.
Proposed law modifies present law by changing the date from Aug. 1, 2018 to Aug. 1, 2026.
Present law provides that a person employed in the maritime industry and appointed on or
after to Aug. 1, 2018 may serve as a board member, subject to present law.
Proposed law modifies present law by changing the date from Aug. 1, 2018 to Aug. 1, 2026.
Present law requires the nominating organization, appointed by the governor, be only
selected from four different entities. Further provides that St. Bernard Parish have one
nominee.
Proposed law increases the nominees from one to two for the St. Bernard Parish nominating
organization.
(Amends R.S. 34:1(A)(1) and (3) and (B)(3))
