1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Louisiana Christian University on the occasion of its one hundred twentieth
3 anniversary and to recognize its longstanding legacy of academic excellence,
4 Christian service, and commitment to the people of Louisiana.
5 WHEREAS, Louisiana Christian University was founded in 1906 as Louisiana
6 College through the vision and support of Louisiana Baptists who sought to establish an
7 institution of higher learning grounded in Christian faith and academic excellence; and
8 WHEREAS, throughout its distinguished one hundred twenty-year history, the
9 university has faithfully fulfilled its mission of preparing graduates to worship intentionally,
10 learn diligently, steward faithfully, and lead courageously through a Christ-centered
11 education grounded in the pillars of worship, scholarship, stewardship, and leadership; and
12 WHEREAS, Louisiana Christian University is located in Pineville in Rapides Parish,
13 Louisiana, and has become a cornerstone institution for Central Louisiana and the state as
14 a whole that has produced generations of educators, ministers, healthcare professionals,
15 business leaders, public servants, military officers, and community leaders; and
16 WHEREAS, in 2021, the institution officially transitioned from Louisiana College
17 to Louisiana Christian University to reflect its continued growth, including expanded
18 academic offerings and graduate and professional programs, while preserving its historic
19 Christian identity and values; and

1 WHEREAS, during its one hundred twenty years of service, Louisiana Christian
2 University has maintained a steadfast commitment to faith, character, and educational
3 opportunity while adapting to meet the changing needs of students and communities
4 throughout Louisiana and beyond; and
5 WHEREAS, the university's faculty, administration, alumni, students, trustees, and
6 supporters have contributed immeasurably to the cultural, spiritual, educational, and
7 economic development of the state of Louisiana; and
8 WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper for the Louisiana House of Representatives to
9 recognize this remarkable milestone and to extend sincere appreciation to Louisiana
10 Christian University for one hundred twenty years of faithful service, educational leadership,
11 and dedication to developing future generations.
12 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
13 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Louisiana Christian University on the
14 occasion of its one hundred twentieth anniversary and does hereby recognize and honor the
15 university for its enduring contributions to higher education, Christian leadership, and the
16 people of the state of Louisiana.
