HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 365
BY REPRESENTATIVE MIKE JOHNSON
A RESOLUTION
To commend Louisiana Christian University on the occasion of its one hundred twentieth
anniversary and to recognize its longstanding legacy of academic excellence,
Christian service, and commitment to the people of Louisiana.
WHEREAS, Louisiana Christian University was founded in 1906 as Louisiana
College through the vision and support of Louisiana Baptists who sought to establish an
institution of higher learning grounded in Christian faith and academic excellence; and
WHEREAS, throughout its distinguished one hundred twenty-year history, the
university has faithfully fulfilled its mission of preparing graduates to worship intentionally,
learn diligently, steward faithfully, and lead courageously through a Christ-centered
education grounded in the pillars of worship, scholarship, stewardship, and leadership; and
WHEREAS, Louisiana Christian University is located in Pineville in Rapides Parish,
Louisiana, and has become a cornerstone institution for Central Louisiana and the state as
a whole that has produced generations of educators, ministers, healthcare professionals,
business leaders, public servants, military officers, and community leaders; and
WHEREAS, in 2021, the institution officially transitioned from Louisiana College
to Louisiana Christian University to reflect its continued growth, including expanded
academic offerings and graduate and professional programs, while preserving its historic
Christian identity and values; and
WHEREAS, during its one hundred twenty years of service, Louisiana Christian
University has maintained a steadfast commitment to faith, character, and educational
opportunity while adapting to meet the changing needs of students and communities
throughout Louisiana and beyond; and
WHEREAS, the university's faculty, administration, alumni, students, trustees, and
supporters have contributed immeasurably to the cultural, spiritual, educational, and
economic development of the state of Louisiana; and
