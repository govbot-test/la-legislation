HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 84
BY REPRESENTATIVES WILEY, BACALA, BRASS, AND EDMONSTON AND
SENATORS LAMBERT AND PRICE
COMMENDATIONS: Commends Barney Arceneaux on the occasion of his retirement
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 To commend Barney Arceneaux on the occasion of his retirement.
3 WHEREAS, it is with immense pride and profound gratitude that the Legislature of
4 Louisiana recognizes Barney Arceneaux for dedicating more than five decades to public
5 service and to commend him on the occasion of his retirement; and
6 WHEREAS, Mr. Arceneaux served in law enforcement from 1972 to 2005; he served
7 as the assistant chief of the Gonzales Police Department from 1979 to 1983 and as chief of
8 the department from 1984 to 1992; he worked in major criminal operations with the
9 Ascension Parish Sheriff's Office from 1993 to 1999, served as a sergeant at arms for the
10 Louisiana House of Representatives in 2004, and worked with the Southern Anti-Terrorist
11 Regional Training Academy from 2004 to 2005; and
12 WHEREAS, Mr. Arceneaux served as the director of planning and development for
13 Ascension Parish from 1999 to 2004 and worked as the district director for Congressman
14 Charlie Melancon from 2005 to 2008; and
15 WHEREAS, Mr. Arceneaux served from 2008 to 2024 as mayor of the city of
16 Gonzales, a role in which he provided a vision and direction for the city's growth and quality
17 of life; during his tenure as mayor, he served as the president of the Louisiana Municipal
18 Association from 2016 to 2017; and
19 WHEREAS, Mr. Arceneaux most recently served as the executive director of the
20 Louisiana Municipal Association, where he provided mentorship and guidance to local
21 elected officials and staff and strengthened the relationship between the state and local

15 of Louisiana; and does hereby extend sincere wishes that he remains prosperous as he begins
16 a new phase of life in retirement.
