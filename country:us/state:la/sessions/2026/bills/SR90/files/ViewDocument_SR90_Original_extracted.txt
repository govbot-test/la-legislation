SLS 26RS-2197 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 90
BY SENATOR PRESSLY
COMMENDATIONS. Commends and congratulates Gerald "Jerry" Badgley on the occasion
of his retirement as principal of C.E. Byrd High School in Shreveport, Louisiana.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend and congratulate Mr. Gerald "Jerry" Badgley for his extraordinary
3 contributions to the Shreveport community and to offer congratulations and best
4 wishes upon the occasion of his retirement after more than twenty years of dedicated
5 service as principal of C.E. Byrd High School in Shreveport, Louisiana.
6 WHEREAS, Principal Badgley is a graduate of the C.E. Byrd High School Class of
7 1976; and
8 WHEREAS, he returned to the classroom at his alma mater in the 1980s and has
9 served as principal since 2005; and
10 WHEREAS, Principal Badgley's unmatched leadership has witnessed growth,
11 change, and continued excellence while honoring the traditions that make C.E. Byrd High
12 School special; and
13 WHEREAS, he has been recognized as a C.E. Byrd Athletic Hall of Fame Inductee,
14 an Educator of Distinction, and Principal of the Year; and
15 WHEREAS, Principal Badgley's career reflects a lifelong commitment to C.E. Byrd
16 High School and to public education in Shreveport, Louisiana; and
17 WHEREAS, he has played a pivotal role in the growth and transformation of C.E.
18 Byrd High School, fostering significant advancements in school curriculum as a result of his

1 leadership and guidance, including a new football practice field and enhanced facilities; and
2 WHEREAS, Principal Badgley's efforts have highlighted the impact of school
3 leaders in the community, whose alumni include current Louisiana State Senators, Thomas
4 A. Pressly and Sam L. Jenkins Jr.; former state legislators including Senator Virginia
5 Kilpatrick Shehee, Senator Phil Short, Senator and Judge Robert Kostelka; former state
6 representatives Art Sour, Frank Fulco, and James C. Gardner; United States Senator J.
7 Bennett Johnston Jr., Saxby Chambliss, a United States Senator from Georgia; John Howard
8 Dalton, former United States Secretary of the Navy; Brandon Friedman, former deputy
9 assistant secretary of the United States Department of Housing and Urban Development;
10 Tom Jarriel, ABC News journalist; United States District Judge Tom Stagg; United States
11 Circuit Court Judge Jacques L. Wiener Jr.; Louisiana Supreme Court Justices Jeffrey P.
12 Victory and Pike Hall Jr.; and many other outstanding public servants; and
13 WHEREAS, C.E. Byrd High School recently celebrated its 100th graduating class
14 on June 5th through 7th, 2025, culminating in the "Ultimate Reunion" on June 7, 2025; and
15 WHEREAS, Principal Badgley's career is a testament to his unwavering
16 commitment, visionary leadership, technical expertise, and genuine care for the scholars at
17 C.E. Byrd High School and the Shreveport academic community at large; and
18 WHEREAS, the community could not be more grateful for Principal Badgley's
19 contributions, leadership, loyalty, and the legacy he leaves behind.
20 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
21 does hereby commend and congratulate Mr. Gerald "Jerry" Badgley on the occasion of his
22 retirement as principal of C.E. Byrd High School and for his extraordinary contributions to
23 the Shreveport community, and does hereby extend "Yellow Jacket" best wishes for a happy
24 and rewarding future.
25 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to C.E.
26 Byrd High School Principal Gerald "Jerry" Badgley.
