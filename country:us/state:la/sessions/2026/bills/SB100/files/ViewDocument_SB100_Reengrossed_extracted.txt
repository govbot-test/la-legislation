Section 1:
SLS 26RS-204 REENGROSSED
2026 Regular Session
BY SENATOR JENKINS
AUTOMOBILE INSURANCE. Provides for proof of insurance of transportation network
companies involved in an accident. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 45:201.9, relative to proof of insurance by transportation
3 network company drivers; to provide that a transportation network company driver
4 shall provide proof of insurance coverage in the event of an accident; to provide that
5 a transportation network company driver shall disclose whether he was logged on to
6 the transportation network company's digital network or on a prearranged ride at the
7 time of an accident; to provide for penalties; and to provide for related matters.
8 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
9 Section 1. R.S. 45:201.9 is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
10 §201.9. Proof of insurance
11 A transportation network company driver shall carry written or digital proof
12 of coverage satisfying R.S. 45:201.6 with him at all times during his use of a vehicle
13 in connection with a transportation network company's digital network. In the event
14 of an accident, a transportation network company driver shall provide this insurance
15 coverage information to the directly interested parties, automobile insurers, and
16 investigating police officers, upon request pursuant to R.S. 32:863.1. Upon such
17 request, a transportation network company driver shall also disclose to directly

DIGEST
SB 100 Reengrossed 2026 Regular Session Jenkins
Present law requires a transportation network company driver carry proof of coverage of
insurance.
Present law requires a transportation network company driver to provide insurance coverage
information, in the event of an accident, to directly interested parties, automobile insurers,
and investigating officer only upon request.
Proposed law provides for penalties for failure to provide the required information.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 45:201.9)
Summary of Amendments Adopted by Senate
Senate Floor Amendments to engrossed bill

Section 2:
1. Restores requirement that, pursuant to R.S. 32:863.1, a transportation
network driver shall disclose proof of insurance through the transportation
network company upon request in the event of an accident.
