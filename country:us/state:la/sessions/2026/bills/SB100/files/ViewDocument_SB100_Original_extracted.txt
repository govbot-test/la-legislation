AUTOMOBILE INSURANCE. Provides for proof of insurance of transportation network
companies involved in an accident. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 45:201.9, relative to proof of insurance by transportation
3 network company drivers; to provide that a transportation network company driver
4 shall provide proof of insurance coverage in the event of an accident; to provide that
5 a transportation network company driver shall disclose whether he was logged on to
6 the transportation network company's digital network or on a prearranged ride at the
7 time of an accident; to provide for penalties; and to provide for related matters.
8 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
9 Section 1. R.S. 45:201.9 is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:

DIGEST
SB 100 Original 2026 Regular Session Jenkins
Present law requires a transportation network company driver carry proof of coverage of
insurance.
Present law requires a transportation network company driver to provide insurance coverage
information, in the event of an accident, to directly interested parties, automobile insurers,
and investigating officer only upon request. Proposed law requires the driver to provide
insurance coverage information without the requirement of a request.
Present law requires a transportation network company driver to disclose upon request to
directly interested parties, automobile insurers, and investigating officer whether the driver
was logged on to the transportation network company's digital network or on a prearranged
ride at the time of an accident. Proposed law requires the driver to provide this information
without the requirement of a request.
Proposed law provides for penalties for failure to provide the required information.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 45:201.9)
