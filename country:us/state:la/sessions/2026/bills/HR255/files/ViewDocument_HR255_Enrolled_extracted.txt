2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 255
CHASSION, GLORIOSO, HEBERT, JACOB LANDRY, AND SPELL
A RESOLUTION
To commend and congratulate the students and faculty of the Lafayette Parish School
System for their first place finish in the Louisiana Governor's Games Thirty-First
Annual Elementary State Championship Fitness Meet.
WHEREAS, the Louisiana Governor's Games are the state's premier amateur
sporting event; and
WHEREAS, the Lafayette Parish School System finished first overall in the Thirty-
First annual Elementary State Championship Fitness Meet; and
WHEREAS, Lafayette secured first place by scoring a total of 2,479 points, which
outpaced the 2,459 points scored by second-place St. Tammany and the 2,428 points scored
by third-place Rapides; and
WHEREAS, the elementary fitness meet competition involves seven fitness-related
events: the fifty-yard dash, the shuttle run, pull-ups, the standing long-jump, curl-ups or
sit-ups, the six hundred-yard run, and the V-sit and reach; and
WHEREAS, the top two boys and girls from each parish compete in Alexandria
annually for the fitness title; and
WHEREAS, Lafayette's top performer, Tianna Franks, led the way with six hundred
forty-eight individual points; she and her teammates, Brennon Poullard, Amir Alsayed, and
Aubrey Porter, anchored their parish to victory; and
WHEREAS the elementary fitness meet has served as the Olympic Games for
elementary school-aged children in thirty parishes across the state since 1997, and more than
two hundred thousand students have competed for the state fitness crown.

HR NO. 255 ENROLLED
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend and congratulate the students, coaches, and
parents of the Lafayette Parish School System on their first place finish in the Louisiana
Governor's Games Thirty-First Annual Elementary State Championship Fitness Meet and
does hereby encourage all of the participants to continue their excellent work and always
strive for greatness in competition.
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
