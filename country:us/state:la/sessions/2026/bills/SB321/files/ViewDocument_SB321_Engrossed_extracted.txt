BY SENATOR KLEINPETER
CRIMINAL PROCEDURE. Provides relative to electronic bonds. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact Code of Criminal Procedure Art. 328(C), relative to electronic bonds;
3 to provide for certain conditions on the use of electronic bonds; and to provide for
4 related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. Code of Criminal Procedure Art. 328(C) is hereby amended and reenacted
7 to read as follows:
8 Art. 328. Bail undertaking
9 * * *
10 C.(1) An electronic bond may shall only be executed in a parish where the
11 sheriff has electronic bond software in place. Agents Only agents may be approved
12 by the sheriff to shall execute an electronic bond. A licensed bail bond producer
13 applying for electronic bond authority may shall make an application to the sheriff
14 and shall be domiciled and maintain a principal place of business in this state and
15 possess a Louisiana bail bond producer license for at least three years preceding the
16 date of application.
17 (2) The approving sheriff authority may require any documents deemed

2 (3) Any parish that elects to implement electronic bonds shall allow both

DIGEST
SB 321 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Kleinpeter
Present law allows for the posting of electronic bonds. Proposed law retains present law.
Proposed law requires any parish implementing the use of electronic bonds to also allow the
use of in-person bonds.
Proposed law requires the provider of an electronic bond to incorporate a geo fence not to
exceed 10 miles from the jail facility. Further requires the implementation of FACE
Recognition Liveness.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Amends C.Cr.P. Art. 328(C))
