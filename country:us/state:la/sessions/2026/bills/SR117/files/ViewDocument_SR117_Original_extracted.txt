SLS 26RS-2612 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 117
BY SENATOR KLEINPETER
2026 LHSAA Division II Non-Select State Championship.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend the Brusly High School boys basketball team upon winning the 2026 LHSAA
3 Division II Non-Select State Championship.
4 WHEREAS, the Brusly High School Panthers boys basketball team has achieved the
5 highest level of success in Louisiana high school athletics by capturing the 2026 Louisiana
6 High School Athletic Association (LHSAA) Division II Non-Select State Championship
7 title; and
8 WHEREAS, the Panthers secured the championship with a hard-fought 45-38 victory
9 over the two-time defending champion Wossman Wildcats on March 13, 2026, at Burton
10 Coliseum in Lake Charles; and
11 WHEREAS, this victory marks only the second state championship in program
12 history for Brusly High School and the first since 1984, making the achievement especially
13 meaningful for the school and its community; and
14 WHEREAS, the Panthers' championship performance was highlighted by senior
15 forward Ben Radford, who was named Most Outstanding Player after a dominant showing
16 of 17 points, 17 rebounds, and 7 blocks; and
17 WHEREAS, senior Micah Bryant made critical contributions to the victory, sparking
18 a decisive 7-0 run in the third quarter and finishing with 12 points, including a key
Page 1 of 2

1 three-pointer to begin the fourth quarter; and
2 WHEREAS, senior Kazi Murray added a crucial layup with 54 seconds remaining
3 to extend the Panthers' lead and help secure the championship; and
4 WHEREAS, the team's defensive effort and composure under pressure proved
5 decisive, as head coach Kirby Loupe credited the Panthers' ability to handle Wossman's
6 defensive intensity as the key to the game, particularly following their semifinal loss to the
7 Wildcats in the previous season; and
8 WHEREAS, the Panthers displayed exceptional teamwork, resilience, and
9 sportsmanship, representing Brusly High School and the community of West Baton Rouge
10 Parish with pride and distinction; and
11 WHEREAS, the team's success reflects the outstanding leadership and commitment
12 of its coaching staff, whose guidance and mentorship have been instrumental in developing
13 both the athletic and personal character of the players; and
14 WHEREAS, the support of families, faculty, students, and the Brusly community
15 played a vital role in encouraging and sustaining the team's championship run; and
16 WHEREAS, the Brusly High School basketball team's achievement serves as an
17 inspiration to future student-athletes and stands as a proud moment in the school's history.
18 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
19 does hereby commend the Brusly High School boys basketball team on winning the 2026
20 LHSAA Division II Non-Select State Championship and further recognizes the players,
21 coaches, and supporters for their outstanding accomplishment.
22 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
23 principal of Brusly High School and the head coach of the boys basketball team.
The original instrument and the following digest, which constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument, were prepared by Senate Legislative Services.
