HB 71 Draft 2026 Regular Session Carlson
Abstract: Provides that armed security officers are authorized persons for concealed
handgun permits and subject to a limitation of liability in certain circumstances.
Present law (R.S. 9:2793.12) provides a limitation of liability for damages related to the
justified use of force or self-defense through the discharge of a firearm by an authorized
person.
Proposed law retains present law and adds "armed security officers" as authorized persons.
(Adds R.S. 9:2793.12(A)(3))
