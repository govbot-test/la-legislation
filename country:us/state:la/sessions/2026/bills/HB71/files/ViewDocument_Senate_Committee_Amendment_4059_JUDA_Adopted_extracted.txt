SCAHB71 359 4059
SENATE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
71 by Representative Carlson
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, line 2, change "enact R.S. 9:2793.12(A)(3)," to "amend and reenact R.S.
3 9:2793.12(B) and to enact R.S. 9:2793.12(A)(3),"
4 AMENDMENT NO. 2
5 On page 1, line 6, change "R.S. 9:2793.12(A)(3)" to "R.S. 9:2793.12(B) is hereby amended
6 and reenacted and R.S. 9:2793.12(A)(3)"
7 AMENDMENT NO. 3
8 On page 1, between lines 13 and 14, insert the following:
9 "B. An authorized person as defined in this Section shall not be liable for
10 damages for any injury, death, or loss suffered by a perpetrator when the injury,
11 death, or loss is caused by a justified use of force or self-defense through the
12 discharge of a firearm by the authorized person. An armed security officer is entitled
13 to the limitation of liability provided for in this Section only if the perpetrator is
14 wielding a dangerous weapon as defined in R.S. 14:2 at the time the armed security
15 officer injures the perpetrator. This provision shall preclude any right of action by
16 the perpetrator, his survivors, or his heirs."
