2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 126
BY REPRESENTATIVES LAFLEUR AND FREIBERG
of Louisiana Day at the state capitol
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate Wednesday, April 15, 2026, as Child Care Association of Louisiana Day at the
3 state capitol and to commend childcare business owners, early childhood educators,
4 and support staff for their outstanding work.
5 WHEREAS, the Child Care Association of Louisiana is the longest-standing,
6 statewide nonprofit organization and represents the interests of licensed childcare providers
7 and the children they serve; and
8 WHEREAS, the mission of the Child Care Association is to educate, advocate, and
9 collaborate to build a premier and proactive childcare industry for Louisiana families; and
10 WHEREAS, the Child Care Association of Louisiana strives to improve the quality
11 and availability of childcare services by calling attention to the importance of high quality
12 childcare services for all children and families within the state; and
13 WHEREAS, Louisiana's future depends on the quality of the early childhood
14 experiences provided to our children; and
15 WHEREAS, providing high quality early childcare services represents a worthy
16 commitment to the future of Louisiana children; and
17 WHEREAS, Tara Emery, president of the Child Care Association of Louisiana, has
18 stated that "Every child should have access to a high quality early childhood educational
19 program and be prepared to enter kindergarten ready to learn", and it is most fitting to
20 recognize both her and the association for their outstanding efforts to achieve this mission.

HLS 26RS-3503 ORIGINAL
HR NO. 126
1 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
2 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby designate Wednesday, April 15, 2026, as Child Care
3 Association of Louisiana Day at the state capitol; does hereby commend childcare business
4 owners, early childhood educators, and support staff for their incredible efforts; and does
5 hereby urge all citizens of our great state to recognize licensed childcare providers for their
6 important work.
DIGEST
