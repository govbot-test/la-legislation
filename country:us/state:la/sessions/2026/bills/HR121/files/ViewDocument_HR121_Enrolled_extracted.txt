HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 121
BY REPRESENTATIVE MUSCARELLO
A RESOLUTION
To commend the Italian Festival Royal Court on the occasion of the 2026 Italian Festival.
WHEREAS, the Italian Festival is a unique event held each April in Tickfaw,
Louisiana, that has become a beloved tradition for both locals and visitors alike; and
WHEREAS, the original festival was organized in 1973 and was known as "The
Little Italy Festival"; the village of Tickfaw began hosting the Italian Festival in 2009; and
WHEREAS, citizens of Italian descent make up a significant portion of south
Louisiana's population, and their communities have had a tremendous impact on Louisiana
culture, from architectural masonry and foodways to jazz and brass band music; and
WHEREAS, the annual Italian Festival in Tickfaw is a family-friendly celebration
of Italian-American culture and heritage featuring carnival rides, entertainment from local
bands, a parade, a ball, and a pageant; there is a spaghetti cook-off where judges select
winners in the categories of gravy, meatballs, and table decorations; and
WHEREAS, Lily DiBenedetto, a young woman of many gifts and talents, reigns as
the 2026 Italian Festival Queen; Kim Notariano reigns as the Spaghetti Queen; Veda Abene
reigns as Grand Marshal; and Carlos Notariano reigns as King; and
WHEREAS, the 2026 Italian Festival royal court also includes Teen Queen, Kailyn
DeMarco; Junior Queen, Bayley Thompson; Deb Queen, Mila Fonseca; Little Queen, Joanna
Oliphant; Petite Queen, Amelia Prima; Tiny Queen, Raelyn Wright; Toddler Queen,
Lakelynn Wright; and Princess, Victoria Bender; Robert Fugarino serves as the chairperson
for this year's festival; and
WHEREAS, the 2026 Italian Festival is being held April 24-26, 2026; and

WHEREAS, the organizers, volunteers, and royal court of the 2026 Italian Festival
in Tickfaw merit a sincere and heartfelt measure of commendation for the tremendous pride
and honor that this wonderful annual event brings to the citizens of Louisiana.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend the Italian Festival Royal Court on the
occasion of the 2026 Italian Festival in Tickfaw, Louisiana, and does hereby recognize this
special event as an integral part of the celebration of Italian-American culture and history
in the state of Louisiana.
