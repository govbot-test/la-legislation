HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 235
BY REPRESENTATIVE YOUNG
A RESOLUTION
To commend the Haynesville High School football team on winning the Louisiana High
School Athletic Association 2025 Division IV Non-Select state championship.
WHEREAS, the Haynesville High School Golden Tornado football team captured
the 2025 Division IV Non-Select state title by defeating Mangham, 39-37, in the
championship game at the Caesar's Superdome; and
WHEREAS, the victory gives the Haynesville football program its eighteenth state
championship and first since 2014; and
WHEREAS, Haynesville's Isaiah Washington was recognized as the Most Valuable
Player of the championship after rushing for eighty-seven yards and two touchdowns and
returning an interception for another touchdown; Elijah Robinson also rushed for ninety-six
yards and two touchdowns and returned a fumble for another touchdown; and
WHEREAS, Haynesville is led by head coach David Franklin; under his leadership,
the Golden Tornado has won titles in 2009, 2013, 2014, and 2025; Coach Franklin's father
is the legendary Haynesville football coach Alton "Red" Franklin who led the program to
eleven of its eighteen state titles; and
WHEREAS, Coach Franklin and the members of the Golden Tornado football team
are deserving of recognition for adding to the outstanding legacy of the Haynesville football
program.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend the Haynesville High School football team
on winning the Louisiana High School Athletic Association 2025 Division IV Non-Select
state championship and does hereby encourage the members of the team to continue their
excellent work and always strive for greatness in competition.
