SLS 26RS-1110 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 74
BY SENATOR KLEINPETER
COMMENDATIONS. Commends and congratulates Mrs. Elizabeth Kimball Lewis on
turning 100 years old.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Mrs. Elizabeth Kimball Lewis on the occasion of her 100th birthday.
3 WHEREAS, Mrs. Elizabeth Kimball Lewis was born on March 10, 1926, in
4 Maringouin, Louisiana, to her loving parents, Georgie and H.M. Kimball Sr.; and
5 WHEREAS, she attended the tiny Shady Grove Elementary School, housed on a
6 plantation, where her love for music on the radio inspired her to take dance and piano
7 lessons; and
8 WHEREAS, she began attending Rosedale United Methodist Church with her family
9 as a child, and continued to play the piano there for many years until the church's closure in
10 2016; and
11 WHEREAS, she continued her education at Shady Grove High School, where she
12 sang in the Shady Grove Girl's Chorus, before graduating in 1943 and attending Soule
13 Business College in New Orleans; and
14 WHEREAS, following her schooling, she worked for Rathborne, Hair, and Ridgway
15 veneer company, and later for H.M. Kimball Esso, also known as Kimball Oil Company,
16 from which she retired in 1999 after a dedicated career; and
17 WHEREAS, in 1943, she met the love of her life, Roy Lewis, whom she married on
18 June 12, 1946, and enjoyed sixty-seven wonderful years together; and

1 WHEREAS, she and her husband opened and operated Roy's Cafe in Livonia,
2 Louisiana, which became a fixture in the community; and
3 WHEREAS, Mrs. Lewis and her husband were blessed with four children, eleven
4 grandchildren, three step-grandchildren, twenty-one great-grandchildren, eleven
5 step-great-grandchildren, and two great-great-grandchildren; and
6 WHEREAS, she has been an active member of multiple community organizations,
7 including the Maringouin Home Demonstration Club, serving on the board of Rosedale
8 United Methodist Church, the Maringouin Twin Bayous Lioness Club, the Maringouin Lions
9 Club, and the Eastern Stars; and
10 WHEREAS, at one hundred years young, Mrs. Lewis finds joy in watching wildlife
11 in her backyard and, above all, cherishes spending time with her family, friends, and beloved
12 chihuahua, Lola; and
13 WHEREAS, her life exemplifies faith, love, resilience, and service, while leaving an
14 indelible mark on the community of Maringouin through her kindness, dedication, and
15 prosperous journey.
16 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
17 does hereby commend and congratulate Mrs. Elizabeth Kimball Lewis on the joyous
18 occasion of her one hundredth birthday and honor her for an extraordinary life filled with
19 faith, enduring love, and meaningful contributions to her family, friends, and community.
20 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to Mrs.
21 Elizabeth Kimball Lewis.
