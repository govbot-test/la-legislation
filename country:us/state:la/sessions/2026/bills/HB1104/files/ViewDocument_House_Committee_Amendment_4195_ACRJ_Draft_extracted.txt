11 "or emulating device or a relay attack device. No person shall knowingly possess such a

13 commit a theft, unauthorized entry into a motor vehicle, or any other criminal offense.
14 (a) Possession alone of a motor vehicle key programming or emulating
15 device or a relay attack device does not constitute a violation of this Section.
16 (b) Criminal intent may be inferred from the circumstances, including but
17 not limited to the following:
18 (i) Possession while attempting to enter a motor vehicle without

20 (ii) Possession of multiple such devices without a lawful purpose.
21 (iii) Possession in conjunction with tools commonly used for vehicle theft."
