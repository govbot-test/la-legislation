A RESOLUTION
Lakeview community in New Orleans following Hurricane Katrina.
WHEREAS, it is fitting to recognize Todd Wallace for his outstanding civic-
mindedness and to commend him for his leadership during the rebuilding of the Lakeview
neighborhood in the wake of Hurricane Katrina; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Wallace, a former president of the Lakeview Civic Improvement
Association, played a pivotal role during the recovery of Lakeview, supported numerous
local businesses, and fostered unity within the community following the devastation wrought
by Hurricane Katrina; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Wallace was integral to the success of the Lot Next Door Program
and the Lakeview-specific Phase II Property Program, both of which transformed blighted
lots into revitalized properties and valuable green spaces and protected property values in
the Lakeview neighborhood; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Wallace's efforts helped to maintain the integrity of Lakeview,
fostered responsible development, and supported the neighborhood's recovery; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Wallace is a founding member of the Friends of Lakeview, which
helped initiate the redevelopment of the New Basin Canal Park; the two-mile stretch of
property, which was destroyed by Hurricane Katrina, has been restored with trees, walkway
lanterns, and sidewalks; and
WHEREAS, Todd Wallace is deserving of appreciation and recognition for inspiring
locals to join the efforts to restore Lakeview and leave a lasting positive impact on the
community.

HR NO. 137 ENROLLED
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Todd Wallace for his leadership during the
recovery and revitalization of the Lakeview community in New Orleans following Hurricane
Katrina and does hereby wish him continued success and happiness in all of his future
endeavors.

and the Lakeview-specific Phase II Property Program, both of which transformed blighted
lots into revitalized properties and valuable green spaces and protected property values in
