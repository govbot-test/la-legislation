SLS 26RS-707 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 28
BY SENATOR BASS
CONDOLENCES. Expresses sincere and heartfelt condolences upon the passing of Patricia
Ann McKinney Pendergrass.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To express the sincere and heartfelt condolences of the Senate of the Legislature of
3 Louisiana upon the death of Patricia Ann McKinney Pendergrass.
4 WHEREAS, it is with profound sorrow that the Senate of the Legislature of
5 Louisiana has learned of the passing of Patricia "Patty" Ann McKinney Pendergrass on
6 February 23, 2026, at the age of seventy-one; and
7 WHEREAS, Patty was born on June 11, 1954, and was the first born of her parents,
8 George David McKinney and Jo Ann Bryan McKinney; and
9 WHEREAS, following her graduation from Louisiana Tech University, Patty
10 answered the call to serve her nation in the United States Air Force from 1976 until 1980,
11 attaining the rank of captain, exemplifying commitment, dedication, and patriotism; and
12 WHEREAS, Patty spent most of her life in her beloved community of Minden,
13 Louisiana, where she was deeply involved in community service, engaged in many of the
14 ministries of First Baptist Church of Minden, and devoted to her students at Glenbrook
15 School; and
16 WHEREAS, Patty was exceptional, capable, conscientious, and compassionate, and
17 all her actions were guided by three great loves: her relationship with Jesus Christ as her
18 Lord and Savior, deep devotion to her family, and abiding love for her hometown of

1 Minden; and
2 WHEREAS, she was persistent and determined, traits she inherited from her
3 grandmother, Elsie Bryan, and when diagnosed with rheumatoid arthritis at the age of
4 twenty-four, Patty never let the disease define her by living a life exhibiting great
5 perseverance, courage, and resilience, which inspired many; and
6 WHEREAS, Patty is survived by her devoted husband of forty-eight years, Ty
7 Pendergrass; three sons: Tristan, Jeremy, and Andy; grandchildren: Luke, Lillian, Jacob,
8 Calvin, Alfred, Josie, and Davy; sisters: Kim and Jody; and a host of family and friends; and
9 WHEREAS, Patricia Ann McKinney Pendergrass leaves a lasting legacy of faith,
10 love, compassion, and determination and will be greatly missed by all whose lives she
11 touched.
12 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
13 does hereby express sincere and heartfelt condolences to the family and friends of Patricia
14 Ann McKinney Pendergrass.
15 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
16 family of Patricia Ann McKinney Pendergrass.
