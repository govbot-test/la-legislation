1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend and congratulate Brogan Murphy Cormier on being selected the 2025-2026

4 WHEREAS, Brogan Murphy Cormier is an exceptional senior at French Settlement
5 High School whose academic excellence, leadership, and integrity distinguish him as a most
6 deserving student of the year; and
7 WHEREAS, a lifelong resident of French Settlement, Brogan has demonstrated
8 sustained academic rigor by completing advanced placement, honors, and dual enrollment
9 course work while maintaining an unweighted grade point average of 3.98 and a weighted
10 grade point average exceeding 4.10; and
11 WHEREAS, Brogan earned a composite score of 27 on the ACT, including scores
12 of 29 in both English and mathematics, and achieved a PSAT/NMSQT score that placed him
13 among the upper national percentiles; and
14 WHEREAS, his performance on Louisiana LEAP assessments reflects consistent
15 excellence, with multiple "Advanced" and "Mastery" designations in English, biology,
16 algebra, geometry, and United States History; and
17 WHEREAS, Brogan has received numerous academic honors, including awards in
18 chemistry, forensic Science, United States History, digital media, English honors,
