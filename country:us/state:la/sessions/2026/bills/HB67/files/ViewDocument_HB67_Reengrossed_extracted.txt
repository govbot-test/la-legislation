9 * * *

HB 67 Reengrossed 2026 Regular Session Bacala
Abstract: Provides relative to publication of certain personal information of a protected
individual.
Present law (R.S. 44:11.2) limits access to personal information of protected individuals.
Further defines the individuals who fall within the class of a "protected individual" as it
relates to present law. Provides for the instances when a protected individual's personal
information shall not be published and when a protected individual may request the removal
of publicly posted personal information.

HLS 26RS-467 REENGROSSED
HB NO. 67
Proposed law retains present law.
Present law provides for an exception to the prohibition on the publication of personal
information of protected individuals. Provides that a protected individual is not exempt from
the publication of his personal information by the office of the secretary of state. Further
provides that a protected individual is not entitled to request the removal of his personal
information when publicly posted by the office of the secretary of state.
Proposed law retain present law. Additionally provides that a protected individual is not
exempt from the publication of his personal information by a district clerk of court or the
board of ethics. Further provides that a protected individual is not entitled to request the
removal of his personal information when publicly posted by a district clerk of court or the
board of ethics.
(Amends R.S. 44:11.2(J))
Summary of Amendments Adopted by House
The House Floor Amendments to the engrossed bill:
1. Provides that the board of ethics may publish personal information of a protected
individual.
