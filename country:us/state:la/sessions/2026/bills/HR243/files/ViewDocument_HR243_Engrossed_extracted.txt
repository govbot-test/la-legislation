HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 243
BY REPRESENTATIVES DICKERSON, MARTINEZ, AND SAWYER
HIGHWAYS: Urges and requests that the parish governing authorities surrounding the
Tickfaw River place safety warning signage on the river
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To urge and request that the parish governing authorities surrounding the Tickfaw River
3 erect safety warning signage on the Tickfaw River.
4 WHEREAS, the Tickfaw River is a one hundred thirteen mile long river that flows
5 from southwest Mississippi through southeast Louisiana, emptying into Lake Maurepas; and
6 WHEREAS, there are currently no parish governing authority signs erected for the
7 safety of boaters on the Tickfaw River; and
8 WHEREAS, on May 20, 2020, a single vessel incident occurred on the Tickfaw
9 River when the vessel attempted to turn from the Natalbany River into the Tickfaw River
10 but struck the west bank of the Tickfaw River; and
11 WHEREAS, two injuries were reported and contributing factors were careless and
12 reckless operation and operator inattention; and
13 WHEREAS, on April 16, 2023, another a single vessel incident occurred on the
14 Tickfaw River when the vessel attempted to turn at a high rate of speed but lost control,
15 traveling into the trees and coming to rest on its side; and
16 WHEREAS, two injuries were reported and contributing factors were excessive
17 speed, a rules of the road violation, and operator inexperience; and
18 WHEREAS, on July 28, 2024, there was a fatal two-boat collision on the Tickfaw
19 River near Kings Point in Livingston Parish, Louisiana, which resulted in two deaths and six
20 injuries; and

1 WHEREAS, on December 15, 2024, a two vessel incident occurred on the Tickfaw
2 River when vessel one was headed upstream on the Tickfaw River struck vessel two, headed
3 downstream and contributing factors were a rules of the road violation, sharp turn, and
4 operator inattention; and
5 WHEREAS, on June 10, 2025, a single vessel incident occurred on the Tickfaw
6 River, when the vessel attempted to turn from the Natalbany River into the Tickfaw River
7 but struck a tree on the bank of the Tickfaw River; and
8 WHEREAS, no injuries were reported and a contributing factor was careless and
9 reckless operation; and
10 WHEREAS, a total of thirteen boating incidents occurred on the Tickfaw River from
11 2020 to 2025, resulting in two fatalities and eighteen injuries, with various contributing
12 factors; and
13 WHEREAS, in the interest of public safety, there is a necessity to erect safety
14 warning signage on the Tickfaw River as a means of making boaters aware of potential
15 hazards on the river.
16 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
17 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby urge and request the parish governing authorities
18 surrounding the Tickfaw River to erect safety warning signage on the Tickfaw River.
19 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
20 presidents of the parish governing authorities surrounding the Tickfaw River.
