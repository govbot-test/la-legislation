HB 389 Original 2026 Regular Session McMakin
Abstract: Provides relative to qualifying major events in the Major Events Incentive
Program.
Present law provides for the Major Events Incentive Program.
Present law defines "qualified major event".
Proposed law provides that any major sport and entertainment event or major concert held
in Louisiana State University's football stadium, Tiger Stadium, as part of the DEATH
VALLEY LIVE stadium series and a College Football Playoff home game held in Louisiana
State University's football stadium, Tiger Stadium shall be considered a qualified major
event.
(Adds R.S. 51:1260(C)(5)(hh) and (ii))
