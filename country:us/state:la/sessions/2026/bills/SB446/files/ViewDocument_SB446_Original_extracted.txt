DIGEST
SB 446 Original 2026 Regular Session Fesi
Present law authorizes the Louisiana Wildlife and Fisheries Commission to promulgate rules
and regulations for the protection and preservation of fish, including the creation of closed
zones and buffer zones within which commercial menhaden fishing is prohibited.
Proposed law retains present law and designates a buffer zone by setting waters subject to
the menhaden season. Prohibits the commission from modifying any commercial menhaden
buffer zone prior to August 1, 2028.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Adds R.S. 56:325.7)
