HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 353
BY REPRESENTATIVE SCHLEGEL
SPECIAL DAY/WEEK/MONTH: Recognizes June 30, 2026, as Arthrogryposis Multiplex
Congenita (AMC) Awareness Day at the state capitol
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To recognize Tuesday, June 30, 2026, as Arthrogryposis Multiplex Congenita (AMC)
3 Awareness Day at the state capitol.
4 WHEREAS, Arthrogryposis multiplex congenita is a condition that causes joints to
5 be stiff and crooked at birth, and a newborn with AMC lacks the normal range of motion in
6 one or more joints; the condition develops prenatally and can be caused by an underlying
7 condition or syndrome; and
8 WHEREAS, AMC is an umbrella diagnosis with many syndromes and subtypes, and
9 the condition occurs in one in every three thousand live births; there are more than four
10 hundred identified types of the condition; and
11 WHEREAS, medical professionals do not fully understand the cause of AMC, but
12 it may be due to an unborn baby not moving properly during fetal development; and
13 WHEREAS, AMC can affect the joints of the hands, feet, hips, knees, elbows,
14 shoulders, wrists, fingers, toes, jaw, and spine, and the symptoms of the disease vary from
15 person to person; and
16 WHEREAS, children across the world battle AMC, and although the condition is not
17 curable, patients receive treatment, including stretching, physical therapy, occupational
18 therapy, and speech therapy, which allows them to live with as much independence in their
19 daily lives as possible; and
20 WHEREAS, on Tuesday, June 30, families across the world wear the color blue to
21 honor those affected by Arthrogryposis; increased awareness and expanded knowledge of
