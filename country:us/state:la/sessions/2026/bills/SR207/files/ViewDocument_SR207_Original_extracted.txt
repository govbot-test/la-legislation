SLS 26RS-4634 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 207
BY SENATOR HENRY
TEACHERS. Expresses support for the governor's efforts to identify and implement any
means authorized by law to fund a stipend for classroom teachers and support staff for the
2026-2027 Fiscal Year.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To express support for the governor's efforts to identify and implement any means
3 authorized by law to fund a stipend for classroom teachers and support staff for the
4 2026-2027 Fiscal Year.
5 WHEREAS, Article VIII, Section 13(B) of the Constitution of Louisiana requires the
6 State Board of Elementary and Secondary Education to annually develop and adopt a
7 formula to determine the cost of a minimum foundation program of education in all public
8 elementary and secondary schools and to equitably allocate funds to parish and city school
9 systems; and
10 WHEREAS, Article VIII, Section 13(B) of the Constitution of Louisiana further
11 requires the legislature to annually appropriate funds sufficient to fully fund the current cost
12 to the state of the minimum foundation program (MFP) as determined by applying the
13 approved formula; and
14 WHEREAS, Senate Concurrent Resolution No. 80 of the 2026 Regular Session of
15 the Legislature creates a task force to study the MFP formula and recommend a long-term,
16 predictable funding structure for teacher and support staff compensation; and
17 WHEREAS, that long term review does not address the immediate fact that the
18 2026-2027 Fiscal Year state budget does not renew the stipend previously provided to

1 classroom teachers and support staff; and
2 WHEREAS, the Senate supports the administration's efforts, including the governor,
3 the commissioner of administration, the state superintendent of education, the State Board
4 of Elementary and Secondary Education, and other appropriate state officials, in identifying
5 and implementing any means authorized by law to fund such stipend, including a reduction
6 and redirection of a portion of the MFP appropriation.
7 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
8 does hereby express support for the governor's efforts to identify and implement any means
9 authorized by law to fund a stipend for classroom teachers and support staff for the
10 2026-2027 Fiscal Year.
11 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that, if any reduction and redirection of the MFP
12 appropriation for the 2026-2027 Fiscal Year is determined to be in the best interest of the
13 state and its teachers and support staff, then such reduction and redirection shall be applied
14 only to funds attributable to non-instructional activities.
15 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
16 office of the governor.
