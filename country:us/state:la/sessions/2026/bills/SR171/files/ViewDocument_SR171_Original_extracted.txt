SLS 26RS-4344 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 171
DUPLESSIS, HARRIS, JACKSON-ANDREWS, JENKINS, LUNEAU
AND PRICE
CONDOLENCES. Expresses sincere and heartfelt condolences to the family and friends of
Troy Carter, general manager of Chicken Shack restaurant, on his passing.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To express sincere and heartfelt condolences of the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
3 upon the passing of Troy Carter, general manager, and the voice for the Chicken
4 Shack restaurant.
5 WHEREAS, it is with deep regret and profound sorrow that the citizens of Baton
6 Rouge, Louisiana, the customers and staff of Chicken Shack, and people worldwide, learned
7 that God Almighty in his infinite wisdom called Troy Carter to eternal rest on May 25, 2026,
8 at the age of sixty-nine; and
9 WHEREAS, Troy was born on November 15, 1956 in Checotah, Oklahoma and
10 enjoyed fishing, watching westerns shows and movies, reading the Bible, and dancing; and
11 WHEREAS, many described the fun loving man as the general manager who worked
12 for over forty-six years and became the voice of Chicken Shack located at 413 North
13 Acadian Thruway, Baton Rouge, LA; and
14 WHEREAS, Troy, in answer to his pastor's challenge to the congregation, "to take
15 the ministry to the marketplace" decided that when he records the restaurant's daily menu
16 on the answering machine, he would add an inspirational message at the end; and
17 WHEREAS, for seventeen years, the inspirational messages became so popular that
18 people from all over the world were known to call in just to listen to Troy's messages, which
Page 1 of 2

1 always addressed the issues of the moments such as violence, bad weather, lost souls, and
2 asked people to pray about those problems; and
3 WHEREAS, Troy regularly reminded people of positive things such as the need to
4 count their blessings, to pray continually for each other, to worship God, and give Him
5 praise, honor, and glory; and
6 WHEREAS, Chicken Shack's staff grieved him by posting on social media "It is with
7 the heaviest heart that we inform our friends and family that our dear family member Troy
8 Carter has gone to be with the Father. We are praying for strength and peace to all the
9 family, friends, and all the people this man has touched in his life. He will be sincerely
10 missed and never forgotten"; and
11 WHEREAS, Chicken Shack's staff tribute to Troy Carter, well describes the feelings
12 of so many that he impacted as he "took the ministry to the marketplace" per his pastor's
13 challenge.
14 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
15 does hereby express sincere and heartfelt condolences upon the passing of Troy Carter,
16 general manager, and the voice for Chicken Shack restaurant on North Acadian Thruway,
17 and extend enduring appreciation for the tremendous pride and honor that his memories will
18 forever bring to one of the oldest restaurant's in Baton Rouge, Louisiana.
19 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to Troy
20 Carter's family and the North Acadian Thruway Chicken Shack.
The original instrument and the following digest, which constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument, were prepared by Senate Legislative Services.
The keyword, summary, and digest do not constitute part of the law or proof
or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
