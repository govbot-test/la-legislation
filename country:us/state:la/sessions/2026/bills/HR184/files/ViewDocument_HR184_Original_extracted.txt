1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate Tuesday, April 21, 2026, as Junior League Day at the state capitol and to
3 commend the Junior League for its contributions to Louisiana.
4 WHEREAS, the Junior League is an organization whose mission is to advance
5 women's leadership for meaningful community impact through volunteer action,
6 collaboration, and training; and
7 WHEREAS, the Junior Leagues of Baton Rouge, Greater Covington, Lafayette,
8 Monroe, New Orleans, and Shreveport-Bossier are made up of several thousand members
9 who work to have a positive impact on the lives of residents across Louisiana; and
10 WHEREAS, the Junior Leagues of Baton Rouge, Greater Covington, Lafayette,
11 Monroe, New Orleans, and Shreveport-Bossier have made significant financial investments
12 in their communities through their community assistance funds and grant opportunities with
13 other partner organizations; these efforts aim to support community projects that enhance
14 the overall quality of life and contribute to the civic vitality of our state; and
15 WHEREAS, the Junior League and all of its affiliates are most deserving of
16 recognition for their continued efforts to uplift Louisiana.
17 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
18 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby designate Tuesday, April 21, 2026, as Junior League
19 Day at the state capitol and does hereby commend the Junior League for its contributions to
20 Louisiana.
