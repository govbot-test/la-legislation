HB 512 Original 2026 Regular Session Schamerhorn
Abstract: Prohibits the sale, manufacturing, or distribution of cell cultured food products
and the mixing of cell cultured food products with other meat products.
Proposed law defines "cell cultured food product" as any cultured animal tissue produced
from in vitro animal cell cultures outside of the organism from which it derived.
Proposed law prohibits the sale, manufacturing, or distribution of any cell cultured food
product. Further prohibits the mixing of any cell cultured food product with any "meat
product", as defined by present law (R.S. 3:4743(11)), for sale as food for human
consumption.
Present law (R.S. 3:4233(A)) enumerates certain prohibitions under present law.
Proposed law expands present law by prohibiting the sale, manufacturing, or distribution of
any cell cultured food product.
(Amends R.S. 3:4233(A)(4) through (14); Adds R.S. 3:4201(24), 4210.1, and 4233(A)(15))
