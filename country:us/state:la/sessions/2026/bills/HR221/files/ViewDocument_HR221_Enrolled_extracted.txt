ENROLLED
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 221
BY REPRESENTATIVE STAGNI
A RESOLUTION
To designate Monday, May 4, 2026, as Louisiana Firefighters Appreciation Day at the state
capitol and to commend firefighters for their service and dedication to public safety.
WHEREAS, firefighting is vital to the communities served by firefighters and critical
to the preservation of life and property, and communities recognize that firefighting is a
dangerous profession; firefighters can be subjected to acute exposure and the chronic effects
of mitigating fires in which hazardous materials are produced; and
WHEREAS, whenever an emergency occurs, firefighters place their lives at risk;
they respond to all types of disasters in-state and have volunteered their time and resources
throughout the United States to help others; and
WHEREAS, firefighters preserve life and property from the ravages of fire and other
disasters, and Louisiana firefighters continue the proud legacy of fire service in America;
it is fitting to honor the service and sacrifices made by firefighters and their families.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby designate Monday, May 4, 2026, as Louisiana
Firefighters Appreciation Day at the state capitol and does hereby commend firefighters
across the state for their dedication to public safety and service to the citizens of Louisiana.
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
