HLS 26RS-4656 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 221
BY REPRESENTATIVE STAGNI
SPECIAL DAY/WEEK/MONTH: Designates May 4, 2026, as Louisiana Firefighters
Appreciation Day at the state capitol
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate Monday, May 4, 2026, as Louisiana Firefighters Appreciation Day at the state
3 capitol and to commend firefighters for their service and dedication to public safety.
4 WHEREAS, firefighting is vital to the communities served by firefighters and critical
5 to the preservation of life and property, and communities recognize that firefighting is a
6 dangerous profession; firefighters can be subjected to acute exposure and the chronic effects
7 of mitigating fires in which hazardous materials are produced; and
8 WHEREAS, whenever an emergency occurs, firefighters place their lives at risk;
9 they respond to all types of disasters in-state and have volunteered their time and resources
10 throughout the United States to help others; and
11 WHEREAS, firefighters preserve life and property from the ravages of fire and other
12 disasters, and Louisiana firefighters continue the proud legacy of fire service in America;
13 it is fitting to honor the service and sacrifices made by firefighters and their families.
14 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
15 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby designate Monday, May 4, 2026, as Louisiana
16 Firefighters Appreciation Day at the state capitol and does hereby commend firefighters
17 across the state for their dedication to public safety and service to the citizens of Louisiana.
