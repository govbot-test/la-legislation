SLS 26RS-1102 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE BILL NO. 434
BY SENATOR FESI
HEALTH CARE. Provides relative to foods that contain vaccines. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 40:602(5) and to enact R.S. 602(13), relative to foods that
3 contain certain substances; to provide for definitions in the State Food, Drug, and
4 Cosmetic Law; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 40:602(5) is hereby amended and reenacted and R.S. 602(13) is
7 hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §602. Definition of terms
9 As used in this Part, unless the context otherwise indicates, the following
10 terms shall have the meaning ascribed to them in this Section:
11 * * *
12 (5) "Drug" includes all substances and preparations recognized in the official
13 compendium, as herein defined. It includes all substances and preparations intended
14 for use in the diagnosis, treatment, or prevention of disease in man or beast, and all
15 substances and preparations, other than food and cosmetics, intended to affect the
16 structure or any function of the body. Drug also includes any food intended for
17 human consumption that intentionally contains a vaccine or vaccine material.

2 (13) "Vaccine or vaccine material" means a substance that is:
3 (a) Intended for use in humans to stimulate the production of antibodies
4 and provide immunity against disease.
5 (b) Prepared from the causative agent of a disease, its products, or a
6 synthetic substitute treated to act as an antigen without inducing the disease.
7 (c) Authorized or approved by the United States Food and Drug
8 Administration.

DIGEST
SB 434 Original 2026 Regular Session Fesi
Present law provides for definitions in the State Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Law.
Proposed law adds a definition for "vaccine or vaccine material".
Proposed law provides that the definition of "drug" also includes any food intended for
human consumption that intentionally contains a vaccine or vaccine material.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 40:602(5); adds R.S. 40:602(13))
