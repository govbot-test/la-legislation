Section 1:
SLS 26RS-538 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY SENATOR MCMATH
HEALTH DEPARTMENT. Provides relative to home health licensure by the Louisiana
Department of Health. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 40:2116.31(B)(5) and 2116.32(C)(6), relative to home health
3 licensure by the Louisiana Department of Health; to provide for definitions; to
4 provide for licensing of home health agencies; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 40:2116.31(B)(5) and 2116.32(C)(6) are hereby amended and
7 reenacted to read as follows:
8 §2116.31. Scope; purpose; definitions
9 * * *
10 B. For the purpose of this Part unless the context clearly otherwise requires:
11 * * *
12 (5) "Home health aide services" means semi-skilled assistance by qualified
13 personnel with activities of daily living provided to the patient who requires
14 assistance in at least two areas of functioning and monitoring of vital signs, reporting
15 to a healthcare professional under a written plan of care, and requiring a clinical
16 note for each patient visit.
17 * * *

SLS 26RS-538 ORIGINAL
SB NO. 242
1 §2116.32. Licensing of home health agencies; applications; fees, disposition of fees
2 * * *
3 C. All initial applicants shall submit a request in writing to the department
4 on application forms furnished by it. Additional information required by the
5 department shall be provided by the applicant on supplemental forms as needed. The
6 application shall be accompanied by a non-refundable application fee, the amount
7 of which shall be promulgated in the minimum standards. Licenses shall be renewed
8 on an annual basis only for those agencies who are in substantial compliance with
9 minimum standards. In addition the applicant shall:
10 * * *
11 (6) Disclose in writing any financial or familial relationship with any other
12 entity providing home health care healthcare services in the state.
13 * * *
The original instrument and the following digest, which constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument, were prepared by Senate Legislative Services.
The keyword, summary, and digest do not constitute part of the law or proof
or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
DIGEST
SB 242 Original 2026 Regular Session McMath
Present law provides for definitions relative to home healthcare providers.
Proposed law makes technical changes to the definition of "home health aide services".
Present law provides for licensing and application requirements for home health agencies.
Proposed law makes technical changes to the disclosure requirements.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 40:2116.31(B)(5) and 2116.32(C)(6))
