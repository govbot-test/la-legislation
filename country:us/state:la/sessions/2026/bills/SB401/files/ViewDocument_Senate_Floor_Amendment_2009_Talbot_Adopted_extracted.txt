1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 6, between line 8 and line 9, insert the following:
3 "B. To the extent a manufacturer cannot fulfill its obligation to report as
4 required in Subsection A of this Section without disclosing proprietary, trade
5 secret, or similarly sensitive information to an individual employed by or
6 representing an entity whose interest in the information conflicts with that of
7 the manufacturer, the manufacturer may instead report information to the
8 commissioner. The commissioner shall review the submitted information to
9 determine whether it should be disclosed to the board without redaction. In
10 reviewing the information, the commissioner shall draw upon all reasonable
11 inferences in favor of sustaining the manufacturer's assertion of confidentiality.
12 Upon reaching a determination, the commissioner shall submit to the board all
13 submitted information deemed not covered by this Subsection and a summary
14 of any information that may be summarized without disclosing the information
15 determined to be proprietary, trade secret, or similarly sensitive, and shall hold
16 all other information strictly confidential. Any information submitted to the
17 commissioner and held confidential pursuant to this Subsection shall be exempt
18 from disclosure under the laws of this state."
