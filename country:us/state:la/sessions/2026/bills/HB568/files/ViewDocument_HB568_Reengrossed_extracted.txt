HB 568 Reengrossed 2026 Regular Session Firment
Abstract: Enhances the penalty for violating the Uniform Controlled Dangerous
Substances Law with respect to marijuana within 2,000 feet of a school zone.
Present law provides that a person who violates certain provisions of the Uniform Controlled
Dangerous Substances Law (UCDS) within 2,000 feet of a school zone shall be punished by
the maximum fine and one and one-half times the longest term of imprisonment provided
in the UCDS.
Proposed law provides that a person who violates the provisions of the UCDS with respect
to marijuana shall be imprisoned with or without hard labor for not more than one year and
may be fined up to $1,000.
(Amends R.S. 40:981.3(D); Adds R.S. 40:981.3(A)(4) and (E))
