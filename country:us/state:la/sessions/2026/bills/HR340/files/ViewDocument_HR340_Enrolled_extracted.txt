2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 340
BY REPRESENTATIVE CHASSION
A RESOLUTION
To designate Wednesday, June 3, 2026, as Prince Hall Shriners Day in the city of Lafayette
and to honor the distinguished legacy, community service, and charitable impact of
the Lafayette Prince Hall Shriners.
WHEREAS, the Prince Hall Shriners have made cultural, historical, and civic
contributions to the city of Lafayette and across the United States; the organization
celebrates the men who continue to exemplify leadership, brotherhood, and service
throughout the Louisiana; and
WHEREAS, the Prince Hall Shriners have a long-standing commitment to uplifting
families, supporting youth, advancing education, and promoting unity through benevolent
works; the organization's philanthropic efforts include scholarship initiatives, community
health awareness, and humanitarian assistance that uplifts residents in times of need; and
WHEREAS, the Prince Hall Shriners exemplify the highest ideals of brotherhood,
civic responsibility, and public service through their ongoing commitment to improving the
quality of life for all citizens, and it is fitting to honor the organization for its dedication,
benevolence, and service.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend the Prince Hall Shriners of Lafayette for
their cultural, historical, and social contributions to Louisiana and does hereby designate
Wednesday, June 3, 2026, as Prince Hall Shriners Day in the city of Lafayette.
