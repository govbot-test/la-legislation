HLS 26RS-5244 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 340
BY REPRESENTATIVE CHASSION
the city of Lafayette
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate Wednesday, June 3, 2026, as Prince Hall Shriners Day in the city of Lafayette
3 and to honor the distinguished legacy, community service, and charitable impact of
4 the Lafayette Prince Hall Shriners.
5 WHEREAS, the Prince Hall Shriners have made cultural, historical, and civic
6 contributions to the city of Lafayette and across the United States; the organization
7 celebrates the men who continue to exemplify leadership, brotherhood, and service
8 throughout the Louisiana; and
9 WHEREAS, the Prince Hall Shriners have a long-standing commitment to uplifting
10 families, supporting youth, advancing education, and promoting unity through benevolent
11 works; the organization's philanthropic efforts include scholarship initiatives, community
12 health awareness, and humanitarian assistance that uplifts residents in times of need; and
13 WHEREAS, the Prince Hall Shriners exemplify the highest ideals of brotherhood,
14 civic responsibility, and public service through their ongoing commitment to improving the
15 quality of life for all citizens, and it is fitting to honor the organization for its dedication,
16 benevolence, and service.
17 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
18 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend the Prince Hall Shriners of Lafayette for
19 their cultural, historical, and social contributions to Louisiana and does hereby designate
20 Wednesday, June 3, 2026, as Prince Hall Shriners Day in the city of Lafayette.
