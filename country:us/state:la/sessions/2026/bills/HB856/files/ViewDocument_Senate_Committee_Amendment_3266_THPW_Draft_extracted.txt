3 and insert the following:
4 "D. Procurements pursuant to this Section may include single award or
5 multiple award ID/IQ construction contracts. The legislature authorizes the
6 department and the office of Louisiana highway construction to procure ID/IQ
7 contracts using low bid and best value methods, and to award such contracts and any
8 resulting work orders using low bid or best value, as specified in the solicitation
9 documents. All procurement documents issued by the department and the office of
10 Louisiana highway construction, respectively, including the NOI and RFP, shall
11 indicate whether a single award or multiple awards will be made. Materials
12 separately acquired by the department or office of Louisiana highway construction
13 may be provided to contractors for use under and as a condition of any ID/IQ
14 contract.
15 E. Procurement and award of ID/IQ construction contacts shall meet the
16 following requirements
17 (1) Single award ID/IQ construction contracts procured on a low-bid basis
18 may be advertised and awarded using one of the following methods:
19 (a) In accordance with R.S. 48:252 and 255 for award to the lowest
20 responsive and responsible bidder; or
21 (b) In accordance with the advertisement and award procedures set forth in
22 this Section.
23 (2) Multiple-award ID/IQ construction contracts may be procured on a low
24 bid or best value basis, as determined by the department and office of Louisiana
25 highway construction, respectively. Such procurements shall be conducted in
26 accordance with applicable Louisiana public bid and procurement laws including the
27 requirements for advertisements and the procedures set forth in this Section.
28 (3)(a)The procurement of any multiple-award ID/IQ construction contract or
29 a single award ID/IQ construction contract not advertised and awarded pursuant to
30 R.S. 48:252 and 255, shall commence with the department and the office of
31 Louisiana highway construction, respectively, issuing a "notice of intent" (NOI)
32 through advertisement on their designated webpages to request letters of interest
33 (LOI). The NOI shall be advertised for at least ten business days prior to the deadline
34 for submission of LOI and shall include a description of the ID/IQ services to be
35 procured. The department and the office of Louisiana highway construction may also
36 readvertise the NOI using additional media or publications and may readvertise as
37 necessary if the number of responses received is deemed insufficient to ensure
38 adequate competition."
39 (b) After the deadline for submission of LOI for a single-award or
40 multiple-award ID/IQ construction contract, the department and office of Louisiana
41 highway construction shall issue a Request for Proposals ("RFP") to all contractors
42 that submitted an LOI. The RFP shall include instructions for proposal preparation
43 and submission, a description of the evaluation methodology, and shall comply with
44 the requirements of this Section.
45 (c) Bid bonds in accordance with R.S. 48:253 shall be specified in the
46 department and the office of Louisiana highway construction's standard
47 specifications or special provisions. The department and the office of Louisiana
48 highway construction may waive the requirement for bid bonds for procurements
49 under this Section.
50 (4)(a) The assistant secretary for project delivery, with the concurrence of the
51 secretary or designee, and the executive director of the office of Louisiana highway
52 construction , respectively, shall establish an evaluation committee for evaluation of
53 proposals received in response to the RFP. The evaluation committee may, in its
54 discretion, be assisted by other departments and office of Louisiana highway
55 construction personnel and external advisors in the evaluation the proposals.
56 (b) The evaluation committee shall evaluate the proposals and make a
57 recommendation of award to the secretary and executive director. The secretary,
58 executive director, or their designees, shall make the final determination of award.
59 (5) Prior to issuing a work order under a multiple-award ID/IQ construction
60 contract, the department and the office of Louisiana highway construction,
61 respectively, shall solicit competitive responses from the contractors awarded ID/IQ
62 contracts under the solicitation in accordance with procedures set forth in the ID/IQ

1 construction contract. The procurement documents or contract shall state the
2 procedures and selection criteria for issuance of work orders. Work orders shall be
3 awarded on a competitive low bid basis, or, where permitted by applicable law and
4 approved for use with federal funds, on a best value basis in which price is a
5 significant evaluation factor.
6 (6) Proposals received by the department and the office of Louisiana highway
7 construction and the evaluation committee's records, including the evaluation
8 committee's findings, grading, score sheets, and recommendations, shall not be
9 public records until the ID/IQ construction contract has been fully executed or the
10 procurement has been cancelled. Following contract execution or cancellation, such
11 records shall be subject to disclosure in accordance with applicable public records
12 laws. Proprietary, confidential, trade secret, or otherwise protected information will
13 remain exempt to the extent permitted by law. The meetings of the evaluation
14 committee will not be subject to the Open Meetings Law in Title 44 of the Louisiana
15 Revised Statutes.
16 F. The department and the office of Louisiana highway construction may
17 include the following items in their procurement documents or contracts:
18 (1) The method for determining price escalation or de-escalation procedures
19 when warranted by particular circumstances.
20 (2) Special provisions specific to ID/IQ construction contracts.
21 (3) Unit pricing or lists of work items specific to the ID/IQ construction
22 contract.
23 (4) A minimum quantity or dollar value if the department and the office of
24 Louisiana highway construction opts to set a minimum.
25 (5) A statement of procedures and selection criteria the department and office
26 of Louisiana highway construction shall use in issuing work orders.
27 (6) Any other information, terms, or requirements the department and the
28 office of Louisiana highway construction deem necessary to include in procurement
29 documents or contracts."
