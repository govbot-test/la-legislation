HCAHB126 4872 2084
HOUSE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
by Representative Mandie Landry
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, line 2, change “R.S. 15:284(A),” to “R.S. 15:284(A), (D), and (F),”
3 AMENDMENT NO. 2
4 On page 1, line 5, change “R.S. 15:284(A) is” to “R.S. 15:284(A), (D), and (F) are”
5 AMENDMENT NO. 3
6 On page 1, line 7, after "allow" and before "witness" insert "a any"
7 AMENDMENT NO. 4
8 On page 1, line 8, after "or" delete the remainder of the line and at the beginning of line 9,
9 delete "victim of a sex offense as defined in R.S. 15:541" and insert "any in a trial for a sex
10 offense as defined in R.S. 15:541"
11 AMENDMENT NO. 5
12 On page 1, after line 11, add the following:
13 "D. To ensure that the presence of a facility dog does not influence the jury
14 or is not a reflection on the truthfulness of any testimony that is offered by the victim
15 or witness, the court shall obscure the presence of the dog from the jury at all times,
16 instruct the jury on the role of the facility dog, and ensure that the facility dog is a
17 trained animal. The facility dog's entry into and exit from the courtroom shall be
18 done outside the presence of the jury.
19 * * *
20 F. This Section may be referred to and may be cited as "Duvall's Duvall and
21 Karissa's Law"."
