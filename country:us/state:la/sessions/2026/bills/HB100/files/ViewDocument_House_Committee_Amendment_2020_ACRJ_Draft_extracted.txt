1 To amend and reenact R.S. 14:99(A), relative to reckless operation of a vehicle; to provide
2 for behavior which constitutes reckless operation of a vehicle; and to provide for
3 related matters.
4 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
5 Section 1. R.S. 14:99(A) is hereby amended and reenaacted to read as follows:
6 §99. Reckless operation of a vehicle
7 A. Reckless operation of a vehicle is any of the following:
8 (1) the The operation of any motor vehicle, aircraft, vessel, or other means
9 of conveyance in a criminally negligent or reckless manner.
10 (2) The operation of a motor vehicle at or above one hundred miles per hour.

HB Draft 2026 Regular Session
Abstract: Adds the operation of a motor vehicle over 100 miles per hour to reckless
operation of a vehicle.
Present law provides that reckless operation of a vehicle is the operation in a criminally
negligent or reckless manner.
Proposed law provides that reckless operation of a vehicle is also the operation at or over
100 miles per hour.
(Amends R.S. 14:99(A))
