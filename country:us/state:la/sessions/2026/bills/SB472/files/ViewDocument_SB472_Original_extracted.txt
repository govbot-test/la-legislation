2026 Regular Session
SENATE BILL NO. 472
BY SENATOR JACKSON-ANDREWS
SUBSTANCE ABUSE. Provides for sober living homes. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 40:2159.3, relative to sober living homes; to provide for minimum standards;
3 to provide for rulemaking by the Louisiana Department of Health; and to provide for
4 related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 40:2159.3 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
7 §2159.3. Sober living homes
8 The Louisiana Department of Health shall develop minimum health and
9 safety standards for sober living homes. Prior to initiating rulemaking pursuant
10 to the Administrative Procedure Act, the department shall submit the standards
11 to the Senate and House health and welfare committees for review and
12 approval.

DIGEST
SB 472 Original 2026 Regular Session Jackson-Andrews
Proposed law requires the La. Dept. of Health to develop minimum health and safety
standards for sober living homes.
Proposed law further requires the department to submit the standards to the Senate and
House health and welfare committees for review and approval prior to promulgating rules.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Adds R.S. 40:2159.3)
