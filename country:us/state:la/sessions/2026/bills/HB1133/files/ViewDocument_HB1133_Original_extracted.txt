1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 39:100.257 and R.S. 40:600.121, relative to the Dream Starter Program; to
3 establish the program to provide assistance to eligible applicants with costs
4 associated with buying a home; to provide for definitions; to provide for eligibility
5 requirements; to provide for assistance terms; to provide for annual reporting; to
6 create the First-Generation Homebuyer Assistance Fund within the state treasury; to
7 provide for funding; to provide for the duties and authority of the Louisiana Housing
8 Corporation; to provide for effectiveness; and to provide for related matters.
9 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
10 Section 1. This Act shall be known and may be cited as the "Dream Starter Act".
11 Section 2. R.S. 39:100.257 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
12 §100.257. The First-Generation Homebuyer Assistance Fund; purpose
13 A. There is hereby created in the state treasury, a special fund, the First-
14 Generation Homebuyer Assistance Fund, hereinafter referred to in this Section as the
15 "fund".
16 B. After compliance with the requirements of Article VII, Section 9(B) of
17 the Constitution of Louisiana relative to the Bond Security and Redemption Fund,
18 the treasurer shall deposit into the fund monies transferred or appropriated by the
19 legislature, and any grants, donations, gifts, or other monies which may become
20 available.

8 Section 3. R.S. 40:600.121 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
9 §600.121. Dream Starter Program
10 A. The legislature hereby finds and declares that expanding sustainable
11 homeownership strengthens workforce stability, local tax bases, and long-term
12 household wealth and that residents without generational wealth or prior exposure
13 to homeownership face barriers that are not fully addressed by income screening
14 alone.
15 B. For the purposes of this Section, the following terms have the following
16 meanings:
17 (1) "First-time homebuyer" means an individual who has not held an
18 ownership interest in residential real estate within the previous three years.
19 (2) "First-generation homebuyer" means a first-time homebuyer who has not
20 previously held sole ownership of residential real estate and who meets one or more
21 additional criteria established by rules promulgated by the corporation pursuant to
22 this Section.
23 (3) "Principal residence" means an owner-occupied residential dwelling,
24 including a single-family residence and other owner-occupied residential structures
25 recognized by rules promulgated by the corporation pursuant to this Section.
26 (4) "Corporation" means the Louisiana Housing Corporation.
27 C.(1) There is hereby established the Dream Starter Program, to be
28 administered by the corporation for the purpose of creating a targeted and
29 accountable program to expand access to first-generation homeownership.

HB 1133 Original 2026 Regular Session Dana Henry
Abstract: Creates the Dream Starter Program for the purposes of providing homeownership
assistance and creates the First-Generation Homebuyer Assistance Fund for the
purpose of funding the program.
Proposed law provides for the creation of the First-Generation Homebuyer Assistance Fund
within the state treasury. Provides that the fund shall be comprised of monies transferred
or appropriated by the legislature, and any grants, donations, gifts, or other monies which
may become available. Provides that monies in the fund shall be invested in the same
manner as monies in the state general fund, with interest earned on investment of monies in
the fund being credited to the fund and unexpended or unencumbered monies in the fund at
the end of the fiscal year remaining in the fund.
Proposed law requires monies in the fund to be appropriated to the La. Housing Corp. for
the purposes of proposed law.
Proposed law provides for the "Dream Starter Act." Provides for purpose.
Proposed law defines "first-time homebuyer", "first-generation homebuyer", "principal
residence", and "corporation".
Proposed law provides for the establishment of the Dream Starter Program, to be
administered by the corporation. Provides that the program shall be funded through the
First-Generation Homebuyers Assistance Fund.
Proposed law provides that to qualify for assistance under the program, an applicant shall
do all of the following:
(1) Meet the definition of first-time homebuyer or first-generation homebuyer pursuant
to proposed law.
(2) Complete homebuyer education and financial counseling approved by the
corporation.
(3) Occupy the property as a principal residence for the period of time established by the
corporation.
Proposed law requires the corporation to submit a report to the legislature by Feb. 1st each
year detailing participation, geographic distribution, loan performance, and program
outcomes.
