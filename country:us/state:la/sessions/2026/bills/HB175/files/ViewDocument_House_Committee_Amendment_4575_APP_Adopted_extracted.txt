Amendments proposed by House Committee on Appropriations to Engrossed House Bill No.

1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, line 3, after "of the board;" delete the remainder of the line in its entirety and
3 delete line 4 in its entirety and insert the following:
4 "to establish the Veterans Service Grant Fund; to provide for the transfer, deposit,
5 and use, as specified, of monies in the Veterans Service Grant Fund;"
6 AMENDMENT NO. 2
7 On page 3, delete lines 2 through 11 in their entirety and insert the following:
8 "A. There is hereby established in the state treasury, as a special fund, the
9 Veterans Service Grant Fund, hereafter referred to in this Section as the "fund".
10 After allocation of monies to the Bond Security and Redemption Fund as provided
11 in Article VII, Section (9)(B) of the Constitution of Louisiana, the treasurer shall
12 deposit into the fund five hundred thousand dollars from the Lottery Proceeds Fund
13 as provided in Article XII, Section 6 of the Constitution of Louisiana. Monies in the
14 fund shall only be appropriated for grants awarded by the Veterans Service Grant
15 Board in accordance with the provisions of R.S. 47:9031.
16 B. Monies in the fund shall be invested in the same manner as monies in the
17 state general fund. Interest earned on investment of monies in the fund shall be
18 credited to the fund. Unexpended and unencumbered monies in the fund at the end
19 of the fiscal year shall be deposited into the Lottery Proceeds Fund."
