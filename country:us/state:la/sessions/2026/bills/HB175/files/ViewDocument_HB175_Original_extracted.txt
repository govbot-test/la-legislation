6 §9031. Veterans Game Board; disbursement of funds
7 A. The Veterans Game Board, hereafter referred to as the "veterans board",
8 is hereby created to be solely responsible for the disbursement of the funds in the
9 Veterans Game Fund to programs or entities with the purpose of bettering the lives
10 of veterans in this state. Veterans board members shall serve without compensation
11 and shall not require confirmation by the Senate.
12 B. The chief executive officer of the Louisiana Lottery Corporation shall
13 serve as chairman until the first meeting of the veterans board, at which time the
14 veterans board shall elect a chairman and vice chairman. The chairman and vice
15 chairman shall be elected annually.
16 C. The Louisiana Lottery Corporation shall provide administrative support
17 and staff as needed by the veterans board. Staff shall maintain all records of the
18 veterans board at the corporation's office. Administrative costs for the operation of
19 the veterans board, except salary or wages of the employees of the Louisiana Lottery
20 Corporation, shall be paid from the Veterans Game Fund as provided for in R.S.
21 47:9032.
22 D. The veterans board shall meet annually in person to review submitted
23 grant applications and disburse grant funds to selected applicants. The veterans
24 board shall meet at the same location the board of directors of the Louisiana Lottery
25 Corporation holds its meetings, unless the chairman finds it necessary or convenient
26 in the performance of the duties of the veterans board to meet at another location.
27 E. The veterans board shall consist of the following members:
28 (1) The secretary of the Department of Veterans Affairs or his designee.
29 (2) The secretary of the Louisiana Department of Health or his designee.

1 §9032. Veterans Game Fund
2 There is hereby created in the state treasury the Veterans Game Fund,
3 hereinafter referred to as the "fund". After satisfying the requirements of the Bond
4 Security and Redemption Fund as provided in Article VII, Section 9(B) of the
5 Constitution of Louisiana, the treasurer shall deposit into the fund all revenue
6 generated from lottery games created pursuant to R.S. 47:9008(D). All unexpended
7 and unencumbered principal monies in the fund at the end of the year shall be
8 returned to the Department of Treasury. Principal monies in the fund shall be
9 invested by the treasurer in the same manner as monies in the state general fund, and
10 any interest earned on such investment shall be deposited in and credited to the fund.

HB 175 Original 2026 Regular Session Hebert
Abstract: Provides for a lottery game and related fund with proceeds dedicated to
benefitting veterans and their families.
Proposed law (R.S. 47:9008(D)) directs the La. Gaming Control Bd. to create a new lottery
game for the benefit of veterans. Further requires the La. Gaming Control Bd. to adopt
regulations in accordance with the APA for purposes of the lottery game prescribed in
proposed law.
Proposed law requires all proceeds of the game to be deposited into the Veterans Game Fund
established in proposed law (R.S. 47:9032).
Proposed law (R.S. 47:9031) creates the Veterans Game Bd. and provides for composition,
recordkeeping, meeting requirements, and other matters relative to board functions.
Requires the La. Lottery Corp. to provide administrative support to the Veterans Game Bd.
Proposed law requires the Veterans Game Bd. to disburse proceeds of the lottery game
through one-time grants. Further provides certain criteria for grant applicants.
Proposed law (R.S. 47:9032) requires the treasurer to deposit all proceeds of the lottery game
into the Veterans Game Fund. Further requires the treasurer to invest proceeds in the same
manner as monies in the state general fund.
Proposed law requires unused principal funds at the end of each year to be returned to the
Dept. of Treasury. Further requires interest earned on investments to remain in the Veterans
Game Fund.
(Adds R.S. 47:9008(D), 9031, and 9032)
