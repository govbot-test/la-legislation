HOUSE BILL NO. 533
BY REPRESENTATIVE BERAULT
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 15:255(AA), relative to costs of court; to provide for the transfer of certain
3 surplus monies in the Twenty-Second Judicial District Court; to provide for
4 definitions; to provide for use of the monies; and to provide for related matters.
5 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
6 Section 1. R.S. 15:255(AA) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
7 §255. Witness fees to off-duty law enforcement officers
8 * * *
9 AA. In the Twenty-Second Judicial District, surplus monies in the special
10 fund in St. Tammany Parish at the end of each calendar year shall be transmitted by
11 the governing authority of St. Tammany Parish to the criminal court fund of the
12 Twenty-Second Judicial District Court. As used in this Subsection, "special fund"
13 means the special fund provided for in Subsection D of this Section, and "surplus
14 monies" means the amount of money which is in the special fund at the end of each
15 calendar year and which is in excess of the total amount paid from the special fund
16 as witness fees for off-duty law enforcement officers in that calendar year. The
17 surplus monies which are required to be transferred by this Subsection may be used
18 for any purpose the other monies in the criminal court fund of the Twenty-Second
19 Judicial District Court may be used. No money obligated to be paid to any officer
