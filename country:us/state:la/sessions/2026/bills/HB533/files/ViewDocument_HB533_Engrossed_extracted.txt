HB 533 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Berault
Abstract: Requires the local governing authority of St. Tammany Parish to transfer surplus
witness fees from the special fund in St. Tammany Parish to the criminal court fund
of the 22nd Judicial District Court (JDC).
Present law requires the collection of court costs, known as "witness fees" to pay off-duty
law enforcement officers appearing as a witness in a criminal case or delinquency
proceeding. Requires each judicial district to establish a schedule of witness fees, which are
placed in a special fund and administered by the parish governing authority.
Proposed law requires the governing authority of St. Tammany Parish to transfer surplus
monies in the special fund in St. Tammany Parish to the criminal court fund of the 22nd JDC
at the end of each calendar year.
(Adds R.S. 15:255(AA))
