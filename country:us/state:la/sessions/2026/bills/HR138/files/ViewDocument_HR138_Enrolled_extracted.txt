HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 138
BY REPRESENTATIVE HILFERTY
A RESOLUTION
To commend Martin Landrieu for his service to the Lakeview community in New Orleans.
WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize Martin Landrieu for his longstanding
dedication to civic involvement and to commend his decades of service to the Lakeview
community; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Landrieu has served as president of both the Lakeview Civic
Improvement Association and the Lakeview Crime Prevention District; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Landrieu served as chairman of the District 5 Neighborhood
Recovery Group, a board created in the aftermath of Hurricane Katrina that oversaw and
coordinated the seven Lake Area neighborhood associations responsible for rebuilding and
revitalizing the district; and
WHEREAS, during the hurricane recovery period, Mr. Landrieu played an
instrumental role in the creation and drafting of the current Lake Area Zoning Districts; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Landrieu has worked tirelessly for more than thirty years to
improve the quality of life, enhance safety, and foster community engagement in the
Lakeview neighborhood; and
WHEREAS, Martin Landrieu is most deserving of the highest recognition for his
civic leadership, which has left a lasting impact on Lakeview and its residents.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Martin Landrieu for his service to the
Lakeview community in New Orleans and does hereby extend enduring appreciation for his
continued efforts to improve the neighborhood and enhance the quality of life for its
residents.
