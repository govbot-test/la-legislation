HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 138
BY REPRESENTATIVE HILFERTY
COMMENDATIONS: Commends Martin Landrieu for his service to the Lakeview
community in New Orleans
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend Martin Landrieu for his service to the Lakeview community in New Orleans.
3 WHEREAS, it is most fitting to recognize Martin Landrieu for his longstanding
4 dedication to civic involvement and to commend his decades of service to the Lakeview
5 community; and
6 WHEREAS, Mr. Landrieu has served as president of both the Lakeview Civic
7 Improvement Association and the Lakeview Crime Prevention District; and
8 WHEREAS, Mr. Landrieu served as chairman of the District 5 Neighborhood
9 Recovery Group, a board created in the aftermath of Hurricane Katrina that oversaw and
10 coordinated the seven Lake Area neighborhood associations responsible for rebuilding and
11 revitalizing the district; and
12 WHEREAS, during the hurricane recovery period, Mr. Landrieu played an
13 instrumental role in the creation and drafting of the current Lake Area Zoning Districts; and
14 WHEREAS, Mr. Landrieu has worked tirelessly for more than thirty years to
15 improve the quality of life, enhance safety, and foster community engagement in the
16 Lakeview neighborhood; and
17 WHEREAS, Martin Landrieu is most deserving of the highest recognition for his
18 civic leadership, which has left a lasting impact on Lakeview and its residents.
19 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
20 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby commend Martin Landrieu for his service to the
21 Lakeview community in New Orleans and does hereby extend enduring appreciation for his
