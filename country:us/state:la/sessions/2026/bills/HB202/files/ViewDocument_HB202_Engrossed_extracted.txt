HLS 26RS-378 ENGROSSED
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE MCMAKIN
CIVIL SERVICE/UNCLASSIFD: Provides relative to job advertisement requirements and
notice to applicants
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 42:375.1, relative to State Civil Service; to provide relative to the
3 announcement of classified job vacancies; to require certain information within the
4 job announcement; to provide for notice of rejection; and to provide for related
5 matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 42:375.1 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §375.1. Vacancies in classified state civil service positions; announcement of job
9 vacancies; required information; notice of rejection
10 A. The Department of State Civil Service shall list the salary or salary range
11 within each job announcement for a vacant classified position.
12 B. If an individual applies for a vacant classified position posted by the
13 Department of State Civil Service and is rejected, the Department of State Civil
14 Service shall notify the applicant of the rejection.

HLS 26RS-378 ENGROSSED
HB NO. 202
DIGEST
The digest printed below was prepared by House Legislative Services. It constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument. The keyword, one-liner, abstract, and digest do not constitute
part of the law or proof or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
HB 202 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session McMakin
Abstract: Requires salary information to be included within a job announcement for a
vacant classified state civil service position and provides for a notice of rejection to
be sent to applicants.
Proposed law requires the Dept. of State Civil Service to list the salary or salary range of a
vacant classified position within the job announcement.
Proposed law provides that if an individual applies for a vacant classified position posted by
the Dept. of State Civil Service and the individual is rejected, the Dept. is required to notify
the individual of the rejection.
(Adds R.S. 42:375.1)
Summary of Amendments Adopted by House
The Committee Amendments Proposed by House Committee on House and
Governmental Affairs to the original bill:

Section 2:
1. Provide that proposed law applies to classified positions instead of unclassified
positions.
