HLS 26RS-378 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE BILL NO. 202
BY REPRESENTATIVE MCMAKIN
CIVIL SERVICE/UNCLASSIFD: Provides relative to job advertisement requirements and
notice to applicants
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 42:375.1, relative to State Civil Service; to provide relative to the
3 announcement of unclassified job vacancies; to require certain information within
4 the job announcement; to provide for notice of rejection; and to provide for related
5 matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 42:375.1 is hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §375.1. Vacancies in unclassified state civil service positions; announcement of job
9 vacancies; required information; notice of rejection
10 A. The Department of State Civil Service shall list the salary or salary range
11 within each job announcement for a vacant unclassified position.
12 B. If an individual applies for a vacant unclassified position posted by the
13 Department of State Civil Service and is rejected, the Department of State Civil
14 Service shall notify the applicant of the rejection.

HB 202 Original 2026 Regular Session McMakin
Abstract: Requires salary information to be included within a job announcement for a
vacant unclassified state civil service position and provides for a notice of rejection
to be sent to applicants.
Proposed law requires the Dept. of State Civil Service to list the salary or salary range of a
vacant unclassified position within the job announcement.
Proposed law provides if an individual applies for a vacant unclassified position posted by
the Department of State Civil Service and the individual has their application rejected, the
Dept. is required to notify the individual of the rejection.
(Adds R.S. 42:375.1)
