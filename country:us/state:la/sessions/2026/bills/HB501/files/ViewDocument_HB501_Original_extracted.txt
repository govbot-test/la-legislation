HB 501 Original 2026 Regular Session Carrier
Abstract: Authorizes the Allen Parish governing authority to determine whether Class VI
carbon dioxide injection wells may be permitted within the parish.
Present law authorizes the Dept. of Conservation and Energy to permit Class VI carbon
dioxide injection wells within the state.
Proposed law creates an exception to the department's permitting authority where Class VI
wells have been prohibited by the local option procedure provided in proposed law.
Proposed law authorizes the Allen Parish governing authority to determine whether Class
VI wells may be permitted within the parish by passing an ordinance or resolution.
Proposed law requires the parish governing authority to notify the department of any
resolution or ordinance passed for the purpose of prohibiting or allowing Class VI injection
wells within the parish.
Effective upon signature of governor or lapse of time for gubernatorial action.
(Adds R.S. 30:1104(F) and 1104.3)
