SLS 26RS-2912 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 60
BY SENATOR EDMONDS
SPECIAL DAY/WEEK/MONTH. Declares May 6, 2026, as Louisiana Recreation and Parks
Association Day at the Louisiana State Capitol.
1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 To designate Wednesday, May 6, 2026, as Recreation and Parks Association Day at the
3 Louisiana State Capitol and to designate the month of May as Recreation and Parks
4 Month in the state of Louisiana.
5 WHEREAS, the Louisiana Recreation and Parks Association's theme for Recreation
6 and Parks Month is "Build Together, Play Together"; and
7 WHEREAS, parks and recreation are essential to Louisiana's local communities
8 because they promote health and wellness, support childhood development, drive economic
9 opportunities, provide space for people to connect and socialize, and provide adaptable
10 infrastructure for resiliency in the face of natural disasters and climate change; and
11 WHEREAS, local parks departments across the state provide families with space to
12 gather and make memories, opportunities to introduce children to sports and nature, and a
13 variety of recreational activities; these parks provide Louisianians with a place to enjoy the
14 state's natural outdoor beauty, exercise, and take in fresh air; and
15 WHEREAS, recreation and parks increase a community's economic prosperity by
16 increasing property values, expanding the local tax base, increasing tourism, attracting and
17 retaining businesses, and reducing crime; and
18 WHEREAS, local parks play a fundamental role in promoting the environmental

1 well-being of Louisiana communities through the conservation of wildlife habitats and
2 native flora, and also provide green infrastructure to mitigate the effects of localized
3 flooding; and
4 WHEREAS, two park systems in Louisiana are part of the less than two percent of
5 American park systems that have received accreditation through the National Recreation and
6 Park Association: the New Orleans Recreation Development Commission and the capital
7 region's Recreation and Park Commission for the Parish of East Baton Rouge (BREC); and
8 WHEREAS, local recreation and parks departments from across Louisiana will join
9 the Louisiana Recreation and Parks Association in gathering at the Louisiana State Capitol
10 to promote the vital role they play in local communities.
11 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of Louisiana does hereby
12 designate Wednesday, May 6, 2026, as Recreation and Parks Association Day at the
13 Louisiana State Capitol and does hereby designate the month of May as Recreation and
14 Parks Month in the state of Louisiana.
15 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
16 Louisiana Municipal Association.
