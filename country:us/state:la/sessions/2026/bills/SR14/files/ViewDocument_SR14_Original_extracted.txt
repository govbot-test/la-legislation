SLS 26RS-101 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 14
BY SENATOR MIZELL
COMMENDATIONS. Commends and recognizes the history of Irish Americans and the
250th anniversary of their signing of the Declaration of Independence.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend and recognize the history of Irish Americans and the 250th anniversary of their
3 signing of the Declaration of Independence.
4 WHEREAS, in 2026, the United States marks two hundred fifty years of
5 independence; and
6 WHEREAS, from the very beginning of America's independence, Irish Americans
7 demonstrated a commitment of faith, culture, and charity while serving as soldiers in
8 Washington's army, leaders in politics, builders of cities, and servants of communities, never
9 forgetting their heritage while helping to build a stronger nation; and
10 WHEREAS, for generations, Irish immigrants and their descendants helped shape
11 America, by digging canals and building railroads, raising churches and schools, and filling
12 the ranks of the military, police, and fire services; and
13 WHEREAS, the Irish Independence Movement was a prolonged struggle for
14 self-rule, which culminated in the 1919-1921 Irish War of Independence, and the 1922
15 guerrilla conflict between the Irish Republican Army and British forces that led to the
16 creation of the Irish Free State, which consisted of twenty-six counties, while Northern
17 Ireland remained in the United Kingdom sparking the divisive 1922-1923 Irish Civil War
18 and shaping modern Irish politics; and

1 WHEREAS, key drivers of the movement included nationalism, republicanism, the
2 legacy of the 1916 Easter Rising, and armed resistance groups like the Irish Republican
3 Brotherhood (IRB); and
4 WHEREAS, Irish American groups are leading initiatives like the "Revolutionary
5 Irish Trail" and "Irish America 250" to highlight the vital, often unsung, roles of Irish
6 immigrants and their descendants in the American Revolution and building of the nation;
7 and
8 WHEREAS, these initiatives showcase the contributions of soldiers, leaders, and
9 community builders through events, trails, and educational projects to honor their heritage
10 and impact on democracy; and
11 WHEREAS, America's Irish roots run deep and have undoubtedly shaped the nation's
12 story by showcasing strength, resilience, and a commitment to democracy from the very
13 beginning; and
14 WHEREAS, America's Irish culture has been preserved and passed down for
15 generations and has contributed to the nation's rich heritage and history in many ways,
16 including through literature, poetry, music, and dance.
17 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
18 does hereby commemorate and recognize the 250th year anniversary of Irish Americans
19 history since the Declaration of Independence.
