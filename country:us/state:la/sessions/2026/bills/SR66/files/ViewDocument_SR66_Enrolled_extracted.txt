SR NO. 66 ENROLLED
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
does hereby commend and congratulate Lieutenant Colonel Geoffrey Shows upon his
retirement from the United States Marine Corps after twenty-three years of honorable and
dedicated service to his country and does hereby express sincere appreciation for his
contributions to the defense of the United States and the protection of the freedoms enjoyed
by all Americans.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to
Lieutenant Colonel Geoffrey Shows as an expression of the Senate's highest regard and best
wishes upon his retirement.
PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE
