SLS 26RS-1865 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 66
BY SENATOR LAMBERT
COMMENDATIONS. Commends LtCol Geoffrey Shows after twenty-three years in the
United States Marine Corps.
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To commend and congratulate Lieutenant Colonel Geoffrey Shows upon his retirement from
3 the United States Marine Corps after twenty-three years of honorable and dedicated
4 military service.
5 WHEREAS, Lieutenant Colonel Geoffrey Shows has rendered more than
6 twenty-three years of faithful and distinguished service to the United States of America as
7 a member of the United States Marine Corps, exemplifying the highest traditions of military
8 professionalism, leadership, and devotion to duty; and
9 WHEREAS, Lieutenant Colonel Shows is a proud graduate of St. Amant High
10 School and continued his education at Louisiana State University before earning his Juris
11 Doctor degree from Tulane University Law School, demonstrating a commitment to
12 academic excellence and public service; and
13 WHEREAS, throughout his distinguished military career, Lieutenant Colonel Shows
14 served in the United States Marine Corps as Staff Judge Advocate, providing essential legal
15 counsel and support to commanders and service members while upholding the rule of law
16 and the core values of honor, courage, and commitment; and
17 WHEREAS, Lieutenant Colonel Shows answered the call of duty during times of
18 conflict and was deployed in support of military operations in both Afghanistan and Iraq,

1 contributing to the defense of freedom and the security of the United States and its allies; and
2 WHEREAS, his final assignment in the United States Marine Corps was at the
3 Pentagon in Washington, D.C., where he continued to serve with distinction at the highest
4 levels of the Department of Defense; and
5 WHEREAS, in recognition of his exemplary service, dedication, and sacrifice,
6 Lieutenant Colonel Shows has earned the respect and gratitude of his fellow Marines, his
7 community, and the citizens of this nation; and
8 WHEREAS, upon the occasion of his retirement, it is fitting and proper to recognize
9 Lieutenant Colonel Geoffrey Shows for his unwavering commitment to duty and to extend
10 best wishes for continued success and fulfillment in the years ahead.
11 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
12 does hereby commend and congratulate Lieutenant Colonel Geoffrey Shows upon his
13 retirement from the United States Marine Corps after twenty-three years of honorable and
14 dedicated service to his country and does hereby express sincere appreciation for his
15 contributions to the defense of the United States and the protection of the freedoms enjoyed
16 by all Americans.
17 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to
18 Lieutenant Colonel Geoffrey Shows as an expression of the Senate's highest regard and best
19 wishes upon his retirement.
