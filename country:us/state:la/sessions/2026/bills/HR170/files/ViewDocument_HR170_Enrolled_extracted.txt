WHEREAS, four bridges on United States Highway 90, constructed nearly one
hundred years ago, were closed by the Louisiana Department of Transportation and
Development in May 2022 due to structural integrity deficiencies; and
WHEREAS, these closures resulted in the cutting off of a significant commercial,
commuter, and hurricane evacuation corridor to Mississippi for residents of and businesses
in St. Tammany, Orleans, and St. Bernard parishes; and
WHEREAS, the removal of these unsafe structures would be the first step in the
eventual reconnection of the critical segment of United States Highway 90 linking the New
Orleans metropolitan area to the state of Mississippi and the eastern gulf coast.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
Legislature of Louisiana does hereby memorialize the United States Congress to take such
actions as are necessary to facilitate the future reconstruction of the segment of United States
Highway 90 in St. Tammany Parish by taking such actions as are necessary to secure ten
million dollars in funding to be provided to the Louisiana Department of Transportation and
Development from the fiscal year 2027 Transportation, Housing, and Urban Development
appropriations request for purposes of removing four closed bridges on United States
Highway 90 in St. Tammany Parish.

To memorialize the United States Congress to take such actions as are necessary to secure
ten million dollars in funding to be provided to the Louisiana Department of
Transportation and Development from the fiscal year 2027 Transportation, Housing,

bridges on United States Highway 90 in St. Tammany Parish.

hundred years ago, were closed by the Louisiana Department of Transportation and

WHEREAS, these closures resulted in the cutting off of a significant commercial,

Legislature of Louisiana does hereby memorialize the United States Congress to take such
actions as are necessary to facilitate the future reconstruction of the segment of United States
Highway 90 in St. Tammany Parish by taking such actions as are necessary to secure ten
million dollars in funding to be provided to the Louisiana Department of Transportation and
