1 A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
2 To commend the Geaux Far Louisiana Coalition and its collaborative of fatherhood-focused
3 partners and to designate Tuesday, April 7, 2026, as Dad's Day at the Louisiana State
4 Capitol.
5 WHEREAS, families are the foundation of our communities, and the contributions
6 of fathers, grandfathers, stepfathers, and father figures are invaluable to the growth and
7 well-being of our society; and
8 WHEREAS, the role of a father encompasses teaching, guiding, nurturing, and
9 inspiring children to become responsible citizens who contribute positively to society; and
10 WHEREAS, the commitment and sacrifices made by fathers and father figures
11 deserve recognition and celebration; and
12 WHEREAS, Dad's Day provides an opportunity for communities across the state of
13 Louisiana to acknowledge and express gratitude for the love, support, and positive influence
14 that fathers provide in their families and neighborhoods; and
15 WHEREAS, the spirit of Dad's Day aligns with our state's values of family,
16 community, and civic responsibility, and fosters a culture of mutual respect and appreciation
17 among all citizens; and
