PUBLIC RECORDS. Provides that certain photographs, audio or video recordings, digital
images regarding a crime scene or used in a criminal prosecution are not public records
available for release or to be copied except by court order. (8/1/26)
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 44:4.1(B)(30) and to enact R.S. 44:3(A)(9) and 4(65), relative
3 to public records; to provide relative to the confidentiality of crime scene
4 photographs, audio or video recordings, digital images, which may be used as
5 evidence in a criminal proceeding; to provide for limited access under specified
6 circumstances; and to provide for related matters.

DIGEST
SB 106 Engrossed 2026 Regular Session Kleinpeter
Present law provides exceptions to public records for law enforcement agencies.
Proposed law creates a new exception to public records for crime scene photographs and
other evidence used in a criminal proceeding.
Present law provides a list of exceptions to public records.
Proposed law provides additional exception to the existing list.
Proposed law creates a new public records exception for crime scene photographs and other
evidence used in a criminal proceeding.
Effective August 1, 2026.
(Amends R.S. 44:4.1(B)(30); adds R.S. 44:3(A)(9) and 4(65))
