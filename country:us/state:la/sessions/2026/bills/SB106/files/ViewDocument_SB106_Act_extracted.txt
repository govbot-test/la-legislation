Section 1:
2026 Regular Session ACT No. 242 ENROLLED
BY SENATOR KLEINPETER
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 44:4(65), relative to public records; to provide relative to the confidentiality
3 of crime scene photographs, audio or video recordings, digital images, which may
4 be used as evidence in a criminal proceeding; to provide for limited access under
5 specified circumstances; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 44:4(65) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
8 §4. Applicability
9 This Chapter shall not apply:
10 * * *
11 (65) To a copy of any audio recording, image, video, or photograph in the
12 custody of a clerk of court after it is introduced into evidence of a criminal
13 prosecution and which is determined by the clerk to depict: an autopsy, dead
14 body, sexual assault, gruesome injury, obscene act, a juvenile victim, a victim's
15 genitals, pubic hair, anus, vulva, or female breast, or any patently offensive
16 sexual act, unless such image, video, or photograph is voluntarily released,
17 otherwise available to the general public, or released by court order.
PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
GOVERNOR OF THE STATE OF LOUISIANA
APPROVED:
