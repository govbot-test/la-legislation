SENATE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
Original Senate Bill No. 106 by Senator Kleinpeter
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 1, line 2, change "19.1" to " 4(65)"
3 AMENDMENT NO. 2
4 On page 1, line 8, change "and 19.1" to "and 4(65)"
5 AMENDMENT NO. 3
6 On page 2, delete lines 4 through 14 on their entirety and insert the following:
7 "(9) Copies of any audio recording, image, video, or photograph
8 in the custody of a clerk of court after it is introduced into evidence of
9 a criminal prosecution and which is determined by the clerk to depict:
10 an autopsy, dead body, sexual assault, gruesome injury, obscene act, a
11 juvenile victim, or any patently offensive ultimate sexual act, unless such
12 image, video, or photograph is voluntarily released, otherwise available
13 to the general public, or released by court order."
14 AMENDMENT NO. 4
15 On page 2, between lines 15 and 16, insert the following:
16 "§4. Applicability
17 This Chapter shall not apply:
18 * * *
19 (65) To a copy of any audio recording, image, video, or
20 photograph in the custody of a clerk of court after it is introduced into
21 evidence of a criminal prosecution and which is determined by the clerk
22 to depict: an autopsy, dead body, sexual assault, gruesome injury,
23 obscene act, a juvenile victim, or any patently offensive ultimate sexual
24 act, unless such image, video, or photograph is voluntarily released,
25 otherwise available to the general public, or released by court order.
26 * * *"
27 AMENDMENT NO. 5
28 On page 2, line 24, delete "19.1,"
29 AMENDMENT NO. 6
30 On page 2, delete lines 26 through 29 and on page 3, delete lines 1 through 9.
