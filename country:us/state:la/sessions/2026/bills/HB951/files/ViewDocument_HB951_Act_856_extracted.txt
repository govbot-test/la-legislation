5 Section 2. R.S. 36:308(E) is hereby enacted to read as follows:
6 §308. Offices; purposes and functions
7 * * *
8 E.(1) The Office of Business Workforce Solutions herein referred to as "the
9 office" shall perform the functions of the state relating to business-related workforce
10 services, providing both strategy and coordination of state, regional, and local
11 workforce service providers across the state. The office shall manage business-
12 related programs including Business Services, the Incumbent Worker Training
13 Program, and federal funding related to layoff aversion and response, as well as other
14 business-related programs and funding administered by Louisiana Works to be
15 determined at the discretion of the secretary.
16 (2) The office shall perform all of the following functions of the state:
17 (a) Assisting businesses in accessing training resources and identifying
18 sources of talent from all local and statewide programs.
19 (b) Reviewing and mapping existing workforce programs statewide across
20 all agencies, schools, postsecondary education, apprenticeships, training programs,
21 nonprofits, and local and regional partners.
22 (c) Coordinating state agencies to ensure high quality, integrated regional
23 workforce solutions are provided and taking into account the separate roles and
24 responsibilities of each agency.
25 (d) Coordinating workforce services on a statewide and regional basis.
26 (e) Managing federal and state workforce development funds to support
27 business needs in high-demand, high-salary jobs and priority industry sectors.
28 (f) Aligning state and regional workforce development services with state
29 and regional economic development goals.

16 Section 4. It is the intent of the legislature to create a new office within Louisiana
17 Works, which shall be known as the Office of Business Workforce Solutions, for the purpose
18 of performing functions of the state relating to business workforce solutions under the
19 guidance and oversight of the secretary of Louisiana Works. The Office of Business
20 Workforce Solutions shall serve as the primary office for business-related workforce
21 solutions and development, managing funding and programs, and providing both strategy
22 and coordination of state, regional, and local workforce service providers across the state.
