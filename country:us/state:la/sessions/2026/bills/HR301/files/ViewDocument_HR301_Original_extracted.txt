HLS 26RS-4663 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 301
BY REPRESENTATIVE BAYHAM
Kappa Epsilon Week in New Orleans
1 A RESOLUTION
2 To designate July 27 through August 2, 2026, as Tau Kappa Epsilon Week in New Orleans.
3 WHEREAS, Tau Kappa Epsilon was founded in 1899 at Illinois Wesleyan
4 University in Bloomington, Illinois; and
5 WHEREAS, Tau Kappa Epsilon is the largest fraternity in North America with more
6 than two hundred twenty active chapters; and
7 WHEREAS, Tau Kappa Epsilon was founded on the principles of love, charity, and
8 esteem, and the fraternity focuses on building better men who work to build a better world;
9 and
10 WHEREAS, Tau Kappa Epsilon will gather in the city of New Orleans for its Sixty-
11 Third Biennial Conclave to conduct the business of the fraternity, including education,
12 legislation, community impact, and leadership sessions; and
13 WHEREAS, the state of Louisiana and the city of New Orleans are proud to welcome
14 Tekes from across the world in celebration of the history and future potential of excellence
15 of Tau Kappa Epsilon.
16 THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the House of Representatives of the
17 Legislature of Louisiana does hereby designate the week of July 27 through August 2, 2026,
18 as Tau Kappa Epsilon Week in New Orleans and does hereby extend best wishes for success
19 to the organization and its members.
