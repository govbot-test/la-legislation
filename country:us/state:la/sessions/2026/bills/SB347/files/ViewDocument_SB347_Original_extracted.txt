DIGEST
SB 347 Original 2026 Regular Session Barrow
Present law provides for the following definitions relative to the Campus Accountability and
Safety Act: "Chancellor", "Confidential advisor", "Employee", "Institution", "Power-based
violence", "Responsible employee", "System president", and "Title IX coordinator".
Proposed law retains present law but adds "Deepfake" to the definition of "Power-based
violence".
Effective upon signature of the governor or lapse of time for gubernatorial action.
(Amends R.S. 17:3399.12(5))
