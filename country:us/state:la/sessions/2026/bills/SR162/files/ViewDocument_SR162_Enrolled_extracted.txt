because it is more than a weekly practice or a game schedule; and
WHEREAS, AVH is a unique way of life, resembling a brotherhood and sisterhood
that continues long after the final whistle; and

bonds formed along life's way that deeply unites the AVH team members.

does hereby commend and congratulate Acadiana Veterans Hockey (AVH) of Louisiana on
winning the 2026 USA Hockey-Long Drink Warrior National Championship Tier VIB.

2026 Regular Session ENROLLED
SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 162
BY SENATOR EDMONDS
A RESOLUTION
To commend and congratulate the Acadiana Veterans Hockey (AVH) of Louisiana on
winning the 2026 USA Hockey-Long Drink Warrior National Championship.
WHEREAS, on Sunday, April 26, 2026, AVH was crowned as Tier VIB champions
in Dallas, Texas; and
WHEREAS, AVH's mission is to support military veterans by bringing them together
through camaraderie, competition, and a shared passion for hockey in an effort to build a
community in the heart of Louisiana; and
WHEREAS, the players of AVH representing every branch of the military and a
wide range of life experiences are excited to step onto the ice and be part of something that
matters, that connects them deeply to each other, and the community; and
WHEREAS, members volunteer, support local events, and work together to raise
funds that allow the team to complete at higher levels while balancing careers, families, and
life after service which is not easy; and
WHEREAS, AVH is built on a foundation of trust, respect, and shared experiences,
it's a place where veterans are understood without explanation, where everyone is accepted,
and where support extends far beyond the hockey rink; and
WHEREAS, when one teammate is struggling, the entire team stands behind them
because it is more than a weekly practice or a game schedule; and
WHEREAS, AVH is a unique way of life, resembling a brotherhood and sisterhood
that continues long after the final whistle; and
WHEREAS, behind every jersey is a life shaped by challenges, growth, and the
bonds formed along life's way that deeply unites the AVH team members.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Senate of the Legislature of Louisiana
does hereby commend and congratulate Acadiana Veterans Hockey (AVH) of Louisiana on
winning the 2026 USA Hockey-Long Drink Warrior National Championship Tier VIB.
