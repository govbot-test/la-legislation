3 AMENDMENT NO. 2

5 AMENDMENT NO. 3
6 On page 1, at the beginning of line 7, delete "year"

8 On page 1, line 14, change "(C)" to "(C)(1)"
9 AMENDMENT NO. 5
10 On page 2, delete lines 12 through 29, and insert the following:
11 "C.(1) The board shall be composed of eleven members, at least five of
12 whom shall be qualified voters of the city of New Orleans, and shall have their
13 principal place of business in, or own property in, the district. Each board member
14 shall be subject to confirmation by the New Orleans city council.
15 (a) The members of the board shall possess additional qualifications and shall
16 be appointed as follows:
17 (i) Two of the members shall be appointed by the mayor of the city of New
18 Orleans.
19 (ii) One of the members shall be appointed by the New Orleans city council
20 member representing City Council District B of the New Orleans city council.
21 (iii) One of the members shall be appointed by New Orleans & Company.
22 (iv) Two of the members shall be appointed by the New Orleans Chamber of
23 Commerce, subject to approval by the president of the New Orleans city council.
24 (v) One of the members shall be appointed by the Greater New Orleans Hotel
25 and Lodging Association, subject to approval by the president of the New Orleans
26 city council.
27 (vi) One of the members shall be appointed by the Louisiana Restaurant
28 Association, subject to approval by the president of the New Orleans city council.
29 (vii) One of the members shall be appointed by the member or members of
30 the Louisiana House of Representatives who represent representing the district.
31 (viii) Two of the members shall be appointed by the member or members of
32 the Louisiana Senate who represent representing the district."
33
34 AMENDMENT NO. 6
35 On page 3, delete lines 1 through 12 in their entirety
36 AMENDMENT NO. 7
37 On page 4, line 15, delete "provided for a term not to exceed fifty years from and after"and
38 insert "provided for a term not to exceed fifty years from and after"
