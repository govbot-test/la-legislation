ENROLLED
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVES BACALA, ADAMS, BAYHAM, BOURRIAQUE, BOYD,
BROUSSARD, CARLSON, CARPENTER, CARVER, CHASSION, DEWITT,
EDMONSTON, EGAN, FREIBERG, GREEN, JORDAN, KNOX, LAFLEUR,
TERRY LANDRY, LYONS, MARCELLE, MARTINEZ, MILLER, MOORE,
SAWYER, TAYLOR, WALTERS, AND WYBLE
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 17:81(B), relative to the use of school buildings; to provide
3 relative to the powers of local public school boards; to authorize a school board to
4 engage in a cooperative endeavor with a credit union or bank to operate a branch at
5 a high school; to provide for the integration of the operation of the branch into a
6 required financial literacy course; and to provide for related matters.
7 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
8 Section 1. R.S. 17:81(B) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
9 §81. General powers of local public school boards
10 * * *
11 B.(1) Each school board may permit school buildings to be used outside of
12 regular school hours for academic purposes including but not limited to tutoring and
13 study hall. The board shall adopt rules and regulations governing the terms and
14 conditions, including fees if any, under which such buildings shall be used.
Page 1 of 2
CODING: Words in struck through type are deletions from existing law; words underscored
are additions.
HB NO. 285 ENROLLED
1 (2) Each school board may engage in a cooperative endeavor with a credit
2 union or bank to operate a branch at a high school. Any such cooperative endeavor
3 shall provide for the integration of the operation of the branch into the financial
4 literacy course required by R.S. 17:270 and provide students with the opportunity to
5 obtain the skills, knowledge, and experience necessary to manage their personal
6 finances and obtain general financial literacy as provided in R.S. 17:270.
7 * * *
