1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 3, delete lines 19 through 24 and insert the following:
3 "A.(1) If an education agency has evidence that an education service
4 provider or vendor of educational products is a foreign adversary, an agent
5 of a foreign adversary, a foreign terrorist organization, or an agent, proxy, or
6 contractor thereof, the agency shall immediately notify the attorney general
7 and provide written notice to the service provider or vendor that the contract
8 is subject to review. Such review shall be conducted in accordance with the
9 due process provisions of R.S. 17:410.21. If, in accordance with those due
10 process provisions, the service provider or vendor is determined to be a
11 foreign adversary, foreign terrorist organization, or an agent, proxy, or
12 contractor thereof, the service provider or vendor shall be ineligible, pursuant
13 to this Part, to participate in any such contract.
14 (2) The education agency shall take all practicable and timely actions
15 to ensure continuity of student services, minimize any disruption resulting
16 from the cancellation of the contract, and provide for the feasible and orderly
17 transition of services to a qualified and eligible provider that is not a foreign
18 adversary or a foreign terrorist organization nor operated by an agent of
19 either a foreign adversary or a foreign terrorist organization.
20 (3) An education service provider or vendor of educational products
21 that is in violation of this Part may cure the violation by divesting ownership
22 or control of the company, removing any individual from a position of
23 ownership, management, or governance who is an agent of a foreign
24 adversary or foreign terrorist organization, or ceasing any financial
25 transactions, payments, or other support to such foreign adversary or foreign
26 terrorist organization within a reasonable period of time as established by the
27 education agency pursuant to rules promulgated by the education agency.
28 Upon satisfactory demonstration of such corrective action, the entity shall be
29 considered in compliance with the provisions of this Part.
30 B. If an education agency has evidence that a nonprofit charter school
31 board has contracted with a charter school management company or charter
32 school management organization that is a foreign adversary, a foreign
33 terrorist organization, or an agent of either, the education agency shall
34 provide prompt written notice to the nonprofit charter school board advising
35 it of the actions taken pursuant to Subsection A of this Section. Such notice
36 shall afford the nonprofit charter school board a reasonable opportunity to
37 ensure the continuity of school operations by doing either of the following:
38 (1) Working with the charter school management company to
39 undertake curative action by removing foreign adversary agents or foreign
40 terrorist agents from the company.
41 (2) Renegotiating or transitioning the charter school management
42 contract to a qualified and eligible management company or organization that
43 is not a foreign adversary or a foreign terrorist organization nor operated by
44 an agent of either a foreign adversary or a foreign terrorist organization."
