ENROLLED
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE BILL NO. 1010
BY REPRESENTATIVE DESHOTEL
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 47:1836 and to enact R.S. 47:2058.1, relative to ad valorem
3 taxes; to provide for duties of parish tax assessors and collectors; to require parish
4 tax assessors and collectors to report certain ad valorem tax assessment and
5 collection data to the Louisiana Tax Commission; to require the commission to
6 include the data in certain reports; to provide for reports of the commission to the
7 governor and the legislature; and to provide for related matters.
8 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
9 Section 1. R.S. 47:1836 is hereby amended and reenacted and R.S. 47:2058.1 is
10 hereby enacted to read as follows:
11 §1836. Studies and reports
12 A. In addition to the duties prescribed elsewhere in this Title, the tax
13 commission shall examine carefully into all cases where evasion or violation of the
14 laws for assessment and taxation of property is alleged, complained of or discovered,
15 and ascertain wherein existing laws are defective or are improperly or negligently
16 administered.
17 B. The tax commission shall also investigate the tax systems of other states
18 and countries and formulate and recommend such legislation as may be deemed
19 expedient to prevent evasion of assessment and secure just and equal taxation.

1 C.(1) The tax commission shall transmit to the governor and to each member
2 of the legislature, not less than thirty days before the meeting of the legislature, a
3 communication report setting forth, in an abbreviated, clear and concise form such
4 facts drawn from the tax commission's general report as may be instructive to the
5 members in regard to existing conditions or needed legislation. The report to the
6 governor and members of the legislature shall include, at minimum, all of the
7 following:
8 (a) The total assessed value of taxable property by parish.
9 (b) The total amount of ad valorem taxes assessed by parish.
10 (c) The total amount of ad valorem taxes collected by parish.
11 (d) A breakdown of the taxes assessed by property classification, including
12 but not limited to residential, commercial, industrial, and public service property.
13 (e) Ad valorem tax collection amounts by parish in comparison to the total
14 assessed value of property in the parish, expressed as a percentage.
15 (2) The tax commission may include in the report required by this
16 Subsection any additional data or analysis as it deems necessary to provide a
17 comprehensive understanding of ad valorem tax assessments and collections.
18 * * *
19 §2058.1. Report of assessed values and collections data
20 A.(1) On or before November fifteenth of each year, the assessor of each
21 parish shall provide to the Louisiana Tax Commission a report of the total amount
22 of ad valorem property taxes assessed within the parish for the preceding tax year.
23 The assessor shall itemize the report by property classification and by property listed
24 in accordance with R.S. 47:1957.
25 (2) On or before November fifteenth of each year, the tax collector of each
26 parish shall provide to the Louisiana Tax Commission a report of the total amount
27 of all ad valorem taxes collected for the preceding tax year on all taxable property
28 within the parish.
