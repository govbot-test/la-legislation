19 "§1836. Studies and reports
20 A. In addition to the duties prescribed elsewhere in this Title, the tax
21 commission shall examine carefully into all cases where evasion or violation of the
22 laws for assessment and taxation of property is alleged, complained of or discovered,
23 and ascertain wherein existing laws are defective or are improperly or negligently
24 administered.
25 B. The tax commission shall also investigate the tax systems of other states
26 and countries and formulate and recommend such legislation as may be deemed
27 expedient to prevent evasion of assessment and secure just and equal taxation.
28 C.(1) The tax commission shall transmit to the governor and to each member
29 of the legislature, not less than thirty days before the meeting of the legislature, a
30 communication report setting forth, in an abbreviated, clear and concise form such
31 facts drawn from the tax commission's general report as may be instructive to the
