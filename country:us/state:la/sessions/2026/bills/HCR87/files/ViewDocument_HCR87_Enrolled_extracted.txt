ENROLLED
2026 Regular Session
HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 87
BY REPRESENTATIVE LACOMBE
A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
To express legislative support for the letter submitted on January 6, 2026, by United States
Senator John N. Kennedy to the Unites States Fish and Wildlife Service.
WHEREAS, a letter was formally submitted on January 6, 2026, by United States
Senator John N. Kennedy to the United States Fish and Wildlife Service.
THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of Louisiana hereby
expresses its full support for the January 6, 2026, letter submitted by United States Senator
John N. Kennedy.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the
director of the United States Fish and Wildlife Service.
SPEAKER OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE
