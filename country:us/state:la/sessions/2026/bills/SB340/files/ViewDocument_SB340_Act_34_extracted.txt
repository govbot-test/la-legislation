2026 Regular Session ACT No. 34 ENROLLED
SENATE BILL NO. 340
BY SENATOR CATHEY
1 AN ACT
2 To amend and reenact R.S. 47:1703.1(A), relative to registration for the homestead
3 exemption; to require the assessor in each parish to provide property owners a form
4 for the permanent registration of the homestead exemption; to provide for an
5 effective date; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 47:1703.1(A) is hereby amended and reenacted to read as follows:
8 §1703.1. Permanent registration of homestead exemption; designated parishes
9 A. The tax assessor of each parish for the parishes of Acadia, Allen,
10 Ascension, Assumption, Avoyelles, Beauregard, Bienville, Caddo, Calcasieu,
11 Caldwell, Cameron, Catahoula, Claiborne, Concordia, DeSoto, East Baton Rouge,
12 East Carroll, East Feliciana, Evangeline, Franklin, Grant, Iberia, Iberville, Jackson,
13 Jefferson, Jefferson Davis, Lafayette, Lafourche, LaSalle, Lincoln, Livingston,
14 Morehouse, Natchitoches, Orleans, Ouachita, Pointe Coupee, Rapides, Red River,
15 Richland, Sabine, St. Bernard, St. Helena, St. James, St. John the Baptist, St. Landry,
16 St. Martin, St. Mary, St. Tammany, Tangipahoa, Tensas, Terrebonne, Union,
17 Vermilion, Vernon, Washington, Webster, West Baton Rouge, West Carroll, West
18 Feliciana, and Winn shall provide a form to property owners within the parish for
19 permanent registration for the benefits of the homestead exemption provided for in
20 R.S. 47:1703.
