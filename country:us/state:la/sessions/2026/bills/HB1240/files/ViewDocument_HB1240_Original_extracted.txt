HB 1240 Original 2026 Regular Session Hilferty
Abstract: Reconstitutes and reconfirms the board of commissioners membership within the
Southeast Louisiana Flood Protection Authority-East.
Present law specifies that the board of commissioners of the Southeast La. Flood Protection
Authority-East be composed of nine members, with specified members residing in certain
parishes within the state. Requires the members be appointed by the governor from
nominations submitted by the nominating committee under specific conditions. Specifies
that within 10 days of an unexpected vacancy of the board, the board must notify the chair
of the nominating committee. Requires notification by the chair be published in the official
journal of the state and of each parish within the territorial jurisdiction of the authority no
later than 30 days of notice of the unexpected vacancy. Requires the nominating committee
meet to determine the notification to send to the governor for appointment to which the
governor must appoint the nominees and submit the appointees to the Senate for
confirmation.
Proposed law requires that, as of Dec. 1, 2026, the composition and membership of the
Southeast La. Flood Protection Authority-East be reconstituted and reconfirmed by the
