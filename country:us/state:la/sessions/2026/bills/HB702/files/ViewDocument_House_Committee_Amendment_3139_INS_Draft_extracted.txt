10 On page 2, between lines 3 and 4, insert the following:
11 "(3) If an insured has purchased uninsured motorist coverage or underinsured
12 motorist coverage pursuant to R.S. 22:1295, the insurer shall permit such coverage
13 to apply when the insured is occupying a transportation network company vehicle
14 as a passenger during a prearranged ride. Nothing in this Section shall prohibit a
15 personal automobile insurer from providing such coverage under these
16 circumstances."
