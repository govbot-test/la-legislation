SCAHB872 4442 2942
SENATE COMMITTEE AMENDMENTS
2026 Regular Session
No. 872 by Representative Zeringue
1 AMENDMENT NO. 1
2 On page 2, delete lines 1 through 3 and insert:
3 "D.(1) All vessels engaged in commercial menhaden reduction fishing shall
4 have installed and utilize an automatic identification system (AIS). This requirement
5 shall apply to a carrier vessel or steamer, commonly called a mother ship, and to any
6 purse boat actively engaged in fishing.
7 (2) No vessel that is required to utilize an AIS under this Paragraph shall
8 engage in fishing without a functioning AIS for more than two consecutive business
9 days. For a group of vessels cooperating on a set as defined in R.S. 56:325.5, the AIS
10 of at least two vessels shall be functioning at all times while actively engaged in
11 fishing."
12 AMENDMENT NO. 2
13 On page 2, after line 7, insert:
14 "Section 3. This Act shall become effective on April 19, 2027."
