Section 1:
HLS 26RS-924 ORIGINAL
2026 Regular Session
BY REPRESENTATIVE ZERINGUE
FISHING/COMMERCIAL: Requires all commercial menhaden reduction fishery vessels
to use an AIS system
1 AN ACT
2 To enact R.S. 56:8(160) and 325.5(Section heading) and (D), relative to commercial
3 menhaden reduction fishery vessels; to require vessels to use automatic identification
4 systems; to provide definitions; to direct the Louisiana State Law Institute to
5 organize and renumber certain definitions; and to provide for related matters.
6 Be it enacted by the Legislature of Louisiana:
7 Section 1. R.S. 56:8(160) and 325.5(Section heading) and (D) are hereby enacted
8 to read as follows:
9 §8. Definitions
10 For purposes of this Chapter, the following words and phrases have the
11 meaning ascribed to them in this Section, unless the context clearly shows a different
12 meaning:
13 * * *
14 (160) "Automatic identification system" means a very high frequency (VHF)
15 radio-based tracking system that allows vessels to automatically exchange data, such
16 as position, speed, and heading, with other vessels, shore stations, and satellites.
17 * * *
18 §325.5. Commercial menhaden; harvest reporting; automatic identification system
19 * * *

HLS 26RS-924 ORIGINAL
HB NO. 872
1 D. All vessels engaged in commercial menhaden reduction fishing shall have
2 and utilize an automatic identification system (AIS). This requirement shall apply
3 to both carrier vessels or steamers and purse boats.
4 Section 2. The Louisiana State Law Institute is hereby authorized and directed to
5 alphabetize and renumber the definitions contained in R.S. 56:8 and to correct any
6 cross-references to the renumbered Paragraphs if necessary, consistent with the provisions
7 of this Act.
DIGEST
The digest printed below was prepared by House Legislative Services. It constitutes no part
of the legislative instrument. The keyword, one-liner, abstract, and digest do not constitute
part of the law or proof or indicia of legislative intent. [R.S. 1:13(B) and 24:177(E)]
HB 872 Original 2026 Regular Session Zeringue
Abstract: Requires all vessels engaged in commercial menhaden reduction fishing to use
automatic identification systems.
Proposed law provides a definition for "automatic identification system".
Proposed law requires all vessels engaged in commercial menhaden reduction fishing to use
an automatic identification system, including the carrier vessels or steamers (also known as
"mother ships") and boats used to deploy purse seines.
Directs the La. State Law Institute to alphabetize and renumber definitions in present law
(R.S. 56:8).
(Adds R.S. 56:8(160) and 325.5(Section heading) and (D))
